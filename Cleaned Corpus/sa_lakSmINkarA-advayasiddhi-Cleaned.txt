Yoginī Lakṣmīṅkarā Advayasiddhi

advayasiddhi yoginī lakṣmīṅkarā viracitā

oṃ namaḥ śrīvajrasattvāya

prakṛtiprabhāsvaraṃ nāthaṃ sarvajñaṃ tribhavodbhavam ।
praṇamya śirasā vajram īpsitārthaphalapradam ।। 

deśakālatithivāranakṣatrair maṇḍalair vinā ।
vakṣye haṃ vajrasattvasya saṃkṣepāt sādhanaṃ param ।। 

niyamavratopavāsair akṣaroccāraṇabhāvanaiḥ ।
atattvayogo na siddhyet kalpakoṭiśatair api ।। 

viḍvajrodakabījādyair nāsikābhyantarodbhavaiḥ ।
pūjayed satataṃ mantrī ātmānaṃ tattvabhāvanaiḥ ।। 

jananīṃ bhaginīś caiva duhitṛbhāgineyikān ।
prajñnopāyavidhānena pūjayed yogavit sadā ।। 

ekāṅgavikalāṃ hīnāṃ śilpanīṃ śvapacīṃ tathā ।
yoṣitāṃ pūjayen nityaṃ jñānavajraprabhāvanaiḥ ।। 

tatremāni bījapadāni – oṃ āḥ hūṃ

yena yena hi baddhyante jantavo raudrakarmaṇā ।
sopāyena tu tenaiva mucyante bhavabandhanāt ।। 

samayān aharahaḥ kuryād divyān pañcakulodbhavān ।
pūjayec ca pradīpādyaiḥ sakṣīrair viśvasambhavaiḥ ।। 

protphullanayano mantrī nityaṃ prahasitānanaḥ ।
cittam āropya saṃbodhau bhāvayej jñānasāgaram ।। 

yāvat sthiracalā bhāvā santy atra tribhavālaye ।
sarve te tattvayogena draṣṭavyā vajradhṛg yathā ।। 

paravādinaś ca kecil lingabhedair vyavastitāḥ ।
te apy atra nāvamantavyā vajrasattvavikurvitaiḥ ।। 

sarvān samarasīkrtya bhāvān nairātmyaniḥsṛtān ।
bhāvayet satataṃ mantrī dehaṃ prakṛtinirmalam ।। 

gandhamālyādibhir vastrair dhūpanaivedyakais tathā ।
gītavādyais tathā nṛtyaiḥ sopāyair bhajate vibhuḥ ।। 

na kaṣṭakalpanāṃ kuryān nopavāsaṃ na ca kriyām ।
snānaṃ śaucaṃ na caivātra grāmadharmavivarjanaṃ ।। 

na cāpi vandayed devān kāṣṭhapāṣāṇamṛṇmayān ।
pūjām asyaiva kāyasya kurvān nityaṃ smāhitaḥ ।। 

makṣikācchardisaṃmiśrair viṇmūtrādyaiś ca bhāvitaiḥ ।
pañcapradīpasaṃyuktaiḥ pūjayed vajradhāriṇam ।। 

abalā svayambhukusumaiḥ sakṣīrair viśvasambhavaiḥ ।
pūjayed devatāṃ tena dehasthāṃ tattvabhāvanaiḥ ।। 

parasvaharaṇaṃ kuryāt paradāraniṣevanam ।
vaktavyaṃ ca mṛṣāvākyaṃ sarvabuddhāṃś ca ghātayet ।। 

śailamṛṇmayacaityādīn na kuryāt pustake ratim ।
na maṇḍalāni svapne api kāyavākcittakarmaṇā ।। 

jugupsāṃ naiva kurvīta sarvavastuṣu mantravit ।
vajrasattvaḥ svayaṃ tatra sākṣād rūpeṇa saṃsthitaḥ ।। 

gamyāgamyavikalpaṃ tu bhakṣyābhakṣyaṃ tathaiva ca ।
peyāpeyaṃ tathā mantrī kuryān naiva samāhitaḥ ।। 

vairocanasamudbhūtān sarvaprāṇyaṅgasambhavān ।
prāṇakān guhyatattvajño bhakṣayet siddhihetunā ।। 

sarvavarṇasamudbhūtā jugupsyā naiva yoṣitaḥ ।
saiva bhagavatī prajñā saṃvṛtyā rūpam āśrityā ।। 

na tithir na ca nakṣatraṃ nopavāso vidhīyate ।
advayajñānayuktasya siddhir bhavati saugatī ।। 

bahunātra kim uktena yad bhaved upalabdhikam ।
tat sarvaṃ tattvayogena draṣṭavyaṃ tattvavedinā ।। 

hastyaśvakharagāvoṣṭrapradīpaṃ śvānasambhavam ।
mahāpradīpasaṃmiśraṃ bhakṣayed yogavit sadā ।। 

na cādhyāsaktiṃ kurvīta ekasminn api yogavit ।
samatācittayogena bhāvanīyo bhavārṇavaḥ ।। 

utpattisthitinirodhaṃ cāsampṛktaṃ pṛthagjanaiḥ ।
tasya bhāve tu saṃsāro nānyatra pralayodbhavaḥ ।। 

dinaṃ tu bhagavān vajrī naktaṃ prajñā vidhīyate ।
evaṃ tu bhāvayed yogī laghu siddhim avāpnuyāt ।। 

yat tad avyaktarūpaṃ tu sarvasattveṣu saṃsthitam ।
guruvaktrāt paraṃ tattvaṃ prāpyate nātra samśayaḥ ।। 

apratiṣṭhitanirvāṇaṃ nirnimittaṃ nirālayam ।
vyāpakaṃ sarvasattveṣu saṃbodhiḥ paramaṃ padam ।। 

evaṃ matvā tu vai yogī yo abhyased buddhimān sadā ।
sa siddhyati na sandeho mandapuṇyo api mānavaḥ ।। 

ācāryāt parataraṃ nāsti trailokye sacarācare ।
yasya prasādāt prāpyante siddhayo anekadhā budhaiḥ ।। 

vajrasattvaḥ sa vai jñeyaḥ sarvabuddhair namaskṛtaḥ ।
ācāryaḥ paramo devaḥ pūjanīyaḥ prayatnataḥ ।। 

sa eva tathatārūpī lokānugrahahetunā ।
rūpam āśritya saṃvṛtyā saṃsthito yogapīṭhake ।। 

mṛtyur eṣa vikalpo yaṃ na bhāvaḥ sarvavastuṣu ।
hanyate svavikalpena pṛthagjanavijṛmbhitaiḥ ।। 

śrīmadoḍīyānavinirgatā mahāyogapīṭhāgatā akhilayogatantratattvagarbhā śrīlakṣmīmukhakamalād viniḥsṛtā svādhiṣṭhānakramodayā advayasiddhir nāma sādhanopāyikā samāpteti
