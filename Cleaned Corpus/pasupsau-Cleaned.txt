atha caturtho adhyāyaḥ

gūḍha-vidyā tapa ānantyāya prakāśate ।।
gūḍha-vrataḥ ।।
gūḍha-pavitra-vāṇiḥ ।।
sarvāṇi dvārāṇi pidhāya ।।
buddhyā ।।
unmatavad eko vicareta loke ।।
kṛtānam utsṛṣṭam upādadīta ।।
unmatto mūḍha ity evaṃ manyante itare janāḥ ।।
asan-māno hi yantrāṇāṃ sarveṣām uttamaḥ smṛtaḥ ।।
indro vā agre asureṣu pāśupatam acarat ।।
sa teṣām iṣṭāūrtam ādatta ।।
māyayā sukṛtayā samavindata ।।
nindā hy eṣāindā tasmāt ।।
nindyamānaś caret ।।
anindita-karmā ।।
sarva-viśiṣṭo ayaṃ panthāḥ ।।
sat-pathaḥ ।।
kupathās tv anye ।।
anena vidhinā rudra-samīpaṃ gatvā ।।
na kaścid brāhmaṇaḥ punar āvartate ।।
atreaṃ brahma japet ।।
tat-puruṣāya vidmahe ।।
mahā-devāya dhīmahi ।।
tan no rudraḥ pracodayāt ।।
