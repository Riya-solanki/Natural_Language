uparyupari tatrāpi gatis tava tapomayī 
yāṃ na vidmo vayaṃ sarve pṛcchanto api pitāmaham ।। 
pitāmaho api bhagavāṃs tvatprasādād yadṛcchayā । yāṃ yoganiratā viṣṇo bhajante brahmavādinaḥ । 
lokas tv arvāg duṣkṛtināṃ nāgalokas tu dāruṇaḥ 
pṛthivī karmaśīlānāṃ kṣetraṃ sarvasya karmaṇaḥ ।।
