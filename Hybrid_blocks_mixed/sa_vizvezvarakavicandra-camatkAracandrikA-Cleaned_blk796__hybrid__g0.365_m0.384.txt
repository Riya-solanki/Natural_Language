atrātadguṇānāmapi kālakaṇṭha yadvikārā viṣkaraṇaṃ so ayamatadguṇa iti vyācakṣate । tadasmin mate api kriyākāraṇānāmasāmarthyādahetvalaṅkāra eva ।
sadṛśādanubhūtārthapratītiḥ smaraṇaṃ matam ।।
yathā
saṃtrāsādupavindhyakānanabilaiḥ paryantaniryanmadhu- srotaskā parilokya gaṇḍadṛṣado vyāmāvakāśaspṛśaḥ ।
uddaṇḍapratigaṇjabhairavahṛtāḥ pratyarthibhiḥ pārthivaiḥ smaryante nijagandhabandhuraghaṭāḥ tṛṭyatkaṭagranthayaḥ ।।
