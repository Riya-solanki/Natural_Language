samavāyābhyupagamācca tadabhāva iti prakṛtenāṇuvādanirākaraṇena saṃbadhyate ।
dvābhyāṃ cāṇubhyāṃ dvyaṇukamutpadyamānamatyantabhinnamaṇubhyāmaṇvoḥ samavaitītyabhyupagamyate bhavatā ।
nacaivamabhyupagacchatā śakyate aṇukāraṇatā samarthayitum ।
kutaḥ ।
sāmyādanavasthiteḥ ।
yathaiva hyaṇubhyāmatyantabhinnaṃ sadvyaṇukaṃ samavāyalakṣaṇena saṃbandhena tābhyāṃ saṃbadhyata evaṃ samavāyo api samavāyibhyo atyantabhinnaḥ sansamavāyalakṣaṇenānyenaiva saṃvandhena samavāyibhiḥ saṃbadhyetātyantabhedasāmyāt ।
tataśca tasya tasyānyonyaḥ saṃbandhaḥ kalpayitavya ityanavasthaiva prasajyeta ।
