jāyante lakṣaṇabhraṣṭā daridrāḥ puruṣādhamāḥ ।
tato niṣkaluṣībhūtā kule mahati yoginaḥ ।।
jāyante lakṣaṇopetā dhanadhānyasamanvitāḥ ।।
iti śrāgāruḍe mahāpurāṇe pūrvakhaṇḍe prathamāṃśākhye ācārakāṇḍe yājñavalkyoktakarmavipākanirūpaṇaṃ nāma caturuttaraśatatamo adhyāyaḥ
