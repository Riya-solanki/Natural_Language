na ekasmin eva tejasi śarīrāntaraprepsāvelāyāṃ jīvaḥ avatiṣṭhate kāryasya śarīrasya anekātmakatvadarśanāt ।
darśayataḥ ca etam arthaṃ praśnaprativacane aāpaḥ puruṣavacasaḥ' iti ।
tat vyākhyātam atryātmakatvāttu bhūyastvāt' iti atra ।
śrutismṛtī ca etamarthaṃ darśayataḥ ।
śrutiḥ apṛthvīmaya āpomayo vāyumaya ākāśamayastojomayaḥ' ityādyā ।
smṛtiḥ api aaṇvyo mātrāvināśinyo daśārdhānāṃ tu yāḥsmṛtāḥ ।
tābhiḥ sārdhamidaṃ sarvaṃ saṃbhavatyanupūrvaśaḥ' ityādyā ।
