iti sāsram ātmanā taṃ nirmañchayañcakruḥ ।
[] tasyāḥ sāntvanārthaṃ samuditā muditās tatraiva tasthuś ca । tatra ca putanayā kṛtam ajanyaṃ jananyāv anyāś ca sva-svadṛṣṭam anyo' nyaṃ nirdiṣṭavatyaḥ । tathā hi,
yathāgatā sā yad uvāca yac ca vā
cakāra tad-grastam amū samūcatuḥ ।
amūḥ samūcuḥ yathā svayaṃ gatā
yathānvapaśyaṃś ca tathā sa-gadgadam ।। [vaṃśasthavilā]
