yadanyadbrahmaṇo akṣaraśabdavācyamihāśaṅkyate tadbhāvādidamambarāntavidhāraṇamakṣaraṃ vyāvartayati śrutiḥ- atadvā etadakṣaraṃ gārgyadṛṣṭaṃ daṣṭraśrutaṃ śrotramataṃ mantravijñātaṃ vijñātṛ' iti ।
tatrādṛṣṭatvādivyapadeśaḥ pradhānasyāpi saṃbhavati ।
draṣṭṛtvādivyapadeśastu na saṃbhavatyacenatvāt ।
tathā anānyadato asti draṣṭṛ nānyadatosti śrotṛ nānyadato asti mantṛ nānyadato asti vijñātṛ' ityātmabhedapratiṣedhāt na śārīrasyāpyupādhimato akṣaraśabdavācyatvam ।
aacakṣuṣkamaśrotramavāgamanaḥ' iti copādhimattāpratiṣedhāt ।
nahi nirupādhikaḥ śārīro nāma bhavati ।
tasmātparameva brahmākṣaramiti niścayaḥ ।।
