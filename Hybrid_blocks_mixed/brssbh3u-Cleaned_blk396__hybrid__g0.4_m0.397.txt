astyātharvaṇikānāmupaniṣadārambhe mantrasamāmnāyaḥ - asarvaṃ pravidhya hṛdayaṃ pravidhya dhamanīḥ pravṛjya śiro abhipravṛjyatridhā vipṛktaḥ' ityādiḥ ।
tāṇḍinām - adeva savitaḥ prasuva yajñam' ityādiḥ ।
śāṭhyāyaninām - aśvetāśvo haritanīlo asi' ityādiḥ ।
kaṭhānāṃ taittirīyāṇāṃ - aśaṃ no mitraḥ śaṃ varuṇaḥ' ityādiḥ ।
vājasaneyināṃ tūpaniṣadārambhe pravargyabrāhmaṇaṃ paṭhyate - adevā ha vai niṣeduḥ' ityādi ।
kauṣītakināmapyagniṣṭomabrāhmaṇam - abrahma vā agniṣṭomo brahmaiva tadaharbrahmaṇaiva te brahmopayanti te amṛtatvamāpnuvanti ya etadaharupayanti' iti ।
kimime sarve pravidhyamādayo mantrāḥ pravargyādīni ca karmāṇi vidyāsūpasaṃhriyerankiṃvā nopasaṃhriyeranniti mīmāṃsāmahe ।
kiṃ tāvannaḥ pratibhāti upasaṃhāra evaiṣāṃ vidyāsviti ।
kutaḥ ।
vidyāpradhānānāmupaniṣadgranthānāṃ samīpe pāṭhāt ।
