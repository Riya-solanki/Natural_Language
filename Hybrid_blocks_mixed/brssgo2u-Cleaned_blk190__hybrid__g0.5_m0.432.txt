ekasyāpi brahmaṇo vicitraśaktiyogādupapadyate vicitro vikāraprapañca ityuktam ।
tatpunaḥ kathamavagamyate paraṃ brahmeti ।
taducyate ।
sarvopetā ca taddarśanāt ।
sarvaśaktiyuktā ca parā devatetyabhyupagantavyam ।
kutaḥ ।
taddarśanāt ।
yathāhi darśayati śrutiḥ sarvaśaktiyogaṃ parasyā devatāyāḥ - asarvakarmā sarvakāmaḥ sarvagandhaḥ sarvarasaḥ sarvamidamabhyātto avākyanādaraḥ', asatyakāmaḥ satyasaṃkalpaḥ', ayaḥ sarvajñaḥ sarvavit', aetasya vā akṣarasya praśāsane gārgisūryācandramasau vidhṛtau tiṣṭhataḥ' ityevañjātīyakā ।।
