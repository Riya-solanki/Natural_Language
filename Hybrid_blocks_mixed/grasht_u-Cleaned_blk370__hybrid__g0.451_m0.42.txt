suvarṇābhidhānabījapraśaṃsanamāha ya ityādi ।।
bhujaṅgāt sīsakāt niḥsṛtaḥ bhujaṅgaśilāvāpena kṣayaṃ nītvā yaḥ pṛthagbhūtaḥ sa rasakesarīvajrapañjaraḥ kathitaḥ rasa eva kesarī siṃhaḥ tadarthaṃ vajrapañjaraḥ vajreṇa vyadhitaḥ pañjaro'tidṛḍhatvāt siṃharakṣaṇasamartha ityarthaḥ ।।
punarviśeṣeṇocyate ayaṃ vajrapañjaro na kiṃtu ayaṃ rasāṅkuśaḥ raso gajarūpaḥ tasyāṅkuśaḥ vaśīkaraṇasamarthaḥ ।।
nāma saṃbhāvanāyām ।।
vikhyātaḥ prakathitaḥ ।।
kasmāt kuṭilāt kimapi vastuharaṇāt kuṭilo vakro bhavati duṣṭasvabhāva eva anena hemnā nāgaharaṇaṃ kṛtam ।।
kathaṃbhūtāt kuṭilāt phaṇī bhujaṅgaḥ hema svarṇaṃ tayorguṇā vidyante yasmin evaṃvidhāt kuṭilādeśo yuktaḥ aṅkuśo'pi vakro bhavatīti ।।
atra bhrāntimānalaṅkāraḥ hemabīje vajrapañjaragajāṅkuśadarśanād bhrāntirjāteti ।।
