aśāmyaṃ vairam utpannaṃ mama kṛṣṇasya cobhayoḥ 
śāntim ekatare śāntiṃ gate yāsyanti yādavāḥ ।। 
tiṣṭha vā gaccha vā mūḍha yatheṣṭaṃ mama pārśvataḥ ।। hantuṃ svajanam udvṛttaṃ so ayaṃ yadukulodvahaḥ । mā bhūd ayaṃ parīvādo loke yādavasattamāḥ । anyathā vadhayogyo asi nātra kāryā vicāraṇā । 
gacchatv ayaṃ dānapatiḥ kṣipram ānayituṃ vrajāt 
nandagopaṃ ca gopāṃś ca karadān mama śāsanāt ।।
