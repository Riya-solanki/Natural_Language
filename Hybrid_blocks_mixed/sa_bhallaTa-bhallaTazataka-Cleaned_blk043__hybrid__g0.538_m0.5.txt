sādhūtpātaghanauga sādhu sudhiyā dhyeyaṃ dharāyām idaṃ ko anyaḥ kartum alaṃ tavaiva ghaṭate karmedṛśaṃ duṣkaram 
sarvasyaupayikāni yāni katicit kśetrāṇi tatrāśaniḥ sarvān aupāyikeṣu dagdhasikatāraṇyeṣv apāṃ vṛṣṭayaḥ ।।
