{kāñcukīyaḥ} - tatas tataḥ 
{pratīhārī} - tado tahiṃ gacchia pucchido kudo imāe vīṇāe āgamo tti 
teṇa bhaṇiaṃ ahmehiṃ ṇammadātīre kuyyagummaḷaggā diṭṭhā 
jai ppaoaṇaṃ imāe uvaṇīadu bhaṭṭiṇotti 
taṃ ca uvaṇīdaṃ aṅke karia mohaṃ gado bhaṭṭā 
tado mohappaccāgadeṇa bapphapayyāuḷeṇa muheṇa bhaṭṭiṇā bhaṇiaṃ diṭṭhāsi ghosavadi sā hu ṇa dissadi tti 
ayya īdiso aṇavasaro 
kahaṃ ṇivedemi 
[tatas tatra gatvā pṛṣṭaḥ kuto asyā vīṇāyā āgama iti । tena bhaṇitam asmābhir narmadātīre kūrcagulmalagnā dṛṣṭā । yadi prayojanam anayā upanīyatāṃ bhartre iti । tāṃ copanītām aṅke kṛṭvā mohaṃ gato bhartā । tato mohapratyāgatena bāṣpaparyākulena mukhena bhartrā bhaṇitaṃ dṛṣṭāsi ghoṣavati sā khalu na dṛśyata iti । ārya īdṛśo anavasaraḥ । kathaṃ nivedayāmi । ]
