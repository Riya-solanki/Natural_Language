tasminnabhiprāye anyamatam āhānya ityādi ।।
eke uktavidhānena cāraṇāṃ kurvanti anye apare rasaṃ pāradaṃ svacchaṃ kṛtvā svedanādyaṣṭasaṃskāropasaṃskṛtaṃ vidhāya vā hiṅgulotthaṃ ghanam abhrakaṃ cārayanti abhrakasya cāraṇāṃ kurvanti ।।
kena śukapicchamukhena śukapicchaṃ saṃdhānaviśeṣaḥ mukhaṃ viśeṣo yeṣāṃ saṃdhānānāṃ tena ।।
kva sati cārayanti āśitagrāse sati bhuktakavale sati punaś cāryam ityarthaḥ ।।
kena siddhopadeśavidhinā siddhā rasasiddhā nityanāthavīranāthādayaḥ pūrvoktāḥ teṣāṃ ya upadeśavidhis tena ।।
na śuṣkeṇa saṃdhānenārdrībhāvāt nīrasatāṃ prāptena punaścāraṇā na syāt ।।
śukapicchaṃ yathā ।
bhasmakṣārān suśuṣkāṃstu kṣārāṃśca lavaṇāni ca ।
āloḍya hy amlavargeṇa śulbabhāṇḍe nidhāpayet ।।
yāvacca śukapicchābhamabhrakaṃ tena bhāvayet ।
grasate tatkṣaṇātsūto golakastu vidhīyate ।।
iti ।।
