budhitāḥ paitṛka-janatā
buddhāḥ śvaśurābhimānino lokāḥ ।
mānitāḥ sarve' py asmābhir
na mato dharmas tu kenāpi ।।
[] yad asmākaṃ bālyād eva tasmin nija-rmaṇatā-bhāvanāṃ dūrīkartum āgraham urīkurvanti, tasmād asmān mā sneha-peṣaṃ piṇḍhi, kintu svābhīṣṭam eva viśaṇḍhi ।
