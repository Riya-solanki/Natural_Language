iti gītāvākyaiśca vivekajñānasyaiva mokṣahetutvamucyate ato vivekajñānameva sākṣādavidyānivṛttyā mokṣahetuḥ ।
yogena kevalatmasākṣātkārastu yogyānupalabdhividhayopādhyādigatadharmābhāvamupādhyādibhedaṃ ca grāhayati tato avidyānivṛttiriti ।
etena sarvabhūteṣu samatājñānam ātmanaḥ sarvātmakatvādijñānaṃ ca śrutismṛtyorgīyamānaṃ vivekajñānasyaiva śeṣabhūtaṃ sarvadarśaneṣu mantavyam ।
jñānāntarāṇāṃ sākṣādabhimānānivartakatvāt brahmamīmāṃsāyāṃ tvayaṃ viśeṣo yatparamātmavivekaśeṣatvam ।
sāṃkhyaśāstre tu sāmānyātmavivekaśeṣatvamiti dik ।।
