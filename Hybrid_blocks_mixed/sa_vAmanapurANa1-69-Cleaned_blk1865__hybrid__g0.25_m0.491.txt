praṇipatya ca kāmārimumāṃ ca girinandinīm ।
dṛṣṭvā hutāśanaṃ prītyā kuṭilāṃ kṛttikāstathā ।।
dadṛśurbālamatyugraṃ ṣaṇmukhaṃ sūryasaṃnibham muṣṇantamiva cakṣuṃṣi tejasā svena devatāḥ ।।
kautukābhivṛtāḥ sarve evamūcuḥ surottamāḥ ।
devakāryaṃ tvayā deva kṛtaṃ devyāgninā tathā ।।
