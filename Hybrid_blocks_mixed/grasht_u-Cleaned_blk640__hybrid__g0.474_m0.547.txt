taccāha kāntetyādi ।।
kāntābhraśulbatāpyaṃ kāntaṃ cumbakaṃ abhrakaṃ gaganaṃ śulbaṃ tāmraṃ tāpyaṃ mākṣikaṃ ityapi mahābījam ।।
eteṣāṃ mahābījānāṃ cet saṃkarabījaṃ pratyekaṃ dravyasya bījasaṃjñā sā tarhi catuḥṣaṣṭipramāṇā syādityarthaḥ ।।
