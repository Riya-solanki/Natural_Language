adhunā saṃskārāṇāṃ sādhane lakṣaṇam āha ।।
tatra prathamoddiṣṭasya svedanasaṃskārasya sādhanaṃ spaṣṭayannāha āsurītyādi ।।
sūtasya pāradasya tridinaṃ dinatrayaparimāṇaṃ yathā syāttathā mṛduvahninā svalpāgninā svedaḥ svedanaṃ kāryam ।।
kena kāñjikena sauvīreṇa ।।
kaiḥ saha āsurīpaṭukaṭukatrayacitrārdrakamūlakaiḥ saha ।।
āsurī rājikā paṭu saindhavaṃ lavaṇaviśeṣaḥ kecitpaṭuśabdena kṣāramapi vyācakṣate kaṭukatrayaṃ śuṇṭhīmaricapippalyaḥ citrakaṃ pratītam ārdrakaṃ kandaviśeṣo nāgarahetuḥ mūlakaṃ kandaviśeṣaḥ prasiddhaḥ ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭairetaiḥ kalāṃśaiḥ ṣoḍaśāṃśaiḥ ṣoḍaśāṃśaḥ pratyekaṃ saṃyujyate ।।
sarvasaṃmatamidaṃ vyākhyānām ।।
atra viśeṣaḥ kāñjike sarvadhānyāmlasaṃdhānaṃ tuṣavarjye tu kārayet ।।
uragā triphalā krāntā laghuparṇī śatāvarī ।।
tena yuktaṃ rasasvinnaṃ tridinaṃ mṛduvahninā ।।
dolāyantreṇa tīvreṇa mardayitvā punaḥ punaḥ ।
iti rasendramaṅgalāt ।।
kṣārāmlair oṣadhair vāpi dolāyantre sthitasya hi ।
pācanaṃ svedanākhyaṃ syānmalaśaithilyakārakam ।
iti paribhāṣā ।।
dravadravyeṇa bhāṇḍasya pūritārdhodarasya ca ।
mukhasyobhayato dvāradvayaṃ kṛtvā prayatnataḥ ।।
tasyopari kṣipeddaṇḍaṃ tanmadhye rasapoṭalīm ।
baddhvā tu svedayedetaddolāyantramiti smṛtam ।
iti dolāyantralakṣaṇam ।।
