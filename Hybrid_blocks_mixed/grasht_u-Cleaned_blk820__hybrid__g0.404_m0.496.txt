vedhavidhānamāha rasetyādi ।।
rasaḥ sūtaḥ daradaṃ hiṅgulaṃ tāpyaṃ mākṣikaṃ gandhakaḥ pratītaḥ manaḥśilā manohvā rājavarttakaṃ rājāvartaṃ vimalaṃ raupyamākṣikaṃ ekavadbhāvadvandvaḥ ।।
puṭamṛtaśulbaṃ rasādīnāṃ puṭena mṛtamityarthaḥ ।।
evaṃvidhaṃ śulbaṃ tāre nirvāhitaṃ iyaṃ hemakṛṣṭiḥ svarṇakaraṇamityarthaḥ ।।
