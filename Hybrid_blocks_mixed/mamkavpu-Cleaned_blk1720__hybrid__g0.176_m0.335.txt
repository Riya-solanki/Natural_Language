liṅgavacanabhedo api upamānopameyayoḥ sādhāraṇaṃ cet dharmamanyarūpaṃ kuryāt । tadā ekatarasyaiva taddharmasamanvayāvagateḥ saviśeṣaṇasyaiva tasyopamānatvamupameyatvaṃ vā pratīyamānena dharmeṇa
pratīyate iti prakāntasyārthasya sphuṭamanirvāhādasya bhagnaprakramarūpatvam । yathā
