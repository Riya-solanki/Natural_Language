saṃsṛṣṭeṣu hi doṣeṣu sarvathā chardanaṃ hitam ।
tatra doṣo atra gamanaṃ śivāstra iva lakṣyate ।।
upadravāśca vikṛtiṃ phalatasteṣu sādhitam ।।
iti śrīgāruḍe mahāpurāṇe pūrvakhaṇḍe prathamāṃśākhye ācārakāṇḍe raktapittanidānaṃ nāmāṣṭacatvāriṃśaduttaraśatatamo adhyāyaḥ
