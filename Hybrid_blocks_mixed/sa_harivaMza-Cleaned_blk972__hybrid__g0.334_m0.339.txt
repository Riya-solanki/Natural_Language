taṃsurogho apratirathaḥ subāhuś caiva dhārmikaḥ 
gaurī kanyā cavikhyātā māndhātṛjananī śubhā । 
sarve vedavratasnātā brahmaṇyāḥ satyavādinaḥ ।। 
sarvekṛtāstrā balinaḥ sarve yuddhaviśāradāḥ । putro apratirathasyāsīt kaṇvaḥ samabhavan nṛpaḥ । medhātithiḥ sutas tasya yasmāt kāṇvo abhavad dvijaḥ । 
ilā nāma tu yasyāsīt kanyā vai janamejaya 
brahmavādiny adhitrī ca taṃsus tām adhyagacchata ।।
