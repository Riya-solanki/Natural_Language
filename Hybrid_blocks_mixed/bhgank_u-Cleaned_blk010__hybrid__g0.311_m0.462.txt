gurujanaguhādeśaḥ śmaśānaṃ rudra eva ca ।। 
[
ārṣatvād vā yathāśrute apy adoṣaḥ ।। 
guruḥ pūrvoktas tadājñayāvatiṣṭhamānas tadāśrito bhavati yathā grāmaṇyam āśrito grāma iti ।। 
tatrādidharmāvasthasya tāvadāyatane vāsa ityatrāyatanaśabdo gurāv eva draṣṭavyo liṅgakartetyādijñāpakādupacārād vā pañcaśabdavat ।। 
yadyapyāyatanasūtre pratiṣedhaḥ śrūyate tathāpi deśaprastāve gururabhipreta evācāryo loka ityādi jñāpakābhyanujñānāt ।। 
ata eva gūḍhavratopadeśādāyatane vāsa ityatrāyatanaśabdo jane vivakṣito maryādayāyatanāditi kṛtvā baddhasya rudrasya hi śivāyatanavāsānupapatteḥ ।। 
yadāpi vāsastadāpi janādhīna evetyato dvitīyāvasthasya jana eva deśaḥ ।। 
janaśabdenātra dharmādharmajananādhikṛtā varṇāśramino abhidhīyanta iti ।। 
tṛtīyāvasthasya guhā deśaḥ ।। 
guhāgrahaṇaṃ sūtrārthopalakṣaṇārtham ataḥ śūnyāgāraguhayoranyataraṃ vyāsaṅgādidoṣavarjitaṃ yatprāpyate tatra vastavyam ।। 
deśagrahaṇaṃ tatrāvasthānamātreṇa guhāyā deśatvajñāpanārthaṃ na tu gurujanavat tadāyattatveneti ।। 
caturthāvasthasya śmaśānadeśaḥ ।। 
śmaśānaṃ ca prasiddhameva grāhyam ।। 
tasya ca na guhāvaddeśatvaṃ kiṃ tv ā dehapātāt tatraivānirgacchatā stheyam ityayaṃ viśeṣaḥ ।। 
pañcamāvasthasya deśo rudraḥ ।। 
rudro bhagavānmaheśvaraḥ ।। 
prāgapi rudrāyattatvāt sādhakasya rudro'styeva deśastathāpi prāganyavyapadeśo apyasti sāmprataṃ punaḥ śarīrādirahitasya sarvadeśavikalatvād avadhāraṇaṃ karoti rudra eveti ।। 
caśabdo nyūnādhikavyavacchedaka iti ।। 
atha kimetaddeśāvasthitimātreṇaivaite lābhāḥ prāpyante kalpavṛkṣadeśāvasthitivat ।। 
ucyate ।। 
na ।। 
kiṃ tarhi deśabhedavadupāyabhedo apyasti ।। 
yatas tatpratipādanārtham āha ।। ]
