anyaccāha ripunihatetyādi ।।
ripunihatalohaṣaṭkaṃ ripubhir arivargair nihataṃ māritaṃ yat lohaṣaṭkaṃ svarṇatāratāmranāgavaṅgalohābhidhānaṃ tat caturmāsaṃ yathā syāttathā dhānyasthito'nnakoṣṭhīdhṛto raso jīrṇaḥ ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭo dhānyasthitaḥ purasurābhyāṃ guggulumadirābhyāṃ sahito militaḥ śatāṃśena ghoṣaṃ kāṃsyaṃ vidhyati kanakaṃ karotītyarthaḥ ।।
