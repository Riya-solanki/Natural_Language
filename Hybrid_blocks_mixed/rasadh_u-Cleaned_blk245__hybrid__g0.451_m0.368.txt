śodhitapāradasya catvāro vallāḥ gandhakavāriṇā ghaṭīdvayaṃ yadi kṣipyante tadā bhasmībhavanti ।।
tataḥ śuddharūpyasya patrāṇi tena bhasmanā liptvā vajramūṣāyāṃ tāni patrāṇi vaṅkalidhamanyā gāḍhaṃ dhmātvā gālayet ।।
tataḥ kṛṣṇaṃ rūpyaṃ bhavati ।।
tato'sya kṛṣṇarūpyasya gadyāṇāḥ sarvottamanavakahemagadyāṇāḥ ubhayaṃ viṃśatigadyāṇakā gālyante pañcadaśavarṇikaṃ hema bhavati ।।
tathā śuddharasaṃ karpare kṣiptvādho mṛdvagniṃ jvālayet ।।
upari gandhakavāri bindunā bindunā kṣipet ।।
evamekaviṃśatidināni nirantaraṃ gandhakavāriṇā siktaḥ susūto niṣpadyate baddhaḥ ṣoṭaśca bhavati ।।
tataḥ pātālagaruḍasya patrāṇi vartayitvā tataḥ piṇḍīṃ kṛtvā piṇḍimadhye ṣoṭaṃ ca kṣiptvā bhūmau kukkuṭasaṃjñaṃ puṭaṃ deyam ।।
paraṃ nirdhūmairjvaladbhiraṃgārais tathā ca kṛte sa ṣoṭaḥ pīto bhavati tataḥ śuddharūpyasya catuḥṣaṣṭigadyāṇān gālayitvā ṣoṭagadyāṇako madhye kṣipyate sarvottamaṃ hema bhavati yata eṣā gandhakadrutipīṭhī catuḥṣaṣṭipravedhikā vartate ।।
iti gandhakadrutipīṭhīkarmāṇi ।।
atha tālakaśodhanam ।।
