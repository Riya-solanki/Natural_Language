yaḥ padārthaḥ kenacidākāreṇa niyataḥ, yadā kadācit anubhūto abhūt sa kālāntare smṛtipratibodhādhāyini tatsamāne vastuni dṛṣṭe sati yat tathaiva smaryate tat bhavet smaraṇam ।
udāharaṇam nimnanābhikuhareṣu yadambhaḥ plāvitaṃ caladṛśāṃ laharībhiḥ ।
tadbhavaiḥ kuharutaiḥ suranāryaḥ smāritāḥ suratakaṇṭharutānām ।।
yathā vā karajuagahiajasoātthaṇamuhaviṇivesiāharapuḍassa ।
samariapañcajaṇṇassa ṇamaha kaṇhassa romāñcaṃ ।।
