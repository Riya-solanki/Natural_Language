aniruddho nāmāhaṅkāraḥ ।
teṣāṃ vāsudevaḥ parā parakṛtiritare saṃkarṣaṇādayaḥ kāryam ।
tamitthaṃbhūtaṃ parameśvaraṃ bhagavantamabhigamanopādānejyāsvādhyāyayogairvarṣaśatamiṣṭvā kṣīṇakleśo bhagavantameva pratipadyata iti ।
tatra yattāvaducyeta yo asau nārāyaṇaḥ paro avyaktātprasiddhaḥ paramātmā sarvātmā sa ātmānātmānamanekadhā vyūhyāvasthita iti, tanna nirākriyate, asa ekadhā bhavati tridhā bhavati' ityādiśrutibhyaḥ paramātmano anekadhābhāvasyādhigatatvāt ।
yadapi tasya bhagavato abhigamanādilakṣaṇamārādhanamajasramananyacittatayābhipreyate,
