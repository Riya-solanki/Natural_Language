rasabandhane adhikāritvaṃ darśayannāha pakṣacchedam ityādi ।।
yo vādī abhrasattvagrasanena vinā pakṣacchedam kṛtvā pakṣāpakartanamavidhāya rasabandhaṃ kartuṃ pāradabandhanaṃ vidhātum īhate ceṣṭate sa vādī na kiṃtu jaḍa evam apaṇḍita iti bhāvaḥ ।।
kaḥ kṛtvā rasabandhanaṃ kartumīhate bījaiḥ raktābhrahemarasakair ityādibhiḥ ।।
evāvyayam anyadravyavyavacchedī ।।
pakṣacchedanaṃ kṛtvā bandhanaṃ kāryamayaṃ vidhiḥ anyathā tv avidhiḥ ।।
avidher dṛṣṭāntamāha yathā ajitendriyo lampaṭaḥ pumān mokṣaṃ vāñchati ।।
ajitendriyajaḍayoḥ sāmyamiti ।।
