yo ayaṃ nirdayadantakhaṇḍanakaro buddhiprayogair haṭhān nīrandhro bhujayantrapīḍanavidhiḥ sollāsam utpāditaḥ ।
tenekṣor iva kāmukasya sarasasyādāya sāraṃ paraṃ veśyābhiḥ kriyate bahir yadi paraṃ niṣkāsanaṃ śalkavat ।।
