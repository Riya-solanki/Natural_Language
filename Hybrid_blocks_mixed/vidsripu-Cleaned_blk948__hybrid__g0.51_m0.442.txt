śṛṅgāre sūtradhāraḥ kusumaśaramuner āśrame brahmacārī nārīṇām ādidevas tribhuvanamahito rāgarājye purodhāḥ ।
jyotsnāsatraṃ dadhānaḥ puramathanajaṭājūṭakoṭīśayālur devaḥ kṣīrodajanmā jayati kumudinīkāmukaḥ śvetabhānuḥ ।।
