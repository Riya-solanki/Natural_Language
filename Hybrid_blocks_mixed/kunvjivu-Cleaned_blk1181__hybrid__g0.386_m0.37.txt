atra śāpa iti evaṃvidhāpacāraprāgbhāraprabhavo bhavatu nāma ।
sānugraha iti anugrahaḥ punarayamanupapanna evāsyāmavasthāyām ।
bhagavatetyanarthadarśanena sahajadayālunā ।
yadi vā bhagavatā śāpo api iti śāpānugrahayordahanavāriṇorivaikakālamekaviṣayavartitvamasatorapi bhagavat saṃpatsāmarthyādeva saṃbhāvyate, adṛṣṭatanayānanapadmaśobhe mayi etasmādevānugrahādavaśyaṃbhāvinaḥ sucirakālābhikāṅkṣitasya sutāvalambanatanorjovitaphalasya vilokanotkaṇṭhāpāravaśyāt ityalamatiprasaṅgena ।
asyā eva prabhedāntaramunmīlayati
