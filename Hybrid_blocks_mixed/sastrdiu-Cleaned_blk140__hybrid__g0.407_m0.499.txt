apare tu svalakṣaṇamātragocaraṃ nirvikalpakamicchanti ।
tadapi pratītiviruddham ।
pratīyate hi saṃmugdhākāraṃ vastu sahasaiva yatpañcājjātidravyaguṇakriyānāmabhiḥ pañcadhā savikalpakena vikalpyate gaurayam, daṇḍayam, śaklo ayam,gacchatyayam, ḍittho ayamiti ।
tatra nirvikalpakamanekākāraṃ vastu saṃmugdhaṃ gṛhṇāti, savikalpaṃ tvekaikākāraṃ jātyādikaṃ vivicya viṣayīkaroti ।
