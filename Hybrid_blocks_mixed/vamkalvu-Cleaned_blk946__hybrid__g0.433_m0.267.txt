astyayaṃ labhiryaḥ prāptyupasarjanāṃ gatimāha asti ca gatyupasarjanāṃ prāptimāheti । atra pūrvasminpakṣe gatyarthatvāllabherṇicyaṇau yaḥ kartā tasya gatyādisūtreṇa karmasaṃjñā । yathā
