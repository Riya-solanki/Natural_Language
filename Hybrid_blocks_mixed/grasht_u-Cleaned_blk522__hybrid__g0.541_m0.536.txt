vaikrāntasatvayogamāha rasetyādi ।।
tadrasavaikrāntakaṃ sattvaṃ hemnā samaṃ svarṇena samabhāgaṃ dvandvānvitaṃ sat dvandvamelāpakauṣadhasahitaṃ sat evamamunā vidhānena milati rase iti śeṣaḥ ।।
punastena satvena saha ghanasatvamabhrasāraṃ nirvyūḍhaṃ nirvāhitaṃ sat tenobhayasatvasaṃyogena rasaḥ sūto bandhamupayāti bandhanamāpnoti ।।
