atha yo aśvādbhedo gavādiṣvanusyūtaḥ so asyālambanamityucyate ।
ko ayaṃ bhedo nāma, yadītaretarābhāvaḥ samāśritastarhyabhāvaḥ ।
atha pṛthaktvaṃ nāma guṇaviśeṣo bheda ityucyate, tatastasya dravyagatatvādguṇeṣu rūpādiṣu araso na bhavati rūpaṃ' arūpaṃ na bhavati rasaḥ' itītaretarābhāvapratyayo na syāt ।।
