catuṣpāde lohaṣairaleṣāparasūtvaṃ tasmāccatuḥṣaṣṭiguṇaṃ jīrṇamanaḥśilāsattvaṃ pāradaṃ ca kṣiptvā thūthāviḍena saṃpiṣann adho mṛduvahnir jvālayet ।। 
atratane ca khāparasattve jīrṇe punaḥ bhāgena tadeva kṣepyam ।। 
evaṃ punaḥ punaḥ kṣiptvā rasādaṣṭaguṇaṃ khāparasattvaṃ jāraṇīyam ।। 
tataḥ pāradaḥ sasneho vā saṃgratyūrdhvam annapathahīrakaṃ jīryati ।। 
iti jīrṇaśilāsattvasya tasya khāparasattvajāraṇaṃ saptamam ।। 
tridhānnapathavakrāṇāṃ madhyād ekaṃ ca hīrakam ।
yena sūtena saṃjīrṇaṃ sattvaṃ puṣpākṣasambhavam ।।
prakṣipya lohasattve tau catuṣpāda ubhāv api ।
thūthāviḍena saṃpiṣyan mṛdvagniṃ jvālayedadhaḥ ।।
jvālyo agnistāvatā yāvaj jīryate sa ca hīrakaḥ ।
hīrake annapathe jīrṇe sarvavyāpī bhavedrasaḥ ।।
