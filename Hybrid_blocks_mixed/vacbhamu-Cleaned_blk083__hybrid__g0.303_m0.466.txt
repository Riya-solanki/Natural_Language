syādetat ।
bhavatu pṛthagjanānāmevam; āgamopapattipratipannapratyagātmatattvānāṃ vyutpannānāmapi puṃsāṃ pramāṇaprameyavyavahārā dṛśyanta iti kathamavidyāvadviṣayāṇyeva pramāṇānītyata āha- paśvādibhiścāviśeṣāt iti ।
vidantu nāma āgamopapattibhyāṃ dehendriyādibhyo bhinnaṃ pratyagātmānam; pramāṇaprameyavyavahāre tu prāṇabhṛnmātradharmatvaṃ nātivartante ।
yādṛśo hi paśuśakuntādīnāmavipratipannamugdhabhāvānāṃ vyavahārastādṛśo vyutpannānāmapi puṃsāṃ dṛśyate ।
tena tatsāmānyātteṣāmapi vyavahārasamaye avidyāvattvamanumeyam ।
caśabdaḥ samuccaye ।
uktaśaṅkānivartanasahitā pūrvoktopapattiḥ avidyāvatpuruṣaviṣayatvaṃ pramāṇānāṃ sādhayatītyarthaḥ ।
etadeva vibhajate- yathā hi paśvādaya iti ।
