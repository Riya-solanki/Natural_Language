rūpaskandhaḥ soniddeśaḥ pariniṣṭhitaḥ । atha vedanā salakṣaṇaprabhedā vedanā katamā । trividho anubhavaḥ । sukhaḥ duḥkhaḥ aduḥkhāsukhaśca ।।
sukho yasmin niruddhe saṃyogecchā jāyate ।।duḥkho yasminnutpanne viyogecchā jāyate ।।aduḥkhāsukho yasminnutpanne nobhayecchā jāyane ।।
vedanaskandhaḥ sonideśanirdeśaḥ parinisṭhataḥ । atha saṃjñā lakṣaṇa prabhedā saṃjñā katamā । viṣayanimittod grahaṇam ।।
sā tridhā parittā mahadgatā apramāṇā ca ।।
saṃjñā skandhaḥ soddeśanirdeśaḥ parinisṭhitaḥ ।
