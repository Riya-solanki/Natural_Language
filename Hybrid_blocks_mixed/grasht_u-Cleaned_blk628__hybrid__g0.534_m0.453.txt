taccāha mākṣīketyādi ।।
mākṣīkakāntaśulbaṃ mākṣīkaṃ tāpyaṃ kāntaṃ cumbakaṃ śulbaṃ tāmraṃ etadapi mahābījaṃ jñeyam ।।
punaḥ śulbābhrakamākṣīkaṃ punaḥ kāntābhrakamākṣīkaṃ kāntaṃ kāntapāṣāṇaṃ abhrakaṃ gaganaṃ mākṣīkaṃ tāpyaṃ tathā tāpyakaśulbābhrakaṃ etadapi ca mahābījaṃ jñeyamiti ।।
