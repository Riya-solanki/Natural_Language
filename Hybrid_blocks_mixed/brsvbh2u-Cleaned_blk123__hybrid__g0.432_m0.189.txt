rūpādimattvācca viparyayo darśanāt ।
yat kila bhūtabhautikānāṃ mūlakāraṇaṃ tadrūpādimān paramāṇurnitya iti bhavadbhirabhyupeyate, tasya cedrūpādimattvamabhyupeyeta paramāṇutvanityatvaviruddhe sthaulyānityatve prasajyeyātāṃ, so ayaṃ prasaṅga
