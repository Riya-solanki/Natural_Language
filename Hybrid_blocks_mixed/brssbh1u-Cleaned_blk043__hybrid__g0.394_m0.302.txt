nanviha jijñāsyavailakṣaṇyamuktam- karmakāṇḍe bhavyo dharmo jijñāsya iha tu bhūtaṃ nityanivṛttaṃ brahma jijñāsyamiti ।
tatra dharmajñānaphalādanuṣṭhānāpekṣādvilakṣaṇaṃ brahmajñānaphalaṃ bhavitumarhati ।
nārhatyevaṃ bhavitum ।
kāryavidhiprayukttasyaiva brahmaṇaḥ pratipādyamānatvāt ।
aātmā vā are draṣṭavyaḥ' iti ।
aya ātmāpahatapāpmā-so anvevyaḥ sa vijijñāsitavyaḥ' aātmetyovopāsīta' aātmānameva lokamupāsata' ।
abrahma veda brahmaiva bhavati' ityādividhāneṣu satsu ko asāvātmā kiṃ tadbrahma ityākāṅkṣāyāṃ tatsvarūpasamarpaṇena sarve vedāntā upayuktāḥ- anityaḥ sarvajñaḥ sarvagato nityatṛpto nityāśuddhabuddhamuktasvabhāvo vijñānamānandaṃ brahma ityevamādayaḥ ।
tadupāsanācca śāstradṛṣṭo api mokṣaḥ phalaṃ bhaviṣyatīti ।
kartavyavidhyananupraveśe vastumātrakathane hānopādānasaṃbhavāt, saptadvīpā vasumatī, rājāsau gacchatītyādivākyavadvedāntavākyānāmānarthakyameva syāt ।
