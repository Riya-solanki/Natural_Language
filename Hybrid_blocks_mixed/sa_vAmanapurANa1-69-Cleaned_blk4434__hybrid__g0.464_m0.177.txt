tamarcya vidhinā brahman pituḥ pitaramīśvaram । kṛtāñjalipuṭo bhūtvā idaṃ vacanamabravīt । saṃsmṛto asi mayā tāta suviṣaṇṇena cetasā tanme hitaṃ ca pathyaṃ ca śreyogryaṃ vada tāta me । 
kiṃ kāryaṃ tāta saṃsāre vasatā puruṣoṇa hi ।
kṛtena yena vai nāsya bandhaḥ samupajāyate ।
saṃsārārṇavamagnānāṃ narāṇāmalpacetasām ।
taraṇe yo bhavet potastanme vyākhyātumarhasi ।।
