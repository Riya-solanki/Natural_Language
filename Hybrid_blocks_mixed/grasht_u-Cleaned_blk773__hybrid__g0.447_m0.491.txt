sāraṇayā pāradaguṇānāha tasmādityādi ।।
tasmāddhetoḥ sūto dravyavidhāyī syāt iti śeṣaḥ dravyakartetyartha ।।
alaghunā bījena analpaparimāṇena mahābījena sāritaḥ san samasāritaḥ san baddho bhavet ityadhyāhāraḥ ।।
mūṣāyāṃ samāvarto dravaṇaṃ syāt vahnidhamanāt iti śeṣaḥ ।।
