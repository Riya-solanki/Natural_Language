atha pathyānāha śālerityādi ।।
śāleḥ ṣaṣṭikādeḥ piṣṭodbhavabhojanaṃ piṣṭakotpannaṃ ca tadbhojanaṃ ceti ।।
ājyaṃ ghṛtam ।।
kaiḥ saha mudgamāṃsarasaiḥ saha mudgāḥ pratītāḥ atra viśeṣāt māṃsāni bhojyāni gokṣīraṃ ca bhojyaṃ punarmastu gorasasaṃbhavaṃ viśeṣāt bhojyam ।।
pāne akṣāraṃ jalaṃ miṣṭajalaṃ prayojyaṃ punaḥ yāni kāni miṣṭāni dravyāṇi atrānuktatamāni praśastāni śreṣṭhāni punaḥ caturjātakakarpūrāmodamuditamukhaṃ yathā syāt tathā dravyaṃ peyaṃ pātavyaṃ caturjātakaṃ tvakpatrailānāgakesaraṃ karpūraṃ ghanasāraṃ eṣām āmodena parimalena muditaṃ yanmukhaṃ vāsitamukham ityarthaḥ ।।
yuvatyā jalpanaṃ kāryaṃ yuvatyā cāṅgamardanam ।
tasyāḥ sparśanamātreṇa dehe kramati sūtakaḥ ।
iti viśeṣo granthāntarāt ।।
