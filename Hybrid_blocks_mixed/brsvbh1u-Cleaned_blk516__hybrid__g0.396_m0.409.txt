ityanayorapi sūtrayorbhāṣye ānandamayasthāne abrahma pucchaṃ pratiṣṭhā'iti pāṭho draṣṭavyaḥ ।
vikārasyānandamayasya brahma pucchamavayavaścetkathaṃ sarvasyāsya vikārajātasya sānandamayasya brahma pucchaṃ kāraṇamucyeta aidaṃ sarvamasṛjata ।
yadidaṃ kiñca'iti śrutyā ।
nahyānandamayavikārāvayavo brahma vikāraḥ san sarvasya kāraṇamupapadyate ।
tasmādānandamayavikārāvayavo brahmeti tadavayavayogyānandamayo vikāra iha nopāsyatvena vivakṣitaḥ, kintu svapradhānamiha brahma pucchaṃ jñeyatveneti siddham ।।
