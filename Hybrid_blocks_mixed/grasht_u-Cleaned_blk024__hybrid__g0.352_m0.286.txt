tāvaditi sākalye yāvattāvad ityetau sākalyāvadhimānavadhāraṇeṣv iti prasādataḥ ।।
idaṃ sukṛtaphalaṃ suvihitakarmaphalam idaṃ kiyat sukṛte śubhānvaye janma svatantrā dhīḥ svādhīnabuddhir ityarthaḥ ।।
apīti niścayena ।।
sā buddhiḥ sakalamahītalatulanaphalā sakalasya niravaśeṣasya mahītalasya tulanaṃ phalaṃ yasyāḥ sā tathoktā ।।
tulanam iti tulayā svatantrabuddhirūpayā sakalamahītalasya tulanaṃ bhavatyeveti yuktaṃ kimākārā kiyanmānā kaiḥ śritā kair dhṛtā ca bhūr iti jyotiṣasiddhāntavidhānād adṛṣṭāntā bhūr buddhyopalakṣyate ।।
punaḥ sukulajanmasvatantrabuddhibhyāṃ bhūtalaṃ suvidheyaṃ pūjyaṃ jñātavyam ।।
ekadeśagrahaṇāt sarvaṃ grāhyaṃ mahāntastokā bhūtalaṃ bahu īdṛśā mahānto yatra tiṣṭhanti tatsthānaṃ pūjyaṃ vettumaśakyatvāt sarvamiti ।।
sukulajanmasambandho vyākhyāyate bhūtaletyādi ।।
bhūtalavidheyatāyāḥ bhūtale pṛthivīmaṇḍale yā vidheyatā sarvakarmapravīṇatā tasyā arthāḥ kāryāṇi kathaṃbhūtāḥ vividhabhogaphalāḥ vividhāśca te bhogāśca vividhabhogāḥ nānābhogāḥ phalaṃ yeṣāṃ te tathoktāḥ ।।
te phalaṃ phalarūpāḥ ।।
bhogāḥ śarīre santi bhavanti ।।
kutaḥ yato vedāntasūtraṃ prārabdhakarmaphalabhogāyatanaṃ śarīram iti ।।
ata eva bhogānām āśrayāḥ śarīram ।।
aho iti kaṣṭe āścarye vā ।।
etaccharīraṃ tu sarvotkṛṣṭamiti tātparyārthaḥ ।।
sā muktiḥ piṇḍapātane iti vacanāt ।।
