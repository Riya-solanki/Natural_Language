abhrasatvavidhānamāha bahalamityādi ।।
vaṭakīkṛtaṃ ca tanmṛtaṃ ca gaganaṃ tathoktaṃ na vaṭako vaṭakaḥ kriyata iti vaṭakīkṛtaṃ atra abhūtatadbhāve cviḥpratyayaḥ ।।
pañcabhirniculapuṭaiḥ pañcasaṃkhyābhirvetasavṛkṣadravabhāvanābhir bhāvitaṃ yanmṛtagaganaṃ mṛtābhraṃ vaṭakīkṛtaṃ satsatvaṃ patati taddrāvakauṣadhayogaṃ vidhāya vahninā vidhamanāditi śeṣaḥ ।।
kiyanmānaṃ satvaṃ patati bahalaṃ bahu anyavidheradhikam ।।
kīdṛśaṃ svarṇavarṇaṃ pītaśvetaṃ prakāśākhyaṃ punarnirañjanaṃ nirmalaṃ kiṭṭarahitaṃ ca ।।
asmin satve kācakiṭṭābhāvaḥ ।।
sarvotkṛṣṭavidhirayaṃ pūrvamuditāt bahalasatvapātādityarthaḥ ।।
kevalasatvacāraṇavidhānamāha tadityādi ।।
tatastadabhrasatvapātanavidheranantaraṃ tadevābhrasatvaṃ cūrṇīkṛtya kalkaṃ vidhāya kṣārāmlairbhāvitaṃ kuryāt kṣārāḥ svarjikādayaḥ amlā jambīrādayaḥ tairbahuśo bahuvāraṃ gharmapuṭitaṃ kuryādityarthaḥ ।।
punaḥ sṛṣṭitrayanīrakaṇātumbururasamarditaṃ sṛṣṭiḥ pūrvoktā tattrayaṃ mūtraśukraśoṇitamiti nīrakaṇā jalapippalī tumbaru pratītaṃ eteṣāṃ rasena marditaṃ kuryāt ।।
abhrasattvamevaṃ kṛtaṃ sat carati grasati rasaḥ ityadhyāhāraḥ ।।
