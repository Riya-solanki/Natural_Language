dhātvarthas tāvat prakṛtyaiva sādhyātmakaḥ ।
viśeṣatas tv ākhyātagatena dhātunocyamānaḥ sa yadi phalasādhanatvena codyate tato asiddharūpatvāt tasya sādhanākāṅkṣāyāṃ satyāṃ nāmapadaṃ guṇavidhitvena nāmadheyatvena vobhayathāpi dṛṣṭenaiva tādarthyaṃ labhate ।
nanu nāmadheyapakṣe nāmapadam api sādhyārthaṃ bhaviṣyatīti dhātutulyaṃ syāt ।
na ।
tasya phalasaṃbandhe guṇavidhitvena siddhārthatvāt ।
api ca
