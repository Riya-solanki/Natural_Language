satyam ।
śarīre bhavati natu śarīra eva bhavati, ajyāyānpṛthivyā jyāyānantarikṣāt', aākāśavatsarvagataśca nityaḥ' iti ca vyāpitatvaśravaṇāt ।
jīvastu śarīra eva bhavati, tasya bhogādhiṣṭhānāccharīrādanyatra vṛttyabhāvāt ।।
