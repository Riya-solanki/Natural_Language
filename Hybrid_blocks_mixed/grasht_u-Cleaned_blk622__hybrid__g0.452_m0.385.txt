kāntetyādi ।।
mākṣikeṇa mṛtaṃ yannāgaṃ tālaṃ haritālaṃ śilā manohvā ca tattathā pūrvoktakalkasahitaṃ yatkalkaṃ rasoparasādīnāṃ tena sahitaṃ yuktaṃ kāntamukhaṃ yathā ।
abhāve'bhrakasattvasya kāntasattvaṃ pradāpayet ।
kāntasatvasya vābhāve tīkṣṇalohaṃ tu dāpayet ।
iti ।।
