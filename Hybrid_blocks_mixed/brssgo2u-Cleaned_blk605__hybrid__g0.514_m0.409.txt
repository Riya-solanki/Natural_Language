naiṣa doṣaḥ ।
atatteja aikṣata tā āpa aikṣanta' iti ca bhūtānāmapi cetanatvaśravaṇāditi ।
evaṃ prāpte abhidhīyate- sa eva parameśvarastena tenātmanāvatiṣṭhamāno abhidhyāyaṃstaṃ taṃ vikāraṃ sṛjatīti ।
kutaḥ ।
talliṅgāt ।
tathāhi śāstram- ayaḥ pṛthivyāṃ tiṣṭhan pṛthivyā antaro yaṃ pṛthivī na veda yasya pṛthivī śarīraṃ yaḥ pṛthivīmantaro yamayati' ityevañjātīyakaṃ sādhyakṣāṇameva bhūtānāṃ pravṛttiṃ darśayati ।
tathā so akāmayata bahu syāṃ prajāyeya iti prastutya asacca tyaccābhavat ।
tadātmānaṃ svayamakuruta' iti ca tasyaiva ca sarvātmabhāvaṃ darśayati ।
