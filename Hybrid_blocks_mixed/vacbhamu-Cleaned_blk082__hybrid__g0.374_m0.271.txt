yadi cidātmā tatra nādhyasyeta ? kathaṃ caiṣa cidātmakartṛko bhavet, yadyantaḥkaraṇaṃ vyāpāravat cidātmani nādhyasyet ? tasmāditaretarādhyāsāccidātmakartṛsthaṃ pramāphalaṃ sidhyati, tatsiddhau ca pramātṛtvam ।
tāmeva ca pramāmurarīkṛtya pramāṇasya pravṛttiḥ ।
pramātṛtvena ca pramā upalakṣyate ।
pramāyāḥ phalasyābhāve pramāṇaṃ na pravarteta ।
tathā ca pramāṇamapramāṇaṃ syādityarthaḥ ।
upasaṃharati- tasmādavidyāvadviṣayāṇyeva pratyakṣādīni pramāṇāni ।
