nanu tarhi prabandhāntarvartināṃ keṣāṃcinnīrasānāṃ padyānāṃ kāvyatvaṃ na syāditi cet ? na, rasavatpadyāntargatanīrasapadānāmiva padyarasena, prabandharaseneva teṣāṃ rasavattāṅgīkārāt ।
