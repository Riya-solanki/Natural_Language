[] atha hari-vāsara-divase surabhiḥ sura-rājam anu vṛndāvana-bhūpari-bhuvar-loka-bhāgam āgamya tad-avasaraṃ pratīkṣāmāsa । tatra ca dūrataḥ sura-patiṃ surabhir abhihitavatī-
nāsau meghaḥ paśya govardhanādrir
nāyaṃ navyas tasya bhāgaḥ sa kṛṣṇaḥ ।
neyaṃ vidyul-lola-pītāṃśuka-śrīr
naitan mandaṃ garjitaṃ narmavārtā ।। [śālinī]
