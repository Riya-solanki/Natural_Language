athāpi parapakṣapratiṣedhajñāpanaṃ prayojanaṃ bravīti, etadapi tādṛgeva।
yo jñāpayati, yo jānāti, yena jñāpayate, yacca jñāpyate-etacca pratipadyate cadi, tadā vaitaṇḍikatvaṃ jahāti।
atha na pratipadyate, parapakṣapratiṣedhajñāpanaṃ prayojanamityetadasya vākyamanarthakaṃ bhavati।
