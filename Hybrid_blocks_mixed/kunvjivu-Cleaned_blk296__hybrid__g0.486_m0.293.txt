sarvatraiva sāmānyalakṣaṇe vihite viśeṣalakṣaṇaṃ vidhātavyamiti kāvyasya "śabdārthau sahitau" ityādi sāmānyalakṣaṇaṃ vidhāya tadavayavabhūtayoḥ śabdārthayoḥ sāhityasya prathamonmeṣa eva viśeṣalakṣaṇaṃ vihitam ।
idānīṃ prathamoddiṣṭasya varṇavinyāsavakratvasya viśeṣalakṣaṇamupakramate
