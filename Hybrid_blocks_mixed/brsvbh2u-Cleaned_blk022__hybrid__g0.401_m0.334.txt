kāryakāraṇayorvailakṣaṇyaṃ tāvatsamānamevobhayoḥ pakṣayoḥ ।
prāgutpatterasatkāryavādaprasaṅgo apītau tadvatprasaṅgaśca pradhānopādānapakṣa eva nāsmatpakṣa iti yadyapyupariṣṭātpratipādayiṣyāmastathāpi guḍajihvikayā samānatvāpādanamidānīmiti mantavyam ।
