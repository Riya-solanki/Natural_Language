ityādi ।
tadevaṃ sattāmātreṇaiva parisphurataḥ padārthasya ko apyalaukikaḥ śobhātiśayavidhāyī vicchittiviśeṣo abhidhīyate, yena nūtanacchāyāmano hāriṇā vāstavasthititirodhānapravaṇena nijāvabhāsodbhāsitasvarūpeṇa tatkālollikhita iva varṇanīyapadārthaparispandamahimā pratibhāsate, yena vidhātṛvyapadeśapātratāṃ pratipadyante kavayaḥ ।
