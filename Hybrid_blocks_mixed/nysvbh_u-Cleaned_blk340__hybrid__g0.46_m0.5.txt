ekatvabuddhistasmiṃstaditipratyaya iti viśeṣahetuḥ mahaditi- pratyena sāmānādhikaraṇyāt ।
aekamidaṃ mahacca' ityekaviṣayau pratyayau samānādhikaraṇau bhavatastena vijñāyate yanmahat tadekamiti।
aṇusamūhātiśayagrahaṇaṃ mahatpratyaya iticet ? so ayam amahatsvaṇuṣu mahatpratyayo atasmiṃstaditipratyayo bhavatīti।
kiṃ cātaḥ ? atasmiṃstaditipratyayasya pradhānāpekṣitvāt pradhānasiddhiriti ubhavitavyaṃ mahatyeva mahatpratyayeneti।
