khalvayaṃ sarvaviprayogaḥ sarvoparamo apavargaḥ, bahu ca kṛcchraṃ ghora pāpakaṃ lupyata iti kathaṃ buddhimān sarvaduḥkhocchedaṃ sarvaduḥkhāsaṃvidamapavargaṃ na rocayediti।
tad yathā, madhuviṣasaṃpṛktamannamanādeyamiti।
evaṃ sukhaṃ duḥkhānuṣaktamanādeyamiti।।
