prasādaṃ te kariṣyāmi matprasādasamaṃ bhuvi 
yena sarvasya lokasya devi devī bhavīṣyasi ।। 
bhaviṣyanti mamāsrāṇi tathā bāhusthitāni te । śārṅgaśaṅkhagadācakra musalaṃ śūlam eva ca । 
saptamo devakīgarbho yo aṃśaḥ saumyo mamāgrajaḥ 
sa saṃkrāmayitavyas te saptame māsi rohiṇīm ।।
