sa deho'pyevaṃvidhaḥ tasmān na cetanaḥ ।। b
nanv aṅganādibhir bhogyatvamanaikāntikaṃ bhogyatve'pi tatrācaitanyābhāvāt ।। b
nānaikāntikaṃ tadīyasya dehasyaiva bhogyatvāt ।। b
