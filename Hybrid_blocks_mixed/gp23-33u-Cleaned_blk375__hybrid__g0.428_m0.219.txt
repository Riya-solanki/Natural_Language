[] atha sakhyāḥ-
satyaṃ jarati mad-ālyā vara-yuji gaṇḍe makara-vataṃsāṅkaḥ । na mayā likhitaḥ kutukān na mṛṣā-muṣyāḥ sapatnyā ca ।।[] atra mama sakhītvena tasyāś ca sapatnītvena virodha-lakṣaṇā-prāpter mayaiva likhita iti tayā dhvanitam । [] tad-anuvādinā kavinā tu tasya mrṣātvam iti ।
[] atha vraja eva vighna-nighnatayā proṣita-bhartṛkā varṇyate । [] tatra śrī-kṛṣṇasya bhāvanā-
rādhāṃ paśyasi jalpasi spṛśasi ca svātmann athāliṅgasi
svasmiṃs tat tad anukriyām api tayārabdhāṃ bhajan nandasi । viśrambhaṃ na hi manyase tadapi ced anyāṃ daśāṃ yāmitas
tat-prāptiṃ manuṣe tayā kathaya bho kiṃ te sukhaṃ setsyati ।। [] śrī-kṛṣṇaṃ prati sakhī-vacanam-
