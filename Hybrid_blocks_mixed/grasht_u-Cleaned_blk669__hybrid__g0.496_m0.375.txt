Comm Mugdhāvabodhinī
taccāha kiṭṭapurasaṃyogāt ।।
kiṭṭaṃ lohamalaṃ puro gugguluḥ tayoḥ saṃyogāt dhmātaiḥ mākṣikarasadaradarūpaiḥ pūrvoktaiḥ kiṭṭo bhavet punaḥ kiṭṭato rasasākaṃ sūtamiśritaṃ sattvaṃ sāraṃ nipatati tatsattvaṃ bhasma janayati utpādayati ।।
tasyāpi bhasmanaḥ sattvaṃ ca nipatatītyarthaḥ ।।
