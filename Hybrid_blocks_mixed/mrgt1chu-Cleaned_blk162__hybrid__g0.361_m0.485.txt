dharmārthakāmalakṣaṇena trivargeṇa pralobhya vamati adho nikṣipatīti vāmaḥ ।। 
svabhāvena rahasyo guhyo bhāvaḥ kathyate ।। 
itthaṃ pṛthag anayoḥ padayorarthaṃ pradarśya vigrahaṃ karoti vāmaṃ dhāmeti ।। 
diverdhātor dyutyarthatvād devaśabdena tejo'bhimatam ।। 
tena vāmam adhastane'dhvani vamanaśīlaṃ yattejas tadguhyam aprakaṭamasyeti vāmadevaguhyaḥ parameśvaraḥ ।।
