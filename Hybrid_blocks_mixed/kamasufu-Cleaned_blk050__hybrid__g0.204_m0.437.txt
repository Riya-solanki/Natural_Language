dvividhā tṛtīya-prakṛtiḥ strī-rūpiṇī puruṣa-rūpiṇī ca
tatra strī-rūpiṇī striyā veṣam ālāpaṃ līlāṃ bhāvaṃ mṛdutvaṃ bhīrutvaṃ mugdhatām asahiṣṇutāṃ vrīḍāṃ cānukurvīta
tasya vadane jaghana-karma tad aupariṣṭakam ācakṣate
sā tato ratim ābhimānikīṃ vṛttiṃ ca lipset
veśyāvac caritaṃ prakāśayet iti strī-rūpiṇī
puruṣa-rūpiṇī tu prachanna-kāmā puruṣaṃ lipsamānā saṃvāhaka-bhāvam upajīvet
saṃvāhane pariṣvajamāneva gātrair ūrū nāyakasya mṛdnīyāt
prasṛta-paricayā coru-mūlaṃ sajaghanam iti saṃspṛśet
tatra sthira-liṅgatām upalabhya cāsya pāṇi-manthena parighaṭṭayet cāpalam asya kutsayantīva haset
kṛta-lakṣaṇenāpy upalabdha-vaikṛtenāpi na codyata iti cet svayam upakramet
puruṣeṇa ca codyamānā vivadet kṛcchreṇa cābhyupagacchet
tatra karmāṣṭa-vidhaṃ samuccaya-prayojyam
[]nimitaṃ []pārśvato-daṣṭaṃ []bāhiḥ-saṃdaṃśo []antaḥ-saṃdaṃśaś []cumbitakaṃ []parimṛṣṭakam []āmra-cūṣitakaṃ []saṃgara iti
teṣv ekaikam abhyupagamya virāmābhīpsāṃ darśayet
itaraś ca pūrvasminn abhyupagate tad uttaram evāparaṃ nirdiśet tasminn api siddhe tad-uttaram iti
karāvalambitam oṣṭhayor upari vinyastam apavidhya mukhaṃ vidhunuyāt tan []nimitaṃ
hastenāgram avacchādya pārśvato nirdaśanam oṣṭhābhyām avapīḍya bhavatv etāvad iti sāntvayet tat []pārśvato-daṣṭam bhūyaś coditā saṃmīlitauṣṭhī tasyāgraṃ niṣpīḍya karṣayantīva cumbet iti [] bāhiḥ-saṃdaṃśaḥ
tasminn evābhyarthanayā kiṃ cid adhikaṃ praveśayet sāpi cāgram oṣṭhābhyāṃ niṣpīḍya niṣṭhīvet iti []antaḥ-saṃdaṃśaḥ
karāvalambitasyauṣṭhavad grahaṇaṃ []cumbitakam
tat kṛtvā jihvāgreṇa sarvato ghaṭṭanam agre ca vyadhanam iti []parimṛṣṭakam
tathābhūtam eva rāga-vaśād ardha-praviṣṭaṃ nirdayam avapīḍyāvapīḍya muñcet ity [] āmra-cūṣitakam
puruṣābhiprāyād eva giret pīḍayec cāparisamāpteḥ iti []saṃgaraḥ
yathārthaṃ cātra stanana-prahaṇanayoḥ prayogaḥ ity aupariṣṭakam
kulaṭāḥ svairiṇyaḥ paricārikāḥ saṃvāhikāś cāpy etat prayojayanti
tad etat tu na kāryam samaya-virodhād asabhyatvāc ca punar api hy āsāṃ vadana-saṃsarge svayam evārtiṃ prapadyeta ity ācāryāḥ
veśyā-kāmino ayam adoṣaḥ anyato api parihāryaḥ syāt iti vātsyāyanaḥ
tasmād yās tv aupariṣṭakam ācaranti na tābhiḥ saha saṃsṛjyante prācyāḥ
veśyābhir eva na saṃsṛjyante āhicchatrikāḥ saṃsṛṣṭā api mukha-karma tāsāṃ pariharanti
nirapekṣāḥ sāketāḥ saṃsṛjyante
na tu svayam aupariṣṭakam ācaranti nāgarakāḥ
sarvam aviśaṅkayā prayojayanti saurasenāḥ
evaṃ hy āhuḥ ṇ ko hi yoṣitāṃ ācāraṃ caritraṃ pratyayaṃ vacanaṃ vā śraddhātum arhati nisargād eva hi malina-dṛṣṭayo bhavanty etā na parityājyāḥ tasmād āsāṃ smṛtita eva śaucam anveṣṭavyam evaṃ hy āhuḥ
vatsaḥ prasravaṇe medhyaḥ śvā mṛga-grahaṇe śuciḥ śakuniḥ phala-pāte tu strī-mukhaṃ rati-saṃgame iti
śiṣṭa-vipratipatteḥ smṛti-vākyasya ca sāvakāśatvād deśa-sthiter ātmanaś ca vṛtti-pratyayānurūpaṃ pravarteta iti vātsyāyanaḥ
bhavanti cātra ślokāḥ pramṛṣṭa-kuṇḍalāś cāpi yuvānaḥ paricārakāḥ keṣāṃ cid eva kurvanti narāṇām aupariṣṭakam
tathā nāgarakāḥ ke cid anyonyasya hitaiṣiṇaḥ kurvanti rūḍha-viśvāsāḥ paraspara-parigraham
puruṣāś ca tathā strīṣu karmaitat kila kurvate vyāsas tasya ca vijñeyo mukha-cumbanavad vidhiḥ
parivartita-dehau tu strī-puṃsau yat paras-param yugapat saṃprayujyete sa kāmaḥ kākilaḥ smṛtaḥ
tasmād guṇavatas tyaktvā caturāṃs tyāgino narān veśyāḥ khaleṣu rajyante dāsa-hastipakādiṣu
na tv etad brāhmaṇo vidvān mantrī vā rāja-dhūrdharaḥ gṛhīta-pratyayo vāpi kārayed aupariṣṭakam
na śāstram astīty etāvat prayoge kāraṇaṃ bhavet śāstrārthān vyāpino vidyāt prayogāṃs tv eka-deśikān
rasa-vīrya-vipākā hi śva-māṃsasyāpi vaidyake kīrtitā iti tat kiṃ syād bhakṣaṇīyaṃ vicakṣaṇaiḥ
santy eva puruṣāḥ kecit santi deśās tathāvidhāḥ santi kālāś ca yeṣv ete yogā na syur nirarthakāḥ
tasmād deśaṃ ca kālaṃ ca prayogaṃ śāstram eva ca ātmānaṃ cāpi saṃprekṣya yogān yuñjīta vā na vā
arthasyāsya rahasyatvāc calatvān manasas tathā kaḥ kadā kiṃ kutaḥ kuryād iti ko jñātum arhati
