yadi ca vācyavācakatvavyatirekeṇa vyaṅgyavyañjakabhāvo nābhyupeyate tadāsādhutvādīnāṃ nityadoṣatvaṃ kaṣṭatvādīnāmanityadoṣatvamiti
vibhāgakaraṇamanupapannaṃ syāt । na cānupapannaṃ sarvasyaiva vibhaktatayā pratibhāsāt । vācyavācakabhāvavyatirekeṇa vyaṅgyavyañjakatāśrayaṇe tu vyaṅgyasya bahuvidhatvāt kvacideva kasyacidevaucityenopapadyata eva vibhāgavyavasthā ।।
{dvayaṃ gataṃ saṃprati śocanīyatāṃ samāgamaprārthanayā kapālinaḥ ।}
