adyābhogini gāḍhamarmanivahe harmāgravedījuṣāṃ sadyaś candanaśoṣiṇi stanataṭe saṅge kuraṅgīdṛśām ।
prāyaḥ praślathayanti puṣpadhanuṣaḥ puṣpākare niṣṭhite nirvedaṃ navamallikāsurabhayaḥ sāyṃnayā vāyavaḥ ।।
