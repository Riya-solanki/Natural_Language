dhṛteśca hetoḥ parameśvara evāyaṃ daharaḥ ।
katham ।
adaharo asminnantarākāśaḥ' iti hi prakṛtyākāśaupamyapūrvakaṃ tasminsarvasamādhānamuktvā tāṃ sminneva cātmaśabdaṃ prayujyāpahatapāpmatvādiguṇayogaṃ copaduśya tamevānativṛttaprakaraṇaṃ nirdiśati- aatha ya ātmā sa seturvidhṛtireṣalokānāmasaṃbhedāya' iti ।
tatra vidhṛtirityātmaśabdasāmānādhikaraṇyādvidhārayitocyate, kticaḥ kartari smaraṇāt ।
yathodakasaṃtānasya vidhārayitā loke setuḥ kṣetrasaṃpadāmasaṃbhedāya, evamayamātmātmaiṣāmadhyātmādibhedabhinnānāṃ lokānāṃ varṇāśramādīnāṃ ca vidhāritā seturasaṃbhedāyāsaṃkarāyeti ।
evamiha prakṛte dahare vidhāraṇalakṣaṇaṃ mahimānaṃ darśayati ।
ayaṃ ca mahimā parameśvara eva śrutyantarādupalabhyate aetasya vā akṣarasya praśāsane gārgi sūryācandramasau vidhṛtau tiṣṭhataḥ' ityādeḥ ।
tathānyatrāpi niścite parameśvaravākye śrūyate- aeṣa sarveśvara eṣa bhūtādhipatireṣa bhūtapāla eṣa seturvidhāraṇa eṣāṃ lokānāmasaṃbhedāya' iti ।
evaṃ dhṛteśca hetoḥ parameśvara evāyaṃ daharaḥ ।।
