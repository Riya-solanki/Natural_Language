ucyate- jñānasvarasādeva viṣayasya tathātvamadhyavasīyate, tadanyathātvaṃ tu kāraṇadoṣajñānādarthānyathātvajñānādvāvagandavyam ।
ato yatra prayatnenānviṣyamāṇo api kāraṇadoṣo bādhakajñānaṃ vā nopalabhyate tatpramāṇam, itaraccāpramāṇam ।
