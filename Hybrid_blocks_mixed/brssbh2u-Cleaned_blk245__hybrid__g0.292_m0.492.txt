na ।
dṛṣṭāntena vilakṣaṇārambhamātrasya vivakṣitatvāt ।
naca dravyasya dravyamevodāhartavyaṃ guṇasya vā guṇa eveti kaścinniyame heturasti ।
sūtrakāro api bhavatāṃ dravyasya guṇamudājahāra- apratyakṣāpratyakṣāṇāmapratyakṣatvātsaṃyogasya pañcātmakaṃ na vidyate' iti ।
yathā pratyakṣāpratyakṣayorbhūmyākāśayoḥ samavayansaṃyogo apratyakṣa evaṃ pratyakṣāpratyakṣeṣu pañcasu bhūteṣu samavayaccharīramapratyakṣaṃ syāt ।
pratyakṣaṃ hi śarīram ।
tasmānna pāñcabhautikamiti ।
etaduktaṃ bhavati- guṇaśca saṃyogo dravyaṃ śarīram ।
adṛśyate tu' iti cātrāpi vilakṣaṇotpattiḥ prapañcitā ।
