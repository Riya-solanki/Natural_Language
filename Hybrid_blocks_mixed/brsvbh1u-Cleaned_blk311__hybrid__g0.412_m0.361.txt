tasmātsarvamavadātam ।
śrutiśca ayato vā'iti janma darśayati, ayena jātāni jīvanti'iti jīvanaṃ sthitim, ayatprayanti'iti tatraiva layam ।
tasya ca nirṇayavākyam ।
atra ca pradhānādisaṃśaye nirṇayavākyam-ānandāddhyeveti ।
etaduktaṃ bhavati-yathā rajjvajñānasahitarajjūpādānā hi dhārā rajjvāṃ satyāmasti, rajjvāmeva ca līyate, evamavidyāsahitabrahmopādānaṃ jagat jāyate, brahmaṇyevāsti, tatraiva ca līyatā iti siddham ।।
