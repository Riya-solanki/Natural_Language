upanāhaḥ katamaḥ । vairānubandha ।।
mrakṣaḥ katamaḥ ātmano avaddhapracchādanā ।।pradāśaḥ katamaḥ caṇḍavacodāśitā ।।īrṣyā katamā parasaṃpattau cetaso vyāropa ।।
mātsarya katamat । dānavirodhī cetasa āgrahaḥ ।।
