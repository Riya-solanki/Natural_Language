upapadyate cātredṛśa eva saṃbandho nānyādṛśaḥ asvamapīto bhavati' iti hi svarūpasaṃbandhamenamāmananti svarūpasya cānapāyitvāt ।
na naranagaranyāyena saṃbandho ghaṭate ।
upādhikṛtasvarūpatirobhāvāttu - asvamapīto bhavati' ityupapadyate ।
tathā bhedo api nānyādṛśaḥ saṃbhavati ।
bahutaraśrutiprasiddhaikeśvaratvavirodhāt ।
tathāca śrutirekasyāpyākāśasya stānakṛtaṃ bhedavyapadeśamupapādayati - ayo ayaṃ bahirdhā puruṣādākāśaḥ', ayo ayamantarhṛdaya ākāśaḥ' iti ।।
