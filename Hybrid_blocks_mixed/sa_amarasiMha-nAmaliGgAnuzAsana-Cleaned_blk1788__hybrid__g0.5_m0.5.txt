। atha saṃkīrṇavargaḥ karma kriyā tatsātatye gamye syuraparasparāḥ sākalyāsaṃgavacane pārāyaṇaparāyaṇe yadṛcchā svairitā hetuśūnyā tvāsthā vilakṣaṇam śamathas tu śamaḥ śāntir dāntis tu damatho damaḥ avadānaṃ karma vṛttaṃ kāmyadānaṃ pravāraṇam vaśakriyā saṃvananaṃ mūlakarma tu kārmaṇam vidhūnanaṃ vidhuvanaṃ tarpaṇaṃ prīṇanāvanam paryāptiḥ syāt paritrāṇaṃ hastadhāraṇamityapi sevanaṃ sīvanaṃ syūtir vidaraḥ sphuṭanaṃ bhidā ākrośanamabhīṣaṅgaḥ saṃvedo vedanā na nā saṃmūrcchanamabhivyāptir yāñcā bhikṣārthanārdanā vardhanaṃ chedane atha dve ānandanasabhājane āpracchannamathāmnāyaḥ saṃpradāyaḥ kṣaye kṣiyā grahe grāho vaśaḥ kāntau rakṣṇas trāṇe raṇaḥ kaṇe vyadho vedhe pacā pāke havo hūtau varo vṛttau oṣaḥ ploṣe nayo nāye jyānir jīrṇau bhramo bhramau sphātir vṛddhau prathā khyātau spṛṣṭiḥ pṛktau snavaḥ srave edhā samṛddhau sphuraṇe sphuraṇā pramitau pramā prasūtiḥ prasave ścyote prādhāraḥ klamathaḥ klame utkarṣo atiśaye sandhiḥ śleṣe viṣaya āśraye kṣipāyāṃ kṣepaṇaṃ gīrṇir girau guraṇamudyame unnāya unnaye śrāyaḥ śrayaṇe jayane jayaḥ nigādo nigade mādo mada udvega udbhrame vimardanaṃ parimalo abhyupapattiranugrahaḥ nigrahas tadviruddhaḥ syādabhiyogas tvabhigrahaḥ muṣṭibandhas tu saṃgrāho ḍimbe ḍamaraviplavau bandhanaṃ prasitiś cāraḥ sparśaḥ spraṣṭopataptari nikāro viprakāraḥ syādākāras tviṅga iṅgitam pariṇāmo vikāre dve same vikṛtivikriye apahāras tvapacayaḥ samāhāraḥ samuccayaḥ pratyāhāra upādānaṃ vihāras tu parikramaḥ abhihāro abhigrahaṇaṃ nihāro abhyavakarṣaṇam anuhāro anukāraḥ syādarthasyāpagame vyayaḥ pravāhas tu pravṛttiḥ syāt pravaho gamanaṃ bahiḥ viyāmo viyamo yāmo yamaḥ saṃyāmasaṃyamau himsākarmābhicāraḥ syāj jāgaryā jāṃgarā dvayoḥ vighno antarāyaḥ pratyūhaḥ syādupaghno antikāśraye nirveśa upabhogaḥ syāt parisarpaḥ parikriyā vidhuraṃ tu praviśleṣe abhiprāyaś chanda āśayaḥ saṃkṣepaṇaṃ samasanaṃ paryavasthā virodhanam parisaryā parīsāraḥ syādāsyā tvāsanā sthitiḥ vistāro vigraho vyāsaḥ sa ca śabdasya vistaraḥ saṃvāhanaṃ mardanaṃ syād vināśaḥ syādadarśanam saṃstavaḥ syāt paricayaḥ prasaras tu visarpaṇam nīvākas tu prayāmaḥ syāt saṃnidhiḥ saṃnikarṣaṇam lavo abhilāṣo lavane niṣpāvaḥ pavane pavaḥ prastāvaḥ syādapasaras trasaraḥ sūtraveṣṭanam prajanaḥ syādupasaraḥ praśrayapraṇayau samau dhīśaktir niṣkramo astrī tu saṃkramo durgasaṃcaraḥ pratyutkramaḥ prayogārthaḥ prakramaḥ syādupakramaḥ syādabhyādānamuddhāta ārambhaḥ saṃbhramas tvarā pratibandhaḥ praviṣṭambho avanāyas tu nipātanam upalambhas tvanubhavaḥ samālambho vilepanam vipralambho viprayogo vilambhas tvatisarjanam viśrāvas tu pratikhyātiravekṣā pratijāgaraḥ nipāṭhanipaṭhau pāṭhe temastemau samundane ādīnavāsravau kleśe melake saṃgasaṃgamau saṃvīkṣanaṃ vicayanaṃ mārgaṇaṃ mṛgaṇā mṛgaḥ parirambhaḥ pariṣvaṅgaḥ saṃśleṣa upagūhanam nirvarṇanaṃ tu nidhyānaṃ darśanālokanekṣaṇam pratyākhyānaṃ nirasanaṃ pratyādeśo nirākṛtiḥ upaśāyo viśāyaś ca paryāyaśayanārthakau artanaṃ ca ṛtīyā ca hṛṇīyā ca ghṛṇārthakāḥ syād vyatyāso viparyāso vyatyayaś ca viparyaye paryayo atikramas tasminnatipāta upātyayaḥ preṣaṇaṃ yat samāhūya tatra syāt pratiśāsanam sa saṃstāvaḥ kratuṣu yā stutibhūmir dvijanmanām nidhāya takṣyate yatra kāṣṭhe kāṣṭhaṃ sa uddhanaḥ stambaghnas tu stambaghanaḥ stambo yena nihanyate āvidho vidhyate yena tatra viṣvaksame nighaḥ utkāraś ca nikāraś ca dvau dhānyotkṣepaṇārthakau nigārodgāravikṣāvodgrāhās tu garaṇādiṣu āratyavarativirataya uparāme athāstriyāṃ tu niṣṭhevaḥ niṣṭhayūtir niṣṭhevananiṣṭhīvanamityabhinnāni javane jūtiḥ sātis tvavasāne syādatha jvare jūrtiḥ udajas tu paśu preraṇamakaraṇirityādayaḥ śāpe gotrāntebhyas tasya vṛndamityaupagavakādikam āpūpikaṃ śāṣkulikamevamādyamacetasām māṇavānāṃ tu māṇavyaṃ sahāyānāṃ sahāyatā halyā halānāṃ brāhmaṇyavāḍavye tu dvijanmanām dve parśukānāṃ pṛṣṭhānāṃ pārśvaṃ pṛṣṭhyamanukramāt khalānāṃ khalinī khalyāpyatha mānuṣyakaṃ nṛṇām grāmatā janatā dhūmyā pāśyā galyā pṛthakpṛthak api sāhasrakārīṣavārmaṇātharvaṇādikam । iti saṃkīrṇavargaḥ atra mūlaślokāḥ
