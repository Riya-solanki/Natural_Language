itaśca puruṣapratipattyarthaiveyamindriyādipravāhoktiḥ ।
yatkāraṇam aeṣa sarveṣu bhūteṣu gūḍhotmā na prakāśate ।
dṛśyate tvagryayā buddhyā sūkṣmayā sūkṣmadarśibhiḥ' iti prakṛtaṃ puruṣamātmetyāha ।
ataścānātmatvamitareṣāṃ vivakṣitamiti gamyate ।
tasyaiva ca durvijñānatāṃ saṃskṛtamitigamyatāṃ ca darśayati ।
tadvijñānāyaiva ayacchedvāṅmanasī prājñaḥ' ityādhyānaṃ vidadhāti ।
tadvyākhyatam aānumānikamapyekamapyekeṣām' ityatra ।
evamanekaprakāra āśayātiśayaḥ śruteḥ puruṣe lakṣyate netareṣu ।
apica aso adhvanaḥ pāramāpnoti tadviṣṇoḥ paramaṃ padam' ityukte kiṃ tadadhvanaḥ pāraṃ viṣṇoḥ paramaṃ padamityasyāmākāṅkṣāyāmindriyādyanukramaṇātparamapadapratipattyartha evāyamāyāma ityavasīyate ।।
