brahma jagadupādānamiti bruvan vedāntasamanvayo viṣayaḥ ।
sa kiṃ yadvibhu tanna dravyopādānamiti vaiśeṣikādinyāyena virudhyate na veti saṃdehe sāṃkhyavṛddhānāṃ tarkākuśalamatitve api vaiśeṣikādīnāṃ tarkamatikuśalatvaprasiddhestadīyanyāyasyābādhitatvādvirudhyata iti pratyudāharaṇena prāpte atidiśati-eteneti ।
phalaṃ pūrvavat ।
nanu sāṃkhyamatasyopadeśastārkikamatasyātideśaḥ kimiti kṛto vaiparītyasyāpi saṃbhavādityāśaṅkya pūrvottarādhikaraṇayorupadeśātideśabhāve kāraṇamāha-vaidikasyeti ।
