eteṣu [vāsaraḥ] ekavāsaro anadhyāyaḥ।
sandhyāstanitadigdāhapariveṣopaleṣu ca] sandhyayormeghagarjitaśrutau, digdāhe, sūryapariveṣadarśane, antarikṣād upalavarṣe caśabdādekāhameva।
