asaṃkalpādevāsya pitaraḥ samuttiṣṭhanti' ityādi śruteḥ manaḥ tāvat saṃkalpasādhanaṃ siddham ।
śarīra indriyāṇi punaḥ prāpta aiśvaryasya viduṣaḥ santi na vā santi iti samīkṣyate ।
tatra bādariḥ tāvat ācāryaḥ śarīrasya indriyāṇāṃ ca abhāvaṃ mahīyamānasya viduṣo manyate ।
kasmāt ।
evaṃ hi āha āmnāyaḥ amanasaitānkāmānpaśyanramate' aya ete brahmaloke' iti ।
yadi manasā śarīra indriyāṇāṃ mokṣe ।।
