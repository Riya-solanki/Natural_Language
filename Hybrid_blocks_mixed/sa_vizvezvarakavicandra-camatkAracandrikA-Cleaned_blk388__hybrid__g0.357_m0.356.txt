iti ślokārthasya ca prapañcakathanaprasiddhasyātrātisaṅkocena kathanāccamatkārātiśayakārī saṃkṣepo nāma guṇaḥ ayam "sa mārutasutānītamahauṣadhidhṛtavyathaḥ" । ityādau kavibhiraṅgīkṛto lakṣaṇīyaḥ ।
supāṃ tiṅāṃ ca vyatpattiśśabdasaṃskāra ucyate ।।
yathā
dikkūlaṅkaṣavaibhavā ghanapathavyāptipriyaṃbhāvukā śrīsiṅgakṣitipālakīrtivibhavāḥ śambhostulālambinaḥ ।
gāmadhyāsitumīśate pragupate paryāptamāryotsavān kalpānte tava kalpitāṃ vivṛṇute dhāmrā mahimrāṃ śriyam ।।
