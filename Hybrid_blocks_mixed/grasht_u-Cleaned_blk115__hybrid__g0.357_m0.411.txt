ṣaṣṭhoddiṣṭaṃ nirodhanasaṃskāraṃ spaṣṭayannāha mardanetyādi ।।
etair mardanamūrchanapātaiḥ saṃskāraviśeṣaṃ kṛtvā mandavīryatvāt kadarthito bhavati ।।
kutsitavidhānena kadarthito bhavatītyarthaḥ punaḥ sūtaḥ sṛṣṭyambujaiḥ saha mardanānantaraṃ nirodhāt mūṣādvayasampuṭe kūpikāyāṃ vā nirodhāt rundhanāt labdhāpyāyaḥ prāptabalaḥ san na ṣaṇḍhaḥ syāt na śukrarahito bhavati ।।
sṛṣṭiḥ mūtraśukraśoṇitarūpā ambujaṃ lavaṇaṃ saindhavaṃ kamalam iti mandāḥ ।।
sṛṣṭiryathā ।
go'jāvinaranārīṇāṃ mūtraṃ śukraṃ ca śoṇitam ।
sṛṣṭireṣā samākhyātā ṣaṇḍhadoṣavināśinī ।
iti śaktyavatārāt ।।
yantraṃ yathā ।
raktasaindhavakhoṭena mūṣādvaṃdvaṃ prakalpayet ।
tatsampuṭe rasaṃ kṣiptvā navasāraṃ sanimbukam ।।
sampuṭasya prayatnena lepayet saṃdhimuttamam ।
vajramṛtsnāṃ samādāya veṣṭayettatprayatnataḥ ।।
chāyāśuṣkaṃ ca tatkṛtvā bhūgarte sthāpayettataḥ ।
aṣṭāṅgulapramāṇena mūṣordhvaṃ tatra pūraṇam ।।
trisaptadinaparyantaṃ karīṣāgniṃ ca kārayet ।
dine dine prakartavyā mūṣā saindhavanūtanā ।।
svedayet tat prayatnena bhūgarbhe sthāpayettataḥ ।
athavā kūpikāmadhye sūtaṃ saindhavasaṃyutam ।।
bhūgarbhe ca tataḥ sthāpyam ekaviṃśaddināvadhi ।
ayaṃ nirodhako nāmnā mahāmukhakaro rase ।।
iti ।।
