yadviparyayātmāno doṣāstān guṇān vicārayituṃ guṇavivecanamadhikaraṇamārabhyate । tatraujaḥprasādādayo guṇā yamakopamādayastvalaṅkārā iti sthitiḥ kāvyavidām । teṣāṃ kiṃ bhedanibandhanamityāha 
kāvyaśobhāyāḥ kartāro dharmā guṇāḥ ।।
ye khalu śabdārthayordharmāḥ kāvyaśobhāṃ kurvanti te guṇāḥ । te caujaḥprasādayaḥ । na yamakopamādayaḥ । kauvalyena teṣāmakāvyaśobhākaratvāt । ojaḥprasādādīnāṃ tu kevalānāmasti kāvyaśobhākaratvamiti ।।
tadatiśayahetavastvalaṅkārāḥ ।।
tasyāḥ kāvyaśobhāyā atiśayastadatiśayastasya hetavaḥ । śabdo vyatireke । alaṅkārāśca yamakopamādayaḥ । atra ślokau
