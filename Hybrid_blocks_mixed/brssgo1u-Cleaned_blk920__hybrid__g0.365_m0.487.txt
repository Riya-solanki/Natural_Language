so ayamitivattattvamasītyakāryakāraṇadravyasāmānādhikaraṇyādatyantābhedo vedāntārthastadbodha eva niḥśreyasasādhanaṃ tasya bādho na yukta ityarthaḥ ।
kiñca bhedābhadavādino jñānakarmabhyāṃ kṛtakaṃ mokṣaṃ kalpayanti, tatrānityatvaṃ doṣaḥ ।
yattu kṛtakamapi nityamiti, tacca ayat kriyāsādhyaṃ tadanityam'iti nyāyabādhitam ।
asmākaṃ tvanarthadhvaṃsasya jñānasādhyatvānnityamuktātmamātratvācca nānityatvadoṣa iti bhāvaḥ ।
tasmānmaitreyībrāhmaṇaṃ pratyagbrahmaṇi samanvitamiti siddham ।।
