śikhiśaśinau svarṇatārakau sāralohākhyau sāralohasaṃjñakāvityarthaḥ ।।
pūtilohasaṃjñānāha tāmretyādi ।।
tāmrāratīkṣṇakāntābhrasatvalohānīti tāmraṃ nepālakaṃ āraṃ rājarītiḥ tīkṣṇaṃ sāraṃ kāntaṃ cumbakodbhavaṃ abhrasatvaṃ gaganasāraṃ lohaṃ muṇḍaṃ etānīti punarvaṅganāgau ete pūtisaṃjñakāḥ kathitāḥ ।।
teṣāṃ pūtilohasaṃjñakānāṃ śodhanaṃ samyaṅmalāpanayanaṃ kāryamiti ।।
