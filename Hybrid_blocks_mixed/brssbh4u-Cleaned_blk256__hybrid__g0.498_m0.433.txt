ucyate ।
yatra avidyākṛta nāma rūpādi viśeṣapratiṣedhāt asthūlādiśabdaiḥ brahma upadiśyate tat param ।
tat eva yatra nāmarūpādiviśeṣeṇa kenacit viśiṣṭam upāsanāya upadiśyate amanomayaḥ prāṇaśarīro bhārūpaḥ' ityādiśabdaistadaparam ।
