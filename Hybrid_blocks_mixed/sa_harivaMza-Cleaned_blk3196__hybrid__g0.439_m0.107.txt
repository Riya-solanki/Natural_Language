pratyuvācātha sā kṛṣṇaṃ priyo asi mama darśane 
tataḥ sā praṇatā bhūtvā pradadāv anulepanam । 
rājārham idam agryaṃ ca tad gṛhāṇānulepanam ।। 
kṛṣṇo atha balabhadraś ca tadādāyānulepanam । aṅgāni cāpy alimpetāṃ śubhaṃ ca susugandhi ca ।। mālayātha tadā viṣṇur baddhayā bahupuṣpayā । govindo gopatiḥ kṛṣṇo rarāja kṛtaśekharaḥ । saṃkarṣaṇo atha balavān rarāja bahumālayā । 
tāv ubhāv anuliptāṅgāv ārdragātrau virejatuḥ 
tīrthagau paṅkadigdhāṅgau yamunāyāṃ yathā vṛṣau ।। 
dārakāś ca tathā sarve virejuḥ kṛtaśekharāḥ । vikṛtaṃ bhūṣayanto api prabhāvāc chārṅgadhanvanaḥ । 
tāṃ tu kubjāṃ tataḥ kṛṣṇo dvyaṅgulenāgrapāṇinā 
śanaiḥ saṃtolayāmāsa kṛṣṇo līlāvidhānavit ।।
