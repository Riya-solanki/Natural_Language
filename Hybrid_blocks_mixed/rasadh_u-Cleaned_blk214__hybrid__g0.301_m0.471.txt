ihāmukhā ulyāmūlāni tvaksaṃyuktānyeva samānīya teṣāṃ śrīkhaṇḍaśeṣādha kṣiptvā sā mūṣāgniṣṭake muktvāgninā dhmātvā thoharadugdhe ḍhālanīyā ।।
anayā yuktyā sukhena hīrakā mriyante ।।
teṣāṃ ca mṛtānāṃ bhasma kumpe kṣipet ।।
anena hīrakabhasmanā yatkarma yaśca prabhāvaḥ so agre bhaṇiṣyate ।।
iti hīrakabhasmakaraṇaṃ pañcamam ।।
evaṃ pañcabhir bhedair hīrakabhasmīkaraṇaṃ bhavati ।।
iti hīrakavidhiḥ ।।
atha gandhakaśodhanam ।।
