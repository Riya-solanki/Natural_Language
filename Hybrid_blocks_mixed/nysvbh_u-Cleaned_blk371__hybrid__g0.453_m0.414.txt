yadā hyayamupayuktopamāno godarśī gavā samānamarthaṃ paśyati tadā ayaṃ gavaya ityasya saṃjñāśabdasya vyavasthāṃ pratipadyate na caivamanumānamiti।
parārthaṃ copamānam yasya hyupameyamaprasiddhaṃ tadarthaṃ prasiddhopameyena kriyate iti।
parārthamupamānamiticet ? na svayamadhyavasāyāt ।
bhavati ca bhoḥ svayamadhyavasāyaḥ- yathā gaurevaṃ gavaya iti।
nādhyavasāyaḥ
