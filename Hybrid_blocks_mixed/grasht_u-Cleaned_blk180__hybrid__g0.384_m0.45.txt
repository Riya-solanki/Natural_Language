athavā hemnā saha pūrvarasavidhānena gandhapiṣṭiṃ kuryāt athavā drutagandhakasya dravībhūtagandhakasya madhye paktvā vahniyogena supakvaṃ kṛtvā rasaṃ piṣṭivat rasahemagandhapiṣṭiṃ kuryādityarthaḥ ।।
pūrvoddiṣṭasya dvaṃdvamelanasya vidhānamāha setyādi ।।
ca punaḥ ।।
sā pūrvoktā hemapiṣṭiḥ rasahemagandhakṛtaretasoḥ sambandhaḥ sadātanaḥ ।।
hemnā militā yā piṣṭimelanaviśet sā hemapiṣṭī bhūyaḥ punaḥ gandhake vipacyate yuktyā pākaḥ kāryaḥ pūrvavad gandhake ।।
ittham uktavidhānena hemnā saha sūtaḥ pārado milati granthimeti kva sati dvaṃdve sati ubhayasaṃyoge sati ।।
tathoktavidhānena dvaṃdve milati sati tatkālād alpakālato mriyate pañcatvamupayāti ।।
alpakriyāntarakaraṇānmṛto bhavatīti bhāvaḥ ।।
iti pakṣacchedavidhiḥ ।।
