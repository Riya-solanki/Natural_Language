naiṣa doṣaḥ ।
ānandamayopasaṃkramaṇanirdeśenaiva pucchapratiṣṭhābhūtabrahmaprāpteḥ phalasyanirdiṣṭatvāt ।
atadapyeṣa śloko bhavati ।
yato vāco nivartante' ityadinā ca prapañcyamānatvāt ।
yā tvānandamayasaṃnidhāne aso akāmayata bahusyāṃ prajāyeyeti' iyaṃ śrutirudāhṛtā sā abrahma pucchaṃ pratiṣṭhā' ityanena saṃnihitatareṇa brahmaṇā saṃbadhyamānā nānandamayasya brahmatāṃ pratibodhayati ।
tadapekṣatvāccottarasya granthasya araso vai saḥ' ityādernānandamayaviṣayatā ।
