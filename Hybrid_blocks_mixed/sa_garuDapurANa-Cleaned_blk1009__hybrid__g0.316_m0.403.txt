vāratrayaṃ padmamudrāṃ bimbamudrāṃ ca darśayet । oṃ āṃ hṛdayāya namaḥ । oṃ arkāya śirase svāhā । 
oṃ aḥ bhūrbhuvaḥ svaḥ jvālini śikhāyai vaṣaṭ ।
oṃ huṃ kavacāya huṃ ।
oṃ bhāṃ netrābhyāṃ vauṣaṭ ।
oṃ vaḥ astrāya phaḍiti ।।
