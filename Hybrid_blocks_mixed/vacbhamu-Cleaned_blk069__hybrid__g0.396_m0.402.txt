tadevaṃ paramārthaparihāramuktvā abhyupetyāpi cidātmanaḥ parokṣatāṃ prauḍhavāditayā parihārāntaramāha- na cāyamasti iti ।
puro avasthita eva, aparokṣa eva ।
kasmādayaṃ na niyama ityata āha- apratyakṣe api iti ।
hiryasmādarthe ।
nabho hi dravyaṃ sat rūpasparśavirahānna bāhyendriyapratyakṣam ।
nāpi mānasam, manaso asahāyasya bāhye apravṛtteḥ; tasmādapratyakṣam ।
atha ca tatra bālā avivekinaḥ paradarśitadarśinaḥ kadācitpārthivacchāyāṃ śyāmatāmāropya, kadācittaijasaṃ śuklatvamāropya, nīlotpalapalāśaśyāmamiti vā rājahaṃsamālādhavalamiti vā nirvarṇayanti ।
tatrāpi pūrvadṛṣṭasya taijasasya vā tāmasasya vā rūpasya paratra nabhasi smṛtirūpo avabhāsa iti ।
evaṃ tadeva talamadhyasyanti avāṅmukhībhūtamahendranīlamaṇimayamahākaṭāhakalpamityarthaḥ ।
upasaṃharati- evam uktena prakāreṇa sarvākṣepaparihārāt, aviruddhaḥ pratyagātmanyapyanātmanām- buddhyādīnām- adhyāsaḥ ।
