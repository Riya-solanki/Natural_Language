Vṛtti:
[sādhyakoṭeḥ] sādhyasyābhipretasya koṭeḥ sampatteḥ alabdhatvāt [tallābho api tadāśrayaḥ] tallābhasya ca gurvāśrayatvāt samayinaḥ prāggṛhasthasya ca gurūpasadanamavaśyaṃ kāryam। yasmād anyavṛttiniṣṭhasya sato [na ca] na tallābhaḥ। tasmāt [tatsādhako bhavet] ācāryaparicaryāsādhanaparau syātām।
[sādhakācārāḥ]
atha sādhakacaryā
