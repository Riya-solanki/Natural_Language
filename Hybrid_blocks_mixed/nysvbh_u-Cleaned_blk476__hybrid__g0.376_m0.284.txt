yadyanupalambhādāvaraṇaṃ nāsti ? āvaraṇānupalabdhirapi tarhyanupalambhānnāstīti tasyā abhāvādapratiṣiddhamāvaraṇamiti।
kathaṃ punarjānīte bhavān nāvaraṇānupalabdhirupalabhyate ? iti।
kimatra jñeyaṃ pratyātmavedanīyatvāt।
samānam ayaṃ khalvāvaraṇamanupalabhamānaḥ pratyātmameva saṃvedayate anāvaraṇamupalabhe' iti, yathā kuḍyenāvṛtasyāvaraṇamupalabhamānaḥ pratyātmameva saṃvedayate, seyamāvaraṇopalabdhivad āvaraṇānupalabdhirapi saṃvedyaiveti, evaṃ ca satyapahṛtaviṣayamuttaravākyamastīti ।।
