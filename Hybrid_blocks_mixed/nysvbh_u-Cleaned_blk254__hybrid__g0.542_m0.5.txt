na vipratipattimātrādavyavasthāmātrādvā saṃśayaḥ, kiṃ tarhi ? vipratipattimupalabhamānasya saṃśayaḥ, evamavyavasthāyāmapīti।
atha vā aastyātmā' ityeke anāstyātmā' ityapare manyante ityupalabdheḥ kathaṃ saṃśayaḥ syāt ? iti।
tathopalabdhiravyavasthitā anupalabdhiś cāvyavasthiteti vibhāgenādhyavasite saṃśayo nopapadyate iti ।।
