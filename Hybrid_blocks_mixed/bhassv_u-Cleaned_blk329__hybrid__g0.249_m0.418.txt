{rājā} - evam etat 
{vidūṣakaḥ} - teṇa hi muhuttaaṃ paḍivāḷedu bhavaṃ jāva oṭṭhagaaṃ karissaṃ 
rāā bahmadatto ṇaaraṃ kaṃpiḷḷaṃ 
{iti bahuśas tadaiva paṭhitvā }
idāṇiṃ suṇādu bhavaṃ 
ayi sutto attabhavaṃ 
adisīdaḷā iaṃ veḷā 
attaṇo pāvaraaṃ gahṇia āamissaṃ 
[tena hi muhūrtakaṃ pratipālayatu bhavān yāvad oṣṭhagataṃ kariṣyāmi । rājā brahmadattaḥ nagaraṃ kāmpilyam । idānīṃ śṛṇotu bhavān । ayi supto atrabhavān । atiśītaleyaṃ velā । ātmanaḥ prāvārakaṃ gṛhītvāgamiṣyāmi । ]
