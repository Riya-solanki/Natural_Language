tatra devavaraṃ śaṃbhumarddhanārīśvaraṃ haram ।
dṛṣṭvārcya saṃpūjya pitṝn mahendraṃ cottaraṃ gataḥ ।।
tatra devavaraṃ śaṃbhuṃ gopālaṃ somapāyinam dṛṣṭvā snātvā somatīrthe sahyācalamupāgataḥ ।।
tatra snātvā mahodakyāṃ vaikuṇṭhaṃ cārcyaṃ bhaktitaḥ ।
surān pitṛn samabhyarcya pāriyātraṃ giriṃ gataḥ ।।
