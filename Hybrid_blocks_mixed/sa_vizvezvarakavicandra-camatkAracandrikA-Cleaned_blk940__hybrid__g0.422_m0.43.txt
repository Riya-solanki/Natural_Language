atra lajjāśīlayā cajidānaṃdabāṣparomāñcakāraṇaṃ nāyakaviṣayamanurāgamapahnutya visadṛśavirāgapavanalakṣaṇakāraṇopanyāsaḥ kalpita iti seyamasādṛśyavatī nāmāpahnutiḥ ।
samādhyuktirmato anyasminnanya dharmādhiropaṇam ।।
anyasyānyadharmasya tadubhayasya ceti vyutpattyā dharmiṇoradhyāse dharmasyādhyāse dharmadharmiṇoradhyāse ca trividhaḥ samādhiḥ ।
