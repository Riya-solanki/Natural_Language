akurvanneveha karmāṇi jijīviṣecchataṃ samāḥ ।
evaṃ tvayi nānyatheto asti na karma lipyate nare' iti ।
tathā aetadvai jarāmaryaṃ satraṃ yadagnihotraṃ jarayā vā hyevāsmānmucyate mṛtyunā vā' ityevañjātīyakānniyamādapi karmaśeṣatvameva vidhyayā iti ।।
