anityā varṇāḥ' ityetasminṛ pakṣe ikārayakārau varṇauṃ ityubhayornityatvād vikārānupapattiḥ, nityatve avināśitvāt kaḥ kasya vikāra iti ?।
atha aanityā varṇāḥ' itipakṣaḥ ? evamapyanavasthānaṃ varṇānām।
kimidamanavasthānaṃ varṇānām ?, utpadya nirodhaḥ, utpadya niruddhe ikāre yakāra utpadyate yakāre cotpadya niruddhe ikāra utpadyate, kaḥ kasya vikāraḥ ?।
tadetad avagṛhya saṃdhāne saṃdhāya cāvagrahe veditavyamiti ।।
