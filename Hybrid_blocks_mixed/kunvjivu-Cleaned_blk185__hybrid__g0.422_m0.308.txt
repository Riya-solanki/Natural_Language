bandho vākyavinyāsastasya saundaryaṃ rāmaṇīyakaṃ lāvaṇyamabhidhīyate lāvaṇyamityucyate ।
kīdṛśamvarṇānāmakṣarāṇāṃ vinyāso vicitraṃ nyasanaṃ tasya vicchittiḥ śobhā vaidagdhyabhaṅgī tayā lakṣitaṃ padānāṃ suptiṅantānāṃ saṃdhānaṃ saṃyojanaṃ tasya sampat, sāpi śobhaiva, tayā lakṣitam ।
kīdṛśyāubhayarūpayāpi svalpayā manāṅmātrayā nātinirbandhanirmitayā ।
tadayamatrārthaḥśabdārthasaukumāryasubhagaḥ saṃniveśamahimā lāvaṇyarūpo guṇaḥ kathyate ।
yathā
