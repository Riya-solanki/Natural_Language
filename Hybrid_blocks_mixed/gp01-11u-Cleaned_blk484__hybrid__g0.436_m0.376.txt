kiṃ ca-
aṅghryor vyakta-darāri-vajra-kamalādyāścarya-cihnair alaṃ
kamrair ujjvalitāṃ tathā kara-yuge taiḥ kaiścid anyair api ।
paśya śrī-vrajanātha nīrada-rucer bālasya sāmudrako-
llaṅghi-śrī-vibhavasya deha-valanām asmāsu citra-pradām ।।
[] tadā ca sarvasminn api vismita-caryā-paryākule baṭavaḥ sahāsa-pāṭavam ūcuḥ-aye ! sarva-śarmada ! nirmala-dharmaṇo bhavataḥ katham aśaucaṃ nāma sāmarthyaṃ samarthayatām ? yato nāḍī-ccheda eva vṛtte tad āmananti sma ।
