kasmāt ।
ambarāntadhṛteḥ- pṛthivyāderākāśāntasya vikārajātasya dhāraṇāt ।
tatra hi pṛthivyādeḥ samastavikārajātasya kālatrayavibhaktasya aākāśa eva tadotaṃ ca protaṃ ca' ityākāśe pratiṣṭhitatvamuktvā akasminnu khalvākāśa otaśca protaśca' ityanena praśnenedamakṣaramavatāritam ।
tathācopasaṃhṛtam- aetasminnu khalvakṣare gārgyākāśa otaśca protaśca' iti ।
naceyamambarāntadhṛtirbrahmaṇo anyatra saṃbhavati ।
yadapi aoṅkāra evedaṃ sarvam' iti tadapi brahmapratipattisādhanatvātstutyarthaṃ draṣṭavyam ।
tasmānna kṣaratyaśnute ceti nityatvāvyāpitatvābhyāmakṣaraṃ parameva brahma ।।
