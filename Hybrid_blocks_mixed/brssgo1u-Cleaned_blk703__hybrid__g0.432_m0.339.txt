iha devādīnāmapi brahmavidyāyāmastyadhikāra iti yatpratijñātaṃ tatparyāvartyate ।
devādīnāmanadhikāraṃ jaiminirācāryo manyante ।
kasmāt ।
madhvādiṣvasaṃbhavāt ।
brahmavidyāyāmadhikārābhyupagame hi vidyātvāviśeṣānmadhvādividyāsvapyadhikāro abhyupagamyeta ।
nacaivaṃ saṃbhavati ।
katham ।
aasau vā ādityo devamadhu' ityatra manuṣyā ādityaṃ madhvadhyāsenopasīran ।
devādiṣu hyupāsakeṣyabhyupagamyamāneṣvādityaḥ kamanyamādityamupāsīta ।
punaścādityavyapāśrayāṇi pañca rohitādīnyamṛtānyupakramya vasavo rudrā ādityā marutaḥ sādhyāśca pañca devagaṇāḥ krameṇa tattatamṛtamupajīvantītyupadiśya asa ya etadevamamṛtaṃ veda vasūnāmevaiko bhūtvāgninaiva mukhenaitadevāmṛtaṃ dṛṣṭvā tṛpyati' ityādinā vasvādyupajīvyānyamṛtāni vijānatāṃ vasvādimahimaprāptiṃ darśayati ।
vasvādayastu kānanyānvasvādīnamṛtopajīvino vijānīyuḥ ।
kaṃ vānyaṃ vasvādimahimānaṃ prepseyuḥ ।
atathā agniḥ pādo vāyuḥ pāda ādityaḥ pādo diśaḥ pādaḥ', avāyurvāva saṃvargaḥ'' aādityo brahmetyādeśaḥ' ityādiṣu devatātmopāsaneṣu na teṣāmeva devatātmānamadhikāraḥ saṃbhavati ।
tathā aimāveva gotamabharadvājā vayameva gotamo ayaṃ bharadvājaḥ' ityādiṣvipyṛṣisaṃbandheṣūpāsaneṣu na teṣāmevarṣīṇāmadhikāraḥ saṃbhavati ।।
