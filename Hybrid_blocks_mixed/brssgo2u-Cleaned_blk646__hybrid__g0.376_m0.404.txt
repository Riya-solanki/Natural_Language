na ।
gandhādiviṣayaviśeṣaparicchedārthatvāt ।
tathāhi darśayati- agandhāya ghrāṇam' ityādi ।
yattu suptādayo na cetayanta iti tasya śrutyaiva parihāro abhihitaḥ ।
suṣuptaṃ prakṛtya ayadvai tanna paśyati paśyanvai tanna paśyati- nahi draṣṭurddaṣṭerviparilopo vidyate avināśitvānna tu taddvitīyamasti tato anyadvibhaktaṃ yatpaśyet' ityādinā ।
etaduktaṃ bhavati- viṣayābhāvādiyamacetayamānatā na caitanyābhāvāditi ।
yathā viyadāśrayasya prakāśasya prakāśyabhāvādanabhivyaktirna svarūpābhāvāttadvat ।
vaiśeṣikāditarkaśca śrutivirodha ābhāsībhavati ।
tasmānnityacaitanyasvarūpa evātmeti niścinumaḥ ।।
