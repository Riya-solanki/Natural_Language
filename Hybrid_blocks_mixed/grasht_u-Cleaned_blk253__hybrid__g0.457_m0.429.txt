rañjitaghanasatvasya cāraṇamāha cāryamityādi ।।
tadvidhaṃ pūrvarañjitaṃ ghanasatvam abhrasatvaṃ ca ghanaṃ kevalaghanodbhavaṃ satvaṃ arañjitaṃ rase pārade cāryaṃ grāsagrasanamānenaitad abhrasatvaṃ rase saṃyojyam ।।
vā satve sarvabījaṃ sarvaṃ rāgadāyi dravyaṃ saṃyojya nirvāhyaṃ nirvāhitaṃ kuryāt ।।
kutaḥ dvandvasaṃkarataḥ dvandvānāṃ saṃkaro melāpaḥ saṅkaro'vakara ityamaraḥ ।।
nirvāhyaṃ iti vaha prāpaṇe ityasya rūpaṃ pūrvavat ।।
niravyayaṃ niścayārthaṃ nirniścayaniṣedhayoḥ ityamaraḥ ।।
