atha śulbābhramāha ghanetyādi ।।
ghanasatvaśulbamākṣikasamabhāganiyojitaṃ ghanasatvam abhrasatvaṃ śulbaṃ tāmraṃ mākṣikaṃ svarṇamākṣikaṃ samabhāgena tulyabhāgena niyojitaṃ prayuktaṃ tanmilitaṃ sat śulbābhraṃ kathitam ।।
atra śulbaṃ kīdṛśaṃ prayojyaṃ tadāha ।
dvāvarkau mlecchanepālau rase nepāla uttamaḥ ।
ghanaghātasahaḥ snigdho raktapatro'malo mṛduḥ ।।
mlecchastu kṣālitaḥ kṛṣṇo rūkṣasnigdho ghanāsahaḥ ।
miśrito nāgalohābhyāṃ na śreṣṭho rasakarmaṇi ।
iti ato nepālakaṃ grāhyamityarthaḥ ।।
tacchulvābhraṃ rasaḥ kṣipraṃ carati punaḥ kṣipraṃ śīghraṃ tacchulbaṃ jīryati jāraṇamāpnoti ।।
atra mākṣikayogaḥ śulbābhrasatvamelanārthaṃ rasaprītyeti bhāvaḥ ।।
śulbābhracāraṇavidhānamāha itītyādi ।।
iti pūrvoktaṃ tāpyaśulbasahitaṃ tāpyaṃ svarṇamākṣikaṃ śulbaṃ tāmraṃ nepālasaṃjñakaṃ tābhyāṃ sahitaṃ miśritaṃ ghanasatvaṃ taptalohakhalvake mṛditaṃ kāryaṃ mardanīyaṃ kaiḥ kṛtvā kāñjikavetasajambīrabījapūrāmlaiḥ kāñjikamuktavidhānaṃ sauvīraṃ vetasaṃ cukraṃ jambīraṃ prasiddhaṃ bījapūro mātuluṅgaḥ eteṣāmamlaiḥ dravarūpaiḥ ।।
tataḥ śulvābhraṃ rasendraḥ pāradaścarati grasati ।।
lohakhalvasya kathanāt taptakhalvake mardanaṃ kuryāditi tātparyārthaḥ ।।
