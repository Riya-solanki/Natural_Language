atra mattajātyupakramasya madavatītyādiguṇavarṇanenānirvāhād guṇatvam ।
asyāpi guṇatā kvāpi yadi chāyā na hīyate ।।
yathā grīṣmo niḥśvasiteṣu locanayuge varṣāḥ śaradgaṇḍayoriti pūrvodāhṛteṣu ṛtuṣvekavacanaprakramasya varṣā iti bahuvacanopādānād bhinnatve api bhūmnyeva varṣā ityanuśāsanād bahuvacanaśruterapyarthaikyasyātyājyatvādekavacanachāyā na hīyata iti guṇatvam ।
yadaśraddheyamatyuktamatimātraṃ taducyate ।।
yathā
śrīsiṅgabhūpāla tavāhitānāṃ niḥśvāsavegā girikānanāni ।
kurvanti nirdagdhanikuñjapuñjaṃ paryastagaṇḍopalamaṇḍalāni ।।
