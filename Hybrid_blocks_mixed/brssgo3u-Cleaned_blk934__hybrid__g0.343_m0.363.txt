yathā mumukṣorvidyāsādhanāvalambinaḥ sādhanavīryaviśeṣādvidyalakṣaṇe phala aihikāmuṣmikaphalatvakṛto viśeṣapratiniyamo dṛṣṭaḥ ।
evaṃ muktilakṣaṇe apyutkarṣīpakarṣakṛtaḥ kaścidviśeṣapratiniyamaḥ ।
syādityāśaṅkyāha - evaṃ muktiphalāniyama iti ।
na khalu muktiphale kaścidevaṃbhūto viśeṣapratiniyama āśaṅkitavyaḥ ।
kutaḥ - tadavasthāvadhṛteḥ ।
muktyavasthā hi sarvavedānteṣvekarūpaivāvadhāryate ।
brahmaiva hi muktyavasthā naca brahmaṇo anekākārayogo asti ।
ekaliṅgatvāvadhāraṇāt aastūlamanaṇu' asa eṣa neti netyātmā' ayatra nānyatpaśyati' abrahmaivedamamṛtaṃ purastāt' aidaṃ sarvaṃ yadayamātmā' asa vā eṣa mahānaja ātmājaro amaro amṛto abhayo brahma' ayatra tvasya sarvamātmaivābhūttatkena kaṃ paśyet' ityādiśrutibhyaḥ ।
apica vidyāsādhanaṃ svavīryaviśeṣāsvaphala eva vidyāyāṃ kañcidatiśayamāsañjayenna vidyāphale muktau ।
taddhyasādhyaṃ nityasiddhasvabhāvameva vidyāyādhigamyata ityasakṛdavādiṣma ।
naca tasyāmapyutkarṣanikarṣātmako atiśaya upapadyate nikṛṣṭāyā vidyātvābhāvādutkṛṣṭaiva hi vidyā bhavati ।
tasmāttasyāṃ cirācirotpattirūpo atiśayo bhavanbhavet ।
natu muktau kaścidatiśayasaṃbhavo asti ।
vidyābhedābhāvādapi tatphalabhedaniyamābhāvaḥ karmaphalavat ।
nahi muktisādhanabhūtāyā vidyāyāḥ karmaṇāmiva bhedo astīti ।
saguṇāsu tu vidyāsu - amanomayaḥ prāṇaśarīraḥ' ityādyāsu guṇāvāpodvāpavaśādbhedopapattau satyāmupapadyate yathāsvaṃ phalabhedaniyamaḥ karmaphalavat ।
tathāca liṅgadarśanam - ataṃ yathā yathopāsate tadeva bhavati' iti ।
naivaṃ nirguṇāyāṃ vidyāyāṃ guṇābhāvāt ।
tathāca smṛtiḥ - anahi gatiradhikāsti kasyacitsati hi guṇe pravadantyatulyatām' iti ।
tadavasthāvadhṛtestadavasthādhṛteriti padābhyaso adhyāyaparisamāptiṃ dyotayati ।।
