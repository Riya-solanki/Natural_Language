atrānunayaḥ kimupabhogalakṣaṇe arthāntare saṃkramitaḥ kimanuraṇananyāyenopabhege, eva vyaṅgaye vyañjakaḥ, iti saṃdehaḥ ।
snigdhaśyāmalakāntiliptaviyato velladbalākā ghanāḥ vātāḥ śīkariṇaḥ payodasuhṛdāmānandakekāḥ kalāḥ ।
kāmaṃ santu dṛḍhaṃ kaṭhorahṛdayo rāmo asmi sarvaṃ sahe vaidehī tu kathaṃ bhaviṣyati ha hā hā devi dhīrā bhava ।।
atra lipteti payodasuhṛdāmiti ca, atyantatiraskṛtavācyayoḥ saṃsṛṣṭiḥ ।
tābhyāṃ saha rāmo asmītyarthāntarasaṃkramitavācyasyānugrāhyānugrāhakabhāvena rāmapadalakṣaṇaikavyañjakānupraveśena cārthāntarasaṃkramitavācyarasadhvanyoḥ saṃkaraḥ ।
evamanyadapyudāhāryam ।।
