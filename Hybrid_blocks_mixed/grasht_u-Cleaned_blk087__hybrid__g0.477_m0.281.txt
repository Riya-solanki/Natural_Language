adhunā pūrvamataṃ draḍhayati brahmādaya ityādi ।।
yasmin brahmādayo viṣṇurudrendrādayo brahmavido yajante saṃgatiṃ kurvanti samāpnuvantītyarthaḥ yaja devapūjāsaṃgatikaraṇadāneṣu atra saṃgatikaraṇam artho darśitaḥ ।।
kiṃ kṛtvā prāpnuvanti divyāṃ tanuṃ paramāṃ samāśritya samprāpya tebhyo brahmādibhyo apyanye apare munayo nāradādayo jīvanmuktā yajante saṃgatiṃ kurvanti ।।
te api kiṃviśiṣṭāḥ kalpāntasthāyinaḥ pralayānte'pi tiṣṭhantīti bhāvaḥ ।।
brahmādayas tiṣṭhanta eva ।।
