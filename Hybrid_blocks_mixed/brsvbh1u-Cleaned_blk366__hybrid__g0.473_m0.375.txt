āropitatadbhāvasya tvanyasya nirūpakatve tena tannirūpitaṃ na syāt ।
tasmāttādṛgātmapratipattividhiparebhyo vedāntebhyastādṛgātmaviniśca ।
tadetatsarvamāha-yadyapīti ।
vidhiparebhyo api vastutattvaviniścaya ityatra vidarśanamuktam-yathā yūpeti ।
ayūpe paśuṃ badhnāti'iti bandhanāya viniyukte yūpe, tasyālaukikatvātko asau yūpa ityapekṣite akhādiro yūpo bhavati', ayūpaṃ takṣati', ayūpamaṣṭāśrīkaroti'ityādibhirvākyaistakṣaṇādividhiparairapi saṃskārāviṣṭaṃ viśiṣṭalakṣaṇasaṃsthānaṃ dāru yūpa iti gamyate ।
eva
