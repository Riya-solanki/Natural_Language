vikāryo vā yathāvaghātasya vrīhayaḥ ।
saṃskāryo vā, yathā prokṣaṇasyolūkhalādayaḥ ।
prāpyo vā yathā dohanasya payaḥ ।
na tāvadutpādyaḥ ।
na khalu ghaṭādisākṣātkāra iva jaḍasvabhāvebhyo ghaṭādibhyo bhinna indriyādyādheyo brahmasākṣātkāro bhāvanādheyaḥ saṃbhavati, brahmaṇo aparādhīnaprakāśatayā tatsākṣātkārasya tatsvabhāvyena nityatayotpādyatvānupapatteḥ, tato bhinnasya
