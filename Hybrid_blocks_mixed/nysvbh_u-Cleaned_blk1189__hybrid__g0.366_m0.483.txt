avayavāntarābhāvāditi, yadyapyekadeśo avayavāntarabhūtaḥ syāt tathāpyavayave avayavāntaraṃ varteta nāvayavīti anyo avayavīti।
anyāvayavabhāvepyavṛtteḥ aavayavino naikadeśena vṛttiḥ anyāvayavābhāvāt' ityahetuḥ।
vṛttiḥ kathamiticet ? ekasyānekatrāśrayāśritasaṃbandhalakṣaṇā prāptiḥ।
āśrayāśritabhāvaḥ kathamiticet ? yasya yato anyatrātmalābhānupapattiḥ sa āśrayaḥ na kāraṇadravyebhyo anyatra kāryadravyamātmānaṃ labhate viparyayastu kāraṇadravyeṣviti।
nityeṣu kathamiticet ? anityeṣu darśanāt siddham।
nityeṣu dravyeṣu kathamāśrayāśrayibhāva iticet ? anityeṣu dravyaguṇeṣu darśanādāśrayāśrayibhāvasya nityeṣu siddhiriti।
tasmādavayavyabhimānaḥ pratiṣidhyate niḥśreyasakāmasya nāvayavī yathā rūpādiṣu mityāsaṃkalpo na rūpādaya iti ।।
