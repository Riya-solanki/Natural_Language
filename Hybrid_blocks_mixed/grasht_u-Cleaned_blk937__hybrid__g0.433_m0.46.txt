atha bhakṣaṇavidhānamāha itītyādi ।।
rasarājasya vidhau rasendrakarmavidhāne vedhavidhānaṃ prasaṅgataḥ prastāvataḥ proktaṃ na tu svaprajñāsamam ।।
adhunā proktānapi api śabdādanubhūtānapi rasāyane jarāvyādhināśanavidhau yogān dravyasamudāyātkān vakṣyāmi kathayāmītyarthaḥ ।।
