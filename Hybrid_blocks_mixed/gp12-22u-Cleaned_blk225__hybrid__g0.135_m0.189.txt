[] akasmād āgate ca tasmin sasmita-krīḍā-narma-juṣām amūṣāṃ sambhrama-bhrama-vismaya-smaya-trapā-patrapādarādara-harṣa-tarṣādayas te bhāvā yugapad eva samutpannāś citrīyamāṇatāṃ citrāyamāṇatām apy āceruḥ । atra ca,
akasmād āyāntaṃ harim anubhavantī kila hriyā
daśām akṣṇor nānā-drutam apidadhe sā nata-mukhī ।
mudā smerā bhugnā vikasitavatī kuñcitavatī
sa-bāṣpā stabdhābhābhavad iti samaṃ yā na ghaṭate ।। [mandākrāntā]
