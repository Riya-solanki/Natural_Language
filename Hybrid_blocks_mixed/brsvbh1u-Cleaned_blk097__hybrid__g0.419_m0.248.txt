vastvantarameva hi vastvantarasyāsattvamāsthīyate abhāvāntaramabhāvo anyo na kaścidanirūpaṇāt'iti vadadbhiḥ, na cāropitaṃ rūpaṃ vastvantaram,taddhi marīcayo vā bhavet, gaṅgādigataṃ toyaṃ vā ।
pūrvasminkalpe marīcayaḥ iti pratyayaḥ syāt, na toyamiti ।
uttarasmiṃstu gaṅgāyāṃ toyamiti syāt, na punariheti ।
deśabhedāsmaraṇe toyamiti syānna punariheti ।
