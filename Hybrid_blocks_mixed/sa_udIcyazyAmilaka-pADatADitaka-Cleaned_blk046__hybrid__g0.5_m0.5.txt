piśācikāyāṃ tṛṇapiśācaḥ api ca śarkarapālaṃ pitaraṃ vyapadiśati bhrātaraṃ ca nirapekṣam prāyeṇa dauṣkuleyāḥ sahaiva dambhena jāyante bhoḥ kiṃ nu khalu pṛccheyam kim asya veśapraveśe prayojanam iti aye ayaṃ jaradviṭo bhaṭṭiravidatta ita evābhivartate yāvad enaṃ pṛcchāmi aṃgho bhaṭṭiravidatta, kaccij jānīte bhavān asya puruṣavetālasya veśapraveśaprayojanam kiṃ bravīṣi - bhavān eva jānīta iti tad gacchatu bhavān kva nu khalv idaṃ puruṣakāntārāvagāhanaśrāntaṃ mano vinodayeyam bhavatu, dṛṣṭam idam aparaṃ priyasuhṛdaḥ suhṛdbhayād arpitārgalaṃ bhavanam veśyāsuratavimardeṣv akṛtavirāmasya rāmasya
