atra kaścit- nityadravyagataviśeṣaguṇatvātsukhādīnāṃ dravyāntarasaṃyogajatvam, pārthivaparamāṇugatarūpādīnāmagnisaṃyogajatvadarśanāt, yacca dravyāntaraṃ tanmana ityāha -tanna yuktam ।
śarīraṃ hi bhogāyatanam ।
kiñca tasya bhogāyatanatvamanyat tatsaṃyogāpekṣasukhādyutpatteḥ, aśitapītādyādhāratvācca na bahiḥ śarīrātsukhādyutpattiḥ ।
ataḥ śarīrasaṃyogādeva siddherna dravyāntarānumānam ।
kiñca pārthivaguṇānāmeva dravyāntarasaṃyogāpekṣā dṛṣṭā na dravyamātraguṇānām ।
tejaḥsaṃyoga eva ca katāraṇatayā dṛṣṭo na dravyamātrasaṃyogastasmānnaivaṃ manaḥsiddhiriti
