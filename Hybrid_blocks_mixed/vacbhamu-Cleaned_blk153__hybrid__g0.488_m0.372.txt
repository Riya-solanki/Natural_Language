syādetat ।
brahmaṇo jijñāsetyukte tatrānekārthatvādbrahmaśabdasya saṃśayaḥ- kasya brahmaṇo jijñāsā ?- iti ।
asti brahmaśabdo vipratvajātau, yathā- brahmahatyeti; asti ca vede, yathā- brahmojjhamiti; asti ca paramātmani, yathā- abrahma veda brahmaiva bhavati' iti ।
tamimaṃ saṃśayamapākaroti- brahma ca vakṣyamāṇalakṣaṇam iti ।
yato brahmajijñāsāṃ pratijñāya tajjñāpanāya paramātmalakṣaṇaṃ praṇayati tato avagacchāmaḥ paramātmajijñāsaiveyaṃ na vipratvajātyādijijñāsā, ityarthaḥ ।
