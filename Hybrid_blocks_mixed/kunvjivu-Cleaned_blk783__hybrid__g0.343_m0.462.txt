ekadeśavivarto yatra tattathoktam, ekadeśa eva vivartate yatra rūpāntareṇāvatiṣṭhate rityarthaḥ ।
teṣāṃ viśeṣaṇasāmarthyanibandhanāyāḥ padārthaśobhāyāḥ rūpātiśayakārirūpakālaṅkāraniṣpatteḥ ।
yathā "upoḍharāgeṇa" ityādau timirāṃśukamityatraikadeśavivarti vidyate ।
śiṣṭaṃ punaḥ śaśinaḥ kāmukatvaṃ, niśāyāśca kāminītvaṃ rūpaṇīyamapi pratītyantaravidhāyi viśeṣaṇaviśiṣṭiliṅgasāmarthyamātrasamadhigamyaṃ śabdenābhi punaruktatā grāmyatā cādhirohati ।
