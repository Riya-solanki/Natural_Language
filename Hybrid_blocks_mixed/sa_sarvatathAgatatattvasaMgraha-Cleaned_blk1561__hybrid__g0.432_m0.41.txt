tatraitāni mahāsatvacatuṣṭayahṛdayāni bhavanti ।
oṃ suṃbha nisuṃbha huṃ gṛṇha gṛṇha huṃ gṛṇha pya huṃ ānaya ho bhagavan vajra huṃ phaṭ ।।
oṃ vajrabhṛkuṭi krodhānaya sarvaratnān hīḥ phaṭ ।।oṃ vajradṛṣṭi krodhadṛṣṭyā māraya huṃ phaṭ ।।oṃ vajraviśva krodha kuru sarvaṃ viśvarūpatayā sādhaya hūṃ phaṭ ।।
praveśenniṣkramedvāpi sutrādhastānmanogataṃ ।
vajravega iti khyātastena rekhāṃ samākramed ।।
