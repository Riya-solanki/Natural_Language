pareṣāmapyeṣa samānaḥ svapakṣe doṣaḥ ।
pradhānavādino api hi niravayavamaparicchinnaṃ śabdādihīnaṃ pradhānaṃ sāvavasya paricchinnasya śabdādimataḥ kāryasya kāraṇamiti svapakṣaḥ ।
tatrāpi kṛtsnaprasaktirniravayavatvātpradhānasya prāpnoti niravayavatvābhyupagamakopo vā ।
