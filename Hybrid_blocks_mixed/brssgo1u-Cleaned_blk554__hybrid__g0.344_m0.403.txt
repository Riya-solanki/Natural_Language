bhūmno brahmatve liṅgāntaramāha-dharmeti ।
sūtram ।
yaduktaṃ bhūmno lakṣaṇaṃ sukhatvamamṛtatvaṃ ca prāṇeṣu yojyamiti tadanūdya vighaṭayati-yo apyasāvityādinā ।
sati buddhyādyupādhāvātmano draṣṭṛtvādiḥ, tadabhāve suṣuptau tadabhāva ityasaṅgatvajñānārthaṃ praśnopaniṣadi ana śṛṇoti na paśyati'iti paramātmānaṃ prakṛtyoktam ।
tathā tatraivātmanaḥ sukhatvamuktaṃ na prāṇasya ।
yataḥ śrutyantaramātmana eva sukhatvamāha tasmādityarthaḥ ।
āmayo nāśādidoṣaḥ tatsahitaṃ sāmayam ।
ārtaṃ naśvaram ।
asa evādhastāt sa upariṣṭāt'iti sarvagatatvaṃ, asa evedaṃ sarvam'iti sarvātmatvaṃ ca śrutaṃ, tasmādbhūmādhyāyo nirguṇe samanvita iti siddham ।।
