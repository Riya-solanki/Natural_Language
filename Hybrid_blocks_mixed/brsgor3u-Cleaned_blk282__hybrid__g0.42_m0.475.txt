samuccaye liṅgam-samāhārāditi ।
aṛgvedināṃ yaḥ praṇavaḥ sa sāmavedināmudgīthaḥ'iti chāndogye prāṇavodgīthayoraikyadhyānavidhirasti, tasya phalārthavādo hotṛṣadanādityādiḥ ।
hotuḥ śaṃsanasthalavācinā hotṛṣadanaśabdena śaṃsanaṃ lakṣyate-udgātā svarādipramādādduṣṭamapyudgīthaṃ samyakkṛtāddhotṛśaṃsanādanusamāhāratyeva nirdeṣaṃ karotyeva kila, śaṃsyamānapraṇavena svīyodgīthasyaikyadhyānabalādityarthaḥ ।
tataḥ kiṃ tatrāha-iti bruvanniti ।
sāmavedasthodgīthadhyānasya ṛgvedoktapraṇavasaṃbandho yo dṛṣṭaḥ sa evāṅgānāṃ sarvavedāntavihitopāstisamuccaye liṅgaṃ praṇavarūpapadārthasyopāstīnāṃ ca vedāntaroktatvasādṛśyādvedāntaroktāṅgasaṃbandhasyāpi samānatvādityarthaḥ ।।
