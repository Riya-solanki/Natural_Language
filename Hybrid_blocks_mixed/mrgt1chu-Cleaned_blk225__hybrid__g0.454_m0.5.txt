sarvakartṛtvād eveśvarasya sarvajñatvaṃ sarvaviṣayajñānaṃ vinā sarvakartṛtānupapatteḥ ।। 
yo hi yadyatkriyāsiddhiṃ tadaṅgāni tatphalāni ca jānāti tasyaiva vicitratattatkārakopayogābhisaṃdhānavatas tattatkartṛtvaṃ ghaṭate kuvindāderiva paṭādikṛtau ।। 
ato yastu tatrājñaḥ sa tatkāryakaraṇāya nālaṃ bāliśa iva nyāyavidyādyupanyāsa ityetatsusthitamityavyabhicārīti ।।
