nanu nāvaśyatvaṃ nityatve satyekatvaṃ bhavati, anekānyeva hi gakārādīni nityāni bhavantvityata āha- asati viśeṣe nityasya nānekatvam iti ।
etaduktaṃ bhavati- pratyabhijñākhyaviśeṣyapratyayabalena hyastanādyatanagakārayorekatvāvagamānnityatvamāśrīyate ।
asticāsāvaviśeṣapratyayo yugapannānāvaktṛbhiruccārite api gakāre nahi tatrāpi pratyayaviśeṣo asti, asati ca viśeṣe nityasya nānekatvaṃ saṃbhavati ।
anekatve hi pratyabhijñāyā aprāmāṇyamasmākamiva bhavato api syāt tadaprāmāṇyeca
