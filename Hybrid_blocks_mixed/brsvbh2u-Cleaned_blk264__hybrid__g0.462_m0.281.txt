annaśabdo ayaṃ vyutpattyā ca prasiddhyā ca vrīhiyavādau tadvikāre caudane pravartate ।
śrutiśca prakaraṇādbalīyasī, sā ca vākyaśeṣeṇopodbalitā ayatra kvacana varṣati'ityetena tasmādabhyavahāryaṃ vrīhiyavādyevātrādbhyo jāyata iti vivakṣitam ।
kārṣṇyamapi hi saṃbhavati kasyacidadanīsya ।
nahi pṛthivyapi kṛṣṇā, lohitādirūpāyā api darśanāt ।
tataśca śrutyantareṇa aadbhyaḥ pṛthivī pṛthivyā oṣadhayaḥ'ityādinā virodha iti pūrvaḥ pakṣaḥ ।
śrutyorvirodhe vastuni vikalpānupapatteranyatarānuguṇatayānyatarā netavyā ।
tatra kim aadbhyaḥ pṛthivī'iti pṛthivīśabdo annaparatayā nīyatāmuta aannamasṛjata'ityannaśabdaḥ pṛthivīparatayeti viśaye, mahābhūtādhikārānurodhātprāyikakṛṣṇarūpānurodhācca atadyadapāṃ śara āsīt'iti ca punaḥ śrutyanurodhācca vākyaśeṣasya cānyathāpyupapatterannaśabdo annakāraṇe pṛthivyāmiti rāddhāntaḥ ।।
