{vidūṣakaḥ} - uidaṃ tattahodo maadharāassa avarahṇakāḷe bhavantaṃ aggado karia suhijjaṇadaṃsaṇaṃ 
sakkāro hi ṇāma sakkāreṇa paḍicchido pīdiṃ uppādedi 
tā uṭṭhedu dāva bhavaṃ 
[ucitaṃ tatrabhavato magadharājasyāparāhṇakāle bhavantam agrataḥ kṛtvā suhṛjjanadarśanam । satkāro hi nāma satkāreṇa pratīṣṭaḥ prītim utpādayati । tad uttiṣṭhatu tāvad bhavān । ]
