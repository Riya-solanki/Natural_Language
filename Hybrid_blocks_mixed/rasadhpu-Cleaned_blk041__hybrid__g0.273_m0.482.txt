iha manusaṃkhyaiś caturdaśabhiḥ saṃskārair anantaroktaiḥ saṃskṛtya yo māritasūtaḥ sukhalve kṣiptvātyarthaṃ piṣyate ।। 
etat pratisāraṇaṃ bhaṇyate ।। 
iti māritasūtasya pratisāraṇasaṃskāraḥ pañcadaśaḥ ।। 
māritaṃ mṛtanāgena hema tasyāpi cūrṇakam ।
yatpratisāraṇe kṣipyam etat krāmaṇam ucyate ।।
