aguṇavyatiriktaṃ dravyam' iti pratijñā arūpādito arthāntarasyānupalabdheḥ' iti hetuḥ soyaṃ pratijñāhetvorvirodhaḥ।
katham ?, yadi guṇavyatiriktaṃ dravyam ? rūpādibhyo arthāntarasyānupalabdheḥ nopapadyate, atha rūpādibhyorthāntarasyānupalabdhiḥ ? guṇavyatiriktaṃ dravyamiti, nopapadyate, guṇavyatiriktaṃ ca dravyaṃ rūpādibhyorthāntarasyānupalabdhiriti virudhyate ।
vyāhanyate ।
na saṃbhavatīti ।।
