iha prathamaṃ varttulākārāṃ mūṣāṃ kṛtvopari gostanākāraṃ nālaṃ ca kṛtvā vajramūṣā vidheyā ।।
tato yena sūtena pūrvoktayuktyā daśaguṇaṃ gandhakatailaṃ jīrṇaṃ tato hemarājikarṣaśca jīrṇaṃ taṃ rasaṃ vajramūṣāyāṃ kṣiptvopari kaṅguṇītailaṃ tathā kṣipedyathā sa pārado bruḍati ।।
tataḥ sā mūṣāgniṣṭake muktvā iṅgālair dhmātavyā ।।
tathā yāvanmātraḥ mūṣāyāmasti tāvanmātrāṃ hemarājiṃ mūṣāyāṃ gālayitvā vajramūṣāstharasamadhye kṣipet ।।
tataḥ sahasravedhī ṣoṭo bhavati sa ca saṃgrāhyaḥ ।।
tathā tārasya tāmrasyādiśabdādvaṅgasya ca gadyāṇasahasrakaṃ pṛthak pṛthak mūṣāyāṃ gālayitvā sahasravedhitasyaikaiko gadyāṇakaḥ pṛthak pṛthak sarvaṣoṭamadhye kṣipyate sarvāṇi tārādīni pṛthak hema bhavanti ।।
iti dvitīyapariṇāmagandhakatailakarmāṇi ।।
atha gandhakavāri ।।
