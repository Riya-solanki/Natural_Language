lāvaṇyādītyādi ।
āsāmevaṃvidhavākyasvarūpāyattāvagamanodāharaṇaṃ pravakṣyati ।
tatsamudāyātmakasya rasavattayā hartuṃ ca saknoti yatā nāyikā tathā ।
kīdṛśī"lāvaṇyādiguṇojjvalā" lāvaṇyaprabhṛtibhiḥ "guṇaiḥ" prathamollekhalakṣaṇairguṇaiḥ "ujjvalā" bhrājiṣṇuḥ "pratipadanyāsaiḥ" "pratipadanyāsaḥ" pratisuptiṅantopanibandhānāni, taiḥ "vilāsāñcitā" śobhātiśayenābhyañcitā "vicchittyā" vaidagdhyabhaṅgyā kayācit "racitaiḥ" upanibaddhaiḥ "alpaiḥ" parimitaiḥ alaṅkaraṇairupamādibhiḥ, "manohāriṇī" hṛyaparañjikā vāk vicchittivihitaparimitālaṅkāraṇavinmayāsā śobhātiśayanidhānatāṃ pratipadyate ।
kīdṛśī vāk"udārābhidhā" audāryaguṇayuktābhihitā ।
"atyarthaṃ rasavattayā" rāgādimattvena "ārdrahṛdayā" sarasābhiprāyā kāntāpi tathāvidhavilāsāñcitā bhavati ।
nāyikāpakṣe lāvaṇyādayaḥ saundaryaprabhṛtayaḥ, padanyāsaḥ pādavikṣepaḥ, vilāsaḥ ceṣṭāviśeṣaḥ vicchittiḥ vaidagdhyam, vāk vākyam, bhūṣaṇāni alaṅkārāḥ, kavyabhidhā vacanam ।
prabandhasāmarthyamiti ।
iti śrīrājānakakuntakaviricite vakroktijīvite kāvyālaṅkāre tṛtīya unmeṣaḥ ।
