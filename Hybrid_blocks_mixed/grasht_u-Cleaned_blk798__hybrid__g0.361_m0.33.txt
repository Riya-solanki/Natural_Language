susaṃskṛtā mukhāntaḥsthā viśadāśca hitārthakāḥ ।
tathā parocitāḥ pūtā bhavantyamarajā giraḥ ।।
atha krāmaṇapraśaṃsām āha itītyādi ।।
uktavidhānena kṛtaḥ sāraṇasya vidhir yasmin sūtarāje evaṃvidhiḥ sūtarāṭ balavān bhavediti śeṣaḥ ।।
kutaḥ kriyāyogāt kṛtyakaraṇāt ।।
yādṛk kṛtyaṃ kriyate tādṛk balavān syād ityabhiprāyaḥ ।।
evaṃvidho'pi krāmaṇārahitaḥ krāmaṇavarjito lohaṃ na viśati lohāntaḥpraveśaṃ na karoti tato hetor lohaṃ dhātuṃ saṃveṣṭya pariveṣṭanaṃ kṛtvā tiṣṭhati bāhyarāgadāyī syāditi ।।
