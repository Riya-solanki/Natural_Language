na tṛtīye sthāne dehalābhāya pañcasaṃkhyāniyama āhutīnāmādartavyaḥ ।
kutaḥ - tathopalabdheḥ ।
tathāhyantareṇaivāhutisaṃkhyāniyamaṃ varṇitena prakāreṇa tṛtīyasthānaprāptirupalabhyate ajāyasva mriyasvetyetattṛtīyaṃ sthānam' iti ।
apica apañcamyāmāhutāvāpaḥ puruṣavacaso bhavanti' iti manuṣyaśarīrahetutvenāhutisaṃkhyā saṃkīrtyate na kīṭapataṅgādiśarīrahetutvena, puruṣaśabdasya manuṣyajātivacanatvāt ।
apica pañcamyāmāhutāvapāṃ puruṣavacastvamupadiśyate nāpañcamyāmāhutau puruṣavacastvaṃ pratiṣidhyate, vākyasya dvyarthatādoṣāt ।
tatra yeṣāmārohāvarohau saṃbhavatasteṣāṃ pañcamyāmāhutau deha udbhaviṣyati ।
anyeṣāṃ tu vinaivāhutisaṃkhyayā bhūtāntaropasṛṣṭābhiradbhirdeha ārabhyate ।।
