Comm Mugdhāvabodhinī
tasyetyādi ।।
tasya bījasya viśuddhiḥ śodhanaṃ bahudhā bahuprakāraiḥ kṛtvā rasoparasadhātūnāṃ bahuvidhatvāt ।।
kaiḥ kṛtvā gaganarasalohacūrṇaiḥ gaganamabhraṃ rasā vaikrāntādayo'ṣṭau vakṣyamāṇāḥ uparasā gandhakādayaḥ lohā dhātavaḥ teṣāṃ cūrṇāni taiḥ ।।
ca punaḥ tair gaganarasoparasalohacūrṇair aśuddhaiḥ śuddhivarjitaistadbījaṃ na śudhyate śuddhihīnaṃ syāt kāraṇānurūpaṃ kāryamitinyāyāt ।।
