kiñca brahmaṇaḥ sakāśājjīvasyotthānaśruterbrahmaiva suṣuptisthānamityāha-sūtrakāraḥ-ataḥ prabodha iti ।
nāḍīpurītatoḥ kvāpyutthānāpādānatvāśravaṇānna suṣuptisthānatvamityarthaḥ, tasmādupādhilaye jīvasya brahmābhedādaupādhika eva bheda iti vivekadvākyārthābhedasiddhiriti sthitam ।।
