atha yadidamasminbrahmapure daharaṃ puṇḍarīkaṃ veśma daharo asminnantarākāśaḥ iti prastutya chandogā adhīyate aeṣa ātmāpahatapāpmā vijaro vimṛtyurviśoko vijighatso apipāsaḥ satyakāmaḥ satyasaṃkalpaḥ' ityādi ।
tathā vājasaneyinaḥ - sa vā eṣa mahānaja ātmā yo ayaṃ vijñānamayaḥ prāṇeṣu ya eṣo antarhadaya ākāśastasmiñchete sarvasya vaśī ityādi ।
tatra vidyaikatvaṃ parasparaguṇayogaśca kiṃ vā neti saṃśaye vidyaikatvamiti ।
tatredamucyate - kāmādīti ।
satyakāmādityarthaḥ ।
yathā devadatto dattaḥ satyabhāmā bhāmeti ।
yadetaccāndogye hṛdayākāśasya satyakāmatvādiguṇajātamulabhyate taditaratra vājasaneyake vaśitvādyupalabhyate tadapītaratra chāndogye eṣa ātmāpahatapāpmā ityatra saṃbadhyate ।
kutaḥ - āyatanādisāmānyāt ।
