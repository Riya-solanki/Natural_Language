[] lakṣitāyāṃ ca dakṣiṇāyāṃ, madhumaṅgalaḥ sa tu narma-śarmāmṛtam adugdha । "bho vraja-mahanīyāḥ, nāsyām akṣīṇāyām api dakṣiṇāyām īrṣayā vayaṃ vīkṣaṇīyāḥ । bhavatāṃ bhuñjānānām ekaika-vyañjana-mūlya-tulyatayāsmākaṃ samastāpi sā na prastāvayati ।"
