satyamuktam ।
utpattireva tvasya na saṃbhavatīti vadāmaḥ ।
kasmāt ।
nityatvācca tābhyaḥ ।
caśabdādajatvādibhyaśca ।
nityatvaṃ hyasya śrutibhyo avagamyate tathājatvamavikāritvamavikṛtasyaiva brahmaṇo jīvātmanāvasthānaṃ brahmātmanā ceti ।
nacaivaṃrūpasyotpattirupapadyate ।
tāḥ kāḥ śrutayaḥ ।
ana jīvo mriyate', asa vā eṣa mahānaja ātmājaro amaro amṛto abhayo brahma', ana jāyate mriyate vā vipaścit', aajo nityaḥ śāśvato ayaṃ purāṇaḥ' atatsṛṣṭvā tadevānuprāviśat', aanena jīvenātmanānupraviśya nāmarūpe vyākaravāṇi', asa eṣa iha praviṣṭa ā nakhāgrebhyaḥ', atattvamasi' aahaṃ brahmāsmi', aayamātmā brahma sarvānubhūḥ' ityevamādyā nityatvavādinyaḥ satyo jīvasyotpattiṃ pratibadhnanti ।
