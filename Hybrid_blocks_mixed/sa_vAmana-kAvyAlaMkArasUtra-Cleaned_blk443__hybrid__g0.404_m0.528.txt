ityādayo vyākhyātāḥ ।।sarvanāmnānusaṃdhirvṛtticchannasya ।।
sarvanāmnānusandhiranusandhānaṃ pratyavamarśaḥ । vṛtticchannasya vṛttau samāse channasya guṇībhūtasya । yathā
