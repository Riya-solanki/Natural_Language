nanu pratibimbaṃ nāmārthāntaraṃ tatra dṛśyate ।
na ।
tasyābhāvādanupalambhācca, na hi mūrtamadhye mūrtāntaraṃ saṃbhavati ।
kiṃ ca śarāvasthamudakaṃ bhūmerupari nābhidadhne dhārayitvā tasyopariṣṭādaratridadhne svamukhaṃ kurvannudakasyādhastādaratrimātre mukhapratibimbaṃ paśyati, tasmiṃśca deśe pārśvasthāḥ puruṣā na kañcidapi paśyanti tena dṛśyādarśananirastaḥ pratibimbākhyor'tho na śakyo abhyupagantum ।
tasmādādarśatejasā jalena ca pratihataṃ nāyanaṃ tejaḥ parāvṛttyā gṛhṇātīti yuktam ।
tasmādanaikāntiko nānādeśopalambho na nānātvaṃ śabdasya sādhayati ।।
