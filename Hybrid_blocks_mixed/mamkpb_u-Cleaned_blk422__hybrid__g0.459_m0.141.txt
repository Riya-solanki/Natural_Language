dvayoratyantavilakṣaṇatayā yat anupapadyamānatayaiva yogaḥ pratīyatejhrṭa yacca kiṃcidārabhamāṇaḥ kartā kriyāyāḥ praṇāśāt na kevalamabhīṣṭaṃ yat phalaṃ na labheta yāvadaprārthitamapyanarthaṃ viṣayamāsādayet jhrṭa tathā satyapi kāryasya kāraṇarūpānukāre tat tayorguṇau kriye ca parasparaṃ viruddhatāṃ vrajataḥ jhrṣṭa sa samavipayaryātmā catūrūṣo viṣamaḥ ।
krameṇodāharaṇam śirīṣādapi mṛdvaṅgī kveyamāyatalocanā ।
ayaṃ kva ca kukūlāgnikarkaśo madanānalaḥ ।।
siṃhikāsutasaṃtrastaḥ śaśaḥ śītāśumāśritaḥ ।
jagrase sāśrayaṃ tatra tamanyaḥ siṃhikāsutaḥ ।।
sadyaḥ karasparśamavāpya citraṃ raṇe raṇe yasya kṛpāṇalekhā ।
tamālanīlā śaradindupāṇḍu yaśastrilokyābharaṇaṃ prasūtai ।।
ānandamamandamimaṃ kuvalayadalalocane dadāsi tvam ।
virahastvayaiva janitastāpayatitarāṃ śarīraṃ me ।।
atrānandadānaṃ śalīlatāpena virudhyate ।
evam vipulena sāgaraśayasya kukṣiṇā bhuvanāni yasya papire yugakṣaye ।
madavibhramāsakalayā pape punaḥ sa purastriyaikatamayaikayā dṛśā ।।
ityādāvapi viṣamatvaṃ yathāyogamavagantavyam ।।
