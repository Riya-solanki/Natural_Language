tadanu pātanaśuddhaṃ sūtakam āroṭam aśnīyāt ।
svedanamūrcchotthāpanapātanarodhāśca niyamaśca ।।
abhrakasahitaḥ pātyo vidhinā yāvatsthiro bhavati ।
athavā mākṣikasahitaḥ pātyaḥ sūto vidhānena ।।
ityāroṭaḥ sūtaḥ kṣetrīkaraṇe niyujyate prathamam ।
atha rasayogamāha tadityādi ।।
tadanu pathyamātropayogānantaraṃ pātanaśuddhaṃ pātanena śuddhaṃ sūtakaṃ āroṭam eva sāmānyam evāśnīyāt bhakṣayet ।।
ca punaḥ svedanamūrchotthāpanarodhāśca svedanaṃ ca mūrchā ca utthāpanaṃ ca pātanāni ca nirodhaśceti dvandvaḥ ete yadyapi santi niyamaśca yadyapyasti tathāpyāroṭaḥ pātanena syād ityarthaḥ ।।
prasaṅgataḥ pātanamāha abhraketyādi ।।
sūtaḥ abhrakasahitaḥ gaganamilitaḥ san pātyaḥ ।।
kena vidhānena ḍamarukayantrādinā ।।
punastāvadyāvatsthiro bhavati ।।
athavā vidhyantare sūto mākṣikasahitaḥ pātyo vidhānena ayamapi yāvatsthiro bhavati ।।
rasāyane sūtasya āroṭādividhānamāha itītyādi ।।
āroṭa iti pūrvoktena pātanakarmaṇā ūrdhvādhastiryagbhavena sādhita āroṭaḥ saḥ prathamaṃ yathā syāttathā rasāyane jarāvyādhināśane niyujyate iti ।।
