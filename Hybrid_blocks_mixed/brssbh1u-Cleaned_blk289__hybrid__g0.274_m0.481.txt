viśeṣaṇaṃ ca vijñānātmaparamātmanoreva bhavati ।
aātmānaṃ rathinaṃ viddhi śarīraṃ rathameva tu' ityādinā pareṇa granthena rathirathādirūpakakalpanayā vijñānātnāṃ rathinaṃ saṃsāramokṣayorgantāraṃ kalpayati ।
aso adhvanaḥ pāramāpnoti tadviṣṇoḥ paramaṃ padam' ।
iti ca paramātmānaṃ gantavyam ।
tathā ataṃ durdarśa gūḍhamanupraviṣṭhaṃ purāṇam ।
adhyātmayogādhigamena devaṃ matvā dhīro harṣaśokau jahāti' iti pūrvasminnapi granthe mantṛmantavyatvenaitāveva viśeṣitau ।
prakaraṇaṃ ceda paramātmanaḥ ।
brahmavido vadanti iti ca vaktṛviśeṣopādanaṃ paramātmaparigrahe ghaṭate ।
tasmādiha jīvaparamātmānāvucyeyātām ।
eṣa eva nyāyaḥ advā suparṇā sayujā sakhāyā' ityevamādiṣvapi ।
tatrāpi hyadhyātmādhikārānna prākṛtau suparṇāvucyete ।
tayoranyaḥ pippalaṃ svāditti ityadanaliṅgādvijñānātmā bhavati ।
anaś nannanyo abhicākaśīti ityanaśanacetanābhyāṃ paramātmā ।
