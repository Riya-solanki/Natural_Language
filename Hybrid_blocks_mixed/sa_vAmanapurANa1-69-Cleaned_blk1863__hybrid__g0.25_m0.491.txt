skanda ityeva vikhyāto gaurīputro bhavatvasau ।
guha ityeva nāmnā ca mamāsau tanayaḥ smṛtaḥ ।।
māhāsena iti khyāto hutāśasyāstu putrakaḥ śāradvata iti khyātaḥ sutaḥ śaravaṇasya ca ।।
evameva mahāyogī pṛthivyāṃ khyātimeṣyati ।
ṣaḍāsyatvān mahābāhuḥ ṣaṇmukho nāma gīyate ।।
