utsannakarmabandhasya viṣayā astaṃ yānti ।।
prāṇāntaḥkaraṇasaṃyogāt na indriyāṇāṃ sphuraṇaṃ bhavet ।।
tathā ca nyāyaśāstre ātmā manasā saṃyujyate mana indriyeṇa indriyamarthenetīndriyāṇāṃ vastuprāpyaprakāśakāritvaniyamād iti ।।
ato hetośca duḥkhasukhe na sphurataḥ ।।
ātmanaḥ prakāśāt prāṇāntaḥkaraṇānāṃ prakāśaḥ prāṇāntaḥkaraṇāni tameva prakāśaṃ prāpyendriyāṇi prakāśayanti ।।
ata ubhayoḥ paraprakāśaḥ ।।
tadvad gahanatama agrāhyamandhakāraṃ cidbhinnaṃ prakāśena prakāśitaṃ syāditi ।।
abhāvapadārthatvād indriyatamasorjaḍatvāt sāmyam ।।
