punaśca janmāntarakarmayogāt sa eva jīvaḥ svapiti prabuddhaḥ ।
puratraye krīḍati yaśca jīvas tatas tu jātaṃ sakalaṃ vicitram ।।
dhāramānandam akhaṇḍabodhaṃ yasmiṃl layaṃ yāti puratrayaṃ ca ।।
etasmāj jāyate prāṇo manaḥ sarvendriyāṇi ca ।
khaṃ vāyur jyotir āpaḥ pṛthivī viśvasya dhāriṇī ।।
