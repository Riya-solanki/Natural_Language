anityaḥ śabdaḥ prayatnānantarīyakatvād ghaṭavadityukte apara āha prāgutpatteranutpanne śabde prayatnānantarīyakatvamanityatvakāraṇaṃ nāsti tadabhāvād nityatvaṃ prāptaṃ nityasya cotpattirnāsti।
anutpattyā pratyavasthānam anutpattisamaḥ ।।
