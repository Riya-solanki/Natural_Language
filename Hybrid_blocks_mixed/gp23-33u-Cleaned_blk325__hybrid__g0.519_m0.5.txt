[] tatra ca śastra-praśastānām iva teṣāṃ kṛta-hastakānāṃ punar lakṣyād acyava eva lakṣitaḥ । tathā hi-
hitvā hitvāpy atra hastāli-bandhaṃ tālālambā hastakāni pradarśya । gaurī-śyāmā lāghavāt pūrṇamānā rāsollāse tadvad āsan punaś ca ।। [śālinī]
puṣpa-vṛndam apatan muhur muhuḥ kṛṣṇa-gaura-tanu-maṇḍalīm anu । vṛṣṭir ūrdhvam adharatra nīra-bhṛd vidyud-ālir iti yatra citratā ।। [rathoddhatā]
[] atra devā devyaś ca bhavya-bhāva-bhavān navya-paribhavāt kamalākṣaṃ sākṣād iva sambodhayantas tathā yoga-māyāyā yogataḥ paraṃ sva-sva-yogyam antaḥ samadhigamya ramyam idaṃ vādya-kolāhalāvṛtam ujjaguḥ ।
