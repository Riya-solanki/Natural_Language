atra sūryastu diśasturaṅgamaissaptabhirākramati, tīvrān kiraṇānaṅgīkaroti, rahugrastatāṃ gacchati nakṣatrāṇāmanullāsāya sāntamudayaṃ dhatta iti dhvanirvidyate । tasmādgambhīratvam ।
prapañcoktistu vistaraḥ ।।
puṇyaiḥ pūrvamupārjitaistanumato janmāstu sanmānuṣaṃ janmāpyasya tadāndhradeśatilake rājācale saṃbhavet ।
tatrāpyeṣa dadhātu tāṃ pariṇatiṃ bhāgyasya yogyāṃ tathā gatvā nandati siṅgabhūvara giraṃ tvatkāṃ camatkāriṇīm ।।
