cakrabandho yathā
sakṣamācakradiśāvilāsivibhunā tigmāṃśuhāriśriyā viśrāmakṣaṇaviśvaraṅgaviyatā kalpaṃ yaśo jyotiṣā ।
vidvatkāntimavāpa yaḥ praviśadāvirbhūtakṛtsnāgamāṃ māṃdhātā sa ca tadviśeṣaviduṣo yāti tviṣā nopamām ।।
