atra "vā puṃsi padmaṃ nalinaṃ'; ityanuśāsane api kavibhiḥ puṃliṅge prāyeṇa na prayujyata iti padmā ityaprayuktamidam ।
padmaṃ tyaktamiti prathamārdhe, na cedasya gataśrīkairiti dvitīyārghe ca pāṭho ramaṇīyaḥ ।।
guṇātvamiṣyate tañjñerasyāpi yamakādiṣu ।।
yathā
yā paṅktirbrāhnaṇendrāṇāmāśāste sar vadaivatāḥ ।
śrī siṅgabhūpa dadhatāmāśāste sarvadaivatāḥ ।।
