atra hīnasādhanā vidviṣaḥ sālaśreṇiṃ prākārapaṅktimāśritya jīvanaṃ prāṇān tṛṇaṃ kurvate tṛṇāya manyanta ityarthaḥ । athavā sālaśreṇiṃ vāṭikāmāśritya hīnasādhanāḥ tṛṇaṃ jīvanaṃ prāṇadhāraṇaṃ kurvata iti ca nāsatpratipakṣatvamasatpratipakṣatvaṃ ve ati saṃśayapratīteḥ sasaṃśayamidam ।
utkarṣādivivakṣāyāmasyāpi guṇagauravam ।।
