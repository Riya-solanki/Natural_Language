apica naivātra vivaditavyaṃ, kiṃ brahmāsminvākye abhidhīyate na veti ।
sukhaviśiṣṭābhidhānameva brahmatvaṃ siddham ।
sukhaviśiṣṭaṃ hi brahma yadvākyopakrame prakrāntaṃ aprāṇo brahma kaṃ brahma khaṃ brahma' iti tadevehābhihitaṃ, prakṛtaparigrahasya nyāyyatvāt ।
aācāryastu te gatiṃ vaktā' iti ca gatimātrābhidhānapratijñānāt ।
