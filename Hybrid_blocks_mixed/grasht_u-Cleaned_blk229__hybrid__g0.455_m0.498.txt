mākṣikasatvāntarasaṃyogāt sattvasya guṇādhikyaṃ darśayannāha mākṣiketyādi ।।
gagane bhavaṃ gaganaṃ aṇpratyayāntaṃ ṇito vā iti sūtreṇa na vṛddhiḥ gaganaṃ abhrasatvam ।।
mākṣike bhavaṃ mākṣikaṃ mākṣikasatvaṃ tena sahitaṃ pūrvasatvaṃ cet dhmātaṃ tadubhayasatvaṃ mukhapradaṃ bhavati rasasya iti śeṣaḥ ।।
mukhaṃ prakṛṣṭena dadātīti samāsaḥ ।।
tadanu ca tatpaścācca nāgair vaṅgaiḥ sahitaṃ pūrvaṃ yadgaganaṃ abhrasatvaṃ mukhapradamityarthaḥ ।।
vā sukhapradaṃ iti pāṭhaḥ asmin yoge bahukleśaṃ vihāya sukhena cāraṇaṃ bhavatīti vikalpārthaḥ ।।
