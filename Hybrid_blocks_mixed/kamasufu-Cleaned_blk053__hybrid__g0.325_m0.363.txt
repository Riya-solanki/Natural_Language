nāgarakaḥ saha mitra-janena paricārakaiś ca kṛta-puṣpopahāre saṃcārita-surabhi-dhūpe raty-āvāse prasādhite vāsa-gṛhe kṛta-snāna-prasādhanāṃ yuktyāpītāṃ striyaṃ sāntvanaiḥ punaḥ pānena copakramet dakṣiṇataś cāsyā upaveśanam keśa-haste vastrānte nīvyām ity avalambanam raty-arthaṃ savyena bāhunānuddhataḥ pariṣvaṅgaḥ
pūrva-prakaraṇa-saṃbaddhaiḥ parihāsānurāgair vacobhir anuvṛttiḥ gūḍhāślīlānāṃ ca vastūnāṃ samasyayā paribhāṣaṇam
sanṛttaṃ anṛtaṃ vā gītaṃ vāditram kalāsu saṃkathāḥ punaḥ pānenopacchandanam
jātānurāgāyāṃ kusumānulepana-tāmbūla-dānena ca śeṣa-jana-visṛṣṭiḥ vijane ca yathoktair āliṅganādibhir enām uddharṣayet tato nīvī-viśleṣaṇādi yathoktam upakrameta
ity ayaṃ ratārambhaḥ
ratāvasānikaṃ rāgam ativāhyāsaṃstutayor iva savrīḍayoḥ parasparam apaśyatoḥ pṛthak pṛthag ācāra-bhūmi-gamanam pratinivṛttya cāvrīḍāyamānayor ucita-deśopaviṣṭayos tāmbūla-grahaṇam acchī-kṛtaṃ candanam anyad vānulepanaṃ tasyā gātre svayam eva niveśayet
savyena bāhunā caināṃ parirabhya caṣaka-hastaḥ sāntvayan pāyayet jalānupānaṃ vā khaṇḍaka-khādyakam anyad vā prakṛti-sātmya-yuktam ubhāv apy upayuñjīyātām
accha-rasaka-yūṣam amla-yavāgūṃ bhṛṣṭa-māṃsopadaṃśāni pānakāni cūta-phalāni śuṣka-māṃsaṃ mātuluṅga-cukrakāṇi saśarkarāṇi ca yathādeśa-sātmyaṃ ca tatra madhuram idaṃ mṛdu viśadam iti ca vidaśya vidaśya tat tad upahāret
harmya-tala-sthitayor vā candrikāsevanārtham āsanam tatrānukūlābhiḥ kathābhir anuvarteta tad-aṅka-saṃlīnāyāś candramasaṃ paśyantyā nakṣatra-paṅkti-vyaktī karaṇam arundhatī-dhruva-saptarṣi-mālā-darśanaṃ ca
iti ratāvasānikam
tatraitad bhavati
avasāne api ca prītir upacārair upaskṛtā savisrambha-kathā-yogai ratiṃ janayate parām
paraspara-prīti-karair ātma-bhāvānuvartanaiḥ kṣaṇāt krodha-parāvṛttaiḥ kṣaṇāt prīti-vilokitaiḥ
hallīsaka-krīḍanakair gāyanair lāṭa-rāsakaiḥ rāga-lolārdra-nayanaiś candra-maṇḍala-vīkṣṇaiḥ
ādye saṃdarśane jāte pūrvaṃ ye syur manorathāḥ punar-viyoge duḥkhaṃ ca tasya sarvasya kīrtanaiḥ
kīrtanānte ca rāgeṇa pariṣvaṅgaiḥ sacumbanaiḥ tais taiś ca bhāvaiḥ saṃyukto yūno rāgo vivardhate
