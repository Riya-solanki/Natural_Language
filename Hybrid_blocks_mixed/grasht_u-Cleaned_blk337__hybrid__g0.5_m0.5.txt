sūtakarmaṇo durbodhatvādgurupādaṃ stuvannāha sūtetyādi ।।
yathā yena prakāreṇa sūtavaraṃ pāradaḥ lakṣayate jñāyate karmakṛteti śeṣaḥ punaryathā bījaṃ upekṣatāṃ na yāti samyak milati tadvattenaiva prakāreṇa gurupādanirdiṣṭaṃ karma ācāryavaryadarśitaṃ pūjyaṃ saṃskārarūpaṃ vidhinā ācāryoktavidhānena kuryāt ।।
gurupādalakṣaṇam ।
sarvaśāstraviśeṣajñaḥ kuśalo rasakarmaṇi ।
evaṃlakṣaṇasaṃyukto rasavidyāgururbhavet ।
iti ।।
iti garbhadrutijāraṇaprakaraṇam ।।
