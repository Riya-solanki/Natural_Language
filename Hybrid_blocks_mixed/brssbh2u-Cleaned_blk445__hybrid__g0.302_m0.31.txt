nanu śamavidhānārthametadvākyam, atajjalāniti śānta upāsīta' iti śruteḥ, naitatsṛṣṭivākyaṃ, tasmādetanna pradeśāntarasiddhaṃ kramamuparoddhumarhatīti ।
atattejo asṛjata' ityetatsṛṣṭivākyam ।
tasmādatra yathāśruti kramo grahītavya iti ।
