svādhyāyasya tathātvena hi samācāre adhikārāc ca savavac ca tanniyamaḥ ।
yairātharvaṇikagranthopāyā vidyā veditavyāṃ teṣāmeva śirovratapūrvādhyayanaprāptagranthabodhitā phalaṃ prayacchati nānyathā ।
anyeṣāṃ tu chāndogyādīnāṃ saiva vidyā cīrṇaśirovratānāṃ phaladetyātharvaṇagranthādhyayanasaṃbandhādavagamyate ।
tatsaṃbandhaśca vedavrateneti anaitadacīrṇavrato adhīte'iti samāmnānādavagamyate ।
ateṣāmevaitāṃ brahmavidyāṃ vadeta'iti vidyāsaṃyoge apyetāmiti prakṛtaparāmarśinā sarvanāmnādhyayanasaṃbandhāvirodhādātharvavihitaiva vidyocyata iti ।
savā homāḥ sapta sauryādayaḥ śataudanāntā atharvaṇikānāṃ ta ekasminnevārtharvaṇike agnau kriyante na tretāyām ।।
