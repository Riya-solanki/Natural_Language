bādhanā pīḍā tāpa iti।
tayānubaddhamanaṣaktamavinirbhāgena vartamānaṃ duḥkhayogād duḥkhamiti।
so ayaṃ sarvaṃ duḥkhenānubaddhamiti paśyan duḥkhaṃ jihāsurjanmani duḥkhadarśī nirvidyate nirviṇṇo virajyate virakto vimucyate ।।
