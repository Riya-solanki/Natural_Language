vidhyantaramāha athavetyādi ।।
darado hiṅgulaḥ śilā manohvā ālaṃ haritālaṃ taiḥ gandhakaḥ pratītaḥ mākṣikaṃ yatpakvaṃ sindūrīkṛtaṃ mṛtanāgaśca taiḥ ।।
punaḥ kaṅkuṣṭhapravālasahitaiḥ kaṅkuṣṭhaṃ viraṅgaṃ pravālaṃ vidrumaṃ tābhyāṃ sahitaiḥ ।।
punaretaiḥ kaṅguṇītaile jyotiṣmatītaile piṣṭaiś cūrṇīkṛtaiḥ madhye sūto yuktaḥ kārya ityagrimaślokasaṃbandhāt ।।
taccāha madhye ityādi ।।
pūrvoktairauṣadhaiḥ kṛtvā madhye auṣadhāntaḥ sūto yuktaḥ kāryaḥ ।।
punarmardayitvā vaṃśanalikāṃ riktodarāṃ prati saṃvedya dinatrayaṃ dolāyantreṇa sveditaṃ kuryādityarthaḥ ।।
taccāha liptvetyādi ।।
etāni sveditauṣadhāni saṃyuktāni taiḥ kṛṣṇaiḥ kṛṣṇalavaṇaiḥ pūrvoktavidhānena sūtakṛṣṭīvidhānena patraṃ liptvā punaḥ prakaṭaṃ yathā syāt tathā stokaṃ alpamalpaṃ krameṇa nāgaṃ dattvā kanakaṃ jāyate ityagrimaślokasaṃbandhaḥ ।।
evaṃ kṛte sati akṣīṇaṃ akṣayaṃ divyaṃ pravaraṃ devayogyaṃ devā indrādayastadyogyaṃ kanakaṃ bhavati ।।
yukto'yamarthaḥ ।।
evaṃ jāritasūte jāritakarmakṛte rase khalu niścitaṃ sarvā haṇḍikāḥ sarve dhātvādyāḥ sakalāḥ saprasavāḥ syurityarthaḥ ।।
