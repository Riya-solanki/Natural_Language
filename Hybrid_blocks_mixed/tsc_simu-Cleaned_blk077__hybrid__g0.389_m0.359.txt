syādetat ।
antaḥkaraṇasya vibhutve paricchinnavṛttilābhasyāvaraṇenopapattāvapi lokāntaragamanādikaṃ nopapadyate ।
ata eva sāṃkhyasūtram "na vyāpakaṃ manaḥ karaṇatvā"diti ।
tadgatiśruteriti veti kiṃ caivaṃ sati lāghavāt caitanyasyaivāvaraṇakalpanamucitaṃ kimarthaṃ vibhvantaḥkaraṇaṃ parikalpyate tatra jñānapratibandhakamāvaraṇaṃ kalpyate ।।
