dyotate divi candro asau jīrṇe'bhre kāntimattayā ।
tatheha tāmupāśritya śālate manujeṣu ca ।।
susiddhabiḍadhātvādijāraṇena rasasya hi ।
pītādirāgajananaṃ rañjanaṃ parikīrtitam ।
iti paribhāṣā ।।
abhrasya chāyāviśeṣam āha jīrṇābhraka ityādi ।।
rasendro yadā jīrṇābhro bhavati tadā ghanānurūpiṇīṃ jīrṇābhrasamavarṇāṃ chāyāṃ darśayati ।।
kṛṣṇe jīrṇe kṛṣṇāṃ rakte'bhre jīrṇe raktāṃ pīte pītāṃ tathā site śubhre sitāṃ evaṃ caturvidhāṃ chāyāṃ darśayati ।।
punardvayostrayāṇāṃ vā caturṇāṃ saṃkare melāpe sati dvitricaturṇām anurūpiṇīṃ chāyāṃ darśayatītyarthaḥ ।।
abhrayogād varṇaviśeṣamāha kṛṣṇetyādi ।।
kṛṣṇābhrakeṇa jīrṇena raso balavān bhavet tu punaḥ asitarāgaiḥ kṛṣṇarāgairyujyate ।।
tathā śvetena śvetai rāgair yujyate raktena raktaiḥ pītena pītaiḥ kapilāgnerdivyasyāpi sarvasyādarśanarūpaṃ kṣayakarasya balairdravyāṇāṃ tādṛśo varṇo rasavarṇato varṇakovidairjñeyaḥ ।।
