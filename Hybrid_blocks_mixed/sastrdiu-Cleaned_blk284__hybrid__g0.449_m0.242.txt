kiṃ ca prayatnavagātmasaṃyogānantaraṃ yugapadeva haste prayatnavadātmasaṃyukte tatsaṃyuktecāṅgade aaṅgulīyake ca tallagne maṇau ca karmotpadyate, athaivamādiṣu na codanāt na prayatnavadātmasaṃyogācca tadanantare ।
tathātmasaṃyogāddhaste, tatsaṃyogādaṅgade, tatsaṃyogānmaṇāvapīti ।
evaṃ tarhyatrāpi śakyate vaktum ।
