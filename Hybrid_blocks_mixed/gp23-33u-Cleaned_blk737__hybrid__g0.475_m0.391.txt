kiṃ bhoktavyaṃ kvāsitavyaṃ svapanīyaṃ kva vāmunā ।
iti mātrādi-dhī-yātrā nātrāyāsyati tṛptatām ।।
sakhāyante sukhādhīnā vismariṣyanti sarvakam ।
smariṣyanti tu tāṃ tvat-kāṃ punaḥ saṃśleṣa-navyatām ।।dāsāś ca tava dāśārha punar āśāṃ sva-mūrtiṣu ।
dhāsyanti sevā-sudhayā vidhāsyanti yad āplavam ।।gāvaḥ prāgvad bhavat-sphūrti-prabhāva-sthita-tad-daśāḥ ।
tadvad eva sadaivāmūr dhāsyanti pramadaṃ tvayi ।।śiśavaḥ paśavaś caiva vayāṃsi ca payāṃsi ca ।
ye' nye hṛdyā janā hṛdyās tava teṣāṃ tu kā kathā ।।
