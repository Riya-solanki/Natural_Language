nirmukhatvenābhrakacāraṇopāyamāha niścandrikam ityādi ।।
hi niścitaṃ yadgaganamabhrakaṃ niścandrikaṃ candrarahitaṃ bhavati vajrasaṃjñakam ityarthaḥ tadgaganaṃ rucirair nirdoṣair manoramair vividhaiḥ kṣārāmlair bhāvitaṃ plāvitaṃ kāryaṃ kṣārā yavakṣārasvarjikṣāraṭaṅkaṇakṣārādayo amlā amlavetasajambīrādyāḥ pūrvoktāḥ ।।
nanu kṣārā rucirāḥ kathaṃ bhavanti ucyate yathā sarjikācūrṇabhāgaikaṃ viṃśadbhāgaṃ jalasya ca ।।
tāvatkvāthyaṃ kṣiped bhāṇḍe yāvatphenaṃ sitaṃ bhavet ।।
kṣīṇe kṣīṇe jalaṃ dattvā śvetaphenaṃ ca gṛhyate ।।
tadā tu ḍekayantreṇa drāvayedagniyogataḥ ।।
triḥsaptavāraṃ kartavyaṃ drāvaṇaṃ mūtrasaṃyutam ।।
svarjikākṣāranāmāyaṃ drāvaṇe paramo mataḥ ।।
iti viśeṣavidhiḥ ।।
evamatyuttamāḥ kṣārāḥ syuḥ sampakvāḥ himāḥ ।।
punaḥ sṛṣṭitrayanīrakaṇātumbarurasamarditaṃ go'jāvinārīṇāṃ mūtraṃ śukraṃ ca śoṇitaṃ sṛṣṭitrayaṃ nīrakaṇā jalapippalī paṭuriti loke tumburu pratītaṃ jalakaṇā ca tumbaruśca anayo rasaḥ sṛṣṭitrayaṃ ca jalakaṇātumbarurasaśca tābhyāṃ mardanaṃ kāryam ।।
rasaḥ pāradaḥ nirmukho'pi evaṃvidhaṃ gaganaṃ carati grāsīkaroti ।।
karaṇaṃ granthāntare'sti ।
dugdhatrayaṃ kumāryambu gaṅgāputraṃ trimūtrakam ।
vaṭaśuṅgamajāraktam ebhir abhraṃ sumarditam ।।
śatadhā puṭitaṃ cāpi jāyate padmarāgavat ।
niścandrikaṃ mṛtaṃ tv abhraṃ vṛddhadehe rasāyanam ।।
kāminīmadadarpaghnaṃ śastaṃ puṃstvopaghātinām ।
iti ।।
