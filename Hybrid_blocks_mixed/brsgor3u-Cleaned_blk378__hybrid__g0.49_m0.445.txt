bahistūbhayathāpi ।
kṛtaprāyaścittaistaiḥ saha kṛtaśravaṇādikaṃ jñānasādhanaṃ na veti saṃdehe teṣāṃ śuddhatvātsādhanamiti prāpte prāyaścittātparaloke teṣāṃ śuddhatve apyatra śuddhabhāvānna sādhanamiti siddhāntayati-yadyūrdhveti ।
sugamaṃ bhāṣyam ।।
