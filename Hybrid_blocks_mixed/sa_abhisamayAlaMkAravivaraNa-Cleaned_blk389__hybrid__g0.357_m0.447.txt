bhavaśāntyoḥ samatā asya samatāprayogasya lakṣaṇādim abhisamayālaṅkāre ekayā kārikayā pratipāditam । tathā hi - 
svapnopamatvād dharmāṇāṃ bhavaśāntyorakalpanā ।
karmābhāvādicodyānāṃ parihārā yathoditāḥ ।।
