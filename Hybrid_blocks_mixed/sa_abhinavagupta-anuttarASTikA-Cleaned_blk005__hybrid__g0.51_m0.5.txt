ānando atra na vittamadhyamadavan naivāṅganāsaṅgavat dīpārkendukṛtaprabhāprakaravan naiva prakāśodayaḥ ।
harṣaḥ saṃbhṛtabhedamuktisukhabhūr bhārāvatāropamaḥ sarvādvaitapadasya vismṛtanidheḥ prāptiḥ prakāśodayaḥ ।।
