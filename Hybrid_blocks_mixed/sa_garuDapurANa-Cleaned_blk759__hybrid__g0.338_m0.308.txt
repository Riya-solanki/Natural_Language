śrīgaruḍamahāpurāṇam- sūta uvāca । gopālapūjāṃ vakṣyāmi bhuktimuktipradāyinīm । dvāre dhātā vidhātā ca gaṅgāyamunayā saha ।। । 
śaṅkhapadmanidhī caiva sāraṅgaḥ śarabhaḥ śriyā ।
pūrve bhadraḥ subhadro dvau dakṣe caṇḍapracaṇḍakau ।।
