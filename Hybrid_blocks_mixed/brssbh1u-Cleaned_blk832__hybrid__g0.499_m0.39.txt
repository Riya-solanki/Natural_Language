naiṣa doṣaḥ ।
viśeṣavijñānavināśābhiprāyametadvināśābhidhānaṃ nātmocchedābhiprāyam ।
aatraiva mā bhagavānamūmuhanna pretya saṃjñāsti' iti paryanuyujya svayameva śrutyārthāntarasya darśitatvāt- ana vā are ahaṃ mohaṃ bravīmyavināśī vā are ayamātmānucchittidharmā mātrāsaṃsargastvasya bhavati' iti ।
etaduktaṃ bhavati- kūṭasthanitya evāyaṃ vijñānaghana ātmā nāsyocchedaprasaṅgo asti ।
mātrābhistvasya bhūtendriyalakṣaṇābhiravidyākṛtābhirasaṃsargo vidyayā bhavati ।
saṃsargābhāve ca prakṛtasya viśeṣavijñānasyābhāvānna pretya saṃjñāstītyuktamiti ।
yadapyuktam- avijñātāramare kena vijānīyāt' iti kartṛvacanena śabdenopasaṃhārādvijñānātmana evedaṃ draṣṭavyamiti, tadapi kāśakṛtsnīyenaiva darśanena parihapaṇīyam ।
apica ayatra hi dvaitamiva bhavati taditara itaraṃ paśyati' ityārabhyāvidyāviṣaye tasyaiva darśanādilakṣaṇaṃ viśeṣajñānaṃ prapañcya ayatra tvasya sarvāmātmaivābhūttatkena kaṃ paśyet' ityādinā vidyāviṣaye tasyaiva darśanādilakṣaṇasya viśeṣavijñānasyābhāvamabhidadhāti ।
punaśca viṣayābhāve api ātmānaṃ vijānīyāt ityāśaṅkya avijñātāramare kena vijānīyāt' ityāha ।
tataśca viśeṣavijñānābhāvopapādānaparatvādvākyasya vijñānadhātureva kevalaḥ saṃnbhūtapūrvagatyā kartṛvacanena tṛcā nirdiṣṭa iti gamyate ।
darśitaṃ tu purastātkāśakṛtsnīyasya pakṣasya śrutimattvam ।
ataśca vijñānātparamātmanoravidyāpratyupasthāpitanāmarūparacitadehādyupādhinim itto bhedo na pāramārthika ityeṣor'thaḥ sarvairvedāntavādibhirabhyupagantavyaḥ ।
asadeva somyedamagra āsīdekamevādvitīyam' aātmaivedaṃ sarvam' ,' brahmaivedaṃ sarvam', aidaṃ sarvaṃ yadayamātmā', anānyo ato asti draṣṭā'
