apare tu prapañcavilayamapavargamāhuḥ ।
avidyānirmito hi prapañcaḥ svapnaprapañcavat prabodhenaiva brahmavidyayā avidyāyāṃ vilīnāyāṃ svayameva vilīyate ।
tathāhi śrutiḥ yatra hi dvaitamiva bhavati taditara itaraṃ paśyati yatra tvasya sarvāmātmaivābhūttatkena kaṃ paśyet
