Vṛtti:
tad liṅgaṃ bubhukṣoḥ bhogecchoḥ [itarasya vā] anyasya vā munerarcane prabhavatīti। yataḥ sarvajñatvamato akhilakartṛprasaram। tathā bāhulyena śailalohamṛdratnadāruprakṛtijaṃ praśastam। kvaccittu svaśāstrāmnātānyaprakṛtijamapi bhāvyam।।
prādhānyātprathamasya ratnajasya mānamāha
