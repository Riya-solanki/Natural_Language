yadapyuktam, adaharo asminnantarākāśaḥ' ityākāśasyālpatvaṃ śrūyamāṇaṃ parameśvare nopapadyate, jīvasya tvārāgropanimitasyālpatvamavakalpata iti tasya parihāro vaktavyaḥ ।
ukto hyasya parihāraḥ parameśvarasyāpekṣikamalpatvamavakalpata iti aarbhakaukastvāttadvyapadeśācca neti cenna nicāyyatvādevaṃ vyomavacca' ityatra ।
sa eveha parihāro anusaṃdhātavya iti sūcayati ।
śrutyaiva cedamalpatvaṃ pratyuktaṃ prasiddhenākāśenopamimānayā ayāvānvā ayamākāśastāvāneṣo antarhṛdaya ākāśaḥ' iti ।।
