yadi aeṣa ātmāpahatapāpmā'ityādivākyaśeṣabalena daharaḥ parastarhi jīvo apītyāśaṅkya niṣedhati-itareti ।
jīvasyāpi vākyaśeṣamāha-atheti ।
daharoktyanantaraṃ muktopasṛpyaṃ śuddhaṃ brahmocyate ।
ya eṣa saṃprasādo jīvo asmātkāryakaraṇasaṃghātātsamyagutthāya ātmānaṃ tasmādvivicya viviktamātmānaṃ svena brahmarūpeṇābhiniṣpadya sākṣātkṛtya tadeva pratyakparaṃ jyotirūpasaṃpadyate prāpnotīti vyākhyeyam ।
