itaśca parameśvara eva vaiśvānaraḥ, yasmātparameśvarasyaivāgnirāsyaṃ dyaurmūrdhetīdṛśaṃ trailokyātmakaṃ rūpaṃ smaryate- ayasyāgnirāsyaṃ dyaurmūrdhā khaṃ nābhiścaraṇau kṣitiḥ ।
sūryaścakṣurdiśaḥ śrotraṃ tasmai lokātmane namaḥ ।।
a iti ।
etatsmaryamāṇaṃ rūpaṃ mūlabhūtāṃ śrutimanumāpayadasya vauśvānaraśabdasya parameśvaraparatve anumānaṃ liṅgaṃ gamakaṃ syādityarthaḥ ।
itiśdo hetvarthaḥ ।
yasmādidaṃ gamakaṃ tasmādapi vaiśvānaraḥ paramātmaivetyarthaḥ ।
yadyapi stutiriyaṃ atasmai lokātmane namaḥ' iti ।
stutitvamapi nāsati mūlabhūte vedavākye samāyagīdṛśena rūpeṇa saṃbhavati ।
adyāṃ mūrdhānaṃ yasya viprā vadanti khaṃ vai nābhiṃ candrasūryau ca netre ।
diśaḥ śrotre viddhi pādau kṣitiṃ ca so acintyātmā sarvabhūtapraṇetā ।।
aityevañjātīyakā ca smṛtirihodāhartavyā ।।
