vārivahnyanilākāśairvṛtaṃ bhūtādinā ca tat ।
tadaṇḍaṃ mahatā rudra ! pradhānena ca veṣṭitam ।।
aṇḍaṃ daśaguṇaṃ vyāptaṃ nārāyaṇaḥ sthitaḥ ।।
iti gāruḍe mahāpurāṇe pūrvakhaṇḍe prathamāṃśākhye ācārakāṇḍe bhuvanakośagatāpātalanarakādinirūpaṇaṃ nāma saptapañcāśattamo adhyāyaḥ
