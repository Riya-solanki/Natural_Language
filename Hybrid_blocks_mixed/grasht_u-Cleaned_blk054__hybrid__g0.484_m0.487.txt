yasya jīvasya yogavaśātsaṃvittirjātā sa kīdṛśa ityāha galitetyādi ।।
galitānalpavikalpa iti galito dūrībhūto analpo bahutaro vikalpo mithyājñānaṃ yasya īdṛk syāt ।।
punaḥ kīdṛk sarvārthavivarjitaḥ sarve ca te arthāśca tair vivarjitāḥ samyagrahito bhavati kāryāṇāṃ smaraṇakaraṇayorabhāva ityarthaḥ ।।
punaḥ kathaṃbhūtaḥ cidānandaś cidā prakāśena ānandaḥ sukhasampattir yasya sa tathoktaḥ ।।
prakāśahetunā ānandatā bhavet jaḍahetunā tadviparyayaḥ ।।
īdṛk saḥ sphurito'pi prakāśamāno'pi asphuritatanorjantuvargasya aprakāśaśarīrasya jīvasamūhasya kiṃ karoti pṛcchāṃ karoti ।।
kimiti pṛcchājugupsayoḥ iti prasādaḥ ।।
pṛcchati ca hasati ca roditi pramattavan mānavo'pi tallīna iti ।।
