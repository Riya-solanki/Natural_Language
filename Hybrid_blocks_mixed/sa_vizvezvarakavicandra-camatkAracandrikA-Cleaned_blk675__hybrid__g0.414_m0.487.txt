vastusvabhāvaparāhato aheturyathā
rūkṣāhaṃkṛtayo na santi lalite bhedaṃ gate na bhruvau dṛkkoṇo api na rāgameti hasitaṃ mandaṃ ca nāntarhitam ।
dṛṣṭvāpi pratigarjatāṃ kṣitibhujāṃ saṃraṃbhagarbhakriyā mūrtirdāruṇaceṣṭayāpyavikṛtā śrīsiṅgapṛthvīpate ।।
