candanabindoḥ pratyakṣato alpīyastvaṃ buddhvā yuktā kalpanā bhavati, yasya tu saṃdigdhamaṇutvaṃ sarvāṅgīṇaṃ ca kāryamupalabhyate tasya vyāpitvamautsargikamapahāya neyaṃ kalpanāvakāśaṃ labhata iti śaṅkārthaḥ ।
naca haricandanabindudṛṣṭāntenāṇatvānumānaṃ jīvasya, pratidṛṣṭāntasaṃbhavenānaikāntikatvādityāha-na cātrānumānamiti ।
śaṅkāmimāmapākaroti-atrocyata iti ।
yadyapi pūrvoktābhiḥ śrutibhiraṇutvaṃ siddhamātmanastathāpi vaibhavācchrutyantaramupanyastam ।।
