uktaṃ pratyakṣādīnāmanimittatvam ।
tata eva ।
tanmūlapuruṣavacanasyāpyanimittatvam ।
evaṃ tarhi śabdasya sarvatra pramāṇāntarāpekṣayaiva prāmāṇyadarśanāttadabhāve tasyāpyaprāmāṇyādabhāvagamyatvamevāyātaṃ dharmasyetyāśaṅkānirākaraṇāya codanāyāḥ prāmāṇyamucyate ।
satyam ।
loke pramāṇāntaramūlānāṃ prāmāṇyaṃ atanmūlānāṃ cāprāmāṇyaṃ dṛśyate, nanvemapi sāpekṣaṃ prāmāṇyaṃ kintu svata eva ।
anāptavākyasya tvaprāmāṇyaṃ na mūlavirahāt kintu duṣṭamūlatayā śabdasya duṣṭatvātsvābhīvikasya prāmāṇyasyāpavādāt ।
