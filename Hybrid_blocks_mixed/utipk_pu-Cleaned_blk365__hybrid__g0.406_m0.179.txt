tatra svasṛṣṭedaṃbhāge buddhyādigrāhakātmanā ।
ahaṃkāraparāmarśa- padam nītam anena tat ।।
maheśvarasya jṛmbhāmaye asmin nirgate tasminn idaṃtāparāmarśe grāhyaṃ yan nirmitaṃ buddhiḥ prāṇo atha śūnyaṃ tad vedyaikadeśarūpam ahaṃkārāvamṛśyatāpādanena paricchinnagrāhakīkṛtam ।।
