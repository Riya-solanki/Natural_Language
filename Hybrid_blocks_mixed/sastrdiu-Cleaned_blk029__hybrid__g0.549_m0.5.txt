bhavatu māṇavakasaṃskārastaddvāreṇa tvadhyāranāṅgaṃ nādhyayanāṅgamiticet, maivaṃ, antaraṅgatvādadhyayanasya tadarthyameva saṃskārasya yuktam ।
adhyayanamapi phalavadadhyāpanamapi tayoḥ kasyāyaṃ māṇavakasaṃskāro aṅgaṃ bhavatvityapekṣāyāmadhyāpanasya puruṣāntaragāmitvena bahiraṅgatvānmāṇavakagatādhyayanārthatvameva yuktam ।
yathaiva hyadhyayanasyācāryakasiddhiprayojanatāmanāhṛtya vedamadhītya snāyād' iti smṛtiṃ bādhitvāntaraṅgatvādarthajñānārthataivāśrīyate, tathopanayanasyāpyadhyayanārthatā veditavyā ।
