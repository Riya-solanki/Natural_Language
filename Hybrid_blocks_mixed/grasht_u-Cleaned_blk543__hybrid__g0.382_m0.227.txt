vidhyantaramāha kadalītyādi ।।
tāpyaṃ mākṣikaṃ kadalīrasaśatabhāvitamadhvairaṇḍatailaparipakvam iti prathamaṃ rambhādraveṇa śatavāraṃ bhāvitaṃ paścāt madhvairaṇḍatailābhyāṃ saha paripakvaṃ samyak pācitaṃ sat satvaṃ muñcati ।।
kaiḥ kṛtvā trisaṃtāpaiḥ trivāraṃ dhamanaiḥ ।।
evaṃ rasakaṃ kharparakamapi satvaṃ muñcatīti ।।
