na ।
udāharaṇopāttenāpyupamānena saṃbandhopapatteḥ ।
atra prāṇotpattivādivākyajātamujadāharaṇam- aetasmādātmanaḥ sarve prāṇāḥ sarve lokāḥ sarve devāḥ sarvāṇi bhūtāni vyuccaranti' ityevañjātīyakam ।
tatra yathā lokādayaḥ parasmādbrahmaṇa utpadyante tathā prāṇā apītyarthaḥ ।
tathā- aetasmājjāyate prāṇo manaḥ sarvendriyāṇi ca khaṃ vāyurjyotirāpaḥ pṛthivī viśvasya dhāriṇī' ityevamādiṣvapi khādivatprāṇānāmutpattiriti draṣṭavyam ।
athavā apānavyāpacca tadvat' ityevamādiṣu vyavahitopamānasaṃbandhasyāpyāśritatvāt ।
yathātītānantarapādādyuktā viyadādayaḥ parasya brahmaṇo vikārāḥ samadhigatāstathā prāṇā api parasya brahmaṇo vikārā iti yojayitavyam ।
kaḥ punaḥ prāṇānāṃ vikāratve hetuḥ ।
śrutatvameva ।
