vyāghraḥ śarīrabhede apyātmānamabhinnamanubhavatīti nāhaṅkārālambanaṃ dehaḥ ।
ata eva nendriyāṇyapyasyālambanam, indriyabhede api ayo ahamadrākṣaṃ sa evaitarhi spṛśāmi'ityahamālambanasya pratyabhijñānāt ।
viṣayebhyastvasya vivekaḥ sthavīyāneva ।
buddhimanasośca karaṇayoḥ aaham'iti kartṛpratibhāsaprakhyānā
