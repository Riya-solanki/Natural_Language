ityatrānucarīti na yuktaḥ । ikāralakṣaṇābhāvāt । tat katham ? । āha careṣṭitvāt । pacādiṣu caraḍiti paṭhyate ।।
kesarālamityalateraṇi ।।
kesarālaṃ śilīndhraṃ ityatra kesarālamiti katham ? । āha alateraṇi । alabhūṣaṇaparyāptivāraṇeṣu ityasmāddhātoḥ kesaraśabde karmaṇyupapade, karmaṇyaṇ ityanenāṇi sati kesarālamiti siddhyati ।।
patralamiti lāteḥ ke ।।
patralaṃ vanamidaṃ virājate ityatra patralamiti katham ? । āha lāteḥ ke । lā ādāne ityetasmāddhātorādānārthāt patraśabde karmaṇyupapade, āto'nupasarge kaḥ iti kapratyaye satīti ।।
mahīdhrādayo mūlavibhujādidarśanāt ।।
mahīdhra-dharaṇīdhrādayaḥ śabdāḥ mūlavibhujādidarśanāt kapratyaye satīti । garhīṃ dharatīti mahīdhra iti । evamādayo'nye'pi draṣṭavyāḥ ।।
brahmādiṣu hanterniyamādarihādyasiddhiḥ ।।
brahmādiṣūpapadeṣu hanteḥ kvibvidhau brahmabhrūṇavṛtreṣu ityatrārihāripuhā ityevamādīnāmasiddhiḥ । niyamāt । brahmādiṣveva, hantereva, kvibeva, bhūtakāla eveti caturvidhaścātra niyamaḥ iti niyamānyataraviṣayo nirūpyaḥ ।।
