naiṣa doṣaḥ ।
ayadataḥ paro jyotiḥ' iti prathamatarapaṭhitena yacchabdena sarvanāmnā dyusaṃbnandhātpratyabhijñāyamāne pūrvavākyanirdiṣṭe brahmaṇi svasāmārthyena parāmṛṣṭe satyarthājjyotiḥśabdasyāpi brahmaviṣayatvopapatteḥ ।
tasmādiha jyotiriti brahma pratipattavyam ।।
