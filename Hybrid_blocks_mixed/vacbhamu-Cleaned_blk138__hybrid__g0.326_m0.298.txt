kratvarthāppraṇayanāśrite godohane adhikāraḥ ।
no khalu godohanadravyamavyāpriyamāṇaṃ sākṣātpaśūn bhāvayitumarhati ।
na ca vyāpārāntarāviṣṭaṃ śrūyate yatastadaṅgakramamatipatet; appraṇayanāśritaṃ tu pratīyate, acamasenāpaḥ praṇayedgodohanena paśukāmāsya' iti samabhivyāhārāt, yogyatvāccāsyāpāṃ praṇayanaṃ prati ।
tasmātkratvarthāppraṇayanāśritatvādgodohanasya tatkrameṇa puruṣārthamapi godohanaṃ kramavaditi siddham ।
śrutinirākaraṇenaiva iṣṭisomakramavadapi kramo apāsto veditavyaḥ ।
