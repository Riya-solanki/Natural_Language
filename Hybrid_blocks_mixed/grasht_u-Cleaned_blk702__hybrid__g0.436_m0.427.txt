Comm Mugdhāvabodhinī
atha tīkṣṇavidhānamāha suradālītyādi ।।
suradālībhasmagalitaṃ suradālī devadālī tasyāḥ bhasma dāhasambhūtaṃ tena galitaṃ trisaptakṛtvā ekaviṃśativāraṃ gojalaṃ surabhimūtraṃ bhāvitaṃ kuryādityadhyāhāraḥ ।।
atha mūṣāgataṃ vajrasaṃjñāyāṃ sthitaṃ tīkṣṇaṃ sāraṃ vāpena nikṣepaṇena jalasadṛśaṃ jalatulyaṃ kurute karmaviditi śeṣaḥ ।।
