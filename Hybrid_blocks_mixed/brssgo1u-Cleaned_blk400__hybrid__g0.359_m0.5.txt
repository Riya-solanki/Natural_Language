antara upapatteḥ ।
upakosalavidyāvākyamudāharati-ya iti ।
tadakṣisthānamasaṅgatvena brahmaṇo anurūpaṃ yato asminkṣiptaṃ vartmanī pakṣmaṇī eva gacchatītyarthaḥ ।
darśanasya laukikatvaśāstrīyatvābhyāṃ saṃśayamāha-tatreti ।
pūrvaṃ apibantau'iti prathamaśrutacetanatvānusāreṇa caramaśrutā guhāpraveśādayo nītāḥ, tadvadihāpi dṛśyata iti cākṣuṣatvānusāreṇamṛtatvādayo dhyānārthaṃ kalpitatvena neyā iti dṛṣṭāntena pūrvapakṣayati-chāyātmeti ।
pūrvapakṣe pratibimbopāstiḥ, sidvānte brahmopāstiriti phalam ।
prasidvavaditi ।
cākṣuṣatvenetyarthaḥ ।
saṃbhāvanāmātreṇa pakṣantaramāha-vijñānātmana ityādinā ।
amano brahma'itivat, aetadbrahmeti'iti vākyasyetipadaśiraskatvānna svārthaparatvamiti pūrvapakṣaḥ ।
amano brahmetyupāsīta'ityatra itipadasya pratyayaparatvāt, iha ca brahmetyuvācetyanvayena itipadasyoktisaṃbandhinor'thaparatvādvaiṣamyamiti sidvāntayati-parameśvara eveti ।
