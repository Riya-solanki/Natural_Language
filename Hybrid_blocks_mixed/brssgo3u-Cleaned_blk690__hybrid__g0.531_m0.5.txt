oṅkārasya dhyeyasya sādhāraṇyādapi tadāśritadhyānānāṃ samuccityānuṣṭhānaṃ gamyata iti liṅgāntaramāha-guṇeti ।
tenoṅkāreṇa, vedatrayoktaṃ karma pravartata ityarthaḥ anvayamukhenoktamevārthaṃ vyatirekato api vyācaṣṭe-athaveti ।।
