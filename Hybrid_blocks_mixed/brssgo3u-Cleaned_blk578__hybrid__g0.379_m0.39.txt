chāndogye vaiśvānaravidyāṃ prakṛtya śrūyate - atadyadbhaktaṃ prathamamāgacchettaddhomīyaṃ sa yāṃ prathamāmāhutiṃ juhuyāttāṃ juhuyātprāṇasya svāhā' ityādi ।
tatra pañca prāṇāhutayo vihitāḥ ।
tāsu ca parastādagnihotraśabdaḥ prayuktaḥ aya etadevaṃ vidvānagnihotraṃ juhoti' iti ayatheha kṣudhitā bālā mātaraṃ paryupāsate ।
evaṃ sarvāṇi bhūtānyagnihotramupāsate' iti ca ।
tatredaṃ vicāryate - kiṃ bhojanalope lopaḥ prāṇāgnihotrasyotālopa itai ।
tadyadbhavati bhaktāgamanasaṃyogaśravaṇādbhaktāgamanasya ca bojanārthatvādbhojanalope lopaḥ prāṇāgnihotrasyeti ।
evaṃ prāpte na lupyeteti tāvadāha ।
kasmāt ।
ādarāt ।
tathāhi vaiśvāravidyāyāmeva jābālānāṃ śrutiḥ - apūrvo atithibhyo aśnīyāt ।
yathā ha vai svayamahutvāgnihotraṃ parasya juhuyādevaṃ tat' ityatithibhojanasya prāthamyaṃ ninditvā svāmibhojanaṃ prathamaṃ prāpayantī prāṇāgnihotra ādaraṃ karoti ।
yā hi na prāthamyalopaṃ sahate netarāṃ sā prāthamyavato agnihotrasya lopaṃ sahateti manyate ।
