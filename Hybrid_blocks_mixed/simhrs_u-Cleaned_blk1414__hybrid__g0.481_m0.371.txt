atha virodhanam-
virodhanaṃ nirodhoktiḥ saṃrabdhānāṃ parasparam ।। 
yathā tatraiva cāraṇaḥ-katham amarṣitābhyāṃ rāma-rāvaṇābhyāṃ pratyupakrāntam iṣu-varṣādvaitam ity upakramya, cāraṇaḥ-nanv ayam oṃkāro rāvaṇa-śiro-maṇḍala-cchedana-vidyāyāḥ ity antena saṃrabdhayo rāma-rāvaṇayoḥ divyāstra-prayoga-rūpa-paraspara-saṃrodha-karaṇād virodhanam ।
