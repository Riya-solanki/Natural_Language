praśrabdhakāyaḥ suvimuktacitto hy asaṃskurvan smṛtimān akopyaḥ ājñāya dharmam avitarkadhyāyī kopaspṛhāstyānadoṣaiḥ viyuktaḥ ।। 
evaṃvihārabahulo bhikṣuḥ pañcaughatīrṇas taratīha ṣaṣṭhaṃ ।
evaṃdhyāyī vipulāṃ kāmatṛṣṇāṃ tīrṇo bhavaty apratilabdhayoktraḥ ।।
