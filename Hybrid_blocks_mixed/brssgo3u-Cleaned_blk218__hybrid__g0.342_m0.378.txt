kalpitadvaite sāvakāśatvācca saprapañcatvaśrutayo durbalā ityāha-prakāśavacceti ।
nanvākāravākyānāmupādhikalpitasarvagandhatvādināthravattvaṃ kimiti varṇyate vaiyarthyamevocyatām, tatrāha-nahi vedavākyānāmiti ।
nanvevamapīti ।
uktarītyobhayarūpatvāṅgīkāreṇa śrutīnāṃ vyavasthitatve apītyarthaḥ ।
upādhīnāṃ kalpitatvādaupādhikasya satyatvādupapatterna satyamubhayarūpatvamiti pūrvamuktaṃ, saṃprati satyaṃ nirviśeṣatvaṃ mithyā saviśeṣatvamityucyata ityubhayarūpatvāṅgīkāre api na pūrvāparavirodha ityāha-neti brūma iti ।
dvaitasya mithyātve jñānena bādhādupāsanādivyavahāro na syādityāśaṅkya bādhātprāgeva sa ityāha-satyamiti ।।
