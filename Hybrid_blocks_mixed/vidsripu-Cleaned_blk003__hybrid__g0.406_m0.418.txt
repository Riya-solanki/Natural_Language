sugatavrajyā
ābāhūdgatamaṇḍalāgrarucayaḥ saṃnaddhavakṣaḥsthalāḥ soṣmāṇo vraṇino vipakṣahṛdayapronmāthinaḥ karkaśāḥ ।
utsṛṣṭāmbaradṛṣṭavigrahabharā yasya smarāgresarā mārā māravadhūstanāś ca na dadhuḥ kṣobhaṃ sa vo avyāj jinaḥ ।।
