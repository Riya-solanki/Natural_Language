na ।
adarśanasya tamaso nityatvābhyupagamāt ।
guṇānāṃ codbhāvābhibhavayoraniyatatvādaniyataḥ saṃyoganimittoparama iti viyogasyāpyaniyatatvātsāṃkhyasyaivānirmokṣo aparihāryaḥ syāt ।
aupaniṣadasya tvātmaikatvābhyupagamādekasya ca viṣayaviṣayibhāvānupapattervikārabhedasya ca vācārambhaṇamātratvaśravaṇādanirmokṣaśaṅkā svapne api nopajāyate ।
vyavahāre tu yatra yathā dṛṣṭastapyatāpakabhāvastatra tathaiva sa iti na codayitavyaḥ parihartavyo vā bhavati ।।
