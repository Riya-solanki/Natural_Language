tatraivobhayavyatireko yathā nāyakasyaiva vaṃśāvalyām
saudaryo balabhadramūrtiraniśaṃ devī priyā rukmiṇī pradyumrastanayo api pautranivaho yasyāniruddhādayaḥ ।
soyaṃ śrīpatirannavotanṛpatiḥ kiṃ tvānanāmbhoruhe dhatte cāru sudarśanaśriyamasau sarvātma hastāmbuje ।।
