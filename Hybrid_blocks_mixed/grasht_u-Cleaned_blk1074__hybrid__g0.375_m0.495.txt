amarasundarīguṭikāvidhānaṃ guṇāṃścāha kāntetyādi ।।
kāntaṃ cumbakaṃ abhraṃ yugmam ।।
abhrakasattvaṃ pratītaṃ hema kanakaṃ tāraṃ ca tat ekavadbhāvo dvandvatvāt punararkaḥ tāmram eṣāṃ kāntādīnāṃ saṃkhyā gaṇanā samāṃśataḥ samabhāgato jñeyā ।।
punaḥ sūtasamāṃśaṃ pāradena tulyabhāgaṃ dhmātaṃ kuryāt andhamūṣāyāṃ iti śeṣaḥ ।।
kiṃkṛtaṃ sat bāhye baddhagolopari rasena māraṇāyām uktadraveṇa liptaṃ sat dhmātaṃ kuryāt ।।
itthaṃ kṛtaṃ khoṭaṃ piṣṭistambhaḥ golākāraṃ bhavati ।।
tat vadanagataṃ mukhāntaḥsthitaṃ śastravārakaṃ syāt asmiṃśca vadanaṃ gate sati śarīre khaḍgādīnāṃ prahāro na lagati ।।
guṭir iyaṃ nāmnā abhidhānena amarasundarīguṭikā jñeyā ।।
iyaṃ śarīrasthā mukhe anyasthale vā sthitā satī śarīrasthān rogān hanti vināśayati ।।
anuktamapi mānaṃ pañcaniṣkapramāṇaṃ jñeyamityarthaḥ ।।
ityamarasundarī guṭikā ।।
hemnā vā rajatena vāpi sahito dhmāto vrajatyekatāmakṣīṇo niviḍo guḍaśca guṭikāḥ karoti dīrghojjvalāḥ ।।
cūrṇaṃ tatpaṭuvatprayāti vihitaghṛṣṭo na muñcenmalaṃ nirgandho dravati kṣaṇātsa hi mato baddhābhidhāno rasaḥ ।
iti granthāntare ।।
