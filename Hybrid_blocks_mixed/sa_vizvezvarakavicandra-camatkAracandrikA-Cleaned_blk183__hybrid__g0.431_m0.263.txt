atra karuṇāṃbudhe dayodadhe ityarthapunaruktiḥ । pāhi naḥ pāhi na iti śabdapunaruktiḥ । tadubhayamapi śatṛsaṃbandhikā vigrahakārigatadainyātiśayavivakṣāyāṃ na duṣyati kintu svārthātiśayaṃ puṣyatīti guṇatvam ।
dūratānvayayogyānāṃ yatra syāttadduranvayam ।।
yathā
śrīsiṅgakṣitipālasaṅgarataleṣvaṅgāni dṛṣṭvā dviṣāmātaṅkena samantataḥ kilakilārāveṇa vaitālikāḥ ।
kāntāḥ khaṇḍavikhṇḍatāṃ parigatānyuccaṇḍakhaḍgāhateḥ ghorāḥ kaṅkakulākuleṣu cakitāśvinvanti minvanti ca ।।
