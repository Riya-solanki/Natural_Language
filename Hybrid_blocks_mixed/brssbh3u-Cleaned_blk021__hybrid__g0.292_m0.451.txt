syādetat ।
kathaṃ punaḥ apañcamyāmāhutāvāpaḥ puruṣavacaso bhavanti' ityetannirdhārayituṃ pāryate ।
yāvatā naiva prathame agnāvapāṃ śravaṇamasti ।
iha hi dyulokaprabhṛtayaḥ pañcāgnayaḥ pañcānāmāhutīnāmādāratvenādhītāḥ ।
teṣāṃ ca pramukhe aasau vāva loko gautamāgniḥ' ityupakramya atasminnetasminnagnau devāḥ śraddhāṃ juhvati'
