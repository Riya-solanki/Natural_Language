syāt sā ca saṃviddhiruddhetyuktam ।
vispaṣṭaṃ cāsya śaktyabhiprāyatvam ।
evaṃ hi śrūyate- yadvai tanna paśyati paśyanvai tanna paśyati, na hi draṣṭurdṛṣṭerviparilopo vidyate, avināśitvāt na tu taddvitīyamasti tato anyadvibhaktaṃ yatpaśyet evamanyānyapi vākyāni jighranvai
