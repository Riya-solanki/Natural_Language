tasya dehasya sthairyeṇa sthirabhāvena kṛtvā rasāyanaṃ jarāvyādhināśanaṃ prati samarthaṃ kārakataram ।।
apīti niścayena ।।
kiṃ sūtalohādiḥ sūtaḥ pāradaḥ lohāḥ svarṇādayo navakāḥ kṛtrimākṛtrimabhedayuktāḥ rājarītikharparīghoṣāḥ kṛtrimāḥ svarṇatāratāmranāgavaṅgalohā akṛtrimāḥ ādiśabdānmahārasā uparasāśca jñātavyāḥ ।।
ke ta ime rasāvatāre yathā ।
hiṅgūlatāpyavimalācalasasyakāntavaikrāntapakṣipatayaś ca mahārasāḥ syuḥ ।
sauvīragandhakaśilālaviraṅgadhātukāsīsakāṃkṣy uparasāḥ kathitā rasajñaiḥ iti ।।
athavā sthairyeṇa samarthe tat dehaṃ prati rasāyanaṃ kiṃ sūtalohādi ।।
sūte yadabhivyāptaṃ grāsamānena lohādi tattathoktaṃ sūte iti abhivyāpake ।।
adhikaraṇe saptamī ।।
taddehaṃ svayamasthiram asthirībhāvatvabhāvaṃ punar dāhyaṃ dagdhuṃ śakyaṃ punaḥ kledyam ārdrībhāvena śīrṇayituṃ śakyaṃ punaḥ śodhyaṃ śoṣayitum agnijalānilaiḥ dāhyaṃ kledyaṃ śoṣyaṃ ca śarīramityarthaḥ ।।
sūtalohādinā dehamanityaṃ nityaṃ bhavet ayameva yatna iti tātparyārthaḥ ।।
