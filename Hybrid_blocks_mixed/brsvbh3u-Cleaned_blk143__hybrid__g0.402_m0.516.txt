jayāmāsminnābhicārike yajña udgīthalakṣaṇasāmabhaktyupalakṣitenodgātreṇa karmaṇeti ।
te ha vācamūcurityādinā saṃdarbheṇa vākprāṇacakṣuḥśrotramanasāmāsurapāpmaviddhatayā ninditvā atha hemamāsanyamāsye bhavamāsanyaṃ mukhāntarbilasthaṃ mukhyaṃ prāṇaṃ prāṇābhimānavatīṃ devatāmūcustvaṃ na udgāyeti ।
tathetyabhyupagamya tebhya eva prāṇa udagāyat te asurā viduranena prāṇenodgātrā no asmān devā atyeṣyantīti ।
tamabhidrutya pāpmanāvidhyannasurā yathāśmānamṛtvā prāpya mṛttvālloṣṭo vā vidhvaṃsata evaṃ vidhvaṃsamānā viṣvañco asurā vineśuḥ ।
tadetat saṃkṣipyāha-
vājasaneyaka iti ।
tathā chāndogye apyetaduktamityāha-
tathā chāndogye apīti ।
viṣayaṃ darśayitvā vimṛśati-
tatra saṃśaya iti ।
