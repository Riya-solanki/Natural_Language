mṛgasya netre akṛṣṇarasenābhyete ।। 
netrayoḥ pīḍā naśyati ।। 
andhacakṣuṣi siddharasāñjane kṛte'ndhaḥ paśyati ।। 
tathā yasya mukhe siddharasaguṭikā tiṣṭhati tasyākāśe gatir devadānavagandharvasiddhayakṣakhecarair na hanyate ।। 
tathā siddharase mukhe sthite viṣeṇa na mriyate ।। 
agninā na dahyate ।। 
ākāśe siddharasavidyaśca bhavati ।। 
mṛto mūrchitaḥ san tatkṣaṇājjīvati ।। 
tathā maṇimantramahauṣadhebhyo api siddharasasya prabhāvātiśayo mahīyān ।। 
asau mṛtajīvano nāma rasabandhaḥ kathitaḥ ।। 
ko arthaḥ mṛta iva mṛtaḥ ।। 
sarpadaṃśavahnidāhajalaplāvaśastraghātaviṣādiduṣṭaprayogair mūrchitaḥ yaḥ kenāpi jīvayituṃ na śakyate so api mukhe kṣiptasyāsya rasasya prabhāveṇa kṣaṇād eva jīvati ।। 
iti jīrṇavajrasūtasya bandhasaṃskāro dvādaśaḥ ।। 
baddhasūtacatuḥṣaṣṭi- palāny āvartayet sudhīḥ ।
hemarājicatuḥṣaṣṭi- palāny āvartayet pṛthak ।।
ḍhālayeddhemarājiṃ tāṃ baddhasūtadravopari ।
raktaṣoṭo bhavatyevaṃ saṃskāraḥ sāraṇo mataḥ ।।
