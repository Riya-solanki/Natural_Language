dvitīyā yathā
tṛṣṇākrāntā jalamiti dhiyā saṃplave bhānubhāsāṃ dhāvaṃ dhāvaṃ dhavaladhavalaṃ dhānvinīṣu sthalīṣu ।
kṣāmakṣāmā ripunṛpatayaḥ siṅgabhūpāladhāṭyāṃ satyākārānapi na payasāṃ viśvasanti sma kūpān ।।
