tatra devahrade snātvā śaṃbhuṃ saṃpūjya bhaktitaḥ ।
vidhivaddadhi ca prāśya maṇimantaṃ tato yayau ।।
tatra tīrthavare snātvā prājāpatye mahāmatiḥ' dadarśa śaṃbhu brahmāṇaṃ deveśaṃ ca prajāpatim ।।
vidhānatastu tān devān pūjayitvā tapodhana ।
ṣaḍrātraṃ tatra ca sthitvā jagāma madhunandinīm ।।
