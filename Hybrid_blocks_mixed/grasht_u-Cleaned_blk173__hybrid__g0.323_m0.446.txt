upadeśavidhānamāha tam ityādi ।
yenopadeśena gandhābhrakapraveśanaṃ gandhapāṣāṇasaṃyogād yadabhrapraveśena bhavati abhrasya pāradāntaḥpraveśo bhavati tamupadeśamahaṃ kavir vakṣyāmi kathayiṣye ।।
atisāmīpyād vartamāna eva ḷṭ ।।
tu punaḥ ।।
pakṣachinnaśca raso rasarasāyanayor yogyaḥ rase jvarādiroganāśake jvarāṅkuśādau rasāyane ca jarāvyādhināśane prayoge yogyaḥ samarthaḥ ।।
yathā mañjaryām ।
mārito dehaśuddhyarthaṃ mūrchito vyādhināśanaḥ ।
rasabhasma kvacidroge dehārthe mūrchitaṃ kvacit ।।
baddhaṃ dvābhyāṃ prayuñjīta śāstradṛṣṭena karmaṇā ।।
iti ।।
gandhābhrakapraveśena pakṣachinno acalo bhaved iti bhāvaḥ ।।
