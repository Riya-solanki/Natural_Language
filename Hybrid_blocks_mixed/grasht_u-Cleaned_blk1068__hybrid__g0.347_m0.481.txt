athottaravidhānena lakṣaṇamāha śatetyādi ।।
śatasahasralakṣavedhī rasaḥ ।।
balisahitaṃ piṣṭaṃ rasaṃ bhuñjīta balinā gandhakena sahitam śatādārabhya sahasralakṣakoṭyarbudānāṃ krameṇa daśaguṇottaraṃ saṃkhyā jñātavyā ।।
evaṃ kṛtaḥ san rasaḥ siddhido yathoktaguṇakṛdbhavatītyarthaḥ ।।
