[] tatas tasyāpi labdha-chidraṃ-manyasya, kūṭa-mayyā sva-kaṇḍūti-vighaṭanecchayā nikaṭam aṭatas, tad akarkaśa-mudrayā sahasā tān hitvā taṃ sa-puccha-pādaṃ gṛhītvā bhramayāmāsa ।
yāvacchaḥ parivartanaṃ bhramayatā vatsasya cakre' munā
tāvacchaḥ pratipat kramān niragamad rūpāntaraṃ cāgamat ।
krīḍāyāḥ phala-pātanārtham iva ca kṣipte kapitthopari
jñātṛtvaṃ naṭavat kalāṃ ca pṛthukās te tasya śaślāghire ।। [śārdūla]
