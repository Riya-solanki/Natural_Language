svataḥ karma-vaśād evādātuṃ karmānusāriṇaḥ ।
īśaś ced asti karmānugāmy asau kā nu devatā ।। sarvaṃ tad bhasmani hutaṃ para-tantre yad arpaṇam ।
gārhapatye hutaṃ tat tu yad ātmātmīya-poṣaṇam ।। tasmād ābhīra-vaiśyatvād aghnyāsevā tu naḥ kṛtiḥ ।
devatā yadi manyeta mantavyā sā tad-āśrayā ।। [anuṣṭubh]
