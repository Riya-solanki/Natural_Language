[] tataś ca kautuka-viśeṣa-lambhanāya vraja-rāja-tanūjaḥ sa-smitam īkṣamāṇaḥ sa-vismayavad utprekṣamāṇaś ca nijāgrajaṃ prati sādara-narma-gandha-prabandhi-vana-varṇanaṃ nirmame, yathā-
nūnaṃ bhavān viśva-patir namanti yad
baliṃ gṛhītvā taravaḥ padaṃ tava ।
paśya prasūnādi-śatena tan nata-
pravāla-śākhā-śikhayā spṛśanti te ।। [upajāti ]
