ityādīni ।
tasyātmānveṣaṇāya pravṛttasyāheyamanupādeyaṃ cātmatattvamupadiśyate ।
aidaṃ sarvaṃ yadayamātmā' ayatra tvasya sarvamātmaivābhūttatkena kaṃ paśyet kena kaṃ vijānīyāt vījñātāramare kena vijānīyāt' aayamātmā brahma' ityādibhiḥ ।
yadapyakartavyapradhānamātmajñānaṃ hānāyopādānāya vā na bhavatīti, tattathaivetyabhyupagamyate ।
alaṅkāro hyayamasmākaṃ yadbrahmātmāvagatau satyāṃ sarvakartavyatāhāniḥ kṛtakṛtyatā ceti ।
tathāca śrutiḥ- aātmānaṃ cedvijānīyādayamastīti pūruṣaḥ ।
kimicchankasya kāmāya śarīramanusaṃjvaret' ।।
iti ।
aetadbuddhvā buddhimānsyātkṛtakṛtyaśca bhārata' ।
iti smṛtiḥ ।
tasmānna pratipattividhiviṣayatayā brahmaṇaḥ samarpaṇam ।
yadapi kecidāhuḥ- apravṛttinivṛttividhitaccheṣavyatirekeṇa kevalavastuvādī vedabhāgo nāsti' iti tanna, aupaniṣadasya puruṣasya puruṣasyānanyaśeṣatvāt ।
yo asāvupaniṣatsvevādhigataḥ puruṣo asaṃsārī brahma utpādyādicaturvidhadravyavilakṣaṇaḥ svaprakaraṇastho ananyośeṣaḥ, nāsau nāsti nādhigamyata iti vā śakyaṃ vaditum, asa eṣa neti netyātmā' ityāmaśabdāt, ātmanaśca pratyākhyātumaśakyatvāt, ya eva nirākartā tasyaivātmatvāt ।
