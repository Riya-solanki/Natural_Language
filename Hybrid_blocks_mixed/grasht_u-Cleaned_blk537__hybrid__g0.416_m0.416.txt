tutthādīnāṃ satvapātanamāha tutthādityādi ।।
tutthāt tutthaṃ śikhigrīvaṃ tasmāt tāpyajasamamiti mākṣikasatvavat mākṣikasatvavidhānenāsya satvapāta ityarthaḥ ।।
samasṛṣṭaṃ samaṃ tāpyena tulyaṃ varṇamārdavābhyāṃ sṛṣṭaṃ kathitamityarthaḥ ।।
evaṃvidhaṃ tutthakasatvaṃ patati ।।
abhraketyādi abhrakaṃ pratītaṃ vaikrāntaṃ rasavaikrāntaṃ kāntaṃ cumbakaṃ itiprabhṛtīnām ityādīnāṃ tattvaṃ patrasatvapātanayoge lohanibhaṃ muṇḍavarṇam ityarthaḥ ।।
