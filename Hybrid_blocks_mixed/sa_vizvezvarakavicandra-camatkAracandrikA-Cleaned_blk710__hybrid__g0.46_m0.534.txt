pratyanīkaṃ yathā
śrotuṃ raṃbhāracanāṃ nirbharānandagarbhāṃ prāsādeṣu pratidiśataṭinīṃ vīkṣituṃ jālamārgeḥ ।
citraṃ siṅgakṣitipa bhavataḥ khaḍgadhārā nimagnā nidrāmudrāṃ niravadhikathāmāśrayanti dviṣantaḥ ।।
