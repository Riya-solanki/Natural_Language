rasāyane bhojyābhojyamāha varjitetyādi ।।
varjitakāñjikaśākaṃ varjitaṃ kāñjikaṃ sauvīraṃ śākaṃ vāstukādi ca yasmin tattathā payasā kṣīreṇa saha śālyodanaṃ bhuñjīta ।।
punaḥ śālyodanaṃ kiyanmānaṃ dvicatuḥṣaṭpalamānam ।।
atra mātrādhamamadhyamajyeṣṭhā dvicatuḥṣaṭpalapramāṇā adhamamadhyamottamabaleṣu prayojyetyarthaḥ ।।
