atra prathamārthe bhavantītyapekṣāyāṃ tadantarbhāvena vaikalye api yatrānya kriyāpadaṃ nāsti tatrāsirbhavatirhi prathamapuruṣe prayujyata ityanuśāsanād guṇatvam । dvītīyārdhe kriyābhāvepyudāttādi guṇasaṃbandhadyotakaniṣṭhāntatvena nirākāṅbhatvāt samarthakatvamiti guṇatvam ।
kevalaṃ tvanabhivyaktacamatkaraṇakāraṇam ।।
yathā
āndolikāyā māsīnamāyāntaṃ rājavartmani ।
striyaḥ paśyanti rājānaṃ puraḥ paścātparicchadam ।।
