maraṇavaśagatasya jantor vivṛddholbaṇaśvāsaśuṣkauṣṭhakaṇṭhasya hikkāpraveśānubandhād vinirdhūyamānorasaḥ kalavikalapadākṣare bhāṣite vihvale cakṣuṣi cchidyamāneṣu marmasv anāmantrya roṣād iva prasthitair vāyubhiḥ । mahati śithilatāṃ gate sandhisaṃghātayantre rujātīkṣṇasūcī- 
nipātāntare kvāpi gantuṃ kṛtābhyudyame jīvite
timiragahanasaṃkaṭaṃ nirjanaṃ mārgam ākrāmato nāsti puṇyād
ṛte kaś cid anyaḥ sakhā tat prayatnaṃ kuru śreyasi ।।
