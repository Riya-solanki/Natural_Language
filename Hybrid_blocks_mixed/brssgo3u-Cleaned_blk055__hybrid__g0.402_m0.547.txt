FN anuśayaḥ karma ।
tat karma ।
pratyavaiti parikṣīṇaṃ bhavati ।
abhivyaktiḥ phalonmukhatā ।
tat tatrāvarohatāṃ jīvānāṃ madhye iha karmabhūmau ramaṇīyacaraṇāḥ puṇyakarmaṇaḥ iti yat tat ābhyāśoha avaśyaṃ hītyarthaḥ ।
kapūyaṃ pāpam ।
ricyamānaṃ viyujyamānam ।
