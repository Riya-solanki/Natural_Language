nanu kārśyādīnāṃ kramavartitvādekasmindevadatte yuktaḥ samāveśaḥ, anunāsikādīnāntu dharmāṇāṃ yugapadanekavaktruccārite varṇe samavetānāṃ kathamekavarṇaviṣayatvam ।
na hi viruddhadharmāneko dharmī yugapadbibharti ।
satyam ।
natvete paramārthato varṇadharmā dhvanidharmāstvete varṇe kevalamābhāsante, yathālpe mahati ca darpaṇe yugapaddṛśyamāne mukhe alpatvaṃ mahatvaṃ ceti ।
tasmādvarṇasya vyaktyekatve api na virodha ityaprāmāṇikī gatvādijātiḥ ।।
