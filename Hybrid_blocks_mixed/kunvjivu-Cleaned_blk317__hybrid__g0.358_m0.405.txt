kvacidaniyataprāyavākyaikadeśe kasmiṃścidavyavadhāne api vyavadhānābhāve apyekasya dvayoḥ samuditayośca bahūnāṃ vā punaḥ punarbadhyamānānāmeṣāṃ manoharinibandhanā hṛdayāvarjakavinyāsā bhavati ।
kācidevaṃ saṃpadyata ityarthaḥ ।
yamakavyavahāro atra na pravartate, tasya niyatasthānatayā vyavasthānāt ।
svarairavyavadhānamatra na vivakṣitam, tasyānupapatteḥ ।
tatraikasyāvyavadhānodāharaṇaṃ yathā
