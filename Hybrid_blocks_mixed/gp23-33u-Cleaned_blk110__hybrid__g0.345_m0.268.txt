dṛṣṭaṃ yad-vanam aty apūrva-kusumair divyartunā pūjitaṃ
dyotaiś carcitam indunā yamunayā cātmānilair nartitam ।
sāmagrīyam udeti deva-nicitāsmākaṃ vihāra-krame
tasmād etad abhāvatas tu va iha stavyā na vāstavyatā ।।
kintu-
diṣṭenāpte tatra gatvā sva-goṣṭhe
kaṣṭenāpi svīya-dharmaṃ kurudhvam ।
patyur bhaktir bāla-vatsādi-pālya-
vyaktīnāṃ yaḥ pālanādiś ca diṣṭaḥ ।।
[] tataḥ kaṣṭa-sādhyaṃ taṃ dharmaṃ bādhyamānaṃ vidhāyātrāvikala-sarva-dharma-phale mama sukha-vihāra-sthale praviśata । [] anyathā punar udyad-vidhu-vanam idaṃ bhavatīnāṃ vidhuvanāya sampatsyata iti yācñā-pakṣābhiprāyaḥ ।
