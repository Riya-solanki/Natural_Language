na kevalamiha brahmaliṅgamevopalabhyate ।
santi hītaraliṅgānyapi ।
amāmeva vijānīhi' itīndrasya vacanaṃ devatātmaliṅgam ।
idaṃ śarīraṃ parigṛhyotthāpayatīti prāṇaliṅgam ।
ana vācaṃ vijijñāsīta vaktāraṃ vidyāt' ityādi jīvaliṅgam ।
ata upapannaḥ saṃśayaḥ ।
tatra prasiddhervāyuḥ prāṇa iti prāpta ucyate-prāṇaśabdaṃ brahma vijñeyam ।
kutaḥ, tathānugamāt ।
tathāhi- paurvāparyeṇa paryalocyamāne vākye padārthānāṃ samanvayo brahmapratipādanapara upalabhyate ।
upakrame tāvat, avaraṃ vṛṇīṣva' itīndreṇoktaḥ pratardanaḥ paramaṃ puruṣārthaṃ varamupacikṣepa- atvameva me vṛṇīśva yaṃ tvaṃ manuṣyāya hitatamaṃ manyase' iti ।
tasmai hitatamatvenopadiśyamānaḥ prāṇaḥ kathaṃ paramātmā na syāt ।
nahyanyatra paramātmajñānāddhitatamaprāptirasti ।
atameva viditvātimṛtyumeti nānyaḥ panthā vidyate ayanāya' ityādiśrutibhyaḥ ।
tathā asa yo māṃ veda na ha vai tasya kenacana karmaṇā loko mīyate na steyena na bhrūṇahatyayā' ityādi ca brahmaparigrahe ghaṭate ।
brahmavijñānena hi sarvakarmakṣayaḥ prasiddhaḥ- akṣīyante cāsya karmāṇi tasmindṛṣṭe parāvare' ityādyāsu śrutiṣu ।
prajñātmatvaṃ ca brahmapakṣa evopapadyate ।
nahyacetanasya vāyoḥ prajñātmatvaṃ saṃbhavati ।
tathopasaṃhāre api aānando ajaro amṛtaḥ' ityānandatvādīni na brahmaṇo anyatra samyak saṃbhavati ।
asa na sādhunā karmaṇā bhūyānbhavati no evāsādhunā karmaṇā kanīyāneṣa hyeva sādhu karma kārayati taṃ yamebhyo lokasya unninīṣate ।
eṣa u bhavāsādhu karma kārayati taṃ yamebhyo lokebhyo adho ninīṣate' iti, aeṣa lokādhipatireṣa lokeśaḥ' iti ca ।
sarvametatparasmaninbrahmaṇyāśrīyamāṇe anugantuṃ śakyate na mukhye prāṇe ।
tasmātprāṇo brahma ।।
