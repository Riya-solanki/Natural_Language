aṇuśca ।
asamāstribhirlokaiḥ'iti vibhutvaśravaṇādvibhuḥ prāṇaḥ, asamaḥ pluṣiṇā'ityādyāstu śrutayo vibhorapyavacchedādbhāviṣyanti ।
yathā vibhuna ākāśasya kuṭakarakādyavacchedātkuṭādisāmyamiti prāpta āha-aṇuśca ।
utkrāntigatyāgatiśrutibhya ādhyātmikasya prāṇasyāvacchinnatā na vibhutvam ।
duradhigamatāmātreṇa ca śarīravyāpino apyaṇutvamupacaryate na tvaṇutvamityuktamadhastāt ।
yattvasya vibhutvānmānaṃ tadādhidaivikena sūtrātmanā samaṣṭivyaṣṭirūpeṇa na tvādhyātmikena rūpeṇa ।
tadāśrayāśca asamaḥ pluṣiṇā'ityevamādyāḥ śrutayo dehasāmyameva prāṇasyāhuḥ svarūpato na tu karakākāśavatparopādhikatayā kathañcinnetavyā iti ।।
