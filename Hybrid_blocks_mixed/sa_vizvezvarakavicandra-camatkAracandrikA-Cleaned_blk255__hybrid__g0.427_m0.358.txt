atra dvitīyādiṣu pādeṣu bhinnārthatvābhāve api kenacidadharmagandho apyasmaddeśe na vidyata iti trirvācā dharmapratiṣṭhāpakasvāmipakṣapātātiśayopakṣiptaṃ sodrekeṇa cetasābhihitatvād guṇatvam ।
sandehadāyakārthaṃ yat sasaṃśayamitīritam ।।
yathā
śrīsiṅgabhūpadhāṭīṣu vidviṣo hīnasādhanāḥ ।
sālaśreṇimupāśritya kurvate jīvataṃ tṛṇam ।।
