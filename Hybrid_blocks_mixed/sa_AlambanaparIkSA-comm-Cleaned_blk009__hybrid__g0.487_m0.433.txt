indriyavaikalyāt dvicandradarśanasya tadābhatve api na tasya viṣayo asti । tadvat saṃghātaḥ dravyato asattvena akāraṇātvāt nālambanam ।
evaṃ bāhyadūyañcaiva na yuktaṃ matigocaraḥ ।।
aṇuḥ kalāpaśceti bāhyo arthaḥ nālambanam, ekāṅgavaikalyāt ।। tatra sādhanaṃ sañcitākāramicchanti kila kecana ।
