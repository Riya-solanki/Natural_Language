anyaccāha mṛtetyādi ।।
mṛtaśulbatāpyacūrṇaṃ mṛtaṃ ca yat śulbaṃ tāmraṃ tāpyaṃ svarṇamākṣikaṃ ca taccūrṇaṃ kiṃviśiṣṭaṃ kāntayutaṃ cumbakamiśritaṃ tena mṛtaśulbatāpyacūrṇena kāntayutena pūrvaṃ niṣpannaṃ khoṭaṃ rañjayet ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭaṃ ghanasattvahemayutaṃ abhrasatvasvarṇamiśritam ।।
evaṃvidhaṃ tatkhoṭaṃ rasāyane jarāvyādhināśane yojyam ।।
