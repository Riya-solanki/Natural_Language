[] tad evaṃ saṃhata-saṃhananam apy etaṃ niḥsandhi-bandhanaṃ visṛjya tat-pada-paddhatiṃ saṃsṛjya tad-gupti-dvāram anusasāra । tataś ca,
sadyo nirbhidyāpidhānaṃ guhāyās
tatrāviśya dyotam āviścakāra ।
tasyās teṣāṃ cārbhakāṇāṃ samantād
ātmālābhād yat tamas taj jahāra ।। [śālinī]
