āpaḥ ।
atideśo ayam ।
tathā hyātharvaṇe muṇḍakagranthe aetasmājjāyate prāṇo manaḥ sarvendriyāṇica ।
khaṃ vāyurjyotirāpaḥ pṛthivī viśvasya dhāriṇī'iti mantre apāṃ brahmajatvaṃ śrutam ।
aagnerāpaḥ'iti śrutyā tasya virodho asti na veti saṃdehe tulyatvādasti virodha iti pūrvapakṣe apāmagnidāhyatvena virodhādagnijatvāsaṃbhavātkramārthā pañcamītyavirodha ityadhikāśaṅkyāmuktejonyāyamatidiśya vyācaṣṭe- ata iti ।
pratyakṣavirodhe kathamapāmagnijatvanirṇayaḥ, tatrāha- sati vacana iti ।
trivṛtkṛtayoraptejasorvirodhe apyagnerāpa iti vacanādatīndriyayostayornāsti virodha iti nirṇīyata ityarthaḥ ।
na kevalaṃ śrutyavirodhajñānāyāyamatideśaḥ kintu pañcabhūcatotpattikramanirṇayārthaṃ cetyāha- tejasastviti ।
tasmāttejobhāvāpanne brahmaṇi śrutisamanvaya iti siddham ।।
