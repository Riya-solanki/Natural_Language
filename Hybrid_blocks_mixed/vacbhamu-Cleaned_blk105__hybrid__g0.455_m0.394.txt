iti smṛtivyākopa ityata āha- arthāntaraprayukta eva hyathaśabdaḥ śrutyā maṅgalaprayojano bhavati ।
arthāntareṣvānantaryādiṣu prayuktaḥ athaśabdaḥ śrutyā śravaṇamātreṇa veṇuvīṇādhvanivanmaṅgalaṃ kurvan maṅgalaprayojano bhavati anyārthamānīyamānodakumbhadarśanavat ।
tena na smṛtivyākopaḥ ।
tena ca iha ānantaryārthasya sataḥ śravaṇamātreṇa maṅgalārthatetyarthaḥ ।
