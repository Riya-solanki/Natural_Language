mukha-kamala-virājan-nāṭya-dṛk-khañjarītā
stana-kanakaja-kumbha-bhrāji-patrāvalīkā । caraṇa-sarasijāṇāṃ haṃsaka-svāna-ramyā
vraja-nija-mahilālī tatra bhavyaṃ babhāra ।।
tataś ca-
na paraṃ cākṣuṣam āsīt tad-vraja-jana-calanaṃ vidūrataḥ kintu । śakaṭa-ghaṭāṭananād vādād apy andha-vedyaṃ ca ।।surāṇāṃ nṝṇāṃ ca pratipada-bahūbhāva-mayatāṃ
sa-vādyair gītādyaiḥ saha kusuma-varṣair jaya-ravaiḥ । mukundālokāya prathita-pathikālī-śavalanaiḥ
samantāt saṅghaṭṭair abhiyayur amī āmbika-vanam ।।tatra ca-
bhrātrā satrā haris tatrāruhya kambali-vāhyakam । vicitra-mitra-gantrīṇāṃ vṛtaṃ pṛtanayāyayau ।।kadācid unnataṃ tatra śakaṭaṃ ghaṭayan pṛthak । abhīṣṭaṃ draṣṭum utkaṇṭhī sa sma suṣṭhu pratiṣṭhate ।। sakhi-bala-balavān vihāra-caryāṃ
pathi vidadhat-kutukaṃ ca lokamānaḥ । muhur upacaritaḥ svayaṃ jananyā
bakaripur āmbika-kānanaṃ viveśa ।। tatra ca-
jaṅghālās te lakṣaśo loka-saṅghās
tīrthaṃ yātāḥ kṛṣṇam āpuś ca daivāt । tṛṣṇā-dīnāḥ pāthasas tat-prayātāḥ
saudhīṃ yadvad vṛṣṭim ante labhante ।। [] tasminn idam adbhutam udbhūtam abhūt-
