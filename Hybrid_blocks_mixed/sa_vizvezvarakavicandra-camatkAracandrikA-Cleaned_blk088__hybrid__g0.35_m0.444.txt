atra gaṅgetyarthe kanīyasi bhagīrathatapaḥphalamiti padādhikyam । tathāpi tasya sagarakutapābanakriyāsūcakatvena gaṅgāśabdādatyutkarṣāpādakatvamiti guṇatvam ।
vedaśāstraikavikhyātamapratītamihocyate ।।
yathā śrīsiṅgadharaṇīpāla bhavataḥ sphyena bhūyate ।
mitraśrīrāmamanunā garvavṛddhikitā dviṣām ।।
