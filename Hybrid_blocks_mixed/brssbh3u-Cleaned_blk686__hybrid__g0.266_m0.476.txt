teṣāmapi ca vidhurādīnāmaviruddhaiḥ puruṣamātrasaṃbandhibhirjapopavāsadevatārādhanādibhirdharmaviśeṣairanugraho vidyāyāḥ saṃbhavati ।
tathāca smṛtiḥ - ajapyenaiva tu saṃsiddhyedbrāhmaṇo nātra saṃśayaḥ ।
kuryādantra vā kuryānmantro brāhmaṇa ucyate' ityasaṃbhavadāśramakarmāṇo api japye adhikāraṃ darśayati ।
janmāntarānuṣṭhitairapi cāśramakarmabhiḥ saṃbhavatyeva vidyāyā anugrahaḥ ।
tathāca smṛtiḥ - aanekajanmasaṃsiddhastato yāti parāṃ gatim' iti janmāntarasaṃcitānāmapi saṃskāraviśeṣānanugrahītṛnvidyāyāṃ darśayati ।
dṛṣṭārthā ca vidyā pratiṣedhābhāvamātreṇāpyartinamadhikaroti śravaṇādiṣu ।
tasmādvidhurādīnāmapyadhikāro na virudhyate ।।
