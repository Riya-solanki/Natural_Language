atra snigdhaprāṇavarṇaprāyatvaṃ sandarbhasya mṛdutvam । śruṅgāratvādarthasta mṛdutvam । atra rūkṣamahāprāṇavarṇapramāyatvena saṃdarbhasya gāḍhatvam । pratibhaṭadhvaṃsanarupatvādarthasyagāḍhatvam ।
mṛdvarthā prauḍhasaṃdarbhā bhāratīvṛttiriṣyate ।।
yathā tajjitvā parirambhaṇaprabhṛtikaṃ dyūte sakhīnāṃ puraḥ śāryāropaṇasūcite paṇavidhau śrīśiṅgapṛthvīkṣitā ।
