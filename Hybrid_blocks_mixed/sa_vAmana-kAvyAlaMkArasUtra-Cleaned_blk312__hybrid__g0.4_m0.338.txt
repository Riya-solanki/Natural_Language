।।
iṣṭaḥ puṃpuṃsakayoḥ prāyeṇa ।।
punnapuṃsakayorupamānopameyayorliṅgabhedaḥ prāyeṇa bāhulyeneṣṭaḥ । yathā candramiva mukhaṃ paśyati iti, induriva mukhaṃ bhāti evamprāyaṃ tu necchanti ।।
laukikyāṃ samāsābhihitāyāmupamāprapañce ca ।।
laukikyāmupamāyāṃ samāsābhihitāyāmupamāyāmupamāprapañce ceṣṭo liṅgabhedaḥ prāyeṇeti । laukikyāṃ yathā
