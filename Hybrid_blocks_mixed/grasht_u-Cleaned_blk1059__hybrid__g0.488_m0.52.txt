taccāha atyamletyādi ।।
atyamlaṃ cukrādi atilavaṇaṃ kṣārādi atikaṭukaṃ nimbakaṭukītyādi etai rasasaṃsrāvo jaro jāraṇakaro bhavati jaratīti jaraḥ ।।
punarapi madhuraiḥ ikṣurasādibhiḥ satatabhuktaiḥ jaṭharavahniḥ koṣṭhāgniḥ vinaśyati abhyāśrayo vinaśyatītyabhiprāyaḥ ।।
