kutaḥ -ubhayaliṅgāt ।
śrutiliṅgātsmṛtiliṅgācca ।
śrutiliṅgaṃ tāvat atametaṃ vedānuvacanena brāhmaṇā vividiṣanti' iti siddhavadutpannarūpāṇyeva yajñādīni vividiṣāyāṃ viniyuṅ kte natu juhvatītyādivadapūrvameṣāṃ rūpamutpādayatīti ।
smṛtiliṅgamapi aanāśritaḥ karmaphalaṃ kāryaṃ karma karoti yaḥ' iti vijñātakartavyatākameva karma vidyotpattyarthaṃ darśayati ।
yasyaite aṣṭācatvāriṃśatsaṃskārā ityādyā ca saṃskāratvaprasiddhirvaidikeṣu karmasu tatsaṃskṛtasya vidyotpattimabhipretya smṛtau bhavati ।
tasmātsādvidamabhedāvadhāraṇam ।।
