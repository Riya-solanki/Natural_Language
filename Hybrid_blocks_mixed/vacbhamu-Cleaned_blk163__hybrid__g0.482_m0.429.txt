syādetat ।
muktau satyāmasyaite śuddhatvādayaḥ prathante, tatastu prāk dehādyabhedena taddharmajanmajarāmaraṇādiduḥkhayogādityata uktam- mukta iti ।
sadaiva muktaḥ sadaiva kevalo anādyavidyāvaśāttu bhrāntyā tathāvabhāsata ityarthaḥ ।
