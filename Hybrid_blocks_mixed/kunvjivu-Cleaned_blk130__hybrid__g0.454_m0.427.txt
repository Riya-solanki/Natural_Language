tra ।।
atra bāṣpanivahādīnāmacetavānāmapi cetanatvādhyāropeṇa kavinā kartṛtvamupanibaddhamyattasyā vivaśāyāḥ satyāsteṣāmevaṃvidho vyavahāraḥ, sā punaḥ svayaṃ na kiñcidapyācarituṃ samarthetyabhiprāyaḥ ।
anyacca kapolādīnāṃ tadavayavānāmetadavasthatvaṃ pratyakṣatayāsmadādigocaratāmāpadyate, tasyāḥ punaryo asāvantarvikāravyatikarastaṃ tadanubhavaikaviṣayatvādvayaṃ na janīmaḥ ।
