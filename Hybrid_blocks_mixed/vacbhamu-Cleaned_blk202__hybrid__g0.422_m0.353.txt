na caikasya pratibhāne anāśvāsa iti yuktam ।
na hi bahūnāmapyajñānāṃ vijñānāṃ vā āśayadoṣavatāṃ pratibhāne yukta āśvāsaḥ; tattvajñānavatastvapāstasamastadoṣasyaikasyāpi pratibhāne yukta evāśvāsaḥ ।
sargādibhuvāṃ ca prajāpatidevarṣīṇāṃ dharmajñānavairāgyaiśvaryasaṃpannānāmupapadyate tatsvarūpāvadhāraṇam; tatpratyayena cārvācīnānāmapi tatra saṃpratyaya ityupapannaṃ brahmaṇaḥ śāstrayonitvam, śāstrasya cāpauruṣeyatvam, prāmāṇyaṃ ceti ।
iti prathamavarṇakam ।
