paṅke vā saikate vā sunibhṛtapadavīvedino yāntu dāśāḥ kuñjeṣu kṣuṇṇavīrunnicayaparicayā ballavāḥ saṃcarantu
vyādhā vyāghrāṭavīṣu svaparapadavido ye ca randhreṣvabhijñā ē̃ ye siddhavyañjanā vā pratimuninilayaṃ te ca cārāścarantu ।।
