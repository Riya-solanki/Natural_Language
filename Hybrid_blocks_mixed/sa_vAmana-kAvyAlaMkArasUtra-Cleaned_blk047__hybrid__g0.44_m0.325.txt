।।
saṃdarbheṣu daśarūpakaṃ śreyaḥ ।।saṃdarbheṣu prabandheṣu daśarūpakaṃ nāṭakādi śreyaḥ ।।
kasmāttadāha 
taddhi citraṃ citrapaṭavadviśeṣasākalyāt ।।
taddaśarūpakaṃ hi yasmāñcitraṃ citrapaṭacat । viśeṣāṇāṃ sākalyāt ।।
tato'nyabhedakḷptiḥ ।।
tato daśarūpakādanyeṣāṃ bhedānāṃ kḷptiḥ kalpanamiti । daśarūpakasyaiva hīdaṃ sarvaṃ vilasitam, yacca kathākhyāyike mahākāvyamiti । tallakṣaṇaṃ ca nātīva hṛdayaṅgamamityupekṣitamasmābhiḥ । tadanyato grāhyam ।।
iti śrīkāvyālaṅkārasūtravṛttau śārīre prathame'dhikaraṇe tṛtīyo'dhyāyaḥ ।।
samāptaṃ cedaṃ śārīraṃ prathamamadhikaraṇam ।
