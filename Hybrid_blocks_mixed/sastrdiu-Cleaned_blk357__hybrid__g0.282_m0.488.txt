cet na ।
asati saṃbandhe pratyāyakatvāsaṃbhavāt ।
kena saṃbandhena śabdor'thaṃ pratyāyayatītyetadeva hi nirūpyate ।
tatra pratyāyakatvādeva pratyāyakatvamityātmāśrayadoṣāpattiḥ, tasmānnāsti saṃbandhaḥ ।
asati ca saṃbandhe vastvantarasya vastvantarabodhakatvāsaṃbhavādanirūpitahetuviśeṣaṃ svapnajñānavadyādṛcchikaṃ śabdārthajñānaṃ na pramāṇaṃ bhavitumarhati ।
laukikastu vyavahāro asatyapi śabdaprāmāṇye
