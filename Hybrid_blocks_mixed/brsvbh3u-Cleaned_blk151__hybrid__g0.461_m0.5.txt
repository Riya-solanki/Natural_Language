vyāpteś ca samañjasam ।
adhyāso nāmeti ।
gauṇī buddhiradhyāsaḥ ।
yathā māṇavake anivṛttāyāmeva māṇavakabuddhivyapadeśavṛttau siṃhabuddhivyapadeśavṛttiḥ siṃho māṇavaka iti, evaṃ pratimāyāṃ vāsudevabuddhirnāmni ca brahmabuddhistathoṅkāra udgīthabuddhivyapadeśāviti ।
apavādaikatvaviśeṣaṇāni
