kūpa-maṇḍūkavat kaṃsaḥ kaṇḍūtiṃ khaṇḍayan nijām ।
tvat-karkaśa-bhujābhogi-saṅgharṣaṃ labdhum icchati ।।
[] yaṃ khalu mātari-puruṣaṃ puruṣottarmas tvam iha jīva-grāhaṃ grhīṣyasi । samūla-ghātaṃ haniṣyasy akṛta-kāraṃ kariṣyasi, kara-grāhaṃ gṛhṇaṃś ca viśrānti-paryantam aśrānti-vikramatayā virkakṣyasi ।
