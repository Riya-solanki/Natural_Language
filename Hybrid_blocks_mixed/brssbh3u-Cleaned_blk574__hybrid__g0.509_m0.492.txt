ajanako ha vaideho bahudakṣiṇena yajñeneje' ayakṣyamāṇo vai bhagavanto ahamasmi' ityevamādīni brahmavidāmapyanyapareṣu vākyeṣu karmasaṃbandhadarśanāni bhavanti ।
tathoddālakādīnāmapi putrānuśāsanādidarśanādgārhasthyasaṃbandho avagamyate ।
kevalāccejjñānātpuruṣārthasiddhiḥ syātkimarthamanekāyāsasamanvitāni karmāṇi te kuryuḥ aarke cenmadhu vindeta kimarthaṃ parvataṃ vrajet' iti nyāyāt ।।
