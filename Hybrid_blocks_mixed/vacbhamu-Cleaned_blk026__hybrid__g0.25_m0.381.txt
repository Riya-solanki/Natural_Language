tadevamuktena krameṇa ahaṃpratyaye pūtikūṣmāṇḍīkṛte bhagavatī śrutiḥ apratyūhaṃ kartṛtvabhoktṛtvasukhaduḥkhaśokādyātmatvamahamanubhavaprasañjitamātmano niṣeddhumarhatīti ।
tadevaṃ sarvapravādaśrutismṛtītihāsapurāṇaprathitamithyābhāvasyāhaṃpratyayasya svarūpanimittaphalairupavyākhyānam - anyonyasmin ityādi ।
anyonyasmindharmiṇyātmaśarīrādau anyonyātmakatāmadhyasya - ahamidaṃ śarīrāditi ।
aidam' iti ca vastutaḥ, na pratītitaḥ ।
lokavyavahāraḥ lokānāṃ vyavahāraḥ ।
sa ca aayamaham' iti vyapadeśaḥ ।
iti śabdasūcitaśca śarīrādyanukūlaṃ pratikūlaṃ ca prameyajātaṃ pramāṇena pramāya tadupādānaparivarjanādiḥ ।
