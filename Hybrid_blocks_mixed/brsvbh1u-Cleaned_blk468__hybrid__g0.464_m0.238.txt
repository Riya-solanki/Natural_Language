ayamabhiprāyaḥ-'jvaritaḥ pathyamaśnīyāt', ana sarpāyāṅguliṃ dadyāt' ityādivacanaśravaṇasamanantaraṃ prayojyavṛddhasya pathyāśane pravṛttiṃ bhujaṅgāṅgulidānonmukhasya ca tato nivṛttimupalabhya bālo vyutpitsuḥ prayojyavṛddhasya pravṛttinivṛttihetū icchādveṣāvanumimīte ।
tathā hi-icchādveṣahetuke vṛddhasya pravṛttinivṛttī svatantrapravṛttinivṛttitvāt, madīyasvatantrapravṛttinivṛttivat ।
