patyādiśabdebhyaḥ ।
sarvasya vaśī ।
vaśaḥ sāmarthyaṃ sarvasya jagataḥ prabhavatyayam, vyūhāvasthānasamartha iti ।
ata eva sarvasyeśānaḥ, sāmarthyena hyayamuktena sarvasyeṣṭe, tadicchānuvidhānājjagataḥ ।
ata eva sarvasyādhipatiḥ sarvasya niyantā ।
antaryāmīti yāvat ।
kiñca sa evaṃbhūto hṛdyantarjyotiḥ puruṣo vijñānamayo na sādhunā karmaṇā bhūyānutkṛṣṭo bhavatītyevamādyāḥ śrutayo asaṃsāriṇaṃ paramātmānameva pratipādayanti ।
tasmājjīvātmānaṃ mānāntarasiddhamanūdya tasya brahmabhāvapratipādanaparo ayo ayaṃ vijñānamayaḥ'ityādivākyasaṃdarbha iti siddham ।।
