yathā prakāśākāśasavitṛprabhṛtayo aṅgulikarakodakaprabhṛtiṣu karmasūpādhibhūteṣu saviśeṣā ivāvabhāsante, naca svābhāvikīmaviśeṣātmatāṃ jahati, evamupādhinimitta evāyamātmabhedaḥ svatatvaikātmyameva ।
tathāhi - vedānteṣvabhyāsenāsakṛjjīvaprājñayorabhedaḥ pratipādyate ।।
