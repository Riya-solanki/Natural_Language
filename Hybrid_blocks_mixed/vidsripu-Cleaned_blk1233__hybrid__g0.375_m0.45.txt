śīrṇakṣudrātapatrī jaṭharavalayitānekamātrāprapañcaś cūḍānirvyūḍhabilvacchada udaradarībhīṣaṇo jīrṇakaṇṭhaḥ ।
dūrādhvabhrāntikhinnaḥ katham api śanakair aṅghripīḍāṃ niyamya svairendhasphoṭanāya dvijabhavanam anu snātakaḥ sāyam eti ।।
