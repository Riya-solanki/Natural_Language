prahartā kvānaṅgaḥ sa ca kusumadhanvālpaviśikhaś calaṃ sūkṣmaṃ lakṣyaṃ vyavahitam amūrtaṃ kva ca manaḥ ।
itīmām udbhūtāṃ sphuṭam anupapattiṃ manasi me rujām āvirbhāvād anubhavavirodhaḥ śamayati ।।
