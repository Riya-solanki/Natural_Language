itthaṃ śaptā ।
tacchravaṇaparyākulābhyāṃ sakhībhyāṃ pravāsyamāno api munipravaraḥ priyatamanyāsāṅgulīyakavilokanaṃ vasānā makārṣot ।
priyaṃ prati yāntyāśca muniduhituralaṅkṛtāṅgulīkisalayasyāṅgalīyakasya kutracit kuṭilatarataraṅgiṇīpayovatārādantarjalamalakṣitaṃ paribhraṣṭasya sasaṃbhramamadabhramarīcimaṇḍalamāṇikyasamullasitasarasāmiṣaviśaṅkākulaśakalīkavalitasya kālāntare tadantakāriṇā kaivartanena punarapi ।
evaṃvidhasya saṃvidhānakasya rasanidhānakalaśāyamānasya māhātmyādikhilasyāpi nāṭakasya kāpi samaye cañcarīkopālambhagarbhagītyavagamanāt muniśāpāpasāritapreyasīsmṛterapi tadadhivāsavāsanāpi ca parisphurantī pauravasya pāravaśyaṃ niścayāmāsa ।
