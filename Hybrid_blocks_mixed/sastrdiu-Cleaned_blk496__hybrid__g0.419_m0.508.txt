prāgapi ca śakyamevāpūrvamavagantum anekeṣāmaṅgapradhānakarmaṇāṃ kṣaṇikānāmapūrvamantareṇa yaugapadyāsaṃbhavāt, paśvādīnāṃ ca dṛṣṭakāraṇāpekṣatvānna kṣaṇamātreṇotpattiḥ saṃbhavatītyavaśyaṃbhāvyapūrvam ।
na ca paścādyutpattimātraṃ phalam upabhogasya tu cirakālatvāt yāvadupabhogamavasthāsyamānena kenacidbhavitavyam ।
na ca kriyāyāstāvankālamavasthānaṃ saṃbhavatītyapūrvamaṅgīkartavyamiti taddvāreṇa
