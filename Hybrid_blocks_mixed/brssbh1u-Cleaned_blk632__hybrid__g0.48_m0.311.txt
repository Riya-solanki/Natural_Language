itaśca na śūdrasyādhikāraḥ, yadvidyāpradeśeṣūpanayanādayaḥ saṃskārāḥ parāmṛśyante- ataṃ hopaninye' ।
a adhīhi bhagava iti hopasasāda' a brahmaparā brahmaniṣṭhāḥ paraṃ brahmānveṣamāṇā eṣa ha vai tatsarvaṃ vakṣyatīti te ha samitpāṇayo bhagavantaṃ pippalādamupasannāḥ' iti ca ।
a tānhānupanīyaiva' ityapi pradarśitaivopanayanaprāptirbhavati ।
śūdrasya saṃskārābhāvo abhilapyate, aśūdraścaturtho varṇa ekajātiḥ'
