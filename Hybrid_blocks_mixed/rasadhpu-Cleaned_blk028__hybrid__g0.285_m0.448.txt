yasmin lohakhalve catvāraḥ pādā mañcikayābhibhavanti sa catuṣpāda ucyate ।। 
tasmin lohakhalve grastābhrakasūtaṃ tathā catuḥṣaṣṭitamena sūtakasya kāntalohacūrṇaṃ kṣiptvā yathā viḍena saha loṣṭikayā mandaṃ mandaṃ mandaṃ mardayet ।। 
adhaśca komalāgnir jvālayet ।। 
evaṃ kurvatā yadi kāntalohacūrṇacatuḥṣaṣṭitamo jīrṇo bhavati tadā punarapi catuḥṣaṣṭitamabhāgena kāntalohacūrṇaṃ kṣiptvā thūthāviḍena peṣaṇīyam ।। 
adhaścāgnir jvālanīyaḥ ।। 
evaṃ pūrvakṣipte lohacūrṇe jīrṇe punaḥ punas tāvac catuḥṣaṣṭitamāṃśena lohacūrṇaṃ kṣepyam ।। 
yāvat sūtād aṣṭaguṇalohacūrṇe jīryati ।। 
ityarthaṃ lohacūrṇasya kaṣāyeṇa rasaḥ pāśitaḥ san godhūmavarṇo bhavati ।। 
tathā rāgāṃśca sahate ।। 
atha prakāśādikarājirāgāś ca jīryati ।। 
atra ca yadi kāntalohaṃ na labhyate tadā tīkṣṇāyasaṃ sāralohac ।। 
tathāya tvagheraṃ thūthakaṃ tat kāñjikena saṃghṛṣya candanaṃ kṣipyate ।। 
etat thūthāviḍam ityucyate ।। 
agre'pi sarvatra thūthāviḍe śabdenedameva jñeyam ।। 
iti grastābhrakasūtasya lohajāraṇaṃ dvitīyam ।। 
lohakhalve catuṣpāde jīrṇalohaṃ rasaṃ kṣipet ।
ayaḥ prakāśe rājeśca catuḥṣaṣṭyaṃśacūrṇakam ।।
thūthāviḍena sampiṣya mṛdvagnir jvālayed adhaḥ ।
sūtādaṣṭaguṇā jāryā rājiścāyaḥprakāśikā ।।
ayaḥprakāśarājau ca jīrṇāyāṃ pītatā bhavet ।
raktatāpādanārthaṃ ca himarājiṃ ca jārayet ।।
