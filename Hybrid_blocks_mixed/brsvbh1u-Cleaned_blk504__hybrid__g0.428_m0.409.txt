api ca mantrabrāhmaṇayorupeyopāyabhūtayoḥ saṃpratipatterbrahmaivānandamayapadārthaḥ ।
mantre hi punaḥ punaḥ aanyo antara ātmā'iti parabrahmaṇyāntaratvaśravaṇāt, tasyaiva ca aanyo antara ātmānandamayaḥ'iti brāhmaṇe pratyabhijñānāt, parabrahmaivānandamayamityāha sūtrakāraḥ-
