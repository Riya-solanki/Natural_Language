syādetat ।
yathā cakṣurādīnāṃ jīvaṃ prati guṇabhūtatvājjīvasya ca śreṣṭhatvājjīvaḥ svatantra evaṃ prāṇo api prādhānyāt śreṣṭhatvācca svatantraḥ prāpnoti ।
naca dvayoḥ svatantrayorekasmin śarīre ekavākyatvamupapadyata ityaparyāyaṃ viruddhānekadikkriyatayā deha unmathyeta ।
iti prāpte, ucyate-
