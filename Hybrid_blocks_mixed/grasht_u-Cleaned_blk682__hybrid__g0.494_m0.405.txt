drutividhānamāha vajravallyā ityādi ।।
vajravallyāḥ svarasena svakīyena rasena gaganamabhrasattvaṃ sauvarcalānvitaṃ rucakasahitaṃ piṣṭaṃ kuryāt iti śeṣaḥ ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭaṃ gaganaṃ niculapuṭair vetasadravabhāvanābhiḥ pakvaṃ vahnipuṭitaṃ tatpakvaṃ san nirlepaṃ saṃparkavarjitaṃ rasarūpaṃ bhavati pāradasya rūpamityarthaḥ ।।
