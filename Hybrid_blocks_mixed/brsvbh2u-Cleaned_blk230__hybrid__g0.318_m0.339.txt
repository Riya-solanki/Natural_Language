na ca kartuḥ karaṇam ।
yadyapyanekaśilpaparyavadātaḥ paraśuṃ kṛtvā tena palāśaṃ chinatti,
yadyapi ca prayatnenendriyārthātmamanaḥ saṃnikarṣalakṣaṇaṃ jñānakaraṇamupādāyātmārthaṃ vijānāti, tathāpi saṃkarṣaṇo akaraṇaḥ kathaṃ pradyumnākhyaṃ manaḥ karaṇaṃ kuryāt ।
akaraṇasya vā karaṇanirmāṇasāmarthye kṛtaṃ karaṇanirmāṇena ।
akaraṇādeva nikhilakāryasiddheriti bhāvaḥ ।।
