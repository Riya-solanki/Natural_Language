abhrakajīrṇarasalakṣaṇam āha dhūmra ityādi ।।
abhrajīrṇarasa evaṃvidho bhavati abhraṃ jīrṇaṃ yasmin sa tathoktaḥ ।।
vahniyogāt prathamaṃ dhūmro dhūmrābho bhavati punaściṭiciṭiśabdo bhavati tato maṇḍūkagatirbhavati punastathā tena prakāreṇa dhṛte sati sakampo bhavati punarvahnau niṣkampaḥ svastho bhavati abhrasatvadrutasya lakṣaṇamiti ।।
tadevāha kapila ityādi ।।
punarevaṃvidho raso'bhrajīrṇo vijñātavyaḥ ।।
kīdṛśaḥ kapilaḥ varṇataḥ nirudgārī sthirabhāvaḥ punaḥ sa raso vipluṣabhāvaṃ cañcalatvaṃ muñcate ।।
punarniṣkampaḥ svasthaḥ punargatirahitaḥ pakṣacchinnaḥ iti lakṣaṇānyabhrajīrṇasya bhavanti ।।
