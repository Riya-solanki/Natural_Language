na ।
sthūlasūkṣmatāratamyakrameṇāparamakāraṇādvināśopapatteḥ ।
yathā pṛthivī dvyaṇukādyapekṣayā sthūlatamā vastubhūtāpi vinaśyati, tataḥ sūkṣmaṃ sūkṣmataraṃ ca pṛthivyekajātīyakaṃ vinaśyati, tato dvyaṇukaṃ, tathā paramāṇavo api pṛthivyekajātīyakatvādvinaśyeyuḥ ।
