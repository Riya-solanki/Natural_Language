।।
lolamānādayaścānaśi ।।
lolamāno vellamāna ityādayaścānaśi draṣṭavyāḥ । śānacastvabhāvaḥ, parasmaipaditvāddhātūnāmiti ।।
labhergatyarthatvāṇṇicyaṇau kartuḥ karmatvākarmatve ।।
astyayaṃ labhiryaḥ prāptyupasarjanāṃ gatimāha asti ca gatyupasarjanāṃ prāptimāheti । atra pūrvasminpakṣe gatyarthatvāllabherṇicyaṇau yaḥ kartā tasya gatyādisūtreṇa karmasaṃjñā । yathā dīrghikāsu kumudāni vikāsaṃ lambhayanti śiśirāḥ śaśibhāsaḥ ।
