atra ripuhiṃsanarūpatvā darthasya prauḍhatvam । alpaprāṇavarṇaprāyatvātsaṃdarbhasya mṛdutvam ।
mṛdvartha īṣatprauḍhasaṃdarbhā madhyamakaiśikī ।।
yathā
tvāmāśriteṣvavasaro api na hi kraśimnāṃ taccitamāśrayati ca tvayi sā natabhrūḥ ।
ākhyāti hanta tanimānamiti prasaṅge kasyāyaśo vimṛśa cetasi siṅgabhūpa ।।
