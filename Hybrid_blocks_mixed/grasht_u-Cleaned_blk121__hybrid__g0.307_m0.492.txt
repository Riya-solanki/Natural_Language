aṣṭamoddiṣṭasya dīpanasaṃskārasya vidhiṃ spaṣṭayannāha bhūkhagetyādi ।।
asau pūrvasaṃskṛtarasa etair auṣadhais tridinaṃ nirantaraṃ yathā syāt tathā svedena dīpitaḥ kṣutpīḍitaḥ san grāsārthī kavalābhilāṣī jāyate ।।
etair oṣadhaiḥ bhūkhagaṭaṅkaṇamaricaiḥ na kevalam etaiḥ punar lavaṇāsurīśigrukāñjikaiḥ ।।
bhūḥ tuvarī khagaḥ kāsīsaṃ ṭaṅkaṇaṃ saubhāgyaṃ maricam ūṣaṇam etaiḥ ।।
punaḥ lavaṇaṃ saindhavam āsurī rājikā śigruḥ saubhāñjanaṃ vṛkṣaviśeṣaḥ kāñjikaṃ pūrvoktam amlībhūtam etaiśceti ।।
viśeṣaścātra ।
svedanaṃ rasarājasya kṣārāmlaviṣamadyakaiḥ ।
bījapūraṃ samādāya vṛntamutsṛjya kārayet ।।
tasya madhye kṣipetsūtaṃ kalāṃśakṣārasaṃyutaṃ ।
dvāraṃ nirudhya yatnena vastramadhye nibandhayet ।।
dolāsvedaḥ prakartavya ekaviṃśaddināvadhi ।
dine dine prakartavyaṃ nūtanaṃ bījapūrakam ।।
lelihāno hi dhātūṃśca pīḍyamāno bubhukṣayā ।
amunaiva prakartavyaṃ rasarājasya dīpanam ।।
tryahaṃ saptadinaṃ vātha caturdaśaikaviṃśatim ।
saṃskāraḥ sūtarāje tu kramāt kramataraṃ varam ।।
iti ।।
