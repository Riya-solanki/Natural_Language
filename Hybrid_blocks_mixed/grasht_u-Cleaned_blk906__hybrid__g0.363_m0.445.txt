svarṇamākṣika gold
tāpyaṃ cāṃgulisaṃjñaṃ cūrṇaṃ kṛtvā tadantare dattvā ।
śulbasya guptamūṣā kāryā puṭitāpyatha ca dhmātā ।।
hemnā militaṃ vidhinā mātrātulyaṃ bhavatyeva ।
tāpyavidhānamāha tāpyamityādi ।।
aṅgulisaṃjñaṃ tāpyaṃ svarṇamākṣikaṃ cūrṇaṃ kṛtvā tadantare taccūrṇam antare madhye dattvā śulbasya tāmrasya guptamūṣā andhamūṣā kāryā tatra nale ityabhiprāyaḥ ।।
sā mūṣā puṭitā dhmātā kāryeti vidhānam uktam ।।
taccāha hemnetyādi ।।
pakvaṃ yanmākṣikacūrṇaṃ taddhemnā kṛtvā ।।
