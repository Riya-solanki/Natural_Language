vedyaṃ hi rūpaṃ vidyāyā dravyadaivatamiva yāgasya ।
vedyaścaika evesvaraḥ śrutinānatve apyavagamyate amanomayaḥ prāṇaśarīraḥ' akaṃ brahma khaṃ brahma' asatyakāmaḥ satyasaṃkalpaḥ' ityevamādiṣu ।
tathā eka eva prāṇaḥ prāṇo vāva saṃvargaḥ' aprāṇo vāva jyeṣṭhaśca śreṣṭhaśca' aprāṇo ha pitā prāṇo mātā' ityevamādiṣu ।
