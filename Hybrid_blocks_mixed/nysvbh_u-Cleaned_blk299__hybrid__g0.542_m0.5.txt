ātmamanaḥsaṃnikarṣo hi kāraṇāntaraṃ noktamiti, na cāsaṃyukte dravye saṃyogajanyasya guṇasyotpattiriti jñānotpattidarśanādātmamanaḥsaṃnikarṣaḥ kāraṇam।
manaḥsaṃnikarṣānapekṣasya cendrinayārthasaṃnikarṣasya jñānakāraṇatve yugapadutpadyeran buddhaya iti manaḥsaṃnikarṣopi kāraṇam ।।
