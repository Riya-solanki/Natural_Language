pramāthagaṇavaṃśasya prathamaḥ syām ahaṃ vibho 
mahākāla iti khyātaḥ khyātiṃ gaccheyam īśvara ।। 
evaṃ bhaviṣyatīty āha devo bāṇaṃ mahādyutiḥ । divyarūpo akṣato gātrair nīrujas tvaṃ mamāśrayāt ।। mamātisargād bāṇa tvaṃ bhava caivākutobyahaḥ । 
bhūyo api te varaṃ dadmi prakhyātabalapauruṣa 
evaṃ bhaviṣyaty avaśyaṃ yaṃ varaṃ tvam ihecchasi । 
taṃ taṃ vṛṇīṣva bhadraṃ te yad icchasi mahāsura ।।
