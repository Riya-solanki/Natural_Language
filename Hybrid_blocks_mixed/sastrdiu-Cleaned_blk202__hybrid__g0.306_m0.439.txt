sahopalambhaniyamo api jñānārthayoraniyamavyāptaṃ bhedaṃ vyāpakaviruddhopalabdhyā pratiṣedhanna bhedamupasthāpayati, bhinnānāṃ ghaṭapaṭādīnāmaniyatasahopalambhatvāt ।
na cāsiddhau vedyopalambhasamaye vitterupalambha iti vācyam ।
uttarakālaṃ sahaiva jñānena viṣayasmaraṇadarśanāt- jñāto ghaṭa iti ।
nacānubhūtasya smaraṇaṃ saṃbhavati, tasmādutpattivelāyāmeva jñānasya
