"atha samādhayo iṣyet tena sūkṣmavajrādārabdhavya samādhimabhirocet taṃ hṛdayayogato abhyaset, tāvadyāvac caturmāsaṃ । tato ante sakalāṃ [rātriṃ paryaṅkāvikṣaptasamāpannena tiṣṭhet । tataḥ prabhāte sarvatathāgatādyāḥ] sarvasiddhaya āmukhībhavanti । tato yādṛśī abhirucistādṛ[śīmavāpnotī-"tyāha bhagavān vajrasatvaḥ ।।]
