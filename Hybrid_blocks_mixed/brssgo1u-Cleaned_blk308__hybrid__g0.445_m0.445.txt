tathā mukhyaprāṇaliṅgamapi- aatha khalu prāṇa eva prajñātmedaṃ śarīraṃ parigṛhyotthāpayati' iti ।
śarīradhāraṇaṃ ca mukhyaprāṇasya dharmaḥ, prāṇasaṃvāde vāgādīnprāṇānprakṛtya- atānvariṣṭhaḥ prāṇa uvāca mā mohamāpadyathāhamevaitatpañcadhātmānaṃ pravibhajyaitadprāṇamavaṣṭabhya vidhārayāmi' iti śravaṇāt ।
ye tu imaṃ śarīraṃ parigṛhya iti paṭhanti teṣāmimaṃ jīvamindriyagrāmaṃ vā parigṛhya śarīramutthāpayatīti vyākhyeyam ।
prajñātmatvamapi jīve tāvaccetanatvādupapannam ।
mukhye api prāṇe prajñāsādhanaprāṇāntarāśrayatvādupapannameva ।
