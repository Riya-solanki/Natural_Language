tatra guṇātiśayabhedeṣu dhairyātiśayo yathā
rūkṣāhaṃkṛtayo na santi lalita bhedaṃ gatena bhruvau dṛkkoṇo api na rāgameti hasitaṃ mandaṃ ca nāntarhitam ।
dṛṣṭvāpi pratigarjatāṃ kṣitibhujāṃ saṃraṃbhagarbhaśriyā mūrtirdāruṇaceṣṭayāpyavikṛtā śrīsiṅgapṛṣvīpateḥ ।।
