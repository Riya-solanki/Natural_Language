neti brūmaḥ ।
kasmāt ।
pratyekamatadvacanāt ।
pratyupādhibhedaṃ hyabhedameva brahmaṇaḥ śrāvayati śāstram - ayaścāyamasyāṃ pṛthivyāṃ tejomayo amṛtamayaḥ puruṣo yaścāyamadhyātmaṃ śārīrastejomayo amṛtamayaḥ puruṣo ayameva sa yo ayamātmā' ityādi ।
ataśca na bhinnākārayogo brahmaṇaḥ śāstrīya iti śakyate vaktum ।
bhedasyopāsanārthatvādabhede tātparyāt ।
