imāvāropādhyavasānarūpau sādṛśyahetū bhedau {gaurvāhīkaḥ} ityatra {gaurayam} ityatra ca ।
atra hi svārthasahacāriṇo guṇā jāḍyamāndyādayo lakṣyamāṇā api gośabdasya parārthābhidhāne pravṛttinimittatvamupayāntiṃ iti kecit । svārthasahacāriguṇābhedena padārthagatā guṇā eva lakṣyante na
parārtho abhidhīyate ityanye । sādhāraṇaguṇāśrayatvena parārtha eva lakṣyate ityapare ।
uktaṃ cānyatra
{abhidheyāvinābhūtapratītirlakṣaṇocyate ।
lakṣyamāṇaguṇairyogādvṛtteriṣṭā tu gauṇatāṃ} iti ।
