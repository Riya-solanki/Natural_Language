lohayogamuktvā vaṅgayogamāha vaṅgamityādi ।।
atha lohakathanānantaraṃ vaṅgaṃ khurasaṃjñakaṃ abhrakaṃ ca etaddvayaṃ tālakaṣaḍbhāgasāritaṃ tālakasya ṣaḍaṃśena ekaśarīratāṃ nītaṃ tatsvarūpaṃ rasaścarati ।।
kasmāt abhiṣavayogāt abhiṣavaḥ saṃmardanaṃ tadyogāt ṣuñ abhiṣave ityasya dhāto rūpaṃ abhiṣavaḥ ।।
punaḥ rasaścarati milati ।।
cāritaṃ yat taddravati taddrutaṃ rase vrajati pṛthaktvāt saṃ milati nātrasandehaḥ niḥ saṃdigdhamiva ।।
vaṅgasya laghudravitvāt ṣaḍaṃśayogastālakasya yuktaḥ lohajāteḥ kāṭhiṇyāt samabhāgatvaṃ uditam ।।
