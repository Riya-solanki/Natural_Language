iyadāmananāt ।
mantradvaye api pratipādanaprakārabhedāt jñeyaikyabhānācca saṃśayamāha-kimatreti ।
ṛtapānavākye aakṣaraṃ brahma yatparam'iti guṇāḥ śrutāḥ, suparṇavākye anaśnatvādayasteṣāṃ mitho anupasaṃhāra iti pūrvapakṣaphalaṃ, siddhānte tūpasaṃhāre brahmasvarūpavākyārthaikyādupasaṃhāra iti vivekaḥ ।
astu vedyaikyādakṣaradhiyāmupasaṃhāraḥ ।
iha tu vedyabhedānnopasaṃhāra iti pratyudāharaṇena pūrvapakṣaḥ ।
nanvayaṃ guṇādhikaraṇe nirasta iti cet, satyaṃ, kintu pibatpadasya mukhyārthatvāya svataḥ kalpanayā ca pānakṛtyāśrayau buddhijīvau pibantau grāhyau, suparṇau tu jīveśvarāvidyādhikāśaṅkāyāṃ mantradvaye api dvivacanaśabdasāmānyādautpattikadvitvaviśiṣṭatayā tulyavastudvayapratyabhijñānasya bādhakābhāvātprakaraṇādyanugrahācca jīvānuvādenāsaṃsāribrahmaṇi mantradvayatātparyamiti prapañcārthamidaṃ sūtramiti bhāvaḥ ।।
