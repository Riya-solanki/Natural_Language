parasamavetakriyāphalaśālitvābhāvāt, caitravat ।
yathā hi caitrasamavetakriyayā caitranagaraprāptāvubhayasamavetāyāmapi kriyamāṇāyāṃ nagarasyaiva karmatā, parasamavetakriyāphalaśālitvāt, na tu caitrasya kriyāphalaśālino api, caitrasamavāyādgamanakriyāyā iti, tanna ।
śrutivirodhāt ।
śrūyate hi asatyaṃ jñānamanantaṃ brahma'iti upapadyate ca, tathā hi-yo ayamarthaprakāśaḥ phalaṃ yasminnarthaśca ātmā ca prathete sa kiṃ jaḍaḥ svayaṃprakāśo vā ।
jaḍaścetviṣayātmānāvapi jaḍāviti kasminkiṃ prakāśetāviśeṣāt, iti prāptamāndhyamaśeṣasya jagataḥ ।
tathā cābhāṇakaḥ ।
aandhasyevāndhalagnasya vinipātaḥ pade pade' ।
na ca nilīnameva vijñānamarthātmānau jñāpayati, cakṣurādivaditi vācyam ।
jñāpanaṃ hi jñānajananam, janitaṃ ca jñānaṃ jaḍaṃ sannoktadūṣaṇamativarteteti ।
evamuttarottarāṇyapi jñānāni jaḍānītyanavasthā ।
tasmādaparādhīnaprakāśā saṃvidupetavyā ।
