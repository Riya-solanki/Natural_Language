pūrvaprakṛtāpekṣāyāśca phalata ānantaryāvyatirekāt ।
asyārthaḥ- na vayamānantaryārthatāṃ vyasanitayā rocayāmahe, kiṃ tu brahmajijñāsāhetubhūtapūrvaprakṛtasiddhaye ।
sā ca pūrvaprakṛtārthāpekṣatve apyathaśabdasya sidhyatīti vyartha ānantaryārthatvāvadhāraṇāgraho asmākamiti ।
tadidamuktam aphalataḥ' iti ।
paramārthatastu kalpāntaropanyāse pūrvaprakṛtāpekṣā; na ceha kalpāntaropanyāsa iti pāriśeṣyādānantaryārtha eveti yuktam ।
