yadi cāvagatamevānumānenāvagamyate tataḥ smaraṇameva pramuṣitatadbhāvamanumānajñānaṃ syāt ।
naca pratyutpannakāraṇajatvādasmṛtitvam, saṃskāraścodbodhitaḥ smṛtiṃ janayatītyavivādam, smṛteśca pramoṣaḥ śuktikārajatādivedaneṣu kḷpta eva ।
tadevaṃ kḷptenaiva
