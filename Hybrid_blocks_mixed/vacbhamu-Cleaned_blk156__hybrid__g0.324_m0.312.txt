nigūḍhābhiprāyaścodayati- nanu śeṣaṣaṣṭhīparigrahe api iti ।
sāmānyasaṃbandhasya viśeṣasaṃbandhāvirodhena karmatāyā avighātena jijñāsānirūpaṇopapatterityarthaḥ ।
nigūḍhābhiprāya eva dūṣayati- evamapi pratyakṣaṃ brahmaṇa iti ।
vācyasya karmatvasya jijñāsayā prathamamapekṣitasya prathamasaṃbandhārhasya cānvayaparityāgena paścātkathañcidapekṣitasya saṃbandhimātrasya saṃbandho, jaghanyaḥ prathamaḥ prathamaśca jaghanyaḥ, iti suvyāhṛtaṃ nyāyatattvam ।
pratyakṣaparokṣatābhidhānaṃ ca prāthamyāprāthamyasphuṭatvāsphuṭatvābhiprāyam ।
