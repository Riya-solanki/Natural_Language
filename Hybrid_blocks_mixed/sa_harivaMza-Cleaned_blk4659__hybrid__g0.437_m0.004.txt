asakṛd devasahitaḥ śacībhartā sureśvaraḥ 
aprāpta eva nagaraṃ pitrā te mṛdito raṇe 
ayaṃ devasamūhasya bhayadas te pitā raṇe ।। 
airāvataḥ sa mṛdito gadayā cāsya bhāmini । nirāśo jīvite so abhūd vajrapāṇiḥ sahāmaraiḥ । mahāsuravaraḥ śrīmān baleḥ putro mahābalaḥ ।। evaṃ sābhihitā sakhyā bāṇaputrī yaśasvinī । svapnarūpaṃ yathā dṛṣtvaṃ nyavedayad aninditā । 
evaṃ saṃdūṣitā sādhvī kathaṃ jīvitum utsahe 
pitaraṃ kiṃ nu vakṣyāmi devaśatrum ariṃdamam ।।
