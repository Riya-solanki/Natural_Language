bhūmestrivṛtkṛtāyāḥ puruṣeṇopabhujyamānāyā māṃsādikāryaṃ yathāśabdaṃ niṣpadyate ।
tathāhi śrutiḥ - aannamaśitaṃ tredhā vidhīyate tasya yaḥ sthaviṣṭho dhātustatpurīṣaṃ bhavati yo madhyamastanmāṃsaṃ yo aṇiṣṭhastanmanaḥ' iti ।
