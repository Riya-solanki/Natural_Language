caṇḍa-vegāṃ rañjayitum aśaknuvan yogān ācaret
rāgasyopakrame saṃbādhasya kareṇopamardanaṃ tasyā rasa-prāpti-kāle ca rata-yojanam iti rāga-pratyānayanam
aupariṣṭakaṃ manda-vegasya gata-vayaso vyāyatasya rata-śrāntasya ca rāga-pratyānayanam
apadravyāṇi vā yojayet
tāni suvarṇa-rajata-tāmra-kālāyasa-gajadanta-gavala-dravya-mayāṇi
trāpuṣāṇi saisakāni ca mṛdūni śīta-vīryāṇi karmaṇi ca dhṛṣṇūni bhavantīti bābhravīyā yogāḥ
dāru-mayāni sāmyataś ceti vātsyāyanaḥ
liṅga-pramāṇāntaraṃ bindubhiḥ karkaśa-paryantaṃ bahulaṃ syāt
ete eva dve saṃghāṭī
tri-prabhṛti yāvat pramāṇaṃ vā cūḍakaḥ
ekām eva latikāṃ pramāṇa-vaśena veṣṭayed ity eka-cūḍakaḥ
ubhayatomukha-cchidraḥ sthūla-karkaśa-vṛṣaṇa-guṭikāyuktaḥ pramāṇa-vaśa-yogī kaṭyāṃ baddhaḥ kañcuko jālakaṃ vā
tad-abhāve alābūnālakaṃ veṇuś ca taila-kaṣāyaiḥ subhāvitaḥ sūtreṇa kaṭyāṃ baddhaḥ ślakṣṇā kāṣṭha-mālā vā grathitā bahubhir āmalakāsthibhiḥ saṃyuktety apaviddha-yogāḥ na tv aviddhasya kasya cid vyavahṛtir astīti
dākṣiṇātyānāṃ liṅgasya karṇayor iva vyadhanaṃ bālasya
yuvā tu śastreṇa chedayitvā yāvad rudhirasyāgamanaṃ tāvad udake tiṣṭhet
vaiśadyārthaṃ ca tasyāṃ rātrau nirbandhād vyavāyaḥ
tataḥ kaṣāyair eka-dināntaritaṃ śodhanam
vetasa-kuṭaja-śaṅkubhiḥ krameṇa vardhamānasya vardhanair bandhanam
yaṣṭī-madhukena madhu-yuktena śodhanam
tataḥ sīsaka-pattra-karṇikayā vardhayet
mrakṣayed bhallātaka-taileneti vyadhana-yogāḥ
tasminn anekākṛti-vikalpāny apadravyāṇi yojayet
vṛttam ekato vṛtaam udūkhalakaṃ kusumakaṃ kaṇṭakitaṃ kaṅkāsthi gaja-karakam aṣṭa-maṇḍalakaṃ bhramarakaṃ śṛṅgāṭakam anyāni vopāyataḥ karmataś ca bahu-karma-sahatā caiṣāṃ mṛdu-karkaśatā yathā-sātmyam
