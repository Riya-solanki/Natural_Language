dṛṣṭānta udāharaṇamiti prakṛtam ।
sādhyavaidharmyādataddharmabhāvī ca dṛṣṭānta udāharaṇamiti ।
anityaḥ śabda utpattidharmakatvāt anutpattidharmakaṃ nityamātmādi, so ayamātmādirdṛṣṭāntaḥ sādhyavaidharmyādanutpattidharmakatvādataddharmabhāvī-yo asau sādhyasya dharmo anityatvaṃ sa tasmin na bhavatīti।
atrātmādau dṛṣṭānta utpattidharmakatvasyābhāvādanityatvaṃ na bhavatīti upalabhamānaḥ śabde vapiryayamanuminoti, utpattidharmakatvasya bhāvādanityaḥ śabda iti।
sādharmyoktasya hetoḥ sādhyasādharmyāt taddharmabhāvī dṛṣṭānta udāharaṇam।
vaidharmyoktasya hetoḥ sādhyavaidharmyādataddharmabhāvī dṛṣṭānta udāharaṇam।
pūrvasmin dṛṣṭānte yau tau dharmauṃ sādhyasādhanabhūtau paśyati sādhye api tayoḥ sādhyasādhanabhāvamanuminoti।
uttarasmin dṛṣṭānte yayordharmayorekasyābhāvāditarasyābhāvaṃ paśyati tayorekatarasya bhāvāditarasya bhāvaṃ sādhye anuminotīti।
tadetaddhetvābhāseṣu na sambhavatītyahetavo hetvābhāsāḥ।
tadidaṃ hetūdāharaṇayoḥ sāmarthyaṃ paramasūkṣmaṃ duḥkhabodhaṃ paṇḍitarūpavedanīyamiti ।।
