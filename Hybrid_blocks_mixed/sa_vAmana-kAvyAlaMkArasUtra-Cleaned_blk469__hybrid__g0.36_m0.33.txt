viṣavṛkṣo'pi saṃvardhya svayaṃ chettumasāṃpratam paṇḍitaṃ mūrjkha iti manyate
iti ।।śakyamiti rūpaṃ karmābhidhāyāṃ liṅgavacanasyāpi sāmānyopakramāt ।।
śakeḥ śakisahośca iti karmaṇi yati sati śakyamiti rūpaṃ bhavati । viliṅgavacanasyāpi viruddhaliṅgavacanasyāpi, karmābhidhāyāṃ karmavacane sāmānyopakramād viśeṣānapekṣāyāmiti । yathā
