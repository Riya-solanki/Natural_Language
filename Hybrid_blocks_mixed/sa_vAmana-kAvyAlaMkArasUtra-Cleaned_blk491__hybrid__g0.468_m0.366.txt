।।
kāmayānaśabdaḥ siddho'nādiścet ।।
kāmayānaśabdaḥ siddhaḥ । āgamānuśāsanamanityam iti mukyakṛte, yadyanādiḥ syāt ।।
sauhṛd-daurhṛdaśabdāvaṇi hṛdbhāvāt ।।
suhṛdayadurhṛdayaśabdābhyāṃ yuvādipāṭhādaṇi kṛte, hṛdayasya hṛdbhāvaḥ । ādivṛddhau sauhṛdadaurhṛdaśabdau bhavataḥ । suhṛddurhṛcchabdābhyāṃ yuvādipāṭhādevāṇi kṛte, hṛdbhagasindhvante iti hṛdantasya taddhite'ṇi kṛte satyubhayapadavṛddhau satyāṃ sauhārdaṃ dauhārdamiti bhavati ।।
virama iti nipātanāt ।।
rameranudāttopadeśatvāt, nodāttopadeśasya ityādinā vṛddhapratiṣedhasyābhāvāt kathaṃ virama iti । āha nipātanāt । etattu yama umarame ityatroparama iti । atantraṃ copasarga iti ।।
uparyādiṣu sāmīpye dvirukteṣu dvitīyā ।।
uparyādiṣu śabdeṣu sāmīpye dvirukteṣu uparyadhyadhasaḥ sāmīpye ityanena uparyādiṣu triṣu dvitīyāmreḍitānteṣu iti dvitīyā । vīpsāyāṃ tu dvirukteṣu ṣaṣṭhyeva bhavati
