aheturiti vartate।
śarīre śarīriṇāṃ jñānavikalpānāṃ bhāvābhāvau saṃvedanīyau aasti me saṃśayajñānam nāsti me saṃśayajñānam' iti evaṃ pratyakṣānumānāgamasmṛtijñāneṣu seyamāvaraṇādy anupalabdhiḥ ।
upalabdhyabhāvaḥ svasaṃvedyaḥ nāsti me śabdasyāvaraṇādyupalabdhiḥ iti a
nopalabhyante śabdasyāgrahaṇakāraṇānyāvaraṇādīnīti, tatra yaduktam atadanupalabdheranupalambhādabhāvasiddhiḥ' iti etannopapadyate ।।
