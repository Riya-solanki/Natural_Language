mānatvāt, yena tatsamabhivyāhṛto athaśabdo adhikārārthaḥ syāt ।
jijñāsāviśeṣaṇaṃ tu brahmatajjñānamadhikāryaṃ bhavet ।
na ca tadapyathaśabdena saṃbadhyate, prādhānyābhāvāt ।
na ca jijñāsā mīmāṃsā, yena yogānuśāsanavadadhikriyeta,nāntatvaṃ nipātya amāṅ māne'ityasmādvā amānapūjāyām'ityasmādvā dhātoḥ amānbadha-'ityādinānicchārthe sani vyutpāditasya mīmāṃsāśabdasya pūjitavicāra
