deśe puṇyatame caiva maharṣibhir abhiṣṭute 
rājyaṃ sa kārayām āsa prayāge pṛthivīpatiḥ ।। 
uttare jāhnavītīre pratiṣṭhānemahāyaśāḥ । eka evapurā devaḥ praṇavaḥ sarvavāṅmayaḥ । devo nārāyaṇo nānya eko agnivarṇa eva ca ।। purūravasa evāsīt trayī tretāmukhe nṛpa । agninā prajayā rājā lokaṃ gāndharvam eyivān । 
tasya putrā babhūvus te ṣaḍ indropamatejasāḥ 
divi jātā mahātmāna āyur dhīmān amāvasuḥ 
viśvāyuś caiva dharmātmā śrutāyuś ca tathāparaḥ । 
dṛḍhāyuś ca vanāyuś ca śatāyuś corvaśīsutāḥ ।।
