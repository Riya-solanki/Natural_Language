saṃbandhabhedau kalpitau na satyadvitīyasādhakāvityāha-sthāneti ।
sthānamupādhibuddhyādiḥ ekasyaivopādhinā bhinnasyopādhiśāntau satyāṃ saṃbandha upacaryate ।
yathā saurālokāderaṅgulyādyupādhinā bhinnasyopādhiviyoge mahālokādyātmanā saṃbandhopacārastadvat tathāditya cakṣuṣoḥ sthānayorbhedāddhiraṇmayapuruṣabhedakalpanetyarthaḥ ।।
