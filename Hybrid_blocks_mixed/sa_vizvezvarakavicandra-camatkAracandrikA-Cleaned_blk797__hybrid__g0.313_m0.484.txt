atra gaṇḍaśailadarśanena tatsadṛśānāṃ nāyakāpahṛtānāṃ nijagajendrāṇāṃ yā smṛtisso ayaṃ smṛtyalaṅkāraḥ ।
abhiprāyānukūlyena pravṛttirbhāva īritaḥ ।।
yathā
śrīsiṅgakṣitināyakena viduṣā tattatkalāpaddhateḥ lokasyāṅgānyakṛtyasauṣṭhavakalāmaṅgoṣu sandhitsatā ।
yadyatsaṃvihitaṃ raho vitanute tattatpratīpakramaṃ, prajñāśītavatī vivekacaturā kācitkuraṅgekṣaṇā ।।
