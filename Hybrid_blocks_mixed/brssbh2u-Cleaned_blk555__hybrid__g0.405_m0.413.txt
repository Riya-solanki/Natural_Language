yathā hi haricandanabinduḥ śarīraikadeśasaṃbaddho api sansakaladehavyapinamāhlādaṃ karotyevamātmāpi dehaikadeśebhyaḥ sakaladehavyāpinīmupalabdhiṃ kariṣyati ।
tvaksaṃbandhanāccāsya sakalaśarīragatā vedanā na virudhyate ।
tvagātmanorhi saṃbandhaḥ kṛtsnāyāṃ tvaci vartate tvakca kṛtsnaśarīravyāpinīti ।।
