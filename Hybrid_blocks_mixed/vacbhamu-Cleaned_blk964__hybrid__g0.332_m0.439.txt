upapūrvamapītyeke bhāvamaśanavattaduktam ।
śrutistāvatsarasato asaṅkacadvṛttirbrahmacārimātrasya naiṣṭhikasyopakurvāṇasya cāviśeṣeṇa prāyaścittamupadiśati sākṣāt ।
prāyaścittaṃ na paśyāmīti tu smṛtiḥ ।
tasyāmapi ca sākṣātprāyaścittaṃ na kartavyamiti prāyaścittaniṣedho na gamyate, na paśyāmīti tu darśanābhāvena so anumātavyaḥ ।
