adhikaṃ yathā
śrī siṅgakṣitipo vahanniśitadhī rākarṇakṛṣṭaṃ dhanuḥ pratyaśvaṃ pratikuñjaraṃ pratinaraṃ muñcannamoghān śarān ।
yāvanti dviṣatāṃ balāni balavatyāścaryameko apyasau tāvadvā samameva tairabhimukhairadhyakṣamālabhyate ।।
