iti evaṃvidhaṃ vastu prasiddhaṃ pratītamyo vācakaḥ pratyāyakaḥ sa śabdaḥ, yo vācyaścābhidheyaḥ sor'tha iti ।
nanu ca dyotakavyañjakāvapi śabdau saṃbhavataḥ, tadasaṃgrahānnāvyāptiḥ, yasmādarthapratītikāritvasāmānyādupacārāttāvapi vācakāveva ।
evaṃ dyotyavyaṅgyayorapyarthayoḥ pratyeyatvasāmānyādupacārādvācyatvameva ।
tasmād vācakatvaṃ vācyatvaṃ ca śabdārthayorloke suprasiddhaṃ yadyapi lakṣaṇam, tathāpyasmin alaukike kāvyamārge kavikarmavartmani ayametayorvakṣyamāṇaḥ paramārthaḥ kimapyapūrvaṃ tattvamityarthaḥ ।
kīdṛśamityāha
