abhrakasattvaṃ vahniyogena drutaṃ āste dravarūpamevāvatiṣṭhate ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭaṃ sattvaṃ nijarasaśataparibhāvitetyādi nijarasena svakīyadraveṇa paribhāvitaṃ yatkañcukikandotthacūrṇaṃ tasya āvāpena yathā śatabhāvitakañcukikandotthacūrṇena sattvaṃ drutamāste tadvatsarvāṇi lohāni drutāni tiṣṭhanti ।।
