middhaṃ katamat । asvataṃtravṛtticetaso abhisaṃkṣepaḥ ।।
vitarkaḥ katamaḥ paryeṣako manojalpaḥ cetanāprajñāviśeṣaḥ yā cittasyaudārikatā ।।
vicāraḥ katamaḥ । paryeṣako manojalpaḥ tathaiva yā cittasya sūkṣmatā ।।
