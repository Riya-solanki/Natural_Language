viśeṣamāha vakṣya ityādi ।।
bho budhāḥ mayā prakāśyamānaṃ sat śṛṇuta sāvadhānā ityadhyāhāryam ।।
tatkiṃ saṃprati yad bījaṃ samarase tulyasūte samyak jīrṇaṃ jāraṇamāpannaṃ tadahaṃ govindanāmā vakṣye kathayāmi ।।
punaḥ piṣṭistambhādividhiṃ piṣṭistambha ādir yasya vidhestaṃ vidhiṃ pāṭakhoṭajalaukākhyaṃ ca vakṣye ।।
