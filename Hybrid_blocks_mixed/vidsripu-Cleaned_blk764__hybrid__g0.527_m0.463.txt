svapne api priyasaṃgamavyasaninī śete na nidrāgamaś citreṇālikhituṃ tam icchati yadi svedaḥ sapatnījanaḥ ।
mugdheyaṃ kurute atha tadguṇakathāṃ manyur girām argalaḥ prāyaḥ puṇyadinānubhāvavalanād āśaṃsitaṃ sidhyati ।।
