rūpyasya bhāgā dvādaśa tāmrasya bhāgāḥ ṣoḍaśa evamaṣṭaviṃśatibhāgān vajramūṣāyāṃ kṣiptvā gālayitvā ca candrārkanāmā ṣoṭaḥ kāryaḥ ।।
tatastasya ṣoṭasya patrāṇyekāṃgulapramāṇāni kārayitvā sarāvasaṃpuṭe muktvā sandhau vastramṛdaṃ dattvā bhūmau chāṇakaiḥ kukkuṭapuṭaṃ dīyate ।।
dvitīyaprakāreṇa pañcadaśavarṇikaṃ hema bhavati ।।
tathā pittalapatrāṇi pūrvoktayuktyā jīrṇahemarājisūtena liptvā tathaiva kukkuṭapuṭe datte pañcadaśavarṇikaṃ hema bhavati ।।
evaṃ tribhiḥ prakārair gandhakatailena hemaniṣpattiḥ ।।
iti gandhakatailakarmāṇi ।।
