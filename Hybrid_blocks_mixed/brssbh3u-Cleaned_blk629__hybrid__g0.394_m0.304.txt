atrocyate - brahmasaṃstha iti hi brahmaṇi parisamptirananyavyāpāratārūpaṃ tanniṣṭhatvamabhidhīyate ।
tacca trayāṇāmāāṇāṃ na saṃbavati ।
svāśramavihitakarmānanuṣṭhāne pryavāyaśravaṇāt ।
parivrājakasya tu sarvakarmasaṃnyāsātapratyavāyo na saṃbhavatyananuṣṭhānanimittaḥ ।
śamadamādistu tadīyo dharmo brahmasaṃsthatāyā upodbalako na virodhī ।
brahmaniṣṭhatvameva hi tasya śamadamādyupabṛṃhitaṃ svāśramavihitaṃ karma yajñādīni cetareṣāṃ tadvyatikrame ca tasya pratyavāyaḥ tathāca anyāsa iti brahmā brahmā hi paraḥ paro hi brahma tāni vā etānyavarāṇi tapāṃsi nyāsa evātyarecayat' avedāntavijñānasuniṣcitārthāḥ saṃnyāsayogādyatayaḥ śuddhasattvāḥ' ityādyāḥ śrutayaḥ ।
smṛtayaśca -'tadbuddhayastadātmānastanniṣṭhāstatparāyaṇāḥ' ityādyā brahmasaṃsthasya karmābhāvaṃ darśayanti ।
tasmātaparivrājakasyāśramamātrādamṛtatvaprāpterjñānānarthakyaprasaṅga ityeteṣo api doṣo nvatarati ।
tadevaṃ parāmarśo apītareṣāmāśramāṇāṃ pārivrājyaṃ tāvadbrahmasaṃsthatālakṣaṇaṃ labhyetaiva ।
anapekṣyaiva jābālaśrutimāśramāntaravidhāyinīmayamācryoṇa vicāraḥ pravartitaḥ ।
vidyata eva tvāśramāntaravidhiśrutiḥ pratyakṣā ।
abrahmacaryaṃ parisampya gṛhī bhavedgṛhī bhūtvā vanībhavedvanī bhūtvā pravrajet ।
yadi vetarathā brahmacaryādeva brahmacaryādeva pravrajedgṛhādvā vanādvā' iti ।
na ceyaṃ śrutiranadikṛtāviṣayā śakyā vaktum ।
aviśeṣaśravaṇāt ।
pṛthagnidhānāccānadhikṛtānāma aatha punareva vratī vāvratī vā snātako vāsnātako votsannāgniranagniko vā' ityādinā ।
brahmadānaparipākāṅgatvācca pārivrājyasya nānadhikṛtaviṣayatvam ।
tacca darśayati - aatha parivrāḍvivarṇavāsā muṇḍo aparigrahaḥ śuciradrohī bhaikṣāṇo brahmabhūyāya bhavati' iti ।
tasmātsiddhā ūrdhvaretasāmāśramāḥ ।
siddhaṃ cordhvaretaḥsu vidānādvidyāyāḥ svātantryamiti ।।
