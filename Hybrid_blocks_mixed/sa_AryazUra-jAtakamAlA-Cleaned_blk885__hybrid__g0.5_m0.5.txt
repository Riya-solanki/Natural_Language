tacchrutvā te vāṇijakā viṣādoparudhyamānamanaso visrasyamānagātrotsāhā niśvasitamātraparāyaṇāstatraiva niṣeduḥ । vyatītya ca tamapi samudraṃ sāyāhnasamaye vilambamānaraśmimaṇḍale salilanidhimiva praveṣṭukāme divasakare samudvartamānasyeva salilanidheraśanīnāmiva ca sampatatāṃ veṇuvanānāmiva cāgniparigatānāṃ visphuṭatāṃ tumulamatibhīṣaṇaṃ śrutihṛdayavidāraṇaṃ samudradhvanimaśrauṣuḥ । śrutvā ca santrāsavaśagāḥ sphuranmanasaḥ sahasaivotthāya samantato anuvilokayanto dadṛśuḥ prapāta iva śvabhra iva ca mahati tamudakaughaṃ nipatantam । dṛṣṭvā ca paramabhayaviṣādavihvalāḥ supāragamupetyocuḥ
