tātkālikī yathā
pūrvāṅgaṃ lalitaṃ nidhāya mṛdule siṃhāsanopāśraye paśyan bāṇamṛjūkṛtaṃ savayasā smerairapāṅgekṣaṇaiḥ ।
kurvan karṇapuṭīṃ sudhārasamayīṃ vācaṃ parīkṣāvatā- māsthānyāmanavotasiṅganṛpatiḥ puṣṇāti netrotsavam ।।
