vividhadhavavanā nāgagarddharddhanānā vivitatagaganānāmamajjajjanānā ।
ruruśaśalalanā nāvavandhundhunānā mama hi hitatanānānanasvasvanānā ।।
anayā ca varṇayamakamālayā padayamakamālā vyākhyātā ।।bhaṅgādutkarṣaḥ ।।utkṛṣṭaṃ khalu yamakaṃ bhaṅgādbhavati ।।śṛṅkhalā parivartakaścūrṇamiti bhaṅgamārgaḥ ।।ete khalu śṛṅkhalādayo yamakabhaṅgānāṃ prakārā bhavanti ।।
tān krameṇa vyācaṣṭe 
varṇavicchedacalanaṃ śṛṅkhalā ।।
varṇānāṃ vicchedo varṇavicchedaḥ । tasya calanaṃ yat sā śṛṅkhalā । yathā kālikāmadhuśabde kāmaśabdavicchede madhuśabdavicchede ca tasya calanam । li-ma-varṇayorvicchedāt ।।
saṅgavinivṛttau svarūpāpattiḥ parivartakaḥ ।।
anyavarṇasaṃsargaḥ saṅgaḥ । tadvinivṛttau svarūpasyānyavarṇatiraskṛtasyāpattiḥ prāptiḥ parivartakaḥ । yathā
