na ।
avidyākṛta nāma rūpa upādhikatayā parihṛtatvāt ।
tasya ca aparabrahma upāsanasya tat saṃnidhau śrūyamāṇam asa yadi pitṛlokakāmo bhavati' ityādi jagat aiśvaryalakṣaṇaṃ saṃsāragocaram eva phalaṃ bhavati ।
anivartitatvāt avidyāyāḥ ।
tasya ca deśaviśeṣa avabaddhatvāt tatprāptyarthaṃ gamanam aviruddham ।
sarvagatatve api ca ātmana ākāśasya iva ghaṭādigamane budhyādi upādhigamane gamanaprasiddhiḥ iti avādiṣma atadguṇasāratvāt' ityatra ।
tasmāt akāryaṃ bādariḥ ityeṣa eva sthitaḥ pakṣaḥ ।
aparaṃ jaiminiḥ' iti tu pakṣāntarapratibhānamātrapradarśanaṃ prajñāvikāsanāya iti draṣṭavyam ।।
