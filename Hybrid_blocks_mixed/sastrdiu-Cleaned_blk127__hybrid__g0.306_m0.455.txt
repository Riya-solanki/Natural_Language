tacca sanmātraviṣayamiti kecit ।
bhedasya vikalpavedyatvādagṛhīte ca bhede viśeṣāṇāmagrahaṇāt ।
kiñcetarābhāvo bhedo pratyakṣeṇa grahītuṃ śakyate natarāṃ avikalpena ।
ataśca neha nānāsti kiñcanetyādiniṣedhānāṃ pratyakṣavirodhābhāvāt ekamevādvitīyamityādiśrutibhiradvitīyam brahma siddham ।
