iti sakhedamāste ।
atra hi kvaciditi keliklamāpanodananimittaṃ niketapṛṣṭhasaṃcāraṇīyāsu līlāsu aprayatnasulabhadarśane tanumātronmīlitasaṃpātabimbalāvaṇyaleśaśaṅkamānatvadānanāntevāsitve candramasi darśitanijavākyopārūḍhapadārthatvāt paryālocanayā pratyāyayati ।
yeneti paryaṅkārdhaśayanamapi pravāsapadamiva pariharatīti tadeva vyanakti ।
kṣaṇāditi etāvantamapi kālaṃ tvayā virahitasya jīvataḥ kiyadaugyaṃ mama ।
punaḥ sakalo apyalīka evāyaṃ premabandho avadhāryatāmiti tathaiva pratipādayati ।
so ayamityādi prāgeva vyākhyātam ।।
evametat, antaravākyakadambakābhivyaktayābhinavabhaṅgyā pūrvasmātsvādādāsvādāntarasampadaṃ kāmapi karuṇasya kurute ।
pañcame aṅke rājā
