asamāsā yathā
rājānaḥ santu loke dinarajanikṛtoratra vaṃśāvataṃsā viṣṇoraitihyasiddhānabhidadhati guṇān kīrtigandhāḥ pravandhāḥ ।
adya kṣoṇī kṛtārthā nṛpatikulagurū cakṣuṣī yasya sau'yaṃ devo recarlavaṃśo viharati bhagavān siṅgabhūpālamūrtiḥ ।।
