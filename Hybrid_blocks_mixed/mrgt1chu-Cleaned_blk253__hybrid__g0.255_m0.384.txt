yatsadbhāva eva yasya bhāvastattasya kāryamiti niyamo'trāvaśyaṃ pratijñātavyo'nyathā tadabhāve'pi bhavatas tatkāryatvāyogāt yathāgnisadbhāva eva sambhavan dhūmo'gnikāryaḥ ।। d
evaṃ ca śarīrārambhakabhūtasadbhāva eva bhāvaḥ tadabhāve cābhāva eva caitanyasya yadi syāt taddehasambandhitayā pratīyeta ।। d
yāvatā śarīrasadbhāve'pi garbhādāv uttarakālaṃ ca cetanāpagamo dṛṣṭaḥ ।। d
tan na sati sattvaṃ dehārambhakacaitanyasādhakam ।। d
nanu mṛtaśarīre'pi prāṇādyātmakasya vāyorūṣmarūpasya ca tejaso'pagamān na jīvāvasthāyāmiva śarīrārambhakabhūtasadbhāvas tasmād atrācetanatvam yac ca tat sati sattvam anaikāntikīkartum aśaktam ।। d
maivam ।। d
gatāsavo'pi hi kecana calatsaṃdhayaḥ soṣmāṇaśca kiyantaṃ kālamupalabhyante ।। d
teṣāṃ ca jīvadavasthāvad dehārambhakabhūtasadbhāve'pi na caitanyasambhavaḥ garbhāvasthāyāṃ ca sakalasāmagrīsadbhāve'pi kadācic caitanyāsaṃbhavaḥ ।। d
tasmātsati sattvamanaikāntikameva ।। d
nanu jīvadavasthāyāṃ pariṇāmavaiśiṣṭyāc caitanyasambhavas tathāvidhapariṇāmābhāvāt gatāsudehe tadabhāva iti punarapi cārvākabhūmikayaivāśaṅkyate ।।
