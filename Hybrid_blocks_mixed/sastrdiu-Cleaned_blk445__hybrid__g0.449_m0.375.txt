tadapyayuktam ।
itthaṃbhāve pramāṇābhāvāt prathamaśrutāvapratītireva pramāṇaṃ sāmayikatve hyapratipannasamayānāmapratītiḥ pratipannasamayānāṃ ca pratītirdevadattādiśabdavadupapannā bhavati, svābhāvike tu pratyāyakatve
