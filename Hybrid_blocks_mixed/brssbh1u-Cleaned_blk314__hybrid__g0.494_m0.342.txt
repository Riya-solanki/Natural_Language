ucyate- aprāṇo brahma kaṃ brahma khaṃ brahma' ityetadagnīnāṃ vacanaṃ śrutvopakosala uvāca- avijānāmyahaṃ yatprāṇo brahma kaṃ ca tu khaṃ ca na vijānāmi' iti ।
tatredaṃ prativacanam- ayadvāva kaṃ tadeva khaṃ yadeva khaṃ tadeva kaṃ' 
iti ।
tatra khaṃśabdo bhūtākāśe nirūḍho loke ।
yadi tasya viśeṣaṇatvena kaṃśabdaḥ sukhavācī nopādīyeta ।
tathā sati kevale bhūtākāśe brahmaśabdo nāmādiṣviva pratīkābhiprāyeṇa prayukta iti pratītiḥ syāt ।
tathā kaṃśabdasya viṣayendriyasaṃparkajanite sāmaye sukhe prasiddhatvāt, yadi tasya khaṃśabdo viśeṣaṇatvena nopādīyeta, laukikaṃ sukhaṃ brahmeti pratītiḥ syāt ।
itaretaraviśeṣatau tu kaṅkhaṃśabdau sukhātmakaṃ brahma gamayataḥ ।
tatra dvitīye brahmaśabde anupādīyamāne kaṃ khaṃ brahmetyevocyamāne kaṃśabdasya viśeṣaṇatvenaivopayuktatvātasukhasya guṇasyādhyeyatvaṃ syāt, tanmā bhūdityubhayoḥ kaṅkhaṃśabdayorbrahmaśabdaśirastvaṃ akaṃ brahma khaṃ brahma' iti ।
iṣṭaṃ hi sukhasyāpi guṇasya guṇavaddhyeyatvam ।
tadevaṃ vākyopakrame sukhaviśiṣṭaṃ brahmopadiṣṭam ।
pratyekaṃ ca gārhapatyādayo agnayaḥ svaṃ svaṃ mahimānamupadiśya aeṣā somya te asmadvidyātmavidyā ca' ityupasaṃharantaḥ pūrvatra brahma nirdiṣṭamiti jñāpayanti ।
aācāryastu te gatiṃ vaktā' iti ca gatimātrābhidhānapratijñānamarthāntaravivakṣāṃ vārayati ।
ayathā puṣkarapalāśā āpo na śliṣyanta evamevaṃvidi pāpaṃ karma na śliṣyate' iti cākṣisthānaṃ puruṣaṃ vijānataḥ pāpenānupaghātaṃ bruvannakṣisthānasya puruṣasya brahmatvaṃ darśayati ।
tasmātprakṛtasyaiva brahmaṇo akṣisthānatāṃ saṃyadvāmatvādiguṇatāṃ coktvārcirādikāṃ tadvido gatiṃ vakṣyāmītyupakramate- aya eṣo akṣiṇi puruṣo dṛśyata eṣa ātmeti hovāca' iti ।।
