apekṣābhedācca ।
tathāhi gorūpeṇa nirūpyamāṇayā jātyā vyaktirabhedena pratīyate gaurayaṃ śābaleya iti ।
yadā tu jātirvayaktyantarātmanā nirūpyate tadeyaṃ vyaktisato bhinnarūpāvasīyate yo asau bāhuleya gauḥ so ayaṃ śābaleyo na bhavatīti ।
evaṃ dharmiṇo dravyasya
