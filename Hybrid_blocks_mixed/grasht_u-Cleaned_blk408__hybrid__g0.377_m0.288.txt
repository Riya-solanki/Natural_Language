biḍāntaramāha sarvetyādi ।।
sarvāṅgadagdhamūlakabhasma pratigālitamiti sarvāṅgena mūlatvakpatrapuṣpaphalena saha dagdhaṃ bhasmatāṃ prāptaṃ yanmūlakakandaṃ tadbhasma surabhimūtreṇa gojalena gālitaṃ kāryaṃ kṣāro grāhya ityarthaḥ ।।
punarbalivasayā balamukhyā yā jalaukā maṇḍūkādīnāṃ vasā yathā ca ।
bhekamatsyavasā yā tu kukkuṭasya vasāthavā ।
bhāvyam ebhiḥ kramādgandhaṃ śiśumāravasāpi vā ।।
ekā balivasā samyak sūtasya bandhinī param ।
rañjanaṃ caiva kurute maṇimūṣāvidhikramāt ।
iti ।।
balivasayā śataṃ śatavāraṃ kṣārabhūtaṃ bhasma bhāvyaṃ punaḥ tacchatabhāvyam auṣadhaṃ tatkṣaṇataḥ tatkālato hema svarṇaṃ jāryate raso grāsabhūtaṃ hema jaratīti biḍayogāditi bhāvaḥ ।।
