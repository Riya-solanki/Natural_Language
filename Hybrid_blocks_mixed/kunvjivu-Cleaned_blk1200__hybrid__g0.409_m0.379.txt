anenonmīlitonmādadaśāvaiśasasya rājñaḥ, kavacitaḥ śiñcitakodaṇḍadaṇḍo darpādāpatan naktañcaro api śakyapratīkāro na tvasau navāmbhoda iti, nārācanicayo api na tathā marmāṇi kṛntati yathāyamāsāradhārānikara iti ।
kiṃ ca nabhasi vā bhūyaḥ saudāminyāḥ anvīkṣaṇadṛṣṭanaṣṭāyāḥ kṣaṇāntare darśanamāsādyate, tathāvidhasthairyāsaṃbhāvitabhūmerapi priyāyāḥ tatkimidamiti cābhiprāyo vākyena pratipādyate ।
"tiṣṭhetkopavaśādi"tyādi, "padbhyā'-miti, "taraṅge' tyādikaṃ prāgudāhṛtamasmābhiranusandheyam ।
