asti vidyāsaṃyuktaṃ yajñādi ya evaṃ vidvānyajetetyādikam ।
asti ca kevalam ।
tatra yathā brāhmaṇāya hiraṇyaṃ dadyādiyukte viduṣe brāhmaṇāya dadyātra brāhmaṇabruvāya mūkhāyeti viśeṣapratilambhaḥ tatkasya hetostasyātiśayavatvāt ।
evaṃ vidyārahitādyajñādervidyāsahitamatiśayavaditi tasyaiva paravidyāsādhanatvamupāttaduritakṣayadvārā netasya ।
tasmādvividiṣanti yajñenetyaviśeṣaśrutamapi vidyāsahite yajñādāvupasaṃhartavyamiti prāpte abhidhīyateyadeva vidyayā karāti tadevāsya vīryavattaramiti tarabarthaśrutervidyārahitasya vīryavattāmātramavagamyate ।
naca sarvathākiñcitkarasya tadupapadyate ।
tasmādastyasyāpi kayāpi mātrayā paravidyotpādopayoga iti vidyārahitamapi yajñādi paravidyārthinānuṣṭheyamiti siddham ।।
