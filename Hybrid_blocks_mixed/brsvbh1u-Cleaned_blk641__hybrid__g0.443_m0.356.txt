sthityadanābhyāṃ ca ।
yadi jīvo hiraṇyagarbho vā dyubhvādyāyatanaṃ bhavet, tatastatprakṛtyā aanaśnannanyo aabhicākaśīti'iti paramātmābhidhānamākasmikaṃ prasajyeta ।
naca hiraṇyagarbha udāsīnaḥ, tasyāpi bhoktṛtvāt ।
naca jīvātmaiva dyubhvādyāyatanaṃ, tathā sati sa evātra kathyate, tatkathanāya ca brahmāpi kathyate, anyathā siddhānte api jīvātmakathanamākasmikaṃ syāditi vācyam ।
