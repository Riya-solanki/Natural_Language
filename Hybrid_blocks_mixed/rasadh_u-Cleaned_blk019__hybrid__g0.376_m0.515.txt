sārdhaślokaḥ spaṣṭaḥ ।।
atra ca jalamadhye mṛttikāpāṣāṇajalāntarasaṃyogāt tathā dugdhopari tarāvac ca ye dṛśyante te kañcukā ucyante ।।
ye punaḥ kodraveṣu madavadbhavanti te doṣā iti viśeṣaḥ ।।
athaiteṣāṃ doṣāṇāṃ pratyekaṃ vikārānāha ।।
