asvargakāmo yajeta' iti sādhyasvargaviśiṣṭo niyojyo avagamyate; sa ca tadeva kāryamavagacchati yatsvargānukūlam ।
na ca kriyā kṣaṇabhaṅgurā āmuṣmikāya svargāya kalpata iti pāriśeṣyādvedata evāpūrve kārye liṅādīnāṃ saṃbandhagraha iti cet, hanta caityavandanādivākyeṣvapi svargakāmādipadasaṃbandhādapūrvakāryatvaprasaṅgaḥ; tathā ca teṣāmapyaśakyaracanatvenāpauruṣeyatvāpātaḥ ।
spaṣṭadṛṣṭena pauruṣeyatvena vā teṣāmapūrvārthatvapratiṣedhe vākyatvādinā liṅgena vedāntānāmapi pauruṣeyatvamanumitamityapūrvārthatā na syāt ।
anyatastu vākyatvādīnāmanumānābhāmatvopapādane kṛtamapūrvārthatvenātra tadupapādakena ।
upapāditaṃ cāpauruṣeyatvamasmābhirnyāyakaṇikāyām; iha tu vistarabhayānnoktam ।
