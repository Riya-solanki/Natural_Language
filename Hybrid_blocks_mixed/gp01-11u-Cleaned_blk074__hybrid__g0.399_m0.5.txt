aṃśā bhāgā dāyās tad-dhita-yogena dāyavantaś ca ।
tat kila jāter bhāgā bakajiti te santi dāyavantaś ca ।। [gīti]
tasminn aṃśo yeṣām iti vā gamyo bahu-vrīhiḥ ।
vrīhi-nibhas tat-premā teṣāṃ vṛttau tad-āśrayo yuktaḥ ।। [udgīti]
