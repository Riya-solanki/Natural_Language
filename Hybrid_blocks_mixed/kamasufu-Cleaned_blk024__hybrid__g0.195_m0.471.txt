cumbana-nakha-daśana-cchedyānāṃ na paurvāparyam asti rāga-yogāt prāk saṃyogād eṣāṃ prādhāyena prayogaḥ prahaṇa-sītkṛtayoś ca saṃprayoge
sarvaṃ sarvatra rāgasyānapekṣitatvāt iti vātsyāyanaḥ
tāni prathama-rate nātivyaktāni viśrabdhikāyāṃ vikalpena ca prayuñjīta tathā-bhūtatvād rāgasya tataḥ param atitvarayā viśeṣavat samuccayena rāga-saṃdhukṣaṇārtham
lalāṭālaka-kapola-nayana-vakṣaḥ-stanoṣṭhāntarmukheṣu cumbanam
ūru-saṃdhi-bāhu-nābhi-mūlayor lāṭānām
rāga-vaśād deśa-pravṛtteś ca santi tāni tāni sthānāni na tu sarva-jana-prayojānīti vātsyāyanaḥ
tad yathā ṇ [] nimittikaṃ [] sphuritakaṃ [] ghaṭṭitakam iti trīṇi kanyā-cumbanāni
balāt-kāreṇa niyuktā mukham ādhatte na tu viceṣṭata iti [] nimittakam
vadane praveśitaṃ cauṣṭhaṃ manāg apatrapāvagrahītum icchantī spandayati svam oṣṭhaṃ nottaram utsahata iti [] sphuritakam
īṣat parigṛhya vinimīlita-nayanā kareṇa ca tasya nayane avacchādayanti jihvāgreṇa ghaṭṭayatīti [] ghaṭṭitakam
samaṃ tiryag udbhrāntam avapīḍitakam iti caturvidham apare
aṅguli-saṃpuṭena piṇḍī-kṛtya nirdaśanam oṣṭha-puṭenāvapīḍayed ity avapīḍitakaṃ pañcamam api karaṇam
dyūtaṃ cātra pravartayet
pūrvam adhara-saṃpādanena jitam idaṃ syāt
tatra jitā sārdha-ruditaṃ karaṃ vidhunuyāt praṇuded daśet parivartayed balād āhṛtā vivadet punar apy astu paṇa iti brūyāt tatrāpi jitā dvi-guṇam āyasyaet
viśrabdhasya pramattasya vādharam avagṛhya daśanāntargatam anirgamaṃ kṛtvā hased utkriśet tarjayed valged āhlayet pranartitat-bhrūṇā ca vicala-nayanena mukhena vihasantītāni tāni ca brūyāt iti cumbana-dyūta-kalahaḥ
etena nakha-daśana-cchedya-prahaṇana-dyūta-kalahā vyākhyātāḥ
caṇḍa-vegayor eva tv eṣāṃ prayogaḥ tat-sātmyāt
tasyāṃ cumbantyām ayam apy uttaraṃ gṛhṇīyāt ity uttara-cumbitam oṣṭha-saṃdaṃśenāvagṛhyāuṣṭha-dvayam api cumbeta iti saṃpuṭakaṃ striyāḥ puṃso vājāta-vyañjanasya
tasminn itaro api jihvayāsyā daśanān ghaṭṭayet tālu jihvāṃ ceti jihvā-yuddham
etena balād vadana-radana-grahaṇaṃ dānaṃ ca vyākhyātam
samaṃ pīḍitam añcitaṃ mṛdu śeṣāṅgeṣu cumbanaṃ sthāna-viśeṣa-yogāt iti cumbana-viśeṣāḥ
suptasaya mukham avalokayantyā svābhiprāyeṇa cumbanaṃ rāga-dīpanam
pramattasya vivadamānasya vānyato abhimukhasya suptābhimukhasya vā nidrā-vyāghātārthaṃ calitakam
cira-rātrāv āgatasya śayana-suptāyāḥ svābhiprāya-cumbanaṃ prātibodhakam
sāpi tu bhāva-jijñāsārthinī nāyakasyāgamana-kālaṃ saṃlakṣya vyājena suptā syāt
ādarśe kuḍye salile vā prayojyāyāś chāyā-cumbanam ākāra-pradarśanārtham eva kāryam
bālasya citra-karmaṇaḥ pratimāyāś ca cumbanaṃ saṃkrāntakam āliṅganaṃ ca
tathā niśi prekṣaṇake svajana-samāje vā samīpe gatasya prayojyāyā hastāṅguli-cumbanaṃ saṃviṣṭasya vā pādāṅguli-cumbanam
saṃvāhikāyās tu nāyakam ākārayantyā nidrā-vaśād akāmāyā iva tasyorvor vadanasya nidhānam ūru-cumbanaṃ cety ābhiyogikāni
bhavati cātra ślokaḥ
kṛte pratikṛtaṃ kuryād tāḍite pratitāḍitam karaṇena ca tenaiva cumbite praticumbitam
