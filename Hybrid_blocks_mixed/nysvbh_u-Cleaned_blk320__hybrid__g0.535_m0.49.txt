"yugapajjñānānutpattirmanaso liṅgam "
iti vyāhanyeta nedānīṃ manasaḥ saṃnikarṣamindriyārthasaṃnikarṣo apekṣate manaḥsaṃyogānapekṣāyāṃ ca yugapajjñānotpattiprasaṅgaḥ।
atha mā bhūd vyāghāta iti sarvavijñānānāmātmamanasoḥ saṃnikarṣaḥ kāraṇamiṣyate ? tadavasthamevedaṃ bhavati ajñānakāraṇatvādātmamanasoḥ saṃnikarṣasya grahaṇaṃ kāryam' iti ।।
