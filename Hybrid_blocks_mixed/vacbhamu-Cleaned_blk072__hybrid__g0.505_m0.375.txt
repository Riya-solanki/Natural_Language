atinirūḍhanibiḍhavāsanānuvidvā avidyā vidyayā apabādhitāpi svavāsanāvaśātpunarudbhaviṣyati pravartayiṣyati ca vāsanādikāryaṃ svocitamityaya āha - tatraivaṃ sati, evaṃbhūtavastutattvāvadhāraṇe sati, yatra yadadhyāsaḥ
