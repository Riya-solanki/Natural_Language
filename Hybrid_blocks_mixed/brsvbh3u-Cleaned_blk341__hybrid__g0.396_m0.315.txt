anāviṣkurvann anvayāt ।
bālyeneti yāvadbālacaritaśruteḥ kāmacāravādabhakṣatāyāśacātyantabālyena prasiddheḥ śaucādiniyamavidhāyinaśca sāmānyaśāstrasyānena viśeṣaśāstreṇa bādhanātsakalabālacaritavidhānamiti prāpte abhidhīyate-vidyāṅgatvena bālyavidhānātsamastabālacaryāyāṃ ca pradhānavirodhaprasaṅgādyattadanuguṇamaprauḍhendriyatvādi bhāvaśuddhirūpaṃ tadeva vidhīyate ।
evaṃ ca śāstrāntarābādhenāpyupapattau na śāstrāntarabādhanamanyāyyaṃ bhaviṣyatīti ।।
