tathātharvaṇaśrutirapyeṣāmekādaśānāmutkrāntimabhivadati ।
tasmācchutyantarebhyaḥ smṛteśca sarvaśabdārthāsaṃkocācca sarveṣāmutkrameṇa sthite asminnaivaṃ yaduktaṃ saptaiveti, kintu pradarśanārthaṃ saptatvasaṃkhyeti siddham ।।
