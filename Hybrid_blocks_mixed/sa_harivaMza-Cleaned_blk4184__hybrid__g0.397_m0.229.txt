tena te ca mahāśailāḥ saritaś ca sarāṃsi ca 
parikṣiptāni bhaumena vanāny upavanāni ca ।। 
prāsādāś caiva saṃvītā lokālokaviśāradāḥ । tasyaiva yadusiṃ hasya bhavanāl lokaviśrutāt ।। tasmād abhyadhikaṃ sadma vihitaṃ viśvakarmaṇā । rāmasya yadusiṃhasya śobhate atimanoharam । 
evam ālokayām āsa dvārakāṃ vṛṣabhekṣaṇaḥ 
apaśyat svagṛhaṃ kṛṣṇaḥ prāsādaśataśobhitam ।।
