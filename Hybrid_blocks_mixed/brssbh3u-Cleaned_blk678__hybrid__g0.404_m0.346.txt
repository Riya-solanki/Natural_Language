sahakāritvasyaivaitadupodbalakaṃ liṅgadarsanamanabhibhavaṃ ta darśayati śrutirbrahmacaryādisādhanasaṃpannasya rāgadibhiḥ kleśaiḥ aeṣa hyātmā na naśyati yaṃ brahmacaryeṇānuvindate' ityādinā ।
tasmādyajñādīnyāśramakarmāṇi ca bhavanti vidyāsahakārīṇi ceti niścitam ।।
