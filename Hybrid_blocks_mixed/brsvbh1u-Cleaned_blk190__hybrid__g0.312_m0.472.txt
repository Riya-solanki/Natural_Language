asa tu dīrdhakālanairantaryasatkārāsevito dṛḍhabhūmiḥ'iti ।
brahmacaryatapaḥśraddhāyajñādayaśca satkārāḥ ।
ata eva śrutiḥ-'tameva dhīro vijñāya prajñāṃ kurvīta brāhmaṇaḥ' ।
iti ।
vijñāya tarkopakaraṇena śabdena prajñāṃ bhāvanāṃ kurvītetyarthaḥ ।
atra ca yajñādīnāṃ śreyaḥparipanthikalmaṣanibarhaṇadvāreṇopayoga iti kecit ।
puruṣasaṃskāradvāreṇetyanye ।
yajñādisaṃskṛto hi puruṣaḥ ādaranairantaryadīrghakālairāsevamāno brahmabhāvanāmanādyavidyāvāsanāṃ samūlakāṣaṃ kaṣati,tato asya pratyagātmā suprasannaḥ kevalo viśadībhavati ।
ata eva smṛtiḥ-'mahāyajñaiśca yajñaiśca brāhmīyaṃ kriyate tanuḥ' ।
ayasyaite aṣṭācatvāriṃśatsaṃskārāḥ'iti ca ।
apare tu ṛṇatrayāpākaraṇe brahmajñānopayogaṃ karmaṇāmāhuḥ ।
asti hi smṛtiḥ-'ṛṇāni trīṇyapākṛtya mano mokṣe niveśayet' ।
iti ।
anye tu atametaṃ vedānuvacanena brāhmaṇā vividiṣanti yajñena'ityādiśrutibhyastattatphalāya coditānāmapi karmaṇāṃ saṃyogapṛthaktvena brahmabhāvanāṃ pratyaṅgabhāvamācakṣate, kratvarthasyeva khādiratvasya
