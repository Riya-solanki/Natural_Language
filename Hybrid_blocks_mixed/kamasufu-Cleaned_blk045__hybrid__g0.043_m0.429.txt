puruṣaḥ śayanasthāyā yoṣitas tad-vacana-vyākṣipta-cittāyā iva nīvīṃ viśleṣayet tatra vivadamānāṃ kapola-cumbanena paryākulayet
sthira-liṅgaś ca tatraināṃ parispṛśet
prathama-saṃgatā cet saṃhatorvor antare ghaṭṭanam
kanyāyāś ca
tathā stanayoḥ saṃhatayor hastayoḥ kakṣayor aṃsayor grīvāyām iti ca
svairiṇyāṃ yathāsātmyaṃ yathā-yogaṃ ca alake cumbanārtham enāṃ nirdayam avalambet hanu-deśe cāṅguli-saṃputena
tatretarasya vrīḍā nimīlanaṃ ca prathama-samāgame kanyāyāś ca
rati-saṃyoge caināṃ katham anurajyata iti pravṛttyā parīkṣeta
yukta-yantreṇopasṛpyamānā yato dṛṣṭim āvartayet tata evaināṃ pīḍayet etad rahasyaṃ yuvatīnām iti suvarṇanābhaḥ
gātrāṇāṃ sraṃsanaṃ netra-nimīlanaṃ vrīḍā-nāśaḥ samadhikā ca rati-yojaneti strīṇāṃ bhāva-lakṣaṇam hastau vidhunoti svidyati daśaty utthātuṃ na dadāti pādenāhanti ratāvamāne ca puruṣātivartinī
tasyāḥ prāg yantra-yogāt kareṇa saṃbādhaṃ gaja iva kṣobhayet ā mṛdu-bhāvāt tato yantra-yojanam
upasṛptakaṃ []manthanaṃ []hulo []avamardanaṃ []pīḍitakaṃ []nirghāto []varāha-ghāto []vṛṣa-ghātaś []caṭaka-vilasitaṃ []saṃpuṭa iti puruṣopasṛptāni
nyāyyam ṛju-saṃmiśraṇam upasṛptakam
hastena liṅgaṃ sarvato bhrāmayed iti []manthanam
nīcī-kṛtya jaghanam upariṣṭād ghaṭṭayed iti []hulaḥ
tad eva viparītaṃ sarabhasam []avamardanam
liṅgena samāhatya pīḍayaṃś ciram avatiṣṭheteti []pīḍitakam
sudūram utkṛṣya vegena sva-jaghanam avapātayed iti []nirghātaḥ
ekata eva bhūyiṣṭham avalikhed iti []varāha-ghātaḥ
sa evobhayataḥ paryāyeṇa []vṛṣāghātaḥ
sakṛn miśritam aniṣkramayya dvis triś catur iti ghaṭṭayed iti []caṭaka-vilasitam
rāgāvasānikaṃ vyākhyātaṃ karaṇaṃ []saṃpuṭam iti 
teṣāṃ strī-sātmyād vikalpena prayogaḥ
