naiṣa doṣaḥ ।
pucchavatpucchaṃ, pratiṣṭhā parāyaṇamekanīḍaṃ laukikasyānandajātasya brahmānanda ityetadanena vivakṣyate, nāvayavatvaṃ, aetasyaivānandasyānyāni bhūtāni mātrāmupajīvanti' iti śrutyantarāt ।
apica ānandamayasya brahmatve priyādyavayavatvena saviśeṣaṃ brahmābhyupagantavyam ।
nirviśeṣaṃ tu brahma vākyaśeṣe śrūyate, vāṅmanasayoragocaratvābhidhānāt- a yato vāco nivartante ।
aprāpya manasā saha ।
ānandaṃ brahmaṇo vidvān ।
na bibheti kutaścaneti' ।
apica ānandapracura ityukte duḥkhāstitvamapi gamyate prācuryasya loke pratiyogyalpatvāpekṣatvāt ।
tathāca sati, ayatra nānyatpaśti nānyacchṛṇoti nānyadvijānāti sa bhūmā' iti bhūmni brahmaṇi tadvyatiriktābhāvaśrutirūparudhyeta ।
pratiśarīraṃ ca priyādibhedādānandamayasyāpi bhinnatvam ।
brahma tu na pratiśarīraṃ bhidyate, asatyaṃ jñānamanantaṃ brahma' ityānantaśruteḥ, aeko devaḥ sarvabhūteṣu gūḍhaḥ sarvavyāpī sarvabhūtāntarātmā' iti ca śrutyantarāt ।
nacānandamayasyābhyāsaḥ śrūyate ।
prātipadikārthamātrameva hi sarvatrābhyasyate- araso vai saḥ, rasaṃhyevāyaṃ labdhvānandī bhavati, ko hyevānyātkaḥ prāṇyāt, yadeṣa ākāśa ānando na syāt' ।
asaiṣānandasya mīmāṃsā bhavati' ।
aānandaṃ brahmaṇo vidvānna bibheti kutaścaneti' aānando brahmeti vyajānāt' iti ca ।
yadica ānandamayaśabdasya brahmaviṣayatvaṃ niścitaṃ bhavet, tata uttareṣvānandamātraprayogeṣvapyānandamayābhyāsaḥ kalpyeta ।
na tvānandamayasya brahmatvamasti, priyaśirastvādibhirhetubhirityavocāma ।
tasmācchrutyantare avijñānamānandaṃ brahma' ityānandaprātipadikasya brahmaṇi prayogadarśanāt, ayadeṣa ākāśa ānando na syāt' ityādirbrahmaviṣayaḥ prayogo na tvānandamayābhyāsa ityavagantavyam ।
yatsvayaṃ mayaḍantasyaivānandaśabdasyābhyāsaḥ - aetamānandamayātmānamupasaṃkrāmati' iti, na tasya brahmaviṣayatvamasti, vikārātmanāmevānnamayādīnāmanātmanāmupasaṃkramitavyānāṃ pravāhe paṭhitatvāt ।
