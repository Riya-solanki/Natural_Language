nanvanātmanyātmabuddhirūpā yā avidyā tasyāḥ kathamātmaviśeṣyakavivekajñānanāśyatvaṃ prakārādibhedāditi cet na tādṛśāvidyāyā anātmaviśeṣyakavivekadvāreṇātmaviśeṣyakavivekanāśyatvāditi ।
yacca yogena nirvikalpakamātmajñānaṃ jāyate ।
tadvivekajñānadvāraiva mokṣakāraṇaṃ bhavati na tu sākṣāt ।
avidyānivartakatvābhāvāt ahaṃ gauraḥ karttā sukhī duḥkhītyādijñānameva hyavidyā saṃsārānarthahetutayā śrutismṛtinyāyasiddhā tasyāśca nivarttikā nāhaṃ gaura ityādirūpā vivekakhyātireva bhavati samāne viṣaye grāhyābhāvatvaprakārakagrāhyābhāvajñānatvenaiva virodhāt anyathā muktinirvikalpakasyāpi idaṃ rajatamiti jñānavirodhitvāpatteḥ ।
kiñca yathoktābhāvajñāne grāhyajñānavirodhitvasyāvaśyakatayā nirvikalpajñānasya bhramanivartakatvaṃ na pṛthak kalpyate gauravāt ।।
api ca athāta ādeśo neti neti na hyetasmāditi netyanyat paramastītyādiśrutyā vivekopadeśāpekṣayottama upadeśo nāstītyucyate ।।
kṣetrakṣetrajñayorevamantaraṃ jñānacakṣuṣā ।
bhūtaprakṛtimokṣaṃ ca ye viduryānti te param ।।
