bhakṣyābhakṣyam abhramāha abhrasyetyādi ।।
abhrakasatvamiha kṣetrīkaraṇe rasāyanināṃ jarāvyādhivināśecchūnāṃ param utkṛṣṭaṃ bhakṣyaṃ aśanayogyaṃ kīrtitam ।।
punastrividhaṃ triprakāraṃ gaganamabhraṃ abhakṣyaṃ abhojyam ।।
kiṃ tat kācaḥ kācarūpaṃ kiṭṭaṃ malarūpaṃ pattrarajaḥ sāmānyābhracūrṇaṃ ceti trividhamityarthaḥ ।।
