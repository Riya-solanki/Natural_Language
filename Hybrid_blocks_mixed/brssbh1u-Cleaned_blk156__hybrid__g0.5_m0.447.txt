aiśvaryamaryādāśruteśca ।
a sa eṣa ye cāmuṣmātparāñco lokāsteṣāṃ ceṣṭe devakāmānāṃ ca' ityādityapuruṣasyaiśvaryamaryādā ।
asa eṣa ye caitasmādarvāñco lokāsteṣāṃ ceṣṭe manuṣyakāmānāṃ ca' ityakṣipuruṣasya ।
naca parameśvarasya maryādāvadaiśvaryaṃ yuktam, aeṣa sarveśvara eṣa bhūtādhipatireṣa bhūtapāla eṣa seturvidhāraṇa eṣāṃ lokānāmasaṃbhedāya' ityaviśeṣaśruteḥ ।
tasmānnākṣyādityayorantaḥ parameśvara ityevaṃ prāpte brūmaḥ- aantastaddharmopadeśāt' iti, aya eṣo antarāditye' aya eṣo antarakṣiṇi' iti ca śrūyamāṇaḥ puruṣaḥ parameśvara eva, na saṃsārī ।
kutaḥ, taddharmopadeśāt ।
tasya hi parameśvarasya dharmā ihopadiṣṭāḥ ।
tadyathā- atasyoditi nāma' iti śrāvayitvā asyādityapuruṣasya nāma asa eṣa sarvebhyaḥ pāpmabhya uditaḥ' iti sarvapāpmāpagamena nirvakti ।
tadeva ca kṛtanirvacanaṃ nāmākṣipuruṣasyāpyatidiśati- ayannāma tannāma' iti ।
sarvapāpmāpagamaśca paramātmana eva śrūyate- aya ātmāpahatapāpmā' ityādau ।
tathā cākṣuṣe puruṣe asaivarktatsamāsa tadukthaṃ tadyajustadbrahma' ityṛksamāsādyātmakatāṃ nirdhārayati ।
sā ca parameśvarasyopapadyate,
