atra pālayāñcakrire iti vaktavye pālayāṃ kecid gopālāśvakrire iti dūrānvaytve api vyavahitamapīcchanti keciditi viśeṣāvyutvādanād guṇatvam ।
smṛtaṃ vākyāṅgasaṅkīrṇamanyavākyāṅgasaṅgatam ।।
yathā
khaḍgānāpṛchya niryānti vadhūrākṛṣṭa dhunvate ।
divaṃ muñcanti yānti kṣmāṃ siṅgabhūpālavidviṣaḥ ।।
