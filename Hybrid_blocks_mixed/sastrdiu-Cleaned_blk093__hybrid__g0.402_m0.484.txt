tadidaṃ bālasaṃmohanam ।
nahyaviśeṣaṇāpi pratiṣedhe vairūpyam ।
yadi hi kratvarthatvapuruṣārthatvarūpaṃ viśeṣeṇoddiśya pratiṣedhaḥ kriyeta tataḥ syādapi vairūpyaṃ pratiṣedhyopasthāpitakratupuruṣagocaratvātpratiṣedhasya ।
yadātu hiṃsāmātramuddiśya pratiṣedhaḥ kriyate tadāsya kiṃ vairūpyam ।
tadyathā yā bhojanārthaṃ māṃsotpādane hiṃsā, yā ca vairiniryātanārthatayā sākṣātpuruṣārthā tasyāmubhayyāmaviśeṣeṇa pratiṣidhyamānāyāmapi nāsti
