[] ca tvayā ca sukhaprāśnikābhidhānenopahasitāsmi tad etat te
sarvam aśeṣataḥ kathitam aham idānīṃ mithyāprajāgaraṃ
divāsvapnenāpaneṣyāmīty uktvā mayānujñātā gatā tadanantarāgatena svāmināpy etac chrutam iti tena hy anenaiva parihāsaplavena tatrabhavataḥ śivadattasya putraṃ śivasvāminaṃ puruṣadambhagambhīrakīrtisāgaram avagāhiṣye paśyatu bhavatī
yo gulguluṃ pibati medasi saṃpravṛddhe
tasya kṣayaṃ vrajati caṇḍy acireṇa medaḥ
strīṇāṃ bhavaty atha sa yauvanaśālinīnām
ālekhyayakṣa iva darśanamātraramyaḥ
eṣā prahasyotthitā yāsyāmīti bhavatv alam añjalinā ito vayam kiṃ nu khalv imāny uddaṇḍapuṇḍarīkavanaṣaṇḍaśobhānukārīṇy udgrīvavadanapuṇḍarīkāṇi vismayavitatākṣimālāśabalāny urasi nihitakarapallavāny anyonyasaṃjñāparivartakāni nivṛttakandukapiñcholākṛtakaputrakaduhitṛkākrīḍanakāni veśarathyāyāṃ pratibhavanacchāyāsu veśakanyakāvṛndakāny avalokayanti aye kiṃ nu khalv idam
