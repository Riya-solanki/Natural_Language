FN āmnāyasyeti pūrvapakṣam ।
asyārthaḥ - āmnāyasya vedasya kriyāpratipādanaparatvādatadarthānāmakriyārthānāṃ aso arodī' dityādivākyānāmānarthakatvaṃ ।
tasmādanityamaniyataṃ vedānāṃ prāmāṇyamucyate ।
tadbhūtānāṃ kriyārthena samanvaya iti siddhāntaḥ ।
tatteṣu vedavākyeṣu bhūtānāṃ siddārthapratipādakavākyānāṃ kriyārthena kriyāpratipādakavākyena abarhiṣi rajataṃ na deyaṃ, paśunā yajete' tyādinā sahānvayaḥ ।
tathāca nindyatvādyarthapūraṇenānvaye kṛte kriyāparatvasiddhyā prāmāṇyaṃ siddham ।
siddhavastujñānātphalabhāvādevetyarthaḥ ।
