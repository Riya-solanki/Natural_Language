tadapi na saṃpat samāhitam ।
yasmādrasādiviśeṣasya satastasya svarūpalobhaḥ ।
teṣāṃ ca cittavṛttiviśeṣatvāt bhūṣaṇatve nipiddhasyāpi tadekarūpatvāt kathaṃ tadupapadyate ।
kiṃ ca pradhānacetanasvarūpatvāt varṇanīyasvabhāvabhūtasya arthātmanaḥ sahajavyañjakāsahiṣṇoḥ vyatiriktapadārthāntarasaṃparkasahatvaṃ svacchasvabhāvatvādeva na kathañcidapi samañjasatāṃ samāsādayatītyevaṃ svarūpādatiriktapadārthāntarasyāpratibhāsanādityādi yathāsaṃbhavamanivāryam ।
yadapi kaiścitprakārāntareṇa samāhitākhyamalakaraṇamākhyātaṃ tasyāpi tathaiva bhūṣaṇatvaṃ na vidyate, tadabhidhatte
