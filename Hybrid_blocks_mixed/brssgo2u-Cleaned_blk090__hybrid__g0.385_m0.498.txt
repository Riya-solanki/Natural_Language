ayatra tvasya sarvamātmaivābhūttatkena kaṃ paśyet' ityādinā brahmātmatvadarśinaṃ prati samastasya kriyākārakaphalalakṣaṇasya vyavahārasyābhāvam ।
nacāyaṃ vyavahārābhāvoṣa'vasthāviśeṣanibaddho abhidhīyata iti yuktaṃ vaktum, atattvamasi' iti brahmātmabhāvasyānavasthāviśeṣanibandhanatvāt ।
taskaradṛṣṭāntena cānṛtābhisaṃdhasya bandhanaṃ satyābhisaṃdhasya ca mokṣaṃ darśayannekatvamevaikaṃ pāramārthikaṃ darśayati ।
mithyājñānavijṛmbhitaṃ ca nānātvam ।
ubhayasatyatāyāṃ hi kathaṃ vyavahāragocaro api janturanṛtābhisaṃdha ityucyeta ।
amṛtyoḥ sa mṛtyumāpnoti ya iha nāneva paśyati' iti ca bhedadṛṣṭimapavadannevaitaddarśayati ।
nacāsmindarśane jñānānmokṣa ityupapadyate, samyagjñanāpanodyasya kasyacinmithyājñānasya saṃsārakāraṇatvenānabhyupagamāt ।
ubhayasatyatāyāṃ hi kathamekatvavijñānena nānātvajñānamapanudyata ityucyate ।
