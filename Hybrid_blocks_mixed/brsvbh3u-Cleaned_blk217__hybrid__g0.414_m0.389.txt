tulyanyāyatayaidapi śakyamudāhartumityudāharaṇāntaraṃ darśitam ।
tatra śābaramudāharaṇamastyādhānaṃ yajurvedavihitam-'ya evaṃ vidvānagnimādhatta'iti ।
tadaṅgatvena yajurveda eva aya evaṃ vidvānvāravantīyaṃ gāyati ya evaṃ vidvānyajñāyajñīyaṃ gāyati ya evaṃ vidvānvāmadevyaṃ gāyati'iti vihitam ।
etāni ca
