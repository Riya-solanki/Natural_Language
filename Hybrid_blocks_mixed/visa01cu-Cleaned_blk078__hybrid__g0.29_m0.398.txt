śṛṅgārapadaṃ cātra mādhuryyavadrasopalakṣakam ।
tena karuṇaśāntarasayośca te heyā ityarthaḥ ।
nanu nityatvānityatvaprayojakameva rasāpakarṣakānapakarṣakatvaṃ, doṣatāprayojakaṃ tu na rasāpakarṣakatvaṃ, kintvabhyuktoktittatsvarūpameva tatprayojakamityata āhakiṃceti ।
