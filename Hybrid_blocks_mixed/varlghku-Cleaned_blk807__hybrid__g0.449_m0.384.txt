sārvadhātuke yak ।।
dhātoryak bhāvakarmavācini sārvadhātuke । sā ca bhāvārthakalakāreṇānūdyate । yuṣmadasmadbhyāṃ sāmānādhikaraṇyābhāvātprathamaḥ puruṣaḥ । tiṅvācyakriyāyā adravya rūpatvena dvitvādyapratīterna dvivacanādi kiṃtvekavacanamevotsargataḥ ।
tvayā mayā anyaiśca bhūyate ।।
