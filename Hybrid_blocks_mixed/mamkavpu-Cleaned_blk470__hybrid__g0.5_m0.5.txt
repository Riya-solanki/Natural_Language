pūrvapaścādbhāvena pratīteḥ kālasya śabdāśrayatvena śabdatadekadeśatadarthavarṇaghaṭanāśrayatvena ca āśrayasya śabdānuśāsabajñānena prakaraṇādisahāyapratibhānairmalyasahitena tena cāvagama iti nimittasya boddhṛmātravidagdhavyapadeśayoḥ pratītimātracamatkṛtyośca karaṇāt kāryasya "gato astamarkaḥ" ityādau pradarśitanayena saṃkhyāyāḥ
