Vṛtti:
[deśikādīnām] ācāryasādhakaputrakasamayināṃ [sāmānyācārasaṃgrahaḥ] sādhāraṇāsādhāraṇācārasamuccayaḥ [avasaraprāptaḥ] kramāyātaḥ [samāsena] saṃkṣepād idānīm upadiśyate abhidhīyata iti।
athaśabdo adhikārārthaḥ। ataḥśabdastu yogapādānantaryam।
