dhikaraṇyadarśanāddehālambano ayamahaṅkāra iti sāṃpratam ।
tadālambanatve hi ayo ahaṃ bālye pitarāvanvabhavaṃ sa eva sthāvire praṇaptṝnanubhavāmi'iti pratisaṃdhānaṃ na bhavet ।
na hi bālasthavirayoḥ śarīrayorasti manāgapi pratyabhijñāna
