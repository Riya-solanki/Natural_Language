na syāt ।
tajjanyaḥ saṃskāro hi māṇavakasamavāyī ।
satyam, māṇavakasamaveto api na tadarthaḥ, kartrarthatvaṃ ca phalasya kartrabhiprāyatvaṃ natu tatsamavāyaḥ, anyathāhi aagnīnādadhītetyātmanepadaṃ na syāt- ādhānaphalasyāhavanīyatvāderagnisamavetatvena kartṛsamavāyābhāvāt ।
iyaṃ ca smṛtiḥ aupanīya tu yaḥ śiṣyam' iti, ato balīyasī dvitīyā śrutiḥ, tasmānnādhyāpanāṅgamupanayanamiti na taddvāreṇādhyāpanasya prayuktiḥ ।।
