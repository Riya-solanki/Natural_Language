vākyam āgamalakṣaṇaṃ purāṇetihāsādigītam ।। b
yathāha mahābhāratādau bhagavān vyāsamuniḥ ।
sa śivas tāta tejasvī prasādād yāti te agrataḥ ।
sendrādiṣu ca deveṣu tasyaivaiśvaryam ucyate ।। b
taṃ gaccha śaraṇaṃ devadevādiṃ bhuvaneśvaram ।
ityādi ।। b
śrutāv api ।
yo rudro agnau yo apsu ya oṣadhīṣu yo vanaspatiṣu ।
yo rudro viśvā bhuvanā viveśa tasmai rudrāya namo astu ।। b
tathā ।
tryambakaṃ yajāmahe ।
ityādi ।। b
tad etad anyathāsiddham atra hi paurvāparyaparyālocanānirākṛto yathāśrutaśrutisamutpādito vākyārthaḥ ।। b
ato naiva pratītimātrāvipralabdhabuddhayas tatheti pratipattiṃ bhāvayanti ।। b
tathā kila viṣaṃ bhakṣaya mā cāsya gṛhe bhuṅkthāḥ ity atra viṣabhakṣaṇe abhyanujñānaṃ śrūyamāṇam api na vākyārthaḥ ।। b
kiṃ tarhi ।। b
pratikūlagṛhabhojananiṣedha eva tātparyam ।। b
evaṃ bhāratādāv upākhyānadvāreṇa sodyogasya vijigīṣor dviṣadupaśamo nirvighnaḥ kṣatriyāṇāṃ ca svadharmānuṣṭhānam abhyudayāyety evam arthaḥ stutivādaḥ ।। b
śrutau tu mantrārthavādapadānāṃ kāryātiśayāvedanaṃ vinā puruṣāpravṛtteḥ śraddhāvahavividhābhyudayajñānopajananapratītyaṅgatvaṃ na svarūpayāthārthyam ity aṅgatvam iti na taduktimātrād viśiṣṭadevatāsadbhāvāvedakatvam ।। b
tathā cāhuḥ ।
stutivādakṛtaś caiṣa janānāṃ mativibhramaḥ ।
paurvāparyāparāmṛṣṭaḥ śabdo anyāṃ kurute mitim ।। b
