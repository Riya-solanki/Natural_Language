nanu kaścidevāṃśo atra duṣṭo na punaḥ sarvo apīti cet, tarhi yatrāṃśe doṣaḥ so akāvyatvaprayojakaḥ, yatra dhvaniḥ sa uttamakāvyatvaprayojaka ityaṃśābhyāmubhayata ākṛṣyamāṇamidaṃ kāvyamakāvyaṃ vā kimapi na syāt ।
na ca kaṃcidevāṃśaṃ kāvyasya dūṣayantaḥ śratiduṣṭādayo doṣāḥ, kiṃ tarhi sarvameva kāvyam ।
tathāhi
kāvyātmabhūtasya rasasyānapakarṣakatve teṣāṃ doṣatvamapi nāṅgīkriyate ।
anyathā nityadoṣānityadoṣatvavyavasthāpi na syāt ।
