jayajrathasya vijayo vijayasya dhṛtiḥ sutaḥ ।
dhṛterdhṛvrataḥ putraḥ satyadharmā dhṛtavratāt ।।
tasya putrastvadhirathaḥ karṇastasya suto abhavat ।।vṛrṣasenastu karṇasya puruvaṃśyāñchaṇuṣva me ।।
iti śrīgāruḍe mahupārāṇe pūrvakhaṇḍe prathamāṃśākhye ācārakāṇḍe candravaṃśavarṇanaṃ nāmaikonacatvāriṃśaduttaraśatatamo adhyāyaḥ
