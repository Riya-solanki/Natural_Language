dharmiṇo anugrāhyasya dharmāṇāṃ yad anuvartanaṃ so anugrāhakasyānugrahaḥ kramavikasvarasvarūpāṇāṃ padmānāmiva vivasvataḥ ।। 
sa iti tathāvidho dharmaḥ kasyacidapi dharmiṇo nāsti yaḥ parameśvareṇa nānuvartyate nādhiṣṭhīyate tasya sarvavyāpakatvāt ।। 
evaṃ ca ।।
