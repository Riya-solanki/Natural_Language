sāritapratisāritādanusāritasya viśeṣamāha anvityādi ।।
tu punaḥ svacchaḥ pradhānasaṃskāraiḥ saṃskṛtaḥ sūtaḥ anusāritena samaḥ triguṇabījena sārito'nusāritastena tulyo yadi syātsa ca lakṣavedhī syāt ।।
evaṃ pratisāritaḥ sūtaḥ samena sārito ayutavedhī syāt ।।
evaṃ svecchātisvacchavṛddhau vedhasyāpi vṛddhiḥ syāditi rahasyam ।।
