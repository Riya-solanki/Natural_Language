upanīyamānaḥ skandakīrtinā baladarśakena svāmino me viṣṇoḥ syālīpatir iti kṛtvā kṛcchrāt pramocita iti vayasyaviṣṇunā me kathitam tat kim ayam idānīm asmād veśasaṃsargād vrīḍita ivātmānaṃ pariharati pārthivakumārasaṃnikarṣa enam anayā pravṛttyā vrīḍayati āścaryam guṇavān khalu guṇavatāṃ saṃnikarṣaḥ yad ayam api nāmaivaṃ guṇābhimukhaḥ tan na śakyam enam apratyabhijñānena sakāmaṃ kartum yāvad aham apy āyatanaṃ pradakṣiṇīkurvan nāma saṃmukhīnam enaṃ parihāsāvaskandena hanmi eṣa māṃ pratimukham evāvalokya prahasitaḥ haṇḍe sūryanāga, kim ayaṃ veśanavāvatāro andhakāranṛttam iva suhṛdavakṣepeṇa viphalīkriyate kiṃ bravīṣi - ka iva mamehārthaḥ ahaṃ hi kārāyām avaruddhasya mātulasya maudgalyasya pāraśavasya
