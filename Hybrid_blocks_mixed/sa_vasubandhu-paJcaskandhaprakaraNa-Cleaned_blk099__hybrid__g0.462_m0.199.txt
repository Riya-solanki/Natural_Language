। aayatanānāṃ salakṣaṇaprapañcaḥ ।।
cakṣurādīni rūpaśabdagandharasāyatanāni yathoktapūrvāṇi ।।spraṣṭavyāyatanaṃ catvāri mahābhūtāni uktaśca yaḥ spraṣṭabyaikadeśaḥ ।।mana āyatanaṃ yo vijñānaskandhaḥ ।।dharmāyatanaṃ vedanā, saṃjñā, saṃskārāḥ, avijñaptiḥ, asaṃskṛtāśca ।।
। asaṃskṛtāḥ salakṣaṇaprabhedāḥ ।।
