gaurgavayasadṛśo gavayasādṛśyapratiyogi tattatsadṛśaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ yugapanna dṛṣṭavānekameva tu gāmupalabhya nagare vane gavayaṃ paśyati so api gāṃ gavayasādṛśyaviśiṣṭāmupaminotyeva tasmānnānumānam ।
śābdatvaṃ tu na śaṅkyameva ।
ataḥ pramāṇāntaram, prasiddhatvācca na parīkṣitavyam ।
ityupamānavādaḥ ।।
