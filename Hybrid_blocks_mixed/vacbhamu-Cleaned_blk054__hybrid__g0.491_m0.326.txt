punarapi cidātmanyadhyāsamākṣipati- kathaṃ punaḥ iti ।
ayamarthaḥ- cidātmā prakāśate na vā ? na cet prakāśate, kathamasminnadhyāso viṣayataddharmāṇām ? na khalvapratibhāsamāne purovartini dravye rajatasya vā taddharmāṇāṃ vā samāropaḥ saṃbhavati ।
pratibhāsamāne vā na tāvadayamātmā jaḍo ghaṭādivatparādhīnaprakāśa iti yuktam ।
na khalu sa eva kartā ca karma ca bhavati, virodhāt ।
parasamavetakriyāphalaśāli hi karma, na ca jñānakriyā parasamavāyinīti kathamasyāḥ karma ? na ca tadeva svaṃ ca paraṃ ca, virodhāt ।
ātmāntarasamavāyābhyupagame tu jñeyasyātmanaḥ anātmatvaprasaṅgaḥ ।
evaṃ tasya tasyetyanavasthāprasaṅgaḥ ।
