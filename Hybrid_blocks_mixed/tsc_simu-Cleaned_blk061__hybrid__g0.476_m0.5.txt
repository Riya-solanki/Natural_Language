tatra śabdatanamātramahaṃkārājjāyata iti vikṛtistasmādākāśaṃ jāyata iti śabdatanmātraṃ prakṛtiḥ ।
tathā sparśatanmātramahaṃkārājjāyata iti vikṛtiḥ tadeva vāyuṃ janayatīti prakṛtiḥ ।
evaṃ rūpatanmātramahaṃkārājjāyata iti vikṛtiḥ tadeva tejo janayatīti prakṛtiḥ ।
rasatanmātramahaṅkārājjāyata iti vikṛtistadevāmbho janayatīti prakṛtiḥ ।
tathā gandhatanmātramahaṃkārājjāyata iti vikṛtiḥ tadevāvaniṃ janayatīti prakṛtiḥ ।।
