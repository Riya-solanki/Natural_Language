atha viśeṣavidhyantaramāha śastamityādi ।।
evaṃvidhaṃ bījaṃ śastaṃ kva sarvadvandve ।।
punaretaiḥ kṛtvā bījaṃ śastaṃ syāt ।।
kaiḥ girijatulelītakendragopādyaiḥ girijatu śilājatu lelītako gandhakaḥ indragopaḥ surendragopo jīvaviśeṣaḥ ete ādyā yeṣāṃ taiḥ ।।
na kevalametair mahiṣīkarṇamalādyaiśca mahiṣyāḥ karṇayormala ādyo yeṣāṃ te ādyaśabdānnāsākṣimalaṃ ca ।।
punaṣṭaṅkaṇālaviṣaiḥ ṭaṅkaṇaṃ saubhāgyaṃ ālaṃ haritālaṃ viṣaṃ kandajaṃ etaiḥ piṣṭairdvandvamelāpaḥ syāditi punaḥ saṃbandhaḥ ।।
