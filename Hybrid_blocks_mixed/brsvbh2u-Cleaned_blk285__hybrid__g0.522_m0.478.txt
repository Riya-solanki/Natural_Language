yata utkrāntyādiśrutibhirjīvānāmaṇutvaṃ prasādhitaṃ tato vyāpakātparamātmanasteṣāṃ tadvikāratayā bhedaḥ ।
tathāca mahattvānantyādiśrutayaḥ paramātmaviṣayā na jīvaviṣayā ityavirodha ityarthaḥ ।
yadi jīvā aṇavastato ayo ayaṃ vijñānamayaḥ prāṇeṣu'iti kathaṃ śārīro mahattvasaṃbandhitvena pratinirdiśyate iti codayati-nanviti ।
pariharati-śāstradṛṣṭyāpāramārthikadṛṣṭyā nirdeśo vāmadevavat ।
yathā hi garbhastha eva vāmadevo jīvaḥ paramārthadṛṣṭyātmano brahmatvaṃ pratipede, evaṃ vikārāṇāṃ prakṛtervāstavādabhedāttatparimāṇatvavyapadeśa ityarthaḥ ।।
