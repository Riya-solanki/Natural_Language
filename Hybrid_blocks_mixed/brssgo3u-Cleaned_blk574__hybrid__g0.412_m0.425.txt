na ।
adahara uttarebhyaḥ' ityatra chāndogye apyākāśaśabdaṃ brahmaiveti pratiṣṭhāpitatvāt ।
ayaṃ tvatra vidyate viśeṣaḥ ।
saguṇā hi brahmavidyā chāndogya upadiśyate aatha ya ihātmānamanuvidya vrajantyetāṃśca satyānkāmān' ityātmavatkāmānamapi vedyatvaśravaṇāt ।
vājasaneyake tu nirguṇameva paraṃ brahmopadiśyamānaṃ dṛśyate aata ūrdhvaṃ vimokṣāya brūhi' asaṅgo hyayaṃ puruṣaḥ ityādipraśnaprativacanasamanvayāt ।
vaśitvādi tu tatstutyarthameva guṇajātaṃ vājasaneyake saṃkīrtyate ।
tathācopariṣṭāt asa eṣa neti netyātmā' ityādinā nirguṇameva brahmopasaṃharati ।
guṇavatastu brahmaṇa ekatvādvibhūtipradarśanāyāyaṃ guṇopasaṃhāraḥ sūtrito nopāsanāyeti draṣṭavyam ।।
