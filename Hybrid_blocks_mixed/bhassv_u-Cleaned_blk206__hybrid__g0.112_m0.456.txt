{padmāvatī} - haṃ ayyautto 
ayye tava kāraṇādo ayyauttadaṃsaṇaṃ pariharāmi 
tā imaṃ dāva māhavīḷadāmaṇḍavaṃ pavisāmo 
[ham āryaputraḥ । ārye tava kāraṇād āryaputradarśanaṃ pariharāmi । tad imaṃ tāvan mādhavīlatāmaṇḍapaṃ praviśāmaḥ । ]
