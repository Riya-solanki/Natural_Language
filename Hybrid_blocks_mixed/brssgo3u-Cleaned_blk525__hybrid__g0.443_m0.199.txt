naiṣo asti virodhaḥ śabdānumānābhyāṃ śrutismṛtibhyāmityarthaḥ ।
tathāhi śrutiḥ - atadya itthaṃ viduḥ' iti pañcāgnividyāvatāṃ devayānaṃ panthānamavatārayantī aye ceme araṇye śraddhātapa ityupāsate' iti vidyāntaraśīlināmapi pcāgnividyāvidbhiḥ samānamārgatāṃ gamayati ।
kathaṃ punaravagamyate vidyāntaraśīlināmiyaṃ gatiriti ।
