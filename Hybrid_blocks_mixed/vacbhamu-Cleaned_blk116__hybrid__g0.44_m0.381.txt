antaḥkaraṇavṛttau brahmasākṣātkāre janayitavye asti tadupāsanāyāḥ karmāpekṣeti cet, na, tasyāḥ karmānuṣṭhānasahabhāvābhāvena tatsahakāritvānupapatteḥ ।
na khalu atattvamasi' ityādervākyānnirvicikitsaṃ śuddhabuddhodāsīnasvabhāvaṃ akartṛtvādyupetaṃ apetabrāhmaṇatvādijātiṃ dehādyatiriktaṃ ekamātmānaṃ pratipadyamānaḥ karmasvadhikāramavaboddhumarhati ।
anarhaśca kathaṃ kartā vādhikṛto vā ।
