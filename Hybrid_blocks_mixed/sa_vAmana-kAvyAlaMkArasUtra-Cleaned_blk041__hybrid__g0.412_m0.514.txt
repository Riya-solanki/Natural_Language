tacca tridhā bhinnamiti darśayitumāha 
gadyaṃ vṛttagandhi cūrṇamutkalikāprāyaṃ ca ।।
tallakṣaṇānyāha 
padyabhāgavadvṛttagandhi ।।
padyasya bhāgāḥ padyabhāgāstadvat vṛttagandhi । yathā pātālatālutalavāsiṣu dānaveṣu iti । atra hi vasantatilakākhyasya vṛttasya bhāgaḥ pratyabhijñāyate ।।
anāviddhalalitapadaṃ cūrṇam ।।
anāviddhānyadīrghasamāsāni lalitānyanuddhatāni padāni yasmiṃstadanāviddhalalitapadaṃ cūrṇamiti । yathā
