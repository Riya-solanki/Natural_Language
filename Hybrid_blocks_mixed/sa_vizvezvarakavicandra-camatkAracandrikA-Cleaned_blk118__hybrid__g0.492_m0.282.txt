atra haridrā niśā rātririti yāvat । rāhustamaḥ dhvāntamiti yāvat । icchā āśā dik । prayuktālaṅkṛtiriti prahāraḥ । viyutagarutāṃ vipakṣāṇām । 
kujarīṃ nagarīm ।
sārkā senā camūḥ ।
praharaḥ yāmaḥ gacchāmaḥ ।
evaṃ haridrādīnāṃ svaparyāyamātrabodhakatvena pratihastatve api senāpatibhirabhiyāti durgocchittaye gataiḥ tadālocitakārya vayaṃ senānvitāḥ timirapihitadaśadiśi niśāyāṃ prahārāya vipakṣāṇāṃ durganagarīṃ yāma ityevaṃ lakṣaṇaṃ mantrabhedabhayād gūḍhalekhamukhena rājñi preṣitamiti na duṣṭatvam ।।
