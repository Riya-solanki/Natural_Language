jñānasādhanavidhyanyathānupapattyāpyātmanaḥ kartṛtvaṃ vācyamityāha-samādhīti ।
muktiphalabhoktureva tadupāyasamādhikartṛtvaṃ yuktam, anyathātmano akartṛtve buddherapi abhoktryāḥ kartṛtvāyogātsamādhyabhāvaprasaṅga ityarthaḥ ।।
