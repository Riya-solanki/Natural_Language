bīje'bhrasatvaṃ pradhānamāha nirvāhaṇetyādi ।।
eṣaḥ kimarthaḥ aśeṣadoṣaśamanāya dhātvādīnāṃ samastadoṣanāśanāyetyarthaḥ ।।
evamamunā prakāreṇa yathā dhātunirvāhaṇavidhistathā bījānāṃ rase nirvāhaṇaṃ kuryāt sarvabījanirvāhaṇe abhrakasatvaṃ prathamaṃ nirvāhyamiti jñeyam ।।
