atha artha-vyaktiḥ-
śrūyamāṇasya vākyasya vinā śabdāntara-spṛhām । 
arthāvagamakatvaṃ yad artha-vyaktir iyaṃ matā ।। athā utphulla-gaṇḍa-yugam ity atra sarveṣāṃ padānām adhyāhāraya-pada-nirākāṅkṣatayā artha-vyaktiḥ ।
