hemno viśeṣamāha kanakāruṇetyādi ।।
kanakaṃ hema aruṇaṃ tāmraṃ samaṃ tulyabhāgaṃ mākṣikaṃ tāpyaṃ ayaṃ gaṇaḥ karañjatailapluto dhmātaḥ kāryaḥ ।।
punaḥ pāte pāte vāraṃvāraṃ nikṣepe sati daśa daśa guṇotkarṣaṃ vidanti dhmāta ityadhyāhāraḥ ।।
hi niścitam ।।
kiyatkālaṃ yāvatkoṭisaṃkhyāṃ vindati ।।
