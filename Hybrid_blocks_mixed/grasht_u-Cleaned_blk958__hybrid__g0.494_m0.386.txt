yogāntaramāha suratarutailam ityādi ।।
devadārutailaṃ saghṛtaṃ sājyaṃ etadubhayaṃ pītvā sakṣīraṃ śālyodanaṃ bhuktvā ṣaṣṭikaudanam ityabhiprāyaḥ punarjīrṇāhāre pratidinaṃ dināntavīradvaye veditavyam ।।
ca punaḥ sakuṣṭhān kuṣṭhaiḥ saha vartante evaṃvidhān pīnasādīn rogān harati dūrīkaroti ।।
