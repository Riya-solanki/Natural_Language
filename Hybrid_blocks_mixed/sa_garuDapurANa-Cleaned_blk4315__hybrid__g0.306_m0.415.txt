vyādhibhiḥ kṣīṇadehasya bhaktacchedakṛśasya ca ।
sarve api rogā nāśāya na tvevaṃ śāghrakāriṇaḥ ।।
hikrāśvāsau yathā tau hi mṛtyukāle kṛtālayau ।।
iti śrīgāruḍe mahāpurāṇe pūrvakhaṇḍe prathamāṃśākhye ācārakāṇḍe hikrānidānā nāmaikapañcāśaduttaraśatatamo adhyāyaḥ
