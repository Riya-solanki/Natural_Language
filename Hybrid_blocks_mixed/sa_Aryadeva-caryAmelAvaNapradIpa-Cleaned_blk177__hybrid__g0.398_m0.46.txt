anayorniḥsvabhāvatvāt tathatāśāntasaṃjñakam । tathatāmaṇḍale yogī sarvabuddhān praveśayed ।। iti । 
nirvāṇāgnimahāgore bhasmaśo api na mucyate ।
na tatra vidyate tattvaṃ nendriyārthā na dhātavaḥ ।।
