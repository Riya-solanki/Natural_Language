virodhetyādi ।
yo alaṅkāraḥ parasparaviruddhārthapratītyā, "viruddhārthavācināṃ" viruddhavastuvacanānāṃ yuktiyuktatāmullikhati tadabhidhāyinā padāntareṇa arthasāmarthyena vā samarpayan yuktyā prakāreṇopapadyamānatayā saṅgatiṃ vidadhāti ।
tasya padārthatvāt samanvayaṃ karoti virodhābhidhānaḥ sa bhaṇyate ।
