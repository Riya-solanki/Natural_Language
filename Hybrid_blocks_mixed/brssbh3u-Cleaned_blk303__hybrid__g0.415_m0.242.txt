) iti prakramya vāgādīnprāṇānasurapāpmaviddhatvena ninditvā mukhyaprāṇaparigrahaḥ paṭhyate - aatha hemamāsanyaṃ prāṇamūcustvaṃ na udgāyeti tatheti tebhya eṣa prāṇa udagāyat' iti ।
tathā chāndogye api - ataddhadevā udgīthamājagmuranenainānabhibhaviṣyāmaḥ' iti prakramyetarānprāṇānasurapāpmaviddhatvena ninditvā tathaiva mukhyaprāṇaparigrahaḥ paṭhyate - aatha ha ya evāyaṃ mukhyaḥ prāṇastamudgīthamupāsāṃcakrire' iti ।
ubhayatrāpi ca prāṇapraśaṃsayā prāṇavidyāvidhiradhyavasīyate ।
tatra saṃśayaḥ - kimatra vidyābhedaḥ syādāhosvidvidyaikatvamiti ।
kiṃ tāvatprāptaṃ pūrveṇa nyāyena vidyaikatvamiti ।
