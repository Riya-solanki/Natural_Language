aniṣṭvā caiva yajñaiśca mokṣamicchanvrajatyadhaḥ' ।।
iti; āha- yathā ca hṛdayādyavadānānāmānantaryaniyamaḥ ।
kutaḥ ? ahṛdayasyāgre avadyati atha jihvāyā atha vakṣasaḥ' ityathāgraśabdābhyāṃ kramasya vivakṣitatvāt ।
na tatheha kramo vivakṣitaḥ, śrutyā tayaivānantaramaniyamasya darśitatvāt, ayadi vetarathā brahmacaryādeva pravrajedgṛhādvā vanādvā' iti ।
etāvatā hi vairāgyamupalakṣayati ।
ata eva ayadahareva virajettadahareva pravrajet' iti śrutiḥ ।
nindāvacanaṃ ca aviśuddhasattvapuruṣābhiprāyam ।
aviśuddhasattvo hi mokṣamicchannālasyāttadupāye apravartamāno gṛhasthadharmamapi nityanaimittikamanācaranpratikṣaṇamupacīyamānapāpmā adhogatiṃ gacchatītyarthaḥ ।
