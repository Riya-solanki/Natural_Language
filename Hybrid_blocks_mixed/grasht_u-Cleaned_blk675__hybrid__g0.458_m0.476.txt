mahābījānāṃ bījānāṃ ca viśeṣavidhānamāha evamityādi ।।
evam uktavidhānena bījaṃ vidhāya rañjanavidhinā rañjanavidhānena surañjanaṃ kāryam ।।
tatkārye rasasya sūtasya triguṇaṃ hema saṃyojyaṃ punas tasya hemnaḥ triguṇaṃ varabījaṃ yojyaṃ iti viśeṣavidhiḥ ।।
