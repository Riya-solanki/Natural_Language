dvitīyoddiṣṭasya mardanasya sādhanaṃ spaṣṭayannāha guḍetyādi ।।
etair auṣadhair dinatrayaṃ parimāṇaṃ rasasya mardanaṃ kāryam ।।
etaiḥ kaiḥ guḍadagdhorṇālavaṇaiḥ guḍa ikṣuvikāraḥ prasiddhaḥ dagdhorṇā dagdhā cāsau ūrṇā ceti samāsaḥ ūrṇā pratītā meṣaromanicayam ityarthaḥ lavaṇaṃ saindhavamekaṃ guḍadagdhorṇārajanī iti vā pāṭhaḥ tatra haridrā grāhyā na saindhavam ।।
rasaṣoḍaśāṃśamānaiḥ rasātṣoḍaśāṃśapramāṇaiḥ ।।
aṃśa ityasya pratyekaṃ sambandhaḥ ।।
viśeṣaścātra vastraiścaturguṇair baddhaḥ sūtaḥ sthāpyaḥ śubhe ahani ।।
lohārkāśmajakhalve tu tapteṣveva tu mardayet ।।
uddiṣṭair oṣadhaiḥ sārdhaṃ sarvāmlaiḥ kāñjikairapi ।
peṣaṇaṃ mardanākhyaṃ syāttadbahirmalanāśanam iti paribhāṣā ।।
atra yantraṃ tu khalvākhyaṃ jñeyam ।।
tallakṣaṇaṃ tu khalvayogyā śilā nīlā śyāmā snigdhā dṛḍhā guruḥ ।।
ṣoḍaśāṅgulikotsedhā navāṅgulikavistarā iti ।।
khalvayogyaśilālakṣaṇaṃ prasaṅgād uktam ।।
utsedhena navāṅgulaḥ khalu kalātulyāṅgulāyāmavān vistāreṇa daśāṅgulo atha munibhir nimnas tathaivāṅgulaiḥ ।।
pālyāṃ hy aṅgulavistaraśca masṛṇo atīvārdhacandropamo gharṣo dvādaśakāṅgulaśca tadayaṃ khalvo mato mardane iti ।।
