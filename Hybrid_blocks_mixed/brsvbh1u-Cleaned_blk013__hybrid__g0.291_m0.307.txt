tvādvātmaiva brahmeti gīyate ।
sa cāyam ākīṭapataṅgebhya ā ca devarṣibhyaḥ prāṇabhṛnmātrasyedaṅkārāspadebhyo dehendriyamanobuddhiviṣayebhyo vivekena aaham'ityasaṃdigdhāviparyastāparokṣānubhavasiddha iti na jijñāsāspadam ।
na hi jātu kaścidatra saṃdigdhe aahaṃ vā nāhaṃ vā'iti ।
na ca viparyasyati anāhameva'iti ।
na ca aahaṃ kṛśaḥ, sthūlaḥ, gacchāmi'ityādidehadharmasāmānā
