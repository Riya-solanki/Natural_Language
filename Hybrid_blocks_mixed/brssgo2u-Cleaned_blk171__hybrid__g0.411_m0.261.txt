niravayavatvāt ।
yadi brahma pathivyādivatsāvayavamabhaviṣyattato asyaikadeśaḥ paryaṇaṃsyadekadeśaścāvāsthāsyat ।
niravayavaṃ tu brahma śrutibhyo avagamyate- aniṣkalaṃ niṣkriyaṃ śāntaṃ niravadyaṃ nirañjanam', adivyo hyamūrtaḥ puruṣaḥ sabāhyābhyantarohyajaḥ', aidaṃ mahadbhūtamanantamapāraṃ vijñānaghana eva', asa eṣa neti netyātmā', aasthūlamanaṇu' ityādyābhyaḥ sarvaviśeṣapratiṣedhinībhyaḥ ।
tataścaikadeśapariṇāmasaṃbhavātkṛtsnapariṇāmaprasaktau satyāṃ mūlocchedaḥ prasajyeta ।
draṣṭavyatopadeśānarthakyaṃ cāpannamayatnadraṣṭatvātkāryasya, tadvyatiriktasya ca brahmaṇo asaṃbhavāt ajatvādiśabdakopaśca ।
athaitaddoṣaparijihīrṣayā sāvayavameva brahmābhyupagamyeta tathāpi ye niravayavatvasya pratipādakāḥ śabdā udāhṛtāste prakuṣyeyuḥ ।
sāvayavatve cānityatvaprasaṅga iti ।
sarvathāyaṃ pakṣo na ghaṭayituṃ śakyata ityākṣipati ।।
