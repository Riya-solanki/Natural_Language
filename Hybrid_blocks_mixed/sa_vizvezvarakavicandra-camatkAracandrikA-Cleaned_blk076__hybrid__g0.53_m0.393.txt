yathā
vāgdevyāḥ prabhaviṣyatā prabhavatā gāḍhaṃ prabhutena ca śreyaḥpākabhareṇa siṅganṛpate bhāvajñacūḍhāmaṇim ।
tvāṃ jetāramajījanat prasavitā sargasya nocediyaṃ satye vā viphalāyuṣāṃ kṛtadhiyāṃ śāpe na jīveta vim ।।
