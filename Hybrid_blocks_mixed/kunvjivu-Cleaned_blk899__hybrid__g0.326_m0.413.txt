atra yasmāttathāvidhānantasāmagrīkasya madhorapi tatsamānopa lapsyate iti nyāyyametayoḥ suvyaktameva sāmyamupagamayati, tathācāsaṃkhyakusumatvādidharmasaukumāryātirekāt sāmyasaṃpattyādirvivakṣitetyādyupamālakṣaṇatātparya vikalpamevātra samarpyate ।
na cārthāntaranyāsabhrāntiratrodbhāvanīyā, tasya vākyārthatātparya lakṣaṇasāmānyamātraviṣayatvāt ।
tasmādanena nyāyena samānavastunyāsopanibandhanā prativastūpamāpi na pṛthagvaktavyatāmarhati, pūrvodāharaṇenaiva samānayogakṣematvāt ।
tathā ca lakṣaṇodāharaṇe tasyāḥ samānavastunyāsena prativastūpamā yathā
