ilāgre tribhiḥ prakārairannapathahīrakāṇāṃ niṣpattiṃ bhaṇiṣyati ।।
tatas teṣāṃ prakāratrayāṇāṃ madhyātprathamamekatamena prakāreṇa niṣpannam annapathe hīrakabhasma tathā jīrṇakhāparasattvaṃ sutaṃ cobhāv api catuṣpāde lohakhalve kṣiptvā thūthāviḍena piṣan kharalādho bhāge komalāgniṃ jvālayet ।।
tāvat yāvatā sarvaṃ hīrakabhasma jīryati ।।
tato hīrake jīrṇe sati rasaḥ sarvavyāpīti dehalohavyāpako bhavati ।।
iti jīrṇakhāparasattvasūtasyānnapathahīrake jāraṇamaṣṭamam ।।
