sattvāṃśo bahulo yasmācchuddhasattvaṃ caturmukhaḥ ।
utpattirmahataścoktā evaṃ ca vinatāsuta ।।
tajjñānānmokṣamāpnoti nānyathā tu kathañcana ।।
iti śrīgāruḍe mahāpurāṇe uttarakhaṇḍe kṛṣṇa garuḍasaṃvāde tṛtīyāṃśe brahmakāṇḍe guṇavaiṣamyabhedabrahmadehasvarūpaguṇasāmyanirūpaṇaṃ nāma caturtho adhyāyaḥ
