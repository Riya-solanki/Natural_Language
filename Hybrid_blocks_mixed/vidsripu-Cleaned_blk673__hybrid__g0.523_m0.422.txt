valatu taralā dṛṣṭā dṛṣṭiḥ khalā sakhi mekhalā skhalatu kucayor utkampān me vidīryatu kañcukam ।
tad api na mayā sambhāṣyo asau punar dayitaḥ śaṭhaḥ sphurati hṛdayaṃ maunenāntar na me yadi tatkṣaṇāt ।।
amarukasya
NB । take dṛṣṭādṛṣṭis as a compound `quick glance, halfglance'
