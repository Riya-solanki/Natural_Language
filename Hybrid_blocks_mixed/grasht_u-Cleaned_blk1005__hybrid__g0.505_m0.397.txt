viśeṣamāha eṣāmityādi ।।
eṣāṃ pūrvoktānāṃ yogānāṃ madhye ādita ārambhataḥ ekaṃ yogaṃ kṛtvā niḥśreyaso mokṣaḥ tatsiddhaye niṣpattaye saṃvatsaraṃ varṣaparimāṇaṃ ayanaṃ ṣaṇmāsaparyantaṃ yojyaṃ bhoktṛṣu iti śeṣaḥ ।।
muktiḥ kaivalyanirvāṇaśreyoniḥśreyasāmṛtam ityamaraḥ ।।
