pratibhādāridrayadainyādatisvalpasubhāṣitena kavinā varṇasāvarṇyaramyatāmātramatroditam, na punarvācyavaicitryakaṇikā kācidapyasti ।
yatkila nūtanatāruṇyataraṅgitalāvaṇyalaṭabhakānteḥ kāntāyāḥ kāmayamānena kenacidevamuyateyadi taruṇi tvaṃ ramaṇamandiraṃ vrajasi tatkiṃ tvadīyaṃ parisaraṇaṃ raṇaraṇakamakāraṇaṃ mama karotītyatigrāmyeyamuktiḥ ।
kiñca, na akāraṇam, yatastasyāstadanādareṇa gamane tadanuraktāntaḥ karaṇasya virahavidhuratāśaṅkākātaratā kāraṇaṃ raṇaraṇakasya ।
yadi vā parisaraṇasya mayā kimarāddhamityākaraṇatāsamarpakam, etadapyatigrāmyataram ।
saṃbodhanāni ca bahūni munipraṇītastotrāmantraṇakalpāni na kāñcidapi tadvidāhlāda kāritāṃ puṣṇantīti yatkiñcidetat ।
vastumātraṃ ca śabdaśobhā tiśayaśūnyaṃ na kāvyavyapadeśamarhati ।
yathā
