vijñānaṃ katamat । ālambanaṃbijñaptiḥ ।।
cittamanasī vijñānaparyāyau sanirvacano taccitaṃ mano api citrīkāratā manoniśrayatāṃ ca upādāya ।।
।। salakṣaṇacittanirdeśaḥ ।।
mūlacittamālayavijñānaṃ tadyathedaṃ sarvasaṃskārāṇāṃ saṃcitaṃ bījam ।।
tatpunarālambanamapyuparichinnamekasantānavarti । yathā nirodhasamāpatyasaṃjñisamāpatyā - saṃjñikebhyovyutthitasyāsmād viṣayavijñaptikaṃ nāma pravṛtti vijñānaṃ jāyate । ālambanapratyayamapekṣya bhinnākāreṣu vṛttitāmucchiddya punarjātatāṃ saṃsāre pravṛttinivṛtitāṃ copādāya tadālambanaṃ nāma vijñānam ।।
ālayavijñānaṃ hi sarvabījālayatāmātmabhāvālayahetutāmātmabhāve sthititāṃ copādāya ।।
ādānavijñānamapi tat । ātmabhāvādānatāmupādāya ।।
