adhikārinirūpaṇārthamāha 
arocakinaḥ satṛṇābhyavahāriṇaśca kavayaḥ ।।
iha khalu dvaye kavayaḥ sambhavanti । arocakinaḥ, satṛṇābhyavahāriṇaśceti । arocakisatṛṇābhyavahāriśabdau gauṇārthau । ko'sāvarthaḥ । vivekitvamavivekitvaṃ ceti ।।
