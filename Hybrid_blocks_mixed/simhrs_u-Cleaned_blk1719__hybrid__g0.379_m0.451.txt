atha anukta-siddhiḥ-
prastāvanaiva śeṣo' rtho yatrānukto' pi gṛhyate । 
anukta-siddhir eṣā syād ity āha bharato muniḥ ।।
Nāṭ prastāvenaiva śeṣo' rthaḥ kṛtsno yan na pratīyate । vacanena vinānukta-siddhiḥ sā parikīrtitā ।।
