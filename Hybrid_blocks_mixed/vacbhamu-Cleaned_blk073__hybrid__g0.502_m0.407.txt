tatkṛtena doṣeṇa guṇena vā aṇumātreṇāpi sa na saṃbadhyate, antaḥkaraṇādidoṣeṇāśanāyādinā cidātmā, cidātmano guṇena caitanyānandādinā antaḥkaraṇādi na saṃbadhyate ।
etaduktaṃ bhavati - tattvāvadhāraṇābhyāsasya hi svabhāva eva sa tādṛśaḥ, yadanādimapi nirūḍhanibiḍavāsanamapi mithyāpratyayamapanayati ।
tattvapakṣapāto hi svabhāvo dhiyām, yathāhurbāhyā api
