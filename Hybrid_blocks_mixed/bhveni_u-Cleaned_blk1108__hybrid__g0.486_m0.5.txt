pāñcālyā manyuvahniḥ sphuṭamupaśamitaprāya eva prasahya vyāsaktaiḥ keśapāśairhatapatiṣu mayā kauravāntaḥpureṣu
bhrāturduḥśāsanasya sravadasṛgurasaḥ pīyamānaṃ nirīkṣya ē̃ krodhātkiṃ bhīmasene vihitamasamaye yattvayāsto abhimānaḥ ।।
