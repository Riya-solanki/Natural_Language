svīkṛtya sarvasarito gaṅgā jaladhau yathā tathā haimam ।
praviśati rase gṛhītvā saṃmiliti sarvalohaguṇān ।।
jīryati milati ca śulbe tatsatvaṃ kiṭṭatāṃ yāti ।
suvṛttaḥ sadrṛtārambhaḥ sujñaḥ saṃjñānadarśakaḥ ।
abhūcca dhanadharmajño hārī kuñjanasaṃpadām ।।
ādau haimapraśaṃsām āha svīkṛtyetyādi ।।
haimaṃ svarṇamākṣikasatvaṃ saṃmiliti kiṃkṛtvā sarvalohaguṇān sarvaloheṣu samastadhātuṣu saṃmilitā miśritāḥ ye guṇās tān gṛhītvā tathā rase praviśati yathā gaṅgā sarvā nadyaḥ saritaḥ svīkṛtya aṅgīkṛtya jaladhau samudre praviśati ।।
tāpyasatvādhikāram āha jīryatītyādi ।।
tat haimaṃ tāpyasatvaṃ śulbe tāmre milati sati sattvaṃ jīryati jāraṇatvamāpnoti rase iti śeṣaḥ ।।
tasmin satve śulbe milati sati kiṭṭatāṃ yāti lohamalasadṛśaṃ syāt ।।
