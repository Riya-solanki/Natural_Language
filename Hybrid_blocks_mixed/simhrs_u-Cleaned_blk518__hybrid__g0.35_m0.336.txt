tatra nirvedaḥ-
tattva-jñānāc ca daurgatyāv āpado viprayogataḥ ।
īrṣyāder api saṃjātaṃ nirvedaḥ svāvamānanam ।। 
Another reading in some manuscripts anubhāvas tu naiṣphalya-matir nirveda ucyate । atra cintāśru-niḥśvasa-vaivarṇyocchvāsa-dīnatā ।।
