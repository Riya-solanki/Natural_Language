pañca bhedā ime nityaṃ sarvāvasthāsu sarvaśaḥ ।
etādṛśyāṃ tu māyāyāṃ vīryamādhatta vīryavān ।।
puruṣākhyo haristasmāttriguṇānasṛjatprabhuḥ ।।
iti śrīgāruḍe mahāpurāṇe tṛtīyāṃśe brahmakāṇḍe bhagavadvīryasvarūpatadādhānadvārakaguṇatraya sṛṣṭijaḍeśabhedādinirūpaṇaṃ nāma tṛtīyo adhyāyaḥ
