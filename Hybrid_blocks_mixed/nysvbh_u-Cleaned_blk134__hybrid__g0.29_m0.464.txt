nitye ca muktasaṃsārasthayoraviśeṣa ityuktam।
yathā muktasya nityaṃ sukhaṃ tatsaṃvedanahetuśca, saṃvedanasya tūparamo nāsti, kāraṇasya nityatvāt, tathā saṃsārasthasyāpīti।
evaṃ ca sati dharmādharmaphalena sukhaduḥkhasaṃvedanena sāhacaryaṃ gṛhyeteti।।
