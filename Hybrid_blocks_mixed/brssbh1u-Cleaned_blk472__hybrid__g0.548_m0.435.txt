nanvetadapyantarvartivastusadbhāvapradarśanāyaiva pradarśyate ।
ataṃ cedbrūyuryadidamasminbrahmapure daharaṃ puṇḍarīkaṃ veśma daharo asminnantarākāśaḥ kiṃ tadatra vidyate yadanveṣṭavyaṃ yadbhāva vijijñāsitavyam' ityākṣipya parihārāvasara ākāśaupamyopakrameṇa dyāvāpṛthivyādīnāmantaḥsamāhitatvadarśanāt ।
