na ketakīnāṃ vilasanti sūcayaḥ pravāsino hanta hasatyayaṃ vidhiḥ ।
taḍillateyaṃ na cakāsti cañcalā puraḥ smarajyotiridaṃ vivartate ।।
vākyārthayostātparyāt tādrūpyamiti na rūpakam ।।
rūpakaṃ tu kīdṛśamityāha 
upamānenopameyasya guṇasāmyāttattvāropo rūpakam ।।
upamānenopameyasya guṇasāmyāttattvasyābhedasyāropaṇamāropo rūpakam । upamānopameyayorubhayorapi grahaṇaṃ laukikyāḥ kalpitāyāścopamāyāḥ prakṛkṣitvamatra yathā vijñāyeteti । yathā
