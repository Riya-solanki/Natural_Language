atra vācāmīśa daivataguro tvāmapyajaiṣīdityekaṃ vākyam । sureśa vayaṃ jitā ityapararaṃ prativākyam । tayoraṃśabhūtasambuddhipadasaṅkare api vākyopavākyatvānna doṣaḥ ।
tadvākyagarbhita yasya garbhe vākyāntarasthitiḥ ।।
yathā
kṛtāntadaṃṣṭrākrūrāya khaḍgāya tava bhūpate ।
kā hi śaṅkā madāndhānāṃ tiṣṭhanti śatrubhūvarāḥ ।।
