svabhāvena mṛtasya bhekasyodaraṃ vidārya sphāṭikojjvalaṭaṅkaṇakṣārasyātisūkṣmāḥ khoṭāṃs tatra kṣiptvā tato ghṛtatailādinā digdhvā madhye sthāli bhekaṃ muktvopari pradhvarāṃ ḍhaṅkaṇīyaṃ dattvā bhūmimadhye sthālī nikhanyate ।। 
ṣaṣṭidinaiḥ punaḥ sthālīmākṛṣya madhyātsarvaṃ gṛhītvā kharale piṣṭvā cūrṇaṃ kāryam ।। 
tataḥ sarvottamaṃ suvarṇagadyāṇakaṃ gālayitvā vallamātraṃ bhekacūrṇaṃ madhye kṣipyate ।। 
tatas tat suvarṇaṃ punarbandhaṃ nāpnoti kiṃtu dravameva tiṣṭhati ।। 
iyamevaṃ suvarṇadrutir bhavati ।। 
iyaṃ ca suvarṇadrutirevaṃ kāntalohe rūpye vaṅge nāge tāmre ca gadyāṇamātre ca gālite vallamātraṃ bhekacūrṇaṃ kṣipyate ।। 
tato dravarūpāṇi sarvāṇi bhaveyus tattannāmnī ca drutir bhavati ।। 
lohadrutiḥ rūpyadrutiḥ vaṅgadrutiḥ nāgadrutis tāmradrutiśceti ।। 
iti ṣaḍlohadrutikaraṇaṃ prathamam ।। 
pītāṅgādeva dālīṃ ca pañcāṅgāṃ kuru khaṇḍaśaḥ ।
karpare jvālayitvā ca kartavyaṃ bhasma sūkṣmakam ।।
śeṣānte eva pañcāṅgāṃ niśāhvāyāṃ ca vartayet ।
tadrasenaiva dātavyā bhāvanāsyaiva bhasmanaḥ ।।
gālite caikagadyāṇe tithivarṇe ca hemaje ।
prakṣipte bhasmano vallaṃ vallaṃ ca prakṣipet punaḥ ।।
evaṃ gadyāṇamadhye ca jāryo gadyāṇakaḥ sadā ।
itthaṃ hemadrutirjātā sarvaloheṣvayaṃ vidhiḥ ।।
