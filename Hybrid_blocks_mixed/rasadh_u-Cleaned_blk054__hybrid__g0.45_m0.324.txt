Comm Rasādhyāyaṭīkā
ghaṭārdhasadṛśe kaṭāhe'dho mṛttikāyāḥ kuṇḍalī kāryate tac ca kaṭāhabundham ityucyate ।।
tataḥ pūrvakṛtāṃ tāṃ pīṭhīṃ tatra kaṭāhabundhe kṣiptvopari ācchādanārthaṃ sabalacikkaṇavastrasampuṭaṃ tāḍayitvā tasya vastrasya prāntān kuṇḍalīṃ paritaś cikkaṇamṛdā tathā limpedyathā jaṭitā iva te bhaveyuḥ ।।
tatastaṃ kaṭāhaṃ jalārdhapūrṇasthālikāmukhopari pradhvaraṃ muktvā sthālīkaṇṭhakaṭāhabundhakasaṃyogasthāne karpaṭamṛdaṃ dṛḍhaṃ dattvā kaṭāhamadhye chāṇakāni ghaṭīcatuṣkaṃ yāvaj jvālyante ।।
tato'gnitāpena raso vastratantuchidrair viniḥsṛtyādhaḥsthālikāsthitajalamadhye yāti ।।
tāmraṃ punarbudhaka eva vilagya tiṣṭhati ।।
tataḥ punarapi pūrvavat tāmracūrṇapāradapīṭhīṃ kṛtvā kaṭāhabundhasajalasthālikāyantreṇa pūrvavadraso jale pātyate ।।
evaṃ saptavāraṃ rasapātane kṛte kuṇṭhatvajaḍatvādayaḥ sūkṣmadoṣā yānti ।।
iti mūrchitotthāpitasūtasya pātanasaṃskāraḥ pañcamaḥ ।।
