sa cedayamanādiranādinā ātmayāthātmyajñānena sahānuvartate, kuto asya nivṛttiḥ avirodhāt ? kutaścātmayāthātmyānanubhavaḥ ? na hi aaham' ityanubhavādanyadātmayāthātmyajñānamasti ।
na ca aaham' iti sarvajanīnasphuṭatarānubhavasamarthita ātmā dehendriyādivyatiriktaḥ śakya upaniṣadāṃ sahasrairapyanyathayitum, anubhavavirodhāt ।
na hyāgamāḥ sahasramapi ghaṭaṃ paṭayitumīśate ।
tasmādanubhavavirodhādupacaritārthā evopaniṣada iti yuktamutpaśyāma ityāśayavānāśaṅkya pariharati- yuṣmadasmatpratyayagocarayoḥ iti ।
