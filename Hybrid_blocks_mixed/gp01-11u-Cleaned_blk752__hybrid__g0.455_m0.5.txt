bālena samam anyo' nyaṃ prābalyaṃ darśayann iva ।
ūrdhvādho-bhāvam āsādya sarvā hāsayati sma saḥ ।। [anuṣṭubh]
mātṝṇām agrato bāhū vikṣipan dhāvati sma saḥ ।
darśayann iva tejaḥ svaṃ hasan pāte ruroda ca ।। [anuṣṭubh]
