kurvantvāptā hatānāṃ raṇaśirasi janā vahnisāddehabhārā- naśrūnmiśraṃ kathaṃciddadatu jalamamī bāndhavā bāndhavebhyaḥ
mārgantāṃ jñātidehānhatanaragahane khaṇḍitāngṛdhrakaṅkai- ē̃ rastaṃ bhāsvānprayātaḥ saha ripubhirayaṃ saṃhriyantāṃ balāni ।।
