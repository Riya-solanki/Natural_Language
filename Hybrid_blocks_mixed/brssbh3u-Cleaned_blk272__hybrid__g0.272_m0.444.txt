bādarāyaṇastvācāryaḥ pūrvoktameveśvaraṃ phalahetuṃ manyate ।
kevalākarmaṇo apūrvādvā kevalātphalamityayaṃ pakṣastuśabdena vyāvartyate ।
karmāpekṣādapūrvāpe akṣādvā yathā tathāstvīśvarātphalamiti siddhāntaḥ ।
kutaḥ - hetuvyapadeśāt ।
darmādharmayorapi hi kārayitṛtveneśvaro heturvyapadiśyate phalasya ca dātṛtvena aeṣa hyeṣa sādhu karma kārayati taṃ yamebhyo lokebhya unninīṣate ।
eṣa u evāsādhu karma kārayati taṃ yamadho ninīṣate' iti ।
smaryate cāyamartho bhagavadgītāsu - ayo yo yāṃ yāṃ tanuṃ bhaktaḥ śraddhayārcitumicchati ।
tasya tasyācalāṃ śradjhadhāṃ tāmeva vidadhāmyaham ।।
sa tayā śraddhayā yuktastasyārādhanamīhate ।
labhate ca tataḥ kāmānmayaiva vihitānhitān' iti ।
sarvavedānteṣu ceśvarahetukā eva sṛṣṭayo vyapadiśyante ।
tadeva ceśvarasya phalahetutvaṃ yatsvakarmānurūpāḥ prajāḥ sṛjatīti ।
vicitrakāryānupapattyādayo api doṣāḥ kṛtaprayatnāpekṣatvādīśvarasya na prasajyante ।।
