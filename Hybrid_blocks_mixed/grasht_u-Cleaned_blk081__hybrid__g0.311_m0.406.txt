śarīrasya vayovibhāgenāsthiratvaṃ darśayannāha bāla ityādi ।।
ṣoḍaśavarṣa iti ṣoḍaśa varṣāṇi yasya vayasi sa ṣoḍaśābdo bāla ityarthaḥ ।।
granthāntare bālatve'pi vayobhedā vartante ।।
yathā padyam ।
ā pañcamācca kaumāraḥ paugaṇḍo navahāyanaḥ ।
ā ṣoḍaśācca kiśoro yauvanaṃ ca tataḥ param ।।
iti ।।
bālo vastujñāne aśaktaḥ ।।
punaḥ ṣoḍaśavarṣebhyaḥ paraḥ viṣayarasāsvādalampaṭo bhavati viṣayāḥ śabdasparśarūparasagandhā rasāḥ śṛṅgārahāsyakaruṇaraudravīrabhayānakabībhatsādbhutaśāntāḥ ।।
kecit śāntaṃ rasaṃ na bruvanti nirvikāratvāt eteṣām āsvādaḥ svādas tatra lampaṭo vyāsaktaḥ atha vā viṣayānantaraṃ snehastatreti ।।
ataḥ parato jātaviveko bhavati utpannavicāro bhavati ।।
tadā vṛddho'kṣamaḥ paraṃ manuṣyaḥ muktiṃ kaivalyaṃ katham āpnuyāt na kathamapītyartho vayasyupaplavabhāvāt ।।
