iti saṃpratyapi tāmevaṃvidhāṃ vīkṣituṃ pravṛttasya mama manaḥ kimiti baddhotkaṇṭhamiti parasamāpte api tathāvidho vastu vinyāso vihitaḥ"athavā premāsamāptotsavam" iti,yena pūrveṣāṃ jīvitamivārpitam ।
yadyapi dvayorapyetayostatprādhānyenaiva vākyārthopanibandhaḥ, tathāpi kavipratibhāprauḍhireva prādhānyenāvitaṣṭhate ।
