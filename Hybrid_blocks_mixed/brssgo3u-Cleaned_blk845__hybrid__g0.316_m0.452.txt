asarvāpekṣā ca -' ityatrāśramakarmaṇāṃ vidyāsādhanatvamavadhāritam ।
idānīṃ tu kimamumukṣorapyaśramamātraniṣṭhasya vidyāmakāmamayamānasya tānyanuṣṭheyānyutāho neti cintyate ।
tatra atametaṃ vedānuvacanena brāhmaṇā vividiṣanti' ityādināśramakarmaṇāṃ vidyāsādhanatvena vihitatvādvidyāmanicchataḥ phalāntaraṃ kāmayamānasya nityanyananuṣṭheyāni ।
atha tasyāpyanuṣṭheyāni na tarhyeṣāṃ vidyāsādhanatvaṃ nityānityasaṃyogavirodhāditi ।
asyāṃ prāptau paṭhati - aāśramamātraniṣṭhasyāpyamumukṣoḥ kartavyanyeva nityāni karmāṇi yāvajjīvamagnihotraṃ juhoti' ityādinā vihitatvāt ।
nahi vacanasyātibhāro nāma kaścidasti ।।
