{tataḥ praviśati vidūṣakaḥ }
{vidūṣakaḥ} - ajja khu devīvioavihurahiaassa tattahodo vaccharāassa padumāvadīpāṇiggahaṇasamīriassa accantasuhāvahe maṅgaḷosave madaṇaggidāho ahiadaraṃ vaḍḍhai 
{padminikāṃ vilokya }
ayi padumiṇiā 
padumiṇie kiṃ iha vattadi 
[adya khalu devīviyogavidhurahṛdayasya tatrabhavato vatsarājasya padmāvatīpāṇigrahaṇasamīritasyātyantasukhāvahe maṅgalotsave madanāgnidāho adhikataraṃ vardhate । padminike kim iha vartate । ]
