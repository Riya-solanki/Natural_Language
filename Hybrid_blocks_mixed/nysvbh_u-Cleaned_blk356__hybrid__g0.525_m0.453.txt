yady atītānāgatau itaretarāpekṣau sidhyetām ? pratipadyemahi vartamānavilopam nātītāpekṣānāgatasiddhiḥ nāpyanāgatāpekṣātītasiddhiḥ।
kayā yuktyā ।
kena kalpena atītaḥ kathamatītāpekṣānāgatasiddhiḥ ? kena ca kalpenānāgataḥ ? iti naitacchakyaṃ nirvaktum ।
avyākaraṇīyametad vartamānalope iti।
yacca manyeta hrasvadīrghayoḥ sthalanimnayoḥ chāyātapayośca yathetaretarāpekṣayā siddhirevam atītānāgatayoriti, tannopapadyate viśeṣahetvabhāvāt dṛṣṭāntavat pratidṛṣṭāntopi prasajyate yathā rūpasparśauṃ gandharasau netaretarāpekṣau sidhyataḥ evamatītānāgatāviti netaretarāpekṣā kasya cit siddhiriti।
yasmādekābhāve anyatarābhāvādubhayābhāvaḥ।
yadyekasyānyatarāpekṣā siddhiḥ ? anyatarasyedānīṃ kimapekṣā ? yadyanyatarasyaikāpekṣā siddhiḥ ? ekasyedānīṃ kimapekṣā ?, evamekasyābhāve anyataranna sidhyatīti ubhayābhāvaḥ prasajyate।
arthasadbhāvavyaṅgyaścāyaṃ vartamānaḥ kālaḥ avidyate dravyam' avidyate guṇaḥ' avidyate karma' iti ।।
