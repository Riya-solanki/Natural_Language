na sthānato api parasyobhayaliṅgaṃ sarvatra hi ।
avāntarasaṃgatimāha-yena brahmaṇā suṣuptādiṣviti ।
yadyapi atadananyatvamārambhaṇaśabdādibhyaḥ'ityatra niṣprapañcameva brahmopapāditaṃ tathāpi prapañcaliṅgānāṃ bahvīnāṃ śrutīnāṃ darśanādbhavati punarvicikitsā atastannivāraṇāyārambhaḥ ।
tasya ca tattvajñānamapavargopayogīti prayojanavān vicāraḥ ।
tatrobhayaliṅgaśravaṇādubhayarūpatvaṃ brahmaṇaḥ prāptam ।
