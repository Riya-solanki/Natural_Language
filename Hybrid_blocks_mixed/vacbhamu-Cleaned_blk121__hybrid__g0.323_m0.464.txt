ityādikā ।
niṣedhaśāstraṃ tu na śraddhāmapekṣate ।
api tu niṣidhyamānakriyonmukho nara ityeva pravartate ।
tathā ca sāṃsārika iva śraddhāvagatabrahmatattvo api niṣedhamatikramya pravartamānaḥ pratyavaitīti na bhinnakarmadarśanābhyupagamaḥ ।
tasmānnopāsanāyāḥ kārye karmāpekṣā ।
