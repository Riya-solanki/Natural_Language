tathottarasattvena guṇādhikyamāha koṭimityādi ।।
sūte sarito bījena grāsanyāyaṃ vihāya samena bījenānusārito yaḥ sa koṭisaṃkhyāṃ dhātūnāmiti śeṣaḥ sūto vidhyati ।।
yathoktasārite sūte daśaguṇavṛddhiḥ sarvatraiveti veditavyam ।।
