no iti ।
tadyathetyādi kṛtavyākhyānam ।
sūtrārthamupasaṃharati-tasmāditi ।
anyadharmeṇānyasyopāsanaṃ kathamityāśaṅkyāśritatvādityāha-anyatrāpīti ।
upādhirjīvaḥ ।
tat anyadharmeṇopāsanam ।
iyamasaṃgatā vyākhyā ।
tathāhi-na tāvadāruṇyādyanekaguṇaviśiṣṭaprāptakrayaṇavadupāsātrayaviśiṣṭasya brahmaṇo vidhiḥ saṃbhavati, siddhasya vidhyanarhatvāt ।
nāpi brahmānuvādenopāsātrayavidhiḥ, vākyabhedāt ।
naca nānādharmaviśiṣṭamekamupāsanaṃ vidhīyata iti vācyaṃ, tādṛśavidhivākyasyātrāśravaṇāt ।
naca ataṃ māmāyuramṛtamityupāḥsva'ityatra māmiti jīvena, āyuriti prāṇena, amṛtamiti brahmaṇā svasvadharmavatā viśiṣṭopāsanāvidhiriti vācyaṃ, sarveṣāṃ dharmāṇāmaśravaṇāt, brahmāśruteśca ।
aprāṇo vā amṛtam'iti prāṇasyaivāmṛtatvaśruteḥ ।
ata upāsanāvidhilubdhena avaktāraṃ vidyāt' aetadevokthamupāsīta' asa ma ātmeti vidyāt'iti jīvaprāṇabrahmopāsanavidhayaḥ, anye guṇavidhaya iti svīkṛtyaikavākyatvaṃ tyājyaṃ, taccāyuktaṃ, upakramādinaikavākyatānirṇayāditi ।
tasmājjñeyapratyagbrahmaparamidaṃ vākyamityupasaṃharati-tasmāditi ।।
