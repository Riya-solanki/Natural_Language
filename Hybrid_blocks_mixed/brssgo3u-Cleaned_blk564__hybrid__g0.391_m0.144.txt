asa yo haitaṃ mahadyakṣaṃ prathamajaṃ veda brahma' ityādinā vājasaneyake satyavidyāṃ sanāmākṣaropāsanāṃ vidhāyānantimāmnāyate - atadyattatsatyamaso sa ādityo ya eṣa etasminmaṇḍale puruṣo yaścāyaṃ dakṣiṇe akṣanpuruṣaḥ' ityādi ।
tatra saṃśayaḥ -' kiṃ dve ete satyavidye kiṃvaikaiveti ।
dve iti tāvatprāptam ।
bhedena hi phalasaṃyogo bhavati jayatīmāṃllokān' iti purastāt ।
hanti pāpmānaṃ jahāti ca ityupariṣcāt ।
prakṛtākarṣaṇaṃ tūpāsyaikatvāditi ।
evaṃ prāpte brūmaḥ - ekaiveyaṃ satyavidyeti ।
kutaḥ - atadyattatsatyam' iti prakṛtākarṣaṇāt ।
