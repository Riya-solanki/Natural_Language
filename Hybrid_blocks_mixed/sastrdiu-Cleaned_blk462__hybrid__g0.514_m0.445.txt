tadidamayuktam ।
cidrūpasyātmano jaḍarūpapariṇāmāsaṃbhavāt ।
ekatve cātmanaḥ sarvaśarīreṣu pratasaṃdhānaṃ syāt- devadatto ahaṃ yajñadatto ahameveti, tathā devadattaśarīre me sukhaṃ yajñadattaśarīre me duḥkhamiti, pāde me sukhaṃ śirasi me
