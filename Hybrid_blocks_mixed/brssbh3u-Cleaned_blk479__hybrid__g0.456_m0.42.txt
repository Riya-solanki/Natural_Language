tatra saṃśayaḥ - kiṃ pṛthagevemau vāyuprāṇāvupagantavyau syātāmapṛthagveti ।
apṛthageveti tāvatprāptaṃ tattvabhedāt ।
nahyabhinne tattve pṛthaganucintanaṃ nyāyyam ।
darśayati ca śrutiradhyātmamadhidaivataṃ ca tattvābhedam - aagnirvāgbhūtvā mukhaṃ prāviśat' ityārabhya ।
tathā ata ete sarva eva samāḥ sarvo anantāḥ' ityādhyātmikānāṃ prāmānāmādhidaivikīṃ vibūtimātmabhūtāṃ darśayati ।
tathānyatrāpi tatra tatrādhyātmamadhidaivataṃ ca bahudhā tattvabhedadarśanaṃ bhavati ।
kvacicca ayaḥ prāṇaḥ sa vāyuḥ iti spaṣṭameva vāyuṃ prāṇaṃ caikaṃ karoti ।
tathodāhṛte api vājasaneyibrāhmaṇe yataścedeti sūryaḥ ityasminnupasaṃhāraśloke aprāṇādvā eṣa udeti prāṇe astameti' iti prāṇenaivopasaṃharannetvaṃ darśayati ।
tasmādekameva vrataṃ caretprāṇyāccaivāpāyānyācca' iti ca prāṇavratenaikenopasaṃharennetadeva draḍhayati ।
tathā chāndogye api parastāt amahātmanaścaturo deva ekaḥ kaḥ sa jāgara bhuvanasya gopāḥ' ityekameva saṃvargaṃ gamayati na bravītyeka ekeṣāṃ caturṇāṃ saṃvargo aparo apareṣāmiti ।
tasmādapṛthaktvamupagamanasyeti ।
evaṃ prāpte brūmaḥ - pṛthageva vāyuprāṇāvupagantavyāviti ।
kasmāt ।
pṛthagupadeśāt ।
ādhyānārtho hyayamadhyātmādhidaivavibhāgopadeśaḥ so asatyādhyānapṛthaktve anarthaka eva syāt ।
