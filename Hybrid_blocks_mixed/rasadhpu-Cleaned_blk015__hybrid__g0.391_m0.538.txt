atra cāyamāmnāyaḥ ।। 
khalve rasopari yathoktauṣadharasaḥ kṣipyate ।। 
yathā yathā sūto bruḍati tathā tathā mardanīyaḥ ।। 
śanaiḥ śanair yadi cāgretanauṣadharasaḥ śuṣyati tadā punaḥ kṣepaṇīyaḥ ।। 
evaṃ saptadināni yadi marditaḥ pārado bhavati tadā kāñjikena kharalo bhriyate punaḥ śanairmṛdyate tato vastravartayaḥ kṣiptvā tathā mucyante tathā vartiprāntāḥ bhūmau laganti ।। 
taiśca kāñjikaṃ galitvā yāti ।। 
evaṃ sūtaḥ sarvair apyauṣadhaiḥ saptadināni piṣṭvā paścāt kāñjikena kṣālyaḥ ।। 
auṣadhāni caikādaśāmūni ।। 
vajrakṣīram ।। 
arkakṣīram ।। 
palāśaparpaṭaḥ ।। 
citrakakvāthaḥ ।। 
vajrakandaḥ ।। 
kaṭutumbā ।। 
bīaṅkaḥ ।। 
triphalākvāthaḥ ।। 
punaścitrakakvāthaḥ ।। 
aśvagandhā ।। 
nāhi ।। 
aṅkudhya ।। 
atra citrakakvātho dvivelaṃ vidyate ato dvādaśabhir auṣadhair ekādaśaiva iti sūtaśodhanasaṃskāras tṛtīyaḥ ।। 
kvāthena trikaṭoḥ kṣuṇṇaṃ prākṛtaṃ śodhitaṃ rasam ।
rasenāsannadūdhilyās tathārdrāyā rasena ca ।।
kākamācīrasenaivaṃ devadālīrasena ca ।
śuṣkaṃ śuṣkaṃ punaḥ kṣuṇṇaṃ saṃmardyāmardya śoṣayet ।।
auṣadhasyauṣadhasyānte kṣālayet kāñjikena ca ।
pratyauṣadhaṃ dinānīha sapta saptaiva mardayet ।।
mūrchitotthāpanaṃ sūte kāryaṃ pañcabhir auṣadhaiḥ ।
tejasvī jāyate avaśyaṃ mūrchitotthitapāradaḥ ।।
