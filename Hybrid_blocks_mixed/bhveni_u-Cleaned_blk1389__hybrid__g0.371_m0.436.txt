kṛṣṇāyāḥ keśapāśaḥ kupitayamasakho dhūmaketuḥ kurūṇāṃ ē̃ so ayaṃ baddhaḥ prajānāṃ viramatu nidhanaṃ svasti rājñāṃ kulebhyaḥ ।।
