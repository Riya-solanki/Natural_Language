karṣaṃ vā kuḍachī vā ghṛtenābhyajya tatastatra gandhakaṃ kṣiptvādho agnir jvālanīyaḥ tāvad yāvad gandhakas tailopamāno bhavati tatas tadgandhakatailaṃ dugdhamadhye ḍhālyate tato dugdhaṃ na gṛhyate ।।
eṣa eva śuddhagandhakaḥ ।।
eva ca prakāradvayena yo gandhakaḥ śodhito bhavati ।।
sa rasakarmaṇi upayujyate nānya iti ।।
iti sugamam ।।
iti gandhakaśodhanaṃ dvitīyam ।।
iti sugamamagre gandhapīṭhā ।।
