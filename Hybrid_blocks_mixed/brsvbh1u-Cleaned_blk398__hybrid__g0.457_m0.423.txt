naca aātmā vā are draṣṭavyaḥ'ityādi kasyacitprakaraṇe samāmnātam ।
na cānārabhyādhītamapi ।
ayasya pūrṇamayī juhūrbhavati'ityavyabhicaritakratusaṃbandhajuhūdvāreṇa juhūpadaṃ kratuṃ smārayadvākyena yathā parṇatāyāḥ kratuśeṣabhāvamāpādayati, evamātmā nāvyabhicāritakratusaṃbandhaḥ, yena taddarśanaṃ kratvaṅgaṃ sadātmānaṃ kratvarthaṃ saṃskuryāt ।
tena yadyayaṃ vidhistathāpi
