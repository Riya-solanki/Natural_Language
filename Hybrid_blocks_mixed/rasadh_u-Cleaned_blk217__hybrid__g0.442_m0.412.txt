sthālikāmadhye dugdhaṃ kṣiptvā mukhe śaithilyadātharaṃ baddhvā tatra dāthare gandhakaṃ kṣiptvā kaṇṭhe mṛttikayā liptvopari agniṣṭaṃ kṣiptvā ghaṭīdvayaṃ yāvajjvālayet ।।
tato gandhako galitvā yo dugdhamadhye yāti sa śuddhagandhako jñeyaḥ ।।
iti gandhakaśodhanam prathamam ।।
atha dvitīyagandhakaśodhanam ।।
