na eṣa doṣaḥ ।
yata jyotiḥ śabdena āvedyate prakaraṇāt aya ātmāpahatapāpmā vijaro vimṛtyuḥ' iti hi prakṛte parasmin ātmani na akasmāt bhautikaṃ jyotiḥ śakyaṃ grahītum ।
prakṛtahāna aprakṛtaprakriyā prasaṅgāt ।
jyotiḥśabdaḥ tu ātmani api dṛśyate ataddevā jyotiṣaṃ jyotiḥ iti ।
prapañcitaṃ caitat' ityatra ।।
