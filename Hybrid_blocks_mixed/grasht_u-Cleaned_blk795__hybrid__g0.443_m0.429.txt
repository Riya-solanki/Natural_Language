punarviśeṣamāha vidhyedityādi ।।
kanakaṃ hema dattvā śanakair nīcais tāvadanuvāhayet yāvaddivyaṃ pravaraṃ kanakaṃ bhavediti śeṣaḥ ।।
punaryāvatsakalaṃ samastaṃ nāgaṃ pronmīlayenniḥśeṣaṃ kuryādityabhiprāyaḥ ।।
punastatkanakaṃ dviguṇaṃ svato dravyaṃ vidhyet vā sūtakena sāritaṃ sat dravyaṃ kanakaṃ śatasahasrādisaṃkhyāto dviguṇasaṃkhyākaṃ dravyaṃ śulbādikaṃ vidhyed iti rahasyam ।।
