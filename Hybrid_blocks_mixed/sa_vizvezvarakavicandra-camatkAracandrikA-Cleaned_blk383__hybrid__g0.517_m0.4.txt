yathā
ekenaiva turaṅgameṇa kakubho vyākramya saumyakramā- naṅgīkṛtya karān kadācidagataḥ kāṃcittamograstatām ।
ullāsāya satāmanantamudayaṃ dhatte tathāpi sphuṭaṃ tvaṃ prājñairnanu siṅgabhūpatilaka jñāto asi bhāsāṃ nidhiḥ ।।
