iti gaganādigrāsapramāṇaṃ kathitam atha cāraṇāvidhānamāha dolanavidhinetyādi ।।
gaganamabhrakaṃ yair auṣadhaiḥ piṣṭaṃ peṣitaṃ bhavati tair evauṣadhair nālpamānair bahumānair nānāvidhabhaṅgasaṃskṛtaṃ kuryāditi śeṣaḥ ।।
nānāvidhā anekaprakārā ye bhaṅgās taraṃgā āgamābdhijātās taiḥ saṃskṛtam upaskṛtam ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭaṃ gaganaṃ māraṇavidhinā pañcatvavidhānena uddiṣṭam uddeśitam ।
evaṃvidham api gaganaṃ dolanavidhinā cāraṇāyāṃ yojyamiti bhāvaḥ ।।
adhunālpamatīnāṃ matam āhānya ityādi ।
anye mahadbhyo apare tucchamatayas tucchā stokā matirbuddhiryeṣāṃ te tathoktāḥ alpabuddhaya iti yāvat ।।
rasaṃ jīrṇaṃ jāraṇasaṃskāropapannaṃ rasaṃ manyante iti ।।
kiṃ gandhakaniṣpiṣṭiśulbapiṣṭirajo gandhakena yā niṣpiṣṭiḥ piṣṭībhūtā śulbena yā piṣṭiḥ piṣṭībhūtā tāmrapiṣṭītyarthaḥ gandhakaniṣpiṣṭiśca śulbapiṣṭiśca tayoryadrajaḥ pāṃśuḥ gandhakapiṣṭī yathā ।
gandhapāṣāṇacūrṇaṃ ca caṇakasya rasena tu ।
bhāvayetsaptavāraṃ tu strīraktena ca saptadhā ।।
śuddhasūtaṃ palaikaṃ tu kharpare dāpayettataḥ ।
bhāvitaṃ gandhakaṃ dadyānnarapiṇḍena saṃyutam ।।
dolāyantre'pi tāpena piṣṭikā bhavati kṣaṇāt ।।
iti ।।
eva śulbapiṣṭyapi jāyate ।।
kimbhūtaṃ gandhakaniṣpiṣṭiśulbapiṣṭirajaḥ dolanavidhinodbhūtaṃ dolikāyantravidhānenotpannam ।।
iti yaduktaṃ tadasamañjasamiti bhāvaḥ ।।
