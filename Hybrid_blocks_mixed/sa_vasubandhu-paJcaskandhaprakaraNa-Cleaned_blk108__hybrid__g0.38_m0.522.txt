।। dhātuprapaścaḥ ।।
cakṣurādayo dhātavo rūpādayo dhātavaśca yathāyatanāni ।।ṣaḍvijñānadhātavaścakṣurāddyāśrayā rūpādyālambanāvijñaptayaḥ ।।manodhātusteṣāmeva samanantaranirudhyaṃ ṣaḍvijñānadeśanāśrayatāmupādāya ।।
।। dhātuvyavasthanam ।।
