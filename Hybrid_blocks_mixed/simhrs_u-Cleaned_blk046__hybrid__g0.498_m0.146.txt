yathā-
vāladhiṃ trātum āvṛtya camareṇārpite gale ।
patantam iṣum anyena sa kṛpālur akhaṇḍayat ।। 
Some MSS have the following example sa dakṣiṇaṃ tūṇa-mukhena vāmaṃ । vyāpārayan hastam alakṣyatājau । ākarṇa-kṛṣṭā sakṛd asya yoddhum । aurvīva bāṇān suṣuve ripu-ghnān ।।
