vyastaṃ yathā
śrīsiṅgakṣitināyakasya yaśasāṃ candreṇa sāndrā ruciḥ yatprāptā tapanaṃ pratāmapabhito loke tadatyadbhutam ।
evaṃ nāticamatkṛtaṃ tadanayoratyantamāśliṣyator āpatyātithayo bhavanti yadamī pratyarthipṛthvībhūjaḥ ।।
