kadācit kuṇḍasyāmbhasi viharate karhy api bahiḥ
kadāpy antaḥ-kuñje kvacana-samaye rāsa-valaye ।
samaṃ rādhā-devyā harir akhila-sakhyāli-sukhadaḥ
purā tat tan nityaṃ smarayati mano naḥ svam abhitaḥ ।।
