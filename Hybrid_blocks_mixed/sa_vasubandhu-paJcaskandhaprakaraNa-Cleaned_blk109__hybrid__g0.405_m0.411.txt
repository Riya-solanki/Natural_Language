ebamaṣṭādaśadhātuvyavasthānam । yo rūpaskandhastāni daśāyatanadhātavaḥ ।।
dharmāyatanaṃ dharmadhātvekadeśaḥ ।।
yo vijñānaskandhastanmana āyatanam, saptama manodhātavaśca ।। anye ye trayaḥ skandhā rūpadhātvekadeśaśca, te asaṃskṛtai sahitā dharmāyatanaṃ dharmadhātuśca ।।
