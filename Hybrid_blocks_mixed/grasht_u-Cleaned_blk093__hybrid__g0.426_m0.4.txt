divyatanorhetutvād rasendrasya sādhanavidhau sādhanopadeśe sudhiyā pūjyamatinā puṃsā prathamam aṣṭādaśasaṃskārāḥ prayatnena jñātavyāḥ ।।
saṃskriyanta iti saṃskārāḥ ।।
puṃsyapi garbhādhānādayaḥ ṣoḍaśa saṃskārā vartante ata eva saṃskārair ubhayoḥ sāmyaṃ doṣābhāvatvaṃ guṇavattvaṃ ca syāt ।।
ato brāhmaṇakṣatriyavaiśyaśūdrāḥ syuḥ ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭāḥ saṃskārāḥ pratikarmanirmalāḥ karma karma prati nirdoṣāḥ ।।
