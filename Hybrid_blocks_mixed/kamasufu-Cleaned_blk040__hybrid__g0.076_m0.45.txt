kalaha-rūpaṃ suratam ācakṣate vivādātmakatvād vāma-śīlatvāc ca kāmasya
tasmāt prahaṇanan-sthānam aṅgam skandhau śiraḥ stanāntaraṃ pṛṣṭhaṃ jaghanaṃ pārśva iti sthānāni
tac catur-vidham ṇ [] apahastakaṃ [] prasṛtakaṃ [] muṣṭiḥ [] samatalakam iti
tad-udbhāvaṃ ca sītkṛtam tasyātirūpatvāt tad aneka-vidham
virutāni cāṣṭau
[ā]hiṃkāra-[a]stanita-[a]kūjita-[a]rudita-[a]sūtkṛta-[a]dūtkṛta-[a]phūtkṛtāni
ambārthāḥ śabdā vāraṇārthā mokṣaṇārthāś cālam-arthās te te [a] cātha-yogāt
[b]pārāvata-[b]parabhṛta-[b]hārīta-[b]śuka-[b]madhukara-[b]dātyūha-[b]haṃsa-[ b]kāraṇḍava-[b]lāvaka-virutāni sīt-kṛta-bhūyiṣṭhāni vikalpaśaḥ prayuñjīta
utsaṅgopaviṣṭāyāḥ pṛṣṭhe muṣṭinā prahāraḥ
tatra sāsūyāyā iva [a]stanita-[a]rudita-[a]kūjitāni pratighātaś ca syāt
yukta-yantrāyāḥ stanāntare [] apahastakena praharet
mandopakramaṃ vardhamāna-rāgam ā parisamāpteḥ
tatra [a]hiṃkārādīnām aniyamenābhyāsena vikalpena ca tat-kālam eva prayogaḥ
śirasi kiṃ cid ākuñcitāgulinā kareṇa vivadantyāḥ [a]phūtkṛtya prahaṇanaṃ tat [] prasṛtakam
tatrāntarmukhena [a]kūjitaṃ [a]phūtkṛtaṃ ca
ratānte ca śvāsita-rudite
pheṇor iva sphuṭataḥ śabdānukaraṇaṃ [a]dūtkṛtam
apsu badarasyeva nipatataḥ [a]phūtkṛtam
sarvatra cumbanādiṣv apakrāntāyāḥ sasītkṛtaṃ tenaiva pratyuttaram
rāga-vaśāt prahaṇanābhyāse [a]vāraṇa-mokṣaṇālam arthānāṃ śabdānāṃ ambārthānāṃ ca satānta-śvāsita-rudita-stanita-miśrī-kṛta-prayogā virutānāṃ ca rāgāvasāna-kāle jaghana-parśvayos tāḍanam ity atvarayā cā parisamāpteḥ
tatra [b]lāvaka-[b]haṃsa-vikūjitaṃ tvarayaiva iti stanana-prahaṇana-yogāḥ bhavataś cātra ślokau
pāruṣyaṃ rabhasatvaṃ ca pauruṣaṃ teja ucyate aśaktir ārtir vyāvṛttir abalatvaṃ ca yoṣitaḥ
rāgāt prayoga-sātmyāc ca vyatyayo api kva cid bhavet na ciraṃ tasya caivānte prakṛter eva yojanam
kīlām usari kartarīṃ śirasi viddhāṃ kapolayoḥ saṃdaṃśikāṃ stanayoḥ pārśvayoś ceti pūrvaiḥ saha prahaṇanam aṣṭa-vidham iti dākṣiṇātyānām tad-yuvatīnām usari kīlāni dṛśyante deśa-sātmyam etat
kaṣṭam anārya-vṛttam anādṛtam iti vātsyāyanaḥ
tathānyad api deśa-sātmyāt prayuktam anyatra na prayuñjīta
ātyayikaṃ tu tatrāpi pariharet
rati-yoge hi kīlayā gaṇikāṃ citrasenāṃ colarājo jaghāna
kartaryā kuṇṭalaḥ śātakarṇiḥ śātavāhano mahā-devīṃ malayavatīm
naradevaḥ kupāṇir viddhayā nāṭīṃ kāṇāṃ cakāra 
bhavanti cātra ślokāḥ
nāsty atra kā cin na ca śāstra-parigrahaḥ pravṛtte rati-saṃyoge rāga evātra kāraṇam
svapneṣu na dṛśyante te bhāvās te ca vibhramāḥ surata-vyavahāreṣu ye syus tat-kṣaṇa-kalpitāḥ
yathā hi pañcamīṃ dhārām āsthāya turagaḥ pathi sthānuṃ śvabhraṃ darīṃ vāpi vegāndho na samīkṣate
evaṃ surata-saṃmarde rāgāndhau kāmināv api caṇḍa-vegau pravartete samīkṣeta na cātyayam
tasmān mṛdutvaṃ caṇḍatvaṃ yuvatyā balam eva ca ātmanaś ca balaṃ jñātvā tathā yuñjīta śāstravit
na saravadā na sarvāsu prayogāḥ sāṃprayogikāḥ sthāne deśe ca kāle ca yoga eṣāṃ vidhīyate
