uttarīyaiḥ kaṇṭhaveṣṭanaṃ kaṇṭhaprāvṛtiḥ। niṣṭhīvo mukhapānīyanirasanam। kāsahāsau prasiddhau। ādigrahaṇād aślīlabhāṣaṇakrīḍādi guroḥ sannidhau na kuryāt। [kaiścid no vādaṃ kuryāt] na ca kenacitsaha vivadet। na ca sannihite gurau [utkṛṣṭaveṣavā] utkṛṣṭaveṣaḥ syāt। udvaranādikaṃ na kuryādityabhisambadhyate। tatra udvartanaṃ prasiddham। aṅgasaṃskāraḥ snānodvapanasamālambhanādi। jṛmbhaṇaṃ vidāritāsyagātravinamanam। āsanasaṃsthitiḥ āsane avasthānam। samānālāpaḥ sāmyenāpihitavaktrasyānamrasya saṃkathanam। etacca paryaṅgabandhadhyānādi ca na kuryāt। ādigrahaṇād anyasyāpyavinayasya lokaprasiddhasya niṣedhaḥ।। [gurau vartanam]
