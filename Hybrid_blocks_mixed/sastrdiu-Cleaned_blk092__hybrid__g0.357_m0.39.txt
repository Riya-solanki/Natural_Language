nanu na hiṃsyāditi niṣedho nārabhyādhītatvātpuruṣārthaḥ tenānena puruṣārtho eva hiṃsāḥ pratiṣeddhavyāḥ, kratvarthaniṣedhe hi so api kratvarthaḥ syāt ।
aviśeṣe tvekasya pratiṣedhavidheḥ kratvarthatvapuruṣārthatvāpattyā vairupyaṃ syāt ।
tasmātpuruṣārthānāmeva hiṃsānāmiha pratiṣedho na kratvarthānāmiti ।।
