ṛto bhāradvājasya ।।
tāsau nityāniṭa ṛdantādeva thalo neṭ bhāradvājasya mate । tena anyasya syādeva । ayamatra saṃgrahaḥ ajanto akāravānvā yastāsyaniṭthali veḍayam ।
ṛdanta īdṛṅnityāniṭ krādyanyo liṭi seḍbhavet ।।
cikṣayitha, cikṣetha । cikṣiya । cikṣiyiva । kṣetā । kṣayatu । kṣayet ।।
