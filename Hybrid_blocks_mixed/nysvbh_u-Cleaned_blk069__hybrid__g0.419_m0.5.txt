pūrvavaditiyatra kāraṇena kāryamanumīyate, yathā medhonnatyā bhaviṣyati vṛṣṭiriti।
śeṣavattatyatra kāryeṇa kāraṇamanumīyate, pūrvodakaviparītamudakaṃ nādyāḥ pūrṇatvaṃ śīghratvaṃ dṛṣṭhvā strotaso anumīyate bhūtā vṛṣṭiriti।
sāmānyatodṛṣṭaṃvrajyāpūrvakamanyatra dṛṣṭasyānyatra darśanamiti, tathā cādityasya, tasmāt astyapratyakṣāpyādityasya vrajyeti।।
