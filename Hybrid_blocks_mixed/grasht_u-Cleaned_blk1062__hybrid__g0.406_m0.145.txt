tadviśeṣamāha ya ityādi ।।
yaḥ punar mūḍho mūrkho ajīrṇānantaraṃ atyamlalavaṇakaṭukāhāraṃ satataṃ nirantaraṃ karoti tasyāgniḥ koṣṭhāgnir vinaśyati rasaśca na krāmati svaguṇānna prakāśayati ।।
tarhi kiṃ bhavedityāha vyādhirbhaved ityarthaḥ ।।
