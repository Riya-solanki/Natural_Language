sāmānyokticchāyā yathā
ko ayaṃ vāñchitamarthināṃ vitanute nanveṣa cintāmaṇiḥ sagrāvā kila kintu naiva hi pumān hāsena kiṃ bhūyasā ।
so ayaṃ śrī anavotasiṅganṛpatiḥ kasmādidaṃ jñāyate ratnollāsini kaṅbtṇe karatale kiṃ darpaṇāpekṣayā ।।
