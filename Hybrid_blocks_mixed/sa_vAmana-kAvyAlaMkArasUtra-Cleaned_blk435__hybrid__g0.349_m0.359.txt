ityādiṣu । cakāro'rdhāntavarjamityasyānukarpaṇārthaḥ ।।
na gādye samāptaprāyaṃ vṛttam, anyatrodgatādibhyaḥ saṃvādāt ।।
gadye samāptaprāyaṃ vṛttaṃ na vidheyam । śobhābhrāṃśat । anyatrodgatādibhyo viṣamavṛttebhyaḥ saṃvādād gadyeneti ।।
na pādādau khalvādayaḥ ।।
pādādau khalvādayaḥ śabdā na prayojyāḥ । ādiśabdaḥ prakārārthaḥ । yeṣāmādau prayogo na śloṣyati te gṛhyante । na punarvatahetaprabhṛtayaḥ ।।
nārdhe kiñcitsamāptaṃ vākyam ।।
vṛttasyārthe kiñcidasamāptaprāyaṃ na prayoktavyam । yathā
