।।
lokamātraprayuktaṃ grāmyam ।।
loka eva yasprayuktaṃ padaṃ, na śāstre; tadgrāmyam । yathā kaṣṭaṃ kathaṃ roditi phūtkṛteyam । anyadapi tallagallādikaṃ draṣṭavyam ।।
śāstramātraprayuktamapratītam ।।
śāstra eva prayuktaṃ yat, na loke tadapratītaṃ padam । yathā
