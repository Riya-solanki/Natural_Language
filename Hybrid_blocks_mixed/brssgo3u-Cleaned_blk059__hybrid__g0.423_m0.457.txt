atāpi syāt ।
yā śrutiranuśayasadbhāvapratipādanāyodāhṛtā- atadya iha ramaṇīyacaraṇāḥ' iti ।
sā khalu caraṇādyonyāpattiṃ darśayati nānuśayāt ।
anyaccaraṇamanyo anuśayaḥ ।
caraṇaṃ cāritramācāraḥ śīlamityanarthāntaram ।
anuśayastu bhuktaphalātkarmaṇo atiriktaṃ karmābhipretam ।
śrutiśca karmācaraṇe bhedena vyapadiśati- ayathākārī yathācārī tathā bhavati' iti, ayānyanavadyāni karmāṇi tāni sevitavyāni no itarāṇi, yānyasmākaṃ sucaritāni tāni tvayopasyāni' iti ca ।
tasmāccaraṇādyonyāpattiśruternānuśayasiddhiriticet ।
naiṣa doṣaḥ ।
yato anuśayopalakṣaṇārthaivaiṣā caraṇaśrutiriti kārṣṇājinirācāryo manyate ।।
