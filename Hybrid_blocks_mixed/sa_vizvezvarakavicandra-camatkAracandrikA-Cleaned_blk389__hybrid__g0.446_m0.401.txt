atra dikkūlaṃkaṣeti priyaṃbhāvuketi gāmadhyāsitumityādau subantānāmīśata ityādiṣu tiṅntānāṃ ca digvyāpino antarikṣavyāpino gavi tiṣṭhantyādibhi rviśeṣapratibhānācchabdasaṃskāra ityucyate ।
harṣādi bhāvavaśato vāgvṛttirbhāvikaṃ matam ।।
yathā
vidyādaivata tāta tāvakaguro sarvajñacūḍāmaṇe stotavyo asi manīṣiṇāṃ tvamathavā vandyo javākairalam ।
itthaṃ siṅgamahīpatiṃ gurujano apyābhāṣate toṣavān tattatpaddhatidharmamarmaṇi paricchedāni saṃvādinam ।।
