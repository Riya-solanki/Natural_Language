udāharaṇam tanuvapurajaghanyo asau karikuñjararudhiraraktakharanakharaḥ ।
tejodhāma mahaḥpṛthumanasāmindro harirjiṣṇuḥ ।।
atraiksamin pade parivartiṃte nālaṃkāra iti śabdāśrayaḥ, aparasmistu parivartite api sa na hīyate, ityarthaniṣṭha ityubhayālaṃkāro ayam ।।
