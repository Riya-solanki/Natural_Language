arthavyaktihetutvamarthavyaktiḥ ।।
yatra jhaṭityarthapratipattihetutvaṃ sa guṇo'rhavyaktiriti pūrvoktamudāharaṇam । pratyudāharaṇaṃ tu bhūyaḥ sulabhaṃ ca ।।
aujjvalyaṃ kāntiḥ ।।
bandhasyojjvalatvaṃ nāma yadasau kāntiriti । yadabhāve purāṇacchāyetyucyate । yathā
