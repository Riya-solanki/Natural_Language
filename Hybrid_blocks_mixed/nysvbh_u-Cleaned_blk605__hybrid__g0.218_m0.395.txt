yā samānāṃ buddhiṃ prasūte bhinneṣvadhikaraṇeṣu
yayā bahūni itaretarato na vyāvatente
yo artho anekatra pratyayānuvṛttinimittaṃ tat sāmānyam, yacca keṣāṃ cidabhedaṃ keṣāñcid bhedaṃ karoti tat sāmānyaviśeṣo jātiriti
।।
