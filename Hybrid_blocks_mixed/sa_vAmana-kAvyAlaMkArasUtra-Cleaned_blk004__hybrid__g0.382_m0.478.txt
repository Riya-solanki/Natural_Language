praṇamya paramaṃ jyotirvāmanena kavipriyā ।
kāvyālaṅkārasūtrāṇāṃ teṣāṃ vṛttirvidhīyate ।।
kāvyaṃ grāhyamalaṅkārāt ।।
kāvyaṃ khalu grāhyamupādeyaṃ bhavati, alaṅkārāt । kāvyaśabdo'yaṃ guṇālaṅkārasaṃskṛtayoḥ śabdārthayorvartate । bhaktyā tu śabdārthamātravacano'tra gṛhyate ।।
