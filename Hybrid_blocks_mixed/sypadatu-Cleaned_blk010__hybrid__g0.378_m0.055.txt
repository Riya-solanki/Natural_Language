[] gam evārtarūpam ātmakarmācakṣamāṇam asāv ahaṃ bhoḥ evaṃkarmā taṃ mā vṛṣalyāḥ pādāvadhūtaśiraskaṃ trātum arhanti traividyavṛddhā ity uktavantam apaśyam evaṃ coktā brāhmaṇāś calakapolasūcitahāsam anyonyam avalokya muhūrtam iva dhyātvoktavantaḥ bhoḥ sādho, avalokitāny asmābhir manuyamavasiṣṭhagautamabharadvājaśaṅkhalikhitāpastambahārītapraceto devalavṛddhagārgyaprabhṛtīnāṃ maharṣīṇāṃ dharmaśāstrāṇi nāsyaivaṃvidhasya mahataḥ pātakasya prāyaścittam avagacchāma iti evaṃ cokto viṣaṇṇataravaktra ucchritya hastāv upākrośat bho bhoḥ, caturtho varṇa iti na mām arhatha bhūmidevāḥ parityaktum kutaḥ
