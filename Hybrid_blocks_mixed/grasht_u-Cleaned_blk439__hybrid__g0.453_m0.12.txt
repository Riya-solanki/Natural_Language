sve sve vikāre vakṣyamāṇamāha balamityādi ।।
dhātuvido rasavaidyā iti śaṃsanti ।।
iti kiṃ kuṭile balaṃ abhyadhikaṃ sarvādhikaṃ punastīkṣṇe'bhyadhiko rāgaḥ rañjanaṃ tu punaḥ pannage nāge'bhyadhikaṃ snehaḥ snigdhatvaṃ tu punaḥ rāgasnehabalāni trīṇyevoktāni kamale tāmre kuṭilatīkṣṇapannagānāṃ jāraṇādrase yathā balarāgasnehā bhavanti tathaikatāmrajāraṇāt trayo bhavantītyarthaḥ ।।
rasabandhanopāyamāha sarvairityādi ।।
biḍayogena pūrvoktena jīrṇo jāraṇamāpanno rasarājo bandhamupayāti bandhanamādatte ।।
kaiḥ saha jīrṇaḥ ebhiḥ pūrvoktaiḥ sarvair lohairdhātubhiḥ ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭaiḥ mākṣikanihataiḥ svarṇamākṣikamāritaiḥ ।।
punaḥ kiṃviśiṣṭaiḥ garbhe rasodare drutairvidrutairiti ।।
