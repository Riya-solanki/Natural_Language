yavāgūpākavadārthaḥ kramaḥ,śrautastu bhaviṣyati, agṛhī bhūtvā vanī bhavet vanī bhūtvā pravrajet'iti jābālaśrutirgārhasthyena hi yajñādyanuṣṭhānaṃ sūcayati ।
smaranti ca-'adhītya vidhivadvedānputrāṃścotpādya dharmataḥ ।
iṣṭā ca śaktito yajñairmano mokṣe niveśayet ।।
anindanti ca-'anadhītya dvijo vedānanutpādya tathātmajān ।
aniṣṭvā caiva yajñaiśca mokṣamicchanvrajatyadhaḥ' ।।
ityata āha-yathā ca hṛdayādyavadānānāmānantaryaniyamaḥ ।
kutaḥ, ahṛdayasyāgre avadyati atha jihvāyā atha vakṣasaḥ'ityathāgraśabdābhyāṃ kramasya vivakṣitatvāt ।
na tatheha krama niyamo vivakṣitaḥ, śrutyā tayaivāniyamapradarśanāt, ayadi vetarathā brahmacaryādeva pravrajedgṛhādvā vanādvā'iti ।
etāvatā hi vairāgyamupalakṣayati ।
ata eva ayadahareva virajettadahareva pravrajet'iti śrutiḥ ।
nindāvacanaṃ ca aviśuddhasattvapuruṣābhiprāyam ।
aviśuddhasattvo hi mokṣamicchannālasyāttadupāye apravartamāno gṛhasthadharmamapi nityanaimittikamanācaranpratikṣaṇamupacīyamānapāpmādho gacchatītyarthaḥ ।
syādetat ।
