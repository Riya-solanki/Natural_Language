sarvopāyena śarīraṃ sthiraṃ kāryam ityāha nāmetyādi ।।
dehasiddheḥ śarīravibhūteḥ nāmāpyabhidhānamapi ko gṛhṇīyānna ko apītyarthaḥ ।।
kena vinā śarīreṇa śarīramantareṇa siddhirastu paraṃ tannāma kenāpi na gṛhyate śarīranāmagrahaṇamiti tātparyārthaḥ ।।
anaśvaraṃ śarīraṃ bhavatu cet tad amalaṃ nirañjanaṃ tattvaṃ brahmāvaśyaṃ prāpyate ।।
tattattvaṃ manaso'pi na gocaraṃ cittenāpi na gamyam ityarthaḥ ।।
tarhi kena gamyam ubhayor melanam ekīkaraṇaṃ yogastenaiva prakṛtipuruṣayor ekīkaraṇenetyarthaḥ ।।
