syādetat ।
aviṣayatve hi cidātmano nādhyāsaḥ, viṣaya eva tu cidātmā asmatpratyayasya, tatkathaṃ nādhyāsa ityata āha- yuṣmatpratyayāpetasya iti ।
viṣayatve hi cidātmano anyo viṣayī bhavet ।
tathā ca yo viṣayī sa eva cidātmā ।
viṣayastu tato anyo yuṣmatpratyayagocaro abhyupeyaḥ ।
tasmādanātmatvaprasaṅgādanavasthāparihārāya yuṣmatpratyayāpetatvam; ata eva aviṣayatvamātmano vaktavyam; tathā ca nādhyāsa ityarthaḥ ।
