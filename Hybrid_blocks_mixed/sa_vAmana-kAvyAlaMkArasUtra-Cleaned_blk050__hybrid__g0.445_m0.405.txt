padadoṣāndarśayitumāha 
duṣṭaṃ padamasādhu kaṣṭaṃ grāmyamapratītamanarthakaṃ ca ।।
krameṇa vyākhyātumāha 
śabdasmṛtiviruddhamasādhu ।।
śabdasmṛtyā vyākaraṇena viruddhaṃ padamasādhu । yathā anyakārakavaiyarthyam iti । atra hi aṣaṣṭhyatṛtīyāsthasyānyasya dugāśīrāśāsthāsthitotsukotikārakarāgaccheṣu iti dukā bhavitavyamiti ।।
śrutivirasaṃ kaṣṭam ।।
śrutivirasaṃ śrotrakaṭu padaṃ kaṣṭam । taddhi racanāgumphitamapyudvejayati । yathā acūcuraccaṇḍi ! kapolaupste kāntidravaṃ drāgviśadaḥ śaśāṅkaḥ ।
