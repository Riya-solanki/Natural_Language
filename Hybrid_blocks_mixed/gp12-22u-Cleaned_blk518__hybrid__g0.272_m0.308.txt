avrīḍa-krīḍatāyāṃ jayavad itarayor vāhayanto vahantas
tasmin vāhānukartṝṃs turaga-tulanayā hāsayanto hasantaḥ ।
maryādā-nirṇaye tu prathita-kali-kulaṃ vādayanto vadantaḥ
prāpur bhāṇḍīram ete vanavana-bhuvi gāś cālayantaś calantaḥ ।।[sragdharā]
[] tad evaṃ kutūhale tu prabale balaṃ pralamba-skandhārūḍhaṃ sandhātuṃ jita-bhujaga-bhujaḥ śrī-balānujaḥ parābhavam agaṇayan durbalam adurbalam avicitya svīkṛtaṃ svagaṇam eva parābhāvayāmāsa ।
