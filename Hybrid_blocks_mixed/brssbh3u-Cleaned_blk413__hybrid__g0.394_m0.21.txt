yadi ca dehādapasṛptasya devayānena pathā prasthitasyārdhapathe sukṛtaduṣkṛtakṣayo abhyupagamyeta tataḥ patite dehe yamaniyamavidyābhyāsātmakasya sukṛtaduṣkṛtakṣayahetoḥ puruṣaprayatnasyecchāto anuṣṭhānānupapatteranupapattireva taddhetukasya sukṛtaduṣkṛtakṣayasya syāt ।
tasmātpūrvameva sādhakāvasthāyāṃ chandato anuṣṭhānaṃ tasyasyāt ।
tatpūrvakaṃ ca sukṛtaduṣkṛtahānamiti draṣṭavyam ।
evaṃ nimittanaimittikayorupapattistāṇḍiśāṭyāyaniśrutyośca saṃgatiriti ।।
