yudapalabhyate tadasti yannopalabhyate tannāstīti anupalambhātmakam asaditi vyavasthitam, upalabdhyabhāvaścānupalabdhiriti seyamabhāvatvānnopalabhyate sacaca khalvāvaraṇaṃ tasyopalabdhyā bhavitavyaṃ na nopalabhyate tasmānnāstīti।
tatra yaduktam "nāvaraṇānupapattiranupalambhāt" ity ayuktamiti ।।
