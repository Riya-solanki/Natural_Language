daharaḥ parameśvara uttarebhyo hetubhya ityuktam ।
ta evottare hetava idānīṃ prapañcyante ।
itaśca parameśvara eva daharaḥ, yasmāddaharavākyaśeṣe parameśvarasyaiva pratipādakau gatiśabdau bhavataḥ- aimāḥ sarvāḥ prajā aharahargacchantya etaṃ brahmalokaṃ na vindanti' iti ।
tatra prakṛtaṃ daharaṃ brahmalokaśabdenābhidhāya tadviṣayā gatiḥ prajāśabdavācyānāṃ jīvānāmabhidhīyamānā daharasya brahmatāṃ gamayati ।
tathā hyaharaharjīvānāṃ suṣuptavasthāyāṃ brahmaviṣayaṃ gamanaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ śrutyantare- asatā somya tadā saṃpanno bhavati' ityevamādau ।
loke api kila gāḍhaṃ suṣuptamācakṣate brahmībhūto brahmatāṃ gata iti ।
tathā brahmalokaśabdo api prakṛte dahare prayujyamāno jīvabhūtākāśaśaṅkāṃ nivartayanbrahmatāmasya gamayati ।
