Viv avyaktā bhavābhavasthitiprādhānyād uṭṭaṅkatvenāprakaṭitā mūrtir yasya tasmai śivāya mahatyāveśe apy aparibhraṃśaśīlāya । nama iti vyākhyātam । śiśutayādyā bhedasvabhāvena sthitā yeyaṃ niśākāntasya kālarātrīramaṇasya parabhairavasya kalanāt kalā saivāghoraghorāghorātighorāvaraṇatrayopetavargāṣṭakadalalālitā, saiva kamalaṃ tanmāline tadādhārāya tadādheyāya vā । niśākāntakalā saṃhāradevī, tathā cāsmatputraḥ
