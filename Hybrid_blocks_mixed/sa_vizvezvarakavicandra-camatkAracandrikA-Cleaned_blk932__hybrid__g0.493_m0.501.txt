ekaviṣayā yathā
śākāhāraparigraho giridarīṣvekāntacintā ratiḥ śayyā sthaṇḍila eva bhūtidhavalā mūrtirjaṭālaṃ śiraḥ ।
aṅgānyasthimayāni hanta sutarāṃ kiṃ vā tapasvī bhavā- nuddaṇḍapratigaṇḍabhairavavibhoḥ kiṃ vā virodhī nṛpaḥ ।।
