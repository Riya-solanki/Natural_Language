madhuni mādhava eva madhuvrataḥ prakurute mudito madhuraṃ ravam ।
vararavo'pi satāṃ ca samāgamaṃ śabalatā kimupaiti na cārutām ।।
saṃkarabījānāṃ madhye mahābījasaṃjñānyāha mākṣiketyādi ।।
etattrikaṃ mahābījaṃ kimetat mākṣīkakāntatīkṣṇaṃ mākṣīkakāntaśulbaṃ tīkṣṇābhrakaṃ ca mākṣīkaṃ tāpyaṃ kāntaṃ cumbakaṃ śulbaṃ tāmraṃ tīkṣṇaṃ lohajātir abhrakaṃ gaganaṃ etattritayaṃ bījaṃ mahābījaṃ etattritrimukhaṃ pratyekaṃ mahāsaṃjñam ।।
viśeṣo'tra ।
bījapākaṃ pravakṣyāmi jāraṇārthaṃ rasasya tu ।
sūtrakramo'yaṃ bījena samajīrṇena śudhyati ।
iti ।।
avāntaratvena ca pratyekaṃ dravyaṃ bījasaṃjñābhimatam ।।
