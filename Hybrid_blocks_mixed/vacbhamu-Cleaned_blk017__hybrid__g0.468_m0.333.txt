syādetat ।
mā bhūddharmiṇoḥ parasparabhāvaḥ; taddharmāṇāṃ tu jāḍyacaitanyanityatvānityatvādīnāṃ itaretarādhyāso bhaviṣyati ।
dṛśyate hi dharmiṇorvivekagrahaṇe api taddharmāṇāmadhyāsaḥ, yathā kusumādbhedena gṛhyamāṇe api sphaṭikamaṇāvatisvacchatayā japākusumapratibimbodgrāhiṇi aaruṇaḥ sphaṭikaḥ' ityāruṇyavibhrama ityata idamuktam - taddharmāṇāmapi iti ।
itaretaratra dharmiṇi dharmāṇāṃ bhāvo vinimayaḥ, tasyānupapattiḥ ।
ayamabhisaṃdhiḥ - rūpavaddhi dravyamatisvacchatayā rūpavato dravyāntarasya tadvivekena gṛhyamāṇasyāpi cchāyāṃ gṛhṇīyāt, cidātmā tu nīrūpo viṣayī na viṣayacchāyāmudgrāhayitumarhati ।
