taccāha sasyakamityādi ।।
sasyakamapi capalamapi raktagaṇairdāḍimakiṃśukabandhūkādibhiḥ subhāvitaṃ kuryāt ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭaṃ sneharāgasaṃsiktaṃ snehaḥ kaṅguṇitumbunyādīnāṃ rāgo raktavarṇadravaḥ tābhyāṃ vahnau taptaṃ sasyakaṃ saṃsiktaṃ secitamiti ghṛtaiḥ saṃsiktaṃ komalaṃ bhāvanāyogyaṃ syāt ।।
katibhirvāraiḥ subhāvitaṃ kuryāt saptabhiḥ saptasaṃkhyākaiḥ ।।
ataḥ paraṃ kārye bījādike yujyate ।।
