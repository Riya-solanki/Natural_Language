atra pādacatuṣṭaye api nātivarṇamṛduprāyatā nātirūkṣākṣaraprāyatā ceti miśrabandhasamatā ।
agranthilā pṛdhagbhūtiḥ patānāṃ mādhurī matā ।।
yathā
cammaṃ pommotta uttaṃ ।
bommo kāpūṇa sommaṃ tvihuvaṇa raaṇaṃ kaṇṇaaṃ kitti saṇṇaṃ dvihā uṃtie ṇāhaṃ kuṇa iva rauṇaṃ maṃgalaṃ siṃgabhūvam ।।
