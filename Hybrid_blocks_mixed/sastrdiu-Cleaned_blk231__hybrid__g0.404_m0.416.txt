nanu vrīhikāryamapūrvaṃ nīvāraḥ sādhayanti ।
naivam ।
vrīhyaṃśā eva tatra kāryaṃ kurvanti, teca vikalāḥ santo apyaśaktyavasthāyāṃ tatsādhanatvena yathāśaktiprayogavidhānādavagatā iti nākāraṇāttatsadṛśātkāryotpattiḥ ।।
