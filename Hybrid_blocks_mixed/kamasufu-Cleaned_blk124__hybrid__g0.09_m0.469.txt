kṛta-paricayāṃ darśiteṅitākārāṃ kanyām ivopāyato abhiyuñjīteti prāyeṇa tatra sūkṣmā abhiyogāḥ kanyānām asaṃprayuktatvāt itarāsu tān eva sphuṭam upadadhyāt saṃprayuktatvāt
saṃdarśitākārāyāṃ nirbhinna-sad-bhāvāyāṃ samupabhoga-vyatikare tadīyāny upayuñjīta
tatra mahārha-gandham uttarīyaṃ kusumaṃ syād aṅgulīyakaṃ ca tad-dhastād gṛhīta-tāmbūlayā goṣṭhī-gamanodyatasya keśa-hasta-puṣpa-yācanam
tatra mahārha-gandhaṃ spṛhaṇīyaṃ sva-nakha-daśana-pada-cihnitaṃ sākāraṃ dadyāt
adhikair adhikaiś cābhiyogaiḥ sādhvasa-vicchedanam
krameṇa ca vivikta-deśe gamanam āliṅganaṃ cumbanaṃ tāmbūlasya grāhaṇaṃ dānānte dravyāṇāṃ parivartanaṃ guhya-deśābhimarśanaṃ cety abhiyogāḥ
yatra caikābhiyuktā na tatrāparām abhiyuñjīta
tatra yā vṛddhānubhūta-viṣayā priyopagrahaiś ca tām upagṛhṇīyāt
ślokāv atra bhavataḥ
anyatra dṛṣṭa-saṃcāras tad-bhartā yatra nāyakaḥ na tatra yoṣitaṃ kāṃ cit suprāpām api laṅghayet
śaṅkitāṃ rakṣitāṃ bhītāṃ saśvaśrūkāṃ ca yoṣitam na tarkayeta medhāvī jānan pratyayam ātmanaḥ
