[antaḥ] vyākhyānakālamadhye [śavaśrutau] śavapaṭaharodanādiśrutau [dāhe] gṛhadāhādiśravaṇe [śavagandhe] śavanikaṭadhūmāghrāṇe [kharānile] paruṣatare vāyau ca vahati tadvirāmaṃ vinā vyākhyānaṃ na kuryāt।
