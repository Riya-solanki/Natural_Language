antarā bhūtagrāmavatsvātmanaḥ ।
ghaṭādikaṃ cidviṣayatvenāparokṣaṃ, brahma tu sākṣādaviṣayatvenāparokṣamiti ।
prathamārthe pañcami ।
atra śrutāvātmadharmo aparokṣatvaṃ brahmaṇayuktaṃ, brahmadharmaḥ sarvāntaratvamātmanyuktaṃ, tena tayoraikyaṃ dṛḍhīkṛtaṃ mantavyaṃ, tanme vyācakṣvetyuṣastapraśne yājñavalkyena prāṇādiprerako dṛṣṭyādisākṣī pratipāditaḥ ।
tathaiva ayadeva sākṣādaparokṣādbrahma ya ātmā sarvāntarastanme vyācakṣva'iti kaholapraśne aśanāyādyatītaḥ pratipāditaḥ ।
tatra brāhmaṇadvaye api praśnādabhyāsātsarvāntaratvapratyabhijñānācca saṃśaye mantrayorvedyaikyādastu vidyaikyaṃ, iha tu brāhmaṇyorvedyaikye api abhyāsādvidyābhedaḥ, yajatyabhyāsātprayājabhedavaditi pratyudāharaṇena pūrvapakṣaḥ, tatra mitho dharmānupasaṃhāraḥ phalaṃ, siddhānte tūpasaṃhāra iti vivekaḥ ।
dvayoḥ sarvāntaratvānupapattyā tāvadbrahmāṇayorekavastuparatvaṃ siddham ।
tathāca vedyaikyānnirguṇavidyaikye na vivādaḥ ।।
