premā vaśayati sarvaṃ khalv iti na mṛṣā prasiddhir udbhāti ।
kṛṣṇa tvam api sa yasmān na bhajasi pūrvāparānusandhānam ।।tasmād vimana-sama-dhiyaṃs tvām aham āgacchamācchannam ।
tāṃ smārayitum avaśyaṃ yā sambhavitā bhaval-līlā ।।loke' smiṃs tava bhaktā bahavaḥ kramaśaś ca te pālyāḥ ।
tasmād avaśyam udiyuḥ paurvāparyeṇa tā līlāḥ ।।
keśinaṃ ditijam aśva-veśinaṃ
yo jaghāna sa bhavān athābhavān ।
tān vidhātum aparān satāṃ parān
uccakaiḥ prabhavitākhilāvitā ।।
tathā hi-
keli-mātreṇa te daityā yad-bhidelimatāṃ gatāḥ ।
pacelimas tena tāpāt kaṃsaḥ pradhvaṃsam eṣyati ।।krūraḥ sa netuṃ sadyas tvām akrūras tu prahāsyati ।
tvaṃ ca mātrādikaṃ hitvā yātrāṃ taṃ hantum āpsyasi ।।
