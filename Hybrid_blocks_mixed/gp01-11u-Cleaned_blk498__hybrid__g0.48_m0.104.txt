śrī-rohiṇyā hari-jani-sukhaṃ śakyate kena vaktuṃ
yasmād veṣaṃ vividham adadhād bhartṛtaḥ proṣitāpi ।
citraṃ citraṃ sukṛta-varimā dṛśyatāṃ viśva-vandyaḥ
śrīman-nando' py amanuta nijaṃ bhāgyam āyātim asyāḥ ।।
