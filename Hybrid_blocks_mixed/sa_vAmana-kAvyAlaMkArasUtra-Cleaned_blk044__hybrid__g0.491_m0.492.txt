kuliśaśikharakharanakharapracaṇḍapeṭāpāṭitamattamātaṅgakumbhasthalagalanmadacchaṭāccharitacārukesarabhārabhāsuramukhe kesariṇi iti ।।
padyamanekabhedam ।।padyaṃ khalvanekena samārdhasamaviṣamādinā bhedena bhinnaṃ bhavati ।।tadanibaddhaṃ nibaddhaṃ ca ।।
tadidaṃ gadyapadyarūpaṃ kāvyamanibaddhaṃ nibaddhaṃ ca । anayoḥ prasiddhatvāllakṣaṇaṃ noktam ।।
kramasiddhistayoḥ sraguttaṃsavat ।।
tayorityanibaddhaṃ nibaddhaṃ ca parāmṛśyate । krameṇa siddhiḥ kramasiddhiḥ । anibaddhasiddhau nibaddhasiddhaiḥ sraguttaṃsavat । yathā sraji mālāyā siddhāyāmuttaṃsaḥ śekharaḥ sidhyati ।।
