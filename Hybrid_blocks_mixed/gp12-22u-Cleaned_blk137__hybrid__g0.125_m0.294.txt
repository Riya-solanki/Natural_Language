[] hasati ca bhakta-sāntvana-tṛṣṇe kṛṣṇe, tās tu bahudhā stava-budhā mṛdākulāḥ pṛdāku-patnyaḥ sakāku vijñāpayāmāsa । tatra saṅkṣepa-nikṣepaś cāyam-
asty eṣa sphurada-matiḥ sadarpa-sarpas
tvaṃ viṣvag-vid asi tathā kṛpāmburāśiḥ ।
asmāsu tvad-anugatāsv amuṣya patnī-
bhāvaś ced ajani yathā-yathaṃ vidhehi ।। [praharṣiṇī]
