ii

ākramate jarāmaraṇaṃ pralujyati śāstuḥ śāsanam vilupyate jīvitendriyaṃ । nirgacchati saddharmaḥ sīdati pratipir jāyate śaithilyaṃ । vivṛyante apāyadvārā । pithīyante amṛtadvārā cchidyate dharmasetur bhidyate dharmapotaḥ saṃsīdati dharmaplavo । niśāmyate dharmolkā ।

utpāṭyate dharmavṛkṣaḥ prapatati dharmadhvajaḥ ucchrapyate māradhvajaḥ śuṣyate dharmasamudro vikīryate jñānasumerur astaṃgacchaṃti vādiśārdūlā karṇādhārāḥ satpathopadeśakāḥ

acirād dhi manuṣyāṇāṃ mṛgasaṃjñā [bh] i ti tasmāt sarvair āyuṣmadbhir udvignaiḥ saṃvegam āpannaiḥ tasyānuttaradaśabaladharadharmarājacakravartinaḥ paurāṇasucaritakarmavipākanirjātasya mṛṣāvādapaiśunyapāruṣyābaddhapralāpavivarjitasya satyānuvartivaca[na] māsāvavādānuśāsanaṃ śrotavyam

iii

śāsayatīti hi śāstraṃ proktaṃ samyak ca śāsayaty eṣaḥ mokṣāya prātimokṣas tasmāc chāstraṃ pravaram etat ।

kāryākāryam avācyaṃ vācyaṃ yac caiva bhikṣuṇā yuktam tad iha nikhilena muninā prakāśitaṃ prātimokṣe asmiṃ ।

duścaritād vārayati trividhāt trividhe śubhe niyojayati । bṛṃhayati kuśalapakṣaṃ kṣapayati doṣāṃ guṇasapatnāṃ ।

mithyājīvam anāryaṃ vināśayati durgatipraṇetāram svargāpavargamārgaṃ viśodhayati cānupūrveṇa ।

yāvat sthāsyaty eṣaḥ prakāśitas tena lokanāthena । tāvat sthāsyati dharmo loke asmiṃ śākyasiṃhasya ।

tasmād ātmahitārthaṃ sthityarthaṃ śāsanasya caiva ciram । śṛṇuta muhūrtam avahitā vakṣyāmi prātimokṣam aham । ।।

i pārājikā dharmāḥ

yaḥ punar bhikṣur bhikṣubhiḥ sārdhaṃ śikṣāsāmīcisamāpannaḥ śikṣām apratyākhyā kṣādaurbalyaṃ vānāviṣkṛtvā maithunaṃ dharmaṃ pratiṣeveta antatas tīryagyonigatayāpi sārdham ayaṃ bhikṣuḥ pārājiko bhavaty asaṃvāsyaḥ 

yaḥ punar bhikṣur adattaṃ steyasaṃkhyātam ādadyād yathārūpeṇādattādānena rājā hy enaṃ gṛhītvā hanyād vā badhnīyād vā pravāsayed vā evaṃ cainaṃ vadec coro asi bālo asi mūḍho asi steyo asīty evaṃrūpaṃ bhikṣur adattam ādadānaḥ pārājiko bhavaty asaṃvāsyaḥ 

yaḥ punar bhikṣur manuṣyaṃ svahastena saṃcintya jīvitād vyaparopayec chastraṃ vainam ādhārayec chastrādhārakaṃ vāsya paryeṣayed evaṃ vainaṃ vaded dhambhoḥ puruṣa kiṃ tavānena pāpakena durjīvitena mṛtaṃ te jīvitād varam iti cittānumataṃ cittasaṃkalpitam anekaparyāyeṇa maraṇāya samādāpayen maraṇavarṇaṃ vāsyānusaṃvarṇayet sa ca tenopakrameṇa kālaṃ kuryād ayam api bhikṣuḥ pārājiko bhavaty asaṃvāsyaḥ 

yaḥ punar bhikṣur anabhijāna n aparijānann uttarimanuṣyadharmam alamāryaviśeṣādhigamaṃ jñānaṃ vā darśanaṃ vā pratijānīyāj jānāmīti paśyāmīti so apareṇa samayena samanuyujyamāno vā asamanuyujyamāno vā evaṃ vaded ajānamāno aham avocaṃ jānāmīty apaśyann avocaṃ paśyāmīti tucchaṃ mṛṣā vilapitam anyatrābhimānād ayam api bhikṣuḥ pārājiko bhavaty asaṃvāsyaḥ ।।

uddiṣṭā mayāyuṣmantaś catvāraḥ pārājikā dharmā yeṣāṃ bhikṣur anyatamānyatamaṃ dharmam āpanno na labhate bhikṣubhiḥ sārdhaṃ saṃvāsaṃ vā saṃbhogaṃ vā yathā pūrvaṃ tathā paścāt pārājiko bhavaty asaṃvāsyaḥ tatrāham āyuṣmataḥ pṛcchāmi kaccit sthātra pariśuddhā dvir api trir api pṛcchāmi kaccit sthātra pa trāyuṣmanto yasmā tūṣṇīm evam evāhaṃ dhārayāmi ।।

ii saṃghāvaśeṣā dharmāḥ
