pañcasaṃvatsaramayaṃ yugādhyakṣaṃ prajāpatim
dinartvayanamāsāṅgaṃ praṇamya śirasā śuciḥ
praṇamya śirasā kālam abhivādya sarasvatīm
kālajñānaṃ pravakṣyāmi lagadhasya mahātmanaḥ
jyotiṣām ayanaṃ kṛtsnaṃ pravakṣyāmy anupūrvaśaḥ
viprāṇāṃ saṃmataṃ loke yajñakālārthasiddhaye
nirekaṃ dvādaśārdhābdaṃ dviguṇaṃ gatasaṃjñikam
ṣaṣṭyā ṣaṣṭyā yutaṃ dvābhyāṃ parvaṇāṃ rāśir ucyate
svar ākramete somārkau yadā sākaṃ savāsavau
syāt tadādi yugaṃ māghas tapaḥ śuklo ayanaṃ hy udak
prapadyete śraviṣṭhādau sūryācandramasāv udak
sārpārdhe dakṣiṇārkas tu māghaśrāvaṇayoḥ sadā
gharmavṛddhir apāṃ prasthaḥ kṣapāhrāsa udaggatau
dakṣiṇe tau viparyāsaḥ ṣaṇmuhūrtyayanena tu
dviguṇaṃ saptamaṃ cāhur ayanādyaṃ trayodaśam
caturthaṃ daśamaṃ ca dvir yugmādyaṃ bahule apyṛtau
vasus tvaṣṭā bhavo ajaś ca mitraḥ sarpāśvinau jalam
dhātā kaścāyanādyāś cārthapañcamastv ṛtuḥ
bhāṃśāḥ syur aṣṭakāḥ kāryāḥ pakṣadvādaśakodgatāḥ
ekādaśaguṇaś conaḥ śukle ardhaṃ caindavā yadi
kāryā bhāṃśāṣṭakasthāne kalā ekānnaviṃśatiḥ
ūnasthāne trisaptatim udvaped ūnasaṃmitāḥ
tryaṃśo bhaśeṣo divasāṃśabhāgaś caturdaśaś cāpy anīya bhinnam
bhārdhe adhike cāpi gate paro aṃśo dvāv uttamaikaṃ pnavakairavedyam
