[] ṛṣir uvāca-tatas tad-ākarṇana-mātrataḥ sarve sarvato nirvarṇanāya saṅgatya satvaram eva cāgatya yathāvat kathayiṣyanti । teṣu kāścin mahilās tu kiñcid apy aprastutya śrī-rādhikādikāḥ pratyapūrvatāpūrvaṃ drakṣyanti । tataḥ paurṇamāsī vakṣyati-kim iva paśyatha ? kim iva ca kiñcin na kathayatha ?
