na cātītānāgatārthagocarāyāḥ saṃvidor'thasahabhāvo api ।
tadviṣayahānopādānopekṣābuddhijananādarthasahabhāva iti cet, na, arthasaṃvida iva hānādibuddhīnāmapi tadviṣayatvānupapatteḥ ।
hānādijananāddhānādibuddhīnāmarthaviṣayatvam, arthaviṣayahānādibuddhijananācca arthasaṃvidastadviṣayatvamiti cet, tatkiṃ dehasya prayatnavadātmasaṃyogo dehapravṛttinivṛttiheturartha ityarthaprakāśo astu ? jāḍyāddehātmasaṃyogo nārthaprakāśa iti cet, nanvayaṃ svayaṃprakāśo api svātmanyeva khadyotavatprakāśaḥ, arthe tu jaḍa ityupapāditam ।
