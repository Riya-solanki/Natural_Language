ekamevādvitīyamityādiśruteśca kathañcidupacaritārthatvena tathā ātmano hi vibhuniyatvādabhinnadeśakālā ityekatvenocyate ajo hyeko juṣamāṇo anuśete jahātyenāṃ bhuktabhogāmajo anya ityādiśruteśca puruṣabhedamavagacchāmaḥ ।
kiṃ punarasya prayojanaṃ niḥśreyasaṃ kiṃ tat akhilānarthamūlabhūtāyā avidyāyāḥ sattvātiśayaviśeṣāvirbhūtavidyayā vināśe sati tatpuruṣasya sahajacaitanyātmanāvasthānaṃ tanmokṣāparaparyāyaṃ niḥśreyasaṃ prakṛtipuruṣavivekajñānaṃ ca taddhetuḥ ।
