[] tataś ca viriñciḥ kiñcin namra-niṭilatā-ghaṭita-muni-vrata-sunirūpita-nija-durnītitayānujñāyāḥ karma-kartṛtvaṃ vyaktīkurvan, bhakti-bharāsakti-pṛktīkṛta-pulaka-saṅkulatayā svāparādha-maya-bādhā-vyakulatayā ca triḥ parikramya bahuśaḥ praṇamya ca nija-harmyam eva jagāma ।
