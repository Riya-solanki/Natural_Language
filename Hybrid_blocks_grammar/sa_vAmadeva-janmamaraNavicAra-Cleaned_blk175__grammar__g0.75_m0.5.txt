tataś ca tasmin śarīrayantre vighaṭite sā saṃvit prāṇanātmatām avalambya ātivāhikena dehena dehāntaraṃ nīyate tataś ca ātivāhikaṃ śarīrakaṃ bhūtabhaviṣyaddehāntarāle yugyasthānīyaṃ sambhavati yadārūḍho asau pudgalaḥ śarīrāntarāsaṅgam anubhavati uktaṃ ca kośabhāṣye mṛtyūpapattibhavayor antarā bhavatīha yaḥ ।।
