anyaccāha ekonapañcāśadityādi ।।
ekonapañcāśadbhāgāḥ tārasya rūpyasya kāryāḥ tathaiva śulvasya tāmrasya ekonapañcāśadbhāgāśca kāryāḥ punaḥ kanakasya hemnaśca eko bhāgaḥ kāryaḥ sūtasya ca ekena bhāgena vedha iti eṣo'pi śatāṃśavidhiḥ ।।
