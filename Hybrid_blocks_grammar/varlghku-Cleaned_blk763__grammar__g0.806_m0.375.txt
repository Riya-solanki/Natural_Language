yaṅo vā ।।
yaṅlugantātparasya halādeḥ pitaḥ sārvadhātukasyeḍ vā syāt । bhūsuvoriti guṇaniṣedho yaṅluki bhāṣāyāṃ na, bobhotu, tetikte iti chandasi nipātanāt । bobhavīti, bobhoti । adabhyastāt । bobhavāñcakāra, bobhavāmāsa । bobhaviṣyati । bobhavītu, bobhotu, bobhūtāt । bobhūtām । bobhūhi । abobhavīt, abobhot । abobhavuḥ । bobhūyātām । bobhūyāt । bobhūyāsuḥ । gātistheti sico luk । yaṅo vetīṭpakṣe guṇaṃ bādhitvā nityatvādvuk । abobhūvīt, abobhot । abobhūtām । abobhaviṣyat ।।
