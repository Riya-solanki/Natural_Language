Vṛtti:
[tataḥ] paścāt [saccarma prasārya] mṛgājinamāstīrya [āsanam] ardhacandrapadmasvastikādyāsanaṃ baddhvā vidvān iti yogapādapradarśitadhyānādividhijño analasaḥ san [mantraṃ dhyāyan japet] tattanmantrajapadhyānaṃ kuryāt।
