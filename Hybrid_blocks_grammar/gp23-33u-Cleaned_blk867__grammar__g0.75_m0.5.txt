[] bhavān vakṣyati-hanta ! vṛndāvana eva tādṛśaṃ prakāśa-vṛndaṃ vartate । yat khalu brahma-hrade nimajjya punas tasmād unmajjya ca bhavadbhiḥ pūrvam apy apūrvaṃ dṛṣṭam asti । śatāṅgasyāsya kāmaṅgāmitayā tat-praveśitāpi prativeśīyate । tato yathājñāpyate prājñānāṃ rājñā ।
