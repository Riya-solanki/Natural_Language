viśeṣamāha sa ityādi ।।
saḥ karañjatailapluto yogo bahuśo vāraṃvāraṃ kaṅguṇītailena secito yathā syāttathāyaṃ ati vilīnaḥ san mākṣikaravinivāpāṃ punaḥ kārya evaṃvidhaṃ ca kanakaṃ śatāṃśena śatavibhāgena vidhyati sitakanakam iti ।।
