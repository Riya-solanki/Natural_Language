athātra maṇḍale yathāvad vidhivistaraṃ kṛtvā, śiṣyānevaṃ brūyāt । "na tvayā adṛṣṭadharmamaṇḍalasya vaktavyaṃ । mā te maraṇakālaḥ śīghramevāsiddhasya syād", ityuktvā, maṇikuladharmajñānānyutpādayet ।
