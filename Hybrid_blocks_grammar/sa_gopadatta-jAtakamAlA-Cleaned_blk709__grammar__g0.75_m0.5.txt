ekaṃ tv atra mahārāja vaidhuram avagacchāmi । yena me vaktum asty etān maharṣīn asmin prayojane saṃkucati mānasam ।। ete hi mahātmāno vivekaratipriyatvād abhiramante himavati śailarāje ।। paśya mahārāja ।।
