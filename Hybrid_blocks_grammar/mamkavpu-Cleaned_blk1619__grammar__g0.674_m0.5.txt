taditi anyat aprākaraṇikaṃ nirdiśyate । tena samānam arthādiha prākaraṇikam āśrīyate । tasya tathāvidhasya dṛṣṭau satyāṃ yat aprākaraṇikatayā saṃvedanaṃ sa bhrāntimān ।
na caiṣa rūpakaṃ prathamātiśayoktirvā tatra vastuto bhramasyābhāvāt iha ca, arthānugamanena saṃjñāyāḥ pravṛtteḥ tasya spaṣṭameva pratipannatvāt । udāharaṇam
