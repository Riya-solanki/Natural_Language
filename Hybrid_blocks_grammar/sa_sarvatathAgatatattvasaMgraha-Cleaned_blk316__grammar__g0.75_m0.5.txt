tato mahāmaṇḍalaṃ yathānupūrvato darśayet । mahāmaṇḍale ca dṛṣṭamātre sarvatathāgatairadhiṣṭhyate, vajrasattvaścāsya hṛdaye tiṣṭhati । nānādyatīvaraśmimaṇḍaladarśanādīni prātīhāryavikurvitāni paśyati । sarvatathāgatādhiṣṭhitatvāt । kadācit bhagavān mahāvajradharaḥ svarūpeṇa darśanaṃ dadāti, tathāgato vati । tataḥ prabhṛti sarvārthāḥ sarvamano abhirucitakāryāṇi sarvasiddhira, yāvad, vajradharatvamapi tathāgatatvaṃ veti ।
