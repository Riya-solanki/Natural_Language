[] tad evam avadhāya sa sarva-darśaḥ śrīmān upanandaḥ parāmamarśa, satyam āśaṅkitaṃ prajāṃ prati prajāvatyor anayoḥ prajāvatyoḥ, yad idaṃ goṣṭha-prakoṣṭham avaduṣṭam iva dṛśyate । bhavatu, vrajarāja-samājam anu vicārayiṣyāmaḥ ।
