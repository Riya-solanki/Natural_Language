pūrvaparāvaradakṣiṇottarāparādharāṇi vyavasthāyāmasaṃjñāyām ।।
eteṣāṃ vyavasthāyāmasaṃjñāyāṃ ca sarvanāmasaṃjñā gaṇasūtrātsarvatra yā prāptā sā jasi vā syāt । asaṃjñāyāṃ kim ? uttarāḥ kuravaḥ । svābhidheyāpekṣāvadhiniyamo vyavasthā । vyavasthāyāṃ kim ? dakṣiṇā gāthakāḥ, kuśalā ityarthaḥ ।।
