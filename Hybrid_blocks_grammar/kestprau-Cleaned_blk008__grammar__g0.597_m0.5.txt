tadeva jñānaṃ sukhaduḥkhecchādveṣaprayatnadharmādharmarūpā aṣṭau bhāvā buddhereva ahaṃ jāne sukhītyādisāmānādhikaraṇyapratīteḥ ।
nanu yatraite dharmāḥ sa eva cetanaḥ kiṃ na syāditi cenna anityadharmarūpatayā pariṇāmitvāt dharmadharmiṇorabhedāt ।
ye punastayorbhedaṃ pratipannāstārkikaprabhṛtayaste tāvannīlo ghaṭā ityādyabhedāvagāhipratyayamapaśyanta upekṣaṇīyāḥ ।
nanu pradhānasyācaitanyasya kathaṃ jagatkartṛtvaṃ yat khalu nāmnā rūpeṇa ca vyākriyate taccetanakartṛkaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ yathā ghaṭādi ।
vivādādhyāsitaṃ ca jagannāmarūpeṇa vyākṛtaṃ tasmāccetanakartṛkamiti sambhāvyate cetano hi buddhāvālikhya nāmarūpe ghaṭā iti nāmnā rūpeṇa ca kambugrīvādinā bāhyaṃ ghaṭaṃ niṣpādayati ।
ata eva ghaṭasya nivartyasyāpyantaḥsaṃkalpātmanā siddhasya karmakārakabhāvo ghaṭaṃ karotīti ।
