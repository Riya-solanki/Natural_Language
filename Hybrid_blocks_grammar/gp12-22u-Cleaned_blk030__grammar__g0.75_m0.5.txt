[] sarva ūcuḥ-yatra gaty-antaraṃ nāsti, tat khalv anantaram eva vidhātuṃ yuktam । yat tu tatra-bhavantas tatra bhītāyante, tat punar vatsa-pālane' pi na tucchāyate । kintu bhavat-tapa eva pratapati pratīpān iti pratīyate । tasmād bhavatād bhavatām anujñā, yā paramparayāpi paraṃ param apy amaṅgalaṃ tāra-yiṣyati ।
