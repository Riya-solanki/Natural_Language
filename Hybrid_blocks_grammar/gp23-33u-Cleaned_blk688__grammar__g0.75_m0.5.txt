[] śrīmān baladevaś ca yathāvad eva tathā valayāmāsa । valayitvā ca vrajaṃ prati pravalitānujāgamana-viśvāsaḥ sva-preyasīr ihaiva vihāya dvārakām api niścinta iva caliṣyati । āgatya ca dvārakāṃ tvām api sāntvayiṣyati । yathā-kartavyāvaśeṣaṃ vidhāya vraja-śleṣam avāpsyati ।
