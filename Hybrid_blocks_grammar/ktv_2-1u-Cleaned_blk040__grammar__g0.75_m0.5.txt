kaḥ punar bhāvaḥ, ke vā bhāvaśabdāḥ itiyāvantaḥ phalasaṃbaddheṣu vākyeṣv ākhyātaśabdāḥ yajati dadāti juhoti ity evamādayaḥ, teṣu pratyayārthaprādhānyāt, kartari ca lakārotpatteḥ, yāgādiviśiṣṭakartrabhidhānam, kathaṃcid vā viparyayāt kartṛviśiṣṭayāgādyabhidhānaṃ manvānasya praśnaḥ ।
