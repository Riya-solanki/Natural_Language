[] tataḥ śrī-vasudeva-devakyor antar yac caturbhujam asya rūpaṃ sphurati, tad eva hi bahir āvirbhavati sma । phalena phala-kāraṇam anumīyate iti nyāyena । tenaiva ca nyāyena śrī-vrajeśvaryos tu paraṃ dvibhuja-mūrtitayā sphūrtir āsīt ।
