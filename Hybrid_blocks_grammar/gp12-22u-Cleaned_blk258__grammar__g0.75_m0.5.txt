[] tal-līlā-kramaḥ khalv ayaṃ duratyayatayā mayāsugamas tal-līlā-śakti-prayutānām asmākaṃ ca tatra pratyag-vadācaraṇe' pi nāradavan na pratyavāyas, tathāpi tathā prathanīyam yathā tāsāṃ na pratyavāyaḥ syād, āyatyām iṣṭāpattiḥ syād, akhilāyatyāṃ pūrna-tad-āpattiś ca syāt iti ।
