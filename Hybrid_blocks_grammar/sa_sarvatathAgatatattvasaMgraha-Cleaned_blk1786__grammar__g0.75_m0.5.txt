mudra athātra karmamaṇḍale samākarṣaṇādikarma kṛtvā, yathāvad vajradhārikarmasamayamudrāṃ badhvaivaṃ vadet ["na tvayā kasyacidadṛṣṭadevakulasyā]jñātakarmasyedaṃ guhyakarma vaktavyaṃ, mā te samayo vyathed!" iti uktvā, vajrācāryaḥ svakarmavajradhārisamayamudrāṃ bandhayet; krodhadṛṣṭyā nirīkṣannidamuttārayet oṃ vajradhāryāveśaya praveśaya nṛtyāpaya sarvakarmasiddhiṃ prayaccha huṃ a hūṃ a la la la la vajri ।।
