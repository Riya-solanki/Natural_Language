atra cakitahariṇīhārīti kriyāviśeṣaṇaṃ netratribhāgāsaṅgasya gurusaṃnidhānavihitāpragalbhatvaramaṇīyasya kāmapi kāma nīyatāmāvahati cakitahariṇīhārivilokana sāmyena ।
ayamaparaḥ padapūrvārdhavakratāyāḥ prakāro yadidaṃ saṃvṛtivakratvaṃ nāmayatra padārthasvarūpaṃ prastāvānuguṇyena kenāpi nikarṣeṇotkarṣeṇa vā yuktaṃ vyaktatayā sākṣādabhidhātumaśakyaṃ saṃvṛtisāmarthyopayoginā śabdenābhidhīyate ।
yathā
