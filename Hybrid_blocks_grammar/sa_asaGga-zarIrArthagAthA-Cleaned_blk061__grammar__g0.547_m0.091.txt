cittanimittasya kovidaḥ pravivekasya ca vindate rasaṃ ।
dhyāyī nipakaḥ pratismṛto bhuṃkte prītisukhaṃ nirāmiṣaṃ ।। 
aśilpajīvī laghur ātmakāmo jitendriyaḥ sarvato vipramuktaḥ । anokasārī hy amamo nirāśaḥ kāmān prahāyaikacaro yas sa bhikṣuḥ ।। dūraṃgamam ekacaram aśarīraṃ guhāśayaṃ । damayati durdamaṃ cittaṃ brāhmaṇaṃ taṃ bravīmy ahaṃ ।।
