-apradhānabrahmatajjñānaprārambhārtho bhavatu yathā adaṇḍau praiṣānanvāha'ityatra apradhānadaṇḍaḥ vivakṣyata iticenna । tatrahi amaitrāvaruṇaḥ praiṣyeti cānvāha'iti mantreṇa prāpte praiṣānuvacane na vidhyanvayitvena vivakṣā kintu aprāpte apradhāne api daṇḍagrahaṇe । ihatu na jijñāsāyā avivakṣākāraṇamityunneyam ।
