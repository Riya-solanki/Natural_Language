[] tvaṃ punaḥ sahasā rathād avatīrya teṣāṃ pathā yathā-vīryaṃ dhāvan sakala-gīrvāṇa-vikīryamāṇa-kusumādi-vāditrādi-kṛtamānaḥ sarvathāpy asambhāvita-samānaḥ param āsaktyā sarvān api tān anarvācīna-śaktyā ca pṛthak pṛthag eva ca yugapad eva ca saṅgacchan samāliṅgaṃs tat tad aṅga-saṅgataṃ svāṅgam apii na tato bhinnam aṅgīkariṣyasi ।
