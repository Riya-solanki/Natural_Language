[] tasyāṃ ca bhrātṛ-dvitīyāyāṃ bhrātṛbhiḥ praśastena bhaginī-hastena bhoktavyam iti parama-dhanyābhir upanandādi-kanyābhir mitho milantībhir nimantritau praṇaya-yantritau tatra sa-tṛṣṇau ca rāma-kṛṣṇau sahacara-nikareṇa saha bhojana-kareṇa saha maham arocayatām ।
