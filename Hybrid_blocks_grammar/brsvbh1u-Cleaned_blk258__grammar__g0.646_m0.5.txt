-naraśiraḥkapālaṃ śuci, prāṇyaṅgatvāt, śaṅkhavadityanumānaṃ anāraṃ spṛṣṭvāsthi sasnehaṃ savāsā jalamāviśet'ityāgamādbādhitaviṣayaṃ tathā sukṛtamanityaṃ, kāryatvādbaṭavadityanumānamapi aakṣayyam'ityāgamādbādhitaṃ bhavatītyāśayaḥ ।
