taṃ hataṃ paridevantyo bhāryās tasya vicukruśuḥ 
tvaritaṃ muktakeśyaś ca jagmuḥ kaṃsaniveśanam ।। 
kṛṣṇo api balabhadraś ca vāsāṃsy ādāya sarvataḥ । paridhāya tu tau pūrvaṃ śobhayetāṃ mahāmatī ।। dārakebhyas tato dattvā suślakṣṇāni mṛdūni ca । te ca sarve suvasanā virejuḥ śataśas tathā ।। śeṣāṇi tu tadā kṛṣṇo vāsāṃsi subahūny api । ācchichācchidya sahasā ciccheda ca pipeṣa ca ।। bhasmasād akarot tasya rajakasya niveśanam । 
tāv apy ubhau suvasanau jagmatur mālyakāraṇāt 
yathāyogaṃ yathābhogyaṃ yathāyogaṃ ni[darśya] ca । 
vīthīṃ mālyāpaṇānāṃ vai gandhāghrātau dvipāv iva ।।
