stutaśastravat ।
ātmā tu dravyaṃ karmaṇi guṇa iti saṃskāro vātmano darśanaṃ vidhīyate ।
yathā darśapūrṇamāsaprakaraṇe apatnyavekṣitamājyaṃ bhavati'iti samāmnātam, prakaraṇinā ca gṛhītamupāṃśuyāgāṅgabhūtājyadravyasaṃskāratayāvekṣaṇaṃ guṇakarma vidhīyate, evaṃ kartṛtvena kratvaṅgabhūte ātmani aātmā vā are draṣṭavyaḥ'iti darśanaṃ guṇakarma vidhīyate ।
