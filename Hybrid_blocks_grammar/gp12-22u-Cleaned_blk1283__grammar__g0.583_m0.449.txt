sarvatra tu mat-sphūrtir na bhavati bata sarvadeti pūrtir naḥ ।
bhavatīr anu sā nityā yaiva ca mat-sannidhāna-rūpaiva ।।tasmāt pati-suta-valitaṃ nilayaṃ tvarayā prayāta tā yūyam ।
mama ca tataḥ sukham uccaiḥ sāmañjasyaṃ hi sarvato vaśmi ।।
