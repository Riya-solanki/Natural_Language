atra vihaṅgamaścakravāko abhipretaḥ । tannāmāni cakrāṇi, tāni bibhratīti vihaṅgamanāmabhṛto rathāḥ । paṅktiriti daśasaṃkhyā lakṣyate । paṅktirdaśa vihaṅgamanāmabhṛto rathā yasya sa paṅktivihaṅgamanāmabhṛddaśarathaḥ । tasya tanayābhyāṃ rāmalakṣmaṇābhyāṃ saṃvalitaṃ plavagasainyaṃ jitam । ulūkajitā indrajitā । śabdendrplūkayorabhidhānamiti śabdavācyatvenendra ulūka uktaḥ । nanu caivaṃ rathāṅganāmādīnāmapi prayogo'nupapannaḥ, na; teṣāṃ nirūḍhalakṣaṇatvāt ।।
aprasiddhārthaprayuktaṃ gūḍhārtham ।।
yasya padasya loke'rthaḥ prasiddhaśrāprāseddhaśca, tadprasiddhe'rthe prayuktaṃ gūḍhārtham । yathā
