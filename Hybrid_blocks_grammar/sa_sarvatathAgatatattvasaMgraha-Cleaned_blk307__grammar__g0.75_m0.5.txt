tataḥ śiṣyāya brūyāt । "adya prabhṛtyahante vajrapāṇiryatte ahaṃ brūyāmidaṃ kuru tatkartavyaṃ, na ca tvayāhamavamantavyo, mā te viṣamāparihāreṇa kālakriyāṃ kṛtvā narapatanaṃ syād" iti uktvā, vaktavyaṃ brūhi, "sarvatathāgatā adhitiṣṭhanto vajrasattvo me āviśatu" ।
