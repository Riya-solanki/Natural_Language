kathaṃ punaḥ pramāṇasya svataḥprāpti, tadarthamidaṃ sarvajñānānyadhikṛtya cintyate kiṃ prāmāṇyamaprāmāṇyaṃ cobhayaṃ svata evāvagamyate, athavānyataradapi svabhāvenānirupitaṃ kāraṇaguṇadoṣapratyayābhyāmavagamyate, āhosvidaprāmāṇyaṃ svato avagamyate, prāmāṇyaṃ svataḥprāptaṃ sadarthānyathātvakāraṇadoṣajñānābhyāmavagamyamānenāprāmāṇyenāpodyate iti ।
tatra sarvakāraṇānāṃ svakāryaśakteḥ svabhāvikatvādubhayamapi jñānasya svarasādevāvagamyata iti kecit ।
tadayuktam ।
nahi jñānaṃ svaviṣayasya tathātvamatathātvaṃ ca yugapacchaknoti vedayituṃ virodhāt ।।
