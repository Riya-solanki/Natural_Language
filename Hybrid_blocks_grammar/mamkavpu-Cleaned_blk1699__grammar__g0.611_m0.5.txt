kutaḥ punareṣa niyamo yadeteṣāṃ tulye api kāvyaśobhātiśayahetutve kaścidalaṅkāraḥ śabdasya kaścidarthasya kaściccobhayasya iti cet । uktamtra yathā kāvye doṣaguṇāḷaṅkārāṇāṃ śabdārthobhayagatatvena
vyavasthāyāmanvayavyatirekāveva prabhavataḥ nimittāntarasyābhāvāt । tataśca yo alaṅkāro yadīyānvayavyatirekāvanuvidhatte sa tadalaṅkāro vyavasthāpyate iti ।
