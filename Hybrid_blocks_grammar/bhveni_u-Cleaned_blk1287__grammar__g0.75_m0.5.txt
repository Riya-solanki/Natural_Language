SktCh hā bhagavanto lokapālāḥ paritrāyadhvaṃ paritrāyadhvam । eṣa khalu somavaṃśarājarṣī rājasūyasaṃtarpitahavyavāhaḥ khāṇḍavasaṃtarpitahutavahasya kirīṭino jyeṣṭho bhrātā sugṛhītanāmadheyo mahārājayudhiṣṭhiraḥ । eṣāpi pāñcālarājatanayā devī yajñavedimadhyasaṃbhavā yājñasenī । dvāvapi niṣkaruṇajvalanasya praveśenendhanībhavataḥ । tatparitrāyadhvamāryāḥ paritrāyadhvam । kathaṃ na ko api paritrāyate । kiṃ vyavasitaṃ devyā devena ca ।
