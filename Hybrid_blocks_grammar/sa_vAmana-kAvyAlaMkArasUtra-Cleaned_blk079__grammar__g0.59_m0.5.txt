vaitālīyayugmapāde laghvakṣarāṇāṃ paṇṇāṃ nairantaryaṃ nipiddham । tacca kṛtamiti bhinnavṛttatvam ।।
virasavirāmaṃ yatibhraṣṭam ।।virasaḥ śrutikaṭurvirāmo yasmiṃstad virasavirāmaṃ yatibhraṣṭam ।।taddhātunāmabhāgabhede svarasaṃdhyakṛte prāyeṇa ।।
tad yatibhraṣṭaṃ dhātubhāgabhede nāmabhāgabhede ca sati bhavati । svarasandhinā kṛte prāyeṇa bāhulyena । dhātubhāgabhede mandākrāntāyāṃ yathā
