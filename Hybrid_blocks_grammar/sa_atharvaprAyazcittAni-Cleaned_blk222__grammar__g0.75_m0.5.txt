ya indreṇa sṛṣṭo yadi vā marudbhir yūpaḥ papāta dviṣatāṃ vadhāya । taṃ nirjagāmo haviṣā ghṛtena śaṃ no astu dvipade śaṃ catuṣpade ।। tvaṣṭre svāhā iti hutvā tvaṣṭā me daivyaṃ vacaḥ iti tvāṣṭraṃ sarvarūpam ālabheta ।
