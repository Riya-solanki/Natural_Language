aṇuhād brahmadattaḥ । tato viṣvaksenas tasyodaksenaḥ । bhallāṭas tasyātmajaḥ । dvimīḍhasya tu yavīnarasaṃjñas tasyāpi dhṛtimāṃs tataḥ satyadhṛtis tataś ca dṛḍhanemiḥ tasmāc ca supārśvas tataḥ sumatis tataś ca sannatimān sannatimataḥ kṛto abhūt । yaṃ hiraṇyanābho yogam adhyāpayām āsa । yaś caturviṃśati prācyasāmagānāṃ saṃhitāś cakāra ।।
