bhrasjo ropadhayo ramanyatarasyām ।।
bhrasjo rephasyopadhāyāśca sthāne ramāgamo vā syādārdhadhātuke । mitvādantyādacaḥ paraḥ । sthānaṣaṣṭhīnirdeśādropadhayornivṛttiḥ । babharjatuḥ । babharjitha, babharṣṭha । babhrajja । babhrajjitha । vraśceti ṣaḥ । babharje, babhrajje । bharkṣyati, bhrakṣyati । kṅiti ramāgamaṃ bādhitvā samprasāraṇaṃ pūrvavipratiṣedhena । bhṛjjyāt । bhṛjjyāsuḥ । bharkṣīṣṭa, bhrakṣīṣṭa । abhārkṣīt, abhrākṣīt । abharṣṭa, abhraṣṭa ।। cakarṣa, cakṛṣe ।।
