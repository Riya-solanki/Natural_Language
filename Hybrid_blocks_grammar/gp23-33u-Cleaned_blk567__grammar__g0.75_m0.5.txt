[] ṛṣir uvāca-vasudevādayas tu naṣṭaṃ cintāmaṇim iva spaṣṭaṃ vindamānās tvāṃ hātuṃ na hi sahiṣyante । [] tava ca tad-anurodhāya yadūnām itas tataḥ palāyana-samavāya-dūnānām avarodhāya ca niścita-cittasya katicid vāsarāṇi bhaviṣyanti । [] tvad-anuṣaṅgatas tatra saṅgatā tad-apekṣitī-kṛtya vraja-kṣiti-pati-prabhṛti-vraja-janatāpi śākaṭa-vāṭa-paṭa-nivāsam asatsyati ।
