japaḥ aghorādijapaḥ homastaddaśāṃśena havanaṃ devatārcanaṃ devatānāṃ gaṇeśaviṣṇuraviśivacaṇḍīnāṃ arcanaṃ eteṣu nirataḥ saktaḥ evaṃvidhiḥ pumān ।।
iti kiṃ hemaniyojitasūtaṃ dhārayet hemnā saha niyojito miśrito yaḥ sūtaḥ taṃ kāntamaṇiḥ kāntaścāsau maṇiśca vā kāntamaṇiḥ kāntasaṃjñako maṇiḥ ca punaḥ vividhaguṭikāḥ vividhāśca tā guṭikāśceti ।।
