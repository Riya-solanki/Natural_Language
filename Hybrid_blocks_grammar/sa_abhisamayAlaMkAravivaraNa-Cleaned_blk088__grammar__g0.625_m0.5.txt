avadhiḥ mahāyānasambhāramārgamārabhya antimakṣaṇasthabodhisattvāvasthāparyantamasya sīmā bhavati । viśeṣataśca upariṣṭāt sākṣānnirdiṣṭaprayogamārgīyoṣmāvasthātaḥ prārabhya antimakṣaṇasthabodhisattvāvasthāparyantamasya vyāptirbhavatīti jñeyam ।
