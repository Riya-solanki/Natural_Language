itarasyānudinam atigurupuruṣabhidyamānasyātiniṣṭhuraprahārapīḍitākhilāvayavasya nirāhāratayā balahāniḥ । nirjitaś ca bhagavatā jāmbavān praṇipatyāha v vyājahāra । asurasurayakṣagandharvarākṣasādibhir apy akhilair bhagavān na jetuṃ śakyaḥ । kim utāvanigocarair alpavīryair narair narāvayavabhūtaiś ca tiryagyonyanusṛtibhiḥ kiṃ punar asmadvidhaiḥ । avaśyaṃ bhagavato asmatsvāmino nārāyaṇasya sakalajagatparāyaṇasyāṃśena bhagavatā bhavitavyam ity uktas tasmai bhagavān akhilāvanibhārāvatāram ācacakṣe । prītyābhyañjitakaratalasparśanena cainam apagatayuddhakhedaṃ cakāra ।।
