[] tataś ca, śayanaṃ pārśvenopapīḍaṃ śayānam amuṃ sukumāra-kumārāpīḍam akasmād vilokya tad-vṛtte dhātrībhir mātre nivedita-mātre sati sātimātrānanda-kandalitā nija-nandana-maṅgalātiśaya-spṛhiṇī śrīman-nanda-kṣitīśa-gṛhiṇī bhartur ājñāṃ sujñātāṃ sambhūya bhūyaḥ sarvāḥ samāhūya tam eva mahotsavam aho mahotsavaṃ cakāra ।
