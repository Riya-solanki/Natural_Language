adhikāricintāṃ rītitattvaṃ ca nirūpya, kāvyāṅgānyupadarśayitumāha 
loko vidyā prakīrṇa ca kāvyāṅgāni ।।
uddeśakrameṇaitadvyācaṣṭe 
lokavṛttaṃ lokaḥ ।।lokaḥ sthāvarajaṅgamātmā tasya vartanaṃ vṛttimiti ।।śabdasmṛtyabhidhānakośacchandovicitikalākāmaśāstradaṇḍanītipūrvā vidyāḥ ।।śabdasmṛtyādīnāṃ tatpūrvakatvaṃ pūrvaṃ kāvyabandheṣvapekṣaṇīyatvāt ।।
tāsāṃ kāvyāṅgatvaṃ yojayitumāha 
śabdasmṛteḥ śabdaśuddhiḥ ।।
śabdasmṛtervyākaraṇācchabdānāṃ śuddhiḥ sādhutvaniścayaḥ kartavyaḥ । śuddhāni hi padāni niḥśaṅkaiḥ kavibhiḥ prayujyante ।।
abhidhānakośāt padārthaniścayaḥ ।।
padaṃ hi racanāprapraveśayogyaṃ bhāvayan saṃdigdhārthatvena na gṛhṇīyānna vā jahyāditi kāvyabandhavighnaḥ । tasmādabhidhānakośataḥ padārthaniścayaḥ kartavya iti । apūrvābhidhānalābhārthatvaṃ tvayuktamabhidhānakośasya, aprayuktasyāprayojyatvāt । yadi prayuktaṃ prayujyate tarhi kimiti saṃdighāthatvamāśaṅkitaṃ padasya ? tanna; tatra sāmānyenārthāvagatiḥ saṃbhavati । yathā śabdena jaghanavasragranthirucyata iti kasyacinnaścayaḥ । striyā vā puruṣasya veti saṃśayaḥ nīvī saṃgrathanaṃ nāryā jaghanasthasya vāsasaḥ iti nāmamālāpratīkamapaśyata iti । atha kathaṃ
