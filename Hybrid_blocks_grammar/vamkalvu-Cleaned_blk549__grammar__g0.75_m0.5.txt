padamenekārthaṃ bhinnārthamekamanekaṃ vā tadvadakṣaranāvṛttaṃ sthānaniyame sati yamakam । svavṛttyā sajātīyena vā kārtsnyaikadeśābhyāmanekapādavyāptiḥ sthānaniyama iti । yāni tvekapādabhāgavṛttīni yamakāni dṛśyante teṣu ślokāntarasthasaṃsthānayamakāpekṣayaiva sthānaniyama iti ।।
