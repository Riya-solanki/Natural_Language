-saṃnihitarajatagocarajñānasārūpyeṇeti । atraivaṃ tajjñānasārūpyaṃ-yathedaṃ rajatamitijñānaṃ idamo rajatasya cāsaṃsargaṃ na gṛhṇāti, tayoḥ saṃsṛṣṭatvāt । evamete api smaraṇagrahaṇe svagatabhedaṃ, svaviṣayāsaṃsṛṣṭatvaṃ na nivedayata iti ।
