ādigrahaṇād bhāvodayabhāvasaṃdhibhāvaśabalatvāni । pradhānatayā yatra sthito rasādistatrālaṅkāryaḥ yathodāhariṣyate । anyatra tu pradhāne vākyārthe yatrāṅgabhūto rasādistatra guṇībhūtavyaṅgye
rasavatpreyaūrjasvisamāhitādayo alaṅkārāḥ । te ca guṇībhūtavyaṅgyābhidhāne udāhariṣyante ।।
