atra tigmaruceḥ kakubhāṃ ca yathā sadṛśaviśeṣaṇavaśena vyaktiviśeṣaparigraheṇa ca nāyakatayā nāyikātvena ca vyaktiḥ, tathā grīṣmadivasaśriyo api pratināyikātvena bhaviṣyatīti kiṃ dayitayeti svaśabdopādānena ।।
śleṣopamāyāstu sa viṣayaḥ yatropamānasyopādānamantareṇa sādhāraṇeṣvapi viśeṣaṇeṣu na tathā pratītiḥ । yathā
