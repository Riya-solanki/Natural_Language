jāyā śīlavivarjitā vyayavatī māyānikāyaḥ suhṛd duṣpūro gurur arthabhoganicayair bandhur daridraḥ śaṭhaḥ ।
dāsaś cauryaparo asalaś ca kupade putraḥ khalaiḥ pātitaḥ so ayaṃ kleśaśatāptasaṃcayanidheḥ pratyakṣalakṣyaḥ kṣayaḥ ।।
