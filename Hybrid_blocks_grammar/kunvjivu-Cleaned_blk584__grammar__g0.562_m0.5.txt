garbhagranthiṣu vīrudhāṃ sumanaso madhye aṅkuraṃ pallavā vāñchāmātraparigrahaḥ pikavadhūkaṇṭhodare pañcamaḥ ।
kiṃ ca trīṇi jaganti jiṣṇu divasairdvitrairmanojanmano devasyāpi cirojjhitaṃ yadi bhavedabhyāsavaśyaṃ dhanuḥ ।।
