atha kadācittasyāṃ śākhāyāṃ pipīlikābhiḥ parṇapuṭāvacchāditaṃ taruṇatvānnātimahadekaṃ phalaṃ na te vānarā dadṛśuḥ । tatkrameṇābhivardhamānaṃ varṇagandharasamārdavopapannaṃ paripākavaśācchithilabandhanaṃ tasyāṃ nadyāṃ nipapāta । anupūrveṇa vāhyamāna nadīstrotasā anyatamasya rājñaḥ sāntaḥpurasya tasyāṃ nadyāṃ salilakrīḍāmanubhavato jālakaraṇḍakapārśve vyāsajyata ।
