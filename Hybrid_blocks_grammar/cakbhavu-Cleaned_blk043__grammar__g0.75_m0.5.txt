Viv tyāgagrahodgamakriyābhāvābhāvavikalpanirmuktam ata eva mātṛmānameyollaṅghanena pramitimātraṃ nirviśeṣeṇa phalarūpam āsanam upalakṣaṇatayā tasyāpi sāpekṣatvāt tad apy ullaṅghitavato avyapadeśyasya tava, te nivedyata iti pāṭhe nivedyate niḥśeṣeṇa vedyate tadbhāvenānubhūyate, trijagato mātṛmānameyalakṣaṇasyādhiṣṭhātaḥ! hānādānādipadārthaṣaṭkanirdeśatvānapekṣam āsanaṃ sūcitam ।।
