[] ṛṣir uvāca-tad evaṃ kāvya-śreṇīr iva nānā-deśa-veśa-bhāṣā-rasair eṇīdṛśaḥ pāṇaukṛtya pūrva-pūrvavad rāginīṃ madrādhipater api kanyāṃ sanyāyatayā varyāṃ bhāryā-varyāṃ kariṣyasi । na kevalā sā, tāsām adharāḥ ṣoḍaśa-sahasra-parimāṇāś cāparāḥ ।
