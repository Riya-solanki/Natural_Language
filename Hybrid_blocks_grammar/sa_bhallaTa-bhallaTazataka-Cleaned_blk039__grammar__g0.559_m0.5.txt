kiṃ jāto asi catuṣpathe ghanatarachāyo asi kiṃ chāyayā saṃyuktaphalito asi kiṃ phalabharaiḥ pūrṇo asi kiṃ sannataḥ 
he sadvṛkṣa sahasva samprati sakhe śākhāśikhākarṣaṇakṣobhāmoṭanabhañjanāni janataḥ svarair eva duśceṣṭitaiḥ ।।
