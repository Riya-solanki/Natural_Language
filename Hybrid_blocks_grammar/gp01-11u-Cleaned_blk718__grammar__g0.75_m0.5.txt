[] tataś ca pūrva-pūrva-nihiṃsanāt prajāta-mati-bhraṃśena dhairya-rahitatayā kaṃsena prahitaḥ sumanasām ahitaḥ sura-vartmani dūrataḥ sthitas tṛṇāvartas taṃ tathā vartamānaṃ dadarśa vimamarśa ca । so' yam eva toyada-varṇaḥ pṛthukaḥ pṛthu-gṛhālindaṃ vindamānāyā mātur aṅke vartata iti śaṅke ।
