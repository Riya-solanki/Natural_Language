atra mandaraḥ samudraṃ vimathya keṣāṃcideva rasanānandakāraṇa mamṛtamutpādayāmāsa । nāyakastu vāhinīṃ vimathya sarvalokaśravaṇānandakāraṇaṃ kīrtirūpamamṛtaṃ dikṣupūrayatīti mandarānnāyakasyādhikyapratipādanāt pratīyamānasya vyatirekasya nātisphuṭatvam ।
