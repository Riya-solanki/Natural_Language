kimiti punaḥ pramātā upādatte pramāṇāni ? atha svayameva kasmānna pravartante ityata āha- na cādhiṣṭhānamantareṇendriyāṇāṃ vyāpāraḥ, pramāṇānāṃ vyāpāraḥ, saṃbhavati ।
na jātu karaṇānyanadhiṣṭhitāni kartrā svakārye vyāpriyante, mā bhūtkuvindarahitebhyo vemādibhyaḥ paṭotpattiriti ।
atha deha evādhiṣṭhātā kasmānna bhavati ? kṛtamatrātmādhyāsenetyata āha- na cānadhyastātmabhāvena dehena kaścidvyāpriyate; suṣupte api vyāpāraprasaṅgāditi bhāvaḥ ।
