atha vajrapāṇirmahābodhisatvaḥ tānapāyabhūmipatitān sarvasatvānāhūyaivamāha । "gṛhṇata mārṣāstriśaraṇagamanasaṅkaṭāt! । eṣa vayaṃ buddhaṃ dharma saṃghaṃ tvaṃ ca śaraṇaṃ gacchāma" iti । atha vajrapāṇiḥ sarvāpāyasphoṭanahṛdayamudājahāra oṃ vajrapāṇi visphoṭaya sarvāpāyabandhanāni pramokṣaya sarvāpāyagatibhya sarvasatvān sarvatathāgatavajra samaya traṭ ।।
