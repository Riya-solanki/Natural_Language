[] tataḥ sāścaryaṃ paryavolokamāneṣu teṣu devī vakṣyati-kathaṃ veha sandehaḥ kriyate ? pūrvata eva tāvad ubhaya-vidhā etā nāpūrvatayā mantavyāḥ । pūrvāsāṃ sajātīyā dvitīyā hi yogamāyājñayā garga-vighnaṃ nighnatyā māyayā mayā nirmitāḥ । saṃjñāyāś chāyā-vat ratyās tan-māyā-kalpitāvac ca ।
