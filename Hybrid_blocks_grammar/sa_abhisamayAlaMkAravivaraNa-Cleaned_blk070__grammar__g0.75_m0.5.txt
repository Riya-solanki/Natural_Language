śravaṇaniyamaḥ mārge apraviṣṭo api śuddhādhyāśayikaḥ śuddhakarmikaḥ pudgalo māhāyānīyāvavādaṃ śrotuṃ bhavyo bhavati । yaśca mārgapraviṣṭo bodhisattvaḥ sa kalpānāmanekāni buddhebhyo bodhisattvebhyaḥ kalyāṇamitrebhyaḥ avavādaṃ śṛṇoti ।
