tāmbūlaṃ dantakāṣṭhañca bhojanaṃ ṛtusevanam । grāmamadhye sthite prete varjayet piṇḍapātanam ।। snānaṃ dānaṃ japo homastarpaṇaṃ surapūjanam । grāmamadhye sthite prete śuddhyarthaṃ jñātidharmataḥ ।। jñātisambandhināmevaṃ vyavahāraḥ khageśvara । vilupya jñātidharmañca pretapāpena lipyate ।।
