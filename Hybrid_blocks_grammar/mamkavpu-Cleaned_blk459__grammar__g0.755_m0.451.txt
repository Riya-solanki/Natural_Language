ye tvabhidadhati so ayamiṣoriva dīrghadīrghataro vyāpāraḥ iti yatparaḥ śabdaḥ sa śabdārthaḥ iti vidhirevātra vācya iti । te apyatātparyajñāstātparyavācoyukterdevānāṃpriyāḥ । tathāhi {bhūtavyasamuccāraṇe bhūtaṃ bhavyāyopadiśyate} iti kārakapadārthāḥ kriyābhisaṇbandhāt sādhyāmānatāṃ prāpnuvanti । tataścādagdhadahananyāyena yāvadaprāptaṃ tāvadvidhīyate । yathā ṛtvikpracaraṇe pramāṇāntarāt siddhe
{lohitoṣṇīṣā ṛtvijaḥ pracaranti} ityatra lohitoṣṇīṣatvamātraṃ vidheyaṃ havanasyānyataḥ siddheḥ {dadhnā juhoti} ityādau dadhyādeḥ karaṇatvamātraṃ vidheyam ।।
kvacidubhayavidhiḥ kvacittrividhirapi yathā {raktaṃ paṭaṃ vayaḥ} ityādau ekavidhirdvividhistrividhirvā । tataśca {yadeva vidheyaṃ tatraiva tātparyam} ityupāttasyava śabdasyārthe tātparya na tu pratītamātre evaṃ hi {pūrvo dhāvati} ityādāvaparādyathapi kvacittātparyaṃ syāt ।
