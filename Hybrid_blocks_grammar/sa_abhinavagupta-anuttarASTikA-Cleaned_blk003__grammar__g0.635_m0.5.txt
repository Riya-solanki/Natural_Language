saṃsāro asti na tattvatas tanubhṛtāṃ bandhasya vārtaiva kā bandho yasya na jātu tasya vitathā muktasya muktikriyā ।
mithyāmohakṛd eṣa rajjubhujagacchāyāpiśācabhramo mā kiṃcit tyaja mā gṛhāṇa vihara svastho yathāvasthitaḥ ।।
