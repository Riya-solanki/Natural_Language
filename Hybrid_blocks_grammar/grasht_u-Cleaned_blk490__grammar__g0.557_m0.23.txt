rasānāṃ krameṇa śodhanamāha svinnamityādi ।।
sakṣārāmlairvaikrāntakaṃ svinnaṃ dolābhidhānena sveditaṃ kuryāt tat svinnaṃ vaikrāntaṃ haṭhāt prābalyāt dhmātaṃ sat dravati sāraṃ muñcati drutamātraṃ satvanirgamamātrameva śudhyati pūrvasaṃbandhāt dravati ।।
