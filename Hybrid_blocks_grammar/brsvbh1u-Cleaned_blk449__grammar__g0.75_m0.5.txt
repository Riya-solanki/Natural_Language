NOTE -yataḥ yadāgneyo aṣṭākapālo amāvāsyāyāṃ paurṇamāsyāṃ cācyuto bhavati ityādivākyena yāgo vidhīyate tasmāt aya evaṃ vidvān paurṇamāsīṃ yajate'ityanuvāde yajata iti śrutaṃ, anyathā saṃbandha eva śrūyetetyarthaḥ । tasmānna kutrāpi saṃbandhavidhānamiti siddham । idamuktaṃ vistareṇa aprakaraṇaṃ paurṇamāsyāṃ rūpāvacanāt' ityatra । diṅmātramiha darśitam ।
