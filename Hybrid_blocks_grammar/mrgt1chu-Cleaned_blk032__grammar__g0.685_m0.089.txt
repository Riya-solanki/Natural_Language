ghaṭate ceṣṭate arthakriyām iti ghaṭaḥ candati hlādayati dīpyate ceti candra ity evaṃvidhayā śabdavyutpattyā śabdavyatiriktavācyārthāsaṃbhavato ghaṭaśabdasyaivodakāharaṇaṃ candraśabdasyaiva cāhlādanādi prāptaṃ na cānayos tad asti api tu tadvācyayoḥ pṛthubudhnodarādyākārabhāsvarabimbasvarūpayos tattadarthakriyākaraṇakṣamatvaṃ dṛṣṭam ।। 
tato na vācyavācakayor aikyam ।। 
tataś ca na śabdamātraṃ devatā kiṃ tarhi tadvācyaiveti siddham ।। 
idānīṃ vākyaṃ tadanyathāsiddham iti paramatam anusaṃdhāya dūṣayati ।।
