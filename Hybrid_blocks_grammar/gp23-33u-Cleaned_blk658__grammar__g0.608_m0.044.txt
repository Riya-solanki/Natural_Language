[] kṛṣṇaḥ sasaṅkoca-locanam uvāca-tad api katham ?
[] ṛṣir uvāca-yādava-prabhavāṇām ayam eva kula-krama-samācāraḥ ।
[] kṛṣṇaḥ svagatam uvāca-na khalv idaṃ jñāteyaṃ, kintu kāpeyam eva । tathāpi bhadram evedam æ yasmād anena sambandhena vraja-bandhavas tad idaṃ maṃsyante । nāsau teṣāṃ gotratayā stotra-viṣayatām ātmani manyate, kintu gotrānta-ramitānām asmākam eva iti ।
