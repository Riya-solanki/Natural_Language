NOTE -tarhi saṃtatādivākyāni viśiṣṭavidhayaḥ syuḥ । agnihotrādivākyaṃ ca tadanuvādaḥ syāt, tatrāha-eva ceti । ayamāśayaḥ-yadyapyatra viśiṣṭo vidhiḥ pratīyate tathāpi bhāvārthadvārā dravyādikamapi viṣayīkaroti । tatra saṃkrānto yadi bhāvārthamanyato vihitaṃ na labheta tarhi gauravamapyuparīkṛtya viśiṣṭaṃ vidadhītātha labheta tata upapadākṛṣṭaśaktirdravyādiparo bhavati । bhāvārthaṃ tvanuvadati । tadihāgnihotrādivākyata eva bhāvārthalābhāddravyaparatetyunneyamiti ।
