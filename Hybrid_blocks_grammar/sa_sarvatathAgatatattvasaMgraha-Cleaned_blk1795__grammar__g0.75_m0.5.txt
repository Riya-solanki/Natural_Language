atha vajrapāṇiḥ sarvavajrakulān sarvasattvārthaṃ [sthitvā] yāvan sanniyojyāvaivartikabhūmau pratiṣṭhāpya, jānanneva bhagavantametadabhāṣat । "ahaṃ bhagavadbhiḥ sarvatathāgataistava guhyadhāritve abhiṣiktaḥ । [yadā]jñāpayasva kintat tathāgataguhyam!" iti ।।
