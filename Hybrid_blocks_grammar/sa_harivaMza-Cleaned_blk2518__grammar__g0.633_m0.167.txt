tadājñāpaya kiṃ kuryāṃ sadārāpatyabāndhavaḥ 
kasya vā vaśatāṃ yāmi jīvitaṃ me pradīyatām ।। 
namas te brahmarudrendra surāsuranamaskṛta । śaraṇaṃ tvāṃ prapanno asmi jīvitaṃ me pradīyatām ।। aparādhaṃ kṛtaṃ yat tu mūḍhenākṛtabuddhinā । darpānvitena ca mayā na tat saṃsmartum arhasi ।। prasanne tvayi deveśa vainateyo na māṃ haret । tasmād vidhatsva yat kāryaṃ mayā kartavyam īśvara । ity uktvā sa śirobhis tu kṛṣṇapādāv apūjayat । 
pañcamūrdhānataṃ dṛṣṭvā sarpaṃ sarpāriketanaḥ 
akruddha eva bhagavān pratyuvācorageśvaram ।।
