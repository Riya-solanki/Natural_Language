[] yathā rādhāyā viśākhā lalitā paryāyānurādhā ca, candrāvalyāḥ śaivyā padmā cety-ādayaḥ । tāsu tu tāḥ sva-mātra-niṣṭhatāṃ niṣṭaṅkya svayam eva sa-sauhārdaṃ nija-hārdam āvedayāñcakruḥ । tathāpi tatra prathamaṃ rāma-sahitatā-pihitam eva taṃ lupta-varṇa-padaṃ varṇayāmāsuḥ ।
