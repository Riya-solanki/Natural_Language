[] dūta uvāca-"atha kiṃ? yat khalu śīghram evāsmai rāja-vyāja-rākṣasāya saṅgamya balir valayitavyo militavyaś cāham" iti । kiṃ cedam api sandiṣṭam, "bhavan-nandanotpatti-samayaṃ samayā vayam atyutkaṇṭhitās tan-maṅgalena saṅgama-nīyāḥ, tathā bhavat-putra-nirviśeṣasya tasya bāla-viśeṣasya vṛttir vartayitavyā" iti ।
