nanu savituḥ sāṃnidhyādhyāsādyuktā nānādeśāvagatiḥ śabde tu katham ।
sarvagatatvāt- sarvagato hi śabdo bhinnadeśairdhvanibhiḥ svesve deśe abhivyajyamāno bhinnadeśo avabhāsate, dhvanayo hi śrotradeśamāgatyāpi śabdaṃ vyañjayantaḥ svotpattidesamiva śabdaṃ bhāsayantīti darśanabalādabhyupagamyate ।।
