yuvayor hi kṛte vṛddhaḥ kaṃsena sa nirasyate 
bhartsyate ca divā rātrau neha sthātavyam ity api ।। 
tad yuvābhyāṃ hi kartavyaṃ pitrarthaṃ sukham uttamam । yathā sukham avāpnoti tad vai kāryaṃ hitānvitam । nāhaṃ yuvābhyāṃ rahitaḥ pravekṣye mathurāṃ purīm । tyaktuṃ nārhasi māṃ nātha bhaktaṃ te bhaktavatsala ।। āgaccha yāma gehān naḥ sanāthān kurv adhokṣaja । punīhi pādarajasā gṛhān no gṛhamedhinām । yacchaucenānutṛpyanti pitaraḥ sāgnayaḥ surāḥ ।। āpas te aṅghryavanejanyas trīn lokān śucayo apunan । śirasādhatta yāḥ śarvaḥ svayaṃ tāḥ sagarātmajāḥ ।। devadeva jagannātha puṇyaśravaṇakīrtana । yadūnām uttamaḥśloka nārāyaṇa namo astu te । 
tam uvāca tataḥ kṛṣṇo yāsyāvo avām atarkitau 
prekṣantau mathurāṃ vīra rājamārgaṃ ca dhārmika ।। 
tasyaiva tu gṛhaṃ sādho gacchāvo yadi manyase । evam ukto bhagavatā so akrūro vimanā iva । purīṃ praviṣṭaḥ kaṃsāya karmāvedya gṛhaṃ yayau । akrūro api namaskṛtya manasā viṣṇum avyayam । jagāma kaṃsapārśvaṃ tu prahṛṣṭenāntarātmanā । tau ca devau jagannāthau balakeśavasaṃjñitau । 
anuśiṣṭau ca tau vīrau prasthitau prekṣakāv ubhau 
ālānābhyām ivotsṛṣṭau kuñjarau yuddhakāṅkṣiṇau ।। 
dārakāś ca tayor mārgam anuvavruḥ samantataḥ । 
tau tu mārgagataṃ dṛṣṭvā rajakaṃ raṅgakārakam 
ayācetāṃ tatas tāni vāsāṃsi virajāni vai ।।
