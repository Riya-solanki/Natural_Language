adhunā viśeṣaprakārāntaram āha svedya ityādi ।
svedyo ghanaḥ pūrvoktaistṛṇasāravikāraiḥ sveditamabhraṃ svedavidhiruktaḥ punarghanasya piṇḍaṃ baddhvā kaiḥ saha māhiṣadadhidugdhamūtraśakṛdājyaiḥ kṛtvā mahiṣyā idaṃ māhiṣaṃ evaṃbhūtaṃ yaddadhi dugdhaṃ mūtraṃ śakṛdviṣṭhā ājyaṃ ghṛtaṃ caitaiḥ piṇḍaṃ baddhvā ।।
atheti samuccaye ।।
pañcagavyaiḥ gavāṃ dugdhadadhimūtraśakṛdājyaiḥ saṃyuktaḥ kāryaḥ piṇḍaṃ baddhvetyarthaḥ ।।
etatpiṇḍaṃ lohanibhaṃ muṇḍaprabhaṃ sattvaṃ pātayati pūrvasaṃbadhāt ghanasya ityadhyāhāraḥ ।।
