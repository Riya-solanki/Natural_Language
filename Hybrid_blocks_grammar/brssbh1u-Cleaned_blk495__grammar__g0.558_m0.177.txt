nāhaṃ khalvayamevaṃ saṃpratyātmānaṃ jānātyayamahamasmīti no evemāni bhūtāni' iti ca suṣuptāvasthāyāṃ doṣamupalabhya aetaṃ tveva te bhūyo anuvyākhyāsyāmi no evānyatraitasmāt iti copakramya śarīrasaṃbandhanindāpūrvakaṃ aeṣa saṃprasādo asmāccharīrātsamutthāya paraṃ jyotirupasaṃpadya svena rūpeṇābhiniṣpadyate sa uttamaḥ puruṣaḥ' iti jīvameva śarīrātsamutthitamuttamapuruṣaṃ darśayati ।
tasmādasti saṃbhavo jīve pārameśvarāṇāṃ dharmāṇām ।
ataḥ adaharo asminnantarākāśaḥ' iti jīva evokta iti cetkaścidbrūyāt, taṃ prati brūyāt- aāvirbhūtasvarūpastu' iti ।
tuśabdaḥ pūrvapakṣavyāvṛttyarthaḥ ।
nottarasmādapi vākyādiha jīvasyāśaṅkā saṃbhavatītyarthaḥ ।
