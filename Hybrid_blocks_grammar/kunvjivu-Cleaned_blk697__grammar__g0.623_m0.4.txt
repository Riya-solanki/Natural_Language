yadi vā na pāramārthikamanaucityamatra vivakṣitamapi tu vibhāvānubhāvavyabhicāryau cityābhivyaṅgyaprastāvāntarāviṣayaniravadyarasāpekṣayā kimapi prastutānuguṇameva, tathā ca "kāmakrodhādikāraṇāt" iti yuktirupanyastetyucyate, tadapi na su sthitasamādhi ।
yasmādaucityaparipoṣapuṣkalarasāpekṣayā kiñcidanaucityayuktarasabhāvopanibandhanaṃ parimitasattvaprāyaprāṇimātraviṣaye kāmādikāraṇāt karaṇīyatāmarhati na punarudāhṛte viṣaye ।
lavibhāvādisamudayasamullāsitaḥ sahajakaviśaktikauśalasamudbhāsitasaukumāryasamarpitasvābhāvikarāmaṇīyakaḥ sarasamatimataḥ candrakāntakaumudīprakāśavadāścaryavilāsāpasāryamāṇopapatteḥ samucito api rasaḥ paramasaundaryamāvahati ।
tat kathamanaucityaparimlānaḥ kāmādikāraṇakalpanopasaṃhatavṛttiralaṅkāratāva bhāsatāṃ prayāsyati ? tathā ca tathāvidhe viṣaye varṇanīyāntarasamānatayā rasavattāṃ parikalpayantaḥ satkavayo nitāntaṃ rājante ।
yathā
