nanu dvitīyābhāve sarvagatatvaśrutivirodha ityata āha-anena sarvagatatvamiti ।
dvitīyaṃ satyaṃ cetsetvādivadbrahmaṇo alpatāsyāt ayatrānyatpaśyati tadalpam'iti śruteḥ kiñca niravayavāsaṃgabrahmaṇaḥ satyaprapañcasaṃbandhāyogāttavaiva sarvagatatvaśrutivirodha iti bhāvaḥ adhiṣṭhānenādhyastaṃ jagadvyāptamadhyastatvāt rajjvā vyāptasarpavat, iti nyāyaḥ ।।
