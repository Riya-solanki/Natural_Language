atra mūrtiḥ kīrtiriti dve vastunī pratipadaṃ dyotakena cakāreṇa ekasyāṃ tanotīti kriyāyāṃ samuccitya niveśite । tena tanuta iti dvivacanaṃ cārthe dvandvasamāsaśva bhavatīti so ayaṃ kriyāyāṃ dravyasamuccayo nāma pratipadadyotako dvipadāśrayassamuccayabhedaḥ ।
