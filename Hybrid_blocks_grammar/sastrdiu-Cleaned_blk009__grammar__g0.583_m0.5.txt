atredaṃ cintyate- kimadhyayanānantaraṃ dharmajijñāsayā gurugṛhe asthātavyam, uta gurugṛhātsamāvartitavyamiti ।
yadi hi svādhyāyādhyayanavidhinā dharmamātrārthamadhyayanaṃ svardādyarthena vidhīyate tatasmānmātreṇaiva śāstrārthasya samāptatvāt aadhītyasnāyāt' iti smṛtivacanabalena ca samāvartitavyamiti ।
