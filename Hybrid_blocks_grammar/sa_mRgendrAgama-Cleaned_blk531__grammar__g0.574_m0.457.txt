[kṛṣṇapakṣe caturdaśyāmaṣṭamyāṃ] kṛṣṇāṣṭamīcaturdaśyoḥ [siddhadarśane] siddhasandarśane [viṣuve] viṣuvaduttarāyaṇārkendugrahaṇādiṣu [sarveṣu parvasu] sarvaparvakāleṣu ca śrāddhaṃ viśeṣataśca aṣṭakādiṣu iti, kanyāgate arke māghatryodaśīprabhṛtiṣu ca kuryāt।
[gurutattulyabandhūnāṃ] guroḥ guruputrādīnāṃ ca tattulyānāmanyeṣāṃ ca vayovidyādharmādivṛddhānāṃ ca [jyāyasāṃ bhrātṝṇāṃ] janmādinā dīkṣādyanugrahataśca jyeṣṭhānāṃ bhrātṝṇāṃ ca [madhuparkādyaiḥ] madhuparkavastrālaṃkārādyaiḥ pūjanaṃ kāryam।।
