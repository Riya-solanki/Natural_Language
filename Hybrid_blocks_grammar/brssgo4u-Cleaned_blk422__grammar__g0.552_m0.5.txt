vakṣasyakṣṇośca pārśve karatalayugale kaustubhābhāṃ dayāṃ ca sītāṃ kodaṇḍadīkṣāmabhayavarayutāṃ vīkṣya rāmāṅgasaṅgaḥ ।
svasyāḥ kva syāditīyaṃ hṛdi kṛtamananā bhāṣyaratnaprabhākhyā, svātmānandaikalubdhā raghuvaracaraṇāmbhojayugmaṃ prapannā ।।
