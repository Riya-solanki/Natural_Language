ataḥ agnihotrādeḥ nityātkarmaṇaḥ anyāpi hi asti sādhukṛtyā yā phalam abhisaṃdhāya kriyate tasyā eṣa viniyoga ukta ekeṣāṃ śākhinām asuhṛdaḥ sādhukṛtyāmupayanti' iti ।
tasyā eva ca idam aghavat aśleṣavināśanirūpaṇam itarasya api evam aśleṣa iti ।
tathā jātīyakasya kāmyasya karmaṇo vidyāṃ prati anupakārakatve saṃpratipattiḥ ubhayoḥ api jaiminibādarāyaṇayoḥ ācāryayoḥ ।।
