sāmānyena sarvalohānāṃ śodhanamāraṇamāha sarva ityādi ।।
sarvo loho dhātuvargaḥ śudhyati mṛtaśca bhavati punaḥ rajyati ca ।।
kena bhṛśaṃ atyarthaṃ yathā syāttathā mākṣikadaradena tāpyahiṅgulena kṛtvā yo vāpaḥ galiteṣu loheṣu mākṣikadaradaprakṣepaṇaṃ tasmāt suragopasaṃnibha indragopasadṛśaḥ sarvo loho bhavet ।।
vā śulbaṃ tāmraṃ mākṣikadaradavāpena suragopasannibhaṃ syāt vā gandhena gandhakavāpena mṛtamapyeva syādityarthaḥ ।।
