kiṃ puṃvākyānāṃ sāpekṣatā bhūtārthatvena, āho pauruṣeyatvena ? yadi bhūtārthatvena tataḥ pratyakṣādīnāmapi parasparāpekṣatvenāprāmāṇyaprasaṅgaḥ; tānyapi hi bhūtārthānyeva ।
atha puruṣabuddhipurvakatayā puṃvākyaṃ sāpekṣam, evaṃ tarhi atatpūrvakāṇāṃ vedāntānāṃ bhūtārthānāmapi nāprāmāṇyaṃ pratyakṣādīnāmiva niyatendriyaliṅgādijanmanām ।
