sarvaṃ vastu sukhāvahaṃ tava punaḥ kautaskutīyaṃ vyathā kasmād evam udīryate jagad idaṃ yat tanmayaṃ bhāṣase ।
hetus savyabhicāra eṣa virahāt dṛṣṭāntayor ity ayaṃ saṃlāpas saha lakṣmaṇena viyujaḥ sītāpateḥ pātu vaḥ ।।
