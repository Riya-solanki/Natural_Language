[] upādhi-kṛta-hāni-vṛddhiṃ vinā kṛta-sneha-samṛddhi-maya-dehatayā gambhīra-svaratayā ca payaḥ-payodhir ivāyaṃ vrajādhīśvaras tu tasya sarva-stutasya vaṃśān kaṃsa-kṛta-dhvaṃsān anuśocan, karma-vāda-rocanayā dhairyaṃ saṃvarmayann ātmanaś ca tasya ca śarma sūnṛtāmṛta-bhṛta-santarpaṇaṃ kṛtavān ।
