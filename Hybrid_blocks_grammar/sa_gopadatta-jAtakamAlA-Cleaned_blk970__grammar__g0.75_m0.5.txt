garbhādhāraṇayantraṇām avigaṇayyāpāyikāṃ mātaraṃ satkārātiśayakṣamaṃ ca pitaraṃ snehadravādhyāśayam । yad viśrambhaniratyayāṃś ca suhṛdo ghnanti kṣatādhyāśayāḥ krodhasyaiva vipākadāruṇatarās tā vṛttayaḥ sādhavaḥ ।।
