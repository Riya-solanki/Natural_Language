kathaṃ punaridamiṣṭādikāriṇāṃ svakarmaphalopabhogāya raṃhaṇaṃ pratijñāyate ।
yāvatā teṣāṃ dhūmapratīkena vartmanā candramasamadhirūḍhānāmannabhāvaṃ darśayati- aeṣa somo rājā taddevānāmannaṃ te devā bhakṣayanti' iti ate candraṃ prāpyānnaṃ bhavanti tāṃstatra devā yathā somaṃ rājānamapyāyasvāpakṣīyasvetyevamenāṃstatra bhakṣayanti' iti ca samānaviṣayaṃ śrutyantaram ।
naca vyāghrādibhiriva devairbhakṣyamāṇānāmupabhogaḥ saṃbhavatīti ।
