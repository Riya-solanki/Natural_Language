rāgadveṣasukhāsukhodayalayāhaṅkāradainyādayo ye bhāvāḥ pravibhānti viśvavapuṣo bhinnasvabhāvā na te ।
vyaktiṃ paśyasi yasya yasya sahasā tattattadekātmatāsaṃvidrūpam avekṣya kiṃ na ramase tadbhāvanānirbharaḥ ।।
