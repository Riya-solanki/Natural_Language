{Pkt}tado a deva pajjalantiṃ sattiṃ pekkhia vialiaṃ aṅgarāassa hatthādo sasaraṃ dhaṇuṃ hiaādo vīrasulaho ucchāho ṇaaṇādo bāpphasalilaṃ vaaṇādo rasidaṃ । hasidaṃ a dhaṇaṃjaeṇa siṃhaṇādaṃ viṇādidaṃ viodaleṇa dukkalaṃ dukkalaṃ tti ākkandidaṃ kurubaleṇa ।
