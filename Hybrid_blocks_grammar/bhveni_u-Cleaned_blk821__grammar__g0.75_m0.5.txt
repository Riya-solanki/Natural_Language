SktCh tataśca deva tathāvidhasya putrasya darśanena saṃgalitamaśrujalamujjhitvānavekṣitaparapraharaṇābhiyogena svāmināṅgarājenābhiyukto dhanaṃjayaḥ । taṃ ca sutavadhāmarṣoddīpitaparākramaṃ vimuktajīvitāśaṃ tathā parākramantaṃ prekṣya bhīmanakulasahadevapāñcālapramukhairantarito dhanaṃjayasya rathavaraḥ ।
