-mā bhūdasataḥ śaktikāryatvam, ayogyatvāt, śaktijñāpyaṃ punaḥ kasmānnabhavediti cet śaktijñāpyatvaṃ śaktiniṣṭhajñāpakatānirūpitajñāpyatāśālitvaṃ vaktavyam, tacca śakyāśrayajñānādanyajñānānupalabdherasaṃbhavītyāha-jñānāntarānupalabdheriti ।
