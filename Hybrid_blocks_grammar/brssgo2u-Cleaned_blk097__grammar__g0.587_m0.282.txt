netyucyate ।
asa vā eṣa mahājana ātmājaro amaro amṛto abhayo brahma' asa eṣa neti netyātmā', aasthūlamanaṇu' ityādyābhyaḥ sarvavikriyāpratiṣedhaśrutibhyo brahmaṇaḥ kūṭasthatvāvagamāt ।
nahyekasya brahmaṇaḥ pariṇāmadharmatvaṃ tadrahitatvaṃ ca śkyaṃ pratipattum ।
