bhedaḥ nirvedhabhāgīyaprayogamārgasthaḥ, kṣāntijñānasaṃgṛhītadarśanamārgasthaḥ, paraśca prābandhikabhāvanāmārgastha iti trividho bhavati avaivartikabodhisattvasaṃgha iti trayo asya bhedā bhavanti ।
