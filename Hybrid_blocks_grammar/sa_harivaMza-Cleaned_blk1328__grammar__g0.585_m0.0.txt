na śastreṇa na cāstreṇa giriṇā pādapena vā 
na śuṣkeṇa na cārdreṇa syān na cānyena me vadhaḥ ।। 
pāṇiprahāreṇaikena sabhṛtyabalavāhanam । yo māṃ nāśayituṃ śaktaḥ sa me mṛtyur bhaviṣyati । nākāśe vā na bhūmau vā rātrau vā divase api vā । nāntar vā na bahir vāpi syād vadho me pitāmaha । paśubhir vā mṛgendrair vā pakṣibhir vā sarīsṛpaiḥ । 
bhaveyam aham evārkaḥ somo vāyur hutāśanaḥ 
salilaṃ cāntarikṣaṃ ca nakṣatrāṇi diśo daśa ।।
