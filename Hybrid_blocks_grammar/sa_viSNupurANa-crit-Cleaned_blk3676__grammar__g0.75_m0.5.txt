kāśyasya kāśīrājas tato dīrghatamāḥ putro abhavat । dhanvantaris tu dīrghatamaso abhavat । sa hi saṃsiddhakāryakaraṇaḥ sakalasaṃbhūtiṣv aśeṣajñānavid bhagavatā nārāyaṇena cātītasaṃbhūtāv asmai varo dattaḥ ।।
