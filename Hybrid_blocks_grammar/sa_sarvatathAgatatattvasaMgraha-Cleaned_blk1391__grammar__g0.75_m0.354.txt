athātra karmamaṇḍale vajrakarmamudrā bhavanti ।
oṃ vajrasatvasiddhijñānasamaye huṃ jjaḥ ।।
oṃ vajrākarṣaṇakarmajñānasamaye huṃ jjaḥ ।।oṃ vajraratirāgakarmajñānasamaye huṃ jjaḥ ।।oṃ vajrasādhukarmajñānasamaye huṃ jjaḥ ।। ।।oṃ vajrabhṛkuṭī vaśīkuru huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajrasūryamaṇḍale vaśīkuru huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajradhvajāgrakeyūre vaśīkuru huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajrāṭṭahāse vaśīkuru huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajrapadmarāge rāgaya huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajratīkṣṇarāge rāgaya huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajramaṇḍalarāge rāgaya huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajravāgrāge rāgaya huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajrakarmasamaye pūjaya huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajrakavacabandhe rakṣaya huṃ ।। ।।oṃ vajrayakṣiṇi māraya vajradaṃṣṭrāyā bhinda hṛdayamamukasya huṃ phaṭ ।। ।।oṃ vajrakarmamuṣṭi siddhya siddhya huṃ phaṭ ।। ।।
