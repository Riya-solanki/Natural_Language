ityādāvabhinayādayaḥ ।
itthaṃ saṃyogādibhirarthāntarābhidhāyakatve nivārite apyanekārthasya śabdasya yat kvacidarthāntarapratipādanaṃ tatra nābhidhā niyamanāttasyāḥ । na ca lakṣaṇā mukhyārthabādhādyabhāvāt । api tvañjanaṃ vyañjanameva vyāpāraḥ । yathā
