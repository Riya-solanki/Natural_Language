tṛtīyādiṣu bhāṣitapuṃskaṃ puṃvadgālavasya ।।
pravṛttinimittaikye bhāṣitapuṃskamigantaṃ klībaṃ puṃvadvā ṭādāvaci । sudhiyā, sudhinetyādi ।। madhunī । he madho, he madhu ।। sulu । sulūni ।। dhātṛṇī । he dhātaḥ, he dhātṛ ।।
