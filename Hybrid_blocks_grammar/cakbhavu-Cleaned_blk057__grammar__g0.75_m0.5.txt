Viv svaprabodhaḥ svavimarśa eva ambaram anāvaraṇatvād vyoma ata evodāram udbhaṭaṃ vikasat sarvātmatayā prasarat sollāsitakāryācchādakatayā vastraṃ tena gharṣaṇaṃ pratyāvṛttinayena svāvadhānaṃ vidhāya, prāpte mahāsāmarasye, vigaliteṣu dvaitodayamahāsaṃtrāsaḍambhareṣv akhaṇḍitasvāvabodhanirmatsarabalīn sarvatodikkaṃ kṣipāmaḥ ।।
