vidhyantaramāha madhvityādi ।।
etaiḥ pūrvoktair yogaiḥ madhusahitaiḥ kṣaudrayutaiḥ tārābhraṃ rūpyagaganaṃ milati ।।
ca punastāpyakanakaṃ mākṣikasvarṇaṃ idaṃ dvandvaṃ ca milati ।।
na kevalaṃ pūrvoktayogair milati punaretair eraṇḍatailaṭaṅkaṇakaṅkuṣṭhaśilendragopaiśca eraṇḍatailaṃ vātārisnehaḥ ṭaṅkaṇaṃ saubhāgyaṃ kaṅkuṣṭhaṃ viraṅgaṃ śilā manohvā indragopako jīvaviśeṣaḥ etaiśca madhusahitaiḥ kṛtvā dvandvaṃ milatītyavaśyam ।।
