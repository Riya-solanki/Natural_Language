tiṣṭhetkopavaśātprabhāvapihitā dīrghaṃ na sā kupyati svargāyotpatitā bhavenmayi punarbhāvārdramasyā manaḥ ।
tāṃ hartuṃ vibudhadviṣo api na ca me śaktāḥ purovartinoṃ sā cātyantamagocaraṃ nayanayoryāteti ko ayaṃ vidhiḥ ।।
