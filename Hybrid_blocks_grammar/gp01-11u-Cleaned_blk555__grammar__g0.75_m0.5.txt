[] atha tenāvrajita-sadeśaḥ śrīmān vraja-maheśaḥ sahasā mahasāvṛtatayā sābhyutthānam utthāya nyāya-paramaḥ, kṛta-tad-abhigamaḥ svam anujam anuraktaḥ pariṣvaktavān pariṣvaktaś cānena na tu kañcit kaścin natavān, jātāv ekasya jyāyastvam anyasya tu jātāv iti । na ca kevalam etad eva kāraṇatām avalambate, api tu paraspara-praṇayātiśayaś ca, yenānyan nānusandhātuṃ śakyate ।
