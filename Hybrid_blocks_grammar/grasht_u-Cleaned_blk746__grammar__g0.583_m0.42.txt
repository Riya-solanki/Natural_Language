anyadyantramāha kṛtvetyādi ।।
pūrvavaddīrghāṃ dhūrtakusumasaṃkāśāṃ dhattūrapuṣpasaṃkāśāṃ pūrvayantranalikāyāḥ sthāne evaṃvidhāṃ ṣaḍaṅgulāṃ nalikāṃ kuryād iti vyaktiḥ ।।
mūṣāpi adho vilagnā nalikāyāstalabhāge mūṣā vilagnā saṃlagnā kāryā ।।
sā ca mṛdā mṛtsnayā lepyā ।।
punarapi aparā sūkṣmā nālikā saptāṅgulā saptāṅgulaparimāṇā sudṛḍhā manoharakaṭhinā kāryā yathā madhye ṣaḍaṅgulanālikāntaḥ praviśati tadvattathā kāryā ।।
kiṃbhūtā dṛḍhamukhā dṛḍhaṃ mukhaṃ yasyāḥ sā evaṃrūpā tasmin saṃsiddhe yantre sāraṇatailānvitaḥ sūtaḥ kṣiptaḥ san madanaruddhamukhaḥ kāryaḥ madanena sikthakena ruddhaṃ mudritaṃ mukhaṃ yasya saḥ ।।
tadanu tatpaścāt hemakoṣṭhikayā hemno yā koṣṭhī eva koṣṭhikā tayā hema svarṇaṃ pradrāvya drāvayitvā tatra kṣipet ityadhyāhāryam ।।
tasminyantre madhye'ntaḥ nalikāgraṃ nalikāyāḥ saptāṅgulāyā agrabhāgaṃ kṣiptvā adhomukhīṃ kuryāt punarūrdhvaṃ bhārākrāntāṃ kuryāt ।।
iti kṛte sati rasaḥ sarati hemnā milati na saṃdehaḥ niyatamityarthaḥ ।।
