yac ca tat kathitaṃ pūrvaṃ bāṇaṃ prati mahāsuram 
tad ahaṃ śrotum icchāmi vistareṇa tapodhana ।। 
kathaṃ ca devadevasya putratvam asuro gataḥ । yo abhiguptaḥ svayaṃ brahmañ śaṃkareṇa mahātmanā । sahavāsaṃ gateneha sagaṇena guhena ca ।। baler balavataḥ putro jyeṣṭho bhrātṛśatasya yaḥ । vṛto bāhusahasreṇa divyāstraśatadhāriṇā । asaṃkhyaiś ca mahākāyair māyāśatasahair balaiḥ । māyāśatabalair daityair amarādibhir arcitaḥ । 
vāsudevena sa kathaṃ bāṇaḥ saṃkhye parājitaḥ 
saṃrabdho dvaṃdvayuddhārthī jīvan muktaḥ kathaṃ ca saḥ ।।
