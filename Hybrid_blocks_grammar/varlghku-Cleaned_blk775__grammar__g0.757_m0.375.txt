śabdavairakalahābhrakaṇvameghebhyaḥ karaṇe ।।
ebhyaḥ karmabhyaḥ karotyarthe kyaṅ syāt । śabdaṃ karoti śabdāyate ।। tatkaroti tadācaṣṭe; iti ṇic ।। prātipadikāddhātvarthe bahulamiṣṭhavacca । prātipadikāddhātvarthe ṇic syāt, iṣṭhe yathā prātipadikasya puṃvadbhāva-rabhāva-ṭilopa- vinmatublopa-yaṇādilopa-prasthasphādyādeśa-bhasaṃjñāstadvaṇṇāvapi syuḥ । ghaṭaṃ karotyācaṣṭe vā ghaṭayati ।।
