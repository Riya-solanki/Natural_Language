-dhātvarthatāvacchedakaphalaśālitvaṃ karmatvamityukte gacchatītyādau saṃyogaphalasya grāma iva caitre api samavetatvāt, caitraścaivaṃ gacchatīti prayogāpattiḥ, tasmātparasamavetakriyāphalaśālitvameva karmatvamityarthaḥ ।
