[] atha vana-kula-gokulābhyāṃ mithaḥ sukham abhimukham āgatayor mahatāṃ samūhayor mahodadhi-tulyayoḥ saṅgamaḥ saṃvṛttaḥ; yathā nityam eva tathānubhavinām api divaukasāṃ camatkṛtir ajāyata; yatra śrī-govinda eva svayam indavati sma, svayam eva ca veṇu-śikṣayā dheṇūḥ pṛthak pṛthag avātastambhat ।
