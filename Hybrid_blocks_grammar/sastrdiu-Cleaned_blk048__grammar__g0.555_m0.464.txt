athārthasvarūpasmārakaiḥ padairanvitajñānamanamīyate, tataḥ kāryavācake apyasiddheranvitābhidhānaṃ na syāt ।
kiñca prathamameva kāryavyabhicāraḥ śakyo anagantum ।
yadā hi putraste jāta ityuktasya kasyācinmukhavikāsaṃ pārśvasthaḥ paśyati tadā harṣaheturanena śabdena pratipādita iti harṣahetubhūtasiddhārthapratipādane śabdasya sāmarthyaṃ kalpayati ।
yadyapi ca tadānīmarthaviśeṣo na jñāyate harṣahetūnāṃ bahūnāṃ saṃbhavāttathāpi siddhārthatā tāvadavagatā, tāvatāpi kārye vyabhicāraḥ siddhaḥ ।
kiñca bahuśaḥ putrajanane sati evaṃ śabdaprayogādanyeṣu harṣahetuṣvaprayogāt putrajananarūpārthaviśeṣo api sujñāna
