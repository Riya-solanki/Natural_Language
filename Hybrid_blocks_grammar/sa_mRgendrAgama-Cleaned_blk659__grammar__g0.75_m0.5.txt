[nirghātolkāmahīkampapaścāccāpeṣu] nirghātaḥ āntarikṣaḥ śabdaḥ। ulkā daṇḍākāratejorūpā। mahīkampaḥ bhūmikampaḥ। paścāccāpaḥ vāruṇyāṃ diśīndradhanuḥ। eteṣu [vāsaraḥ] ekavāsaro anadhyāyaḥ। sandhyāstanitadigdāhapariveṣopaleṣu ca] sandhyayormeghagarjitaśrutau, digdāhe, sūryapariveṣadarśane, antarikṣād upalavarṣe caśabdādekāhameva।।
