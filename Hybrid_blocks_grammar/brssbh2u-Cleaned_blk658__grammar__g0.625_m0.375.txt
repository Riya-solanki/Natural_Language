ucyate- naitadevam ।
anaṃśitvamapi hi jīvasyābhedavādinyaḥ śrutayaḥ pratipādayanti- atatsṛṣṭvā tadevānuprāviśat', anānyo ato asti draṣṭā', amṛtyoḥ sa mṛtyumāpnoti ya iha nāneva paśyati', atattvamasi' aahaṃ brahmāsmi' ityevañjātīyakāḥ ।
