lakṣmīr dānaphalā śrutaṃ śamaphalaṃ pāṇiḥ surārcāphalaś ceṣṭā dharmaphalā parārtiharaṇakrīḍāphalaṃ jīvitam ।
vāṇī satyaphalā jagatsukhaphalasphītā prabhāvonnatir bhavyānāṃ bhavaśānticintanaphalā bhūtyai bhavaty eva dhiḥ ।।
