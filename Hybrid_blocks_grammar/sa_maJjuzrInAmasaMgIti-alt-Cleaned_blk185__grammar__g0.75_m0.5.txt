evaṃ vajrapāṇe vajradhara aprameyaguṇasamanvāgato asau mantramukhacaryācārī bhaviṣyati । anyaiś cāprameyair evaṃprakārair evaṃjātīyair guṇagaṇaiḥ samanvāgato bhaviṣyati । acirād eva vajrapāṇe vajradhara paramārthanāmasaṃgītisaṃdhārakapuruṣapuṃgavaḥ susaṃbhṛtapuṇyajñānasaṃbhāraḥ ksiprataraṃ buddhaguṇān samudānīyānuttarāṃ samyaksaṃbodhim abhisaṃbhotsyate । analpakalyāṇaparinirvāṇadharmaḥ sarvasatvānām anuttaradharmadeśako adhiṣṭhito daśadiksaddharmadundubhir dharmarāja iti ।
