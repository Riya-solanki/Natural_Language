śryukaḥ kiti ।।
śriña ekāca ugantācca gitkitoriṇ na । paramapi svaratyādivikalpaṃ bādhitvā purastātpratiṣedha kāṇḍārambha sāmarthyādanena niṣedhe prāpte krādiniyamānnityamiṭ । dudhuviva । adhāvīt, adhaviṣṭa, adhoṣṭa । adhaviṣyat, adhoṣyat । adhaviṣyatām, adhoṣyatām । adhaviṣyata, adhoṣyata ।।
