yo līlātālavṛnto rahasi nirupadhiryaśca kilīpradīpaḥ kopakrīḍāsu yo astraṃ daśanakṛtarujo yo adharasyaikasekaḥ ।
ākalpe darpaṇa yaḥ śramaśayanavidho yaśca gaṇḍopadhānaṃ devyāḥ sa vyāpadaṃ vo haratu harajaṭākandalīpuṣpaminduḥ ।।
