athātra maṇiguhyamaṇḍale yathāvatkarma kṛtvā, śiṣyāṃ praveya, brūyāt । "na tvayā kasyacidayaṃ nayo vaktavyaḥ । mā te mahādāri dyamakālakriyā narakapatanaṃ syād", ityuktvā svamaṇisamayajñānānyutpādayet ।
