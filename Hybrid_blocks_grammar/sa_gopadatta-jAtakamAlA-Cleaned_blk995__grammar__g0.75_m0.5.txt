yataś ca te mahāsattvāḥ svaṃ svaṃ vādam abhiniviṣṭā na kathāparyavasānam āsādayanti tato bodhisattvam upetya nyāyataḥ praṇamyemam artham ūcuḥ । bhagavan kṣudrāgakrodhamaraṇabhayānāṃ kiṃ nu duḥkham । kasya vāsmākaṃ subhāṣitam ity athainān sa munivaryo abravīt ।
