drumilas tv evam uktas tu jagāmākāśam eva tu 
tenaiva rathamukhyena divyenāpratigāminā । 
jagāma ca purīṃ dīnā mātā tad ahar eva me ।। 
mām evam uktvā bhagavān nārado munisattamaḥ । dīpyamānas tapovīryāt sākṣād agnir iva jvalan ।। vallakīṃ vādyamānas tu saptasvaravimūrchitām । gāyano lakṣyavīthīṃ ca jagāma brahmaṇo antikam ।। śṛṇuṣvedaṃ mahāmātra nibodha vacanaṃ mama । tathyaṃ coktaṃ nāradena traikālajñena dhīmatā ।। ahaṃ balena vīryeṇa nayena vinayena ca । prabhāveṇaiva śauryeṇa tejasā vikrameṇa ca ।। satyena caiva dānena nānyo asti sadṛśaḥ pumān । viditvā sarvam ātmānaṃ vacanaṃ śraddadhāmy aham । 
kṣetrajo ahaṃ sutas tv evam ugrasenasya hastipa 
mātāpitṛbhyāṃ saṃtyaktaḥ sthāpitaḥ svena tejasā ।।
