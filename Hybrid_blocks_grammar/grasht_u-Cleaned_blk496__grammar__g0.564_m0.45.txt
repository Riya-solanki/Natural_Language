taccāha kṣārairityādi ।।
vimalaṃ raupyamākṣikaṃ ādau prathamaṃ kṣāraiḥ svarjikādibhiḥ snehaistailaiḥ kaṅguṇyādīnāṃ bhāvitaṃ kuryāt paścādamlena jambīrādinā bhāvitaṃ kuryāt evaṃvidhaṃ kṛtaṃ sat śudhyati ।।
tathā tenaiva vidhinā rasakaṃ kharparakaṃ śudhyati daradaṃ hiṅgulaṃ caivaṃ mākṣikamapyeva śudhyati ।।
