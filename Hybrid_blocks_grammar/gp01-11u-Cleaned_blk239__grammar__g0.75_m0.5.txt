[] atha rāmānujaṃ hitavatī rohiṇy abhihitavatī, "tāta, yaśodāmāta, bālyād eva lālya-bhāvān mātur upadeśaṃ jātu na ca manyase । mama tu taṃ na matāntaram ātanoṣi । tataḥ sakṛd api mama nideśam asakṛd iva manyasva । mātur manas-tāpa-vistārān nistārāya nija-vadanāṃśu-sudhāṃ vistāraya tvaritam" iti ।
