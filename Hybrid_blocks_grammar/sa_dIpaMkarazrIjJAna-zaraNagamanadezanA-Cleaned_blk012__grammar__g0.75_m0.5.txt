tatra svabhāvo hrī-apatrapāyuktatā avijñaptpādaśca । tatra mānaṃ tu svīkaraṇam, śaraṇakaraṇaṃ vijñaptyutpādaśca । tatra nayastu śaraṇagamanavidhiḥ । sa ca guroravagantavyaḥ । tatra śikṣā sādhāraṇī asādhāraṇī ca gurupādebhyo jñeyā । tatra niruktistu dāsabhāvaḥ taditarasvāminamananviṣya śaraṇagamanam ।
