śobhetyayaṃ śabdaḥ sādhuḥ । śubha śumbha śobhārthau iti śubherbhidāderākṛtigaṇatvādaṅ siddha eva । guṇapratiṣedhābhāvastu nipātyata iti । śobhārthāvityatraikadeśe, kiṃ śobhā, āhosvicchobha iti viśeṣāvagatirācaryaparamparopadeśāditi ।।
