sa tatprabhavānveṣaṇakṛtamatiḥ svabuddhyā vimamarśavyaktamayaṃ taruvara ito nātidūre nadītīrasaṃniviṣṭaśca yasyedaṃ phalaṃ tathā hyanupahatavarṇagandharasamadīrghakālasalilasaṃparkādaparikṣatamajarjaraṃ ca yataḥ śakyamasya prabhavo adhigantumiti niścayamupetya tadrasatṛṣṇayā ākṛṣyamāṇo viramya jalakrīḍāyāḥ samyak puravare sve rakṣāvidhānaṃ saṃdiśya yātrāsajjena mahatā balakāyena parivṛtastāṃ nadīmanusasāra । krameṇa cotsādayan saśvāpadagaṇāni vanagahanāni samanubhavaṃ ścatrāṇi rasāntarāṇi paśyannakṛtrimaramaṇīyaśobhāni vanāntarāṇi saṃtrāsayan paṭaharasitairavanyagajamṛgān mānuṣajanadurgamaṃ tasya vanaspateḥ samīpamupajagāma ।
