naitadasti ।
agāyatrī vā idaṃ sarvam' iti gāyatrīmupakramya tāmeva bhūtapṛthivīśarīrahṛdayavākyapramāṇabhedairvyakhyāya asaiṣā catuṣpādā ṣaḍvidhā gāyatrī tadetadṛcābhyanuktaṃ tāvānasya mahimā' iti tasyāmeva vyākhyātarūpāyāṃ gāyatryāmudāhṛto mantraḥ kathamakasmādbrahma catuṣpādabhidadhyāt ।
yo api tatra ayadvai tadbrahma' iti brahmaśabdaḥ so api chandasaḥ prakṛtatvācchandoviṣaya eva aya etāmevaṃ brahmopaniṣadaṃ veda' ityatra hi vedopaniṣadamiti vyācakṣate, tasmācchandobhidhānānna brahmaṇaḥ prakṛtatvamiticet ।
