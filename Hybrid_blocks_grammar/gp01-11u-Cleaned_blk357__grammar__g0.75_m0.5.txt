[] yathā-atha sarva-śruti-purāṇādi-kṛta-praśaṃsasya vṛṣṇi-vaṃśasya vataṃsaḥ śrī-devamīḍha-nāmā parama-guṇa-dhāmā madhurāmadhyāsāmāsa । tasya cāryāṇāṃ śiromaṇer bhāryā-dvayam āsīt । prathamā dvitīya-varṇā, dvitīyā tu tṛtīya-varṇeti । tayoś ca krameṇa yathāvad āhvayaṃ putra-dvayaṃ prathamaṃ babhūva śūraḥ parjanya iti । tatra śūrasya śrī-vasudevādayaḥ samudayanti sma । śrīmān parjanyas tu mātṛvad varṇa-saṅkaraḥ iti nyāyena vaiśyatām evāviśya gavām evaiśyaṃ vaśyaṃ cakāra । bṛhad-vana eva ca vāsam ācacāra । sa cāyaṃ bālyād eva brāhmaṇa-darśaṃ pūjayati, manoratha-pūraṃ deyāni varṣati, vaiṣṇava-vedaṃ snihyati, yāvad vedaṃ vyavaharati, yāvaj-jīvaṃ harim arcayati sma । tasya mātur vaṃśaś ca vyāpta-sarva-diśāṃ viśāṃ vataṃsatayā paraṃ śaṃsanīyaḥ । ābhīra-viśeṣatayā sadbhir udīraṇād eṣa hi viśeṣaṃ bhajate sma ।
