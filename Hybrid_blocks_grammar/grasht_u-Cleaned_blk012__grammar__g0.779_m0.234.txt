suretyādi ।।
tasmāt sūtarājāt anyo dvitīya pavitratara atiśayena pavitraḥ kaḥ kila śrūyate na ko api ।।
yasmāddhetoḥ apīti niścayena asādhyaṃ rujaṃ śamayati sarvarūpānvitamasādhyaṃ divyauṣadhibhir api karmavipākenāpi sādhyate tacca kiṃviśiṣṭam asādhyaṃ suretyādiḥ surāś ca guravaś ca gāvaś ca dvijāś ca teṣāṃ yā hiṃsā hananam avamānanaṃ vātād utpanno yaḥ pāpakalāpo duṣkṛtapaṭala etasmād udbhavatīti ।।
