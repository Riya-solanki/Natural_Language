tan nūnam asya sakāśe sa mahāmaṇiḥ syamantakākhyas tiṣṭhati । tasya hy evaṃvidhāḥ prabhāvāḥ śrūyante । ayam api ca yajñād anantaram anyat kratvantaraṃ anantaram ca tasmād yajñāntaraṃ yajatīti । alpopādānaṃ cāsyā saṃśayam atrāsau varamaṇis tiṣṭhatīti kṛtādhyavasāyo anyat prayojanam uddiśya sakalayādavasamājam ātmageha evācīkarat ।।
