syasicsīyuṭtāsiṣu bhāvakarmaṇorupadeśe ajjhanagrahadṛśāṃ vā ciṇvadiṭ ca ।।
upadeśe yo ac tadantānāṃ hanādīnāṃ ca ciṇīvāṅgakāryaṃ vā syātsyādiṣu bhāvakarmaṇorgamyamānayoḥ syādīnāmiḍāgamaśca । ciṇvadbhāvapakṣe ayamiṭ । ciṇvadbhāvād vṛddhiḥ । bhāviṣyate, bhaviṣyate । abhūyata । bhāviṣīṣṭa, bhaviṣīṣṭa ।।
