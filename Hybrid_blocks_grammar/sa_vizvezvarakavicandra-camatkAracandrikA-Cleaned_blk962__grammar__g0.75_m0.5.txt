atra prathamārdhe kṣudraśaṅkhasamutpattisthānajalāśayānāmupamānākathanādeva tadupameyānāṃ pratīyamānasādṛsyānāṃ kṣudrakṣudrakṣatriyotpattisthānānāmanyanarapālavaṃśānāmuktteḥ siddhāvupamānadvāreṇaivopameya garhā pratīyate । uttarārdhe ca dakṣimaṇāvartaśaṅkhajanakakṣīrasāgareṇaivopamānena kathitasādṛśyasya nāyakāva taramapariṇatabhāgadheyasya recarlavaṃśasyoktisiddhā vupamānamukhenaivopameyasya ślāghā pratīyate । tadiyaṃ garhāślāghāsaṃkaravatī samāsoktiḥ । evamanye bhedā lakṣaṇīyāḥ । eṣāṃ vikṛtā api saṃbhavatanti ।
