pūjapūjakapūjyabhedasaraṇiḥ keyaṃ kathānuttare saṃkrāmaḥ kila kasya kena vidadhe ko vā praveśakramaḥ ।
māyeyam na cidadvayāt paratayā bhinnāpy aho vartate sarvaṃ svānubhavasvabhāvavimalaṃ cintāṃ vṛthā mā kṛthāḥ ।।
