tṛtīyāsaptamyorbahulam ।।
adantādavyayībhāvāttṛtīyāsaptamyorbahulamambhāvaḥ syāt । adhigopam, adhigopena, adhigope vā । kṛṣṇasya samīpam upakṛṣṇam । madrāṇāṃ samṛddhiḥ sumadram । yavanānāṃ vyṛddhirduryavanam । makṣikāṇāmabhāvo nirmakṣikam । himasyātyayo atihimam । nidrā saṃprati na yujyata ityatinidram । hariśabdasya prakāśa itihari । viṣṇoḥ paścādanuviṣṇu । yogyatāvīpsāpadārthānativṛttisādṛśyāni yathārthāḥ । rūpasya yogyamanurūpam । arthamarthaṃ prati prartṣatham । śaktimanatikramya yathāśakti ।।
