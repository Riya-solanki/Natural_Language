tatpratyarthitayā vṛto na tu kṛtaḥ samyak svatantro bhayāt svasthas tān na nipātayed iti yathākāmaṃ na sampoṣitaḥ 
saṃśuṣyan pṛṣadaṃśa eṣa kurutāṃ mūkaḥ sthito apy atra kiṃ gehe kiṃ bahunā adhunā gṛhapateś caurrāś caranty ākhavaḥ ।।
