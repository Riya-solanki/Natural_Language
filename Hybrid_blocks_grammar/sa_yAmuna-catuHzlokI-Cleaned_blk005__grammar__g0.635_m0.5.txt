śāntānantamahāvibhūti paraṃ yad brahma rūpaṃ harer mūrtaṃ brahma tato api tat priyataraṃ rūpaṃ yad atyadbhutam ।
yāny anyāni yathāsukhaṃ viharato rūpāṇi sarvāṇi tāny āhuḥ svair anurūparūpavibhavair gāḍhopagūḍhāni te ।।
