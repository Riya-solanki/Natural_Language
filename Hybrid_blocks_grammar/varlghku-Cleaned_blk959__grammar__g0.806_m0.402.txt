prātipadikārthaliṅgaparimāṇavacanamātre prathamā ।।
niyatopasthitikaḥ prātipadikārthaḥ । mātraśabdasya pratyekaṃ yogaḥ । prātipadikārthamātre liṅgamātrādyādhikye parimāṇamātre saṃkhyāmātre ca prathamā syāt । prātipadikārthamātre - uccaiḥ । nīcaiḥ । śrīḥ । liṅgamātre - taṭaḥ, taṭī, taṭam । parimāṇamātre - droṇo vrīhiḥ । vacanaṃ saṃkhyā । ekaḥ, dvau, bahavaḥ ।।
