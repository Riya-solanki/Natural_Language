kṣiprabhāvanādṛṣṭyā ekaḥ prayogaḥ, yathā dharmataiṣā yasmānmahāratnānāṃ bahavo antarāyāḥ sambhavanti, tasmāt paramaratnasvabhāvāyā mātuḥ saṃvatsareṇa bahvantarāyatve api likhanaṃ śīghrameveti avadhiprayogaḥ ।
