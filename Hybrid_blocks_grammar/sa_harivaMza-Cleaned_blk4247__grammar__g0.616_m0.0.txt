so atha saṃpūjitaḥ pūjyaḥ śūrais tair yadupuṃgavaiḥ 
kare saṃspṛśya govindaṃ viveśa mahad āsanam ।। 
tataḥ prāha mahābāhur āhuko nṛpatir munim । mune vācaya kiṃ cid vai purāṇaṃ paramottamam ।। tatheti hariṇā bhūpa harivaṃśasya pustakam । nikṣiptam āsane puṇye nārado avācayat tadā ।। śrotavyo apy uddhavaś cāśu bhūtvā maunam upāśritaḥ । sukhopaviṣṭas tūṣṇīṃ tāṃ nārado avācayat kathāṃ । 
sukhopaviṣṭas tān vṛṣṇīn upaviṣṭān uvāca ha 
āgataṃ śakravacanāj jānīdhvaṃ māṃ nararṣabhāḥ ।।
