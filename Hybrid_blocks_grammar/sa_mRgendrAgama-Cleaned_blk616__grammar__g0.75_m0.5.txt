śaivāḥ kāmikādayo vakṣyamāṇā daśa bhedāḥ। raudrā vijayādayo aṣṭādaśa। ete [ūrdhvake] ūrdhvasrotasi [mahābhedāḥ] bhedāḥ। tatra śaivāḥ śivabhaṭṭārakeṇa upadiṣṭāḥ। raudrāstu [śivāviṣṭaiḥ] śivādhiṣṭhai rudraiḥ [udgīrṇāḥ। na svabuddhitaḥ] na svamanīṣikayā।।
