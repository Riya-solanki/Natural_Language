kiṃ citraṃ yadi veda śāstrakuśalo vipro bhavetpaṇḍitaḥ kiṃ citraṃ yadi daṇḍanītikuśalo rājā bhaveddhārmikaḥ ।
kiṃ citraṃ yadi rūpayauvanavatī sādhvī bhavetkāminī taccitraṃ yadi nirdhano api puruṣaḥ pāpaṃ na kuryātkracit ।।
