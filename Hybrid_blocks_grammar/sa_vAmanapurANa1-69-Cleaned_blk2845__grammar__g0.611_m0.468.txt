trinayana hara bhava śaṅkara umāpate jīmūtaketo śuhāgṛha śmaśānanirata bhūtivilepana sūlapāṇe paśupate gopate tatpuruṣasattma namo namaste ।
itthaṃ stutaḥ kavivarema haro atha bhaktyā prīto varaṃ varaya dadmi tavetyuvāca ।
sa prāha devavara dehi varaṃ mamādya yadvai tavaiva jaṭharāt pratinirgamo astu ।।
