[] prakaṭaṃ covāca - atra khalv idam asya śrī-vraja-rāja-tanūjasya rahasyam udbhāvayato mama samatikramaḥ svayam amunaiva bāḍhaṃ soḍhavyaḥ । tathā hi asmin sarvato labdhātirekakā saṃsiddhiḥ khalv ekā vartate । yad atikrānta-sarveha-sneha-maya-hṛdaya eva sadā vartamānaḥ snighdatā-digdha-janānāṃ bhāva-mudrayā parokṣaṃ kṛtayāpi svahṛdi pratibimbatatayā mudrito bhavati । asya svarūpeṇāvirbhāvaś ca sneha-maya-sphūrti-pūrti-vaśībhāvata eva sarvathā, na tv anyathā । putratayāvirbhāve ca bījaṃ pitṛ-bhāva-maya-sneha eva nānyeṣām ivānyat । jāte ca kutracit putratayāvirbhāve tat-tat-sambandha-maya-sneha-kṛta-caya-sphūrtir eva tathā tathā bhāvenāvirbhāve nibandhanaṃ bhavati ।
