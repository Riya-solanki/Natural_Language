evaṃ prāpte abhidhīyate- jño nityacaitanyo ayamātmāta eva, yasmādeva notpadyate parameva brahmāvikṛtamupādhisaṃparkājjīvabhāvenāvatiṣṭhate ।
parasya hi brahmaṇaścaitanyasvarūpatvamāmnātam- avijñānamānandaṃ brahma', asatyaṃ jñānamantaṃ brahma', aanantaro abāhyaḥ kṛtsnaḥ prajñānaghana eva', ityādiṣu śrutiṣu ।
tadeva cetparaṃ brahma jīvastasmājjīvasyāpi nityacaitanyasvarūpatvamagnyauṣṇyaprakāśavaditi gamyate ।
vijñānamayaprakriyāyāṃ ca śrutayo bhavanti- asuptaḥ asuptānabhicākaśīti' aatrāyaṃ puruṣaḥ svayañjyotirbhavati' iti, anahi vijñāturnirvijñāterviparilāpo vidyate' ityevaṃrūpāḥ ।
aatha yo vededaṃ jighrāṇīti sa ātmā' iti ca sarvaiḥ karaṇadvārairidaṃ vededaṃ vedeti vijñānenānusaṃdhānāttadrūpatvasiddhiḥ ।
