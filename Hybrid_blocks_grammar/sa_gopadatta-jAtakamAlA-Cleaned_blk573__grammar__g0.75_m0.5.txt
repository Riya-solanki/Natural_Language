atha vṛddhacaṇḍālī taṃ dārakaṃ kṣatavikṣataśarīraṃ śanaiḥ śanaiḥ pāṇinā parāmṛśantī provāca । kena te putrakeyam avasthā kṛtā nirdayena pāpakarmaṇā puruṣādhameneti । sa prāha । amba āryacandanena puruṣo abhihito nirvāsayainam amaṅgalam alakṣmīkam aprekṣaṇīyaṃ caṇḍālādhamam । tenāham evaṃ pīḍita iti । atha sā vṛddhacaṇḍālī dīrgham uṣṇaṃ viniśvasya pañcāṅgulam abhinipīḍayantī kopaparuṣākṣaram ity avocat ।
