tatastāṃ mālāṃ mahāmaṇḍale kṣepayedanena hṛdayena pratīccha vajra hoḥ ।। tato yatra patati so asya sidhyati । tatastāṃ mālāṃ gṛhya tasyaidha śirasi bandhayedanena hadayena oṃ pratigṛṇhatvamimaṃ satvaṃ mahābalaḥ ।। tayā bandhayā tena mahāsattvena pratīcchito bhavati, śīghraṃ cāsya sidhyati ।
