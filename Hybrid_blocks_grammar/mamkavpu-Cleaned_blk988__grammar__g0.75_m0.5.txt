ityuktavat bhrukuṭyādivikāravarjitaḥ krodhaḥ sadyaḥphaladaḥ svargapātālagagasamudrollaṅghanādyutsāhaśca divyeṣyeva । adhikaṃ tu nibadhyamānamasatyapratibhāsena anāyakavadvartitavyaṃ na pratināyakavat'; ityupadeśe na paryavasyet । divyādivyeṣu ubhayathāpi । evamuktasyaucityasya divyādīnāmiva dhīrodāttādīnāmapyanyathāvarṇanaṃ viparyayaḥ । tatrabhavan bhagavannityuttamena na adhamena muniprabhṛtau na rājādau bhaṭṭāraka-iti nottamena rājādau prakṛtiviparyayāpattervācyam । evaṃ deśakālavayojātyādīnāṃ veṣavyavahārādikamucitamevopanibanddhavyam ।।
