atha yaduktaṃ pūrvasminnapi vākye na brahmabhihitamiti, agāyatrī vā idaṃ sarvaṃ bhūtaṃ yadidaṃ kiñca' iti gāyatryākhyasya chandaso abhihitatvāditi, tatparihartavyam ।
kathaṃ punaśchandobhidhānānna brahmābhihitamiti śakyate vaktuṃ, yāvatā atāvānasya mahimā' ityetasyāmṛci catuṣpādbrahma darśitam ।
