jñānam ekaṃ sphuṭāvabhāsasvalakṣaṇānubhavasvarūpaṃ nirvikalpakaṃ te kathayanti । aparaṃ tu śabdāruṣaṇayā smṛtisaṃśayotprekṣādibahubhedaṃ vikalpasaṃjñam । ubhayam apy etan na yujyate bodharūpajñānātiriktasyānyasya saṃbandhitayā tasyānupalabdheḥ । ko asau sthirarūpa ātmā ? sābhilāpāhaṃpratyayenāpi śarīrādikavedyavastūttīrṇo vedayitā na kaś cid lokair avadhāryate ।।
