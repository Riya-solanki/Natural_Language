[] lalita-mādhave śrī-rādhikādīnāṃ tathā-varṇanaṃ tu sphuṭam eva nirvarṇitam । ujjvala-nīlamaṇau sākṣāt-kathanayā kumārikāṇāṃ sākṣād anya-sambandhābhāva-prathanayā ca parāsām api yad eva samṛddhimad-ākhya-sambhoge lalita-mādhavānusāreṇa sphuṭam aṅgīkṛtam asti ।
