[] tad etad ākalayya kalitaṃ hāsa-kolāhalaṃ, gokula-kuleśvarī gṛhād avakalayantī svayam anala-pakva-tulitāni sūrya-pakvāni bahūny anupa-bhukta-carāṇi vihāpayāmāsa, yena bahulam eva sahāsa-kutūhalaṃ nikhilaḥ kalayām-babhūva ।
