evaṃ ca yathā punaruktavadābhāsaḥ paraṃparitarūpakaṃ cobhayor bhāvānuvidhāyitayā, ubhayālaṅkārau tathā śabdahetukārthāntaranyāsaprabhṛtayo api draṣṭavyāḥ । arthasya tu tatra vaivitryam utkaṭatayā pratibhāsate, iti vācyālaṅkāramadhye vastusthitimanapekṣyaiva lakṣitāḥ ।।
