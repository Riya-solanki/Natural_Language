tāmrapātraṃ prathamaṃ nimbukānāṃ rasaiḥ punaḥ punaḥ prakṣālyātyujjvalaṃ ca kṛtvā sarvaśuddharasasya rantī tathā śuddhagandhakarantī ca prakṣipyāṅgulyā gāḍhaṃ pramardya gandhakasūtapīṭhī kāryā ।।
iti gandhakapīṭhī prathamā ।।
