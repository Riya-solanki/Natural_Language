jñānadṛṣṭyā trīṇi jñānāni darśanamārgaḥ । prathamaṃ yad darśanamārgabhūtaṃ śrāvakamārgaṃ jānāti, dvitīyaṃ yat pratyekabuddhamārgaṃ jānāti । tṛtīyaṃ yanmahāyānamārgaṃ jānātīti trayo bhedā bhavanti ।
