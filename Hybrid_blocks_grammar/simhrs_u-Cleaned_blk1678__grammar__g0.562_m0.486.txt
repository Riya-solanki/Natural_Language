yathā veṇī-saṃhāre, bhīmaḥ-bho bho alam alam āśaṅkayā ।
kartā dyūta-cchalānāṃ jatu-maya-śaraṇoddīpanaḥ so' timānī kṛṣṇākeśottarīya-vyapanayana-marut pāṇḍavā yasya dāsāḥ ।
rājā duḥśāsanāder gurur anuja-śatasyāṅga-rājasya mitraṃ kvāste duryodhano' sau kathayata na ruṣā draṣṭum abhyāgatau svaḥ ।।
