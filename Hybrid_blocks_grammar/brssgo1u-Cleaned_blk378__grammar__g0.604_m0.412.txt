itaśca paramātmaivehāttā bhavitumarhati, yatkāraṇaṃ prakaraṇamidaṃ paramatmanaḥ, ana jāyate mriyate vā vipaścit' ityādi prakṛtagrahaṇaṃ ca nyāyyam ।
aka itthā veda yatra saḥ' iti ca durvijñānatvaṃ paramātmaliṅgam ।।
