pṛthuśravasaś ca putras tamaḥ । tasmād uśanā yo vājimedhānāṃ śatam ājahāra । tasya ca śitapur nāma putro abhūt । tasyāpi rukmakavacaḥ । tataḥ parāvṛt parāvṛto rukmeṣupṛthujyāmaghapālitaharitasaṃjñās tasya pañcātmajā babhūvuḥ ।।
