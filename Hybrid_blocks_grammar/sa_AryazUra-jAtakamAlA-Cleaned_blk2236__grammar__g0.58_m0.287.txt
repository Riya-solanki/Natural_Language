atha bodhisattvastathāpyasatkāraviprakṛtaḥ protsāhyamāno api tayā vanadevatayā svāṃ prakṛtibhadratāṃ pradarśayannuvāca - alamalamanena krameṇa । naiṣa mārgo asmadvidhānām । 
ārte pravṛttiḥ sādhūnāṃ kṛpayā na tu lipsayā ।
tāmavaitu paro mā vā tatra kopasya ko vidhiḥ ।।
