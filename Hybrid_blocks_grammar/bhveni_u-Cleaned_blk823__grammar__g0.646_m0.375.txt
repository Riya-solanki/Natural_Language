{Pkt}tado deva sallena bhaṇidaṃ । aṅgarāa kkhalidatulaṃgamo mathidacakkaṇemikūbaro de raho । tā ṇa juttaṃ bhīmajjuṇehiṃ saha ājujjiduṃ । tti bhaṇia ṇivattido raho odārido sāmī sandaṇādo bahuppaāraṃ a samassāsido ।
