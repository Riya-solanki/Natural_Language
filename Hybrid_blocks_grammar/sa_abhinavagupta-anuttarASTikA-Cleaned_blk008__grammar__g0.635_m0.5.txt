bhāvānāṃ na samudbhavo asti sahajas tvadbhāvitā bhānty amī niḥsatyā api satyatām anubhavabhrāntyā bhajanti kṣaṇam ।
tvatsaṅkalpaja eṣa viśvamahimā nāsty asya janmānyataḥ tasmāt tvaṃ vibhavena bhāsi bhuvaneṣv ekopy anekātmakaḥ ।।
