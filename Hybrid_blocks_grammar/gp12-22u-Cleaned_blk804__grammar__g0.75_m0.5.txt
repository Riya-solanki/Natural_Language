[] prathamaṃ tāvat prathamānena vātena vātena te pralayaṃ gatā iti sandihya, tadaiva tad-daivata-śatam eva tatra prabhūta-dūtatayā prasthāpitavān । tat tu tasmād atisatvaram āgatvaratayā tad abhīṣṭaṃ pratyācaṣṭa । tataś ca balārātir balāt pravartita-prasāreṇa jalāsāreṇa tad-vilāpanaṃ pratītya ca satyatā-pratipattaye jalada-devān eva tad-vārtāyāṃ vartayāmāsa । vartitāś ca te tad-vīkṣitena pathā pratyāgatya yathā-pūrvaṃ tat pratyāśā-padaṃ pratyākhyāya sthitavantaḥ । tataś ca paramam apūrvaṃ matvā, manasy atīva dhūrvaṇaṃ gatvā, ghanāghana-bṛṃhaṇaḥ sa ghanāghanaḥ sva-vāhanaṃ ghanāghanaṃ krodhād aṅkuśena maṅkṣu jaghāna । hatvā ca kiñcid agrato gatvā satvaram airāvatīya-vahnim ahni cāpahnuta-netra-vīryaṃ vikīrya dūtyāya tad-devatām avatārayāmāsa । sā ca tato nivṛtya pravṛtty-antaraṃ nivedayāmāsa-deva, paramāścarya-caryeyam avadhāryatām, yat khalu sa eva bali-bhojana-valita-balaḥ sann acalaḥ samyag utpatann ivāvalokyate ।
