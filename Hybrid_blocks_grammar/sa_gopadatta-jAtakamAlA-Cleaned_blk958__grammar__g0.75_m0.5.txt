kaṇṭhopāntavirodhagadgadataraṃ kūjañ chanaiḥ pṛṣṭhato rāgād raktatarekṣaṇaḥ sacakitaṃ nirbhartsyamāno muhuḥ । śyenāyātam asaṃpradhārya dayitām anveti pārāvato rāgābhyāsakalaṅkitasya manasaḥ kā vañcanā dūrataḥ ।।
