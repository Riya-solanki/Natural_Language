nanvabhedo apyastīti kiṃ na jñātāḥ ? pratyuta jñānameva teṣāṃ yuktam; kāraṇābhāve hi kāryabhāva autsargikaḥ; sa ca kāraṇasattayā apodyate ।
asti cābhede kāraṇasatteti kanake jñāte jñātā eva kuṇḍalādaya iti tajjijñāsājñānāni cānarthakāni syuḥ ।
tena yasmin gṛhyamāṇe yanna gṛhyate tattato bhidyate; yathā karabhe gṛhyamāṇe agṛhyamāṇe rāsabhaḥ karabhāt ।
gṛhyamāṇe ca dūrato hemni na gṛhyante tasya bhedāḥ kuṇḍalādayaḥ; tasmātte hemno bhidyante ।
