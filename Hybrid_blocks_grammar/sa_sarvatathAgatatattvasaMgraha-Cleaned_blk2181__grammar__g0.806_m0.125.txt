tatrāsāṃ vidyāhṛdayāni bhavanti ।
oṃ padmabhūriṇī huṃ ।।
oṃ viśvakarmeśvari hūṃ ।।oṃ tathāgateśvaryābhiṣekakarmavidye hūṃ ।।oṃ dharmakarmeśvari jñānapūjāsamaye hūṃ ।।oṃ amogha[karmeśvari hūṃ] ।।oṃ padmakarmabuddhe hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakarmavajriṇi hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakāmini māraṇapūjākarmasamaye hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakarmatuṣṭi hūṃ ।।oṃ padma[karma bhṛ]kuṭi hūṃ traḥ ।।oṃ padmakarmasūrye hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakarmadhvaje hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakarmahāse haḥ ।।oṃ padmakarmatāre hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakarmakumāri hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakarmanārāyaṇi hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakarmabrāhmi hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakarmanṛtyeśvari hūṃ ।।oṃ padmarakṣakarmasamaye hūṃ ।।oṃ mahāpracaṇḍi ghātani padmadaṃṣṭrākarmakari hūṃ ।।oṃ padmakarmamuṣṭi ghātaya hūṃ ।।oṃ ratipūje hūṃ jaḥ ।।oṃ abhiṣekapūje hūṃ hoḥ ।।oṃ gītapūje hūṃ dhaḥ ।।oṃ nṛtyapūje hūṃ vaḥ ।।oṃ dhūpapūje aḥ ।।oṃ puṣpapūje hūṃ traḥ ।।oṃ ālokapūje hūṃ dhīḥ ।।oṃ gandhapūje hūṃ vaṃ ।।oṃ hayagrīve ānaya hūṃ jaḥ ।।oṃ amoghapāśakrodhe pīḍaya hūṃ phaṭ ।।oṃ padmaśaṅkalabandhe hūṃ phaṭ ।।oṃ padmaghaṇṭāveśaya hūṃ phaṭ ।।
athātra karmamaṇḍale ākarṣaṇādividhivistaraṃ kṛtvā, yathāvatpraveśyaivaṃ vadet । "na tvayā kulaputra kasyacidayaṃ vaktavyaḥ mā te narakapatanaṃ bhaved" iti ।
