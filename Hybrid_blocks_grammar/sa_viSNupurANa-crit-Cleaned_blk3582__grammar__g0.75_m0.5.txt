rāmasya tu kuśalavau putrau lakṣmaṇasyāṅgadacandraketū takṣapuṣkarau bharatasya subāhuśūrasenau śatrughnasya kuśasyātithir atither api niṣadhaḥ putro abhavat । niṣadhasyāpi nalas tasya api nabho nabhasaḥ puṇḍarīkaḥ । tattanayaḥ kṣemadhanvā tasya ca devānīkas tasyāpy ahīnagus tato rurus tasya ca pāriyātraḥ pāriyātrād dalo dalāc chalas tasyāpy uktha ukthād vajranābhas tasmāc chaṅkhanābhas tato vyutthitāśvas tataś ca viśvasaho jajñe ।।
