grāvāṇo atra vibhūṣaṇaṃ trijagato maryādayā sthīyate nanv atraiva vidhuḥ sthito hi vibudhāḥ sambhūya pūrṇāśiṣaḥ 
śete codgatanābhipadmavilasadbrahmeha devaḥ svayaṃ daivād eti jaḍaḥ svakukṣibhrtaye so apy ambudhir nimnatām ।।
