yayā tatraiva unmatta-daśānana-nāmani pañcamāṅke mālyavān -vṛddha-buddhir hi prathamaṃ paśyati caramaṃ kāryam । yan mayā dhūrjaṭi-dhanur-adhikṣepataḥ prabhṛti mati-cakṣuṣā dṛṣṭam eva yad uta daśakandharo' nusandhāsyati sītā-haraṇam ।
