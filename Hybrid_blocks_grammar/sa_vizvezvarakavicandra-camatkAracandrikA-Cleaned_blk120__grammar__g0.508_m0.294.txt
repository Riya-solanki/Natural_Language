atra tiraśvāṃ rutaṃ vāsitam । bhūṣaṇaśabdaḥ siñijitamiti maryādāyāṃ "vāśṭa śabdakutsāyām', "śija avyakte śabde'; iti ca yogamātreṇa prayuktau vāśitaśiñjitaśabdau heṣābṛṃhitayoravācakau ।
asyāpi guṇatā grāhyā tacchāsrasvīkṛtādiṣu ।।
yathā
śrī siṅgabhūmiramaṇe bharitapratāpe nānye sphuranti raṇasīmani rājalokāḥ ।
kiṃ vā sphuranti raṭitaspṛśi pāñcajanye līlānukāri taruṇījanakūjitāni ।।
