itaśca na śūdrasyādhikāraḥ ।
yatsatyavacanena śūdratvābhāve nirdhārite jābālaṃ gautama upanetumanuśāsituṃ ca pravavṛte anaitadbrāhmaṇo vivaktumarhati samidhaṃ somyāharopa tvā neṣye na satyādagāḥ' iti śrutiliṅgāt ।।
