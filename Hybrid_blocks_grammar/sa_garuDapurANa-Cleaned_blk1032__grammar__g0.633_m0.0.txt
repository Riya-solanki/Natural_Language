oṃ hāṃ śivaparivārebhyo namaḥ । oṃ hāṃ indrāya surādhipataye namaḥ । oṃ hāṃ agnaye tejo adhipataye namaḥ । oṃ hāṃ yamāya pretādhipataye namaḥ । oṃ hāṃ nirṛtaye rakṣo adhipataye namaḥ । oṃ hāṃ varuṇāya jalādhipataye namaḥ । oṃ hāṃ vāyave prāṇādhipataye namaḥ । oṃ hāṃ somāya netrādhipataye namaḥ । 
oṃ hāṃ īśānāya sarvavidyādhipataye namaḥ ।
oṃ hāṃ anantāya nāgādhipataye namaḥ ।
oṃ hāṃ brahmaṇe sarvalokādhipataye namaḥ ।
oṃ hāṃ dhūlicaṇḍeśvarāya namaḥ ।।
