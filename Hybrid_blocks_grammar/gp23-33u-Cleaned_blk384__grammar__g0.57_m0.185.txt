tvāṃ veṇuṃ kila hartum asya kutukāt prāhaiṣam asmi drutam । taṃ tu tvaṃ parihṛtya cāgatavatī śrānteva ca prekṣyase
tad-vartmāny adurī-cakartha rabhasā yan mādṛśāṃ durgamam ।।
