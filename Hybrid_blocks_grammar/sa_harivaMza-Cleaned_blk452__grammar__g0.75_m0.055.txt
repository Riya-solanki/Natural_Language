bālyāt kāmāc ca mohāc ca saṃharṣāc cāpalena ca 
jahāra kanyāṃ kāmāt sa kasya cit puravāsinaḥ ।। 
ekasmin divase rāja putro atyantaparākramī । dadarśa vicaran svīye pattane cārulocanām । udvāhayantīṃ vedikāyāṃ vaiśyavaryasya kasya cit ।। anullaṅghitamaryādāṃ saptapadyā vicakṣaṇaḥ । bhartrā pānigrahayutāṃ balād gṛhya mahīpatiḥ । gāndharveṇa vivāhena svayaṃ udvāhayad balāt ।। vaiśyāḥ sarve samāgamya rājānam idam ūcatuḥ । kumāreṇa hṛtā kanyā vedikāyā mahadbalāt । rājñā na rakṣito lokas tadā naṣṭo bhaved dhruvam ।। na pūrvais taiḥ kṛtaṃ pūrvaṃ na kariṣyati cāpadi । yathā kumāreṇa kṛtaṃ rājan karma vigarhitam । 
adharmaśaṅkunā tena rājā trayyāruṇo atyajat 
tam adharmeṇa saṃyuktaṃ pitāsūryāruṇo jahau । 
apadhvaṃseti bahuśo vadan krodhasamanvitaḥ ।।
