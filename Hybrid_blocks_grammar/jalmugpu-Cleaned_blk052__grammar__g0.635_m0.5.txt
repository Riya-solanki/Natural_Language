veśyābhir vivaśīkṛtaḥ kupuruṣaḥ saṃjāyate durgato daurgatyena durodare nipatitaḥ svaṃ hārayaty eva sa ।
ruddho dyūtakaraiḥ karoti vidhuraś cauryaṃ tatas taskaro vadhyaḥ syān nṛpater aho nu viṣayāsakter durantā gatiḥ ।।
