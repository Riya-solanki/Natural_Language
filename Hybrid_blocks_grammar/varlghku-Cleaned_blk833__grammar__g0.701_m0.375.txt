tavyattavyānīyaraḥ ।।
dhātorete pratyayāḥ syuḥ । edhitavyam, edhanīyaṃ tvayā । bhāve autsargikamekavacanaṃ klībatvaṃ ca । cetavyaścayanīyo vā dharmastvayā pacelimā māṣāḥ । paktavyā ityarthaḥ । bhidelimāḥ saralāḥ । bhettavyā ityarthaḥ ।।
