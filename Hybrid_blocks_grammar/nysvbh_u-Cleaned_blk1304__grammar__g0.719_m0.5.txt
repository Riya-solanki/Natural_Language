sādhanasyāpi sādhanaṃ vaktavyamiti prasaṅgena pratyavasthānaṃ prasaṅgasamaḥ pratiṣedhaḥ kriyāhetuguṇayogī kriyāvān loṣṭa itiheturnāpadiśyate na ca hetumantareṇa siddhirastīti।
pratidṛṣṭāntena pratyavasthānaṃ pratidṛṣṭāntasamaḥ akriyāvānātmā kriyāhetuguṇayogāt loṣṭavat' ityukte pratidṛṣṭānta upādīyate akriyāhetuguṇayuktamākāśaṃ niṣkriyaṃ dṛṣṭam' iti, kaḥ punarākāśasya kriyāheturguṇaḥ ? vāyunā saṃyogaḥ saṃskārāpekṣaḥ vāyuvanaspatisaṃyogavaditi ।।
