saṃgataś cāsmi lokānām āścaryeṇeha rūpiṇā 
ataḥ parataraṃ kṛṣṇa nāścaryaṃ draṣṭum utsahe ।। 
ko vāyaṃ triṣu lokeṣu vismayaṃ bhavatā vinā । namaḥ sarvātmane tubhyaṃ vismayāya jagatpate । namo astu devadeveśa tubhyaṃ sarvātmane namaḥ । namo astu viṣṇave tubhyam ameyāya jagatpate ।। kiṃ vānena jagannātha kṛtakṛtyo asmi sāṃpratam । kṛtyaśeṣaṃ sadā viṣṇo kim utsṛjasi sāṃpratam । 
tad āgaccha gamiṣyāmaḥ kaṃsarājapurīṃ prabho 
yāvann āstaṃ vrajaty eṣa divasānte divākaraḥ ।।
