[] tad evaṃ bahula-kutūhalataś cālaṃ cālam amī gopa-grāmaṇyas tad eva ramyaṃ yajña-dhāma samāgamya paramānandād vāṇinī-prabhṛti-kṛta-nṛtya-vādya-gīta-grāmiṇīṃ yāminīṃ viśrāmyanti sma । prātaś ca dvitīyāyām ahimakara-kanyāyā mahima-śravaṇataḥ praṇatatayā tasyāṃ snānam ācarya nikāyyam evāgacchanti sma ।
