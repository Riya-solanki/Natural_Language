sarvasattvānāṃ anāgatamokṣasukhopasaṃhārārthena hitakāritram, lokasya duḥkhadaurmanasyādīni prahāya aihikasukhopasaṃhāreṇa sukhakāritram, duḥkharahitāvipākadharmatāyāṃ sthāpanāt trāṇakāritramiti trīṇi ।
