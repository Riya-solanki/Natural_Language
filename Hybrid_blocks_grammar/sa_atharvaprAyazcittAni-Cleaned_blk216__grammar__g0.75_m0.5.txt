dviṣantam agne dviṣatāṃ ca vittaṃ gaccha tvam ādāya parāvato anyān pitrye praṇīta upaśāmyamāna iha prajāṃ dīrgham āyuś ca dhehi । yas tvam agne pramattānāṃ praṇīta upaśāmyasi sukalpam agne ta tvayā punas tvoddīpayāmasi ity ucyamāne agniṃ praṇīya prajvālyaindrasya kukṣir asi iti dvābhyāṃ samidhāv abhyādadhyāt ।।
