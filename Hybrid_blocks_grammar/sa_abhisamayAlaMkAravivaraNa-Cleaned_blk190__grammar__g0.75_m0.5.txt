bhedaḥ asya pañca bhedā bhavanti, tadyathā - rūpādisarvadharmāviparītadarśanaṃ svabhāvaḥ, nānyathā buddhatvasamprāptiriti bhāvanāmārgasya śreṣṭhatā, sarvadharmaviśeṣānutpādanena adhigamaprayogo anabhisaṃskāraḥ, sarvadharmāṇāṃ paramārthato anupalambhatayā arpaṇā, buddhatvamahārthasādhanānmahārthateti ।
