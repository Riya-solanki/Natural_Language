so aparyavasitakukāryaparyākulam upaśamavirodhiprakṛṣṭopāyāsam arthopārjanapradhānakāmarativyavahārāsārakarmadharmabhūyiṣṭham avinayakalahavairavigrahāyatanam īrṣyāmātsaryacauryādiniketabhūtaṃ śokaparidevaduḥkhadaurmanasyopāyāsabahulaṃ māyāśāṭhyamadamānagahanaṃ sāhasāvalepādhiṣṭhitam anāryavyavahārānupravṛttijihmasattvabhāvaṃ pramādapadasthānam anekarūpaparibhavopasṛṣṭaṃ saṃnihitadainyam anabhibhūtaviṣādaṃ pratyāsannavighātabhayadaurgatyam aviṣahyopadravasamudraṃ kaṇṭakagarbhādabhraśvabhrapratibhayākaram apāsya gārhasthyam anyatamam aśramapadam abhyalaṃcakāra ।
