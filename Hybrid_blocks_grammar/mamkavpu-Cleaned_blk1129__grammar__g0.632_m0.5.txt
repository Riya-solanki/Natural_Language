prathamapādādigatāntyārghādibhāgo dvitīyapādādigate ādyārdhādibhāge yamyate ityādyanvarthatānusaraṇenānekabhedam । ādyantikam । madhyādikam । antamadhyam । teṣāṃ samuccayaḥ । tathā tasminneva pāde
ādyādibhāgānāṃ madhyādibhāge aniyate ca sthāne āvṛttiriti prabhūtatamabhedam । tadetatkāvyāntargaḍubhūtam iti nāsya bhedalakṣaṇaṃ kṛtam । diṅmātramudāhriyate ।
