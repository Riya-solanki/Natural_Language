mamedaṃ pitṛdhanam ity atīva ca satyabhāmā spṛhāṃ cakāra । balabhadrasatyānanāvalokanāt kṛṣṇo apy ātmānaṃ cakrāntarāvasthitam iva mene । sakalayādavapratyakṣaṃ cākrūram āha । etad dhi maṇiratnam ātmasaṃśodhanāya eṣāṃ yadūnāṃ darśitam etac ca mama balabhadrasya ca sāmānyaṃ pitṛdhanaṃ caitat satyabhāmāyā nānyasya ।।
