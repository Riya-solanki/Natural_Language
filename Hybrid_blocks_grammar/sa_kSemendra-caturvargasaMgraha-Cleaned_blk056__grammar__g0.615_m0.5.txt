tāvad dharmakathā manobhavarucir mokṣaspṛhā jāyate yāvat tṛptisukhodayena na janaḥ kṣutkṣāmakukṣiḥ kṣaṇam ।
prāpte bhojanacintanasya samaye vittaṃ nimittaṃ vinā dharme kasya dhiyaḥ smaraṃ smarati kaḥ kenekṣyate mokṣabhūḥ ।।
