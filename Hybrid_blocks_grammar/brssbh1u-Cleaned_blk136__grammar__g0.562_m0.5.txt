ānandamayādhikāre ca aso akāmayata bahusyāṃ prajāyeya' iti kāmayitṛtvanirdeśānnānumānikamapi sāṃkhyaparikalpitamacetanaṃ pradhānamānandamayatvena kāraṇatvena vāpekṣitavyam ।
aīkṣaternāśabdam' iti nirākṛtamapi pradhānaṃ pūrvasūtrodāhṛtāṃ kāmayitṛtvaśrutimāśritya prasaṅgātpunarnirākriyate gatisāmānyaprapañcanāya ।।
