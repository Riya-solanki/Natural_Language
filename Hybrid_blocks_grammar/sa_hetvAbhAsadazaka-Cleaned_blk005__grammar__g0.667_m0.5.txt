bhīto ahaṃ virahī tuṣārakiraṇāt so ayaṃ samujjṛmbhate vijñātaḥ katham eṣa te mama yatas tāpaṃ karoty uccakaiḥ ।
liṅgāt ko anuminoti sādhyavirahavyāptād iyaṃ mugdhatā mā bhaiṣīr iti pātu vo raghupatis saumitriṇā sāntvitaḥ ।।
