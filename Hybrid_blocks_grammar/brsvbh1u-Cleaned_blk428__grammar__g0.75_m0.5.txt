-bhāvārthāḥ-dhātvarthoparaktabhāvanā yeṣu bhāti tādṛśā ye karmaśabdāḥ-kriyāvācakā yajatyādayastebhyaḥ kriyā-apūrvaṃ pratīyeta na dravyaguṇaśabdebhya iti tadarthaḥ । bhāvanāvācibhyo bhāvanā, bhāva ityādibhyo nāpūrvaṃ pratīyata iti karmaśabdā ityuktam । karmaśabdebhyo api yāgahomaśabdebhyo nāpūrvādhigatiriti prathamadalopanyāsa ityavagantavyam ।
