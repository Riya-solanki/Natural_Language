sahasraṃ gāvo akṣīṇi yasya sa sahasragurindraḥ । tasyeveti gośabdasyākṣivācitvaṃ kaviṣvaprasiddhamiti ।।
asamyārthāntaramasamyasmṛtihetuścāślīlam ।।
yasya padasyānekārthasyaiko artho'sabhyaḥ syāt, tadasabhyārthāntaram । yathā varcaḥ iti padaṃ tejasi viṣṭhāyāṃ ca । yattu padaṃ sabhyārthavācakamapyekadeśadvāreṇāsabhyārthaṃ smārayati, tadasabhyasmṛtihetuḥ । yathā kṛkāṭikā iti ।।
na guptalakṣitasaṃvṛtāni ।।
apavādārthamidam । guptaṃ lakṣitaṃ saṃvṛttaṃ ca nāślīlam ।।
