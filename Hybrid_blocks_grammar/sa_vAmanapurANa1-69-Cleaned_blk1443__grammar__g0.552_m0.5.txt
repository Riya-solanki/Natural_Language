āyāti tripurāntake saha gaṇairbrahmarṣibhiḥ saptabhirārohatpulako babhau girivaraḥ saṃhṛṣṭatittaḥ kṣaṇāt ।
cakre divyaphalairjalena śucinā mūlaiśca kandādibhiḥ pūjāṃ sarvagaṇeśvaraiḥ saha vibhoradristrinetrasya tu ।।
