atha bodhisattvo abhigataḥ prathame bisabhāgasthāne bisabhāgavirahitaṃ padminīpatraṃ parivyākulīkṛtopahāramabhisamīkṣya gṛhītaḥ kenāpi me bisapratyaṃśa ityavadhṛtamatirapetacetaḥsaṃkṣobhasaṃrambhastata eva pratinivṛtya praviśya parṇaśālāyāṃ yathocitaṃ dhyānavidhimārebhe । vaimanasyaparihārthaṃ cetareṣāmṛṣīṇāṃ tamarthaṃ na nivedayāmāsa । itare tvasya bhrātaro nūnamanena gṛhītaḥ pratyaṃśa iti manyamānā yathocitāneva svānsvānanukrameṇa bisabhāgānādāya yathāsvaṃ parṇaśālāsu paribhujya dhyāyanti sma । evaṃ dvitīye tṛtīye caturthe pañcame ca divase śakrastasya taṃ bisapratyaṃśamupanidadhe । bodhisattvo api ca mahāsattvastathaiva niḥsaṃkṣobhapraśāntacitto babhūva ।
