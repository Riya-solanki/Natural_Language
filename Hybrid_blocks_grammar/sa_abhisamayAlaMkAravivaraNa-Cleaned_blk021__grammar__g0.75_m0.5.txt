lakṣaṇam samastadharmagatapudgalanairātmyasākṣātkāriṇyā prajñayopāttatvena hainayānikajātīyābhisamayasvarūpaṃ āryasantatisthaṃ jñānaṃ mārgajñatāyā lakṣaṇam । hainayānikāryasantatisthaṃ yajjñānaṃ tacchūnyatāyāḥ pudgalanairātmyāparaparyāyāḥ sākṣātkāri, tenopāttatvāt sarvajñatāyā lakṣaṇe hainayānikajātīyeti viśeṣaṇam ।
