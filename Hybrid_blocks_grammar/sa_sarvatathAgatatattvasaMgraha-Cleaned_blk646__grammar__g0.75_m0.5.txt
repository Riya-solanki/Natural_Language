athāsmin bhāṣitamātre sarvatathāgatahṛdayebhyo vajrapāṇirviniḥsṛtya, sarvatathāgatasūkṣmajñānavajrabimbamātmānamadhiṣṭhāya, sarvatathāgatanāsikāgreṣu sthitvedamudānamudānayāmāsa । aho hi sarvabuddhānāṃ sūkṣmavajramahaṃ mahat । yanmahatvātsa sūkṣmo api traidhātukamapi sphared ।।
