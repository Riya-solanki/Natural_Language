FN yasya paramātmano brahma kṣatraṃ cobhe jātī prasiddhānnavadodanau bhavataḥ, yasya mṛtyuḥ sarvamārakaḥ sannupasecanamodanamiśraghṛvattiṣṭhati, yatra so attā kāraṇātmā vartate, taṃ nirviśeṣamātmānaṃ anāvirato duścaritāt' iti mantroktopāyavānyathā veda itthā itthamanyastadrahito na vedetyarthaḥ ।
pradarśanamupalakṣaṇam ।
naca brahkṣatre evātra vivakṣite, mṛtyūpasecanena prāṇabhṛnmātropasthāpanāt ।
prāṇiṣu pradhānatvena ca brahmakṣatropanyāsasyopapatteḥ ।
