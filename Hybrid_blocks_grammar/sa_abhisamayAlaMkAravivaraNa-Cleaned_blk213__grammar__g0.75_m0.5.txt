lakṣaṇam mahākaruṇayā saṃvṛtyapekṣaśamāntavyāvartako māhāyāniko abhisamayo asyāḥ sarvajñatāyā lakṣaṇam । iyaṃ ca prajñāpāramitā kṛpayā sattvārthakaraṇāt na nirvāṇe śamānte sthitā । māhāyānikabhāvanāmārgīyāryapudgalasantatisthā mahākaruṇā asyāḥ kṛpayā na śame sthitiritimārgajñatāyā upalakṣikā ।
