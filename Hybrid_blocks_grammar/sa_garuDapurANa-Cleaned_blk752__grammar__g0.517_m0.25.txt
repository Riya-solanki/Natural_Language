śrīgarūḍamahāpurāṇam- sūta uvāca । anantaraṃ karanyāsaḥ । vidyākarī śuddhiḥ kāryā । padmamudrāṃ baddhvā mantranyāsaṃ kuryāt । kaiṃ kaniṣṭhāyai namaḥ । naiṃ anāmikāyai namaḥ । maiṃ madhyamāyai namaḥ । 
taiṃ tarjanyai namaḥ ।
aṃ aṅguṣṭhāyai namaḥ ।
lāṃ karatalāyai namaḥ ।
vāṃ karapṛṣṭhāyai namaḥ ।।
