NOTE -prajāpativratanyāyaṃ vibhajate niṣedheṣu tadabhāvāya-nekṣeteti । atasya brahmacāriṇo vratam'iti vrataśabdopakramāt, ekasmiṃśca vākye upasahārasya prakramādhīnatvādākhyātayogināpi nañā pratīto niṣedho ananuṣṭheyatvādupekṣyate tataścānīkṣaṇasaṃkalpalakṣaṇā yuktā । na caivaṃ ana hanyāt'itivākye kalpanīyamiti bhāvaḥ ।
