sthitiḥ prapaṃcāś ca na santi yasya yaḥ saṃdānaṃ parighaṃ cātivṛttaḥ । taṃ nistṛṣṇaṃ muniṃ carantaṃ na vijānāti sadevako api lokaḥ ।। yasya vitarkā vidhūpitā adhyātmam avikalpitā aśeṣaṃ । saṃgaṃ so atītya rūpasaṃjñāṃ caturyogāpagato na jātim eti ।। 
dadataḥ puṇyaṃ pravardhate vairaṃ saṃyamato na cīyate ।
kuśalī prajahāti pāpakaṃ kleśānāṃ kṣayatas tu nirvṛtaḥ ।।
