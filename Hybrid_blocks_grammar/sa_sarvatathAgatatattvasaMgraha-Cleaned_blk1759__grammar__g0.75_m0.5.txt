atha [vajrāmṛtakrodhā imāṃ svakarmasamayāmabhāṣat oṃ vajrāmṛte sarvaduṣṭān gṛhṇa bandha hana paca vidhvaṃsaya vināśaya bhinda cchinda bhasmīkuru mūrdhantānuya [vajreṇa ye ketumamamukasya] vidhnavināyakāstān dāmaya dīptakrodhavajriṇi hūṃ phaṭ ।।
