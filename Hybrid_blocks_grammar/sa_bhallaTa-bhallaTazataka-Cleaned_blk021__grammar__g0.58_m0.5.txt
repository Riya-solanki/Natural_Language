so apūrvaḥ rasanāviparyayavidhis tat karṇayoś cāpalaṃ dṛṣṭiḥ sā madavismṛtasvaparadik kiṃ bhūyasoktena vā 
itthaṃ niścitavān asi bhramara he yad vāraṇo adyāpy asā antaḥśūnyakaro niṣeyata iti bhrātaḥ ka eṣa grahaḥ ।।
