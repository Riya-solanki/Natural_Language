yasyās te mahimānam ātmana iva tvadvallabho api prabhur nālaṃ mātum iyattayā niravadhiṃ nityānukūlaṃ svataḥ ।
tām tvāṃ dāsa iti, prapanna iti ca stoṣyāmy ahaṃ nirbhayo lokaikeśvari lokanāthadayite dānte dayāṃ te vidan ।।
