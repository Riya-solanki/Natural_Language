nanvevamaprasiddhasaṃbandhasya kathamanapyanupapannam- yadyapi hi tadā vaktuḥ prasiddhasaṃbandhatvātsaṃbandhakathanāya vākyoccāraṇaṃ saṃbhavati tathāpi śrotāro aprasiddhasamastapadārthā bālāḥ kathaṃ vākyena saṃbadhaṃ pratipadyeran, tasmātkaraṇavatkathanamapyanupapannam ।
naiṣa doṣaḥ ।
kathayitustāvanna karturivāśaktiḥ- upāyasaṃbhavāt, pratipattārastūpāyābhāvānna pratipadyanta iti dṛṣṭaviruddham, dṛśyante hyaprasiddhasamastaśabdārthā bālā vṛddhebhyaḥ saṃbandhaṃ pratipadyamānāḥ ।।
