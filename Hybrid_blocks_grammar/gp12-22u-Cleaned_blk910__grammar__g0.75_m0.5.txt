[] tataś ca surabhir abhihitavatī-śrīmad-vrajendra-kula-candra! bhavad-bhavadīya-svīya-caraṇānām anucaraṇatas tāvad atratyāni tīrthāni sārthakāny eva jātāni । dhenavaś ca yuṣmad-vihita-dugdha-dhayanād dhanyatām adhuḥ । [] tataś ca svarga-sthita-tīrtha-varasya gaṅgā-nirjharasya tathātmīyasyāpy ūdho-bharasya tvad-ārādhanayā vyarthatāvadhāya payaḥ samāhartuṃ bhavantaṃ vidhim arthayāmahe । athavā, yathādiśyate tathāvaśyaṃ prathayāmaḥ ।
