kvacitpadārthopamāyāmupameyānāmupamānānāṃ ca samasaṃkhyākānāṃ sūhadvaye prathamaṃ vivakṣitasamūhasaṃkhyābhedasamāhi dvitvameva samāśritya parasparasāmyasamanvayādupamānopameyabhāvamabhidhātumekasmin vācake prayukte samudāyadharmāṇāṃ samudāyapūrvakatvāttu paścādeteṣāṃ pratyekaṃ svasaṃbandhinaṃ prati sādhāraṇadharmasaṃbandhasāmarthyādupamānopameyabhāvaḥ paryavasyati ।
