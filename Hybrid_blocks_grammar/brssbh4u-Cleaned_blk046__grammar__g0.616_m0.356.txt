iti ca udgīthameva upāsyatvena upakramya ādityādimatīḥ vidadhāti ।
yat tu uktam udgīthādimatibhiḥ upāsyamānā ādityādayaḥ karmabhūyaṃ bhūtvā phalaṃ kariṣyanti iti ।
tat ayuktam ।
svayameva upāsanasya karmatvāt phalavattva upapatteḥ ।
ādityādibhāvena api ca dṛśyamānānām udgīthādīnāṃ karmātmakatvān apāyāt ।
atadetadetasyāmṛcyadhyūḍhaṃ sāma' iti tu lākṣaṇika eva pṛthivī agnyoḥ ṛk sāmaśabdaprayogaḥ ।
lakṣaṇā ca yathāsaṃbhavaṃ saṃnikṛṣṭena viprakṛṣṭena vā svārthasaṃbandhena pravartate ।
tatra yadi api ṛk sāmayoḥ pṛthivī agnyoḥ ca saṃnidhānayoreva eṣa ṛk sāmaśabdaprayoga ṛk sāmasaṃbandhāt iti niścīyate ।
kṣattṛśabdaḥapi hi kutaścit kāraṇāt rājānam upasarpan na nivārayituṃ pāryate ।
aiyamevarka' iti ca yathā akṣaranyāsam ṛca eva pṛthivītvam avadhārayati ।
pṛthivyā hi aktve avadhāryamāṇa iyam ṛk eva ityakṣaranyāsaḥ syāt ।
aya evaṃ vidvānsāma gāyati' iti ca aṅgāśrayameva vijñānam upasaṃharati na pṛthivyādi āśrayam ।
tathā alokeṣu pañcavidhaṃ sāmopāsīta' iti yadi api saptamīnirdiṣṭā lokāḥ tathāpi sāmnyeva te adhyasyeran dvitīyānirdeśena sāmna upāsyatva avagamāt ।
sāmani hi lokeṣu adhyasyamāneṣu sāma lokātmana upāsitaṃ bhavati ।
anyathā punaḥ lokāḥ sāmā ātmana upāsitāḥ syuḥ ।
etena aetadgāyatraṃ prāṇeṣu protam' ityādi vyākhyātam ।
yatra api tulyo dvitīyanirdeśaḥ - aatha khalvamumādityaṃ saptavidhaṃ sāmopāsīta' iti, tatra api asamastasya khalu sāmna upāsanaṃ sādhu' aiti ca pañcavidhasya' aatha saptavidhasya' iti ca sāmna eva upāsyatva upakramāt tasmin eva ādityādi adhyāsaḥ ।
etasmāt eva ca sāmna upāsyatva avagamāt apṛthivī hiṅkāraḥ' ityādinirdeśaviparyaye api hiṅkārādiṣu eva pṛthivyādi dṛṣṭiḥ ।
tasmāt anaṅgāśrayā ādityādimatayaḥ aṅgeṣu udgīthādiṣu kṣipyeran iti siddham ।।
