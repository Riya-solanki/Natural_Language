atrābhidhīyate- pratyakṣabalasiddhasya sāmānyasya kutarkataḥ ।।
na śakyo apahnavaḥ kartuṃ sarvaṃ vijayate hi tat ।।
sarveṣvapi vastuṣu iyamapi gauriyamapi gauḥ, ayamapi vṛkṣo ayamapīti vyāvṛttānuvṛttākāraṃ pratyakṣaṃ deśakālāvasthāntareṣvaviparyastamudīyamānaṃ sarvameva tarkābhāsaṃ vijitya dvyākāraṃ vastu vyavasthāpayat kenānyena śakyate bādhitum, na hi tato anyadbalavattaramasti pramāṇam, tanmūlatvātpramāṇāntarāṇāṃ tadbādhasāmarthyābhāvāt ।
kiñcānumānānyapi sāmānyāpekṣatvātsutarāṃ sāmānyaṃ samarthayante na tu bādhituṃ śaknuvanti, nahi yadyapekṣaṃ tattasya bādhaṃ
