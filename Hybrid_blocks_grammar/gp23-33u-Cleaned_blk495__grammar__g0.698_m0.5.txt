[] tatra ca prajākṛtaṃ rājanīti rājanīti-nyāyena bhavadīya-rājāyamānāyām asyām eva sā jāyamānā syād ity asāv eva tatra pradhānatayāvadhīyate ।
[] sakhya ūcuḥ-bhavatu, tathāpi yathā-katham api bhavat-prasaṅgata eva saṅgataḥ khalv ayaṃ doṣa iti tan-moṣa-kṛte bhavat-kṛtam evānukartavyam ।
