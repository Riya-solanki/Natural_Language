yāñcāṃ dainyaparigrahapraṇayinīṃ nekṣvākavaḥ śikṣitāḥ sevāsaṃvalitaḥ kadā raghukule maulau nibaddho añjaliḥ ।
sarvaṃ tadvihitaṃ tathāpyudadhinā naivoparodhaḥ kṛtaḥ pāṇiḥ saṃprati me haṭhāt kimaparaṃ spraṣṭuṃ dhanurdhāvati ।।
