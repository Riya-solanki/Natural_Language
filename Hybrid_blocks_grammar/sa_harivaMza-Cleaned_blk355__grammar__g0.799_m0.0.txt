pituḥ samīpagā sā tu pitrā nirbhartsitā śubhā 
bhartuḥ samīpaṃ gaccheti niyuktā ca punaḥ punaḥ ।। 
cintām avāpa mahatīṃ strīṇāṃ dhik ceṣṭitaṃ tv iti । nininda bahudhātmānaṃ strītvaṃ cātinininda sā ।। sthātavyaṃ na kvacit strīṇāṃ dhig asvātantryajīvitam । śaiśave yauvane vārddhe pitṛbhartṛsutād bhayam ।। tyaktaṃ bhartṛgṛhaṃ maugdhyād bata durvṛttayā mayā । avijñātāpi vedhāyām atha patyur niketanam ।। tatrāsti sā savarṇā vai paripūrṇamanorathā । naṣṭaṃ bhartṛgṛhaṃ maugdhyāc chreyo atra na pitur gṛhe । 
agacchad vaḍavā bhūtvāc chādya rūpam aninditā 
kurūn athottarān gatvā tṛṇāny eva cacāra sā ।।
