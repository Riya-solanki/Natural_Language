[] madhumaṅgalaḥ sa-roṣaṃ śrī-kṛṣṇam avalocya vakti sma-para-vaktavyā khalv iyaṃ vaktavyā bhavatīti nāsmākaṃ vaktavyā bhavitum arhati । kintu seyaṃ tāvad atra nirudhyatām yāvad ahaṃ tad vṛttam avabudhya sudhy-agraṇīr bhavantam avaruṇadhmi ।
