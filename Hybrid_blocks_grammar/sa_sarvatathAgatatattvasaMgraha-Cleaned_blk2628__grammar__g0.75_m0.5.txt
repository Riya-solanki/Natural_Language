athātra karmamaṇḍale yathāvadvidhivistaraṃ kṛtvā praveśyaivaṃ vadet । "na tvayā kasyacidayaṃ vaktavyaḥ । mā te karmāvaraṇadhiṣṭhitasyaiva maraṇaṃ bhaved" iti, uktvā, maṇikarmajñānāni śikṣayet ।
