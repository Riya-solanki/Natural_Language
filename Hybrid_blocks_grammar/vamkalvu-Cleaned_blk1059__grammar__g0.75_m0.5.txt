rājavaṃśyāḥ, sūryavaṃśyā ityādayaḥ śabdāḥ, tatra sādhuḥ ityanena sādhyarye yati pratyaye sati sādhayo bhavanti । bhāvārthe punardigādipāṭhe'pi vaṃśaśabdasya vaṃśaśabdāntānna yat pratyayaḥ । tadantavidheḥ pratiṣedhāt ।।
