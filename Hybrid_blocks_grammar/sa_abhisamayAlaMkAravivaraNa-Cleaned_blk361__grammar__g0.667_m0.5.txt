mārgajñatāsvabhāvāḥ pañca paramārthāsatsarvasattvaparinirvāpaṇaduṣkarasaṃnāhatvena duṣkarasvabhāvaḥ, anya yānāpātalakṣaṇa aikāntikasvabhāvaḥ, uttamaścirasādhya uddeśasvabhāvaḥ, bhāvyabhāvakabhāvanānupalambhāt anupalambhasvabhāvaḥ, samastabhāvābhiniveśaniṣedhād anabhiniveśasvabhāva iti pañca ।
