yasya padasyānekārthasyaiko artho'sabhyaḥ syāt, tadasabhyārthāntaram । yathā varcaḥ iti padaṃ tejasi viṣṭhāyāṃ ca ।
yattu padaṃ sabhyārthavācakamapyekadeśadvāreṇāsabhyārthaṃ smārayati, tadasabhyasmṛtihetuḥ । yathā kṛkāṭikā iti ।।
