yadi hāṭakādabhinnaḥ kaṭakaḥ kathamayaṃ kuṇḍalādiṣu nānuvartate ? nānuvartate cet kathaṃ hāṭakādabhinnaḥ kaṭakaḥ ? ye hi yasminnanuvartamāne vyāvartante te tato bhinnā eva, yathā sūtrātkusumabhedāḥ ।
nānuvartante cānuvartamāne api hāṭakatve kuṇḍalādayaḥ; tasmātte api hāṭakādbhinnā eveti ।
sattānuvṛttyā ca sarvavastvanugame aidamiha nedam, idamevaṃ nedam' iti vibhāgo na syāt, kasyacit kvacit kadācit kathañcidvivekahetorabhāvāt ।
api ca dūrātkanakamityavagate na tasya kuṇḍalādayo viśeṣā jijñāsyeran, kanakādabhedātteṣām, tasya ca jñātatvāt ।
