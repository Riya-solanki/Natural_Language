atha kadācitsa mahāsattvastasya vanasya paryante vicaran yatastatkāntāraṃ tato janaśabdamupaśuśrāva । tasya cintā prādurabhūt - kiṃ nu khalvidam? na tāvadanena pradeśena kaściddeśāntaragāmī mārgo asti । evaṃ mahatkāntāraṃ ca vyatītya mṛgayāpi na yujyate prāgeva mahāsamārambhaparikhedamasmatsayūthyagrahaṇam ।
