dvividhaṃ bhasma ūrdhvagaṃ talabhasma ca varṇabhedena ṣaḍvidhaṃ śvetaṃ bhasma pītaṃ bhasma haritaṃ bhasma raktaṃ bhasma kṛṣṇaṃ bhasma karburaṃ bhasma iti ṣaḍvidhaṃ tatkṛtvā kṣetrīkaraṇe niyujyate prathamam ।।
rasayogamāha mākṣiketyādi ।।
mākṣikaṃ tāpyaṃ śilājatu prasiddhaṃ lohacūrṇaṃ māritamuṇḍasya rajaḥ pathyā harītakī akṣo vibhītakaḥ viḍaṅgaṃ kṛmighnaṃ ghṛtam ājyaṃ madhu kṣaudraṃ etaiḥ samprayuktaṃ rasaṃ kṣetrīkaraṇāya prayuñjīteti vākyārthaḥ ।।
