saṃkrāmo atra na bhāvanā na ca kathāyuktir na carcā na ca dhyānaṃ vā na ca dhāraṇā na ca japābhyāsaprayāso na ca ।
tat kiṃ nāma suniścitaṃ vada paraṃ satyaṃ ca tacchrūyatāṃ na tyāgī na parigrahī bhaja sukhaṃ sarvaṃ yathāvasthitaḥ ।।
