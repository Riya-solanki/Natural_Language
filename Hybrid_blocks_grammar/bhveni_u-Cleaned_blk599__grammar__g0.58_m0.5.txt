yo yaḥ śastraṃ bibharti svabhujagurumadaḥ pāṇḍavīnāṃ camūnāṃ yo yaḥ pāñcālagotre śiśuradhikavayā garbhaśayyāṃ gato vā
yo yastatkarmasākṣī carati mayi raṇe yaśca yaśca pratīpaḥ ē̃ krodhāndhastasya tasya svayamapi jagatāmantakasyāntako aham ।।
