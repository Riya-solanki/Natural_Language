sanyaṅoḥ ।।
sannantasya yaṅantasya ca dhātoranabhyāsasya prathamasyaikāco dve sto ajādestu dvitīyasya । paṭhitumicchati pipaṭhiṣati । karmaṇaḥ kim ? gamanenecchati । samāna kartṛkāt kim ? śiṣyāḥ paṭhantvitīcchati guruḥ । vā grahaṇādvākyamapi ।।
