deśika ityācāryaḥ। [mantravṛttiḥ] mantra evānanyaniṣṭhā vṛttiḥ yasya sa sādhakaḥ। śaivānāmeva deśikādīnāmiha lakṣaṇam। śāktādayastu te anyādṛśā eveti vijñāpayituṃ śaivapadopādānam। dvividhāstu te śaivāḥ। vratino [avratino api] vratavarjitāśca।
