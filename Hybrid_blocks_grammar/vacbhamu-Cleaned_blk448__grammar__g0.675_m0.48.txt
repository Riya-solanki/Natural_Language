athāsmai brahmacaryasaṃpadunmṛditakalmaṣāya maghavate ya eṣo akṣiṇi yaśca svapne suṣupte anusyūta eṣa ātmāpahatapāpmādiguṇako darśitaḥ, tameva amaghavan martyaṃ vai śarīram' ityādinā vispaṣṭaṃ vyācaṣṭe prajāpatiḥ ।
ayamasyābhisaṃdhiḥ-yāvatkiñcita sukhaṃ duḥkhamāgamāpāyi tatsarvaṃ śarīrendriyāntaḥkaraṇasaṃbandhi, na tvātmanaḥ sa punaretāneva śarīrādīn anādyavidyāvāsanāvaśādātmatvenābipratītastadgatena sukhaduḥkhena tadvantamātmānamabhimanyamāno anutapyate ।
yadā tvayamapahatapāpmatvādilakṣaṇamudāsīnamātmānaṃ caitanyānandaghane rūpe vyavasthitaḥ samastalokakāmān prāpto bhavati ।
