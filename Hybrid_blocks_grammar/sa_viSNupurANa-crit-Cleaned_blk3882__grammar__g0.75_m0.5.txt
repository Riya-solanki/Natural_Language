mudgalāc ca maudgalyāḥ kṣatropetā dvijātayo babhūvuḥ । mudgalād vadhyaśvo vadhyaśvād divodāso ahalyā ca mithunam abhūt । śaradvato ahalyāyāṃ śatānando abhavat । śatānandāt satyadhṛtir dhanurvedāntago jajñe । satyadhṛtes tu varāpsarasam urvaśīṃ dṛṣṭvā retaḥ skannaṃ śarastambe papāta ।।
