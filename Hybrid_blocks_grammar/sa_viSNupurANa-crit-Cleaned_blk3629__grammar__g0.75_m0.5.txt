ākhyāhi me samayam ity atha pṛṣṭā punar abravīt । śayanasamīpe mamoraṇakadvayaṃ putrabhūtaṃ nāpaneyam । bhavāṃś ca mayā nagno na draṣṭavyaḥ । ghṛtamātraṃ ca mamāhāra ity evam eveti bhūpatir āha । tayā ca sahāvanīpatir alakāyāṃ caitrarathādivaneṣv amalapadmaṣaṇḍeṣu saraḥsv abhiramamāṇa ekaṣaṣṭivarṣāṇy anudinapravardhamānapramodo anayat ।।
