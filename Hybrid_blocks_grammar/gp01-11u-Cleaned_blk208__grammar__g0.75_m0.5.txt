[] atha so' pi tad avadhārayann eva, tad evāvadhārayan mātaram anu kātara iva niṣkramaṇa-klama-samanujñā-yācanam anusandhāya, praṇāmādinā paurṇamāsīm uṭaja-gṛhāya vihāya, śrī-rāmam agre vidhāya, śrīdāmādīn parito nidhāya, paścimāgrima-prema-dolāyamāna-svāntas tato niṣkrāntaḥ; sahasā mahasā vṛtaḥ sabhyālibhir abhyālokayāñcakre ।
