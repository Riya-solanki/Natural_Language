rākṣasaḥ
{Pkt}vaśāgandhe ajja kkhu ahaṃ śāmiṇīe hiḍimbādevīe śavahumāṇaṃ śaddābia āṇatte jaha luhilappiā ajja pahudi tue ajjauttaśśa bhīmaśeṇaśśa piṭṭhadoṇupiṭṭhaṃ śamale āhiṇḍidavvaṃ tti । tā taśśa aṇumaggagāmiṇo hadamāṇuśaśoṇiaṇaīdaṃśaṇappaṇaṭṭhabubhukkhāpibāśaśśa iha evva me śaggaloo huvīadi । tumaṃ bi viśśaddhā bhavia luhilavaśāhiṃ kumbhaśahaśśaṃ śaṃcehi ।
