bhedaḥ dānādiṣaṭpāramitāparipūraṇena, buddha-dharma-saṃgha-śīla-tyāga-devatā-nāmanusmaraṇena, rūpādisarvadharmābhāve svabhāvāvabodhena yo adhigamaḥ sa ānupūrvikābhisamayastrayodaśavidha ityasya trayodaśa bhedā bhavanti ।
