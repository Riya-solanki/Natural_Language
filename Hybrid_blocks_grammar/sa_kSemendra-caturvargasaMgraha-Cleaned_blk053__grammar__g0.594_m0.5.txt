jātaḥ satkulajaḥ sa nīcavinayī sevāvamānair adhas tasyograṃ patitaḥ svamānayaśasor droheṇa śāpānalaḥ ।
tenāsvastimatā svahastanihitaṃ yācñāviṣaṃ bhakṣitaṃ vittaṃ yena na rakṣitaṃ ripumukhaṃ tena sthitaṃ vīkṣitam ।।
