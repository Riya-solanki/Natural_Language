atha tā devyastasmin rājani krodhasaṃrambhakarkaśahṛdaye pratyāhatapraṇayāḥ prajānānāśca tasya rājñaścaṇḍatāṃ duranuneyatāṃ ca vaimanasyadainyākrāntamanasaḥ strījanādhikṛtairbhayaviṣādavyākulitairhastasaṃjñābhirapasāryamāṇā vrīḍāvanatavadanāstamṛṣivaryaṃ samanuśocantyastato apacakramuḥ ।
