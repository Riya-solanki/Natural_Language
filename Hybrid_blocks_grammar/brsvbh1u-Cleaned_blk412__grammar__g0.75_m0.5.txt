-śravaṇaṃ nāma brahmātmani atattvamasi'ityādivākyakadambasya tātparyanirṇayānukūlo vyāpāraḥ tarkarūpaḥ । tatrāsati viṣayāvagame kathaṃ tatkartavyatābodhaḥ, avagame ca kartumakartumanyathākartumaśakyatvam । evaṃ mananāderapi boddhavyamiti ।
