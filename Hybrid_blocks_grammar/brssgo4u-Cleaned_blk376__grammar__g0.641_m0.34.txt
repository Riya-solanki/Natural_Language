jaiminiḥ tu ācāryo manovat śarīrasya api sendriyasya bhāvaṃ muktaṃ prati manyate ।
yataḥ asa ekadhā bhavati tridhā bhavati' ityādinā anekadhā abhāvavikalpam āmananti ।
na hi anekavidhatā vinā śarīrabhedena āñjasī syāt ।
yadi api nirguṇāyāṃ bhūmavidyāyām ayam anekadhā abhāvavikalpaḥ paṭhyate tathā api vidyamānameva idaṃ saguṇāvasthāyām aiśvaryaṃ bhūmavidyā stutaye saṃkīrtyata iti ataḥ saguṇavidyāphalabhāvena upatiṣṭhata iti ।।
