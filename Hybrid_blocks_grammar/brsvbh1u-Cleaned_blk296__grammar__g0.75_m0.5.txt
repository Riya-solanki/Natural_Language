-atredaṃ bodhyam-utpattimātrasya nimittakāraṇasādhāraṇatve api layādhāratvoktyā upādānatvaṃ sidhyet । nahi daṇḍādiṣu ghaṭādayo līyante । tasmāditaravaiyarthyamiticenna । nahyatropādānatvasūcanāyaiva taduktamapitu prakṛtivikārābhedanyāyenādvaitasiddhaye api tena brahma upādānaṃ bhavatu, adhiṣṭhātā tvanyaḥ syāt, kumbhakāra iva kumbhasyotpattāviti śaṅkānirāso boddhavyaḥ ।
