somadattasyāpi bhūribhūriśravaḥśalyasaṃjñās trayaḥ putrāḥ । śāṃtanor apy amaranadyāṃ gaṅgāyām udārakīrtir aśeṣaśāstrārthavid bhīṣmaḥ putro abhūt । satyavatyāṃ ca citrāṅgadavicitravīryau putrāv utpādayām āsa śāṃtanuḥ । citrāṅgadas tu bāla eva citrāṅgadena gandharveṇāhave vinihataḥ । vicitravīryo api kāśīrājatanaye ambikāmbālike upayeme । tadupabhogātikhedāc ca yakṣmaṇā gṛhītaḥ pañcatvam agamat । satyavatīniyogāc ca tatputraḥ kṛṣṇadvaipāyano mātur vacanam anatikramaṇīyam iti vicitravīryakṣetre dhṛtarāṣṭrapāṇḍū tatprahitabhujiṣyāyāṃ ca viduraṃ utpādayām āsa ।।
