atha bhagavān vajradhātustathāgatastasmin eva kṣaṇe sarvatathāgatasamatājñānābhisaṃbuddha sarvatathāgatavajrasamatājñānamudrāguhyasamayapraviṣṭaḥ sarvatathāgatadharmasamatājñānādhigamasvabhāvaśuddhaḥ sarvatathāgatasarvasamatāprakṛtiprabhāsvarajñānākarabhūtastathāgato arhān samyaksaṃbuddhaḥ saṃvṛtta iti ।।
