atha suvarṇadrutividhānamāha suragopakadeharaja iti ।।
indragopaśarīracūrṇaṃ suradālīphalaiḥ samāṃśakaiḥ suragopacūrṇatulyabhāgaiḥ kṛtvā vāpo deyaḥ drute satyuparikṣepa iti suvarṇe vāpe kṛte suvarṇaṃ drutamāste kiṃviśiṣṭaṃ rasaprakhyaṃ jalatulyam ityarthaḥ ।।
