atha vajrapāṇirmahābodhisatva i[maṃ] vajrakrūrakrodhamabhāṣat oṃ mahāvajrakrūrakrodha pātaya sarvaduṣṭamaṇḍalān, vināśaya sarvaduṣṭasamayān, vikira vidhvaṃ[saya spho]ṭaya bhaṃjaya sarvaduṣṭasamayamudrābandhān, gṛhṇa hana daha paca sarvaduṣṭasamayasatvān, vajra samaya huṃ phaṭ ।।
