-jyotiṣṭome anyonyaṃ saṃbadhya yajñaśālāto nirgacchatāmṛtvijāṃ vicchedanimittaṃ prāyaścittaṃ śrūyate ayadyudgātāpacchidyetādakṣiṇena yajeta, yadi pratihartāpacchidyeta sarvasvadakṣiṇeneti'tatrodgātṛpratihartroḥ krameṇa vicchede viruddhaprāyaścittayoḥ samuccayāsaṃbhavāt kiṃ pūrvaṃ kāryamuta paramiti saṃdehe rāddhāntaḥ apūrvaṃ paramajātatvādabādhitvaiva jāyate ।
parasyānanyathotpādānnātvabādhena saṃbhavaḥ iti ।
