svaśabdenaiva ca sarvajña īśvaro jagataḥ kāraṇamiti śrūyate śvetāśvatarāṇāṃ mantropaniṣadi sarvajñamīśvaraṃ prakṛtya asa kāraṇaṃ karaṇādhipādhipo na cāsya kaścijjanitā na cādhipaḥ' iti ।
tasmāt sarvajñaṃ brahma jagataḥ kāraṇaṃ, nācetanaṃ pradhānamanyadveti siddham ।।
