atra airāvaṇaśakrau madamānamuktau jātāviti vyaṅgyamapi śabdenocyate । tena yadevocyate tadeva vyaṅgyam । yathā tu vyaṅgyaṃ na tathocyate । yathā gavi śukle calati dṛṣṭe {gauḥ śuklaścalati} iti vikalpaḥ । yadeva dṛṣṭaṃ tadeva vikalpayati na tu yathā dṛṣṭaṃ tathā ।
yato abhinnāsaṃsṛṣṭatvena dṛṣṭam bhedasaṃsargābhyāṃ vikalpayati ।।
