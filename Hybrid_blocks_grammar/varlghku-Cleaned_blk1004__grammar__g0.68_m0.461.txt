caturthī tadarthārthabalihitasukharakṣitaiḥ ।।
caturthyantārthāya yat tadvācinā arthādibhiśca caturthyaṃntaṃ vā prāgvat । yūpāya dāru yūpadāru । । teneha na - randhanāya sthālī । । dvijārthā yavāgūḥ । bhūtabaliḥ । gosukham ।।
