[] atha mādhava-pūrṇimām āsājya, sarvato virajya ca, pūrṇa-śikṣā-vilakṣaṇaṃ-manyamanā mādhavaḥ kiñcana durgamaṃ gahanam avagāhamānaḥ parama-muralī-dhamana-līlayā dhairyam ūnayann, amūḥ pradhūyanati sma; muhuḥ pradharṣayann ākarṣati sma ca, sādhāraṇatayā rādhāpi samāgamiṣyatīti ।
