atha vajrapāṇirmahābodhisatvastānanyān nārāyaṇādān sarvatrilokādhipatīnevamāha । "praviśadhvaṃ mārṣā asminsarvatathāgatavajrasamayamahāmaṇḍale; praviṣṭvā sarvatathāgatasamayamanupālayata!" । ta evamāhuḥ । "yathā jñāpayasi tathā kurma" iti ।।
