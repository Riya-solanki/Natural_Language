tasyāṃ cāsau krathakaiśikasaṃjñau putrāv ajanayat । punas tṛtīyaṃ romapādasaṃjñaṃ kumāram ajījanat । romapādād babhruḥ babhroḥ putro dhṛtiḥ । dhṛteḥ kaiśikaḥ kaiśikasyāpi cediḥ putro abhūd yasya saṃtatau caidyā bhūpālāḥ । krathasya snuṣāputrasya kuntir abhavat ।।
