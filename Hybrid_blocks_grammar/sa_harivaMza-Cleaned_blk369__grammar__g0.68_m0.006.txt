anukūlaṃ tu te deva yadi syān mama tan matam 
rūpaṃ nirvartayāmy adya tava kāntam ariṃdama ।। 
rūpaṃ vivasvataś cāsīt tiryagūrdhvasamaṃ tu vai । tenāsau saṃbhṛto devo rūpeṇa tu vibhāvasuḥ ।। tasmāt tvaṣṭuḥ sa vai vākyaṃ bahu mene prajāpatiḥ । samanujñātavāṃś caiva tvaṣṭāraṃ rūpasiddhaye । 
tato abhyupagamāt tvaṣṭā mārtaṇḍasya vivasvataḥ 
bhramim āropya tat tejaḥ śātayāmāsa bhārata ।। 
kṛtavān aṣṭamaṃ bhāgaṃ na vyaśātayad avyayam ।। yat sūryād vaiṣṇavaṃ tejaḥ śātitaṃ viśvakarmaṇā । tvaṣṭaiva tejasā tena viṣṇoś cakram akalpayat ।। triśūlaṃ caiva śarvasya śibikāṃ dhanadasya ca । śaktiṃ guhasya devānām anyeṣāṃ ca varāyudham ।। tat sarvaṃ tejasā tena viśvakarmā hy akalpayat । 
tato nirbhāsitaṃ rūpaṃ tejasā saṃhṛtena vai 
kāntāt kāntataraṃ draṣṭum adhikaṃ śuśubhe tadā ।। 
mukhe nirvartitaṃ rūpaṃ tasya devasya gopateḥ । tataḥprabhṛti devasya mukham āsīt tu lohitam ।। mukharāgaṃ tu yat pūrvaṃ mārtaṇḍasya mukhacyutam । ādityā dvādaśaiveha saṃbhūtā mukhasaṃbhavāḥ ।। dhātāryamā ca mitraś ca varuṇo aṃśo bhagas tathā । indro vivasvān pūṣā ca parjanyo daśamas tathā । tatas tvaṣṭā tato viṣṇur ajaghanyo jaghanyajaḥ ।। harṣaṃ lebhe tato devo dṛṣṭvādityān svadehajān । gandhaiḥ puṣpair alaṃkārair bhāsvatā mukuṭena ca ।। evaṃ saṃpūjayāmāsa tvaṣṭā vākyam uvāca ha ।। gaccha deveśa svāṃ bhāryāṃ kurūṃś carati cottarān । vaḍavārūpam āsthāya vane carati śāḍvale ।। tāṃ tathārūpam āsthāya svāṃ bhāryāṃ śubhalīlayā । 
dadarśa yogam āsthāya svāṃ bhāryāṃ vaḍavāṃ tataḥ 
adhṛṣyāṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ tejasā niyamena ca ।। 
vaḍavāvapuṣā rājaṃś carantīm akutobhayām । 
so aśvarūpeṇa bhagavāṃs tāṃ mukhe samabhāvayat 
maithunāya viveṣṭantīṃ parapuṃso viśaṅkayā ।।
