śūrasya kuntir nāma sakhābhavat । tasmai cāputrāya pṛthām ātmajāṃ vidhinā śūro dattavān । tāṃ ca pāṇḍur uvāha । tasyāṃ ca dharmānilaśakrair yudhiṣṭhirabhīmārjunākhyās trayaḥ putrāḥ samutpāditāḥ । pūrvam anūḍhāyāś ca bhagavatā bhāsvatā kānīnaḥ karṇo nāma putro ajanyata ।।
