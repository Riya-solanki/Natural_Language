dyudbhyo luṅi ।।
dyutādibhyo luṅaḥ parasmaipadaṃ vā syāt । adyutat, adyotiṣṭa ।। ñiṣvidā snehanamocanayoḥ ।। ñikṣvidā cetyeke ।। ruca dīptāvabhiprītau ca ।। ghuṭa parivartane ।। śubha dīptau ।। kṣubha saṃcalane ।। ṇabha tubha hiṃsāyām ।। sraṃsu bhraṃsu dhvaṃsu avasraṃsane ।। dhvaṃsu gatau ca ।। ।। ।। vartate । vartitā ।।
