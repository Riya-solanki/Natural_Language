-'agnihotraṃ juhoti yavāgūṃ pacati'ityāmnāyate । tatra kramasaṃśaye dravyamantarā yāgāniṣpatteḥ, anyadravyānayane śrutavaiyarthyāt dṛṣṭaprayojane ārādupakārakatvasyānyāyyatvāt aarthācca'yavāgūṃ paktvā juhotīti kramaḥ । tatheha na saṃbhavatīti bhāvaḥ ।
