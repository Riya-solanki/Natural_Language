bhagavanto akhilasaṃsāraduḥkhasaṃghātasya chettāro na hy etāvaj jagaty anyad duḥkham asti yac charīrātmanor viyoge bhavati । tad aham icchāmi sakalalokalocaneṣu vastuṃ na punaḥ śarīragrahaṇaṃ kartum ity evam uktair devair asāv aśeṣabhūtānāṃ netreṣv avatāritaḥ ।।
