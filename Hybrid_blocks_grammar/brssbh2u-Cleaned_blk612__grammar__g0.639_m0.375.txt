yo apyayamaupaniṣadātmapratipattiprayojanaḥ samādhirupadiṣṭo vedānteṣu- aātmā vā are draṣṭavyaḥ śrotavyo mantavyo nididhyasitavya so anveṣṭavyaḥ sa vijijñāsitavyaḥ', aātmetyevaṃ dhyāyatha ātmānam' ityevaṃlakṣaṇaḥ, so apyasatyātmanaḥ kartṛtve nopapadyeta ।
tasmādapyasya kartṛtvasiddhiḥ ।।
