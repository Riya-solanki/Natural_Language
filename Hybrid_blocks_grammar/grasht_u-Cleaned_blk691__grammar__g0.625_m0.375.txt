anyaccāha gaganetyādi ।।
gaganaṃ abhrasāraṃ cikuratailaghṛṣṭaṃ cikuratailaṃ keśatailaṃ pratītaṃ grantheṣu tena ghṛṣṭaṃ marditaṃ gomayaliptaṃ gomayena liptaṃ yathā syāttathā kuliśamūṣāyāṃ vajrābhidhānāyāṃ sudhmātaṃ sat acireṇālpakālena jalākāraṃ bhavatītyanvayaḥ ।।
