susakhīyati te subhrūr asau gopa-matallikā । śape tubhyaṃ lapeyaṃ ca svayaṃ sakhyam urīkuru ।। [] tatra kathaṃcid api tad-āgamane vṛddhābhiḥ kiṃcid ivāvagate kuñjāneva sva-līlayā kañja-locanas tābhiḥ samaṃ rañjayati ।
[] tatra tat-preyasyā vṛddhābhivañcanā-cañcutā, yathā-
yas tvāṃ rātriṣu vṛddhe mām api devo vibhīṣayate । sa tu mantribhir utkalito goṣṭhāt kuñjān vimardayati ।।[] tasmād atrānyathā māvagās tatra ca māgā iti dhvanitam ।
