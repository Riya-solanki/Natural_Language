vitathasya bhavan manyuḥ putro abhūt । bṛhatkṣatramahāvīryanaragargā abhavan manyuputrāḥ । narasya saṃkṛtiḥ saṃkṛte ruciradhīrantidevau । gargāc chinis tato gārgyāḥ śainyāḥ kṣatropetā dvijātayo babhūvuḥ ।।
