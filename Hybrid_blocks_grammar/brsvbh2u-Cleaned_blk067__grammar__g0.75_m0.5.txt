kulālādibhyastāvadbāhyakaraṇāpekṣebhyo devādīnāṃ bāhyanapekṣāṇāmāntarakaraṇāpekṣasṛṣṭhīnāṃ pramāṇena dṛṣṭo yathā viśeṣo nāpahnotuṃ śakyaḥ, yathā tu jāgratsṛṣṭerbāhyakaraṇāpekṣāyāstadanapekṣāntarakaraṇamātrasādhyā dṛṣṭā svapne rathādisṛṣṭiraśakyāpahnotum, evaṃ sarvaśakteḥ parasyā devatāyā āntarakaraṇānapekṣāyā jagatsarjanaṃ śrūyamāṇaṃ na sāmānyato dṛṣṭamātreṇāpahnavamarhatīti ।।
