buddhyādibhiḥ svakārye ahaṅkārādau janayitavye prakṛteranugrahasahāyopekṣyate anyathā buddhiḥ kṣīṇā satī nālamahaṅkāraṃ janayitumiti sakalopi sargastadanugrahamūlaka evetyarthaḥ ।
ayameva prakṛtyāpūra ityucyate tathā ca prakṛtyaivāyaṃ sargo neśvareṇa nāpi brahmopādāno nāpyakāraṇo neśvarādhiṣṭhitaprakṛtita iti sūcyate ।।
