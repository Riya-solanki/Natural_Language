-nanu sarvasya vedasya kāryāvabodhaparatvāt siddhe brahmaṇi vedāntānāmaprāmāṇyaṃ syāditicettatrāhaaviśiṣṭastviti । lokavedayorvākyārthasyāviśeṣāt, mantrāṇāmiva devatādau vedāntānāṃ brahmaṇi prāmāṇyaṃ syādityarthaḥ । yatpunaruktaṃ kāryaparatvaṃ vedasya taddharmaviṣayamunneyamiti vakṣyati ।
