ubhayasaṃskārapakṣe tūbhaye api doṣāḥ samuccitya darśayitavyāḥ, tathā sati hi kaiścideva kaścideva śabdaḥ śrūyata ityupapadyate, anyathā śrotravyavasthā cobhayamapi na syāt ।
kica nānādeśasthaiśca vaktṛbhiruccāritaḥ śabdo yugapannānādeśeṣūpalabhyate tadekasya nityasyānupapannamiti śā bhāṣyam ।
tatra nityasyānupapannamiti pratijñā, ekasyeti hetuḥ, nityo hyasevakaḥ syāt ekasyānupapannaṃ nānādeśeṣūpalambhanamiti ।।
