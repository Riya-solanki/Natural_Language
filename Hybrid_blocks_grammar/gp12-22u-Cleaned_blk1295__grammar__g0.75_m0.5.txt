[] madhumaṅgalas tu sa-krodham ivovāca-yuvarāja ! gardhanatayā kukṣim-bharayas te eta eva muhur aśanāyāṃ smarayantaḥ sarvatra ca ghasmarā eva bhaṇyante, bhikṣāyām akṣāma-samudyamatayā sarvānnīnāś ca lakṣyante, na tu vayam ।
