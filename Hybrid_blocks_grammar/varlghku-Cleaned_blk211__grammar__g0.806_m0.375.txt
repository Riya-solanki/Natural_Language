eranekāco asaṃyogapūrvasya ।।
dhātvavayavasaṃyogapūrvo na bhavati ya ivarṇastadanto yo dhātustadantasyānekāco aṅgasya yaṇajādau pratyaye । pradhyaḥ । pradhyau । pradhyi । evaṃ grāmaṇīḥ ।। anekācaḥ kim ? nīḥ । niyau । ami śasi ca paratvādiyaṅ, niyam ।। asaṃyogapūrvasya kim ? suśriyau । yavakriyau ।।
