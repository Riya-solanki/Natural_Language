vijayaś ca dhṛtiṃ putram avāpa । tasyāpi dhṛtavrataḥ putro abhūt । dhṛtavratāt satyakarmā satyakarmaṇas tv adhirathaḥ । yo asau gaṅgāṃ gato mañjūṣāgataṃ pṛthāpaviddhaṃ karṇaṃ putram avāpa । karṇād vṛṣasena ity ete aṅgāḥ ।।
