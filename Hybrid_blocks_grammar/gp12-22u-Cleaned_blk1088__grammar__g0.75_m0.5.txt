[] atha taṃ prāhuḥ-hā, hā, drutam anena haimanena pavanena śīta-parītatayā jīvana-viparītatāṃ vayam āyātāḥ, tasmān marma-bhedakaṃ narma vṛthā mā kṛthāḥ, kintu vastram eva sevaya । yad vā vāñchasi, tat tu lāñchanam aṅgīkṛtyāpi kariṣyāmaḥ ।
