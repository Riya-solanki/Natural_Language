[] kiṃ ca, sarvāntaryāmy apy enam evorarīcarīkarīti । dṛśyatām asyāṃ bhāsamānāyāṃ sabhāyāṃ sarveṣāṃ netra-paṭalī-ṣaṭpadaval-līlāyamānā kevalam asya mukhaṃ kamalam iva saṃvalate । tathā prathamata eva tad-ānukūlyam atrākalpyate । parikalpyatām apīdaṃ mama nāmnaiva । tasmād asmākam ayam eva rājeti ।
