yathā tatraiva -vatsa rāmabhadra praśasto muhūrto vartate । tad adhyāssva pitryaṃ siṃhāsanam ity upakramya, vasiṣṭhaḥ-rāmabhadra dhanyo' si । yasya te bhagavān kubero' rthī ity antena vasiṣṭhena rāmabhadrasyābhiṣekāṅgīkaraṇa-kubera-vimāna-pratyarpaṇa-rūpayor arthayor darśanāt pūrva-bhāvaḥ ।
