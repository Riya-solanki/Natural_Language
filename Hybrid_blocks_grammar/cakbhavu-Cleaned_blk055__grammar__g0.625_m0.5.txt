Viv mahānādādyakalāvacūlāya ṣoḍaśasvararūpakalākalāpārambhādyavarṇarūpaśiroṅkuśarūpayā anacchakalayā baindhavaṃ tīrtham ubhayaśūnyabilarūpaṃ saṃśodhya nirgranthibandhaṃ vidhāya, nirodhanādanādāntavartiparamatṛptitālakṣaṇarasena kāryam abhiṣecanam ।।
