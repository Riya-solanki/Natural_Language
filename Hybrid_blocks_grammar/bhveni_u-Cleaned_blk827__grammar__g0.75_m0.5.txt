SktCh tataśca svāminā suciraṃ vilapya parijanopanītamanyaṃ rathaṃ prekṣya dīrghaṃ niḥśvasya mayi dṛṣṭirvinikṣiptā । sundaraka ehīti bhaṇitaṃ ca । tato ahamupagataḥ svāmisamīpam । tato apanīya śīrṣasthānātpaṭṭikāṃ śarīrasaṃgalitaiḥ śoṇitabindubhirliptamukhaṃ bāṇaṃ kṛtvābhilikhya preṣito devasya saṃdeśaḥ ।
