eṣā ca virodhabādhinī na virodhaḥ bhinnādharatayaiva dvayoriha virodhitāyāḥ pratibhāsāt । virodhe tu virodhitvam ekāśrayaniṣṭamanuktamapi paryavasitam apavādaviṣayaparihāreṇotsargasya vyavasthiteḥ । tathā caivaṃ nidarśitam ।।
