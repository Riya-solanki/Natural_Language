pratipādayitumupakrāntamevānenocyate- nāvidyamānasyātmana utpattirna vidyamānasya vināśaḥ sarve evātmano anutpattivināśadharmāṇo nityā iti, na punaḥ kāryajātasya sarvasya nityatvābhiprāyam, asarvagatatvādanupalabdhivirodhāccetyuktam ।।
