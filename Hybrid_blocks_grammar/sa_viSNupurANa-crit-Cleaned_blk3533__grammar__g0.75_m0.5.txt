bhagavann ebhiḥ sagaratanayair asamañjasaś caritam anugamyate । katham evam ebhir anusaradbhir jagad bhaviṣyatīty ārtajagatparitrāṇāya ca bhagavato atra śarīragrahaṇam ity ākarṇya bhagavān alpair eva dinair ete vinaṅkṣyantīty uktavān ।।
