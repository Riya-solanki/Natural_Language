andhaka uvāca ।
oṃ namasye bhāvānīṃ bhūtabhavyapriyāṃ lokadhātrīṃ janitrīṃ skandamātaraṃ mahādevapriyāṃ dhāriṇīṃ syandinīṃ cetanāṃ trailokyamātaraṃ dharitrīṃ devamātaramathejyāṃ smṛtiṃ dayāṃ lajjāṃ kāntimagryāmasūyāṃ matiṃ sadāpāvanīṃ daityasainyakṣayakarīṃ mahāmāyāṃ vaijayantī suśubhāṃ kālarātriṃ govindabhaginīṃ śailarājapūtrīṃ sarvadevārcitāṃ sarvabhūtārcitāṃ vidyāṃ sarasvatīṃ trinayanamahiṣīṃ namasyāmī mṛḍānīṃ śaraṇyāṃ śaraṇamupāgato ahaṃ namo namaste ।
itthaṃ stutā sāndhakena parituṣṭā vibhāvarī ।
prāha putra prasannāsmi vṛṇuṣva paramuttamam ।।
