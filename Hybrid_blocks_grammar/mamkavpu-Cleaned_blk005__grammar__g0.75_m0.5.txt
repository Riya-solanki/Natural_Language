niyatiśaktyā niyatarūpā sukhaduḥkhamohasvabhāvā paramāṇvādyupādānakarmādisahakārikāraṇaparatantrā ṣaḍrasā na ca hṛdyaiva taiḥ, tādṛśī brahmaṇo nirmitirnirmāṇam । etadvilakṣaṇā tu kavivāṅnirmitiḥ । ata eva jayati । jayatyarthena ca namaskāra ākṣipyate iti tāṃ pratyasmi praṇata iti labhyate ।।
