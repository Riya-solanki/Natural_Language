[vratino jaṭilā muṇḍāḥ] vratināmapi dvau bhedau jaṭitvaṃ muṇḍitā ca।jaṭilo api vakṣyamāṇabhautikanaiṣṭhikabhedād dvividhaḥ। [teṣu] sarveṣu caiteṣu madhyād [agryāḥ] brāhmaṇāḥ [bhasmapāṇḍarāḥ] bhasmoddhūlitasarvāṅgāḥ। [bhūmipādayaḥ tilakaiḥ puṇḍrakaiḥ paṭṭaiḥ bhūṣitāḥ] kṣatriyāstilakālaṃkṛtatanavaḥ। vaiśyāstripuṇḍrakamaṇḍitāḥ। śūdrāstu paṭṭākṛtinā bhasmoddhūlanena bhūṣitāḥ syuriti śeṣaḥ।।
