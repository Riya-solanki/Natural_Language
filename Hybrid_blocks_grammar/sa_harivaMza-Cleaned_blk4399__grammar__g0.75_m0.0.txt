praveśayaināṃ bhavanaṃ pūjyā hy eṣā snuṣā mama 
cirapranaṣṭaṃ ca sutaṃ bhajasya punarāgatam ।। 
śrutvā tu vacanaṃ devī kṛṣṇenodāhṛtaṃ tadā । praharṣam atulaṃ lebhe rukmiṇī vākyam abravīt ।। aho dhanyatarāsmīti vīraputrasamāgamāt । adya me saphalaḥ kāmaḥ pūrṇaś cādya manorathaḥ । cirapranaṣṭaputrasya darśanaṃ priyayā saha ।। āgaccha putra bhavanaṃ viśasva saha bhāryayā ।। tato abhivādya caraṇau govindaṃ mātaraṃ ca saḥ । pradyumnaḥ pūjayām āsa halinaṃ ca mahābalam ।। utthāpya taṃ pariṣvajya mūrdhny upāghrāya vīryavān । pradyumnaṃ balināṃ śreṣṭhaṃ keśavaḥ paravīrahā ।। snuṣāṃ cotthāpya tāṃ devīṃ rukmiṇī rukmabhūṣaṇāṃ । pariṣvajyopasaṃgṛhya snehād gadgadabhāṣiṇī ।। sametaṃ bhavanaṃ patnyā śacyendram aditir yathā । praveśayām āsa tadā rukmiṇī sutam āgatam । evam uktā tu kṛṣṇena rukmiṇī yoṣitāṃ varā । putraṃ prītyā pariṣvajya mūrdhni cāghrāya tāṃ snuṣām । gṛhaṃ praveśayām āsa snuṣayā saha bhāminī । 
hṛto yadaiva pradyumnaḥ śambareṇātmaghātinā 
tam eva māsaṃ sāmbas tu jāmbavatyām ajāyata ।।
