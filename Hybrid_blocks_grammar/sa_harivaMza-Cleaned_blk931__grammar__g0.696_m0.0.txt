yadānyebhyo na bibheti yadā cāsmān na bibhyati 
yadā necchati na dveṣṭi brahma saṃpadyate tadā ।। 
yā dustyajā durmatibhir yā na jīryati jīryataḥ । yo asauprāṇāntiko rogas tāṃ tṛṣṇāṃ tyajataḥ sukham ।। jīryanti jīryataḥ keśā dantā jīryanti jīryataḥ । dhanāśā jīvitāśā ca jīryato api na jīryati ।। yac ca kāmasukhaṃ loke yac ca divyaṃ mahat sukham । tṛṣṇākṣayasukhasyaite nārhataḥ ṣoḍaśīṃ kalām । tāṃ tṛṣṇāṃ duḥkhanivahāṃ śarmakāmo drutaṃ tyajet । cakṣuḥśrotre ca jīryete tṛṣṇaikātaruṇāyate । 
evam uktvā sa rājarṣiḥ sadāraḥ prāviśad vanam 
kālena mahatā cāpi cacāra vipulaṃ tapaḥ ।।
