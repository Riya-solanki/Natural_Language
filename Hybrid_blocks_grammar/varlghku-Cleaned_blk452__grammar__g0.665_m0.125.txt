liṅnimitte ḷṅ kriyātipattau ।।
hetuhetumadbhāvādi liṅnimittaṃ tatra bhaviṣyatyarthe ḷṅ syāt kriyāyā aniṣpattau gamyamānāyām । abhaviṣyatām । abhaviṣyaḥ । abhaviṣyata । abhaviṣyāva । suvṛṣṭiścedabhaviṣyattadā subhikṣamabhaviṣyat, ityādi jñeyam ।। ata sātatyagamane ।। ।।
