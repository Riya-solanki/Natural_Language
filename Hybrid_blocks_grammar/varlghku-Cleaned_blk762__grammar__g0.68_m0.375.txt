yaṅo'ci ca ।।
yaṅo'ci pratyaye luk syāt, cakārāttaṃ vināpi kvacit । anaimittiko aya mantaraṅgatvādādau bhavati । tataḥ pratyayalakṣaṇena yaṅantatvāddvitvam । dhātutvāllaḍādayaḥ । śeṣātkartarīti parasmaipadam । carkarītaṃ cetyadādau pāṭhācchapo luk ।।
