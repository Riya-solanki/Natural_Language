arthākṛṣṭiṃ vidhātuṃ kaṭuraṭanapaṭur dāmbhikaḥ śrījaḍānāṃ jānāty anyāsahiṣṇur vitathanijaguṇastotrapāṭheṣv alajjaḥ ।
āryaḥ kuryān na sevaṃ kulavinayaguṇais tena mūko arthanāyāṃ tīkṣṇānāṃ mārgaṇānāṃ bhavati hi purataḥ kṣmāpater lakṣalābhaḥ ।।
