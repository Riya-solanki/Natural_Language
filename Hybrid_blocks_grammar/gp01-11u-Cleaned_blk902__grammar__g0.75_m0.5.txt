[] kṛṣṇas tad etad aśrṇvann iva sāsraṃ pitṛ-mukham īkṣate sma । rohiṇī tu saṅkarṣaṇaṃ tasya saṅkarṣaṇāya sañjñayā jñāpayāmāsa । tena gṛhīta-hastaḥ punar asau nirasta-tad-dhastatayā vidrutya pitur utsaṅga-saṅgī babhūva । tathābhavaṃś ca bhujābhyām avaguṇṭhita-tat-kaṇṭhaḥ kṛta-bāṣpa-vṛṣṭiṃ tad-dṛṣṭim eva paśyaṃs tam atīva vaśyam ācarann āsīt ।
