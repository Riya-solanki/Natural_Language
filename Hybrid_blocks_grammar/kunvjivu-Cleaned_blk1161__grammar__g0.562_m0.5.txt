cakṣuryasya tavānanādapagataṃ nābhūtkvacinnarvṛtaṃ yenaiṣā satataṃ tvadekaśayanaṃ vakṣaḥsthalī kalpitā ।
yenoktāsi vinā tvayā bata jagacchūnyaṃ kṣaṇājjāyate so ayaṃ dambhadhṛtavrataḥ priyatame kartuṃ kimapyudyataḥ ।।
