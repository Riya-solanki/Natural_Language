kṛṣṇo api balabhadram āha । tāvad atraiva syandane bhavatā stheyam aham enam adhamācāraṃ padātinam anugamya yāvad ghātayāmi । atra hi bhūbhāge dṛṣṭadoṣā hayāḥ । naite aśvā bhavatemaṃ bhūmibhāgam ullaṅghya neyāḥ ।।
