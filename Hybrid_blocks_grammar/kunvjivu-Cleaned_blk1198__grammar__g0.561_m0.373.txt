"sāṅkādeḥ kāpi vakratā" "aṅkādeḥ" aṅkasargādeḥ prakaraṇasya "sā kāpi" alaukikī "vakratā" vakrabhāvo bhavatīti saṃbandhaḥ ।
"yatrāṅgirasaniṣyandanikaṣaḥ ko api" lakṣyate" "yatra"yasyāṃ aṅgī yaḥ "rasaḥ" prāṇarūpaḥ, tasya niṣyandaḥ pravāhaḥ tasya, kāñcanasyeva nikaṣaḥ parīkṣopalavadviṣayaviśeṣaḥ "ko api" abhūtanirmāṇanirupamo lakṣyate yojane dayairiva viśeṣaḥ ।
kiṃ viśiṣṭaḥ "pūrvottarairasaṃpādyaḥ"prakparavṛttibhiraṅkādyaiḥ saṃpādayitumaśakyaḥ ।
idamatra tātparyam ।
pradhānarasasarvasvakrīḍāniketanaṃ tātkimapi prakaraṇaṃ prakaṭataraṃ ca vakratāvicchittirvidyotate ।
yadīyalāvaṇyātiśayaṃ manāṅmātramapi pūrvāṇyaparāṇi vā prakaraṇāntarāṇi nānukartuṃ śakruvanti ।
yathā vikramorvaśyāmunmattāṅkaḥ tatra hi prastutarasāsādhāraṇa mādhuryasaṃpattyā vipralambhaśṛṅgārasyāṅginaḥ sa ko api deḥ rasaniṣyandaparispandaḥ paristīryate, yaḥ na kevalaṃ prakaraṇāntare prabandhāntare apyaśakyakāmanīyakakaṇikānukāraḥ ।
tathā ca tadupakrama eva rājā ā durātman, tiṣṭha tiṣṭha ! kva nu khalu priyatamāmādāya gacchisi ? kathaṃ śailaśikharāt gaganamutplutya bāṇairmāmabhivarṣati ? kathaṃ vipralabdho asmi
