atrāpi jvalantyombudhārāḥ sūryamaṇḍalāt niṣpatantyo na saṃbhavantītyupanibadhyamāno artho anaucityameva puṣṇāti ।।
utprekṣāyāmapi saṃbhāvanaṃ dhruvevādaya eva śabdā vaktuṃ sahante na yathāśabdo api kevalasyāsya sādhārmyameva pratipādayituṃ paryāptatvāt tasya cāsyāmavivakṣitatvāditi tatrāśaktirasyāvācakatvaṃ doṣaḥ ।
