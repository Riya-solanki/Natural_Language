"athamudrāsādhanamicchettena sarvatathāgatasatvavajrimudrāṃ badhvā paṭasyāgrato vajravācāṃ śatasahasraṃ yathākāmakaraṇīyatayā yathāvad bandhan muñcaṃśca japet । tato ante sakalāṃ rātriṃ, aviśramato kiñcitkālaṃ muñcan, yathāvacca bandhan japet । tato mudrā jvalatyāviśatyuttiṣṭhati vācaṃ muñcatīti । tato mudrābandhanenotpatati kāmarūpī bhavati antardhāti sarvakarmāṇi ca karoti । sarvamudrāśca bandhamātrā yathāvatsarvakarmāṇi kurvantī-"tyāha bhagavān vajradharaḥ ।।
