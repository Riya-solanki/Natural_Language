yadapyuktam {kāvyaśobhāyāḥ kartāro dharmā guṇās tadatiśayahetavastvalaṅkārāḥ} iti tadapi na yuktam । yataḥ kiṃ samastairguṇaiḥ kāvyavyavahāraḥ uta katipayaiḥ । yadi samastaiḥ tatkathamasamastaguṇā gauḍī pāñcālī ca rītiḥ kāvyasyātmā ।
atha katipayaiḥ tataḥ
