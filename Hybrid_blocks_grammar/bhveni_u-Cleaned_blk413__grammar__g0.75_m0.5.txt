SktCh yadi sindhurājavadhadivasa iva divase divase samarakarma pratipadyate arjunastadā paryāptabhṛtakoṣṭhāgāraṃ māṃsaśoṇitairme gṛhaṃ bhaviṣyati । atha kva nu khalu rudhirapriyo bhaviṣyati । tadyāvadetasminsamare priyabhartāraṃ rudhirapriyamanveṣyāmi । bhavatu śabdāyiṣye tāvat । are rudhirapriya rudhirapriya ita ehīta ehi ।
