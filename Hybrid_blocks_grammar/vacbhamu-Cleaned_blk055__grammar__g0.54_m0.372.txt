syādetat ।
ātmā jaḍo api sarvārthajñāneṣu bhāsamāno api kartaiva na karma, parasamavetakriyāphalaśālitvābhāvāt, caitravat ।
yathā hi caitrasamavetakriyayā caitranagaraprāptāvubayasamavetāyāmapi kriyamāṇāyāṃ nagarasyaiva karmatā, parasamavetakriyāphalaśālitvāt, na tu caitrasya kriyāphalaśālino api, caitrasamavāyādgamanakrīyāyā iti ।
