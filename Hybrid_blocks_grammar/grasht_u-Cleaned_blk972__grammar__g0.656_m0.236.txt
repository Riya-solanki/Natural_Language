ityuktavidhānena kalkīkṛtaṃ sūtaṃ bhuktvā amaratāṃ devatvaṃ gacchet ।।
iti kiṃ ghanakāntamadhughṛtādisaṃyuktaṃ ghano'bhrakaḥ kāntaṃ lohajāti madhu kṣaudraṃ ghṛtam ājyaṃ ādiśabdāt sitā grāhyā etaiḥ saṃyuktaṃ sat kalkīkṛtaṃ idaṃ ca pradhānaṃ kṣetrīkaraṇaṃ kṣetrī kriyate'neneti ।।
ityāroṭakriyā ।।
