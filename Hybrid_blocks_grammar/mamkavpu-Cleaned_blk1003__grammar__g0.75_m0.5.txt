ityatrādyamardhaṃ bādhyatvenaivokyam । jīvitādapi adhikamapāṅgabhaṅgasyāsthiratvamiti prasiddhabhaṅguropamānatayopāttaṃ śāntameva puṣṇāti na punaḥ śṛṅgārasyātra pratītistadaṅgāpratipatterḥ । na tu vineyonmukhīkaraṇamatra parihāraḥ śāntaśṛṅgārayornairantaryasyābhāvāt । nāpi kāvyaśobhākaraṇaṃ rasāntarādanuprāsamātrādvā tathābhāvāt ।।
