asyaivopakrame aantavatkila te sāma'itiantavattvadoṣeṇa śālāvatyasyeti ।
na cākāśaśabdo gauṇo api vilambitapratipattiḥ, tatra tatra brahmaṇyākāśaśabdasya tatparyāyasya ca prayogaprācuryādatyantābhyāsenāsyāpi mukhyavatpratipatteravilambanāditi darśanārthaṃ brahmaṇi prayogaprācuryaṃ vaidikaṃ nidarśitaṃ bhāṣyakṛtā ।
tatraiva ca prathamāvagatānuguṇyenottaraṃ nīyate, yatra tadanyathā kartuṃ śakyam ।
yatra tu na śakyaṃ tatrottarānuguṇyenaiva prathamaṃ nīyata ityāha-vākyopakrame apīti ।।
