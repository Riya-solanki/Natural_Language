yā gṛhītvā kṛtinam ātmānaṃ manyeta na ca samānair nindyeta tasyāṃ pravṛttir iti ghoṭakamukhaḥ
tasyā varaṇe mātā-pitarau saṃbandhinaś ca prayateran mitrāṇi ca gṛhīta-vākyāny ubhaya-saṃbaddhāni
tāny anyeṣāṃ varayitṝṇāṃ doṣān pratyakṣān āgamikāṃś ca śrāvayeyuḥ kaulān pauruṣeyān abhiprāya-saṃvardhakāṃś ca nāyaka-guṇān viśeṣataś ca kanyā-mātur anukūlāṃs tadātvāyati-yuktān darśayeyuḥ
daiva-cintaka-rūpaś ca śakuna-nimitta-graha-lagna-bala-lakṣaṇa-darśanena nāyakasya bhaviṣyantam artha-saṃyogaṃ kalyāṇam anuvarṇayet
apare punar asyānyato viśiṣṭena kanyā-lābhena kanyā-mātaram unmādayeyuḥ
daiva-nimitta-śakunopaśrutīnām ānulomyena kanyāṃ varayed dadyāc ca
na yadṛcchayā kevala-mānuṣāyeti ghoṭakamukhaḥ
suptāṃ rudatīṃ niṣkrāntāṃ varaṇe parivarjayet
apraśasta-nāmadheyāṃ ca guptāṃ dattāṃ ghonāṃ pṛṣatām ṛṣabhāṃ vinatāṃ vikaṭāṃ vimuṇḍāṃ śuci-dūṣitāṃ sāṃkarikīṃ rākāṃ phalinīṃ mitrāṃ svanujāṃ varṣakarīṃ ca varjayet
nakṣatrākhyāṃ nadī-nāmnīṃ vṛkṣa-nāmnīṃ ca garhitām la-kāra-rephopāntāṃ ca varaṇe parivarjayet
yasyāṃ manaś-cakṣor nibandhas tasyām ṛddhiḥ netarām ādriyeta ity eke
tasmāt pradāna-samaye kanyām udāra-veṣāṃ sthāpayeyuḥ aparāhṇikaṃ ca nityaṃ prāsādhitāyāḥ sakhībhiḥ saha krīḍā yajña-vivāhādiṣu jana-saṃdrāveṣu prāyatnikaṃ darśanam tathotsaveṣu ca paṇya-sadharmatvāt
varaṇārtham upagatāṃś ca bhadra-darśanān pradakṣiṇa-vācaś ca tat-saṃbandhi-saṅgatān puruṣān maṅgalaiḥ pratigṛhṇīyuḥ
kanyāṃ caiṣām alaṃkṛtām anyāpadeśena darśayeyuḥ
daivaṃ parīkṣaṇaṃ cāvadhiṃ sthāpayeyuḥ ā pradāna-niścayāt
snānādiṣu niyujyamānā varayitāraḥ sarvaṃ bhaviṣyatīty uktvā na tad-ahar evābhyupagaccheyuḥ
deśa-pravṛtti-sātmyād vā brāhma-prājāpatyārṣa-daivānām anyatamena vivāhena śāstrataḥ pariṇayet

iti śrīvātsyāyanīye kāma-sūtre sāṃprayogike tṛtīye adhikaraṇe varaṇa-vidhānaṃ saṃbandha-niścayaś ca prathamo adhyāyaḥ

dvitīyo adhyāyaḥ
prakaraṇa kanyā-visrambhaṇa-prakaraṇam saṃgatayos tri-rātram adhaḥ śayyā brahmacaryaṃ kṣāra-lavaṇa-varjam āhāras tathā saptāhaṃ satūrya-maṅgala-snānaṃ prasādhanaṃ saha-bhojanaṃ ca prekṣā saṃbandhināṃ ca pūjanam iti sārvavarṇikam

tasminn etāṃ niśi vijane mṛdubhir upacārair upakrameta
tri-rātram avacanaṃ hi stambham iva nāyakaṃ paśyantī kanyā nirvidyeta paribhavec ca tṛtīyām iva prakṛtim iti bābhravīyāḥ
upakrameta visrambhayec ca na tu brahmacaryam ativarteta iti vātsyāyānaḥ
upakramamāṇaś ca na prasahya kiṃ cid ācaret
kusuma-sadharmāṇo hi yoṣitaḥ sukumāropakramāḥ tās tv anadhigata-viśvāsaiḥ prasabham upakramyamāṇaḥ saṃprayoga-dveṣiṇyo bhavanti tasmāt sāmnaivopacaret
yuktyāpi tu yataḥ prasaram upalabhet tenaivānu praviśet
tat-priyeṇāliṅgenācaritena nātikālatvāt
pūrva-kāyeṇa copakramet viṣahyatvāt
dīpāloke vigāḍha-yauvanāyāḥ pūrva-saṃstutāyāḥ bālāyā apūrvāyāś cāndhakāre
aṅgī-kṛta-pariṣvaṅgāyāś ca vadanena tāmbūla-dānam tad-apratipadyamānāṃ ca sāntvanair vākyaiḥ śapathaiḥ pratiyācitaiḥ pāda-patanaiś ca grāhayet vrīḍā-yuktāpi yoṣid atyanta-kruddhāpi na pāda-patanam ativartate iti sārvatrikam
tad-dāna-prasaṅgeṇa mṛdu viśadam akāhalam asyāś cumbanam
tatra siddhām ālāpayet
tac-chravaṇārthaṃ yat kiṃ cid alpākṣaram abhidheyam ajānann iva pṛcchet
tatra niṣpratipattim anudvejayan sāntvanā-yuktaṃ bahuśa eva pṛcchet
yatrāpi avadantīṃ nirbadhnīyāt
sarvā eva hi kanyāḥ puruṣeṇa prayujyamānaṃ vacanaṃ viṣahante na tu laghu-miśrām api vācaṃ vadanti iti ghoṭakamukhaḥ
nirbadhyamānā tu śiraḥ-kampena prativacanāni yojayet kalahe tu na śiraḥ kampayet
icchasi māṃ necchasi vā kiṃ te aham rucito na rucito veti pṛṣṭā ciraṃ sthitvā nirbadhyamānā tad-ānukulyena śiraḥ kampayet prapañcyamānā tu vivadet
saṃstutā cet sakhīm anukūlām ubhayato api visrabdhāṃ tām antarā kṛtvā kathāṃ yojayet tasminn adho-mukhī vihaset tāṃ cātivādinīm adhikṣiped vivadec ca sā tu parihāsārtham idam anayoktam iti cānuktam api brūyāt tatra tām apanudya prativacanārtham abhyarthyamānā tūṣṇīm āsīta nirbadhyamānā tu nāham evaṃ bravīmīty avyaktākṣaram anavasitārthaṃ vacanaṃ brūyāt nāyakaṃ tu vihasantī kadā cit kaṭākṣaiḥ prekṣeta ity ālāpa-yojanam
evaṃ jāta-paricayā cānirvadantī tat-samīpe yācitaṃ tāmbūlaṃ vilepanaṃ srajaṃ nidadhyāt uttarīye vāsya nibadhnīyāt
yathāyuktām ācchuritakena stana-mukulayor upari spṛśet
vāryamāṇaś ca tvam api māṃ pariṣvajasva tato naivam ācariṣyāmīti sthityā pariṣvañjayet svaṃ ca hastam ā nābhideśāt prasārya nirvartayet krameṇa cainām utsaṅgam āropyādhikam adhikam upakramet apratipadyamānāṃ ca bhīṣayet
ahaṃ khalu tava dantapadāny adhare kariṣyāmi stana-pṛṣṭhe ca nakha-padam ātmanaś ca svayaṃ kṛtvā tvayā kṛtam iti te sakhī-janasya purataḥ kathayiṣyāmi sā tvaṃ kim atra vakṣyasīti bāla-vibhīṣikair bāla-pratyāyanaiś ca śanair enāṃ pratārayet
dvitīyasyāṃ tṛtīyasyāṃ ca rātrau kiṃ cid adhikaṃ visrambhitāṃ hastena yojayet
sarvāṅgikaṃ cumbanam upakrameta
ūrvoś copari vinyasta-hastaḥ saṃvāhana-kriyāyāṃ siddhāyāṃ krameṇoru-mūlam api saṃvāhayet nivārite saṃvāhane ko doṣa ity ākulayed enām tac ca sthirīkuryāt tatra siddhāyā guhya-deśābhimarśanam
raśana-viyojanaṃ nīvī-visraṃsanaṃ vasana-parivartanam ūru-mūla-saṃvāhanaṃ ca ete cāsyānyāpadeśāḥ yukta-yantrāṃ rañjayet na tv akāle vrata-khaṇḍanam
anuśiṣyāc ca ātmānurāgaṃ darśayet manorathāṃś ca pūrva-kālikān anuvarṇayet āyatyāṃ ca tad-ānukūlyena pravṛttiṃ pratijānīyāt sapatnībhyaś ca sādhvasam avacchindyāt kālena ca krameṇa vimukta-kanyā-bhāvām anudvejayann upakrameta iti kanyā-visrambhanam

natyantam ānulomyena na cātiprātilomyataḥ siddhiṃ gacchati kanyāsu tasmān madhyena sādhayet
ātmanaḥ prīti-jananaṃ yoṣitāṃ māna-vardhanam kanyā-visrambhaṇaṃ vetti yaḥ sa tāsāṃ priyo bhavet
atilajjānvītety evaṃ yas tu kanyām upekṣate so anabhiprāya-vedīti paśuvat paribhūyate sahasā vāpy upakrāntā kanyā-cittam avindatā bhayaṃ vitrāsam udvegaṃ sadyo dveṣaṃ ca gacchati
sā prīti-yogam aprāptā tenodvegena dūṣitā puruṣa-dveṣiṇī vā syād vidviṣṭā vā tato anya-gā

prakaraṇa bālopakramaṇam

dhana-hīnas tu guṇa-yukto api madhyastha-guṇo hīnāpadeśo vā sadhano vā prātiveśyaḥ mātṛ-pitṛ-bhrātṛṣu ca paratantraḥ bāla-vṛttir ucita-praveśo vā kanyām alabhyatvān na varayet
bālyāt prabhṛti caināṃ svayam evānurañjayet
tathā-yuktaś ca mātula-kulānuvartī dakṣiṇapathe bāla eva mātrā pitrā ca viyuktaḥ paribhūta-kalpo dhanotkarṣād alabhyāṃ mātula-duhitaram anyasmai vā pūrva-dattāṃ sādhayet
anyām api bāhyāṃ spṛhayet
bālāyām evaṃ sati dharmādhigame saṃvananaṃ ślāghyam iti ghoṭakamukhaḥ
tayā saha puṣpāvacayaṃ grathanaṃ gṛhakaṃ duhitṛkā-krīḍāyojanaṃ bhakta-pāna-karaṇam iti kurvīta paricayasya vayasaś cānurūpyāt
ākarṣa-krīḍā paṭṭikā-krīḍā muṣṭi-dyūta-kṣullakādi-dyūtāni madhyamāṅguli-grahaṇaṃ ṣaṭ-pāṣāṇakādīni ca deśyāni tatsātmyāt tad-apta-dāsa-ceṭibhis tayā ca sahānukrīḍeta
kṣveditakāni sunimilita-kām ārabdhikāṃ lavaṇa-vīthikām anila-tāḍitakāṃ godhūma-pañjikām aṅguli-tāḍikāṃ sakhībhir anyāni ca deśyāni
yāṃ ca viśvāsyām asyāṃ manyeta tayā saha nirantarāṃ prītiṃ kuryāt paricayāṃś ca budhyeta
dhātreyikāṃ cāsyāḥ priya-hitābhyām adhikam upagṛhṇīyāt sā hi prīyamāṇā viditākārāpy apratyādiśantī taṃ tāṃ ca yojayituṃ śaknutyāt anabhihitāpi pratyācāryakam
aviditākārāpi hi guṇān evānurāgāt prakāśayet yathā prayojyānurajyeta
yatra yatra ca kautukaṃ prayojyāyās tad anu praviśya sādhayet
krīḍanaka-dravyāṇi yāny apūrvāṇi yāny anyāsāṃ viralaśo vidyeraṃs tāny asyā ayatnena saṃpādayet
tatra kandukam aneka-bhakti-citram alpa-kālāntaritam anyad anyac ca saṃdarśayet tathā sūtra-dāru-gavala-gaja-dantamayīr duhitṛkā madhūcchiṣṭa-piṣṭa-mṛnmayīś ca
bhakta-pākārtham asyā mahānasikasya ca darśanam
kāṣṭha-meḍhrakayoś ca saṃyuktayoś ca strī-puṃsayor ajaiḍakānāṃ deva-kula-gṛhakāṇāṃ ca śuka-parabhṛta-madana-sārikā-lāva-kukkuṭa-tiri-piñjarakāṇāṃ ca vicitrākṛti-saṃyuktānāṃ jala-bhājanānāṃ ca yantrikāṇāṃ vīṇikānāṃ paṭolikānām alaktaka-manaḥśilā-haritāla-hiṅgulaka-śyāmavarṇakādīnāṃ tathā candana-kuṅkumayoḥ pūga-phalānāṃ pattrāṇāṃ kāla-yuktānāṃ ca sakti-viṣaye pracchannaṃ dānaṃ prakāśa-dravyāṇāṃ ca prakāśam yathā ca sarvābhiprāya-saṃvardhakam enaṃ manyeta tathā prayatitavyam
prachanna-dānasya tu kāraṇam ātmano guru-janād bhayaṃ khyāpayet deyasya cānyena spṛhaṇīyatvam iti
vardhamānānurāgaṃ cākhyānake manaḥ kurvatīm anvarthābhiḥ kathābhiś citta-hāriṇībhiś ca rañjayet
vismayeṣu prasahyamānām indra-jālaiḥ prayogair vismāpayet kalāsu kautukinīṃ tat-kauśalena gīta-priyāṃ śruti-harair gītaiḥ āśva-yujyām aṣṭamī-candrake kaumudyām utasaveṣu yātrāyāṃ grahaṇe gṛhācāre vā vicitrair āpīḍaiḥ karṇa-pattra-bhaṅgaiḥ sikthaka-pradhānair vastrāṅgulīyaka-bhūṣaṇa-dānaiś ca no ced doṣa-karāṇi manyeta
anya-puruṣa-viśeṣābhijñatayā dhātreyikāsyāḥ puruṣa-pravṛttau cātuḥ-ṣaṣṭikān yogān grāhayet
tad-grahaṇopadeśena ca prayojyāyāṃ rati-kauśalam ātmanaḥ prakāśayet
udāra-veṣaś ca svayam anupahata-darśanaś ca syāt bhāvaṃ ca kurvatīm iṅgitākāraiḥ sūcayet
yavatayo hi saṃsṛṣṭam abhīkṣṇa-darśanaṃ ca puruṣaṃ prathamaṃ kāmayante kāmayamānā api tu nābhiyuñjata iti prāyovādaḥ

bāla-krīḍanakair bālā kalābhir yauvane sthitā vatsalā cāpi saṃgrāhyā viśvāsya-jana-saṃgrahāt

caturtho adhyāyaḥ
prakaraṇa eka-puruṣābhiyoga-prakaraṇam

darśiteṅgitākārāṃ kanyām upāyato abhiyuñjīta
dyūte krīḍanakeṣu ca vivadamānaḥ sākāram asyāḥ pāṇim avalambeta
yathoktaṃ ca spṛṣṭakādikam āliṅgana-vidhiṃ vidadhyāt
pattra-cchedya-kriyāyāṃ ca svābhiprāya-sūcakaṃ mithunam asyā darśayet
evam anyad viralaśo darśayet
jala-krīḍāyāṃ tad-dūrato apsu nimagnaḥ samīpam asyā gatvā spṛṣṭvā caināṃ tatraivonmajjayet
navapattrikādiṣu ca saviśeṣa-bhāva-nivedanam
ātma-duḥkhasyānirvedena kathanam
svapnasya ca bhāva-yuktasyānyāpadeśena
prekṣaṇake sva-jana-samāje vā samīpopaveśanam tatrānyāpadiṣṭaṃ sparśanam
apāśrayārthaṃ ca caraṇena caraṇasya pīḍanam
tataḥ śanakair ekaikām aṅgulim abhispṛśet
pādāṅuṣṭhena ca nakhāgrāṇi ghaṭṭayet
tatra siddhaḥ padāt padam adhikam ākāṅkṣet
kṣānty-arthaṃ ca tad evābhyaset
pāda-śauce pādāṅguli-saṃdaṃśena tad-aṅguli-pīḍanam
dravyasya samarpaṇe pratigrahe vā tad-gato vikāraḥ
ācamanānte codakenāsekaḥ
vijane tamasi ca dvandvam āsīnaḥ kṣāntiṃ kurvīta samāna-deśa-śayyāyāṃ ca
tatra yathārtham anudvejayato bhāva-nivedanam
vivikte ca kiṃ cid asti kathayitavyam ity uktvā nirvacanaṃ bhāvaṃ ca tatropalakṣayet yathā pāradārike vakṣyāmaḥ
vidita-bhāvas tu vyādhim apadiśyaināṃ vārtā-grahaṇārthaṃ svam udavasitam ānayet
āgatāyāś ca śiraḥ-pīḍane niyogaḥ pāṇim avalambya cāśyāḥ sākāraṃ nayanayor lalāṭe ca nidadhyāt
auśādhāpadeśārthaṃ cāsyāḥ karma vinirdiśet
idaṃ tvayā kartavyam na hy etad-ṛte kanyāyā anyena kāryam iti gacchantīṃ punar āgamanānubandham enāṃ visṛjet
asya ca yogasya trirātraṃ trisaṃdhyaṃ ca prayuktiḥ
abhīkṣṇa-darśanārtham āgatāyāś ca goṣṭhīṃ vardhayet
anyābhir api saha viśvāsanārtham adhikam adhikaṃ cābhiyuñjīta na tu vacā nirvadet
dūra-gata-bhāvo api hi kanyāsu na nirvedena sidhyātīti ghoṭakamukhaḥ
yadā tu bahu-siddhāṃ manyeta tadaivopakramet pradoṣe niśi tamasi ca yoṣito manda-sādhvasāḥ surata-vyavasāyinyo rāgavatyaś ca bhavanti na tu puruṣaṃ pratyācakṣate tasmāt tat-kālaṃ prayojayitavyā iti prāyovādaḥ
eka-puruṣābhiyogānāṃ tv asaṃbhave gṛhītārthayā dhātreyikayā sakhyā vā tasyām antarbhūtayā tam artham anirvadantyā sahainām aṅkam ānāyayet tato yathoktam abhiyuñjīta
svāṃ vā paricārikām ādāv eva sakhītvenāsyāḥ praṇidadhyāt
yajñe vivāhe yātrāyām utsave vyasane prekṣaṇaka-vyāpṛte jane tatra tatra ca dṛṣṭeṅgitākārāṃ parīkṣita-bhāvām ekākinīm upakrameta
na hi dṛṣṭa-bhāvā yoṣito deśe kāle ca prayujyamānā vyāvartanta iti vātsyāyanaḥ

ity ekapuruṣābhiyogaḥ 

prakaraṇa

mandāpadeśā guṇavaty api kanyā dhana-hīnā kulīnāpi samānair ayācyamānā mātāpitṛ-viyuktā vā jñāti-kula-vartinī vā prāpta-yauvanā pāṇi-grahaṇaṃ svayam abhīpseta
sā tu guṇavantaṃ śaktaṃ sudarśanaṃ bāla-prītyābhiyojayet
yaṃ vā manyeta mātāpitror asamīkṣayā svayam apy ayam indriya-daurbalyān mayi pravartiṣyata iti priya-hitopacārair abhīkṣṇa-saṃdarśanena ca tam āvarjayet
mātā caināṃ sakhībhir dhātreyikābhiś ca saha tad-abhimukhīṃ kuryāt
puṣpa-gandha-tāmbūla-hastāyā vijane vikāle ca tad-upasthānam kalā-kauśala-prakāśane vā saṃvāhane śirasaḥ pīḍane caucitya-darśanam prayojyasya sātmya-yuktāḥ kathā-yogāḥ bālāyām upakrameṣu yathoktam ācaret 
na caivāntarāpi puruṣaṃ svayam abhiyuñjīta svayam abhiyoginī hi yuvatiḥ saubhāgyaṃ jahātīty ācāryāḥ
tat-prayuktānāṃ tv abhiyogānām ānulomyena grahaṇam
pariṣvaktā ca na vikṛtiṃ bhajet ślakṣṇam ākāram ajānatīva pratigṛhīyāt vadana-grahaṇe balāt-kāraḥ
rati-bhāvanām abhyarthyamānāyāḥ kṛcchrād guhya-saṃsparśanam
abhyarthitāpi nātivivṛtā svayaṃ syāt anyatrāniścaya-kālāt 
yadā tu manyetānurakto mayi na vyāvartayiṣyata iti tadaivainam abhiyuñjānaṃ bāla-bhāva- mokṣāya tvaret
vimukta-kanyā-bhāvā ca viśvāsyeṣu prakāśayet

iti prayojyasyopāvartanam 

prakaraṇa

bhavanti cātra ślokāḥ
kanyābhiyujyamānā tu yaṃ manyetāśrayaṃ sukham anukūlaṃ ca vaśyaṃ ca tasya kuryāt parigraham
anapekṣya guṇān yatra rūpam aucityam eva ca kurvīta dhana-lobhena patiṃ sāpatnakeṣv api
tatra yukta-guṇaṃ vaśyaṃ śaktam balavad arthinam upāyair abhiyuñjānaṃ kanyā na pratilobhayet
varaṃ vaśyo daridro api nirguṇo apy ātma-dhāraṇaḥ guṇair yukto api na tv evaṃ bahu-sādhāraṇaḥ
prāyeṇa dhanināṃ dārā bahavo niravagrahāḥ bāhye saty upabhoge api nirvisrambhā bahiḥ-sukhāḥ
nīco yas tv abhiyuñjīta puruṣaḥ palito api vā videśa-gati-śīlaś ca na sa saṃyogam arhati
yadṛcchayābhiyukto yo dambha-dyūtādhiko api sapatnīkaś ca sāpatyo na sa saṃyogam arhati
guṇa-sāmye abhiyoktṝṇām eko varayitā varaḥ tatrābhiyoktari śraiṣṭhyam anurāgātmako hi saḥ 

iti śrīvātsyāyanīye kāma-sūtre kanyā-saṃprayuktake tṛtīye adhikaraṇe eka-puruṣābhiyogā abhiyogataś ca kanyāyāḥ pratipattiś caturtho adhyāyaḥ

pañcamo adhyāyaḥ
prakaraṇa [vivāha-yoga-prakaraṇam]

prācuryeṇa kanyāyā vivikta-darśanasyālābhe dhātreyikāṃ priya-hitābhyām upagṛhyopasarpet
sā cainām aviditā nāma nāyakasya bhūtvā tad-guṇair anurañjayet tasyāś ca rucyān nāyaka-guṇān bhūyiṣṭham upavarṇayet
anyeṣāṃ varayitṝṇāṃ doṣān abhiprāya-viruddhān pratipādayet mātāpitroś ca guṇānabhijñatāṃ lubdhatāṃ ca capalatāṃ ca bāndhavānām
yāś cānyā api samāna-jātīyāḥ kanyāḥ śakuntalādyāḥ sva-buddhyā bhartāraṃ prāpya saṃprayuktā modante sma tāś cāsyā nidarśayet
mahā-kuleṣu sāpatnakair bādhyamānā vidviṣṭāḥ duḥkhitāḥ parityaktāś ca dṛśyante
āyatiṃ cāsya varṇayet
sukham anupahatam eka-cāritāyāṃ nāyakānurāgaṃ ca varṇayet
samanorathāyāś cāsyā apāyaṃ sādhvasaṃ vrīḍāṃ ca hetubhir avacchindyāt
dūtī-kalpaṃ ca sakalam ācaret 
tvām ajānatīm iva nāyako balād grahīṣyatīti tathā suparigṛhītaṃ syād iti yojayet
pratipannām abhipretāvakāśa-vartinīṃ nāyakaḥ śrotriyāgārād agnim ānayya kuśān āstīrya yathā-smṛti hutvā ca triḥ parikramet
tato mātari pitari ca prakāśayet
agni-sākṣikā hi vivāhā na nivartanta ity ācārya-samayaḥ
dūṣayitvā caināṃ śanaiḥ sva-jane prakāśayet
tad-bāndhavāś ca yathā kulasyāghaṃ pariharanto daṇḍa-bhayāc ca tasmā evaināṃ dadyus tathā yojayet
anantaraṃ ca prīty-upagraheṇa rāgeṇa tad-bāndhavān prīṇayed iti
gāndharveṇa vivāhena vā ceṣṭeta
apratipadyamānāyām antaś-cāriṇīm anyāṃ kula-pramadāṃ pūrva saṃsṛṣṭāṃ prīyamāṇāṃ copagṛhya tayā saha viṣahyam avakāśam enām anya-kāryāpadeśenānāyayet
tataḥ śrotriyāgārād agnim iti samānaṃ pūrveṇa 
āsanne ca vivāhe mātaram asyās tad-abhimata-doṣair anuśayaṃ grāhayet
tatas tad-anumatena prātiveśyā-bhāvane niśi nāyakam ānāyya śrotriyāgārād agnim iti sam~ānaṃ pūrveṇa
bhrātaram asyā vā samāna-vayasaṃ veśyāsu para-strīṣu vā prasaktam asukareṇa sāhāyya-dānena priyopagrahaś ca sudīrgha-kālam anurañjayet ante ca svābhiprāyaṃ grāhayet
prāyeṇa hi yuvānaḥ samāna-śīla-vyasana-vayasāṃ vayasyānām arthe jīvitam api tyajanti tatas tenaivānya-kāryāt tām ānāyayet viṣahyaṃ sāvakāśam iti samānaṃ pūrveṇa
aṣṭamī-candrikādiṣu ca dhātreyikā madanīyam enāṃ pāyayitvā kiṃ cid ātmanaḥ kāryam uddiśya nāyakasya viṣahyaṃ deśam ānayet tatraināṃ madāt saṃjñām apratipadyamānāṃ dūṣayitveti samānaṃ pūrveṇa
suptāṃ caikacāriṇīṃ dhātreyikāṃ vārayitvā saṃjñām apratipadyamānāṃ dūṣayitveti samānaṃ pūrveṇa 
grāmāntaram udyānaṃ vā gacchantīṃ viditvā susaṃbhṛta-sahāyo nāyakas tadā rakṣiṇo vitrāsya hatvā vā kanyām apaharet 
iti vivāha-yogāḥ
pūrvaḥ pūrvaḥ pradhānaṃ syād vivāho dharmataḥ sthiteḥ pūrvābhāve tataḥ kāryo yo ya uttara uttaraḥ
vyūḍhānāṃ hi vivāhānām anurāgaḥ phalaṃ yataḥ madhyamo api hi sad-yogo gāndharvas tena pūjitaḥ
sukhatvād abahu-kleśād api cāvaraṇād iha anurāgātmakatvāc ca gāndharvaḥ pravaro mataḥ 

iti śrīvātsyāyanīye kāma-sūtre kanyā-saṃprayuktake tṛtīye adhikaraṇe vivāha-yogāḥ pañcamo adhyāyaḥlivre bhāryādhikārikaṃ caturtham adhikaraṇam

prathamo adhyāyaḥ
prakaraṇa [eka-cāriṇī-vṛtta-prakaraṇam]

bhāryaikacariṇī gūḍha-viśrambhā devavat patim ānukulyena varteta
tan-matena kuṭumba-cintām ātmani saṃniveśayet
veśma ca śuci susaṃmṛṣṭa-sthānaṃ viracita-vividha-kusumaṃ ślakṣṇa-bhūmi-talaṃ hṛdya-darśanaṃ triṣavaṇācarita-bali-karma pūjita-devatāyatanaṃ kuryāt
na hy ato anyad gṛhasthānāṃ citta-grāhakam astīti gonardīyaḥ
guruṣu bhṛtya-vargeṣu nāyaka-bhaginīṣu tat-patiṣu ca yathārhaṃ pratipattiḥ 
paripūteṣu ca harita-śāka-vaprān ikṣu-stambāñ jīraka-sarṣapājamoda-śatapuṣpā-tamāla-gulmāṃś ca kārayet 
kubjakāmalaka-mallikā-jātī-kuraṇṭaka-navamālikā-tagara-nandyāvartajapā-gulmān anyāṃś ca bahu-puṣpān bāla-kośīraka-pātālikāṃś ca vṛkṣa-vāṭikāyāṃ ca sthaṇḍilāṇi mano-jñāni kārayet 
madhye kūpaṃ vāpīṃ dīrghikāṃ vā khānayet
bhikṣukī-śramāṇā-kṣapaṇā-kulaṭā-kuhakekṣaṇikā-mūla-kārikābhir na saṃsṛjyeta
bhojane ca rucitam idam asmai dveṣyam idaṃ pathyam idam apathyam idam iti ca vindyāt
svaraṃ bahir upaśrutya bhavanam āgacchataḥ kiṃ kṛtyam iti bruvatī sajjā bhavana-madhye tiṣṭhet
paricārikam apanudya svayaṃ pādau prakṣālayet
nāyakasya ca na vimukta-bhūṣaṇaṃ vijane saṃdarśane tiṣṭhet
ativyayam asad-vyayaṃ vā kurvāṇaṃ rahasi bodhayet
āvāhe vivāhe yajñe gamanaṃ sakhībhiḥ saha goṣṭhīṃ devatābhigamanm ity anujñātā kuryāt
sarva-krīḍāsu ca tad-ānulomyena pravṛttiḥ
paścāt saṃveśanaṃ pūrvam utthānam anavabodhanaṃ ca suptasya
mahānasaṃ ca suguptaṃ syād darśanīyaṃ ca
nāyakāpacāreṣu kiṃ cit kaluṣitā nātyarthaṃ nirvadet
sādhikṣepa-vacanaṃ tv enaṃ mitra-jana-madhya-stham ekākinaṃ vāpy upālabheta na ca mūla-kārikā syāt
na hy ato anyad apratyaya-kāraṇam astīti gonardīyaḥ
durvyāhṛtaṃ durnirīkṣitam anyato mantraṇaṃ dvāra-deśāvasthānaṃ nirīkṣaṇaṃ vā niṣkuṭeṣu mantraṇaṃ vivikteṣu ciram avasthānam iti varjayet
sveda-danta-paṅka-durgandhāṃś ca budhyeteti virāga-kāraṇam
bahu-bhūṣaṇaṃ vividha-kusumānulepanaṃ vividhāṅga-rāga-samujjvalaṃ vāsa ity ābhigāmiko veṣaḥ
pratanu-ślakṣṇālpa-dukūlatā parimitam ābharaṇaṃ sugandhitā nātyulvaṇam anulepanam tathā śuklāny anyāni puṣpāṇīti vaihāriko veṣaḥ
nāyakasya vratam upavāsaṃ ca svayam api karaṇenānuvarteta vāritāyāṃ ca nāham atra nirbandhanīyeti tad-vacaso nivartanam
mṛd-vidala-kāṣṭha-carma-loha-bhāṇḍānāṃ ca kāle samargha-grahaṇam 
tathā lavaṇa-snehayoś ca gandha-dravya-kaṭuka-bhāṇḍauṣadhānāṃ ca durlabhānāṃ bhavaneṣu pracchannaṃ nidhānam
mūlakāluka-pālaṅkī-damanakāmrātakairvāruka-trapu-savārtāka-kūṣmāṇḍālābu-sūraṇa-śukanāsā- svayamguptā-tila-parṇikāgnimantha-laśuna-palāṇḍu-prabhṛtīnāṃ sarvauṣadhīnāṃ ca bīja-grahaṇaṃ kāle vāpaś ca
svasya ca sārasya parebhyo nākhyānaṃ bhartṛ-mantritasya ca
samānāś ca striyaḥ kauśalenojjvalatayā pākena mānena tathopacārair atiśayīta
sāṃvatsarikam āyaṃ saṃkhyāya tad-anurūpaṃ vyayaṃ kuryāt
bhojanāvaśiṣṭād go-rasād ghṛta-karaṇam tathā tela-guḍayoḥ karpāsasya ca sūtra-kartanam sūtrasya vānam śikya-rajju-pāśa-valkala-saṃgrahaṇam kuṭṭana-kaṇḍanāvekṣaṇam āma-cāmaṇḍa-tuṣaka-khakuṭy-aṅgārāṇām upayojanam bhṛtya-vetana- bharaṇa-jñānam kṛṣi-paśu-pālana-cintā-vāhana-vidhāna-yogāḥ meṣa-kukkuṭa-lāvaka- śuka-śārikā-parabhṛta-mayūra-vānara-mṛgāṇām avekṣaṇam daivasikāya-vyaya-piṇḍī- karaṇam iti ca vidyāt
taj-jaghanyānāṃ ca jīrṇa-vāsasāṃ saṃcayas tair vividha-rāgaiḥ śuddhair vā kṛta-karmaṇāṃ paricārakāṇām anugraho mānārtheṣu ca dānam anyatra vopayogaḥ surā-kumbhīnām āsava-kumbhīnāṃ ca sthāpanaṃ tad-upayogaḥ kraya-vikrayāv āya-vyāyāvekṣaṇam
nāyaka-mitrāṇāṃ ca srag-anulepana-tāmbūla-dānaiḥ pūjanaṃ nyāyataḥ
śvaśrū-svaśura-paricaryā tat-pāratantryam anuttara-vāditā parimitāpracaṇḍālāpa-karaṇam anuccair hāsaḥ tat-priyāpriyeṣu sva-priyāpriyeṣv iva vṛttiḥ
bhogeṣv anutsekaḥ
parijane dākṣiṇyam
nāyakasyānivedya na kasmai cid dānam
sva-karmasu bhṛtya-jana-niyamanam utsaveṣu cāsya pūjanam

ity ekacāriṇī-vṛttam 

prakaraṇa [pravāsa-caryyā-prakaraṇam]

pravāse maṅgala-mātrābharaṇā devatopavāsa-parā vārtāyāṃ sthitā gṛhān avekṣeta
śayyā ca guru-jana-mūle tad-abhimatā kārya-niṣpattiḥ nāyakābhimatānāṃ cārthānām arjane pratisaṃskāre ca yatnaḥ
nitya-naimittikeṣu karmasūcito vyayaḥ tad-ārabdhānāṃ ca karmaṇāṃ samāpane matiḥ
jñāti-kulasyānabhigamanam anyatra vyasanotsavābhyām tatrāpi nāyaka-parijanādhiṣṭhitāyā nātikālam avasthānam aparivartita-pravāsa-veṣatā ca
guru-janānujñātānāṃ karaṇam upavāsānām paricārakaiḥ śucibhir ājñādhiṣṭhitair anumatena kraya-vikraya-karmaṇā sārasyāpūraṇaṃ tanū-karaṇaṃ ca śaktyā vyayānām
āgate ca prakṛtisthāyā eva prathamato darśanaṃ daivata-pūjanam upahārāṇāṃ cāharaṇam
iti pravāsa-caryā 
bhavataś cātra ślokau
sad-vṛttam anuvarteta nāyakasya hitaiṣiṇī kula-yoṣā punarbhūr vā veśyā vāpy ekacāriṇī dharmam arthaṃ tathā kāmaṃ labhante sthānam eva ca niḥsapatnaṃ ca bhartāraṃ nāryaḥ sad-vṛttam āśritāḥ

iti śrīvātsyāyanīye kāmasūtre bhāryādhikārike caturthe adhikaraṇe ekacāriṇī-vṛttaṃ pravāsa-cāryā ca prathamo adhyāyaḥ

dvitīyo adhyāyaḥ
prakaraṇa [jyeṣṭhādi-vṛtta-prakaraṇam]

jāḍya-dauḥśīlya-daurbhāgyebhyaḥ prajānutpatter ābhīkṣṇyena dārikotpatter nāyaka-cāpalād vā sapatny-adhivedanam
tad ādita eva bhakti-śīla-vaidagdhya-khyāpanena parijihīrṣet prajānutpattau ca svayam eva sāpatnake codayet
adhividyamānā ca yāvac chakti-yogād ātmano adhikatvena sthitiṃ kārayet
āgatāṃ caināṃ bhaginīvad īkṣeta nayaka-viditaṃ ca prādoṣikaṃ vidhimatīva yatnād asyāḥ kārayet saubhāgyajaṃ vaikṛtam utsekaṃ vāsyā nādriyeta
bhartari pramādyantīm upekṣeta yatra manyetārtham iyaṃ svayam api pratipatsyata iti tatrainām ādarata evānuśiṣyāt
nāyaka-saṃśrave ca rahasi viśeṣān adhikān darśayet
tad-apatyesv aviśeṣaḥ parijana-varge adhikānukampā mitra-varge prītiḥ ātma-jñātiṣu nātyādaraḥ taj-jñātiṣu cātisaṃbhramaḥ
bahvībhis tv adhivinnā avyavahitayā saṃsṛjyeta
yāṃ tu nāyako adhikāṃ cikīrṣet tāṃ bhūta-pūrva-subhagayā protsāhya kalahayet
tataś cānukampeta
tābhir ekatvenādhikāṃ cikirṣītāṃ svayam avivadamānā durjanīkuryāt
nāyakena tu kalahitām enāṃ pakṣapātāvalambanopabṛmhitām āśvāsayet

viraktaṃ ca nityam eva prakṛti-vikriyāto vidyān mukha-varṇāc ca

pratilomaiḥ sambadhyate

prakaraṇa

saktaṃ tu pūrvopakāriṇam apy alpa-phalaṃ vyālīkenānupālayet
asāraṃ tu niṣpratipattikam upāyato apavāhayet anyam avaṣṭabhya
tad-aniṣṭa-sevā ninditābhyāsaḥ oṣṭha-nirbhogaḥ pādena bhūmer abhighātaḥ avijñāta-viṣayasya saṃkathā tad-vijñāteṣv avismayaḥ kutsā ca darpa-vighātaḥ adhikaiḥ saha saṃvāsaḥ anapekṣaṇam samāna-doṣāṇāṃ nindā rahasi cāvasthānam
jugupsā pariṣvaṅge bhujamayyā sūcyā vyavadhānam stabdhatā gātrāṇām sakthnor vyatyāsaḥ nidrā-paratvaṃ ca śrāntam upalabhya codanā aśaktau hāsaḥ śaktāv anabhinandanam divāpi bhāvam upalabhya mahājanābhigamanam
vākyeṣu cchala-grahaṇam anarmaṇi hāsaḥ narmaṇi cānyam apadiśya hasati vadati tasmin kaṭākṣeṇa parijanasya prekṣaṇaṃ tāḍanaṃ ca āhatya cāsya kathām anyāḥ kathāḥ tad-vyālīkānāṃ vyasanānāṃ cāparihāryāṇām anukīrtanam marmaṇāṃ ca ceṭikayopakṣepaṇam
āgate cādarśanam ayācya-yācanam ante svayaṃ mokṣaś ceti parigrahakasyeti dattakasya
bhavataś cātra ślokau
parīkṣya gamyaiḥ saṃyogaḥ saṃyuktasyānurañjanam raktād arthasya cādānam ante mokṣaś ca vaiśikam
evam etena kalpena sthitā veśyā parigrahe nātisandhīyate gamyaiḥ karoty arthāṃś ca puṣkalān 

iti śrī vātsyāyanīye kāmasūtre vaiśike ṣaṣṭhe adhikaraṇe arthāgamopāyā virakta-liṅgāni virakta-pratipattir niṣkāsana-kramās tṛtīyo adhyāyaḥ

prakaraṇa [viśīrṇa-pratisaṃdhāna-prakaraṇam]

anyatra gatas tarkayitavyaḥ sa kārya-yuktyā ṣaḍ-vidhaḥ
itaḥ svayam apasṛtas tato api svayam evāpasṛtaḥ [] itas tataś ca niṣkāsitāpasṛtaḥ []
itaḥ svayam apasṛtas tato niṣkāsitāpasṛtaḥ []
itaḥ svayam apasṛtas tatra sthitaḥ []
ito niṣkāsitāpasṛtas tataḥ svayam apasṛtaḥ []
ito niṣkāsitāpasṛtas tatra sthitaḥ []
itas tataś ca svayam evāpasṛtyopajapati ced ubhayor guṇān apekṣī cala-buddhir asandheyaḥ []
itas tataś ca niṣkāsitāpasṛtaḥ sthira-buddhiḥ sa ced anyato bahu labhamānayā niṣkāsitaḥ syāt sasāro api tayā roṣito mamāmarṣād bahu dāsyatīti saṃdheyaḥ []
niḥsāratayā kadaryatayā vā tyakto na śreyān []
itaḥ svayam apasṛtas tato niṣkāsitāpasṛto yady atiriktam ādau ca dadyāt tataḥ pratigrāhyaḥ []
itaḥ svayam apasṛtya tatra sthita upajapaṃs tarkayitavyaḥ
viśeṣārthī cāgatas tato viśeṣam apasyann āgantu-kāmo mayi māṃ jijñāsitu-kāmaḥ sā āgatya sānurāgatvād dāsyati tasyāṃ vā doṣān dṛṣṭvā mayi bhūyiṣṭhān guṇān adhunā paśyati sa guṇa-darśī bhūyiṣṭhaṃ dasyāti []
bālo vā naikatra-dṛṣṭir atisaṃdhāna-pradhāno vā haridrā-rāgo vā yat-kiṃ-cana-kārī vety avetya saṃdadhyān na vā []
ito niṣkāsitāpasṛtas tataḥ svayam upasṛta upajapaṃs tarkayitavyaḥ
anurāgād āgantu-kāmaḥ sa bahu dāsyati mama guṇair bhāvito yo anyasyāṃ na ramate []
pūrvam ayogena vā mayā niṣkāsitaḥ sa māṃ śīlayitvā vairaṃ niryātayitu-kāmo dhanam abhiyogād vā mayāsyāpahṛtaṃ tad-viśvāsya pratīpam ādātu-kāmo nirveṣṭu-kāmo vā māṃ vartamānād bhedayitvā tyaktu-kāma ity akalyāṇa-buddhir asandheyaḥ []
anyathā-buddhiḥ kālena lambhayitavyaḥ []
ito niṣkāsitas tatra sthita upajapann etena vyākhyātaḥ []
teṣūpajapatsv anyatra sthitaḥ svayam upajapet
vyālīkārthaṃ niṣkāsito mayāsāv anyatra gato yatnād ānetavyaḥ
itaḥ pravṛtta-saṃbhāṣo vā tato bhedam avāpsyati
tad-arthābhighātaṃ kariṣyati
arthāgama-kāle vāsya sthāna-vṛddhir asya jātā labdham anenādhikaraṇam dārair viyuktaḥ pāratantryād vyāvṛttaḥ pitrā bhrātrā vā vibhaktaḥ
anena vā pratibaddham anena sandhiṃ kṛtvā nāyakaṃ dhaninam avāpsyāmi
vimānitā vā bhāryayā tam eva tasyāṃ vikramayiṣyāmi
asya vā mitraṃ mad-dveṣiṇīṃsapatnīṃ kāmayate tad amunā bhedayiṣyāmi
cala-cittatayā vā lāghavam enam āpādayiṣyāmīti
tasya pīṭhamardādayo mātur dauḥśīlyena nāyikāyāḥ saty apy anurāge vivaśāyāḥ pūrvaṃ niṣkāsanaṃ varṇayeyuḥ
vartamānena cākāmāyāḥ saṃsargaṃ vidveṣaṃ ca
tasyāś ca sābhijñānaiḥ pūrvānurāgair enaṃ pratyāpayeyuḥ
abhijñānaṃ ca ta-kṛtopakāra-saṃbaddhaṃ syād

iti viśīrṇa-pratisaṃdhānam 
apūrva-pūrva-saṃsṛṣṭayoḥ pūrva-saṃsṛṣṭaḥ śreyān sa hi vidita-śīlo dṛṣṭa-rāgaś ca sūpacāro bhavatīty ācāryāḥ
pūrva-saṃsṛṣṭaḥ sarvato niṣpīḍitārthatvān nātyartham artha-do duḥkhaṃ ca punar viśvāsayitum apūrvas tu sukhenānurajyata iti vātsyāyanaḥ
tathāpi puruṣa-prakṛtito viśeṣaḥ
bhavanti cātra ślokāḥ
anyāṃ bhedayituṃ gamyād anyato gamyam eva vā sthitasya copaghātārthaṃ punaḥ saṃdhānam iṣyate
bibhety anyasya saṃyogād vyālīkāni ca nekṣate atisaktaḥ pumān yatra bayād bahu dadāti ca
asaktam abhinandeta saktaṃ paribhavet tathā anya-dūtānupāte ca yaḥ syād ativiśāradaḥ
tatropayāyinaṃ pūrvaṃ nārī kālena yojayet bhavec cācchinna-saṃdhānā na ca saktaṃ parityajet
saktaṃ tu vaśinaṃ nārī saṃbhāvyāpy anyato vrajet tataś cārtham upādāya saktam evānurañjayet
āyatiṃ prasamīkṣyādau lābhaṃ prītiṃ ca puṣkalām sauhṛdaṃ pratisaṃdadhyād viśīrṇaṃ strī vicakṣaṇā 

iti śrī vātsyāyanīye kāmasūtre vaiśike ṣaṣṭhe adhikaraṇe viśīrṇa-pratisaṃdhānaṃ caturtho adhyāyaḥ pañcamo adhyāyaḥ

prakaraṇa [lābha-viśeṣa-prakaraṇam]

gamya-bāhulye bahu pratidinaṃ ca labhamānā naikaṃ pratigṛhṇīyāt
deśaṃ kālaṃ sthitim ātmano guṇān saubhāgyaṃ cānyābhyo nyūnātiriktāṃ cāvekṣya rajanyām arthaṃ sthāpayet
gamye dūtāṃś ca prayojayet tat-pratibaddhāṃś ca svayaṃ prahiṇuyāt
dvis triś catur iti lābhātiśaya-grahārtham ekasyāpi gacchet parigrahaṃ ca caret
gamya-yaugapadye tu lābha-sāmye yad dravyārthinī syāt tad dāyini viśeṣaḥ pratyakṣa ity ācāryāḥ
apratyādeyatvāt sarva-kāryāṇāṃ tan-mūlatvād hiraṇya-da iti vātsyāyanaḥ
suvarṇa-rajata-tāmra-kāṃsya-loha-bhāṇḍopaskārāstaraṇa-prāvaraṇa-vāso-viśeṣa-gandha-dravya- kaṭuka-bhāṇḍa-vṛta-taila-dhānya-paśu-jātīnāṃ pūrva-pūrvato viśeṣaḥ
yat tatra sāmyād vā dravya-sāmye mitra-vākyād atipātitvād āyatito gamya-guṇataḥ prītitaś ca viśeṣaḥ
rāgi-tyāginos tyāgini viśeṣaḥ pratyakṣa ity ācāryāḥ
śakyo hi rāgiṇi tyāga ādhātum
lubdho api hi raktas tyajati na tu tyāgī nirbandhād rājyata iti vātsyāyanaḥ
tatrāpi dhanavad-adhanavator dhanavati viśeṣaḥ tyāgi-prayojana-kartroḥ prayojana-kartari viśeṣaḥ pratyakṣa ity ācāryāḥ
prayojana-kartā sakṛt kṛtvā kṛtinam ātmānaṃ manyate tyāgī punar atītaṃ nāpekṣata iti vātsyāyanaḥ
tatrāpy ātyayikato viśeṣaḥ
kṛtajña-tyāginos tyāgini viśeṣaḥ pratyakṣa ity ācāryāḥ
ciram ārādhito api tyāgī vyalīkam ekam upalabhya pratigaṇikayā vā mithyā-dūṣitaḥ śramam atītaṃ nāpekṣate
prāyeṇa hi tejasvina ṛjavo anādṛtāś ca tyāgino bhavanti
kṛtajñas tu pūrva-śramāpekṣī na sahasā virajyate parīkṣita-śīlatvāc ca na mithyā dūṣyata iti vātsyāyanaḥ
tatrāpy āyatito viśeṣaḥ
mitra-vacanārthāgamayor arthāgame viśeṣaḥ pratyakṣa ity ācāryāḥ
so pi hy arthāgāmo bhavitā mitraṃ tu sakṛd-vākye pratihate kaluṣitaṃ syād iti vātsyāyanaḥ
tatrāpy atipātato viśeṣaḥ
tatra kārya-saṃdarśanena mitram anunīya śvo-bhūte vacanam astv iti tato atipātinam arthaṃ pratigṛhṇīyāt
arthāgamānartha-pratighātayor arthāgame viśeṣaḥ pratyakṣa ity ācāryāḥ
arthaḥ parimitāvacchedaḥ anarthaḥ punaḥ sakṛt-prasṛto na jñāyate kvāvatiṣṭhata iti vātsyāyanaḥ
tatrāpi guru-lāghava-kṛto viśeṣaḥ
etenārtha-saṃśayād anartha-pratīkāre viśeṣo vyākhyātaḥ
deva-kula-taḍāgārāmāṇāṃ karaṇam sthalīnām agni-caityānāṃ nibandhanam go-sahasrāṇāṃ pātrāntaritaṃ brāhmaṇebhyo dānam devatānāṃ pūjopahāra-pravartanam tad-vyaya-sahiṣṇor vā dhanasya parigrahaṇam ity uttama-gaṇikānāṃ lābhātiśayaḥ
sārvāṅgiko akaṅkāra-yogo gṛhasyodārasya karaṇam mahārhair bhāṇḍaiḥ paricārakaiś ca gṛha-paricchādasyojjvalateti rūpa-jīvānāṃ lābhātiśayaḥ
nityaṃ śuklam ācchādanam apakṣudam anna-pānaṃ nityaṃ saugandhikena tāmbūlena ca yogaḥ sahiraṇya-bhāgam alaṅkaraṇam iti kumbha-dāsīnāṃ lābhātiśayaḥ
etena pradeśena madhyamādhamānām api lābhātiśayān sarvāsām eva yojayed ity ācāryāḥ
deśa-kāla-vibhāva-sāmarthyānurāga-loka-pravṛtti-vaśād aniyata-lābhād iyam avṛttir iti vātsyāyanaḥ
gamyam anyato nivārayitu-kāmā saktam anyasyām apahartu-kāmā vā anyāṃ vā lābhato viyuyukṣamāṇāgamya-saṃsargād ātmanaḥ sthānaṃ vṛddhim āyatim abhigamyatāṃ ca manyamānā anartha-pratīkāre vā sāhāyyam enaṃ kārayitu-kāmā saktasya vānyasya vyalīkārthinī pūrvopakāram akṛtam iva paśyantī kevala-prīty-arthinī vā kalyāṇa-buddher alpam api lābhaṃ pratigṛhṇīyāt
āyaty-arthinī tu tam āśritya cānarthaṃ praticikīrṣantī naiva pratigṛhṇīyāt
tyakṣyāmy enam anyataḥ pratisandhāsyāmi gamiṣyati dārair yokṣyate nāśayiṣyaty anarthān aṅkuśa-bhūta uttarādhyakṣo asyāgamiṣyati svāmī pitā vā sthāna-bhraṃśo vāsya bhaviṣyati cala-cittaś ceti manyamānā tadātve tasmāl lābham icchet pratijñātam īśvareṇa pratigrahaṃ lapsyate adhikaraṇaṃ sthānaṃ vā prāpsyati vṛtti-kālo asya vā āsannaḥ vāhanam asyāgamiṣyati sthala-pattraṃ vā sasyam asya pakṣyate kṛtam asmin na naśyati nityam avisaṃvādako vety āyatyām icchet parigraha-kalpaṃ vācaret
bhavanti cātra ślokāḥ
kṛcchrādhigata-vittāṃś ca rāja-vallabha-niṣṭhurān āyatyāṃ ca tadātve ca dūrād eva vivarjayet
aartho varjane yeṣāṃ gamane abhyudayas tathā prayatnenāpi tān gṛhya sāpadeśam upakramet
prasannā ye prayacchanti svalpe apy agaṇitaṃ vasu sthūla-lakṣān mahotsāhāṃs tān gacchet svair api vyayaiḥ 

iti śrī vātsyāyanīye kāmasūtre vaiśike ṣaṣṭhe adhikaraṇe lābha-viśeṣāḥ pañcamo adhyāyaḥ

prakaraṇa [arthānarthānubandha-saṃśaya-vicāra-prakaraṇam]

arthān ācaryamāṇān anarthā apyanūdbhavanty anubandhāḥ saṃśayāś ca 
te buddhi-daurbalyād atirāgād atyabhimānād atidambhād atyārjavād ativiśvāsād atikrodhāt pramādāt sāhasād daiva-yogāc ca syuḥ
teṣāṃ phalaṃ kṛtasya vyayasya niṣphalatvam anāyatir āgamiṣyato arthasya nivartanam āptasya niṣkramaṇaṃ pāruṣyasya prāptir gamyatā śarīrasya praghātaḥ keśānāṃ chedanaṃ pātanam aṅga-vaikalyāpattiḥ
tasmāt tān ādita eva parijihīrṣed artha-bhūyiṣṭhāṃś copekṣeta
artho dharmaḥ kāma ity artha-trivargaḥ
anartho adharmo dveṣa ity anartha-trivargaḥ
teṣv ācaryamāṇeṣv anyasyāpi niṣpattir anubandhaḥ
saṃdigdhāyāṃ tu phala-prāptau syād vā na veti śuddha-saṃśayaḥ
idaṃ vā syād idaṃ veti saṃkīrṇaḥ
ekasmin kriyamāṇe kārye kārya-dvayasyotpattir ubhayatoyogaḥ
samantād utpattiḥ samantatoyoga iti tān udāhariṣyāmaḥ
vicārita-rūpo artha-trivargaḥ tad-viparīta evānartha-trivargaḥ
yasyottamasyābhigamane pratyakṣato artha-lābho grahaṇīyatvam āyatir āgamaḥ prārthanīyatvaṃ cānyeṣāṃ syāt so artho arthānubandhaḥ
lābha-mātre kasya cid anyasya gamanaṃ so artho niranubandhaḥ
anyārtha-parigrahe saktād āyati-cchedanam arthasya niṣkramaṇaṃ loka-vidviṣṭasya vā nīcasya gamanam āyati-ghnam artho anarthānubandhaḥ
svena vyayena śūrasya mahāmātrasya prabhavato vā lubdhasya gamanaṃ niṣphalam api vyasana-pratīkārārthaṃ mahataś cārtha-ghnasya nimittasya praśamanam āyati-jananaṃ so anartho arthānubandhaḥ
kadaryasya subhaga-māninaḥ kṛta-ghnasya vātisandhāna-śīlasya svair api vyayais tathārādhanam ante niṣphalaṃ so anartho niranubandhaḥ
tasyaiva rāja-vallabhasya kraurya-prabhāvādhikasya tathaivārādhanam ante niṣphalaṃ niṣkāsanaṃ ca doṣa-karaṃ so anartho anarthānubandhaḥ
evaṃ dharma-kāmayor apy anubandhān yojayet

ity anubandhāḥ 
paritoṣito api dāsyati na vety artha-saṃśayaḥ
niṣpīḍitārtham aphalam utsṛjantyā artham alabhamānāyā dharmaḥ syān na veti dharma-saṃśayaḥ
abhipretam upalabhya paricārakam anyaṃ vā kṣudraṃ gatvā kāaḥ syān na veti kāma-saṃśayaḥ
prabhāvavān kṣudro anabhigato anarthaṃ kariṣyati na vety anartha-saṃśayaḥ
atyanta-niṣphalaḥ saktaḥ parityaktaḥ pitṛ-lokaṃ yāyāt tatrādharmaḥ syān na vety adharma-saṃśayaḥ

iti śuddha-saṃśayāḥ
atha saṃkīrṇāḥ 
āgantor avidita-śīlasya vallabha-saṃśrayasya prabhaviṣṇor vā samupasthitasyārādhanam artho anartha iti saṃśayaḥ
śrotriyasya brahmacāriṇo dīkṣitasya vratino liṅgino vā māṃ dṛṣṭvā jāta-rāgasya mumūrṣor mitra-vākyād ānṛśaṃsyāc ca gamanaṃ dharmo adharma iti saṃśayaḥ lokād evākṛta-pratyayād aguṇo guṇavān vety anavekṣya gamanaṃ kāmo dveṣa iti saṃśayaḥ
saṃkirec ca paraspareṇa

yatra parasyābhigamane arthaḥ saktāc ca saṃgharṣataḥ sa ubhayato arthaḥ

yatrābhigamane artho bhaviṣyati na vety āśaṅkā sakto api saṃgharṣād dāsyati na veti sa ubhayato artha-saṃśayaḥ
yatrābhigamane vyayavati pūrvo viruddhaḥ krodhād apakāraṃ kariṣyati na veti sakto vāmarṣito dattaṃ pratyādāsyati na veti sa ubhayato anartha-saṃśayaḥ
ity auddālaker ubhayato-yogāḥ
bābhravīyās tu 
yatrābhigamane artho anabhigamane ca saktād arthaḥ sa ubhayato'rthaḥ
yatrābhigamane niṣphalo vyayo anabhigamane ca niṣpratīkāro anarthaḥ sa ubhayato'narthaḥ
yatrābhigamane nirvyayo dāsyati na veti saṃśayo anabhigamane sakto dāsyati na veti sa ubhayato'rtha-saṃśayaḥ
yatrābhigamane vyayavati pūrvo viruddhaḥ prabhāvavān prāpsyate na veti saṃśayo anabhigamane ca krodhād anarthaṃ kariṣyati na veti sa ubhayato'nartha-saṃśayaḥ
eteṣām eva vyatikare anyato artho anyato anarthaḥ anyato artho anyato artha-saṃśayaḥ anyato artho anyato anartha-saṃśayaḥ
iti ṣaṭ-saṃkīrṇa-yogāḥ
