akṣarāṇāṃ samāsena daśatritayam uttamam ।b akṣarāṇāṃ samāsena rasasaṅkhyā udāhṛtā ।b akṣarāṇāṃ samāsena śakrasaṅkhyā varānane ।b ajarāmarīṇāṃ padaṃ cātra kathitaṃ pañcaguṇāvaham ।a
kāmānande dagdhe prītiratī rodanātmike duḥsaham ।a kruddhe ti ca tathā cānyam aṣṭamaṃ śubhalakṣaṇam ।a daśākṣaraṃ samākhyātaṃ kathitaṃ vīranāyike ।b dṛṣṭvā te rudamāne nānaṅgaḥ patir bhavati mā rudathaḥ ।b
prakaṭaṃ naiva kartavyaṃ na meruṃ laṅghayet kvacit ।a mantroddhāraṃ varānane। māṃsaśoṇitasurāsavapriye navamaṃ daśamaṃ tu hasadvayam ।b me na ṇa ña ṅa pañcamaṃ hrauṃ hrīṃ hrāṃ ṣaṣṭhamaṃ padam ।a
yathotpattis tathā kṣayaḥ । yaikābjikuśrī saptamaṃ tivagabha mona aṣṭamam ।b śaṅkhasphaṭikarudrākṣaputrañ jīvakariṣṭakāḥ ।b sarvatrāpratihatagatīnāṃ padaṃ ṣaṣṭhaṃ varānane ।a
sarvamātṛguhyahṛdayaparamasiddhaṃ padaṃ tu navamaṃ bhavet ।a svarūpapararūpaparivartanīnāṃ padaṃ saptamakaṃ bhavet ।a
