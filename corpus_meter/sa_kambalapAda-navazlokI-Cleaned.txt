prajñāpāramitāmbodhau śubharatnākare svayam । sarvā pāramitās tatra tādātmyena vyavasthitāḥ ।। niṣprapañcā nirābhāsā nirvikalpā nirālayā । niḥsvabhāvā parā sūkṣmā bindunādavivarjitā ।।
prajñāpāramitā mātā sarvabuddhodayā parā । trayānupalabdhirūpā sarvajñajñānagocarā ।। prajñāpāramitāṃ saṃyag yo bhāvayitum icchati । tenārthato navaślokāś cintanīyāḥ samāsataḥ ।।
karmaprabhāvasaṃbhūtaṃ ṣaḍāyatanalakṣaṇam । punarbhavam iti khyātaṃ pratibhāsopamaṃ hi tat ।। nirmitaṃ nagaraṃ yadvad vilokayati nirmitaḥ । tadvat paśyati rūpāṇi karmabhir nirmitaṃ jagat ।। dharmaṃ deśayataḥ śabdā ye kecit śrutigocarāḥ ।
pratiśrutkopamāḥ sarve prodbhūtaśrutinaḥ śrutāḥ ।। āghrataṃ svāditaṃ tathā spraṣṭaṃ viṣayalālāsaiḥ । svapnatulyam idaṃ sarvam upalabdhaṃ na vidyate ।। māyāyantro naro yadvad bhinnāṃ ceṣṭāṃ karoti vai ।
tadvad ceṣṭāṃ karoty eva dehayantro nirātmakaḥ ।। nānopalabdhayo yāś ca pratikṣaṇasamudbhavāḥ । marīcisadṛśāś caite dṛṣṭanaṣṭāḥ vilakṣaṇāḥ ।। pratibimbanibhaṃ grāhyam anādicittasaṃbhavam ।
tadākāraṃ ca vijñānam anyonyapratibimbavat ।। dhyāyināṃ svacchasaṃtāne yaj jnanendusamudbhavam । udakacandropamaṃ tad dhi pratyakṣaṃ na vidyate ।। yoginām api yaj jñānaṃ tad apy ākāśalakṣaṇam । tasmāj jñānaṃ ca jñeyaṃ ca sarvam ākāśalakṣaṇam ।।
iti cintayataḥ tattvaṃ sarvabhāveṣv anāśritam । bodhipraṇidhicittena jñānaṃ agraṃ bhaviṣyati ।।
