upadeśāya śiṣyāṇāṃ saṃtoṣāya manīṣiṇām । kṣemendreṇa nijaślokaiḥ kriyate vargasaṃgrahaḥ ।। dharmaḥ śarma paratra ceha ca nṛṇāṃ dharmo andhakāre raviḥ sarvāpatpraśamakṣamaḥ sumanasāṃ dharmābhidhāno nidhiḥ । dharmo bandhur abāndhave pṛthupathe dharmaḥ suhṛn niścalaḥ saṃsārorumarusthale suratarur nāsty eva dharmāt paraḥ ।।
karṇe dharmakathā mukhe paricitaṃ dharmābhirāmaṃ vacaś citte dharmamanorathaḥ praṇayinī sarvatra dharmasthitiḥ । kāye dharmamayī kriyā parikaraḥ so ayaṃ śubhaprāptaye kalpāpāyapade apy upaplavalavair aspṛṣṭavelāphalaḥ ।। nindyaṃ janma pramohasthirataratamasāṃ yan manuṣyatvahīnaṃ buddhyā hīno manuṣyaḥ śubhaphalavikalas tulyaceṣṭaḥ paśūnām । buddhiḥ pāṇḍityahīnā bhramati sadasatos tattvacarcāvicāre pāṇḍityaṃ dharmahīnaṃ śukasadṛśagirāṃ niṣphalakleśam eva ।।

paraprāṇatrāṇapraṇihitadhiyāṃ dharmajananī dayaivaikā loke sakalajanatājīvitasudhā । asāmānyaṃ puṇyaṃ munibhir uditaṃ jñānanayanair ahiṃsā saṃsāre svapurakuśalaślāghyasaraṇiḥ ।। prāṇāṇāṃ parirakṣaṇāya satataṃ sarvāḥ kriyāḥ prāṇiṇāṃ prāṇebhyo apy adhikaṃ samastajagatāṃ nasty eva kiṃcit priyam । puṇyaṃ tasya na śakyate gaṇayituṃ yaḥ pūrṇakāruṇyavān prāṇānām abhayaṃ dadāti sukṛtī teṣām ahiṃsāvrataḥ ।।

vandyaḥ sa puṃsāṃ tridaśābhivandyaḥ kāruṇyapuṇyopacayakriyābhiḥ । saṃsārasāratvam upaiti yasya paropakārābharaṇaṃ śarīram ।। paradraviṇaniḥspṛhaḥ parakalatraniṣkautukaḥ parapraṇayavatsalaḥ paranikārabaddhakṣamaḥ । parastutiviśāradaḥ paragūnāpavādojjhitaḥ parārtiharaṇodyato bhavati bhūripuṇyair naraḥ ।।
padmānām iva sā satāṃ saguṇatā yā saṃśrayārhā śriyaḥ sā śrīr bhadragajendramūrtir iva yā dānena vibhrājate । tad dānaṃ navacandravad yad aniśaṃ mānena saṃpūryate māno asau ṭṛṇavan na yaḥ paricayamlānaḥ śanaiḥ śuṣyati ।। lakṣmīr dānaphalā śrutaṃ śamaphalaṃ pāṇiḥ surārcāphalaś ceṣṭā dharmaphalā parārtiharaṇakrīḍāphalaṃ jīvitam । vāṇī satyaphalā jagatsukhaphalasphītā prabhāvonnatir bhavyānāṃ bhavaśānticintanaphalā bhūtyai bhavaty eva dhiḥ ।।
śīlaṃ śilayatāṃ kulaṃ kalayatāṃ sadbhāvam abhyasyatāṃ vyājaṃ varjayatāṃ guṇaṃ gaṇayatāṃ dharme dhiyaṃ badhnatām । kṣāntiṃ cintayatāṃ tamaḥ śamayatāṃ tattvaśrutiṃ śṛṇvatāṃ saṃsāre na paropakārasadṛśaṃ paśyāmi puṇyaṃ satām ।। tīrthāni dīrghādhvapariśramāṇi bahuvyayāni kratuḍambarāṇi । tapāṃsi muktvā tanuśoṣaṇāni hiṃsāvirāme ramatāṃ matir vaḥ ।।

mānyaḥ kulīnaḥ kulajāt kalāvān vidvān kalājñād viduṣaḥ suśīlaḥ । dhanī suśīlād dhanino api dātā dātur jitā kīrtir āyācakena ।। taptais tīvravrataiḥ kiṃ vikasati karuṇāsyandinī yady ahiṃsā kiṃ dūrais tīrthasārair yadi śamavimalaṃ mānasaṃ satyapūtam । yatnād anyopakāre prasarati yadi dhīr dānapuṇyaiḥ kim anyaiḥ kiṃ mokṣopāyayogair yadi śucimanasām ucyate bhaktir asti ।।

dānādidharmaḥ kriyate dhanena dhanena dhanyā dhanam āpnuvanti । dhanair vinā kāmakathāpi nāsti trivargamūlaṃ dhanam eva nānyat ।। yat klībair bhaṭakukkuṭotkaṭaraṇakrīḍā samādiśyate yan mūrkhaiḥ sukhalilayā kaviśukālāpaściraṃ carvyate । nīcair uccataraś ca sevakahayaḥ svāmyena yad vāhyate tad vittasya vilāsanṛttavasater udvṛttavṛttāyitam ।।
pūjā dhanenaiva na satkulena kīrtir dhanenaiva na vikrameṇa । rūpaṃ dhanenaiva na yauvanena kriyā dhanenaiva na jīvitena ।। vṛddhāḥ prasiddhā vibudhā vidagdhāḥ śūrā śrutijñāḥ kavayaḥ kulīnāḥ । vilokayantaḥ sadhanasya vaktraṃ jayeti jīveti sadā vadanti ।।
kruddhyā yutaṃ niścalapakṣmanetraṃ maunānvitaṃ devam ivākulīnam । dīnaḥ kulīnaḥ praṇamatkulīnaḥ kṛtāñjaliḥ stauti dhanābhikāmaḥ ।। tasmād alabdhadraviṇasya lābhe labdhasya rakṣāniyame yatena । saṃrakṣamāṇasya sadā vivṛddhau vṛddhasya ca sthānavibhāgasarge ।।
