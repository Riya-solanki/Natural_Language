yā tvaṃ sarvānavadyāṅgi niravadyair nirīkṣyase ।। yas tvāṃ paśyati bhāvena sa paśyati tathāgatam ।। na paśyanty antaraṃ santaś candracandrikayor iva ।। sukhenāyānti māhātmyam atulaṃ bhaktivatsale ।।
tenāpi niyataṃ siddhiḥ prāpyate amoghadarśane ।। poṣikā janayitrī ca mātā tvam asi vatsalā ।। tena tvam api kalyāṇi sarvasattvapitāmahī ।। candralekheva tārābhir anuyātāsi sarvadā ।।
bahurūpā tvam evaikā nānānāmabhir īḍyase ।। tvāṃ prāpya pralayaṃ yānti doṣā vādāś ca vādinām ।। āśvāsajananī cāsi viduṣāṃ saumyadarśanā ।। tasyāmba katham anyatra rāgadveṣau bhaviṣyataḥ ।।
sthāneṣv api ca sarveṣu vidvadbhir nopalabhyase ।। prapadya ca vimucyante tad idaṃ mahad adbhutam ।। tvām eva mucyate paśyann apaśyann api mucyate ।। sudurbodhāsi māyeva dṛśyase na ca dṛśyase ।।
mārgas tvam ekā mokṣasya nāsty anya iti niścayaḥ ।। kṛpayā lokanāthais tvam ucyase ca na cocyase ।। sarvavāgviśayātītāṃ yā tvaṃ kva cid aniśritā ।। tvām astutyām api satīṃ tuṣṭūṣantaḥ sunirvṛtāḥ ।।
tenāstv āśu jagat kṛtsnaṃ prajñāpāraparāyaṇam ।।
