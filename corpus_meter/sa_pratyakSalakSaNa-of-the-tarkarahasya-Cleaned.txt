"na so'sti pratyayo loke yaḥ śabdānugamād ṛte। anuviddhamiva jñānaṃ sarvaṃ śabdena bhāsate।।" taddṛṣṭāv eva dṛṣṭeṣu saṃvitsāmarthyabhāvinaḥ। smaraṇād abhilāṣeṇa vyavahāraḥ pravartate।। astīyam api yā tv antarupaplavasamudbhavā।। doṣodbhavāprakṛtyā sā vitathapratibhāsinī। anapekṣitasādharmyadṛgādi taimirādivat।।
ekaṃ dharmiṇam uddiśya nānādharmasamāśrayam। vidhāv ekasya tadbhājam ivānyeṣāmupekṣakam।। niṣedhe tadviviktañ ca tadanyeṣām apekṣakam। vyavahāram asatyārthaṃ prakalpayati dhīr yathā।। taṃ tathaivāvikalpyārthabhedāśrayamupāgatāḥ। anādivāsanodbhūtaṃ bādhante'rthaṃ na laukikam।। vikalpavyavahāreṣu samānau bālapaṇḍitau।
ekapratyavamarśārthajñānādyekārthasādhane। bhede'pi niyatāḥ kecit svabhāvenendriyādivat।। "naimittikyāḥ śruter artham arthaṃ vā pāramārthikam। śabdānāṃ pratirundhāno na bādhyastena varṇitaḥ।।" vicchedaṃ sūcayannekam apratikṣipya vartate। yadānyaṃ tena sa vyāpta ekatvena ca bhāsate।। sāmānādhikaraṇyaṃsyāt tadā buddhyanurodhataḥ।
"rūpādiśaktibhedānām anākṣepeṇa vartate। tatsamānaphalāhetuvyavacchedeghaṭaśrutiḥ।। tatona rūpaṃ ghaṭa ity ekādhikaraṇā śrutiḥ। bhedaścāyam ato jātisamudāyābhidhāyinoḥ।।"iti saṃsthānavarṇāvayavair viśiṣṭe yaḥ pravartate। śabdo na tasyāvayave pravṛttir upalabhyate।। "apravṛttiḥpramāṇānām apravṛttiphalāsati।"
"pratibhāso'sya nāsyeti nopapattes tu gocaraḥ।" iti, "saṃvedanasya tādātmye na vivādo'sti kasya cit।" "viśeṣaṇaṃviśeṣyañ ca sambandhaṃ laukikīṃ sthitim। gṛhītvā saṅkalayyaitat tathā pratyeti nānyathā।। "sthūlapratyavamarśasya hetutvāt sthūlatā dhiyaḥ। sthūladhīhetubhāvena bhāvānāṃsthūlatā matā।।" "astīyam api yā tv antarupaplavasamudbhavā। doṣodbhavā prakṛtyā sā vitathapratibhāsinī।।"
