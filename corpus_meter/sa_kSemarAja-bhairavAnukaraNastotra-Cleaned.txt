oṃ namaś cidbhairavāya । citibhūmisamāśrayaṇāś citibhūtiṃ śrayata mā mudhā bhramata । prathayann iti bhaktimatāṃ jayati śivo anugrahapravaṇaḥ ।। cidbhairavam eva paraṃ paramāmṛtarūpam ekam atidīptam ।
ullasitakaraṇacakragrastasamastaṃ śivaṃ vande ।। stutyaḥ stotā stutir iti yad api vibhinnaṃ na kiṃcid astīha । mṛṣati yathā yad rūpaṃ cidrūpatayā tathā bhavaty etat ।। vigalitasarvavibhedaṃ sarvavibhedātma cidghanānandam ।
yat tava tattvaṃ bhagavaṃs tasyānukṛtiṃ tvadākṛtiṃ vande ।। rekhāpuruṣaḥ puruṣaṃ varṇalipir varṇasaṃcayaṃ yadvat । tadvat vicitrarūpaṃ tvām ākāro vyanakti paramātman ।। tatrāvikalpam eva tvāṃ cidrūpaṃ samāviśya ।
vimṛśāmaḥ kim api manāṅ na hi tattvaṃ kalpanāviṣayaḥ ।। svātmaprabhāṃ vidhāya vāgdevīṃ viṣayakusumakḷptārcaḥ । prathamonmeṣaṃ gurum atha vanditvā hṛdayam āviśya ।। staumi vimṛśan maheśvaram ātmānaṃ svaṃ cidekaghanam ।
srāvitapāśakadambakam etasmin dehadevagṛhe ।। cityānandecchāvitkaraṇākhyāḥ śaktayo jayanti vibho । sūkṣmasthūlabhidāptā vaktratvaṃ brahmamuṇḍabhidhā ।। sarvajñatādiviṣayaṃ hṛdayādikam aṅgaṣaṭkaṃ te ।
sakalaṃ sadāśivāntaṃ pretātmatayā tavāsanaṃ svāmin ।। paramaprakāśavapuṣo vimarśaśaktiḥ prabho parā devī । asyā eva prasaraḥ sarvā brāhmyādikā devyaḥ ।। dahanajalāmṛtalekhāviṣādi bibhrat tvam ādiśasy etat ।
viśvaṃ viruddham api saccidrūpe mayi virudhyate naitat ।। saṃvitsūtranilīno niḥsāro mātṛnivaho ayam । karaṇamuṇḍamālabhārin darśayasītīva naḥ svāmin ।। paśyata paśyata pāśā viṣamās ta ime bahiṣkṛtāḥ śaśvat ।
ity antradhāraṇamiṣat kathayaṃs tvaṃ naḥ samābhāsi ।। māyāpaṭaḥ sarāgo vyomni pare asmin sa eṣa nikṣiptaḥ । iti gajacarma saraktaṃ mūrdhni vahan kathayasīśa ।। antaḥśaktikṛpāṇīm vyanakṣi saṃsṛtivibhedinīm asinā ।
nijaśaktimahimasvīkṛtasamastaviśvā hi vīravarāḥ ।। bhavabhayahantā so aham sānāthye avasthito asmi mā bhaiṣṭa । ity āsphoṭitakheṭakadarśanato diśasi naḥ svāmin ।। nijaśaktipāśavalitaś cidvapuṣā kalyate mayā kālaḥ ।
iti pāśadhāraṇavaśāt prathayasi naḥ kālakālas tvam ।। bhedamayam akhilam etan nijaśaktyaivākṣipāmi saṃhartum । ity aṅkuśadhāraṇataḥ sphuṭayati parabhairavo asmākam ।। kodaṇḍārūdhaśarapradarśanād brahmaviṣṇurudreśān ।
sasadākhyaśivāñ chaktyā kāraṇahariṇaṃ bhinatsi mṛgavat tvam ।। varadena pāṇinā tvaṃ viśvavibhūtipradatvam abhinayasi । na khalu paratattvaniṣṭho vibhavamalaiḥ spṛśyate jātu ।। abhayena ca bhavabhayāni unmūlayatā prakāśyate satatam ।
viśvānugrahakāraṇasvabhāvatā tava karatalena ।। akhyātirūpam asamaṃ māyāyāḥ śakalitaṃ śiro atraitat । parabodhamayena mayā muṇḍaṃ bibhrat prakāśayasy evam ।। niḥśeṣahṛtasārā mayy eva jagatsthitiś cidekaghane ।
iti khaṭvāṅgakaraṅkodvahanacchalato dadāsy ājñām ।। vīṇāghaṇtāḍamarūn uḍḍāmara vādayann idaṃ diśasi । vṛttitribhedabhinnaṃ nādaṃ śṛṇvan sthito asmīti ।। kiṃ ca parādikaśaktitritayārāsundarām mahāśaktim ।
devānukaroṣi bahis triśūladhāraṇamiṣeṇa nityaṃ tvam ।। icchādikanijaśaktiprakāśitādhaḥsthagocaratritayām । svām eva parāṃ śaktiṃ vajramayīṃ vahasi ṣaḍarāṃ tvam ।। jagad akhilaṃ macchaktyā damitaṃ sarvavyāvasthitīr dhatte ।
iti daṇḍadhāraṇavaśād vyanakṣi cidbhairavāsmākam ।। mudgaraparaśū bibhrad baindavanādānukārarūpau tvam । bhedavibhedanaśakalanaparatvam īśāna nirdiśasi ।। darśanabhedāt svāminn anyānyām āhṛtiṃ dadhānas tvam ।
bhairavaparabodhamayaṃ prathayasi nijadhāma sarvatra ।। śāktāṇḍakhaṇḍamadhye viśvarasāḥ sarvam ahaṃ pibāmi sadā । vyañjayasi narakapālagarudhiramiṣād etad iva me antaḥ ।। abhimānahṛdayabandhānutkhātau tāv imau saśikhau ।
mūrtāv iva muṇdābjau prathayan dhatse mahākālaḥ ।। akhilajagadbījabhuvaṃ prathamasthām anukaroṣi bodhanidhe । karatalagabījapūrakadarśanalīlāyitād deva ।। bhairavarūpo apy asi yat tatrāpi kare akṣamālikāṃ dhatse ।
tat sarvākṣavikāsād viśvaṃ parivartayasy antaḥ ।। asthikaramuṇḍamālābhasmābharaṇo nṛraktaruciḥ । viśvaṃ brahmamayatvāc chuddham itīśaḥ samādiśasi ।। svātmārāmamayānāṃ viṣamā api viṣayabudbudhāḥ kim amī ।
smaradahanam alikanayanam prathayan paramāgnirūpam ādiśasi ।। antarnibaddhalakṣyaḥ pravikāsikaraṇo api yat sadā bhavasi । tat prathayasi sarvadaśaṃ gatamohakalaṅkam ātmānam ।। deva digambaratā te vadati nirāvaraṇam ātmano rūpam ।
paramabrahmamayārkān māyāvaraṇam kathaṃ vṛṇuyāt ।। saṃhartā sarveṣām arkādīnām ahaṃ tamobhūmiḥ । nirmalaśāradagaganaprabheṇa vapuṣeti darśayasi ।। paramabrahmamayas tvaṃ deva yadāśrayasi bhairavākāram ।
tat prathayasi tattvajñaḥ saty api bhede vimukta iti ।। vividhaśmaśānamālāvetālāvṛtaḥ prakāśayase । saṃvittiviṣayakalanāmadhyaś citibhairavo asmīti ।। tvayy evāmṛtalekhā paraśaktimayī parapramātātaḥ ।
nātha tvam eva jagatām jīvanam iti bhāsi no hṛdaye ।। tava śabdarāśivapuṣaḥ pratyaṣṭakam eva bāhyaparivāraḥ । parasūkṣmādivibhedād bhairava lokeśvarapramukhāḥ ।। mudrayati nikhilam etan niḥśeṣasvasvarūpasaṃkramaṇāt ।
modayati bhaktavargaṃ gatimudaṃ pūrṇatāprathanāt ।। mocayati pāśajālaṃ drāvayati vibhedam īśa te mūrtiḥ । mudrayati vividhasaṃviddraviṇaṃ mudrā tataḥ kathitā ।। jayati paratattvabhūmipravare nipāyabhūtaiṣa ।
sphuṭam adhivasatu satattvaprathayā hṛdayasya meyamalam ।। pratikalam udeti saṃvid yā kila tatra sphuraty adho viśvam । śivaśaktivibhavarūpaṃ jānanti maheśa yāgasthāḥ ।। itthaṃ sphurasi sadā me cidbhairava sarvabhūtātma ।
tad yāgādijapāntaṃ sampannam ayatnataḥ sarvam ।। iti śrīmahāmāheśvarācāryaśrīkṣemarājaviracitaṃ bhairavānukaraṇastotraṃ samāptam ।।
