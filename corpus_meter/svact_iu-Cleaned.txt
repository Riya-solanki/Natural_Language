abhimukhakhaḍgani pātitaśūraśiraḥ śoṣitaṃ samādāya ।a asurendracakravartinamasurendraguruṃ vā tamānayatyanilavegāt ।b ākramya bhūmilikhitaṃ sādhyābhimukho ardharātrakāle tu ।b ānayati mahāpuruṣaṃ kṣitipatimapi divasaśatabhāgāt ।a
ānayati śacīmahalyāmathavā divasasya śatabhāgāt ।b udbaddhastrītanuvāmāṅghreḥ pāṃsulīṃ samādāya ।a kṛtvā kapālasampuṭamatha mṛtasūtreṇa veṣṭayet samyak ।a kṛtvā yakṣasurāsurapannaganārīḥ samānayatyāśu ।b
kroṅkārāṅkuśayogādānayati surāsurān kṣipram ।a kṣitipatimapi sāmātyaṃ cānayati nimeṣaśatabhāgāt ।a khadirāṅgārasutaptaṃ sikthakaliptaṃ tu tatpunaḥ kṛtvā ।b khadirānale vidhūme asuragurumapyānayatyanilavegāt ।a
khadirānale sutaptāṃ rātryardhe sammukho japaśatena ।a gandhodakena liptaṃ nṛkapālaṃ vai dvitīyamādāya ।b gandhodvartitavāmahastena tu tattvabījayuktena ।a tatra trirūpagaditaṃ dhāma likhitvābhipūjayedyastu ।a
tasya haripavanakamalajadhanadayamendrāḥ sasiddhagandharvāḥ ।b nāma ca tasya lalāṭe mantreṇa vidarbhitaṃ samālikhya ।a nijavāmakare alaktakarocanayā sādhyanāma parilikhitam ।a nṛkapālamadhyalikhitaṃ rocanayā raktamiśrayā sādhyam ।b
pretānale sutaptaṃ vidhāya niśi yatkṛte śataṃ japati ।a pretānale sutaptāṃ śatābhijaptāṃ svanāmamantrayutām ।a pretālaktakanijarudhirarocanābhirvilikhya sādhyatanum ।b pretālaktakalikhitaṃ naraśirasi pretavahnisantaptam ।a
bhittau gairikalikhitaṃ mantrārṇavidarbhitaṃ tadabhidhānam ।a mantravidarbhitametajjapaśatayuktaṃ sutāpitaṃ rātrau ।b mṛtanāryā vāmapadādudbaddhāyāstu pāṃsulīṃ samādāya ।a yamalokādapyacirādānayati balena pūrvavat sādhyam ।b
yāvat sikthakametat kapālalagnaṃ vilīyate tāvat ।a raktālaktakalikhitaṃ sādhyatanau mantrayuktamabhidhānam ।b raṇaśastraghātapatitaṃ narapiśitaṃ trimadhusaṃyutaṃ juhuyāt ।b rudhirālaktakarocanayā sādhyatanuṃ mantrasaṃyuktām ।b
viparītacakramudrāṃ baddhvā sādhyaṃ tu nikṣipenmadhye ।a vividhavarasiddhijātaṃ vidadhati vicitrāstathāparāḥ siddhīḥ ।a vyaktāvyaktaṃ tathā vyaktamavyaktaṃ tu trirūpakam ।b śitaśastrapātarahitadhvajanaraśīrṣaṃ pragṛhya lakṣamayutam ।b
sampīḍitakarasampuṭavihvalavaktraṃ karāntare dhyātvā ।b sādhyamabhidhānalikhitaṃ bhūmitale gairikeṇa raktena ।b sādhyābhimukho rātrau vāmakarākrāntamatha japan kruddhaḥ ।b surapatimapyākarṣati japaśatayogānnimeṣamātreṇa ।b
