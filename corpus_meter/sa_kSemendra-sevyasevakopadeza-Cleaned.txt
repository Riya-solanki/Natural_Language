mahākaviśrīkṣemendrakṛtaḥ sevyasevakopadeśaḥ । vibhūṣaṇāya mahate tṛṣṇātimirahāriṇe । namaḥ saṃtoṣaratnāya sevāviṣavināśine ।। utsṛjya nijakāryāṇi sadbhir bāṣpākulekṣaṇam ।
sevyasevakasevānāṃ kriyatām anuśāsanam ।। darpād ekaḥ paro lobhād dvāv andhau sevyasevakau । dhanoṣmadainyavikṛtī mukhe kaḥ kasya paśyati ।। durvāramohalobhāndho yadi na syād ayaṃ janaḥ ।
kaḥ krūrakrodhavidhuraṃ saheta dhanināṃ mukham ।। yaḥ pṛthvīm api darpāndho na paśyati puraḥsthitām । sa dainyalaghutāṃ yātaṃ kathaṃ sevakam īkṣate ।। agatiṃ vāhayaty eko badhiraṃ stauti cāparaḥ ।
aho jagati hāsyāya nirlajjau sevyasevakau ।। dūraṃ huṃkāramātreṇa visṛṣṭo mārgaṇaḥ sadā । guṇabhraṣṭaḥ kriyāhīno nodvegaṃ yāti sevakaḥ ।। manye sukṛtinā tena bhāgīrathyāṃ kṛtaṃ tapaḥ ।
vairāgyabhāgī rathyāṃ yaḥ sevāsu na vigāhate ।। kathitakleśavāpena śāpeneva vipākinā । sevātāpena pacyante na hy aduṣkṛtino narāḥ ।। adainyapuṇyamanasāṃ yaśas teṣāṃ virājate ।
sevāpaṅkakalaṅkānāṃ yair na pātrīkṛtaṃ śiraḥ ।। prabhupraṇāme jaṭharaṃ dainyamūkaṃ vilokayan । praveṣṭuṃ sevakaḥ śaṅke vilakṣaḥ kṣitim īkṣate ।। sevādhvajo añjalir mūrdhni hṛdi dainyaṃ mukhe stutiḥ ।
āśāgrahagṛhītānāṃ kiyatīyaṃ viḍambanā ।। akālāgamanakrodhavidhureśvaracakṣuṣā । kṣipraṃ smara ivāyāti sevako dugdhabhūtitām ।। punaḥ sevāvamānānāṃ tena dattaṃ tilodakam ।
praviśya vāhinīṃ yena khaḍgapātrīkṛtaṃ vapuḥ ।। kaṇavṛttiparikliṣṭaḥ kaṣṭaṃ sevakacātakaḥ । ghanāśānirato nityam udgrīvaḥ pariśuṣyati ।। niḥsaṃtoṣaḥ parityajya bhramaraḥ puṣpitaṃ vanam ।
sevate dānalobhena mātaṅgam api sevakaḥ ।। jaḍasevāparikliṣṭas tīvradambhabakavrataḥ । kṛcchreṇa kṣaṇikāṃ prītim āsādayati sevakaḥ ।। nityam unnatikāmo api mānabhaṅgād adhomukhaḥ ।
yat satyam ubhayabhraṣṭas triśaṅkur iva sevakaḥ ।। svamāṃsavikrayāsaktaḥ prabhuvetālaghaṭṭitaḥ । niḥsattvaḥ pralayaṃ yāti rātrisevāsu sevakaḥ ।। bhūmiśāyī nirāhāraḥ śītavātātapakṣataḥ ।
munivrato api narakakleśam aśnāti sevakaḥ ।। karoti sevakaḥ sevāṃ tāvad āśām ivāyatām । yāvad bhogarasajñena yauvanena viyujyate ।। cakṣur dvāry añjalau pāṇiḥ stutau jihvā natau śiraḥ ।
aho nu sevakaiḥ kāyaḥ paropakaraṇīkṛtaḥ ।। hanta yācñāvamānena sevako laghutāṃ gataḥ । sevāpaṅkapade magno yad anviṣṭo na labhyate ।। aviśrāntyāviraktasya dīrghocchvāsena śuṣyataḥ ।
jareva duḥsahā jantoḥ sevā saṃkocakāriṇī ।। truṭyati klinnatantrīva māleva mlāyati kṣaṇāt । sevā nāracayaty eva dinacchedena naśyati ।। sevāsaṃkṣapitāṅgānāṃ rājarathyāsu śuṣyatām ।
tīvratṛṣṇāviṣārtānāṃ śaraṇam śamavāridaḥ ।। lobhasya mohādhvani vṛddhabhāve jātā durāśāśayitasya sevā । adhomukhī sā satataṃ salajjā jaratkumārīva na kasya śocyā ।। āśeva śūnyeṣu vivartamānā tṛṣṇeva saṃtoṣaparāṅmukhīyam ।
divāniśaṃ karṣaṇadīrgharajjuḥ sevā surāṇām api dainyabhūmiḥ ।। nisargavandhyāsu phalāśayā ye kurvanti sevāsu sadā prayatnam । kṛṣipravṛttāḥ śaśaśṛṅgakoṭyā khananti mūrkhāḥ khalu svasthalīṃ te ।। antaḥsthite apy ātmani yaiḥ kriyante sevāpravṛttaiḥ puruṣaiḥ praṇāmāḥ ।
dugdhābdikallolini kūlakacche tṛṣṇāturās te jalam arthayante ।। bhāgīrathītīravanāntareṣu phalāvanamreṣu mahādrumeṣu । kṣuttāpatṛṣṇāśamaneṣu satsu kiṃ dainyasevāvyasanāvamānaiḥ ।। nityaprasaktyā na sṛjaty avajñāṃ na laulyagarhāgaṇanāṃ karoti ।
atyantayācñāsu na yāti khedaṃ bhikṣābhujām pātram ato nu sevyam ।। jayanti te svasti namo astu tebhyaḥ prabhūpraṇāmacyutamānaratnam । sevāprayāsavyasaneṣu mithyā rathyārajobhāgi śiro na yeṣām ।। kiṃ rājabhir durjanajanmajālavyāptair avāptair api kaṣṭadṛṣṭaiḥ ।
santy eva sevyā bhuvi bhūbhṛtas te taṭeṣu yeṣāṃ munayo niviṣṭāḥ ।। tṛṣṇāturāṇāṃ rajasā vṛtānāṃ sevākukūlānalatāpitānām । śāntyai hitaṃ candrakirīṭajūṭamaitrīpavitraṃ tridaśāpagāmbhaḥ ।। utsṛjya tīvraṃ viṣayābhilāṣaṃ saṃtoṣapoṣaṃ kuśalo bhajasva ।
ity arthyamānaḥ sukhasevayāyam ātmāiva sarvaṃ sudhiyāṃ dadāti ।। ekasya jāne sukhabhogabhājaḥ ślāghyasya sevāvyasanaṃ nivṛttam । sadaiva śiśnodaravarjitatvād ayācyayācñārahitasya rahoḥ ।। sevāvrate prāya ivāpaviṣṭaḥ sarvātmanā niścitajīvanāśaḥ ।
dvāḥsthaiḥ prayatnena nivārito api na durgrahaṃ muñcati naṣṭasaṃjñaḥ ।। tamo'dhvatāpena ca cintayā ca śitena dainyena ca piṇḍitāṅgaḥ । sukhāśayā duḥkhasahasrabhāgī sevāvrataṃ mugdhamatir bibharti ।। gacchāmi gacchāmi dinaṃ gataṃ me dine gato ahaṃ na nṛpo adya dṛṣṭaḥ ।
iti bruvāṇasya sadā janasya jīrṇātarāṃ tasya tanur na tṛṣṇā ।। vicchedakārī sahasā kathānām anīpsito dīpa iva praviṣṭaḥ । saṃsaktavastrāntaramakṣikeva na sevakaḥ kasya karoti duḥkham ।। dhūmāyamāno akṣivipakṣabhūtaś cāṭukramotpāditakarṇaśūlaḥ ।
kusevakaḥ pādatale avalagnaḥ pade pade kaṇṭakatām upaiti ।। ekaḥ kham eva kṣitim īkṣate anyaḥ sa nirjanārthī sa ca gāḍhalagnaḥ । svasvārthitā tasya bhṛśaṃ sa cārthī kathaṃ sa sevyaḥ sa ca sevako astu ।। chāyāgraho mūrta ivānuyāyī sthitaś ciraṃ vṛścikaniścalaś ca ।
akālapātī sukhakelilīlāyantrotpalaḥ sevakadurvidagdhaḥ ।। visṛjyamāno apy aniśaṃ na yāti tīvroparodhapraṇayena mūrkhaḥ । bhartuḥ prasāde vihitaprayatno jātaḥ paraṃ pratyuta kopahetuḥ ।। śūtkāridīrghaśvasitānubandhair niveditodvegagatiprayāsaḥ ।
atipraveśāt kharamārgaṇo asau nirasyamāno viphalatvam eti ।। śrīratnacandravaravājigajorjite asmin gambhīrarājakulanāmni mahāsamudre । aśnāti mūḍhamatir īśvaratām avāptum āśāvaśāt kṣitipater bahu kālakūṭam ।। virama virama neyaṃ pāntha namrāmramālā badhira khadirapālī niṣphalaiṣā prayāhi ।
iti bahuvidham uktaḥ sevako anyāpadeśais tyajati na vipulāśāpāśabaddhaḥ kusevām ।। pramlānā sarasatvam eti na punar māleva lagnātapā bhagnā kācamayīva saṃdhighaṭanā yogena na śliṣyati । sevā dīpaśikheva durgatagṛhe saṃdhāryamāṇā paraṃ niḥsnehā kṣayam eti khedajanitair ucchvāsamālānilaiḥ ।। kas tvaṃ gatvara sevakas tvaritatā kasmāt prabhur dṛśyate lābhaḥ kas tava tatra vetrivipulāghātaiḥ śirastāḍanam ।
kiṃ mṛdgāsi mudā viśālacaraṇair mithyaiva rathyām imām bandho vandhyanarendrabhavyabhavanabhrāntyā viḍambyāmahe ।। suptākarṣaṇadāma dhāma mahato mohasya dainyasya vā sevākleśam aharniśaṃ viṣahate yo vittaleśāśayā । prasphūrjadvaḍavāgnigarbhagahanodbhūtormimālākulaṃ saṃkṣubdhaṃ makarākaraṃ praviśati śrīratnalabdhyai na kim ।। vyarthātyarthagatāgatavyatikarair aśrāntatīvratvaro dvārālokanatatparaḥ parijanair utsāryamāṇaḥ param ।
yatnenaiva puraḥ praveśakalanaiḥ kleśāvamānāśrayaḥ sarvāpatpiśunaḥ śunaḥ sadṛśatām ālapsyate sevakaḥ ।। kūjatkrūrakapāṭapīḍanaruṣā dattaprahāraiḥ paraṃ dvārāvasthaganāya susthirabhujair dvāḥsthair bhṛśaṃ bhartsitaḥ । kubjībhūya janasya jānuvivaraiḥ kṣiprapraveśotsukaḥ pṛcchaty antaranirgatānavasaraṃ maunavratān sevakaḥ ।। dvāre ruddham upekṣate katham api prāptaṃ puro nekṣate vijñaptau gajamīlanāni kurute gṛhṇāti vākyacchalam ।
niryātasya karoti doṣagaṇanāṃ svalpāparādhe yamaḥ sa svāmī yadi sevyate marutaṭe kiṃ naḥ piśācaiḥ kṛtam ।। nityaṃ rājakule na pūjyayajane taddvārapālārcanaṃ bhaktiṃ bhūtisamudbhave na tu bhave dhyānaṃ dhane nātmani । dṛṣṭādṛṣṭavicintanaṃ narapatau na svocite karmaṇi śrīhetor bata niṣphalaṃ prakurute sarvaṃ jaḍaḥ sevakaḥ ।। sā tīvraṃ jaḍanimnapātanaratā no nityasaktā tvarā rathyāpaṅkakalaṅkitaṃ na vadanaṃ na dvārapālaiḥ kaliḥ ।
yasmiṃs taccirasevakaḥ pravitataṃ puṇyaṃ vanaṃ gamyatāṃ krūrās te na bhavanti tatra vikṛtakrodhoddhatāḥ pārthivāḥ ।। haṃho kaṣṭavinaṣṭa sevaka sakhe khinno asi poṣāśayā rāgaś ced vibhave tad eṣa kupatiḥ klībaḥ kim āsevyate । sevyaḥ ko api maheśvaraḥ sa bhagavān yasya prasādekṣaṇair akṣuṇṇaiḥ karuṇāspadīkṛtamarunnātho maruttaḥ kṛtaḥ ।। rājñām ajñatayā kṛtaṃ yad aniśaṃ dainyaṃ tad utsṛjyatāṃ saṃtoṣambhasi mṛjyatām api rajaḥ pādapraṇāmārjitam ।
saṃtoṣaḥ paramaḥ purāṇapuruṣaḥ saṃvinmayaḥ sevyatāṃ yatsmṛtyā na bhavanti te sumanasāṃ bhūyo bhavagranthayaḥ ।। utsṛjya prājyasevāṃ vijanasukhajuṣāṃ bhūbhujāṃ vyājabhājāṃ chittvāśāpāśabandhān vimalaśamajalais tīvratṛṣṇāṃ nivārya । sthitva śuddhe samādhau kim aparam amṛtaṃ mṛgyatāṃ jyotir antar yasmin dṛṣṭe vinaṣṭotkaṭatimirabhare labhayte mokṣalakṣmīḥ ।। vṛttyā jīvati lokaḥ sevāvṛttir nijaiva keṣāṃcit ।
asthāne tīvratarā nindyā tu tadarthināṃ sevā ।। vidvajjanārādhanatatpareṇa saṃtoṣasevārasanirbhareṇa । kṣemendranāmnā sudhiyāṃ sadaiva sukhāya sevāvasaraḥ kṛto ayam ।। iti śrīvyāsadāsāparākhyamahākaviśrīkṣemendrakṛtaḥ sevyasevakopadeśaḥ samāptaḥ ।
