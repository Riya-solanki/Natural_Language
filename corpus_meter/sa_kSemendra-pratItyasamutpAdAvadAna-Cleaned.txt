sarvam avidyāmūlaṃ saṃsārataruprakāravaicitryam । jñātum vaktuṃ hantuṃ kaḥ śakto anyatra sarvajñāt ।। śrāvastyāṃ svastimān pūrvaṃ jino jetavane sthitaḥ । aśeṣadarśī bhagavān bhikṣusaṃgham abhāṣata ।।
śṛṇuta śreyase jñānālokanirmalamānasāḥ । pratītyasamutpādaṃ vaḥ kathayāmi yathākramam ।। avidyāvāsanaiveyaṃ duḥkhaskandhasya bhūyasaḥ । saṃsāraviṣavṛkṣasya mūlabandhavidhāyinī ।।
tatpratyayas tu saṃskārāḥ kāyavāṅmānasātmakāḥ । saṃskārotthaṃ ca vijñānaṃ manaḥṣaṣṭhendriyātmakamn ।। tatpratyayaṃ nāmarūpaṃ saṃjñāsaṃdarśanābhidham । manaḥṣaṣṭhendriyasthānaṃ ṣaḍāyatanam apy ataḥ ।।
ṣaḍāyatanasaṃśleṣaḥ sparśa ity abhidhīyate । ṣaṭsparśānubhavo yaś ca vedanā sā prakīrtitā ।। tayā viṣayasaṃkleśarāgāt tṛṣṇā prajāyate । kāmādiṣu tadudbhūtam upādānaṃ pravartate ।।
upādānodbhavaḥ kāmarūpārūpyamayo bhavaḥ । nānāyoniparāvṛttyā jātir bhavasamudbhavā ।। jarāmaraṇaśokādisaṃtatir jātisaṃśrayā । avidyādinirodhena teṣāṃ vyuparamakramaḥ ।।
sa cintyo yuṣmābhir vijanavanaviśrāmaśamibhiḥ । tanutvaṃ saṃprāptaḥ sukhataranivāryaś ca bhavati ।। iti kṣemendraviracitāyāṃ bodhisattvāvadānakalpalatāyāṃ pratītyasamutpādāvadānaṃ pañcasaptatitamaḥ pallavaḥ ।
