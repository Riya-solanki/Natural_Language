astināstivyatikrāntā buddhir yeṣāṃ nirāśrayā । gambhīras tair nirālambaḥ pratyayārtho vibhāvyate ।। nagysk saṃsāraṃ caiva nirvāṇaṃ manyante atattvadarśinaḥ । na saṃsāraṃ na nirvāṇaṃ manyante tattvadarśinaḥ ।। nagysk
nirvāṇaṃ ca bhavaś caiva dvayam etan na vidyate । parajñānaṃ bhavasyaiva nirvāṇam iti kathyate ।। nagysk tat tat prāpya yad utpannaṃ notpannaṃ tat svabhāvataḥ । svabhāvena yan notpannam utpannaṃ nāma tat katham ।। nagysk
sarvam astīti vaktavyam ādau tattvagaveṣiṇaḥ । paścād avagatārthasya niḥsaṅgasya viviktatā ।। nagysk mamety aham iti proktaṃ yathā kāryavaśāj jinaiḥ । tathā kāryavaśāt proktāḥ skandhāyatanadhātavaḥ ।। nagysk
mahābhūtādi vijñāne proktaṃ samavarudhyate । tajjñāne vagamaṃ yāti nanu mithyā vikalpitam ।। nagysk hetutaḥ saṃbhavo yasya sthitir na pratyayair vinā । vigamaḥ pratyayābhāvāt so astīty avagataḥ katham ।। nagysk
rāgadveṣodbhavas tīvraduṣṭadṛṣṭiparigrahaḥ । vivādās tatsamutthāś ca bhāvābhyupagame sati ।। nagysk sa hetuḥ sarvadṛṣṭīnāṃ kleśotpattir na taṃ vinā । tasmāt tasmin parijñāte dṛṣṭikleśaparikṣayaḥ ।। nagysk
parijñā tasya keneti pratītyotpādadarśanāt । pratītya jātaṃ cājātam āha tattvavidāṃ varaḥ ।। nagysk bālāḥ sajjanti rūpeṣu vairāgyaṃ yānti madhyamāḥ । svabhāvajñā vimucyante rūpasyottamabuddhayaḥ ।। nagysk
