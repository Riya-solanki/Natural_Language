samo viśeṣa uta vāpi hīno yo manyate sa vivadeta tena ।
vidhātraye asmin na vikampate yaḥ samo viśiṣṭaś ca na tasya bhavati ।।

geya bhikṣo bhikṣo ogham atārṣīḥ । āma devate । anālambe apratiṣṭhe ogham atārṣīḥ । āma devate । yathā kathaṃ tvaṃ bhikṣo anālambe apratiṣṭhe ogham atārṣīḥ । yathā yathāhaṃ devate āyūhāmi tathā tathā saṃsīdāmi । yathā yathā saṃsīdāmi tathā tathā saṃtiṣṭhe । yathā yathā saṃtiṣṭhe tathā tathā uhye । yathā yathāhaṃ devate nāyūhāmi tathā tathā na saṃsīdāmīti vistareṇa śuklapakṣo veditavyaḥ ।।

subhāṣitaṃ hy uttamam āhur āryāḥ priyaṃ vaden nāpriyaṃ tad dvitīyaṃ ।
satyaṃ vaden nānṛtaṃ tat tṛtīyaṃ dharmaṃ vaden nādharmaṃ tat caturtham ।।

sthitiḥ prapaṃcāś ca na santi yasya yaḥ saṃdānaṃ parighaṃ cātivṛttaḥ । taṃ nistṛṣṇaṃ muniṃ carantaṃ na vijānāti sadevako api lokaḥ ।। yasya vitarkā vidhūpitā adhyātmam avikalpitā aśeṣaṃ । saṃgaṃ so atītya rūpasaṃjñāṃ caturyogāpagato na jātim eti ।। 
dadataḥ puṇyaṃ pravardhate vairaṃ saṃyamato na cīyate ।
kuśalī prajahāti pāpakaṃ kleśānāṃ kṣayatas tu nirvṛtaḥ ।।

cittanimittasya kovidaḥ pravivekasya ca vindate rasaṃ ।
dhyāyī nipakaḥ pratismṛto bhuṃkte prītisukhaṃ nirāmiṣaṃ ।। 
aśilpajīvī laghur ātmakāmo jitendriyaḥ sarvato vipramuktaḥ । anokasārī hy amamo nirāśaḥ kāmān prahāyaikacaro yas sa bhikṣuḥ ।। dūraṃgamam ekacaram aśarīraṃ guhāśayaṃ । damayati durdamaṃ cittaṃ brāhmaṇaṃ taṃ bravīmy ahaṃ ।।
