āśākāśāvakāśaprasṛmarayaśaso bhāgyasaubhāgyarāśeḥ raicarteyānvayendo raghupatisadṛśaḥ kaumudīkāntakānteḥ ।
bāhau śeṣāhidīrghe jalajakaratale bhāti viśvaṃbhareyaṃ krudhyatkālāhidaṃṣṭrāṃ mukhavikaṭamukhīṃ khaḍgarekhāṃ dadhāne ।।
