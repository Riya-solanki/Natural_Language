phullat-pūratayā sphurad-ghana-rasā haṃsādi-gīḥ-śiñjitāḥ ।
unmaryāda-daśām itā muraripuṃ dūre' bhisṛtyāgatā
bhaṅgālola-bhujaiḥ saroja-valayas tasyāṅghri-yugmaṃ dadhuḥ ।। [śārdūla]
