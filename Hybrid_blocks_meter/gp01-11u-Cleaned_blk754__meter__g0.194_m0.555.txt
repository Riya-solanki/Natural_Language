nāmādeśaṃ yadā mātā diśate nayanādikam ।
kṛṣṇaś ca kurute bāḍhaṃ cakre tasyā na kiṃ tadā? ।। [anuṣṭubh]
rudantam indave mantha-gargaryāṃ pratirūpiṇe ।
piṇḍena nāvanītena vṛddhāgardhayatārbhakam ।। [anuṣṭubh]
svasya svalpāpahāre' pi cakranda maṇi-hānivat ।
kṛtvānya-maṇi-hāniṃ ca prāhasīd bāla-kṛṣṇakaḥ ।। [anuṣṭubh]
