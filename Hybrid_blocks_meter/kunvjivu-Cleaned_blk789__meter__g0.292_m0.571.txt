tatropanibandhanavakratāvicchittyantarādhirūḍhāyāḥ ramaṇīyatā tadeva tattvaṃ paraṃ pratibhāsate ।

kasmānnayanti"pratibhāvaśāt"svaśakterāyattatvāt ।
