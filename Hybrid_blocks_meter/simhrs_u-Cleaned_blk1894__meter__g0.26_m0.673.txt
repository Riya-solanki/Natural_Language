atha prakaraṇam-
yatretivṛttam utpādyaṃ dhīra-śāntaś ca nāyakaḥ ।
rasaḥ pradhānaṃ śṛṅgāraḥ śeṣaṃ nāṭakavad bhavet ।। 
tad dhi prakaraṇaṃ śuddhaṃ dhūrtaṃ miśraṃ ca tat tridhā ।
kula-strī-nāyakaṃ śuddhaṃ mālatī-mādhavādikam ।। 
gaṇikā-nāyikaṃ dhūrtaṃ kāmadattāhvayādikam ।
kitava-dhyputakārādi-vyāpāraṃ tv atra kalpayet ।। 
miśraṃ tat kulajā-veśye kalpite yatra nāyike ।
dhūrta-śuddha-kramopetaṃ tan mṛcchakaṭikādikam ।।
