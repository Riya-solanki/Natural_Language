jagattritayavaicitryacitrakarmavidhāyinam ।
śivaṃ śaktiparispandamātropakaraṇaṃ numaḥ ।।
yathātattvaṃ vivecyante bhāvāstrailokyavartinaḥ ।
yadi tannādbhutaṃ nāma daivaraktā hi kiṃśukāḥ ।।
svamanīṣikayaivātha tattvaṃ teṣāṃ yathāruci ।
sthāpyate prauḍhimātraṃ tatparamārtho na tādṛśaḥ ।।
ityasattarkasandarbhe svatantre apyakṛtādaraḥ ।
sāhityārthasudhāsindhoḥ sāramunmīlayāmyaham ।।
yena dvitayamapyetattattvanirmitilakṣaṇam ।
tadvidāmadbhutāmodacamatkāraṃ vidhāsyati ।।
