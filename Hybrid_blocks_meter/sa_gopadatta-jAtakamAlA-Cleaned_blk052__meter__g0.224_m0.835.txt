lajjāmātravibhūṣaṇāṃ savinayāṃ cārūpacārāṃ priyāṃ
dṛṣṭvā prītivijṛmbhamāṇanayano viśrambhaniryantraṇām ।
krodhaprajvalitākṣikośaviṣamabhrūbhaṅgaraudrānanān
ālokyāntakakiṃkarān abhimukhān saṃmoham āyāsyase ।।
