bhūbhyāṃ sadā brāhmaṇabhūṣitāyāṃ kṣatrānvitāyāṃ dṛḍhavāpitāyām ।
śuśruṣaṇāsaktasamudbhavāyā mṛddhiṃ prayāntīha narādhipendrāḥ ।।
tasmād dvijāgryāḥ śrutiśāstrayuktā narādhipāṃste kratubhirdvijendrā yajñāgnidhūmena nṛpasya śāntiḥ ।।
tapo adhyayanasaṃpannā yājanādhyāpane ratāḥ ।
santu viprā bale pūjyāstvatto anujñāmavāpya hi । ।
