ramye kuṅkumaśākhinām aviralacchāyākuthāśītale
mūle komalanīlaśādvalavati pravyaktapuṣpotkare ।
vaṃśais tālaravaiḥ sagītamadhuraiḥ pracchedasaṃpādibhiḥ
saṃgītāhitacetasaḥ pramuditā gāyanty amī kinnarāḥ ।।
