jñātuḥ khalu jñānasādhanānyupapadyante
acakṣuṣā paśyati' aghrāṇena jighrati, asparśanena
spṛśati' evaṃ mantuḥ sarvaviṣayasya matisādhanam antaḥkaraṇabhūtaṃ
sarvaviṣayaṃ vidyate yenāyaṃ manyate iti,
evaṃ sati jñātaryātmasaṃjñā na mṛṣyate manaḥsaṃjñābhyanujñāyate
manasi ca manaḥsaṃjñā na mṛṣyate matisādhanasaṃjñā
mṛṣyate, tadidaṃ saṃjñābhedamātraṃ nārthe
vivāda iti।
pratyākhyāne vā sarvendriyavilopaprasaṅgaḥ ।
atha mantuḥ sarvaviṣayasya matisādhanaṃ
sarvaviṣayaṃ pratyākhyāyate nāstīte ? evaṃ
rūpādigrahaṇasādhanānyapi na santīti sarvendriyavilopaḥ
prasajyate iti ।।
