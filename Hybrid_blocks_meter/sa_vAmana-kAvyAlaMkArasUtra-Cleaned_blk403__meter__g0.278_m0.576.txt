vyatirekādviśeṣokterbhedaṃ darśayitumāha 
ekaguṇahānikalpanāyāṃ sāmyadārḍhyaṃ viśeṣoktiḥ ।।
ekasya guṇasya hāneḥ kalpanāyāṃ śeṣairguṇaissāmyaṃ yattasya dārḍhyaṃ viśeṣoktiḥ । rūpakaṃ cedaṃ prāyeṇeti । yathā
