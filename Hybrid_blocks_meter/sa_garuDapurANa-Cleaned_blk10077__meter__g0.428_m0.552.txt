śrīgaṇeśāyanamaḥ । śrīlakṣmīnṛsiṃhāya namaḥ । śrīdattātreyāya namaḥ । śrīvedavyāsāya namaḥ । 
śrīhayagrīvāya namaḥ ।
atha gāruḍe brahmakāṇḍastṛtīya ārabhyate ।
oṃ mallānāmaśanirnṛṇāṃ naravaraḥ strīṇāṃ smaro mūrtimān gopānāṃ svajano asatāṃ kṣitibhṛtāṃ śāstā svapitroḥ śiśuḥ ।
mṛtyurbhojapatervidhātṛvihita stattvaṃ paraṃ yogināṃ vṛṣṇīnāṃ ca patiḥ sadaiva śuśubhe raṅge acyutaḥ sāgrajaḥ ।।
