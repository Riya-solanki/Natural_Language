nabhasvatā lāsitakalpavallī pravālabālavyajanena yasya ।
uraḥ sthale akīryata dakṣiṇena sarvāspadaṃ saurabhamaṅgarāgaḥ ।।
āyojya mālāmṛtubhiḥ prayatna- saṃpāditāṃmaṃsataṭe asya cakre ।
karāravindaṃ makarandabindu- syandi śriyā vibhramakarṇapūraḥ ।।
