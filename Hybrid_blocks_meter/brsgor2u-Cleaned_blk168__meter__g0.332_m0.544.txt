itaścāsaṃgato anaikāntavāda ityāha- svargeti ।
kiñcānādisiddhor'hanmuniḥ, anye tu hetvanuṣṭhānānmucyante, ananuṣṭhānādbadhyanta ityārhatatantrāvadhṛtasvabhāvānāṃ trividhajīvānāṃ traividhyaniyamo api na syādityāha- anādīti ।
prapañcitaṃ sūtrārthaṃ nigamayati- evamiti ।
eteneti ।
sattvāsattvayorekatra nirāsenetyarthaḥ ।
paramāṇusaṃghātāḥ pṛthivyādaya iti ।
digambarasiddhāntaḥ kimiti sūtrakṛtopekṣitaḥ, tatrāha- yattviti ।।
