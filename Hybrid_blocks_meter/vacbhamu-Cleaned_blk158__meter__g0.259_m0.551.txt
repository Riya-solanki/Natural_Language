tadevamabhimataṃ samāsaṃ vyavasthāpya jijñāsāpadārthamāha- jñātum iti ।
syādetat ।
na jñānamicchāviṣayaḥ ।
sukhaduḥkhāvāptiparihārau vā tadupāyo vā taddvāreṇecchāgocaraḥ ।
na caivaṃ brahmajñānam ।
na khalvetadanukūlamiti vā pratikūlanivṛttiriti vānubhūyate ।
nāpi tayorupāyaḥ; tasminsatyapi sukhabhedasyādarśanāt, anuvartamānasya ca duḥkhasyānivṛtteḥ ।
tasmānna sūtrakāravacanamātrādiṣikarmatā jñānasyetyata āha- avagatiparyantam iti ।
na kevalaṃ jñānamiṣyate kiṃ tvavagatiṃ sākṣātkāraṃ kurvadavagatiparyantaṃ sanvācyāyā icchāyāḥ karma ।
kasmāt ? phalaviṣayatvādicchāyāḥ tadupāyaṃ phalaparyantaṃ gocarayatīccheti śeṣaḥ ।
