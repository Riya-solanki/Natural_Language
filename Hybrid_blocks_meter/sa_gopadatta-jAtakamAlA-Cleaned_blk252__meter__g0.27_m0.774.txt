krodhāraktavilocanaṃ giriśilāśyāmāyatoraḥsthalaṃ
vallīkuṇḍalakāvabaddhakapilavyādhūtakeśāśivam ।
dṛṣṭvā mṛtyum ivāpatantam atha taṃ tadgocarāḥ prāṇino
yātāḥ kvāpi vilaṅghya kaṇṭakalatānaddhān prapātān api ।।
