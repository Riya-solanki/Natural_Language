nacānavagataparvatastasyāgnimattāmavadhārayitumalam ।
yadi cedānīntanaṃ parvatasyāgnimattvaṃ prāgevāvagataṃ syāt tato agnyarthī vinaiva dhūmadarśanenāgnaye parvatamārohet ।
nahyagnyarthino avagategnau dhūmadarśanenārthaḥ kaścit ।।
