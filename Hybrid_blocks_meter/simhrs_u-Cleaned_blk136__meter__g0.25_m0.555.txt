dhīrādhīrādi-bhedena madhyā-prauḍhe tridhā tridhā ।
jyeṣṭhā-kaniṣṭḥā-bhedena tāḥ pratyekaṃ dvidhā dvidhā । 
mugdhā tv eka-vidhā caivaṃ sā trayodaśadhoditā ।। tha parakīyā-
anyāpi dvividhā kanyā paroḍhā ceti bhedataḥ ।। 
tatra kanyā tv anūḍhā syāt sa-lajjā pitṛ-pālitā ।
sakhī-keliṣu visrabdhā prāyo mugdhā-guṇānvitā ।।
