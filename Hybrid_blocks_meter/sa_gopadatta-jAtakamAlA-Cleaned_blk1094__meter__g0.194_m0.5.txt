mātā bālye pratisaravatīṃ veṇikāṃ yatra cakre
yasmin kānto maṇimakarikāṃ yauvanāgre babandha ।
vipraiḥ siktaṃ sukusumalavair yac ca śāntyambuleśaiḥ
kaṣṭaṃ dhṛṣṭaḥ spṛśati balibhuk tac chiro adyāṅganāyāḥ ।।
