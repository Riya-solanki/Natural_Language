nirvāhaṇavidhānamāha chāgetyādi ।।
evaṃvidhāṃ mūṣāṃ kṛtvā dhātunirvāhaṇaṃ kuryāt iti śeṣaḥ ।।
kiṃbhūtāṃ chāgāsthibhasmanirmitamūṣāmiti chāgo bastastasyāsthīni tadbhasmanā nirmitā kṛtā yā mūṣā tām ।।
punaḥ kiṃbhūtāṃ mallakākārāṃ gostanasadṛśīm ।।
punaḥ kiṃviśiṣṭāṃ ghanarandhrāṃ nibiḍachidrām ।।
punaḥ ṭaṅkaṇaviṣaguñjākṛtalepāṃ ṭaṅkaṇaṃ saubhāgyaṃ viṣaṃ saktukaṃ guñjā raktikā tābhiḥ kṛto lepo yasyāṃ sā tām ।।
evaṃbhūtā mūṣā dalayoge patramelane kāryetyarthaḥ ।।
