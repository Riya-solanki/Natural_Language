atha bhayam-
bhayaṃ tu mantunā ghora-darśana-śravaṇādibhiḥ ।
cittasyātīva cāñcalyaṃ tat prāyo nīca-madhyayoḥ ।। 
uttamasya tu jāyeta kāraṇair atilaukikaiḥ ।
bhaye tu ceṣṭā vaivarṇyaṃ stabdhatvaṃ gātra-kampanam ।। 
palāyanaṃ parāvṛtya vīkṣaṇaṃ svātma-gopanam ।
āsya-śoṣaṇam utkrośa-śaraṇānveṣaṇādayaḥ ।।
