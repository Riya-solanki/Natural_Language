ślokatrayam api spaṣṭam ।। 
kevalaṃ dvau dvau ca vaṅganāgajau ।। 
iti dvau vaṅgajau doṣau dvau ca nāgajau ।। 
kapāli kālikā ceti caturthapañcamau ।। 
vaṅgajau doṣau śyāmakapālikā ceti ṣaṣṭasaptamau nāgajau eteṣāṃ caturṇāṃ lakṣaṇamāha ।। 
dugdhe kvathite sati yādṛśī tarā upari bhavati ।। 
rase'pi tādṛśī vaṅgajā ।। 
nāgajā ca kapālikā upari bhavati ।। 
śyāmā ṣaṣṭo doṣaḥ pañcavarṇo bhavati kālikā ca pañcamo doṣaḥ kṛṣṇavarṇa iti ।। 
atha rasasya pañca doṣān āha ।। 
maladoṣo bhavedeko dvitīyo vahnisambhavaḥ ।
viṣadoṣastṛtīyastu caturtho darpasaṃjñakaḥ ।।
unmattaḥ pañcamo doṣo doṣāḥ pañca sadā rase ।
