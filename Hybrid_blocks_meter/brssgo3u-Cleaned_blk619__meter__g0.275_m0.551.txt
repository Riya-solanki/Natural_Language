naivaṃ prakaraṇasāmarthyātkriyāśeṣatvamadhyavasāya svātantrayapakṣo bādhitavyaḥ ।
śrutyāderbalīyastvāt ।
balīyāṃsi hi prakaraṇācchrutiliṅgavākyānīti sthitaṃ śrutiliṅgasūtre ।
tāni ceha svātantryapakṣaṃ sādhayanti dṛśyante ।
katham ।
śrutistāvat ate haite vidyācita eva' iti ।
tathā liṅm asarvadā sarvāṇi bhūtāni cinvantyapi svapate' iti ।
tathā vākyamapi avidyayā haivaita evaṃvidāśritābhavanti' iti ।
avidyācita eva' iti hi sāvadhāraṇeyaṃ śrutiḥ kriyānupraveśe amīṣāmabhyupagamyamāne pīḍitā syāt ।
