godantī haritālāyās tāvat patrāṇi dāpaya ।
yāvanna syurdvidhā tāni dolāyantre kṣipettataḥ ।।
aśmacūrṇasya yā cāchibhṛtā sthālī tayā dṛḍhā ।
dolāyantre tathā kāryā vastraṃ bundhe lagenna hi ।।
tajjñena svedanīyāni yāmayugmaṃ haṭhāgninā ।
svedanasvedanasyānte jalena kṣālayettathā ।।
niḥsaranti yathā tebhyo nīlapītādikṛṣṇikāḥ ।
sadaivātapaśuṣkāṇi dolāyaṃtre parikṣipet ।।
yaṃtre yaṃtre punastāni svedyāni praharadvayam ।
luṇayuktyā tu nālena dvivelaṃ svedayettataḥ ।।
tato dugdhe gavādīnāṃ svedayettatkrameṇa ca ।
kuṣmāṇḍaṃ khaṇḍaśaḥ kṛtvā svedayettadrasena ca ।।
vartanīyāni sāhāyāṃ vaḍavāpyājvalānvitaḥ ।
susūkṣmāścandanākārāḥ svedayettadrasena ca ।।
śodhanaiḥ pañcabhiḥ śuddhāḥ godaṃtī nirviṣībhavet ।
hṛdutkledamaśuddhyā sā ghūrmāraṃ ca karoti ca ।।
tāpaṃ ca nāḍisaṃkocam antastāpaṃ karoti ca ।
ataḥ prāgeva saṃśuddhā haritālāmṛtopamā ।।
