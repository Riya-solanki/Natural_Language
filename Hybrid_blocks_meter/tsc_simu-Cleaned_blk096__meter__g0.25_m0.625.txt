yatra yatra mano dehī dhārayet sakalaṃ dhiyā ।
snehād dveṣād guṇādvāpi yāti tattatsarūpatām ।। iti smṛteḥ ।
so ayaṃ pratinivṛttākṣo gurudarpaṇabodhitaḥ ।
svato anyāṃ vikriyāṃ mauḍhyādasthitāmañjasaikṣata ।। 
athāsau prakṛtirnāhamiyaṃ hi kaluṣātmikā ।
śuddhabuddhasvabhāvo ahamiti tyajati tāṃ vidan ।।
evaṃ dehendriyādibhyaḥ śuddhatvenātmani smṛte ।
nikhilā savikāreyaṃ tyak taprāyā hi carmavat ।।
