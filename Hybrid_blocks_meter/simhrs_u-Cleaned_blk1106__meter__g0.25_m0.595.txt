kecit samāna-balayo rasayoḥ saṅkaraṃ viduḥ । 
na parīkṣākṣamam idaṃ mataṃ prekṣāvatāṃ bhavet ।।
tuṣyatve pūrva āsvādaḥ katarasyety aniścayāt । 
spardhāparatvād ubhayor anāsvāda-prasaṅgataḥ ।।
tayor anyatarasyaiva prāyeṇāsvādanād api । 
yugapad rasanīyatvaṃ nobhayor upapadyate ।
eṣām aṅgāṅgi-bhāvena saṅkaro mama saṃmataḥ ।।
