badhvā caikavarāṃ samyag[mudrāṃ] samayasaṃjñitāṃ ।।
tayā gṛhya tu vai padmaṃ dadyād vaśyakaro bhavet ।।
badhvā cakatamāṃ mudrāṃ samādhivihitāṃ śubhāṃ ।
jñānapadmaṃ dadedyasya so asya śīghaṃ vaśībhavet ।।
