gandhakāmalasārākhyo haritālo manaḥśilā ।
turyaḥ suvarṇamākṣīkaḥ kartavyāḥ samatulyakāḥ ।।
catvāro vāriṇā piṣṭvā kāryāste rābasadṛśāḥ ।
puṣpālyā ghanapuṣpāṇi nisāhāyāṃ pravartayet ।।
piṇḍaṃ piṣṭasya kṛtvātha tanmadhye jātyahīrakān ।
kṣiptvātha golakaṃ kṛtvā vajramūṣāntare kṣipet ।।
dhmātāṃ tāmagnivarṇābhāṃ rābāmadhye kṣipenmuhuḥ ।
evamitthaṃvidhiḥ kāryo vārānekacaturdaśa ।।
anāyāsena vajrāṇi bhasmāni syur na saṃśayaḥ ।
cūrṇaṃ vidhāya teṣāṃ ca prakṣipetkumpake sudhīḥ ।।
