alpasyāpi śubhasya vistaramimaṃ dṛṣṭvā vipākaśriyaḥ
syātko nāma na dānaśīlavidhinā puṇyakriyātatparaḥ ।
naiva draṣṭumapi kṣamaḥ sa puruṣaḥ paryāptavitto api san
yaḥ kārpaṇyatamisrayāvṛtamatirnāpnoti dānairyaśaḥ ।।
