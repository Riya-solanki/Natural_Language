atrocyate bhāva-tattva-vedinā siṃha-bhūbhujā । 
ādyaḥ prāg eva bhāvādi-samutpatteś ca śaiśave ।।
kanyā-vinoda-mātratvād anubhāveṣu neṣyate । 
prema-visrambha-mātratvān nānyasyāpy anubhāvatā ।
ato viṃśatir ity eṣā saṅkhyā saṅkhyāvatāṃ matā ।।
