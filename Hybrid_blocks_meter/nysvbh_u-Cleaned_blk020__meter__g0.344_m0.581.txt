sādhanīyasyārthasya yāvati śabdasamūhe siddhiḥ parisamāpyate, tasya pañcāvayavāḥ pratijñādayaḥ samūhamapekṣyāvayavā ucyante।
teṣu pramāṇasamavāyaḥ।
āgamaḥ pratijñā, heturanumānam, udāharaṇaṃ pratyakṣame upamānamupanayaḥ, sarveṣāmekārthasamavāye sāmarthyapradarśanaṃ nigamanamiti।
tadāśrayā ca tatvavyavasthā।
te caite avayavāḥ
