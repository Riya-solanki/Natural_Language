yogābhyāsajanito dharmo janmāntare ḥpyanuvartate,
pracayakāṣṭhāgate tattvajñānahetau dharme prakṛṣṭāyāṃ
samādhibhāvanāyāṃ tattvajñānamutpadyate iti,
dṛṣṭaśca samādhinārthaviśeṣaprābalyābhibhavaḥ
anāhametadaśrauṣam nāhametadajñāsiṣam
anyatra me mano abhūt' ityāha laukika iti
।।
