aāṇḍajaṃ jīvajamujadbhijam' ityatra tṛtīyenodbhijjaśabdenaiva svedajopasaṃgrahaḥ kṛtaḥ pratyetavyaḥ ।
ubhayorapi svedajodbhijjayorbhūmyudakodbhedaprabhavatvasya tulyatvāt ।
sthāvarodbhedāttu vilakṣaṇo jaṅgamodbheda ityanyatra svedajodbhijjayorbhedavāda ityavirodhaḥ ।।
