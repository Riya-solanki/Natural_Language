prayukte hi hetau yaḥ prasaṅgo jāyate sā jātiḥ।
sa ca prasaṅgaḥ sādharmyavaidharmyābhyā pratyavasthānamupālambhaḥ pratiṣedha iti।
udāharaṇasādharmyāt sādhyasādhanaṃ hetuḥ ityasyodāharaṇavaidharmyeṇa pratyavasthānam।
tathā udāharaṇavaidharmyātsādhyasādhanaṃ hetuḥ ityasyodāharaṇasādharmyeṇa pratyavasthānam।
pratyanīkabhāvājjāyamāno artho jātirati।।
