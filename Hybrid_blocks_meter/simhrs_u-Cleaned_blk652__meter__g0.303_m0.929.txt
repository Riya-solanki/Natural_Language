atha avahitthā-
avahitthākāra-guptir jaihmya-prābhava-nītibhiḥ । 
lajjā-sādhvasa-dākṣiṇya-prāgalbhyāpajayādibhiḥ ।।
anyathā-kathanaṃ mithyā-dhairyam anyatra vīkṣaṇam । 
kathā-bhaṅgādayo' py asyām anubhāvā bhavanty amī ।।
