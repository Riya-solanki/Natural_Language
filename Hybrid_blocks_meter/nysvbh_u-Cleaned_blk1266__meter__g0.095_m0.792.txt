tasya ।
apavargasyādhigamāya yamaniyamābhyāmātmasaṃskāraḥ,
yamaḥ
usamānamāśramiṇāṃ dharmasādhanam, niyamastu viśiṣṭamdva ātmasaṃskāraḥ
punaradharmahānaṃ dharmopacayaśca, yogaśāstrāccādhyātmavidhiḥ
pratipattavyaḥ sa punaḥ tapaḥ prāṇāyāmaḥ pratyāhāro
dhyānaṃ dhāraṇeti, indriyaviṣayeṣu prasaṃkhyānābhyāso
rāgadveṣaprahāṇārthaḥ, upāyastu yogācāravidhānamiti
।।
