atha yaduktamanabhyupagamyamāne vidyābheda āmnānabhedānupapattiriti tatparihartavyam ।
atrocyate - nāyaṃ doṣaḥ ।
upadeśāntaravadupapatteḥ ।
yathā tāṇḍināmupaniṣadi ṣaṣṭhe prapāṭhake - asa ātmā tattvamasi śvekateto' iti navakṛtvo apyupadeśe na vidyābhedo bhavatyevamihāpi bhaviṣyati ।
kathaṃ na navakṛtvo apyupadeśe vidyābhedo na bhavati ।
upakramopasaṃhārābhyāmekārthatāvagamāt ।
abhūya eva mā bhagavānvijñāpayatu' iti caikasyaivārthasya punaḥ punaḥ pratipipādayiṣitavyatvenopakṣepāt ।
āśaṅkāntaranirākaraṇena cāsakṛdupadeśopapatteḥ ।
evamihāpi praśnarūpābhedāt ato anyadārtam' iti ca parisamāptyaviśeṣādupakramopasaṃhārau tāvadekārthaviṣayau dṛśyate ।
ayadeva sākṣādaparokṣādbrahma' iti dvitīye praśna evakāraṃ prayuñjānaḥ pūrvapraśnagatamevārthamuttaratrānukṛṣyamāṇaṃ darśayati ।
pūrvasminśca brāhmaṇo kāryakaraṇavyatiriktasyātmanaḥ sadbhāvaḥ kathyate ।
uttarasmiṃstu tasyaivāśanāyādisaṃsāradharmātītatvaṃ kathyate ।
ityekārthatopapattiḥ ।
tasmādekā vidyeti ।।
