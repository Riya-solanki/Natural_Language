kimidamātyantikatvaṃ pūrvotpannānāṃ śarīrendriyaviṣayāṇāṃ vināśaḥ, anutpannānāṃ cātyantiko anutpādaḥ ।
kathamatyantānutpattiḥ, utpādakayordharmādharmayorniḥśeṣayoḥ parikṣāyāt ।
so ayaṃ prapañcasaṃbandho bandhasyadvimokṣaśca mokṣaḥ ।
