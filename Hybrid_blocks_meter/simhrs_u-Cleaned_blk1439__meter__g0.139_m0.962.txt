yathā tatraiva -
baddhaḥ setur lavaṇa-jaladho krodha-vahneḥ samittvaṃ
nītaṃ rakṣaḥ-kulam adhigatāḥ śuddhimantaś ca dārāḥ ।
tenedānīṃ vipina-vasatāveṣa pūrṇa-pratijño
diṣṭyāyodhyāṃ vrajati dayitā-prītaye puṣpakeṇa ।।
