ātmādiṣu sukhādiṣu ca pratyakṣalakṣaṇaṃ vaktavyam, anindriyārthasannikarṣajaṃ hi taditi।
indriyasya vai sato manasa indriyebhyaḥ pṛthagupadeśo dharmaṃbhedāt।
bhautikānīndriyāṇi niyataviṣayāṇi, saguṇānāṃ caiṣāmindriyabhāva iti।
manastvabhautikaṃ
