atha dvitīya-yauvanam-
stanau pīnau tanur madhyaḥ pāṇipādasya raktimā । 
ūrū karikarākārāv aṅgaṃ vyaktāṅga-sandhikam ।
nitambo vipulo nābhir gabhīrā jaghanaṃ ghanam ।। 
vyaktā romāvalī snaigdhyam aṅga-keśaradākṣiṣu ।
dvitīya-yauvane tena kalitā vāma-locanā ।। 
sakhīṣu svāśayajñāsu snigdhā prāyeṇa māninī ।
na prasīdaty anunaye sapatnīṣv abhyasūyinī ।। 
nāparādhān viṣahate praṇayerṣyākaṣāyitā ।
rati-keliṣv anibhṛtā ceṣṭate garvitā rahaḥ ।।
