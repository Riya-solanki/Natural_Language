atvagekamindriyam' ityāha।
kasmāt ?
avyatirekāt ।
na tvacā kiñcidindriyādhiṣṭhānaṃ na prāptam
na cāsatyāṃ tvaci kiñcidviṣayagrahaṇaṃ bhavati
yayā sarvendriyasthānāni vyāptāni yasyāṃ
ca satyāṃ viṣayagrahaṇaṃ bhavati sā tvagekamindriyamiti
।।
