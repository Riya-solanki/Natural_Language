kaniṣṭhā tu mātṛvat sapatnīṃ paśyet
jñāti-dāyam api tasyā aviditaṃ nopayuñjīta
ātma-vṛttāntāṃs tad-adhiṣṭhitān kuryāt
anujñātā patim adhiśayīta
na vā tasyā vacanam anyasyāḥ kathayet
tad-apatyāni svebhyo adhikāni paśyet rahasi patim adhikam upacaret
ātmanaś ca sapatnī-vikāra-jaṃ duḥkhaṃ nācakṣīta
patyuṣ ca saviśeṣakaṃ gūḍhaṃ mānaṃ lipset
anena khalu pathya-dānena jīvāmīti brūyāt
tat tu ślāghayā rāgeṇa vā bāhir nācakṣīta
bhinna-rahasyā hi bhartur avajñāṃ labhate
jyeṣṭhā-bhayāc ca nigūḍha-saṃmānārthinī syād iti gonardīyaḥ
durbhāgam anapatyāṃ ca jyeṣṭhām anukampeta nāyakena cānukampayet
prasahya tv enām ekacāriṇī-vṛttam anutiṣṭhet
