syādetat ।
prāṇādyupāsanā api vedānteṣu bahulamupalabhyante ।
tatkathaṃ sarveṣāṃ vedāntānāmātmaikatvapratipādanamartha ityata āha- yathā cāyamarthaḥ ityādi ।
śarīrameva śarīrakam; tatra nivāsī śārīrako jīvātmā ।
tasya tvaṃpadābhidheyasya tatpadābhidheyaparamātmarūpatayā yā mīmāṃsā sā tathoktā ।
