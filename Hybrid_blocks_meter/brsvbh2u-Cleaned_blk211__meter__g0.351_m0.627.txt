evaṃ cātmākārtsnyam ।
evaṃ ceti cena samuccayaṃ dyotayati ।
śarīraparimāṇatve hyātmano akṛtsnatvaṃ paricchinnatvam ।
tathā cānityatvam ।
ye hi paricchinnāste sarve anityā yathā ghaṭādayastathā cātmeti ।
tadetadāhayathaikasmindharmiṇīti ।
idaṃ cāparamakṛtsnatvena sūtritamityāhaśarīrāṇāṃ cānavasthitaparimāṇatvāditi ।
manuṣyakāyaparimāṇo hi jīvo na hastikāyaṃ kṛtsnaṃ vyāptumarhatyalpatvādityātmanaḥ kṛtsnaśarīrāvyāpitvādakārtsnyam, tathā ca na śarīraparimāṇatvamiti ।
tathā hastiśīraraṃ parityajya yadā puttikāśarīro bhavati tadā na tatra kṛtsnaḥ puttikāśarīre saṃmīyetetyakārtsnyamātmanaḥ ।
sugamamanyat ।
codayatisyādetat ।
anantāvayava iti ।
yathā hi pradīpo ghaṭamahāharmyodaravartī saṃkocavikāśavānevaṃ jīvo api puttikāhastidehayorityarthaḥ ।
tadetadvikalpya dūṣayatiteṣāṃ
