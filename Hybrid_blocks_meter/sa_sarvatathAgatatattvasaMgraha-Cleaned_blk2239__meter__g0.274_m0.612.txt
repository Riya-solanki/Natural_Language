athāryāvalokiteśvaro bodhisatvo mahāsatva idaṃ caturmudrāmaṇḍalamabhāṣat । [athātaḥ saṃpravakṣyāmi] caturmudrāgramaṇḍalaṃ ।
vajradhātupratīkāśaṃ mahāmaṇḍalasannibhaṃ ।।
mudrāmaṇḍalamadhye tu buddhabimbaṃ niveśayet ।
tasya pārśveṣu sarveṣu [vajrapadmādiṃ vai likhe]t ।।
