[] madhukaṇṭhaḥ sa-smitam uvāca-na kim api, kintu tatra stuti-samaye tāvat ।
govindaḥ smitam atanot stuvānam enam
dṛṣṭvā yat kim api dadarśa tatra citram ।
ekasmin vadati caturmukhe hi tasmiṃś
catvāro dadhati rutīr iti bhramaḥ syāt ।। [praharṣiṇī]
