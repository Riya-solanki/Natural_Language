yādṛśaṃ svadhiyā jñātaṃ tādṛśaṃ likhitaṃ mayā ।
yadi kvacid vyalīkaṃ syāttadā śodhyaṃ vicakṣaṇaiḥ ।।
khyātastathā yādavavaṃśaratnabhūjāladevābhidharāulo abhūt ।
tadātmajanmā mahipābhidhānas tasyātmajo bhādiganāmadheyaḥ ।।
tadātmajaś campanāmadheyo rasajñageyojjvalakāntikīrtiḥ ।
paropakāraikarasaḥ kalāvān manāgajākau kila yasya bandhū ।।
