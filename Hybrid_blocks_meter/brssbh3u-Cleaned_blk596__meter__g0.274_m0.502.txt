yadapyuktam - ataṃ vidyākarmaṇī samanvārabhete' ityetatsamānvārambhavacanamasvatantrye vidyāyā liṅgamiti tatpratyucyate ।
vibhāgo atra draṣṭavyo vidyānyaṃ puruṣamanvārabhate karmānyamiti ।
śatavat ।
yathā śatamābhyāṃ dīyatāmityukte vibhajya dīyate pañcāśadekasmai pañcāśadaparasmai tadvat ।
nacedaṃ samanvāramabhavacanaṃ mumukṣuviṣayam aiti nu kāmayamānaḥ' iti saṃsāriviṣayatvopasaṃhārāt ।
aathākāmayamānaḥ' iti ca mumukṣoḥ pṛthagupakramāt ।
tatra saṃsāriviṣaye vidyā vihitā pratiṣiddhā ca parigṛhyate viśeṣābhāvāt ।
karamāpi vihitaṃ pratiṣiddhaṃ ca yathāprāptānuvāditvāt ।
evaṃ satyavibhāgenāpīdaṃ samanvārambhavacanamavakalpate ।।
