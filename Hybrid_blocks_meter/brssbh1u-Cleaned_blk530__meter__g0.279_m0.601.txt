aaṅguṣṭamātraḥ puruṣaḥ madhya ātmani tiṣṭhati' iti śrūyate ।
tathā aṅguṣṭamātraḥ puruṣo jyotirivādhūmakaḥ ।
īśāno bhūtabhavyasya sa evādya sa u śva etadvai tat' iti ca ।
tatra yo ayamaṅguṣṭhamātraḥ puruṣaḥ śrūyate sa kiṃ vijñānātmā kiṃvā paramātmeti saṃśayaḥ ।
tatra parimāṇopadeśāttāvadvijñānātmeti prāptam ।
nahyanantāyāmavistārasya paramātmano aṅguṣṭhaparimāṇamupapadyate ।
vijñānātmananastūpādhimattvātsaṃbhavati kayācitkalpanayāṅguṣṭhamātratvam ।
smṛteśca- aatha satyavataḥ kāyātpāśabaddhaṃ vaśaṃ gatam ।
aṅguṣṭhamātraṃ puruṣaṃ niścakarṣaṃ yamo balāt' ।।
