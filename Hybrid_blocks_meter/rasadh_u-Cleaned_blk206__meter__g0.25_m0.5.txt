ketakīnāṃ stanāneva nisāhāyāṃ jalaṃ vinā ।
vartayitvā raso grāhyo vastrapūto hi nirmalaḥ ।।
rasenānena sūkṣmā ca vartanīyā manaḥśilā ।
tayā saṃveṣṭya vajrāṇi vajramūṣāntare kṣipet ।।
vidhmāpyārkadugdhena dhmātāṃ tām agninibhāṃ kṛtām ।
evamicchanmuhuḥ kāryaḥ saptavelam ayaṃ vidhiḥ ।।
sadvajrāṇi mriyante ca sukhasādhyāni niścitam ।
tacca cūrṇaṃ kumpe kṣepyaṃ mṛtahīrakasambhavam ।।
