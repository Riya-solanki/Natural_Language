yāvat praviśāmi 
{praviśya} aye āśramaviruddhaḥ khalv eṣa janaḥ 
{anyato vilokya} athavā tapasvijano apy atra 
nirdoṣam upasarpaṇam 
aye strījanaḥ 
{kāñcukīyaḥ} - svairaṃ svairaṃ praviśatu bhavān 
sarvajanasādhāraṇam āśramapadaṃ nāma 
{vāsavadattā} - haṃ 
{padmāvatī} - ammo parapurusadaṃsaṇaṃ pariharadi ayyā 
bhodu suparivāḷaṇīo khu maṇṇāso 
[ammo parapuruṣadarśanaṃ pariharaty āryā । bhavatu suparipālanīyaḥ khalu mannyāsaḥ । ]
