trividhā cāsya śāstrasya pravṛttirūddeśo lakṣaṇaṃ parīkṣā ceti।
tatra nāmadheyena padārthamātrasyābhidhānamuddeśaḥ।
tatroddiṣṭasyātattvavyavacchedako dharmo lakṣaṇam।
lakṣitasya yathālakṣaṇamupapadyate na veti pramāṇairavadhāraṇaṃ parīkṣā।
tatroddiṣṭasya pravibhaktasya lakṣaṇamucyate, yathā pramāṇānāṃ prameyasya ca।
uddiṣṭasya lakṣitasya ca vibhāgavacanam, yathā chalasya
vacanavighāto arthavikalpopapattyā chalam []
tat trividham []
iti।।
athoddiṣṭasya vibhāgavacanam
