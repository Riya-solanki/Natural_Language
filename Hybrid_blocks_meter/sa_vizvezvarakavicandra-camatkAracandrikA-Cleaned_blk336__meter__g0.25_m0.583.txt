dvibhadramapi cecchanti bhāṣā naikavidhā yadi ।
khyātaṃ hi raktaṃ kalyāṇaṃ yogassaṃskṛtadeśyayoḥ ।।
evaṃ bhāṣādvayīyoge yogyaṃ nāma prakalpayet ।।
tadeva nanābirudairaṅkitaṃ birudāvaliḥ ।
tattatsamayakrartavyasūcanādiphalā tu sā ।।
