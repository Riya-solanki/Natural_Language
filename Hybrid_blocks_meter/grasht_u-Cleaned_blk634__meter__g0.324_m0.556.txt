taccāha mākṣīkatīkṣṇatāramiti ।।
mākṣīkaṃ tāpyaṃ tīkṣṇaṃ sāraṃ tāraṃ rūpyam ।।
punastārāruṇamākṣikaṃ evamuktavidhānena idamapi tāraṃ rūpyaṃ aruṇaṃ tāmraṃ mākṣīkaṃ tāpyam ।।
punaḥ kānte cumbake'bhivyāpake adhikaraṇe śulbaṃ tāmraṃ tāpyaṃ svarṇamākṣikaṃ yat prayuktam ।।
apīti niścayena ।।
śulbābhratāpyakāñcanaṃ vā śulbaṃ tāmraṃ abhraṃ gaganaṃ tāpyaṃ svarṇamākṣikaṃ kāñcanaṃ hema etaccatuṣṭayamapi mahābījaṃ jñeyam ।।
mahābījasaṃbandhaḥ pratyekamiti vyaktiḥ ।।
