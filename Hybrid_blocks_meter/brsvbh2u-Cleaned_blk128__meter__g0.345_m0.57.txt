sāmānyamupādānaṃ, tacca nityam ।
naca tatsaṃyogasacivamekatvāt, saṃyogasya dviṣṭhatvenaikasminnabhāvāt ।
tasmātsāmānyasya paramārthasato anirvācyā viśeṣāvasthāstadadhiṣṭhānā bhujaṅgādaya iva rajjvādyupādānāṃ upajanāpāyadharmāṇa iti sāṃpratam ।
prakṛtamupasaṃharati-tasmāditi ।।
