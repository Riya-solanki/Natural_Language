karmaṇo anavasthāyino grahaṇāditi
kṣiptasyeṣorāpatanāt kriyāsaṃtāno
gṛhyate, gatyarthaniyamācca buddhīnāṃ
kriyāsaṃtānavad buddhisaṃtānopapattiriti,
avasthitagrahaṇe ca vyavadhīyamānasya pratyakṣanivṛtteḥ ।
avasthite ca kumbhe gṛhyamāṇe saṃtānenaiva
buddhirvartate prāg vyavadhānāt tena
vyavahite pratyakṣaṃ jñānaṃ nivartate, kālāntarāvasthāne
tu buddherdṛśyavyavadhānepi
pratyakṣamavatiṣṭheteti।
smṛtiścāliṅgaṃ buddhyavasthāne saṃskārasya buddhijasya smṛtihetutvāt।
yaśca manyeta
avatiṣṭhate buddhiḥ dṛṣṭā hi
buddhiviṣaye smṛtiḥ sā ca buddhāvanityāyāṃ
kāraṇābhāvānna syāditi, tadidamaliṅgam,
kasmāt ? buddhijo hi saṃskāro
guṇāntaraṃ smṛtiheturna buddhiriti ।।
hetvabhāvādayuktamiticet ? buddhyavasthānāt
pratyakṣatve smṛtyabhāvaḥ ।।
yāvadavatiṣṭhate
buddhistāvadasau boddhavyārthaḥ pratyakṣaḥ pratyakṣe
ca smṛtiranupapanneti ।।
