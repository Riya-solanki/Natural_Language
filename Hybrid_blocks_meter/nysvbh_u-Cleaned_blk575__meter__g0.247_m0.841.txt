yathādarśanaṃ vikṛtā varṇā vibhaktyantāḥ padasaṃjñā bhavanti।
vibhaktirdvayī nāmikī ākhyātikī ca abrāhmaṇaḥ'
apacatītyudāharaṇam।
upasarganipātāstarhi
na padasaṃjñāḥ lakṣaṇāntaraṃ vācyamiti,
śiṣyate ca khalu nāmikyā vibhakteravyayāllopastayoḥ
padasaṃjñārthamiti।
padenārthasaṃpratyaya iti
prayojanam ।।
