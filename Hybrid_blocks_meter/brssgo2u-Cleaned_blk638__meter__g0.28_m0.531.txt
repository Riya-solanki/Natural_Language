FN uparodho bādhaḥ ।
sarūpaśabdo jīvavācīti śeṣaḥ ।
jālmaḥ kāmajaḍaḥ ।
sa yathā strīparatantraḥ strīmayo vpadiśyate ।
etebhyo dehātmanā pariṇatebhyo bhūtebhyaḥ sāmyenotthāya janitvā tānyeva līyamānānyanu paścādvinaśyati ।
mohaṃ mohakaraṃ vākyam ।
