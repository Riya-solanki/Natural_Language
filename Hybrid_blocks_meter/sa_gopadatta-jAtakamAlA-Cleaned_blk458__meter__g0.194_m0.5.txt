divyaṃ prāpya sukhaṃ pure ramaṇake saṃcoditaḥ karmaṇā [!]
āyāto asmi niṣevaṇāya paramaṃ saukhyaṃ sadāmattakam ।
saṃprāpto asmi tataḥ svakarmakuśaleneṣṭaṃ puraṃ nandanaṃ
tasmād āgatakasya yūyam adhunā pronmūlitā bhūmayaḥ ।।
