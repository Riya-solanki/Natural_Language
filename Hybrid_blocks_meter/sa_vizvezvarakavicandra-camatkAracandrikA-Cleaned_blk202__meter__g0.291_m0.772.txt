atra tṛtīyākṣaravikalatvācchandovikalamidam ।
anyatrasaṃskaṭatādasya guṇatālpāpi gaṇyate ।।
yathā
sirisiṃgabhūmi vihuṇo jasa bharagaṃgappavāha majjhammi ।
pariṇhauṇa samaddhaṃ tellokkaṃ hoi parisuddham ।।
