aho para-sparśa-subhīru yad vapur
yat kṛṣṇasāra-praṇayaika-śarma ca ।
tāsāṃ mṛgīṇām iva tat-paraṃ prati
praditsavo vyādha-manāṃsi bibhrati ।।
