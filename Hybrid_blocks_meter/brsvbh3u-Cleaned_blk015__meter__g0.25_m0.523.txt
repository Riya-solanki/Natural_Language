caraṇāditi cennopalakṣaṇārtheti kārṣṇājiniḥ ।
anena niranuśayā evāvarohantīti pūrvapakṣabījaṃ nigūḍhamuddhāṭya nirasyati ।
yadyapi aakrodhaḥ sarvabhūteṣu karmaṇā manasā girā ।
anugrahaśca jñānaṃ ca śīlametādvidurbudhāḥ ।।
aiti smṛteḥ śīlamācāro anuśayādbhinnastathāpyānuśayāṅgatayānuśayopalakṣaṇatvaṃ kārṣṇājinirācāryo mene ।
tathāca ramaṇīyacaraṇāḥ kapūyacaraṇā ityanenānuśayopalakṣaṇātsiddhaṃ sānuśayānāmevāvarohaṇamiti ।।
