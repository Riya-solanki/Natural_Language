ityuccārya samāhūya svān bhṛtyān vākyamabravīt ।
adyātithirayaṃ pūjyaḥ sadaiva svajano mama ।।
asmin samāgate pretāḥ prītirjātā mamātulā ।।
evaṃ hi vadatastasya mṛtpātraṃ sudṛḍhaṃ navam ।
dadhyodanena saṃpūrṇamājagāma yathepsitam ।।
