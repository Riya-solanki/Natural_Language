itaścāsaṃsārisvarūpapratipādanaparamevaitadvākyamityavagantavyam ।
yadasminvākye patyādayaḥ śabdā asaṃsārisvarūpapratipādanaparāḥ saṃsārisvabhāvapratiṣedhanāśca bhavanti ।
asarvasya vaśī sarvasyeśānaḥ sarvasyādhipatiḥ' ityevañjātīyakā asaṃsārisvabhāvapratipādanaparāḥ ।
asa na sādhunā karmaṇā bhūyānno evāsādhunā kanīyān' ityevañjātīyakāḥ saṃsārisvabhāvapratiṣedhanāḥ ।
tasmādasaṃsārī parameśvara ihokta ityavagamyate ।।
