rasabhāvādibhedena saṃpadyante tathocitam ।
tathāpi prabhuvandyādiharṣaṇārabhaṭīspṛśām ।।
vakṣye ca caturbhadrādīnāṃ keṣāṃcidiha lakṣaṇam ।।
padyānantarabaddhānāṃ gadyānāṃ hi yathāruci ।
avaiṣamyādiyattāyā niyamo bhadramucyate ।।
