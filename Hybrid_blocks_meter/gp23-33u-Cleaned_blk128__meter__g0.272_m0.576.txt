tathā vilāpa-tāpena na tāsāṃ vivyathe hariḥ ।
yathā rādhā preṅkhita-bhrū-dhanur iṅkhi-dṛg-āśugaiḥ ।।
atha vyathita-mānasaḥ prathita-bhāva-kāruṇyataḥ
prahasya muditānanaḥ svaka-rahasyam ullāsayan ।
pratisvam anayat priyā druta-matiḥ sva-pārśvaṃ balī
balād api balānujo valayati sma līlāṃ prati ।।
āmṛśann aṅgulīḥ pāṇiṃ prakoṣṭhaṃ bāhum apy atha ।
tāsāṃ hari-karas tṛptiṃ nāgāndhanam ivādhanaḥ ।।yad api ca rādhā tāsu svam abhijugopāyutādi-saṅkhyāsu ।
tad api ca hariṇā spaṣṭaṃ dṛṣṭā tārāsu candralekheva ।।rādhā yarhi svakara-spṛṣṭā jātā murārāteḥ ।
ativismitaye jajñe tarhi ca sā tatra candralekheva ।।
gopyaḥ kṛṣṇaṃ tatra kṛṣṇaś ca rādhām
āśliṣoccair āvrajan narma-śarma ।
bhāva-śreṇyaḥ sthāyi-bhāvaṃ svabhāvo
yadvat puṇyād vāsanāṃ pūrva-labdhām ।।
[] tatra kāścid bhūrur iva nibhālya vana-mālyaḥ parīhāsataḥ parasparam ūcuḥ-
