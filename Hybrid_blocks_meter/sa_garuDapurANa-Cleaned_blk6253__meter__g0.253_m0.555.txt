।
upasthitapracupitaṃ prathame aghrau masau jabhau ।
gau dvitīye sanajarā gastṛtīye nanau ca saḥ ।
nau najau yaścaturthe syāccheṣa pādāśca pūrvavat ।।
tṛtīrye aghrau viśeṣaśca vṛttaṃ syānnau sanau nasau ।।
ārṣabhaṃ tajarāḥ pāde tṛtīye anyacca pūrvavat ।
pūrvavatprathamaṃ śeṣe tajrāḥ śuddhavirāḍbhavet ।।
