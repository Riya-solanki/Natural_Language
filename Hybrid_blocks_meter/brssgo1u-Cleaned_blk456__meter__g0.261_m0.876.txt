FN agnirdyulokaḥ vivṛtā veda vāk, padbhyāṃ pādau ।
tanumahimno alpaśakteḥ ।
yaśca sūryo dyulokāgneḥ samidha iva bhāsakaḥ ।
hiraṇyagarbhaḥ agre samavartata ।
jātaḥ san bhūtagrāmasyaikaḥ patirbabhūveti śeṣaḥ ।
kasmai prajāpataye ।
vidhema paricarema ।
