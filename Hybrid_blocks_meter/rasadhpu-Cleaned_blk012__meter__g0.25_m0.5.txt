snuhīdugdhena khalve rasaḥ kṣiptvā sapta dināni mṛdyate ।। 
tato bhūmijaḥ kañcuko yāti ।। 
evaṃ sapta dināni arkadugdhena mardite pāṣāṇakañcuko yāti ।। 
parpaṭān brahmavṛkṣasya jalaklinnāṃś ca vartayet ।
tadrasena ca saptāhaṃ piṣṭād yātyambukañcukaḥ ।।
