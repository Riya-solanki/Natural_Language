rāga-kāle viśālayanty eva jaghanaṃ mṛgī saṃviśed uccarate
avahrāsayantīva hastinī nīca-rate
nyāyyo yatra yogas tatra samapṛṣṭham
ābhyāṃ baḍavā vyākhyātā
tatra jaghanena nāyakaṃ pratigṛhṇīyāt
apadravyāṇi ca saviśeṣaṃ nīcarate
[] utphullakaṃ [] vijṛmbhitakam [] indrāṇikaṃ ceti tritayaṃ mṛgyāḥ prāyeṇa
śiro vinipātyordhvaṃ jaghanam [] utphullakam
tatrāpasāraṃ dadyāt
anīce sakthinī tiryag avasajya pratīched iti [] vijṛmbhitakam
pārśvayoḥ samam ūrū vinyasya pārśvayor jānunī nidadhyād ity abhyāsa-yogād [] indrāṇī
tayoccatara-ratasyāpi parigrahaḥ
saṃpuṭena pratigraho nīca-rate
etena nīca-tara-rate api hastinyāḥ
[] saṃpuṭakaṃ [] pīḍitakaṃ [] veṣṭitakaṃ [] bāḍavakam iti
ṛju-prasāritāv ubhāv apy ubhayoḥ caranāv iti [] saṃpuṭaḥ
sa dvi-vidhaḥ ṇ pārśva-saṃpuṭa uttāna-saṃpuṭāś ca tathā karma-yogāt
pārśveṇa tu śayāno dakṣiṇena nārīm adhiśayīteti sārvatrikam etat
saṃpuṭaka-prayukta-yantreṇaiva dṛḍham ūrū pīḍayed iti [] pīḍitakam
ūrū vyatyasyed iti [] veṣṭitakam
baḍaveva niṣṭhuram avagṛhṇīyād iti [] bāḍavakam ābhyāsikam
tad āndhrīṣu prāyeṇa iti saṃveśana-prakārā bābhravīyāḥ
sauvarṇanābhās tu
ubhāv apy ūrū ūrdhvāv iti tad bhugnakam 
caraṇāv ūrdhvaṃ nāyako asyā dhārayed iti jṛmbhitakam
tat-kuñcitāv utpīḍitakam
tad ekasmin prasārite ardha-pīḍitakam
nāyakasyāṃsa eko dvitīyakaḥ prasārita iti punaḥ punar vyatyāsena veṇu-dāritakam
ekaḥ śirasa upari gacched dvitīyaḥ prasārita iti śūlacitakam ābhyāsikam
saṃkucitau svasti-deśe nidadhyād iti kārkaṭakam
ūrdhvāv ūrū vyatyasyed iti pīḍitakam
jaṅghā-vyatyāsena padmāsanavat
pṛṣṭhaṃ pariṣvajamānāyāḥ parāṅmukheṇa parāvṛttakam ābhyāsikam jale ca saṃviṣṭopaviṣṭa-sthitātmakāṃś citrān yogān upalakṣayet tathā sukaratvād iti suvarṇanābhaḥ
vārtaṃ tu tat śiṣṭair apasmṛtatvād iti vātsyāyanaḥ
