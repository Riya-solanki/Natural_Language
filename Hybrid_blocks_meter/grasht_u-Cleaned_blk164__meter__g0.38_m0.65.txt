samukhacāraṇāntarbhūtaṃ vāsanāmukhacāraṇaṃ darśayannāha tailetyādi ।।
rasarājaḥ pārado hāṭakatārādi svarṇarūpyādi dhātudravyaṃ kṛtrimākṛtrimātmakanavasaṃkhyākaṃ pūrvamuktaṃ carati bhakṣati ।।
kena golakamukhena ।।
golakaśca mukhaviśeṣaḥ tena viḍasya golakenetyarthaḥ ।।
kva sati tailādikataptarase sati ।।
tailam ādiḥ yeṣāṃ te tailādikās tailavasāmūtraśukrapuṣpāḥ etaistapto yo rasa uṣṇatvaṃ nīto yo asau pāradastasmin satyevaṃ ghanamabhrakaṃ carati rasendraḥ ।।
punarhemādibhirnavakairgrāsīkṛtaiḥ piṇḍatvameti nibiḍatvaṃ prāpnoti ।।
tailāni yathā ।
kaṅguṇītumbinīghoṣākarañjaśrīphalodbhavam ।
kaṭuvātārisiddhārthasomarājīvibhītajam ।।
atasījaṃ mahākālīnimbajaṃ tilajaṃ tathā ।
apāmārgo devadālī dantītumbaruvigrahāḥ ।।
aṅkolonmattabhallātaphalebhyas tailasambhavaḥ ।
iti ।।
vasā yathā ।
ajoṣṭrakharameṣāṇāṃ mahiṣasya vasā tathā ।
iti ।।
mūtrapuṣpaśukrāṇi yathā ।
mūtrāṇi hastikarabhamahiṣīkharavājinām ।
striyaḥ puṃsas tathā mūtraṃ puṣpaṃ vīryaṃ ca yojayet ।
iti ।।
