arthobhayālaṅkārasaṅkaro yathā
santrāsaudviṣatāṃpratidiśaṃ tvatkīrtirādhāyate tvatkītau paridhāvanaṃ pratipadaṃ gāyanti vairistriyaḥ ।
glānirvairibahadhūlatāsu dharaṇī viśrāmyati tvadbhuje viśrāmo bhuvi siṅgabhūpatimaṇeṃ nidrāṃnti vairistriyaḥ ।।
