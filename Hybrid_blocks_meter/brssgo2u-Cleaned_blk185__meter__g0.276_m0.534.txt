evavamapyanityatvādidoṣaprasaṅgaḥ ।
atha śaktaya eva kāryavaicitryasūcitā avayavā ityabhiprāyaḥ ।
tāstu brahmavādino apyavaśiṣṭāḥ ।
tathāṇuvādino apyaṇuraṇvantareṇa saṃyujyamāno niravayavatvādyadi kārtsyena saṃyujyeta tataḥ prathimānupapatteraṇumātratvaprasaṅgaḥ ।
athaikadeśena saṃyujyeta tathāpi niravayavatvābhyupagamakopa iti svapakṣe api samāna eṣa doṣaḥ ।
samānatvācca nānyanyatarasminneva pakṣa upakṣeptavyo bhavati ।
parihṛtastu brahmavādinā svapakṣe doṣaḥ ।।
