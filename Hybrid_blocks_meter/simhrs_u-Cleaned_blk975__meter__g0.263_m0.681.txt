atha unmādaḥ-
sarvāvasthāsu sarvatra tan-manaskatayā sadā । 
atasmiṃs tad iti bhrāntir unmādo virahodbhavaḥ ।।
tatra ceṣṭās tu vijñeyā dveṣaḥ sveṣṭe' pi vastuni । 
dīrghaṃ muhuś ca niḥśvāso nirnimeṣatayā sthitiḥ ।
nirnimitta-smita-dhyāna-gāna-maunādayo' pi ca ।।
