yathākrameṇa boddhavyamabhimantavyameva ca ।
saṅkalpitavyaṃ śrotavyaṃ spraṣṭavyaṃ draṣṭavyameva ca ।।
rasayitavyaṃ ghrātavyaṃ gantavyamutsraṣṭavyaṃ ca ।
ānandayitavyamityevamadhibhūtaṃ prakīrtitam ।।
adhidaivataṃ ca ।
brahmā rudraścandramāśca ākāśo vāyureva ca ।
sūryo varuṇa indraśca viṣṇumitraṃ tathaiva ca ।।
anenaiva krameṇedaṃ saṃproktamadhidaivatam ।
catvāri yo vedayate yathā ca-
kṣuṣā svarūpāṇyadhidaivataṃ vā ।
vimuktayāthāgatadoṣasaṅgo-
guṇāṃstu bhuṅkte na guṇaiḥ sa yujyate ।।
