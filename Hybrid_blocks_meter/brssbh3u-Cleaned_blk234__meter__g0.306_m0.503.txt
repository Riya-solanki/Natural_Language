yadetannirastasamastaprapañcaṃ brahma nirdharitamasmātparamanyattattvamasti nāstīti śrutivipratipatteḥ saṃśayaḥ ।
kāniciddhi vākyānyāpātenaiva pratibhāsamānāni brahmaṇo api paramanyattattvaṃ pratipādayantīva ।
teṣāṃ hi parihāramabhidhātumayamupakramaḥ kriyate ।
paramato brahmaṇo anyattattvaṃ bhavitumarhati ।
kutaḥ - setuvyapadeśādunmānavyapadeśātsaṃbandhavyapadeśādbhedavyapadeśācceti ।
setuvyapadeśastāvat - aatha ya ātmā sa seturvidhṛtiḥ' ityātmaśabdābhihitasya brahmaṇaḥ setuttavaṃ saṃkīrtayati ।
setuśabdaśca hi loke jalasaṃtānavicchedakare mṛddārvādipracaye prasiddhaḥ ।
iha tu setuśabda ātmani prayukta iti laukikasetorivātmasetoranyasya vastuno astitvaṃ gamayati ।
asetuṃ tīrtvā' iti ca taratiśabdaprayogāt ।
yathā laukikaṃ setuṃ tīrtvā jāṅgalamasetuṃ prāpnotyevamātmānaṃ setuṃ tīrtvānātmānamasetuṃ prāpnotīti gamyate ।
unmānavyapadeśaśca bhavati atadetadbrahma catuṣpādaṣṭāśaphaṃ ṣoḍaśakalami'ti ।
yacca loke unmitametāvadidamiti pariccinnaṃ kārṣāpaṇādi tato anyadvastvastīti prasiddham ।
tathā brahmaṇo apyunmānāttato anyena vastunā bhavitavyamiti gamyate ।
tathā saṃbandhavyapadeśo api bhavati - asatā somya tadā saṃpanno bhavati' iti aśārīra ātmā' aprājñenātmanā saṃpariṣvaktaḥ' iti ca ।
mitānāṃ ca mitena saṃndho dṛṣṭo yathā narāṇāṃ nagareṇa ।
jīvānāṃ ca brahmaṇā saṃbandhaṃ vyapadiśati suṣuptau ।
atastataḥ paramanyadamitamastīti gamyate ।
bhedavyapadeśaścaitamevārthaṃ gamayati ।
