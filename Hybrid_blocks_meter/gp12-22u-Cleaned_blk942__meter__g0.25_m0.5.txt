īdṛśas te vrajādhīśa sutaḥ sūta-mukhotsavaḥ ।
yo gavendratayā gāṃ ca gāṃ ca gāṃ ca sukhākarot ।। [anuṣṭubh]
subhagambhaviṣṇu jātaṃ jagad api kṛṣṇābhiṣekataḥ paścāt ।
andhambhāvukam āsīd bhāvuka-śūnyaṃ kulaṃ tu danujānām ।। [gīti]
