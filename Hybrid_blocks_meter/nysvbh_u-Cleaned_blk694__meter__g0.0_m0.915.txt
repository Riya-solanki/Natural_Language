bhinnaḥ khalvayaṃ dravyadharmo guṇadharmaśca,
mahadanekadravyavacca viṣaktāvayavamāpyaṃ dravyaṃ
pratyakṣato nopalabhyate sparśastu śīto gṛhyate
tasya dravyasyānubandhāt hemantaśiśirau
kalpete, tathāvidhameva ca taijasaṃ dravyamanudbhūtarūpaṃ
saha rūpeṇa nopalabhyate sparśastv asyoṣṇa upalabhnapayate
tasya dravyasyānubandhād grīṣmavasantau kalpete
।।
