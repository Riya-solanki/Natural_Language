yā vā śrīr adhigamyate paravadhavyāsaṅgadagdhātmanā
tāṃ vāñched bhramaropagītakusumāṃ ko vadhyamālām iva ।
yo api svārhanibandhanapraṇayinā saṃprasthito vartmanā
prāg eva vyasanāturasya jagato yo ahaṃ hitāyodyataḥ ।।
