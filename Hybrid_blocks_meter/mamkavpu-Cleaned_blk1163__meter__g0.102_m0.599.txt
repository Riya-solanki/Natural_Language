svayaṃ ca pallavātāmrabhāsvatkaravirājitā । ityabhaṅgaḥ
{prabhātasaṃdhyevāsvāpaphalalubdhehitapradā ।।}
iti sabhaṅgaḥ iti dvāvapi śabdaikasamāśrayāviti dvayorapi śabdaśleṣatvamupapannaṃ
na tvādyasyārthaśleṣatvam । arthaśleṣasya tu sa viṣayaḥ yatra śabdaparivartane api na śleṣatvakhaṇḍanā । yathā
