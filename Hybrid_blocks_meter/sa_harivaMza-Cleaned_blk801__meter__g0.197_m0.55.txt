uvāca cainaṃ bhagavān sarvabhūtānukampakaḥ 
brahmadatta prabhāte tvaṃ kalyāṇaṃ samavāpsyasi 
ity uktvā bhagavān devas tatraivāntaradhīyata ।। 
brahmadatto api rājarṣiḥ sarvasattvarutajñatām । upadiṣya ca bhāryāyai vanāt pratyāgataḥ puram । 
caturṇāṃ tu pitā yo asau brāhmaṇānāṃ mahātmanām 
ślokaṃ so adhītya putrebhyaḥ kṛtakṛtya ivābhavat ।।
