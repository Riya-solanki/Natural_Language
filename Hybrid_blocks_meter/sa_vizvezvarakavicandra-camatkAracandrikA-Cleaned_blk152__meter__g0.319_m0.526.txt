lakṣakavākyaṃ yathā
uccairāśritavatsaloyamamṛṣābhāṣīti yad gīyase śrīsiṅgakṣitināyakaitadadhunā nivyūḍhemetacvayā ।
madbhāgyāni pacelimāni nitarāmevaṃ sapatnījane- ṣvārūḍhāsmi cirāya tāvakakṛpāsaṃbhāvanāyā padam ।।
