kāryamutpatteḥ prāgasadeva syāt, svaviruddhakāraṇātmanā sattvāyogādityapasiddhāntāpattimāśaṅkya mithyātvātkāryasya kālatraye api kāraṇātmanā sattvamaviruddhamiti samādhatte-asaditicedityādinā ।
asatyāditi sattvapratiṣedho nirarthaka ityarthaḥ ।
kāryasatyatvābhāve śrutimāha-sarvaṃ tamiti ।
mithyātvamajānataḥ śaṅkāmanūdya pariharati-nanvityādinā ।
vistareṇa caitaditi ।
mithyātvamityarthaḥ ।।
