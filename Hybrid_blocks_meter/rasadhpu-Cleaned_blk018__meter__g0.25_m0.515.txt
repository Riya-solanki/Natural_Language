mūrchitotthitapāradasya palāni sarvottamatāmracūrṇasya palāni dvātriṃśattame bhāge lavaṇaṃ palāni ityarthaḥ ।। 
sarva sarve ekīkṛtya nimbukarasena sammṛdya mṛkṣaṇopamā pīṭhī kāryā ।। 
ayamatrāmnāyaḥ ।। 
yāvanmātro rasastasya caturthe bhāge tāmracūrṇaṃ dvātriṃśattamabhāge lavaṇam ।। 
nimbukarasaś ca takhā kṣepyo yathā sarvamauṣadhaṃ majjati ।। 
adhike doṣo nahi hīne tu doṣaḥ ।। 
śuṣke śuṣke punaḥ kṣepyaḥ ।। 
mardanaṃ ca tāvad yāvan mṛkṣaṇopamā pīṭhī ।। 
muktvā kaṭāhabundhe tāṃ pradadyād vastrasampuṭam ।
vastrāntāni mṛdā limpej jāritānīva bundhake ।।
sajalasthālikāvaktre kaṭāhaṃ pradhvaraṃ kṣipet ।
kaṇṭhaṃ kaṭāhabandhaṃ ca limped vastraṃ mṛdā tathā ।।
chāṇakāni kaṭāhātte yāmārdhaṃ jvālayedyathā ।
jalamadhye raso yāti tāmraṃ tiṣṭhati bundhake ।।
saptavāramidaṃ kāryaṃ śuddhaṃ syād rasapātanam ।
sūkṣmadoṣā vilīyate mūrchitotthitapātane ।।
