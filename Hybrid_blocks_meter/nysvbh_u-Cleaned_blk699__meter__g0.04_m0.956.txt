yathā cetanasyārtho
viṣayopalabdhibhūtaḥ sukhaduḥkhopalabdhibhūtaśca
kalpate tathendriyāṇi vyūḍhāni, viṣayaprāptyarthaśca
raśmeścākṣuṣasya vyūhaḥ, rūpasparśānabhivyaktiśca
vyavahāraprakḷptyarthā, dravyaviśeṣe ca pratīghātādāvaraṇopapattirvyavahārārthā,
sarvadravyāṇāṃ viśvarūpo vyūha indriyavat
karmakāritaḥ puruṣārthatantraḥ।
karma tu dharmādharmabhūtaṃ
cetanasyopabhogārthamiti ।।
