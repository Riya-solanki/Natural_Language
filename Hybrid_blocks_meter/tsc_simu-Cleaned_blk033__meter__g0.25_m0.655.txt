prāṇo apānaḥ samānaśca udāno vyāna eva ca ।
ityete vāyavaḥ pañca śarīreṣu śarīriṇām ।।
mukhanāsādhicārī yaḥ prāṇanāt prāṇa ucyate ।
nābheradhicaratyevopānaye apāna iṣyate ।।
hṛdyadhiṣṭhānanayanāt samāna iti kīrtitaḥ ।
kaṇṭhādūrdhvāvagamanādudāna iti ca smṛtaḥ ।।
sandhyadhiṣṭhānato vyāno vikṣepaṇavijṛmbhaṇāt ।
