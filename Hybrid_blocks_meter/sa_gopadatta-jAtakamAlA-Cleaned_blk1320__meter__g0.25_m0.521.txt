jetuṃ sāgaramekhalāṃ vasumatīṃ bāhudvitīyo apy ayaṃ
śaktaḥ śaktimatāṃ varaḥ karikaravyālambibāhudrumaḥ ।
tvaṃ śakto asya parābhavaṃ katham are kartuṃ bṛhadvakṣasaḥ
tat tvaṃ brūhi parābhavanti na mṛgāḥ siṃhaṃ sphuratkesaram ।।
