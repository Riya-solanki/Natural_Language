tasmai viśva-janīnāya yās tad-bhogīnam āharan ।
tā evāsann ātmanīnā dīnās tu bata mādṛśāḥ ।।
mā jīvan yasya kṛṣṇāya kramate cakṣur ādi na ।
mriyamāṇaś ca mā yasya tasmai tan na pravartate ।।
sa kiñjīvaḥ sa kubrahmaḥ suparva-hatakaś ca saḥ ।
rājīva-nayanaṃ yas tu nājīvanatayā bhajet ।।
atha kṛṣṇāya tṛṣṇāṃ te dadhur vṛṣṇi-pura-dvijāḥ ।
kintu dhṛṣṇak kaṃsa-bhiyā nājagmuḥ kṛṣṇam īkṣitum ।।
ekena kenacit pūrvaṃ bhāryāmāryātigam iva ।
nivārya vāryatālabdhā vāryatāyām iti śrutam ।।
[] tataś ca teṣām ucitam eva, yataḥ-
