buddhyāhaṅkāramanasī śrotraṃ tvak cakṣuṣī tathā ।
jihvā ghrāṇaṃ pādapāyūpasthamadhyātmasañjñitam ।।
yattu mokṣadharme
ādau mana ahaṅkārāt jāyate vṛttayascatāḥ ।।
śabdarāgāt śrotramasya jāyate bhāvitātmanaḥ ।
rūparāgāt tathā cakṣurghrāṇaṃ gandhajidhṛkṣayā ।।
