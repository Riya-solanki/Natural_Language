taduktam-'aśabdam'ityādivākyaṃ tañjñeyaparamityāha-apiceti ।
tarhi rūpaṃ kutaḥ, tatrāha-sarveti ।
yatra tūpāsyatvenocyate tatretyadhyāhṛtya sarvakāraṇatvātprāptarūpavatvaṃ asarvakarmā'ityādiśrutyā nirdiśyata iti yojanā ।
maryādāvadaiśvaryamīśvarasya netyuktaṃ nirākaroti-aiśvaryeti ।
adhyātmādhidaivatadhyānayorvibhāgaḥ pṛthakprayogastadapekṣameva, natvaiśvaryasya paricchedārthamityarthaḥ ।।
