śaktirevāsya viśveśituḥ karaṇaṃ tayaitatkriyāniṣpādanāt sā cec chādirūpeti vakṣyāmaḥ ।। 
uktaṃ ca śrīmatkiraṇe ।। 
icchaiva karaṇaṃ tasya yathā sadyogino matā ।
iti ।। 
śaktiśca cetanarūpā nahi citsvabhāvasyācidrūpā śaktirbhavati ।। 
yac caitac chaktyātmakaṃ karaṇam ।।
