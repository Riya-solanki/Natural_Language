netyucyate ।
yadapyadhikāralakṣaṇe nirṇītaṃ prāyaścittam aavakīrṇipaśuśca tadvadādhānasyāprāptakālatvāt' (jai sū ।
) iti tadapina naiṣṭhikasya bhavitumarhati ।
kiṃ kāraṇam - aārūḍho naiṣṭhikaṃ dharmaṃ yastu pracyavate pinaḥ ।
prāyaścittaṃ na paśyāmi yena śuddhyetsa ātmahā' ityapratisamādheyapatanasmaraṇācchinnaśirasa iva pratikriyānupapatteḥ ।
upakurvāṇasya tu tādṛkpatanasmaraṇābhāvādupapadyate tatprāyaścittam ।।
