hāsāyāsavilolakuṇḍalamaṇicchāyoparāgāruṇe
yasmin prāṇasamaś cakāra muditaḥ pattrāṅkuraṃ yatnataḥ ।
bālāmbhoruhapattrakomaladale tatraiva gaṇḍasthale
vaktraṃ raktakalaṅkitaṃ nikaṣati sthitvā lalāṭe aṇḍajaḥ ।।
