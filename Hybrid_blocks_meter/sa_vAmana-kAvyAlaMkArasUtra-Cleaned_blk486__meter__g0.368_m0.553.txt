ityatra dhṛtadhanuṣi ityasaṃjñāyāṃ na yuktaḥ prayogaḥ । dhanuṣaśca ityaṅvidhānāt । saṃjñāyāṃ hyanaṅ vikalpitaḥ । vā saṃjñāyām iti ।।
durgandhipada iddurlabhaḥ ।।
durgandhiḥ kāyaḥ ityādiṣu durgandhipade it samānānto durlabhaḥ । utpūtyādiṣu duḥśabdasyāpāṭhāt ।।
sudatyādayaḥ pratividheyāḥ ।।
sā dakṣaroṣātsudatī sasarja iti, śikharadati patati raśanā
