yathā mamaiva-
subhadrāyāḥ śrutvā tad-anumatimat tena haraṇaṃ
kṛtaṃ kaunteyena kṣubhita-manasaḥ stabdha-vapuṣaḥ ।
namad-vaktrāḥ svānte kim api vilikhanto' tikuṭilair
apaśyann udbāṣpair yadu-patim apāṅgair yadu-bhaṭāḥ ।।
