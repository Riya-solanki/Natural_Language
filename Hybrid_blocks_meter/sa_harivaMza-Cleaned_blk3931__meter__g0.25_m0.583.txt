tasyābhipatataḥ khaḍgaṃ ciccheda yudhi keśavaḥ 
nārācaiś ca tribhiḥ kruddho bibhedainam athorasi ।।

visaṃjño mūrchito rājā vajreṇeva hato giriḥ ।।
