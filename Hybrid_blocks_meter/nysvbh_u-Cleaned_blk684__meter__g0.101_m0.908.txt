mahaditi
mahattaraṃ mahattamaṃ copalabhyate yathā nyagrodhaparvatādi ।
aṇviti aṇutaramaṇutamaṃ ca gṛhyate yathā
nyagrodhadhānādi, tadubhayamupalabhyamānaṃ cakṣuṣo
bhautikatvaṃ bādhate bhautikaṃ hi yāvat
tāvadeva vyāpnoti abhautikaṃ tu vibhutvāt
sarvavyāpakamiti ।।
