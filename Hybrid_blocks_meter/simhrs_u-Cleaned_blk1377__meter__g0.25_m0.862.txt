atha vimarśa-sandhiḥ-
yatra pralobhana-krodha-vyasanādyair vimṛśyate । 
bījārtho garbha-nirbhinnaḥ sa vimarśa itīryate ।।
prakarī-niyatāptyānuguṇyād atrāṅga-kalpanam । 
apavādo' tha sampheṭo vidrava-drava-śaktayaḥ ।।
dyuti-prasaṅgau chalana-vyavasāyau nirodhanam । 
prarocanā vicalanam ādānaṃ syus trayodaśa ।।
