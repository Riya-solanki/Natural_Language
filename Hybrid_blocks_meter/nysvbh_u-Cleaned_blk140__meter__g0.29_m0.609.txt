nityasukharāgasyāprahāṇe mokṣādhigamābhāvo rāgasya bandhanasamājñānāt।
yadyayaṃ mokṣe nityaṃ sukhamabhivyajyata iti nityasukharāgeṇa mokṣāyaghaṭamānā na mokṣamadhigacchet, nādhigantumarhatīti।
bandhanasamājñāto hi rāgaḥ।
na ca bandhane satyapi kaścinmukta ityupapadyata iti।।
