prakṛtitvasyāyamabhyuccayaḥ ।
itaśca prakṛtirbrahma, yatkāraṇaṃ sākṣādbrahmaiva kāraṇamupādāyobhau prabhavapralayāvāmnāyate- asarvāṇi ha vā imāni bhūtānyākāśādeva samutpadyante ।
ākāśaṃ pratyastaṃ yanti' iti ।
yaddhi yasmātprabhavati yasmiṃśca pralīyate tattasyopādānaṃ prasiddham ।
yathā vrīhiyavādīnāṃ pṛthivī ।
sākṣādite sākṣāditi copādānāntarānupādānaṃ darśayatyākāśādeveti ।
pratyastamayaśca nopādānādanyatra kāryasya dṛṣṭaḥ ।।
