mukhyaśca prāṇa itaraprāṇavadbrahmavikāra ityatidiśati ।
taccāviseṣeṇaiva sarvaprāṇānāṃ brahmavikāratvamākhyātam ।
aetasmājjāyate prāṇo manaḥ sarvendriyāṇi ca' iti sendriyamanovyatirekeṇa prāṇasyotpattiśravaṇāt ।
asa prāṇamasṛjata' ityādi śravaṇebhyaśca ।
kimarthaḥ punaratideśaḥ,
