atha kāryatvānavagame api tatkāryāvagatisādṛśyātpravṛttiḥ, tatastatkāryāvagatisādṛśyamapyastīti nivṛttirapi syāt sādṛśyayoraviśeṣāt ।
nacāsati kāraṇe kāraṇasadṛśaṃ tatkāryaṃ sādhayitumarhati- māsīṣadhacchuktikārajatakāryamābharaṇam ।।
