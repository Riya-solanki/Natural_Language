tatra proṣita-patikā-
dūra-deśaṃ gate kānte bhavet proṣita-bhartṛkā । 
asyās tu jāgaraḥ kārśyaṃ nimittādi-vilokanam ।।
mālinyam anavasthānaṃ prāyaḥ śayyā-niveṣaṇam । 
jāḍya-cintā-prabhṛtayo vikriyāḥ kathitā budhaiḥ ।। athā mama-
dūre tiṣṭhati so' dhunā priyatamaḥ prāpto vasantodayaḥ
kaṣṭaṃ kokila-kūjitāni sahasā jātāni dambholayaḥ ।
aṅgāny apy avaśāni yānti tanutāṃ yātīva me cetanā
hā kaṣṭaṃ mama duṣkṛtasya mahimā candro' pi caṇḍāyate ।।
