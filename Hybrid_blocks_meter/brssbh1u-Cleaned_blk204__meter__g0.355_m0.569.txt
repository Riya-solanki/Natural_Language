itaścaivamabhyupagantavyamiti, pūrvasminvākye prakṛtaṃ brahmeti ।
yato bhūtādīnpādānvyapadiśati ।
bhūtapṛthivīśarīrahṛdayāni hi nirdiśyāha- asaiṣā catuṣpadā ṣaḍvidhā gāyatrī' iti ।
nahi brahmānāśrayaṇe kevalasya chandaso bhūtādayaḥ pādā upapadyante ।
apica brahmānāśrayaṇe neyamṛksaṃbodhyeta- atāvānasya' iti ।
anayā hi ṛcā svarasena brahmaivābhidhīyate, apādo asya sarvā bhūtāni tripādasyāmṛtaṃ divi' iti sarvātmatvopapatteḥ ।
puruṣasūkte apīyamṛgbrahmaparatayaiva samāmnāyate ।
smṛtiśca brahmaṇa evaṃrūpatāṃ darśayati- aviṣṭabhyāhamidaṃ kṛtsnamekāṃśena sthito jagat' iti ।
ayadvai tadbrahma' iti ca nirdeśa evaṃ sati mukhyārtha upapadyate ।
apañca brahmapuruṣāḥ' iti ca hṛdayasuṣiṣu brahmapuruṣaśrutirbrahmasaṃbandhitāyāṃ vivakṣitāyāṃ saṃbhavati ।
tasmādasti pūrvasminvākye brahma prakṛtam ।
tadeva brahma jyotirvākye dyusaṃbandhātpratyabhijñāyamānaṃ parāmṛśyata iti sthitam ।।
