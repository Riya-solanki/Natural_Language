mercury rañjana 
tālakadaradaśilābhiḥ snehakṣārāmlalavaṇasahitābhiḥ ।
samakadviguṇatriguṇān puṭo vahedvaṃgaśastrādīn ।।
raktasnehaniṣekaiḥ śeṣaṃ kuryādrasasya kṛṣṭiriyam ।
cāraṇajāraṇamātrātkurute rasamindragopanibham ।।
