kiṃ dvitīyamityāśaṅkya taṃ spaṣṭayannāha pītetyādi ।।
ādau prathamaṃ mukhaṃ vidheyam ityadhyāhāraḥ pāradasya mukhaṃ kāryamityarthaḥ ।।
tataścetpītakriyā bhavanti svarṇasambandhīni kāryāṇi bhavanti tadā pītaṃ svarṇaṃ deyam ।।
śvetakriyāsu śvetaṃ deyaṃ śvetaṃ tāram ityarthaḥ ।।
svarṇatārādikaṃ khalve dattaṃ kṛtamukho rasaścarati ।।
punarnirmukho rasaḥ akṛtamukho rasaḥ khalve ghṛṣṭo gharṣitaḥ san pūrvoktaṃ carati bhakṣayati ।।
kābhiḥ saha divyauṣadhībhir vakṣyamāṇābhiḥ saha ।।
yathā padyam ।
sāsyo rasaḥ syātpaṭuśigrututthaiḥ sarājikair vyoṣaṇakais trirātram ।
piṣṭastataḥ svinnatanuḥ suvarṇamukhānayaṃ khādati sarvadhātūn ।।
iti ।।
nirmukhaśca amlavargeṇa saṃyuktaṃ yathālābhena mardayet ।
abhrakādīṃśca carate sūtako vāsanāmukhaḥ ।।
iyanmānasya sūtasya bhojyadravyātmikā mitiḥ ।
iyatītyucyate yāsau grāsamānamitīritam ।।
iti paribhāṣā ।।
iti śrīmatkuralakulapayodhisudhākaramiśramaheśātmajacaturbhujamiśraviracitāyāṃ mugdhāvabodhinyāṃ rasahṛdayaṭīkāyāṃ rasaśodhanātmako dvitīyo'vabodhaḥ ।।
