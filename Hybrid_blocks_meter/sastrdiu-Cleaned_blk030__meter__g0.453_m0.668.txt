vyākaraṇādapi pāṇinīyādadhyayanāṅgataivopanayanasyānagamyate ।
adhyayanāṅgatā hi smarannācāryakaraṇe nayaterātmanepadaṃ vidhatte, adhyāpanāṅgatve hi kartrabhiprāyatvātkriyāphalasya tritvādevātmanepadaṃ sidhyet ।
aupanīya tu yaḥ śiṣyam' iti ktvāpratyayo hi kathañcit adarśapūrṇamāsābhyāmiṣṭvetivadasatyapyaṅgāṅgibhāve avakalpyate punarvidhānaṃ
