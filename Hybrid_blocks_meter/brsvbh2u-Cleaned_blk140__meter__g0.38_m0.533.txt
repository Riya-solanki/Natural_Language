tasmātsamavāya evāyamityata āhayathā cotpannamātrasyeti ।
saṃyogajo api hi saṃyogo bhavadbhirabhyupeyate na kriyāja evetyarthaḥ ।
na cāprāptipūrvikaiva prāptiḥ saṃyogaḥ, ātmākāśasaṃyoge nitye tadabhāvāt, kāryasya cotpannamātrasyaikasmin kṣaṇe kāraṇaprāptivirahācceti ।
api ca saṃbandhirūpātirikte saṃbandhe siddhe tadavāntarabhedāya lakṣaṇabhedo anuśrīyeta sa eva tu saṃbandhyatirikto asiddhaḥ, uktaṃ hi parastādatiriktaḥ saṃbandhibhyāṃ saṃbandho asaṃbaddho na saṃbandhinau ghaṭayitumiṣṭe ।
saṃbandhisaṃbandhe cānavasthitiḥ ।
tasmādupapattyanubhavābhyāṃ na kāryasya kāraṇādanyatvam, api tu kāraṇasyaivāyamanirvācyaḥ pariṇāmabheda iti ।
tasmāt kāryasya kāraṇādanatirekān kiṃ kena saṃbaddhaṃ, saṃyogasya ca saṃyogibhyāmanatirekāt kastayoḥ saṃyoga ityāhanāpi saṃyogasyeti ।
vicārāsahatvenānirvācyatāmasyāparibhāvayannāśaṅkatesaṃbandhiśabdapratyavyatirekeṇeti ।
nirākarotina ।
ekatve api svarūpabāhyarūpāpekṣayeti ।
tattadanirvacanīyānekaviśeṣāvasthābhedāpekṣayaikasminnapi nānābuddhivyapadeśopapattiriti ।
yathaiko devadattaḥ svagataviśeṣāpekṣayā manuṣyo brāhmaṇo avadātaḥ, svagatāvasthābhedāpekṣayā bālo yuvā sthaviraḥ, svakriyābhedāpekṣayā śrotriyaḥ, parāpekṣayā tu pitā putraḥ pautro bhrātā jāmāteti ।
nidarśanāntaramāhayathā caikāpi satī rekheti ।
dārṣṭāntike yojayatitathā saṃbandhinoriti ।
aṅgulyornairantaryaṃ saṃyogaḥ, dadhikuṇḍayorauttarādharyaṃ saṃyogaḥ ।
kāryakāraṇayostu tādātmye apyanirvācyasya kāryasya bhedaṃ vivakṣitvāsaṃbandhinorityuktam ।
nāpi saṃbandhiviṣayatve saṃbandhaśabdapratyayayoḥ
