anyatrāpi amanomayaḥ prāṇaśarīraḥ' ityādāvupādhidharmeṇa brahmaṇa upāsanamāśritam ।
ihāpi tadyujyate vākyasyopakramopasaṃhārābhyāmekārthatvāvagamātprāṇaprajñābrahmaliṅgāvagamācca ।
tasmādbrahmavākyametaditi siddham ।।
