ṣoṭamadhyādratīmātraṃ prage'mṛtakalānvitam ।।
ādatte niyataṃ velaṃ valistasya na jāyate ।
palitaṃ mūlato yāti kṛṣṇāḥ keśā bhavanti ca ।।
śarīre jāyate dārḍhyaṃ śarīraṃ kṣīyate na hi ।
dehakāntiḥ suvarṇābhā nityaṃ ṣoṭasya sevanāt ।।
hemavajrādibhūnāgasatvairniṣpāditastribhiḥ ।
dehakṛcchatavedhī ca ṣoṭo jāto ayamadbhutaḥ ।।
