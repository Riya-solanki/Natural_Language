iṣṭādhigamārthā pravṛttiriti cet? na, aniṣṭoparamārthatvāt।
idam anumānam, iṣṭādhigamārtho mokṣopadeśaḥ pravṛttiśca mumukṣūṇām, nobhayamanarthakamiti।
etaccāyuktam, aniṣṭoparamārtho mokṣopadeśaḥ, pravṛttiśca mumukṣūṇāmiti।
neṣṭamaniṣṭenānanubaddhaṃ sambhavatīti aniṣṭānubandhāt iṣṭamapyaniṣṭasampadyate, aniṣṭahānāya ca ghaṭamāna iṣṭamapi jahāti, vivekahānasyāśakyatvāditi।।
