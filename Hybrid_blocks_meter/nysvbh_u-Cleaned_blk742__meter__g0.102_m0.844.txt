ātmā manasā saṃbadhyate mana indriyeṇa
indriyaṃ sarvārthaiḥ saṃnikṛṣṭamiti ātmendriyamano arthasaṃnikarṣebhyo
yugapad grahaṇāni syuḥ na ca yugapad rūpādayo
gṛhyante tasmānnaikamindriyaṃ
sarvaviṣayamastīti ।
asāhacaryācca viṣayagrahaṇānāṃ naikamindriyaṃ
sarvaviṣayakam, sāhacarye hi viṣayagrahaṇānāmandhādyanupapattiriti
।।
