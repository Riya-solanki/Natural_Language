vipratipatteriti।
vyāhatamekārthadarśanaṃ vipratipattiḥ, vyāghātaḥ virodho asahabhāva iti।
astyātmekaṃ darśanam nāstyātmetyaparam।
na ca sadbhāvāsadbhāvau sahaikatra sambhavataḥ।
na cānyatarasādhako heturūpalabhyate।
tatra tattvānavadhāraṇaṃ saṃśaya iti।।
