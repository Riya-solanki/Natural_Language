svapnāvasthā parīkṣitā suṣuptāvasthedānīṃ parīkṣyate ।
tatraitāḥ suṣuptiviṣayāḥ śrutayo bhavanti ।
kvacicchrūyate- atadyatraitatsuptaḥ samastaḥ svapnaṃ na vijānātyāsu tadā nāḍīṣu sṛpo bhavati' iti ।
anyatra tu nāḍīrevānukramya śrūyate - atābhiḥ pratyavasṛpya purītati śete' iti ।
tathānyatra nāḍīrevānukramya atāsu tadā bhavati yadā suptaḥ svapnaṃ na kañcana paśyatyathāsminprāṇa evaikadhā bhavati' iti ।
tathānyatra aya eṣo antarhṛdaya ākāśastasmiñchete' iti ।
tathānyatra asatā somya tadā saṃpanno bhavati svamapīto bhavati' iti ।
aprājñenātmanā saṃpariśvakto na bāhyaṃ kiñcana veda nāntaram' iti ca ।
tatra saṃśayaḥ - kimetāni nāḍyādīni parasparanirapekṣāṇi bhinnāni suṣuptisthānānyāhosvitparasparāpekṣayaikaṃ suṣuptisthānamasti ।
kiṃ tāvatprāptaṃ bhinnānīti ।
kutaḥ - ekārthatvāt ।
nahyekārthānīṃ kvacitparasparāpekṣatvaṃ dṛśyate vrīhiyavādīnām ।
nāḍyādīnāṃ tvekārthatā suṣuptau dṛśyate - anāḍīśu sṛpto bhavati' apurītati śete'
