mahadahaṅkārau tāvadaprasiddhau, ahaṅkāraprakṛtikatvena tanmātrāṇyapyaprasiddhāni smartuṃ na śakyanta ityāha-itareṣāṃ ceti ।
nanu amahataḥ paramavyaktam'itiśrutiprasiddhāni mahadādīnītyata āha-yadapīti ।
sūtratātparyamāha-kāryeti ।
sāṃkhyasmṛtermahadādiṣviva pradhāne api prāmāṇyaṃ neti niścīyata ityarthaḥ ।
sāṃkhyasmṛterbādhe api taduktayuktīnāṃ kathaṃ bādha ityata āha-tarketi ।।
