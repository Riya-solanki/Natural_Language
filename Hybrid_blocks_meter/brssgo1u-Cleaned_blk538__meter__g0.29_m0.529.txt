tasmātparameva brahma dyubhvādyāyatanam ।
tadetat aadṛśyatvādiguṇako dharmokteḥ' ityanenaiva siddham ।
tasyaiva hi bhūtayonivākyasya madhya idaṃ paṭhitam ayasmindyauḥ pṛthivī cāntarikṣam' iti ।
prapañcārthaṃ tu punarupanyastam ।।
