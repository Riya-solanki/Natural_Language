śatanirvyūḍhe ca samaṃ pādonaṃ pañcasaptativyūḍhe ।
pañcāśati tadardhaṃ pādaḥ syātpañcaviṃśatike ।।
aṣṭāṃśaṃ tu tadardhe ṣoḍaśāṃśaṃ tadardhanirvyūḍhe ।
tasyārdhe dvātriṃśaccatuḥṣaṣṭyaṃśaṃ tadardhanirvyūḍhe ।।
