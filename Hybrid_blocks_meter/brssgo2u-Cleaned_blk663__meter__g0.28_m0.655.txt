nanu ayo ayaṃ vijñānamayaḥ prāṇeṣu' iti śārīra eva mahattvasaṃbandhitvena pratinirdiśyate ।
śāstradṛṣṭyā tveṣa nirdeśo vāmadevavaddraṣṭavyaḥ ।
tasmātprājñaviṣayatvātparimāṇāntaraśravaṇasya ca jīvasyāṇutvaṃ virudhyate ।।
