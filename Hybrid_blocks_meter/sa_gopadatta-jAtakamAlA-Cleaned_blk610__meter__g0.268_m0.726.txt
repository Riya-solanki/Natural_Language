yair nirdagdhā matihutabhujā skandhasaṃbandhalakṣyī
tṛṣṇā naikavyasanaviśikhāpātasaṃdhānamaurvī ।
te asmin dhanyā jagati sukhinas te ta evānavadyās
teṣāṃ bhadraṃ sthitam abhimukhaṃ te bhayebhyo vimuktāḥ ।।
