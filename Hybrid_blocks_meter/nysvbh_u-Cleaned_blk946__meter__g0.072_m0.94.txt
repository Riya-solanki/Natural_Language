asti khalu vai jñānāyaugapadyam
ekaikasyendriyasya yathāviṣayaṃ
karaṇasyaikapratyayanirvṛttau sāmarthyāt
na tadekatve manaso liṅgam yattu khalvindriyāntarāṇāṃ
viṣayāntareṣu jñānāyaugapadyamiti talliṅgam।
kasmāt ? saṃbhavati khalu vai bahuṣu manaḥsu
indriyamanaḥsaṃyogayaugapadyamiti
jñānayaugapadyaṃ syāt na tu bhavati
tasmād viṣaye pratyayaparyāyādekaṃ mano
bhavatīti ।।
