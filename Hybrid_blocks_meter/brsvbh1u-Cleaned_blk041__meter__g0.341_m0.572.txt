bhāvasya svakārye pramitāvanapekṣatvāt ।
pramitāvanapekṣatve apyutpattau pratyakṣāpekṣatvāttadvirodhādanutpattilakṣaṇamaprāmāṇyamiti cenna ।
utpādakāpratidvandvitvāt ।
na hyāgamajñānaṃ sāṃvyavahārikaṃ pratyakṣasya prāmāṇyamupahanti yena kāraṇābhāvānna bhavedapi tu tāttvikam ।
na ca tattasyotpādakam ।
atāttvikapramāṇabhāvebhyo api sāṃvyavahārikapramāṇebhyastattvajñānotpattidarśanāt ।
tathā ca varṇe hrasvatvadīrghatvādayo anyadharmā api samāropitāḥ tattvapratipattihetavaḥ ।
na hi laukikāḥ anāgaḥ'iti vā"nagaḥ"iti vā padātkuñcaraṃ vā taruṃ vā pratipadyamānā bhavanti bhrāntāḥ ।
na cā
