atha bījaḥ-
yat tu svalpam upakṣiptaṃ bahudhā vistṛtiṃ gatam । 
kāryasya kāraṇaṃ prājñais tad bījam iti kathyate ।
uptaṃ bījaṃ taror yadvad aṅkurādi-prabhedataḥ ।। 
phalāya kalpate tadvan nāyakādi-vibhedataḥ ।
phalāyaitad bhaved yasmād bījam ity abhidhīyate ।।
