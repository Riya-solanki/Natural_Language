atha harṣaḥ-
manorathasya lābhena siddhyā yogyasya vastunaḥ । 
mitra-saṅgama-devādi-prasādādeś ca kalpitaḥ ।।
manaḥ-prasādo harṣaḥ syād atra netrāsya-phullatā । 
priyābhāṣaṇam āśleṣaḥ pulakānāṃ prarohaṇam ।
svedodgamaś ca hastena hasta-sampīḍanādayaḥ ।।
