kiñca tarkasya saṃbhāvitadoṣatvāttena nirdeṣavedāntasamanvayo na bādhya ityāha-tarkāpratiṣṭhānādapīti ।
puruṣamatīnāṃ vicitratve api kapilasya sarvajñatvāttadīyatarke viśvāsa iti śaṅkate-atheti ।
akapilo yadi sarvajñaḥ kaṇādo neti kā pramā'iti nyāyena pariharati-evamapīti ।
sūtramadhyasthaśaṅkābhāgaṃ vyācaṣṭe-athocyeteti ।
