avyaktabuddhyahaṅkārāstanmātrāṇāṃ ca pañcakam ।
aṣṭau prakṛtayastvetāḥ sapta ca vyaktatāmiyāt ।।
avyaktaprakṛtijanyatvāt avyaktānīti cocyate ।
vyajyante ca yathā loke ghaṭādyā na tathā hi tat ।।
avyaktaṃ prakṛtirmāyā pradhānaṃ brahma kāraṇam ।
avyākṛtaṃ tamaḥpuṣpaṃ kṣetramakṣaranāmakam ।।
bahudhātmakādināmāni tasyāmī te jagurbudhāḥ ।
vyajyate nendriyairyasmādavyaktamata ucyate ।
anādimadhyanidhanānniraṅgatvena tat tathā ।।
