viḍa for jāraṇa
kadalīpalāśatilaniculakanakasuradālivāstukairaṇḍāḥ ।
varṣābhūvṛṣamokṣakasahitāḥ kṣāro yathālābham ।।
ānīya kṣāravṛkṣān kusumaphalaśiphātvakpravālairupetān kṛtvātaḥ khaṇḍaśastān vipulataraśilāpiṣṭagātrātiśuṣkān ।
dagdhvā kāṇḍaistilānāṃ karisurabhihayāmbhobhir āsrāvya vastrair bhasma tyaktvā jalaṃ tanmṛduśikhini pacedvaṃśapākena bhūyaḥ ।।
tacchuṣyamāṇaṃ hi sabāṣpabudbudān yadā vidhatte kṣaṇabhaṅgurān bahūn ।
tadā kṣipettryūṣaṇahiṃgugandhakaṃ kṣāratrayaṃ ṣaḍlavaṇāni bhūkhagau ।।
dravyāṇi saṃmiśrya nivṛtya bhūtale vyavasthitaṃ śastrakaṭorikāpuṭe ।
saṃsthāpayetsaptadināni dhānyagataṃ prayojyaṃ rasajāraṇādikam ।।
