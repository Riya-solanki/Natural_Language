gaganacāraṇaṃ nirdiśyānyasaṃskāraṃ stuvannāha itthamityādi ।।
mayā granthakartrā asmin śāstre karmaṇyati saṃvādī prakāraḥ agrimaprakaraṇe sattvaniṣkāsanarūpaḥ nirdiśyate nirdeśaḥ kriyate ।।
saṃvedyate saṃskriyate saṃskāravidbhiriti saṃvādī ।।
kiṃ kṛtvā itthamuktaprakāreṇa anekairdoṣaiḥ anekakaṣṭaiḥ bahuśramairbahvāyāsairgaganacāraṇaṃ matvā abhrakacāraṇaṃ jñātvā ।।
gaganacāraṇānantaramanyotkṛṣṭaprakāro nirdiśyate iti bhāvaḥ ।।
