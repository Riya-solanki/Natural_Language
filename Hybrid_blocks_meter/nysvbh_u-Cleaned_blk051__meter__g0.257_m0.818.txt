sā ceyaṃ pramitiḥ pratyakṣaparā।
jijñāsitamarthamāptopadeśāt pratipadyamāno liṅgadarśanenāpi bubhutsate।
liṅgadarśanenānumitaṃ ca pratyakṣato didṛkṣate।
pratyakṣata upalabdhe arthe jijñāsā nivartate।
pūrvoktamudāharaṇamagniriti।
