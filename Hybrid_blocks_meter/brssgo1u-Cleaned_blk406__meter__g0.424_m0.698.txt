sthānanāmarūpāṇāṃ dhyānārthaṃ śrutyantare apyupadeśādakṣisthānatvoktiratra na doṣa iti sūtrayojanā ।
anavakḷptiḥ akḷptakalpanā tadā bhavet, yadyatraiva nirdiṣṭaṃ bhavedityanvayaḥ ।
nanvanucitabāhulyoktirasamādhānamityāśaṅkya yuktimāha-nirguṇamapīti ।।
