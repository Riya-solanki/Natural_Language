atha garbha-sandhiḥ-
dṛṣṭādṛṣṭasya bījasya garbhas tv anveṣaṇaṃ muhuḥ । 
atrāpy ācāpatākānurodhād aṅgāni kalpayet ।।
abhūtāharaṇaṃ mārgo rūpodāharaṇe kramaḥ । 
saṅgrahaś cānumānaṃ ca toṭakādhibale tathā ।
udvegaḥ sambhramāksepau dvādaśaiṣāṃ tu lakṣaṇam ।।
