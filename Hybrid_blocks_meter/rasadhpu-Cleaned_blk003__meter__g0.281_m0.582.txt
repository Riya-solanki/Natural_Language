iha rasakarmāṇi caturaśītiḥ guṭikābhedāścaturaśītiḥ añjanāni caturaśītiḥ ।। 
evaṃ te dvipañcāśadadhike dve śate bhedāḥ ।। 
tata eteṣāṃ bhedānāṃ madhyād yadaikā kācit kriyā prārabhyate tadā prārambhātpūrvaṃ māsamekaṃ brahmacaryarūpaṃ tapaḥ kartavyam ।। 
brahmacaryasyaiva sarvatapomūlatvāt ।। 
tadrahitasyāparatapasaḥ sarvasyāpi niṣphalatvāt ।। 
yadvā brahmacaryaṃ dhāryam ।। 
tathācāryādinā yathāyuktyā tapaḥ kāryam ।। 
dvayorapi sarvakarmaphalānām ādimūlatvāt ।। 
tathā haviṣyānnaṃ ghṛtaśarkarāmiśraṃ pāyasaṃ kṣīrabhojanaṃ kāryam ।। 
aparair āhārair dehaśuddherabhāvāt ।। 
prāṇāyāmasādhanābhāvāc ca ।। 
tata evaṃ karma pālayatā tatkarmaphalaṃ yāvan netavyam ।। 
yato rasādikā kriyāḥ sādhake bhraṣṭe yathoktabhojanam akurvati na sidhyanti ।। 
taponaṣṭe na ca phalanti ।। 
etac ca granthānte svayaṃ spaṣṭayiṣyati ।। 
athādhyāyaṃ samāyātaṃ śrīkaṅkālayayoginaḥ ।
vadāmi vyañjito yatra yuktibhiḥ śṛṅkhalārasaḥ ।।
