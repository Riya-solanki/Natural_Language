saṃbhāvanamathotprekṣā prakṛtasya samena yat ।
samena, upamānena ।
udārahaṇam unmeṣaṃ yo mama na sahate jātivairī niśāyām intorintīvaradaladṛśā tasya saundaryadarpaḥ ।
nītaḥ śāntiṃ prasabhamanayā vaktrakāntyeti harṣā- llagrā manye lalitatanu te pādayoḥ pajhalakṣmīḥ ।।
limpatīva tamo aṅgāni varṣatīvāñjanaṃ nabhaḥ ।
asatpuruṣaseveva daṣṭirviphalatāṃ gatā ।।
ityādau vyāpanādi lepanādirūpatayā saṃbhāvitam ।।
