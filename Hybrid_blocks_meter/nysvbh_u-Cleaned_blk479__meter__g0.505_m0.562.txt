yathānupalabhyamānāpyāvaraṇānupalabdhirasti evamanupalabhyamānamapyāvaraṇamastīti।
yadyabhyanujānāti bhavān anānupalīyamānāvaraṇānupalabdhirastīti abhyanujñāvaya ca vadati anāstyāvaraṇamanupalambhāt' ityetat etasminnapyabhyanujñāvāde pratipattiniyamo nopapadyate iti ।।
