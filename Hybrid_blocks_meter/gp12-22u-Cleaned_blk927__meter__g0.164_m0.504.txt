indraḥ krūra-caritram ācaritavān yat tena cintāgame
cittaṃ kampam iyarti ced giri kathaṃ cittena tad vyajyatām ।

āyāti svayam eva ced giri kathaṃ cittena sā vyajyatām? ।। [śārdūla]
