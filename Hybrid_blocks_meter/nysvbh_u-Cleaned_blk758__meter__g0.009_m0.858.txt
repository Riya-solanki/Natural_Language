dṛṣṭo hi vāyvādīnāṃ bhūtānāṃ guṇaviśeṣābhivyaktiniyamaḥ
uvāyuḥ sparśavyañjakaḥ āpo rasavyañjikāḥ
tejo rūpavyañjakam pārthivaṃ kiñcid dravyaṃ
kasyacid dravyasya grandhavyañjakamadva asti cāyamindriyāṇāṃ
bhūtaguṇaviśeṣopalabdhiniyamaḥ tena bhūtaguṇaviśeṣopalabdhermanyāmahebhūtaprakṛtīnīndriyāṇi
nāvyaktaprakṛtīniti ।।
