vipākasaṃvedanāt karmāśayakṣaye śarīrapātaḥ
prāyaṇam karmāśayāntarācca punarjanma।
bhūtamātrāttu karmanirapekṣāt śarīrotpattau kasya kṣayāt śarīrapātaḥ ।
prāyaṇamiti ? prāyaṇānupapatteḥ khalu vai
nityatvaprasaṅgaṃ vidmaḥ, yādṛcchike tu prāyaṇe
prāyaṇabhedānupapattiriti ।।
