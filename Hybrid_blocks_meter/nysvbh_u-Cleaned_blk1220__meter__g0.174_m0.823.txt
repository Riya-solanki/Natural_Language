yāvad mūrtimad yāvacca saṃyujyate tat sarvaṃ sāvayavamityanavasthākāriṇāvimau hetū
sācānavasthā nopapadyate
satyāmanavasthāyāṃ satyau hetū syātāṃ
tasmādapratiṣedhoyaṃ niravayavatvasyeti।
vibhāgaśca vibhajyamānahānernopapadyate tasmāt pralayāntatā nopapadyate iti।
anavasthāyāṃ capratyadhikaraṇaṃ dravyāvayavānāmānantyāt
parimāṇabhedānāṃ gurutvasya cāgrahaṇaṃ samānaparimāṇatvaṃ
cāvayavāvayavinoḥ paramāṇvavayavavibhāgādūrdhvamiti
।।
