evaṃ pakvaṃ vidhinā bījavaraṃ sūtarāṭ tathāmlena ।
kartavyaḥ saṃsvedyo yāvatpiṣṭī bhavecchlakṣṇā ।।
tailena tena vidhinā svinnā piṣṭī bhavedakhilam ।
athavā ślakṣṇaṃ śilayā nighṛṣṭabījaṃ bhavetpiṇḍī ।।
pāko vaṭakavidhinā kartavyastailayogena ।
krāmaṇapiṇḍe kṣiptvā māṣaiśca syāt sudṛḍhapiṇḍatvam ।।
mṛdvagninā supakvaṃ dagdhaṃ yāvanna bhavetpiṇḍam ।
ākṛṣya cātha sūtaṃ piṇḍe śeṣaṃ tathā punaḥ pācyam ।।
athavāpyauṣadhapiṇḍe dolātapte kharpare vidhinā ।
punarapi piṇḍe kṣepyaṃ garbhe yāvaddrutirbhavati ।।
evaṃ drutaṃ hi garbhe bījavaraṃ jarati rasarāje ।
garbhadrutyā rahitaṃ biḍayogairjarati garbhe ca ।।
