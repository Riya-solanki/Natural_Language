hanyām addhā svayam atha yadi śravyam etan na tarhi ।
itthaṃ kṛṣṇa tvam iha kalayan bhīmasenena yuddhād
dharyād daitya-prakṛtim amukaṃ ghātayiṣyasy asy upetya ।।
