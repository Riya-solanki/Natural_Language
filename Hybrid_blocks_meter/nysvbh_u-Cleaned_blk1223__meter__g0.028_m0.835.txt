yathā aayaṃ tanturayaṃ tantuḥ' iti pratyekaṃ
tantuṣu vivicyamāneṣu nārthāntaraṃ kiñcidupalabhyate
yat paṭabuddherviṣayaḥ syāt yāthātmyānupalabdheḥ,
asati viṣaye paṭabuddhirbhavantī mithyābuddhirbhavati,
evaṃ sarvatreti ।।
