devako ghacotkacaśca hyabhimanyuśca sarvagaḥ ।
suhotro niramitraśca parīkṣidabhimanyujaḥ ।।
janamejayo asya tato bhaviṣyāṃśca nṛpāñchṛṇu ।।
iti śrīgāruḍe mahāpurāṇe pūrvakhaṇḍe prathamāṃśākhye ācārakāṇḍe candravaṃśavarṇanaṃ nāma catvāriṃśaduttaraśatatamo adhyāyaḥ
