kasyacidamlaphalasya gṛhītatadrasasāhacarye rūpe
gandhe vā kena cidindriyeṇa gṛhyamāṇe rasanasyendriyāntarasya
vikāro rasānusmṛtau rasagardhipravartito
dantodakasaṃplavabhūto gṛhyate tasyendriyacaitanye anupapattiḥ
nānyadṛṣṭamanyaḥ smarati ।।
