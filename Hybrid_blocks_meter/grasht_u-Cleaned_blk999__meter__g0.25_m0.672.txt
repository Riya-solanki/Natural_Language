atha jīrṇarasasyādhikyaṃ darśayannāha ghanetyādi ।।
ghanasatvaṃ pādapramāṇaṃ jīrṇaṃ yasmin saḥ ।।
kāntajīrṇa iti pādapramāṇakāntajīrṇa ityarthaḥ ।।
punastīkṣṇaṃ samaṃ jīrṇaṃ yasminnevaṃvidho rasaḥ ।।
kṣetrīkaraṇaṃ pradhānaṃ sarvatretyarthaḥ ।
baddho yaḥ khoṭatāṃ yāto dhmāto dhmātaḥ kṣayaṃ vrajet ।
khoṭo bandhaḥ sa vijñeyaḥ śīghraṃ sarvagadāpahaḥ ।
iti granthāntare ।।
