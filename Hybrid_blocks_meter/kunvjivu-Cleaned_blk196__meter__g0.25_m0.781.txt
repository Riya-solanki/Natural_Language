pratibhāprathamodbhedasamaye yatra vakratā ।
sabhdābhidheyayorantaḥ sphuratīva vibhāvyate ।।
alaṅkārasya kavayo yatrālaṅkāraṇāntaram ।
asaṃtuṣṭā nibadhnanti hārādermaṇibandhavat ।।
ratnaraśmicchaṭotsekabhāsurairbhūṣaṇairyathā ।
kāntāśarīramācchādya bhūṣāyai parikalpyate ।।
yatra tadvadalaṅkārairbhrājamānairnijātmanā ।
svaśobhātiśayāntaḥ sthamalaṅkāryaṃ prakāśate ।।
yadapyanūtanollekhaṃ vastu yatra tadapyalam ।
uktivaicitryamātreṇa kāṣṭhāṃ kāmapi nīyate ।।
yatrānyathābhavat sarvamanyathaiva yathāruci ।
bhāvyate pratibhollekhamahattvena mahākaveḥ ।।
pratiyamānatā yatra vākyārthasya nibadhyate ।
vācyavācakavṛttibhyāṃ vyatiriktasya kasyacit ।।
svabhāvaḥ sarasākūto bhāvānāṃ yatra badhyate ।
kenāpi kamanīyena vaicitryeṇopabṛṃhitaḥ ।।
vicitro yatra vakroktivaicitryaṃ jīvitāyate ।
parisphurati yasyāntaḥ sā kāpyatiśayābhidhā ।।
so atiduḥsaṃcaro yena vidagdhakavayo gatāḥ ।
khaḍgadhārāpatheneva subhaṭānāṃ manorathāḥ ।।
