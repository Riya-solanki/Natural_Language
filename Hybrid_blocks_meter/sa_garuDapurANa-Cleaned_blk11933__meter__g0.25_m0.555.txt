tadudaramativegātsampraviśyāvahantīṃ jagadaghatatihantuḥ pādakiñjalkaśuddhām ।
nikhilamalanihantrīṃ darśanātsparśanācca sakṛdavagahanādvā bhaktidāṃ viṣṇupāde ।
