hasta-tyāga-maye navye saṃstavye gati-śikṣaṇe ।
putre skhalati sā jīyān mātuḥ putrasya ca tvarā ।। [anuṣṭubh]
dvitra-kramaṃ gataḥ kṛṣṇaś calitaḥ skhalane rudan ।
putra putreti cumbantīm ambām ālolayan muhuḥ ।। [anuṣṭubh]
kiñcid dūraṃ yad ānañca svaka-tejaḥ-prapañcakaḥ ।
sthirībhūya prasū-vaktraṃ sa-smitaṃ sa vyalokata ।। [anuṣṭubh]
dūraṃ mātur yadā yāti tadāsau mantharāyate ।
samīpaṃ tu yadā tarhi smayamāno drutāyate ।। [anuṣṭubh]
