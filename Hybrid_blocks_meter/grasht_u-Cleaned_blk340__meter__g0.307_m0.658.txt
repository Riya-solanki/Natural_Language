bāhyadrutiṃ praśaṃsannāha bāhyadrutir ityādi ।।
drutirdvidhoktā garbhadrutirbāhyadrutiśceti ।।
garbhadrutiḥ pūrvamuktā bāhyadrutir adhunābhidhīyate ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭā ativimalā nirmalatarā ।।
eṣā ca punaḥ keṣāṃcideva siddhānāṃ sphurati siddhā rasavidyāpāragā nityanāthādayaḥ teṣāṃ te jānantīti ।।
duṣprāpyatvāt bāhyadrutiriha viralā ।।
tebhyaḥ samyak jñātvā tathā tenaiva siddhodeśavidhānena drutayo bāhyadrutayaḥ ।।
