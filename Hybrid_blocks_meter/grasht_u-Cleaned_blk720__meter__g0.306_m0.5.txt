adhikadruter jāraṇād adhikaguṇe raso bhavatītyāha samajīrṇa iti ।।
iti samā tulyabhāgā drutirjīrṇā yasminniti ।।
drute dviguṇā yā drutiḥ tasyāścaraṇāt kramaśaḥ koṭivedhī koṭyaṃśena vedhakaḥ syāt ।।
drutibhāgo vṛddhau hyadhikaḥ syāditi vyaktiḥ ।।
