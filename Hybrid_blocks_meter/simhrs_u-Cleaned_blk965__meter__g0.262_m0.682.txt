atha cintā-
kenopāyena saṃsiddhiḥ kadā tasya samāgamaḥ । 
dūtī-mukhena kiṃ vācyam ity ādy ūhas tu cintanam ।।
atra nīvyādi-saṃsparśaḥ śayyāyāṃ parivartanam । 
sa-bāṣpākekarā dṛṣṭir mudrikādi-vivartanam ।
nirlakṣya-vīkṣaṇaṃ caivam ādyā vikṛtayo matāḥ ।।
