artha-śṛṅgāraḥ-
yatra kāmena sambaddhair arthair arthānubandhibhiḥ । 
bhujyamānaiḥ sukha-prāptir artha-śṛṅgāra īritaḥ ।।
sārvabhauma-phala-prāpti-hetunā vatsa-bhūpateḥ । 
ratnāvalyā samaṃ bhogo vijñeyā tad udāhṛtiḥ ।।
