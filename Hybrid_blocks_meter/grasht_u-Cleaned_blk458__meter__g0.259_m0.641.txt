mercury rañjana
triguṇena mākṣikeṇa tu kanakaṃ ca mṛtaṃ rasakatālayutam ।
paṭusahitaṃ tatpakvaṃ haṇḍikayā yāvadindragopanibham ।।
taccūrṇaṃ sūtavare triguṇaṃ cīrṇaṃ hi jīrṇaṃ tu ।
drutahemanibhaḥ sūto rañjati lohāni sarvāṇi ।।
