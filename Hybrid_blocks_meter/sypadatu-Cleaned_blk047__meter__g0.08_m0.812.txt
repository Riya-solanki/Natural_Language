[] vidhṛto asmi ciraṃ mṛgayamānaiḥ
api ca tato mayāvadhṛtam
gaṇikāyāḥ kāyasthān
kāyasthebhyaś ca vimṛśato gaṇikāḥ
gaṇikāyai dātavyaṃ
ratir api tāvad bhavaty asyām iti
diṣṭyā kāyasthavāgurātītaṃ bhavantam akṣataṃ paśyāmi sarvathā pratibuddho asi idānīm iyam āśīḥ
kalamadhuraraktakaṇṭhī
śayane madirālasā samadanā ca
vaktrāparavaktrābhyām
upatiṣṭhatu vāramukhyā tvām
eṣa satalaghātaṃ prahasya prasthitaḥ ito vayam aye ayam aparaḥ
srasteṣv aṅgeṣv āḍhakāṃl lāṭabhaktyā
dattvā citrān ko ayam āyāti mattaḥ
vibhrāntākṣo gaṇḍavichinnahāso
veśasvargaṃ kiṃkṛte ayaṃ praviṣṭaḥ
bhavatu vijñātam
śarkarapālasya gṛhe
jātaḥ kīreṇa carmakāreṇa
eṣa khalu koṅkaceṭyāṃ
