avidyāvatpuruṣaviṣayatvaṃ nātivartata iti yaduktaṃ tadeva sphuṭayati- tathā hi iti ।
varṇādhyāsaḥ arājā rājasūyena yajeta' ityādiḥ ।
āśramādhyāsaḥ agṛhasthaḥ sadṛśīṃ bhāryāṃ vindeta' ityādiḥ ।
vayo adhyāsaḥ akṛṣṇakeśo agnīnādadhīta' ityādiḥ ।
avasthādhyāsaḥ aapratisamādheyavyādhīnāṃ jalādipraveśena prāṇatyāgaḥ' ityādiḥ ।
ādigrahaṇaṃ pātakopapātakasaṃkarīkaraṇāpātrīkaraṇamalinīkaraṇādyadhyāsopasaṃgrahārtham ।
