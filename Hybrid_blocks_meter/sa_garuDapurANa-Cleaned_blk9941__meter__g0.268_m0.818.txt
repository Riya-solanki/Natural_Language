śrīgaruḍamahāpurāṇam- garuḍa uvāca । śrutā mayā dayāsindho hyajñānājjīvasaṃsṛtiḥ ।
adhunā śrotumicchāmi mokṣopāyaṃ sanātanam ।।
bhagavandevadeveśa śaraṇāgatavatsala ।
asāre ghorasaṃsāre sarvaduḥ khamalīmase ।।
