svarṇarūpyayoradhikāraviśeṣam āhādāv ityādi ।।
tato'nantaraṃ hemnaḥ svarṇasya piṣṭīṃ khalve mṛditāṃ vakṣyamāṇena iti śeṣaḥ ।।
tāṃ piṣṭīṃ rasaścarati ।।
kiṃ kṛtvā piṣṭīṃ dadyāt ādau prathamataḥ sūteśvare gaganamabhrakaṃ dattvā ।।
hemno'dhikāro darśitaḥ ।।
hemakarmaṇi hemaiva tārakarmaṇi tārameva dadyāt ।।
hemni viśeṣaḥ ।
svarṇaṃ pañcavidhaṃ proktaṃ prākṛtaṃ sahajāgnijam ।
etatsvarṇatrayaṃ caiva yojyaṃ ṣoḍaśavarṇakam ।।
khanijaṃ rasavādotthaṃ supattrīkṛtaśodhitam ।
taccaturdaśavarṇāḍhyaṃ manujānāṃ rujāpaham ।।
iti ।।
tāre'pi viśeṣaḥ ।
kailāse sahajaṃ rūpyaṃ khanijaṃ kṛtrimaṃ tathā ।
vyutkrameṇa guṇaiḥ śreṣṭhaṃ nāgottīrṇe rase hitam ।।
iti ।।
tāramapi pūrvavarṇaṃ cāryam ।।
