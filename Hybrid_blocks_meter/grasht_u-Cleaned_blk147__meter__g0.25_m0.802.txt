āranāla for jāraṇa
sarjīkṣitikhagaṭaṅkaṇalavaṇānvitam arkabhājane tridinam ।
paryuṣitamāranālaṃ gaganādiṣu bhāvane śastam ।।
tasminnāgaṃ śuddhaṃ pradrāvya niṣecayecchataṃ vārān ।
vaṅgaṃ vā tāravidhau rasāyane naiva tadyojyam ।।
gaganarasoparasāmṛtaloharasāyasādicūrṇāni ।
sarvamanena ha bhāvyaṃ yatkiṃcit cāraṇāvastu ।।
