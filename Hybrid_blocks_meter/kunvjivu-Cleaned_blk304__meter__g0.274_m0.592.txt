iyamaparā varṇavinyāsavakratā tridhā tribhiḥ prakārairukteti "ca'-śabdenābhisambandhaḥ ।
ke punarasyāstrayaḥ prakārā ityāhavargāntayoginaḥ sparśāḥ ।
sparśāḥ kādayo makāraparyantā vargāstadantaiḥ ṅakārādibhiryogaḥ saṃyogo yeṣāṃ te tathoktāḥ, punaḥ punarbadhyamānāḥprathamaḥ prakāraḥ ।
ta-la-nādayaḥ takāra-lakāra-nakāra-prabhṛtayo dviruktā dviruccāritā dviguṇāḥ santaḥ, punaḥ punarbadhyamānāḥdvitīyaḥ ।
tadvyatiriktāḥ śiṣṭāśca vyañjanasaṃjñā ye varṇāste rephaprabhṛtibhiḥ saṃyuktāḥ punaḥ punarbadhyamānāḥtṛtīyaḥ ।
svalpāntarāḥ parimitavyavahitā iti sarveṣāmabhisabandhaḥ ।
te ca kīdṛśāḥprastutaucityaśobhinaḥ ।
prastutaṃ varṇyamānaṃ vastu tasya yadaucityamucitabhāvastena śobhante ye te yathoktāḥ ।
na punarvarṇasāvarṇyavyasanitāmātreṇopanibaddhāḥ prastutaucityamlānatvakāriṇaḥ ।
prastutaucityaśobhitvāt kutracitparuṣarasaprastāve tādṛśānevābhyanujānāti ।
atha prathamaprākārodāharaṇaṃ yathā
