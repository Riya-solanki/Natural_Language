atha mārdavam-
spṛṣṭaṃ yatrāṅgam aspṛṣṭam iva syān mārdavaṃ hi tat ।। athā-
yābhyāṃ dukūlāntara-lakṣitābhyāṃ
visraṃsate snaigdhya-guṇena dṛṣṭiḥ ।
nirmāṇa-kāle' pi tatas tad-ūrvoḥ
saṃsparśa-śaṅkā na vidheḥ karābhyām ।।
