nanu satyapi vidheyatve vidheḥ kratvarthātpratiṣedhasya ca puruṣārthatvādviṣayabhedenāvirodhānna bādhaḥ ।
nahīdaṃ viruddhaṃ kratorhiṃsayā bhavitavyaṃ puruṣasya tu tadvarjaneneti ।
satyam ।
aṅgavidhimātrālocanāyāṃ nāsti virodhaḥ ।
jyotiṣṭomavidhistu sāṅgaṃ pradhānaṃ puruṣasya vidadhatpuruṣārthāhiṃsāniṣedhamagnīṣomīyahiṃsāyāmavatarantaṃ niruṇaddhiḥ ।
nahyagnīṣomīyahiṃsāṃ varjayatā
