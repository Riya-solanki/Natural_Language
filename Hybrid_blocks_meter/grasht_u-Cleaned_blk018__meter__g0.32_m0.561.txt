mūrchitabaddhamṛtasyāvasthayā trividhaṃ sūtarājasya bandhanaṃ praśaṃsati kaviḥ rasa ityādi ।।
bho janāḥ rasabandhaḥ pāradabandhanaṃ dhanyaḥ yasya prārambhe satataṃ nirantaraṃ karuṇā jāyata iti śeṣaḥ ।।
kiṃrūpā ahaṃ govindanāmā rase siddhe sati samyagbandhanatvaṃ prāpte sati mahīṃ medinīṃ nirjarāmaraṇaṃ yathā tathā kariṣye ।।
atisāmīpyādvartamāna eva ḷṭ ।।
nirjarāmaraṇamiti kriyāviśeṣaṇam ।।
jarā pālityaṃ maraṇaṃ prāṇatyāga ābhyāṃ rahitaṃ yathā mahyāṃ jarāmaraṇaṃ na yuktam ।।
atra mahīpadena mahīmadhikṛtya nivasanti ye manujādayas ta eva lakṣaṇāvṛttitvāt ।।
punā rasāyanavaśājjarāniṣedho bhaved iti yuktam ।।
hitopadeśe yathā ।
yajjarāvyādhividhvaṃsi bheṣajaṃ tad rasāyanam ।
ādye vayasi madhye vā śuddhakāyaḥ samācared ।
iti ।।
maraṇaniṣedhaḥ kathamauṣadhena anyauṣadhiśaktihrāsato na yuktaḥ raseśvaraśaktyādhikyād yuktaḥ ।।
rasaratnākare yathā ।
mṛtyorjarāviṣadharasya ca vainateya tubhyaṃ namāmi suravanditasūtarāja ।
iti ।।
