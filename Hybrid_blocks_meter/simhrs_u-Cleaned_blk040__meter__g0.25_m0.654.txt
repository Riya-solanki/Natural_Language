tatra nāyakaḥ
ālambanaṃ mataṃ tatra nāyako guṇavān pumān ।
tad-guṇās tu mahā-bhāgyam audāryaṃ sthairya-dakṣate ।।
aujjvalyaṃ dhārmikatvaṃ ca kulīnatvaṃ ca vāgmitā ।
kṛtajñatvaṃ nayajñatvaṃ śucitā māna-śālitā ।।
tejasvitā kalāvattvaṃ prajā-rañjakatādayaḥ ।
ete sādhāraṇāḥ proktāḥ nāyakasya guṇā budhaiḥ ।।
