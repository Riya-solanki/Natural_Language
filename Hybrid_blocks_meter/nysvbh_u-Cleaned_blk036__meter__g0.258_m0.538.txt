atha śubhā, śarīreṇa dānaṃ paritrāṇaṃ paraciraṇaṃ ca।
vācā satyaṃ hitaṃ priyaṃ svādhyāyaṃ ceti।
manasā dayāmaspṛhāṃ śraddhāṃ ceti।
seyaṃ dharmāya।
atra pravattisādhanau dharmādharmauṃ pravṛttiśabdenoktau, yathānnasādhanāḥ prāṇāḥ, annaṃ vai prāṇinaḥ prāṇāḥ iti।
seyaṃ pravṛttiḥ kutsitasyābhipūjitasya ca janmanaḥ kāraṇam।
janma punaḥ śarīrendriyabuddhivedanānāṃ nikāyaviśiṣṭaḥ prādurbhāvaḥ।
tasmin sati duḥkham, tatpunaḥ pratikūlavedanīyaṃ bādhanā pīḍā tāpa iti।
ta hame mithyājñānādayo duḥkhāntā dharmā avicchedena pravartamānāḥ saṃsāra iti।।
