abhidhyopadeśaścātmanaḥ kartṛtvaprakṛtitve gamayati aso akāmayata bahu syāṃ prajāyeyeti' atadaikṣata bahu syāṃ prajāyeya' iti ca ।
tatrābhidhyānapūrvikāyāḥ svātantryapravṛtteḥ karteti gamyate ।
bahu syāmiti pratyagātmaviṣayatvādbahubhavanābhidhyānasyaprakṛtirityapi gamyate ।।
