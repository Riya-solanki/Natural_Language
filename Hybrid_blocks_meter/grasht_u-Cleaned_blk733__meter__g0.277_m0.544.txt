siddhatailakṛtyamāha paṭetyādi ।।
tatsiddhatailaṃ paṭagālitaṃ vastrapūtaṃ gṛhītvā tadanu tatpaścāt ।।
sampūrṇadīrghamūṣāyāṃ gostanākārāyāṃ taptataile koṣṇasāraṇataile sūtaṃ kṣipet ।।
kiṃ kṛtvā bījaṃ samaṃ samabhāgaṃ rasatulyaṃ yadbhāvyaṃ mahābījaṃ tat bhāvitaṃ kṛtvetyarthaḥ ।।
sāraṇayantrasya vidhānamāha mūṣetyādi ।
samakālam ekakālaṃ yathā syāttathā bījaṃ mūṣāntarnikṣipya tato'nantaraṃ mūṣāvaktraṃ sthagayet ācchādayet ।।
kena tailārdrapaṭena sāraṇatailārdravastreṇa ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭena latādvayena vastracīradvayena protaṃ ca tadvitataṃ vistīrṇaṃ ca tat naddhaṃ baddhaṃ tena ।।
akṣīyamāṇo milati na kṣīyata ityarthaḥ ।।
sūte milati baddho jñeyaḥ bījaiḥ saha milito baddho bhavatītyarthaḥ ।।
pītādivarṇakathanenāpi kartuṃ sūcitam ।।
