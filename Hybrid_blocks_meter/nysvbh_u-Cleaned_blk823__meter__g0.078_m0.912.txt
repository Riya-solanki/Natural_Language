asphaṭikasyābhedenāvasthitasyopadhānabhedānnānātvābhimānaḥ'
ityayamavidyamānahetukaḥ pakṣaḥ, kasmāt ?
sphaṭikepyaparāparotpatteḥ
usphaṭike apy anyā vyaktaya utpadyante anyā nirudhyante
iti, katham ? kṣaṇikatvād vyaktīnām, kṣaṇaścālpīyān
kālaḥ kṣaṇasthitikāḥ kṣaṇikāḥ।
kathaṃ punargamyate kṣaṇikā vyaktayaḥ ? iti,
upacayāpacayaprabandhadarśanāt
śarīrādiṣu
upaktinirvṛttasyāhārarasasya śarīre
rudhirādibhāvenopacayo apacayaśca prabandhena
pravartate upacayād vyaktīnāmutpādaḥ apacayād
vyaktinirodhaḥ evaṃ ca satyavayavapariṇāmabhedena
vṛddhiḥ śarīrasya kālāntare gṛhyate iti
soyaṃ vyaktiviśeṣadharmo vyaktimātre veditavya
iti ।।
