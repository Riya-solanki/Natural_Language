yathānavasthāyināṃ
varṇānāṃ śravaṇaṃ bhavatyevameṣāṃ vikāro
bhavatīti ।।
asaṃbandhādasamarthā।
arthapratipādikā varṇopalabdhirna vikāreṇa saṃbandhādasamarthā
yā gṛhyamāṇā varṇavikāramanumāpayediti,
tatra yādṛgidam yathā gandhaguṇā pṛthivī evaṃ
śabdasukhādiguṇāpīti tādṛgetad bhavatīti।
naca varṇopalabdhirvarṇanivṛttau varṇāntaraprayogasya
nirvartikā, yo ayamivarṇanivṛttau yakārasya
prayogaḥ yadyayamivarṇopalabdhyā nirvartate
tadā tatropalabhyamāna ivarṇo yatvamāpadyate
iti gṛhyeta ?, tasmādvarṇopalabdhiraheturvarṇavikārasyeti
।।
