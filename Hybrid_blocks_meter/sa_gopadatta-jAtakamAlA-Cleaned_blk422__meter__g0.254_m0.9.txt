ete parvataśṛṅgacandanatarucchāyāsthalaṃ saṃsṛtāḥ
karṇaprāvaraṇaṃ navāruṇakaracchāyāsamānaśriyaḥ ।
prekṣante madavārilolamadhuliṭprollīḍhagaṇḍasthalaṃ
