ittham ityanenoktaprakāreṇa mantrātmako vigraho yasya sa bhagavān itthaṃvigrahaḥ ।। 
aneneti ।
karaṇaṃ ca na śaktyanyat ।। 
iti prāguktenātmanā apratihatasāmarthyena karaṇena sthityādikāryajātaṃ sarvakālaṃ sarvaṃ nirvartayati ।। 
na paraṃ yugapatkaroti yāvadyadā yadupapadyate tattadā karoti krameṇāpi sarvaṃ kāryaṃ karotītyarthaḥ ।। 
taduktaṃ prāk ।
kramākramasamutpatteḥ kramādyutpattiśaktimat ।। 
iti ।। 
atha prathamataḥ parameśvaraḥ kiṃ karotītyāha ।।
