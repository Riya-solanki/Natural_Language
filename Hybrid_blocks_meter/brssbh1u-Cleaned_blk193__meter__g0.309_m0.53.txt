nāyaṃ doṣaḥ ।
prakaraṇādbrahmāvagame satyanayoḥ śabdayoraviśeṣakatvāt ।
dīpyamānakāryajyotirupalakṣite brahmaṇyapi prayogasaṃbhavāt ।
ayena sūryastapati tejaseddhaḥ' iti ca mantravarṇāt ।
yadvā nāyaṃ jyotiḥśabdaścakṣurvṛtterevānugrāhake tejasi vartate, anyatrāpi prayogadarśanāt ।
avācaivāyaṃ jyotiṣāste', amano jyotirjuṣatām' iti ca, tasmādyadyatkasyacidavabhāsakaṃ tattajyotiḥśabdenābhidhīyate ।
tathā sati brahmaṇo api caitanyarūpasya samastajagadavabhāsahetutvādupapanno jyotiḥśabdaḥ ।
atameva bhāntamanubhāti sarvaṃ tasya bhāsā sarvamidaṃ vibhāti' ataddevā jyotiṣāṃ jyotirāyurhepāsate amṛtam' ityādiśrutibhyaśca ।
yadapyuktaṃ dyumaryādatvaṃ sarvagatasya brahmaṇo nopapadyata iti ।
atrocyate- sarvagatasyāpi brahmaṇa upāsanārthaḥ pradeśaviśeṣaparigraho na virudhyate ।
