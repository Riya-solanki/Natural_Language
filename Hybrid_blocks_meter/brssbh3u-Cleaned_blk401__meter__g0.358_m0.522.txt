naiṣāmupasaṃhāro vidyāsviti ।
kasmāt ।
vedhārthabhedāt ।
hṛdayaṃ pravidhyetyevañjātīyakānāṃ hi mantrāṇāṃ yer'thā hṛdayavedhādayo bhinnā anabhisaṃbaddhāsta upaniṣaduditābhirvidyābhiḥ ।
na teṣāṃ tābhiḥ saṃgantuṃ sāmarthyamasti ।
