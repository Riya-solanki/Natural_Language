atha geya-padam-
vīṇādi-vādanenaiva sahitaṃ yatra bhāvyate ।
lalitaṃ nāyikā-gītaṃ tad geya-padam ucyate ।। 
cañcat-puṭādinā vākyābhinayo nāyikā-kṛtaḥ ।
bhūmi-cārī-pracāreṇa sthita-pāṭhyaṃ tad ucyate ।। 
bhrū-netra-pāṇi-caraṇa-vilāsābhinayānvitam ।
yojyam āsīnayā pāṭhyam āsīnaṃ tad udāhṛtam ।। 
nānā-vidhena vādyena nānā-tāla-layānvitam ।
lāsyaṃ prayujyate yatra sā jñeyā puṣpa-gandhikā ।। 
anyāsaṅgama-śaṅkinyā nāyakasyātiroṣayā ।
prema-ccheda-prakaṭanaṃ lāsyaṃ pracchedakaṃ viduḥ ।। 
aniṣṭhura-ślakṣṇa-padaṃ sama-vṛttair alaṅkṛtam ।
nāṭyaṃ puruṣa-bhāvāḍhyaṃ trimūḍhakam udāhṛtam ।। 
deśa-bhāṣā-viśeṣeṇa calad-valaya-śṛṅkhalam ।
lāsyaṃ prayujyate yatra tat saindhavam iti smṛtam ।। 
cārībhir lalitābhiś ca citrārthābhinayānvitam ।
spaṣṭa-bhāva-rasopetaṃ lāsyaṃ yat tad dvimūḍhakam ।। 
aparijñāta-pārśvasthaṃ geya-bhāva-vibhūṣitam ।
lāsyaṃ sotkaṇṭha-vākyaṃ tad uttamottamakaṃ bhavet ।। 
kopa-prasāda-janitaṃ sādhikṣepa-padāśrayam ।
vākyaṃ tad ukta-pratyuktaṃ yūnoḥ praśnottarātmakam ।। 
śṛṅgāra-mañjarī-mukhyam asyodāharaṇaṃ matam ।
lāsyāṅga-daśakaṃ tatra lakṣyaṃ lakṣya-vicakṣaṇaiḥ ।।
