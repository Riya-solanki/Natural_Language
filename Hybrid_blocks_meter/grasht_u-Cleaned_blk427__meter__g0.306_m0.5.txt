abhrasatvādīnāṃ yoge rase vyavasthāmāha balamityādi ।।
abhrakasatve'dhikaraṇe balamāste abhrasatvasaṃyogena raso balamāpnotītyarthaḥ ।।
punastīkṣṇe lohabhede jāraṇarāgā jāraṇena tīkṣṇasthā rāgāḥ pratiṣṭhitā bhavantītyarthaḥ ।।
sāraṇamatha nāgavaṅgābhyām iti nāgavaṅgābhyāṃ duḥsaraṇaṃ sāraṇadravyaṃ sarata iti ।।
