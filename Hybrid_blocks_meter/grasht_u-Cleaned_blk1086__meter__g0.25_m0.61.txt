rasavādasyānantatvaṃ sūcayannāha rasetyādi ।।
rasavādo anantaguṇaḥ anantā guṇā yasya sa tathoktaḥ ।।
anantā aparimitāḥ ।।
kena kṛtvā rasavādo'nantaḥ dravagolakakalkānāṃ pratyekamanantatvāt rasavādo'pyanantaḥ ।।
kiṃbhūtaḥ pradhānasiddhaiḥ nityanāthādibhiḥ kalitaḥ racitaḥ ।।
yairmahadbhiḥ siddhaiḥ rasavādo dṛṣṭaste narā jayanti sarvotkarṣeṇa vartante ।।
