atā āpa aikṣanta bahvayaḥ syāma prajāyemahīti tā annamasṛjanta' iti śrūyate ।
tatra saṃśayaḥ - kimanenānnaśabdena vrīhiyavādyabhyavahāryai caudanādyucyate kiṃ vā pṛthivīti ।
tatra prāptaṃ tāvadvrīhiyavādyodanādi vā parigrahītavyamiti ।
tatra hyannaśabdaḥ prasiddho loke vākyaśeṣo apyetamarthamupodvalayati ।
atasmādyatra kvaca varṣati tadeva bhūyiṣṭamannaṃ bhavatīti' ।
vrīhiyavādyeva hi sati varṣaṇe bahu bhavati na pṛthivīti ।
evaṃ prāpte brūmaḥ pṛthivyeveyamannaśabdenādbhyo jāyamānā vivakṣyata iti ।
kasmāt ।
adhikārādrūpācchabdāntarācca ।
adhikārastāvat- atattejo asṛjata' atadapo asṛjata' iti mahābhūtaviṣayo vartate ।
tatra kramaprāptāṃ pṛthivīṃ mahābhūtaṃ vilaṅghya nākasmādvrīhyādiparigraho nyāyyaḥ ।
tathā rūpamapi vākyaśeṣe pṛthivyanuguṇaṃ dṛśyate ayatkṛṣṇaṃ tadannasya' iti ।
nahyodanāderabhyavahāryasya kṛṣṇatvaniyamo asti ।
nāpi vrīhyādīnām ।
