sāraṇakramasya guṇānāha śatetyādi ।।
yaḥ sāryaḥ samasāritaḥ sa śatavedhī syād ityabhiprāyaḥ ।।
punaḥ pratisāritaḥ dviguṇabījena sārito yaḥ sūtaḥ sa sahasravedhī syāt ।।
ca punaḥ anusāritaḥ triguṇabījena sāritaḥ sa ayutena ayutavedhī syāditi vyaktiḥ ।।
vidhinā ityuktavidhānena ।।
balābalaṃ jñātvā nyūnādhikyaṃ matvā vidhinā yukta iti śeṣaḥ ।।
