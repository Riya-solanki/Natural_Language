dhṛtiḥ śraddhā sukhecchā vividiṣā tadabhāvakau ।
sāmānyataḥ samuddiṣṭāḥ pañcaite karmayonayaḥ ।।
vāci karmaṇi saṃkalpe pratītiryābhirajyate ।
tanniṣṭhastatpratiṣṭhaśca dhṛteretaddhi lakṣaṇam ।।
anasūyā brahmacaryaṃ yajanaṃ yājanaṃ tapaḥ ।
pratigrahaśca homaśca śraddhāyā lakṣaṇaṃ smṛtam ।।
sukhaṃ sukhārthī seveta vidyā karma tapāṃsi ca ।
prāyaścittaparo nityaṃ sukheyaṃ parikīrttitā ।।
viṣadhūmamūrchitavad vividiṣā dhyānināṃ prajñāyoniḥ ।
ekatvaṃ ca pṛthaktvaṃ ca nityaṃ vedamacetanam ।।
sūkṣmasatkāryasaṃkṣobhyaṃ jñeyā vividiṣā hi sā ।
kāryakaraṇakṣamakarī vividiṣā prākṛtī vṛttiḥ ।।
dhṛtiḥ śraddhā vividiṣā sukhā ceti catuṣṭayam ।
jñeyaṃ bandho vividiṣā mokṣāya parikalpitā ।
karmayonisvarūpo ayaṃ viśiṣya parikīrttitaḥ ।।
