bhāvayannirvidyate, nirviṇṇasya vairāgyam, viraktasyāpavarga iti।
janmamaraṇaprabandhocchedaḥ sarvaduḥkhaprahāṇamapavarga iti।।
astyanyadapi dravyaguṇakarmasāmānyaviśeṣasamavāyāḥ prameyam, tadbhedena cāparisaḍkhyeyam।
asya tu tattvajñānādapavargo mithyājñānātsaṃsāra ityata etadupadiṣṭaṃ viśeṣeṇeti ।।
