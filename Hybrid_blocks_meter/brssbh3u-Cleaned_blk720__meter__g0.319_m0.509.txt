yasminpakṣe bhedadarśanaprābalyānna prāpnoti tasminneṣa vidhiriti ।
vidhyādivat ।
yathā - adarśapūrṇamāsābhyāṃ svargakāmo yajeta' ityevañjātīyake vidhyādau sahakāritvenāgnyanbādhānādikamaṅgajātaṃ vidhīyate ।
evamavipradhāne apyasminvidyāvākye maunavidhirityarthaḥ ।।
