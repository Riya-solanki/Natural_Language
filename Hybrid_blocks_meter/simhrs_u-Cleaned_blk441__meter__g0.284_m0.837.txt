athārabhaṭī -
māyendra-jāla-pracurāṃ citra-yuddha-kriyā-mayīm । 
chedyair bhedyaiḥ plutair yuktāṃ vṛttim ārabhaṭīṃ viduḥ ।।
aṅgāny asyās tu catvāri saṅkṣiptir avapātanam । 
vastūtthāpana-sampheṭāv iti pūrve babhāṣire ।।
