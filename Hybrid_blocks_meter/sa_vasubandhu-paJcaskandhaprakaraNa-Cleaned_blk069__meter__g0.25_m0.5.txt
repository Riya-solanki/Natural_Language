āśraddhyaṃ katamat । karmaphalasatyaratneṣvanabhisaṃpratyayaḥ । śraddhāvipakṣaścetaso aprasādaḥ ।।
kauśiddyaṃ katamatṃḥ kuśale cetaso anabhyutsāho vīryavipakṣa ।।
pramādaḥ katamaḥ । yā lobhadveṣamohekauśīddyaiścitasyānārakṣā kuśalasyābhāvanā ।।
