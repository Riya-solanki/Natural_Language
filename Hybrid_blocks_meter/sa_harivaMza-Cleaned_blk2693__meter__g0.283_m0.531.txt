kṛṣṇo api gopasthito viveśa vrajam eva ha 
giriyajñapravṛttena tenāścaryeṇa vismitaḥ ।। 
gopāḥ sabālavṛddhā vai tuṣṭuvur mudhusūdanam । nṛtyaṃ gītaṃ prakurvāṇāḥ sahakṛṣṇā vrajaṃ yayuḥ । 
mahe pratihate śakraḥ sakrodhas tridaśeśvaraḥ 
saṃvartakaṃ nāma gaṇaṃ toyadānām athābravīt ।।
