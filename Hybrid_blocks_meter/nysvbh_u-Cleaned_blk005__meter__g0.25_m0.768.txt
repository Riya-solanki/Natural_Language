kiṃ punastatvam ? sataśca sadbhāvo asataścāsadbhāva iti।
satsaditi gṛhyamāṇaṃ yathābhūtamaviparītaṃ tatvaṃ bhavati।
asaccāsaditi gṛhyamāṇaṃ yathābhūtamaviparītaṃ tacvaṃ bhavati।
kathaṃ punaruttarasya pramāṇenopalabdhiriti ?
satyupalabhyamāne tadvadanupalabdheḥ pradīpavat।
yathā darśakena pradīpena dṛśye guhyamāṇe tadiva yanna gṛhyate tatrāsti।
