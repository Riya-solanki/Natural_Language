tatraiṣāṃ hṛdayāni bhavanti ।
oṃ prajvalita pradīptavajra hūṃ ।।
oṃ vajrasaumya hūṃ ।।oṃ vajradaṇḍa hūṃ ।।oṃ vajravikṛta huṃ ।।
tatastu vajravegena likhed dvāracatuṣṭaye ।
vajraśauṇḍādayaḥ sarve yathāvadanupūrvaśaḥ ।।
