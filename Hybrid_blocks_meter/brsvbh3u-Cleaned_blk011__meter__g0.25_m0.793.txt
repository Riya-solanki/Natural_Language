sūtrāntaramavatārayituṃ śaṅkate-kathaṃ punariti ।
somaṃ rājānamāpyāyasvāpakṣīyasvetyevamenāṃstatra bhakṣayantīti ।
kriyāsamabhihāreṇāpyāyanāpakṣayau yathā somasya tathā bhakṣayanti somamayāṃllokānityarthaḥ ।
