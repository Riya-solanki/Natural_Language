nāstikāḥ śaucarahitāḥ kṛpaṇā bhogavarjitāḥ ।
steyānṛtakathāyuktā narāḥ śaṅkhaśritā bale ।।
ityevaṃ kathitastubhyaṃ teṣāṃ dānava nirṇayaḥ ।।
ahaṃ sā rāgiṇī nāma jāyaśrīstvāmupāgatā ।
mamāsti dāvanapate pratijñā sādhusaṃmatā ।।
