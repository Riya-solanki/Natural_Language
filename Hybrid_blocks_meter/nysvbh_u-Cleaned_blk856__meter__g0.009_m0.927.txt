"tadātmaguṇatvepi tulyam "
ityetadanena samuccīyate, dvividho hi guṇanāśahetuḥ
guṇānāmāśrayābhāvo virodhī ca guṇaḥ,
nityatvādātmano anupapannaḥ pūrvaḥ, virodhī
ca buddherguṇo na gṛhyate tasmādātmaguṇatve
sati buddhernityatvaprasaṅgaḥ ।।
