atra vipralambhatvādarthasya mṛdutvam । bandhasya saṃyuktavarṇahrasvākṣaraprāyatvenorjitatvādīṣadgāḍhatvam ।
madyamārabhaṭī prauḍhe apyarthe nātimṛdukramā ।।
yathā
saṃvartapāvakaśikhā kimu kiṃ nu jihvā mṛtyoriyaṃ kimu yugāntakṛtāntadaṃṣṭrā ।
ityūhyate samarasīmaniṣevyadeśe śrīsiṅgabhūpakarakampitakhaḍgarekhā ।।
