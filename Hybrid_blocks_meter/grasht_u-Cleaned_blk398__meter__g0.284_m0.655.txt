jāraṇāyāṃ vidhidvayamāha evamityādi ।।
evamamunā prakāreṇa kacchapayantre nidhāya jīryati grāsaṃ rasa iti śeṣaḥ ।।
yathā raso na kṣayati kṣayaṃ nāpnoti tathā kāryo vidhiḥ iti śeṣaḥ ।।
kāryasyāśrayarakṣaṇāyeti bhāvaḥ ।।
punaḥ kṣārabiḍaiḥ kṣayameti nāśamāpnoti grāsa ityadhyāhāraḥ ।।
punaḥ sa rasa uparasairgandhādibhiḥ grāsaṃ utprāvalyena girati gilatītyarthaḥ ।।
kṣārairamlaiśca gandhādyair mūtraiḥ paṭubhireva ca ।
rasagrāsasya jīrṇārthaṃ biḍaḥ sa parikīrtitaḥ ।
iti paribhāṣā ।।
biḍaśabdena piṇḍaḥ ।।
