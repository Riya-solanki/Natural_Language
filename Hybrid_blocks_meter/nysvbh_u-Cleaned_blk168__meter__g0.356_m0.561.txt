yasyārthasya siddhāvanye arthā anuṣajyante, na tairvinā so arthaḥ sidhyati te arthā yadadhiṣṭhānāḥ, so adhikaraṇasiddhāntaḥ।
yathā dehendriyavyatirikto jñātā darśanasparśanābhyāmekārthagrahaṇāditi।
atrānuṣaṅgiṇo arthā
indriyanānātvam, niyataviṣayāṇīndriyāṇi, svaviṣayagrahaṇaliṅgāni jñāturjñānasādhanāni, gandhādiguṇavyatiriktaṃ dravyam, guṇādhikaraṇamaniyataviṣayāścetanā iti।
pūrvārthasiddhāvete arthāḥ sidhyanti।
na tairvinā so arthaḥ sambhavatīti।।
