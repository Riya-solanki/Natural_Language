praṇamāmi parāṃ devīṃ mūlādhāradhṛtodayām ।
yādvivarttamimaṃ kṛtsnnaṃ prapañcaṃ paricakṣate ।
āsīt kapiñjalakulakṣīrākūpāracandramāḥ trikaliṅgadhipadharādhāmadhīsacivaḥ kṛtī ।
aśeṣabhāṣāramaṇībhujaṅgaḥ sāhityavidyarṇavakarṇadhāraḥ ।
dhvanyadhvaniprauḍhadhiyāṃ purogaḥ śrīvaśvanāthaḥ kavicakavartto ।
svalpākṣaraḥ subodhārthaḥ pradhvastāśeṣaḍhūṣaṇaḥ ।
sāhityadarpaṇo nāma granthastena vinirmitaḥ ।
pāraṃ sāhityavidyābdhergantuṃ vāñchanti ye kṣitau kṛtireṣā taristeṣāṃ viśvanāthamahākaveḥ ।
śravyābhineyālaṅkāratattvaṃ satkavisammatam ।
yadihāsti tadanyatra yannehāsti na tat kvacit ।
