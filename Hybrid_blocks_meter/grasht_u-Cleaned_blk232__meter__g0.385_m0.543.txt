mākṣikasatvaṃ mukhyatvenāha mākṣiketyādi ।।
sūtakaḥ pārado ghanasatvaṃ abhrasatvaṃ nikhilaṃ samastaṃ carati ।।
kasmāt mākṣikasatve yogāt mākṣikaṃ svarṇamākṣikaṃ tatsatve yo yogastasmāt ।।
niyataṃ niścitam ।।
mākṣikasattvayogād ghanasattvaṃ carati raso garbhadrāvī garbhe drāvayati sattvaṃ dravarūpaṃ vidhatte yaḥ sa tathoktaḥ ।।
sūtasyodare mākṣikābhrasatvaṃ drutirūpaṃ tiṣṭhatītyarthaḥ ।।
evamamunā vidhānena saha sūtakaḥ pārado rajyate rāgavān bhavati badhyate baddhaśca bhavatītyarthaḥ ।।
