ucyate- satyamevametat ।
tathāpi mandadhiyāṃ śabdamātrakṛtāśaṅkānivṛttyartho ayamatideśaḥ kriyate ।
saṃvargavidyādiṣu hyupāsyatayā vāyormahābhāgatvaśravaṇāt, astamayapratiṣedhādibhyaśca bhavati nityatvāśaṅkā kasyaciditi ।।
