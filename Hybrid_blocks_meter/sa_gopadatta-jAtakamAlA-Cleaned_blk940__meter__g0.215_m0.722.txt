te rājāntararakṣiṇām api śirasy ādhāya pādaṃ khagāḥ 
kṣudduḥkhopanipātavismṛtabhayā hṛtvāmiṣaṃ bhuñjate ।
vegenābhipatanti śastrakalilaprākāraduḥsaṃcarāñ
śūlāmāṃsalavābhilāṣakṛpaṇā gacchanti ke cit kṣayam ।।
