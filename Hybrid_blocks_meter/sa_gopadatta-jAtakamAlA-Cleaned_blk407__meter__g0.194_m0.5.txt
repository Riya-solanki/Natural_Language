ye santo hitavādināṃ sphuṭadhiyāṃ saṃpādayante giraḥ
śreyas te samavāpnuvanti niyataṃ kravyādapuryāṃ yathā ।
ye tūtsṛjya mahārthasāradayitāṃ vācaṃ śrayante anyathā
dustare vyasanodadhau nipatitāḥ śocanti te ahaṃ yathā ।।
