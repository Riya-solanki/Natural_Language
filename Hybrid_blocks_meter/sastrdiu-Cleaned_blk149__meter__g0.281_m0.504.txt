āhaca- āvirbhāvatirobhāvadharmakeṣvanuyāyi yat ।।
taddharmi yatra ca jñānaṃ prāgdharmagrahaṇādbhavediti ।
tathā- yādṛśamasmābhirabhihitaṃ dravyaṃ tādṛśasyaiva hi sarvasya guṇa eva bhidyate na svarūpamiti ।
tatsiddhaṃ dravyam ।
tacca jātyādibhiḥ pañcadhā savikalpena vibhajyate ।
ataḥ savikalpakamapi pratyakṣaṃ, tatpūrvakaṃ cānumānādīti siddhaṃ teṣāmapyanimittatvamiti pratyakṣasūtram ।
