samāsoktirityādi ।
yeyaṃ "samāsoktiḥ" alaṅkṛtira "nālaṅkāratayā matā" na vibhūṣaṇabhāvena pratibhātā, yasmāt pṛthaṅ nopapadyate ।
kena hetunā "alaṅkārāntaratvena" vibhūṣaṇāntarabhāvena ।
"śobhāśūnyatayā tathā" kāntirahitatvena ca ।
tadidamuktaṃ bhavatiyadi rāmaṇīyakatvaṃ saṃbhavati prakarāntaravihite tasmin bhūṣaṇāntare antarbhavati ।
ramaṇīyatvābhāve śobhāśūnyatvaṃ paryavasyati tasyāḥ ।
tathā ca lakṣaṇodāharaṇe
