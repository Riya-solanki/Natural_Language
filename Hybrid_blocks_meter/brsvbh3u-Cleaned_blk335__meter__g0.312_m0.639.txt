ārtvijyam ity auḍulomiḥ tasmai hi parikrīyate ।
upākhyānāttāvadupāsanamaudgātramavagamyate ।
tadbalavati sati bādhake anyathopapādanīyam ।
na cartvikkartṛka upāsane yajamānagāmitā phalasyāsaṃbhavinī tena hi sa parikrītastadgāmino phalāya ghaṭate ।
tasmānna vyasanitāmātreṇopākhyānamanyathayituṃ yuktamiti rāddhāntaḥ ।।
