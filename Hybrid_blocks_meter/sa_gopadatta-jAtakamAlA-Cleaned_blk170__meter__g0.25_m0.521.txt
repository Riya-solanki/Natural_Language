ye janmaivādhimuktāḥ śamasukhamahatīṃ nirvṛtiṃ śuddhasattvā
yeṣāṃ kleśāvaśeṣo apy upaśamitabalo yo apy upādānam eva ।
ātmatvenābhyupetā jagad idam akhilaṃ ye kṛpākrāntacittās
teṣāṃ ślāghyā pravṛttir gatiṣu na tu bhavāsvādaparyastabuddhiḥ ।।
