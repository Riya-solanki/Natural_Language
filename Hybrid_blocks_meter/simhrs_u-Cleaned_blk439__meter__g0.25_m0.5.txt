pūrva-sthito vipadyeta nāyako yatra cāparas tiṣṭhet । 
tam apīha narma-garbhaṃ pravadati bharato hi nāṭya-veda-guruḥ ।। athā-
mayena nirmitāṃ laṅkāṃ labdhvā mandodarīm api ।
reme mūrtāṃ daśagrīva-lakṣmīm iva vibhīṣaṇaḥ ।।
