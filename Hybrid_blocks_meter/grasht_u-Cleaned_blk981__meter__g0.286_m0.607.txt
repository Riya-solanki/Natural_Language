taccāha trikaṭuketyādi ।।
vyoma abhrakaṃ trikaṭukaviḍaṅgatriphalāmākṣikaśilājatuyutaṃ pippalīmaricanāgaraharītakīvibhītakāmalakakṣaudraśilājatumilitaṃ aśnīyād bhakṣayet ।।
punaḥ kṣīraudanaṃ pathyamaśnīyāt ।।
eva kṛte sati jantuḥ śataṃ jīvati jantujanyuśarīriṇaḥ ityamaraḥ ।।
iti patrābhrakaprakriyā ।।
