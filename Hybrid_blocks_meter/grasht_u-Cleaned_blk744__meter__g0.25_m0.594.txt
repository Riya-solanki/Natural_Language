sāraṇāyantra
kṛtvā nalikāṃ dīrghāṃ ṣaḍaṃgulāṃ dhūrtakusumasaṃkāśām ।
mūṣāpyadho vilagnā kartavyā vai mṛdā lepyā ।।
aparā sūkṣmā nalikā kāryā saptāṃgulā sudṛḍhā ।
madhye praviśati ca yathā tadvatkāryā ca dṛḍhamukhā ।।
tasminsūtaḥ kṣiptaḥ sāraṇatailānvito madanaruddhamukhaḥ ।
tadanu bṛhattamayā hema pradrāvya hemakoṣṭhikayā ।।
tasminmadhye kṣiptvā nalikāgramadhomukhīṃ kuryāt ।
antarūrdhvaṃ bhārākrāntāṃ sarati raso nātra saṃdehaḥ ।।
