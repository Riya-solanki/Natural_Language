tṛṇādi sarpad dravyaṃ kāce abhrapaṭale
vā pratihataṃ dṛṣṭam, avyavahitena saṃnikṛṣyate
vyāhanyate vai prāptirvyavadhāneneti, yadi
ca raśmyarthasaṃnikarṣo grahaṇahetuḥ syāt ? na vyavahitasya saṃnikarṣa ity agrahaṇaṃ syāt,
asti ceyaṃ kācābhrapaṭalasphaṭikāntaritopalabdhaḥ
sā jñāpiyati aprāpyakārīṇīndriyāṇi ata evābhautikāni prāpyakāritvaṃ hi bhautikadharma
iti ।।
