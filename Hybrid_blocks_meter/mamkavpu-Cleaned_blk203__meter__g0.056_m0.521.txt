{prasthānaṃ valayaiḥ kṛtaṃ priyasakhairasrairajasraṃ gataṃ
dhṛtyā na kṣaṇamāsitaṃ vyavasitaṃ cittena gantuṃ puraḥ ।
yātuṃ niścitacetasi priyatame sarve samaṃ prasthitā
gantavye sati jīvita priyasuhṛtsārthaḥ kimu tyajyate ।।}
