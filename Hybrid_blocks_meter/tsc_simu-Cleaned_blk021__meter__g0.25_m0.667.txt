sāmyāvasthā guṇānāṃ yā prakṛtiḥ sā svabhāvataḥ ।
kālakṣobheṇa vaiṣamyāt kṣetre parayute purā ।।
buddhistataścāhaṅkārastrividho api vyajāyata ।
tanmātrāṇīndriyāṇi mahābhūtāni ca kramāt ।।
evaṃ krameṇaivotpattiḥ sañcaraḥ parikīrttitaḥ ।
