nāpyatra lakṣaṇamupamālaṅkārādalaṅkārāntarasya kāraṇam ।
pūrvoktayā nītyā tasya nirākṛtatvāt ।
।
nopamānopameyabhāve pratibhāso abhedapratibhāso api sapra ।
na ca upamānāntaranirāsalakṣaṇatātparyādalaṅkārāntaratvam, mukhamindurityādau upamānetarasvabhāvagandhasyāpyavidyamānatvā ।
pratibhāsi prauḍhipratipāditamabhidhāprakāravaicitryamātramevātra vijṛmbhate na punaralaṅkaraṇāntaratvam ।
vācyāntareṣvapi vaicitryāntaradar ।
yathā
