pramāṇasya tattvaviṣayatvāt, na khalu bhoḥ parīkṣamāṇena
dṛṣṭānumitā arthāḥ śakyā niyoktum aevaṃ
bhavata' iti nāpi pratiṣeddhum aevaṃ na bhavata'
iti, na hīdamupapadyate arūpavad gandhopi
cākṣuṣo bhavatu' iti agandhavadvā rūpaṃ cākṣuṣaṃ
mā bhūta' iti, aagnipratipattivad dhūmenodakapratipattirapi
bhavatu' iti, audakāpratipattivadvā dhūmenāgnipratipattirapi
mā bhūt' iti।
kiṃ kāraṇam ?, yathā khalvarthā
bhavanti ।
ya eṣāṃ svo bhāvaḥ ।
svo dharma iti tathābhūtāḥ pramāṇena pratipadyante
iti tathābhūtaviṣayakaṃ hi pramāṇathmati।
imau khalu niyogapratiṣedhau bhavatā deśitau
kācābhrapaṭalādivadvā kuḍyādibhirapratighāto
bhavatu kuḍyādivadvā kācābhrapaṭalādibhirapratighāto
mā bhūt iti, na dṛṣṭānumitāḥ khalvime
dravyadharmāḥ ।
pratighātāpratighātayorhi upalabdhyanupalabdhī
vyavasthāpike vyavahitānupalabdhyānumīyate
kuḍyādibhiḥ pratighātaḥ, vyavahitopalabdhyānumīyate
kācābhrapaṭalādibhirapratighāta iti ।।
