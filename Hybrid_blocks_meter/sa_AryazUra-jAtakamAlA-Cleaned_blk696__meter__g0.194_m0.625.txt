dānaṃ nāma dhanodaye sati jano datte tadāśāvaśaḥ
syācchīle api ca lokapaṃktyabhimukhaḥ svarge ca jātaspṛhaḥ ।
yā tveṣā parakāryadakṣiṇatayā tadvatpravṛttistayor
nāvidvatsu na sattvayogavidhureṣveṣā samālakṣyate ।।
