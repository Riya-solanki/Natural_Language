yasyeha dṛśyate arthaḥ sa dṛṣṭārthaḥ।
yasyāmutra pratīyata so adṛṣṭārthaḥ।
evamṛṣilaukikavākyānāṃ vibhāga iti।
kimarthaṃ punaridamucyate? sa na manyeta dṛṣṭārthaṃ evāptopadeśaḥ pramāṇam, arthasyāvadhāraṇādita, adṛṣṭārtho api
pramāṇamarthasyānumānāditi।
iti pramāṇabhāṣyam ।।
