{śaktirnistriṃśajeyaṃ tava bhujayugale nātha doṣākaraśrī-
rvaktre pārśve tathaiṣā prativasati mahākuṭṭanī khaḍgayaṣṭiḥ ।
ājñeyaṃ sarvagā te vilasati ca puraḥ kiṃ mayā vṛddhayā te
procyevetthaṃ prakopācchaśikarasitayā yasya kīrtyā prayātam ।।}
