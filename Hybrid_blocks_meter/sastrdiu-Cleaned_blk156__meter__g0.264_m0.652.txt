naca sākṣādbādhakamastītyavyatirekaśabdenocyate ।
anupalabdhārthatvācca nānuvādalakṣaṇamaprāmāṇyamastīti arthe anupalabdhe ityanenoktam ।
etacca viśeṣaṇatrayamupāgadadānena sūtrakāreṇa kāraṇadoṣabādhakajñānarahitamagṛhītagrāhijñānaṃ pramāṇamiti pramāṇalakṣaṇaṃ sūcitam ।
