amarasundarīguṭikā
kāntābhrasattvahematāraṃ cārkaḥ samāṃśataḥ saṃkhyā ।
baddhaṃ sūtasamāṃśaṃ dhmātaṃ golaṃ kṛtaṃ khoṭam ।।
bāhye rasena liptaṃ vadanagataṃ śastravārakaṃ rogān ।
hanti hi śarīrasaṃsthān nāmnāmarasundarī guṭikā ।।
