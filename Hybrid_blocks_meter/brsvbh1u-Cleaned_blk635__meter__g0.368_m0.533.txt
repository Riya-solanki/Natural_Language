praṇabhṛcca ।
cenātacchabdatvaṃ heturanukṛṣyate ।
svayaṃ ca bhāṣyakṛdatra hetumāha-na copādhiparicchinnasyeti ।
na samyak saṃbhavati ।
nāñjasamityarthaḥ ।
bhogyatvena hi āyatanatvamiti kliṣṭam ।
syādetat ।
yadyatacchabdatvādityatrāpi heturanukraṣṭavyaḥ, hanta kasmātpṛthagyogakaraṇaṃ, yāvatā ana prāṇabhṛdanumāne'ityeka eva yogaḥ kasmānna kṛta ityata āha-pṛthagiti ।
abhedavyapadeśāt'ityādinā hi prāṇabhṛdeva niṣidhyate, na pradhānaṃ, taccaikayogakaraṇe durvijñānaṃ syāditi ।।
