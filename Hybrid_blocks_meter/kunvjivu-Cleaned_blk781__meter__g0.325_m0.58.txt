atraiva prakārāntaraṃ vicārayati "ekadeśavivarti ca" iti ।
atra pūrvācāryairvyākhyātamyathā yadekadeśena vivartate vighaṭate viśeṣeṇa vā vartate tattathoktamiti ।
ubhayathāpyetaduktaṃ bhavatiyadvākyasya kasmiṃścideva sthāne svaparispandasamarpaṇātmakaṃ rūpaṇamādadhāti ।
kvacideveti tadekadeśavivartirūpakam ।
yathā
