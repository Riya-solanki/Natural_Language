atha khaṇḍitā-
ullaṅghya samayaṃ yasyāḥ preyān anyopabhogavān । 
bhoga-lakṣmāñcitaḥ prātar āgacchet sā hi khaṇḍitā ।।
asyās tu cintā niḥśvāsas tūṣṇīṃ-bhāvo' śru-mocanam । 
kheda-bhrānty-asphuṭālāpā ity ādyā vikriyā matāḥ ।।
