tuśabda āśaṅkāmapanudati ।
na khalu mṛdabravīdityevañjātīyakayā śrutyā bhūtendriyāṇāṃ cetanatvamāśaṅkanīyam ।
yato abhimānivyapadeśa eṣaḥ ।
mṛdādyabhimāninyo vāgādyabhimāninyaśca cetanā devatā vadanasaṃvadanādiṣu cetanociteṣu vyavahāreṣu vyapadiśyante na bhūtendriyamātram ।
kasmāt ।
viśeṣānugatibhyām ।
viśeṣo hi bhoktṛṇāṃ bhūtendriyāṇāṃ ca cetanācetanapravibhāgalakṣaṇaḥ prāgabhihitaḥ ।
sarvacetanatāyāṃ cāsau nopapadyeta ।
apica kauṣītakinaḥ prāṇasaṃvāde karaṇamātrāśaṅkāvinivṛttaye adhiṣṭhātṛcetanaparigrahāya devatāśabdena viśiṣanti- aetā ha vai devatā ahaṃśreyase vivadamānāḥ' iti ।
atā vā etāḥ sarvā devatāḥ prāṇe niḥśreyasaṃ viditvā' iti ca ।
anugatāśca sarvatrābhimāninyaścetanādevatā mantrārthavādetihāsapurāṇādibhyo avagamyante ।
aagnirvāgbhūtvā mukhaṃ prāviśat' ityevamādikā ca śrutiḥ karaṇeṣvanugrāhikāṃ devatāmanugatāṃ darśayati ।
prāṇasaṃvādavākyaśeṣe ca ate ha prāṇāḥ prajāpatiṃ pitarametyocuḥ' iti śreṣṭhatvanirdhāraṇāya prajāpatigamanaṃ, tadvacanācaikaikotkramaṇenānvayavyatirkābhyāṃ prāṇaśraiṣṭhyapratipattiḥ ।
atasmai baliharaṇam' iti caivañjātīyako asmadādiṣviva vyavahāro anugamyamāno abhimānivyapadeśaṃ draḍhayati ।
atatteja aikṣata' ityapi parasyā eva devatāyā adhiṣṭātryāḥ svavikāreṣvanugatāyā iyamīkṣā vyapadiśyata iti draṣṭavyam ।
tasmādvilakṣaṇamevedaṃ brahmaṇo jagat ।
vilakṣaṇatvācca na brahmaprakṛtikamityākṣipte pratividhatte ।।
