śṛkṛṣṇa uvāca ।
śṛṇu dānaṃ pravakṣyāmi sarvāśu bhavināśanam ।
santaptahāṭakamayaṃ ghaṭakaṃ vidhāya brahmeśakeśavayutaṃ saha lokapālaiḥ ।
kṣīrājyapūrṇaviviraṃ praṇipatya bhaktyā viprāya dehi tava dānaśataiḥ kimanyaiḥ ।।
