sāraṇa
paṭagālitaṃ gṛhītvā sūtaṃ sampūrṇadīrghamūṣāyām ।
tadanu khalu taptataile pradrāvya samaṃ kṣiped bījam ।।
mūṣāvaktraṃ sthagayel latādvayaprotavitatanaddhena ।
tailārdrapaṭena tato bījaṃ prakṣipya samakālam ।।
piśitānuguṇaṃ bījaiḥ sāraṇavidhinā niyojitaḥ sūtaḥ ।
akṣīyamāṇo milati ca bījair baddho bhavatyeva ।।
