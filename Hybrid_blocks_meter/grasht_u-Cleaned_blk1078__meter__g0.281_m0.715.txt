vajriṇīguṭikā
kāntaghanasattvakamalaṃ hema ca tāraṃ yathā kṛtadvandvam ।
samajīrṇaṃ bījavaraṃ vajrayutaṃ vajriṇī guṭikā ।।
eṣā mukhakuharagatā kurute navanāgatulyabalam ।
tadvapurapi durbhedyaṃ mṛtyujarāroganirmuktam ।।
