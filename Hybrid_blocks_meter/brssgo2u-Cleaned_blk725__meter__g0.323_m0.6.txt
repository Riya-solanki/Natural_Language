tadguṇasāratvādhikāreṇaivāparo api jīvadharmaḥ prapañcyate ।
kartā cāyaṃ jīvaḥ syāt ।
kasmāt ।
śāstrārthatvāt ।
evañca ayajeta' ajuhuyāt' adadyāt' ityevaṃvidhaṃ vidiśāstramarthavadbhavati ।
anyathā tadanarthakaṃ syāt ।
taddhi kartuḥ sataḥ kartavyaviśeṣamupadiśati ।
nacāsati kartṛtve tadupapadyeta ।
tathedamapi śāstramarthavadbhavati eṣa hi draṣṭā śrotā mantā boddhā kartā vijñānātmā puruṣaḥ iti ।।
