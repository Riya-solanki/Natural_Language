śrīkaṅkālayayogisamīpāt samāyātam adhyāyaṃ vadāmīti śiṣyavacanam ।। 
yatrādhyāye śṛṅkhalāraso yuktibhir auṣadhabhāvanāgnipuṭādividhibhiḥ prakaṭīkṛto asti ।। 
yathā kila śṛṅkhalā pūrvapūrvatarāvayavair anyonyānupraveśāt sambaddhā bhavati tathā atrāpi ।। 
pūrvaṃ rasasya śodhanam ।। 
tataḥ śodhitarasasya mūrchitotthāpanam ।। 
mūrchitotthāpitasya pātanam ।। 
pātitasya ca punarutthāpanam ।। 
pātitotthāpitasya svedanam ।। 
svedena sveditasya ca niyāmitvam ।। 
niyāmitasya nirodhakatvam ।। 
nirodhitasya ca vakraprasāraṇam ।। 
vakraprasāritasya cābhrakajāraṇam ।। 
jīrṇābhrakasya ca lohajāraṇam ।। 
jīrṇalohasya cāyaḥprakāśarājijāraṇam ।। 
jīrṇāyaḥprakāśarājeś ca hemarājijāraṇam ।। 
jīrṇahemarājer gandhakajāraṇam ।। 
jīrṇagandhakasya ca manaḥśilāsattvajāraṇam ।। 
jīrṇaśilāsattvasya ca khāparasattvajāraṇam ।। 
jīrṇakhāparasattvasya cānnapathahīrakajāraṇam ।। 
jīrṇavajrasya ca bandhaḥ ।। 
bandhasya sāraṇam ।। 
sāritasya ca māraṇam ।। 
māritasya ca tasya krāmaṇam ।। 
krāmitasya ca bandhaḥ ।। 
bandhasya codghāṭanam ।। 
iti ।। 
evaṃ pūrvapūrvatarasaṃskārasya rasasyāgretanasaṃskāro granthito'stīty asau procyata iti tattvam ।। 
sūtadoṣāṃs tataḥ śuddhiṃ saṃskārāt prāk vadāmyaham ।
rasānāṃ phalamutpattiṃ dehaloharasāyanam ।।
