rāgo anaikavidho jarā paribhavo bhogārjanā rakṣaṇā
pradhvaṃsavyasanaṃ mahātyayam asatsaṃparkajaṃ vaikṛtam ।
iṣṭāniṣṭaviyogayogasulabhās te te manoviplavā
jātiṃ prāpya yato bhavanti jagataḥ saiveha duḥkhaṃ tataḥ ।।
