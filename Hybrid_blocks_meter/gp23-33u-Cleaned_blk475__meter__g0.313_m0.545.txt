[] vāsarāvasaraś cāyaṃ mad-ukti-nidigdha-dig-darśanataḥ parāmarśam ānīyatām । yathā cāha śrī-parāśaraḥ-
sa tathā saha gopībhī rarāma madhusūdanaḥ । yathābda-koṭi-pratimaḥ kṣaṇas tena vinābhavat ।।[ViP ]
yathā ca śrī-śukaḥ-
gopyaḥ kṛṣṇe vanaṃ yāte tam anudruta-cetasaḥ । kṛṣṇa-līlāḥ pragāyantyo ninyur duḥkhena vāsarān ।।
