FN ādigrahaṇādakramo api gṛhyate ।
aṃmayaśarīrapracurasvargaloko ambhaḥśabdārthaḥ ।
sūryaraśmivyāpto antarikṣaloko marīcayaḥ ।
maro maraṇadharmā martyaḥ ।
abbahulāḥ pātālaloka āpa iti śrutyarthaḥ ।
prakriyā sṛṣṭiḥ ।
tat tatra kāraṇe ।
eke bāhyāḥ ।
tadviṣayeṇa brahmaviṣayeṇa ।
miṣat savyāpāram ।
aviruddhārthakatvāt ।
śuṅgena kāryeṇa ।
anyathānyatheti vīpsā draṣṭavyā ।
avatārāya brahmadhījanmane ।
atastadanyathātve api brahmaṇi na bhedaḥ ।
jñeye vigānaṃ na ।
