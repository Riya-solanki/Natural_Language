antardyotir apāsya yasya tamasām āyāminīṃ vāsanām
akliṣṭām api nirdadhāva nivṛtiṃ kālatrayavyāpinīṃ ।
sarvākāraparopakāraviṣayaṃ yasyāparapratyayaṃ
prajñānaṃ bhagavān asau vijayate buddho jagacchaṅkaraḥ ।।
