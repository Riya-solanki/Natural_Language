naiṣa doṣaḥ ।
sarvatrāpi gamyamānasya jyotiṣaḥ aparo divaḥ' ityupāsanārthaḥ pradeśaviśeṣaparigraho na virudhyate ।
natu niṣpradeśasyāpi brahmaṇaḥ pradeśaviśeṣakalpanā bhāginī ।
asarvataḥpṛṣṭeṣvanuttameṣūttameṣu lokeṣu' iti cādhārabahutvaśrutiḥ kārye jyotiṣyupapadyatetarām ।
aidaṃ vāva tadyadidamasminnāntaḥ puruṣe jyotiḥ' iti ca kaukṣeye jyotiraṣi paraṃ jyotiradhyasyamānaṃ dṛśyate ।
sārūpyanimittāścādhyasā bhavanti ।
yathā- atasya bhūriti śira ekametadakṣaram' iti ।
kaukṣeyasya tu jyotiṣaḥ prasiddhamabrahmatvam ।
atasyaiṣā dṛṣṭiḥ' atasyaiṣā śrutiḥ' iti cauṣṇyaghoṣaviśiṣṭatvasya śravaṇāt ।
atadetaddṛṣṭaṃ ca śrutaṃ cepyupāsīta' iti ca śruteḥ ।
acakṣuṣyaḥ śruto bhavati ya evaṃ veda' iti cālpaphalaśravaṇādabrahmatvam ।
mahate hi phalāya brahmopāsanamiṣyate ।
