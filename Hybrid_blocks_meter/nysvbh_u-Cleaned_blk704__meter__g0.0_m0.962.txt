yathānekadravyeṇa samavāyādrūpaviśeṣāccopalabdhiriti
satyupalabdhikāraṇe madhyandinolkāprakāśo
nopalabhyate ādityaprakāśenābhibhūtaḥ, evaṃ
mahadanekadravyavattvād rūpaviśeṣāccopalabdhiriti
satyupalabdhikāraṇe cākṣuṣo raśmirnopalabhyate
nimittāntarataḥ, tacca vyākhyātamanudbhūtarūpasparśasya
dravyasya pratyakṣato anupalabdhiriti, atyantānupalabdhiścābhāvakāraṇam
।।
