tasyedamupavyākhyānam apūrvadṛṣṭa-' ityādi ।
pūrvadṛṣṭasyāvabhāsaḥ pūrvadṛṣṭāvabhāsaḥ ।
mithyāpratyayaśca āropaviṣayāropaṇīyamithunamantareṇa na bhavatīti pūrvadṛṣṭagrahaṇenānṛtamāropaṇīyamupasthāpayati ।
tasya ca dṛṣṭatvamātramupayujyate na vastusatteti dṛṣṭagrahaṇam ।
tathāpi vartamānaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ darśanaṃ nāropopayogīti pūrva- ityuktam ।
tacca pūrvadṛṣṭaṃ svarūpeṇa sadapyāropaṇīyatayā anirvācyamityanṛtam ।
āropaviṣayaṃ satyamāha- paratra iti ।
paratra śuktikādau paramārthasati ।
tadanena satyānṛtamithunamuktam ।
