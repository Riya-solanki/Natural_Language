[] sakalakṣititalasamāgatāḥ priyakalahāḥ kalahānāṃ ca niveditāro dhūrtamiśrāḥ śṛṇvantu śṛṇvantu bhavantaḥ
kāmas tapasviṣu jayaty adhikārakāmo
viśvasya cittavibhur indriyavājyadhīśaḥ
bhūtāni bibhrati mahānty api yasya śiṣṭiṃ
vyāvṛttamaulimaṇiraśmibhir uttamāṅgaiḥ
