anavasthāyitvepi buddheḥ
teṣāṃ dravyāṇāṃ grahaṇaṃ vyaktaṃ pratipattavyam।
katham ?
pradīpārciḥsaṃtatyabhivyaktagrahaṇavat
upradīpārciṣāṃ saṃtatyā vartamānānāṃ
grahaṇānavasthānaṃ grāhyānavasthānaṃ ca, pratyarthaniyatatvādvā
buddhīnāṃ yāvanti pradīpārcīṣi tāvatyo
buddhaya iti dṛśyate cātra vyaktaṃ
pradīpārciṣāṃ grahaṇamiti ।।
