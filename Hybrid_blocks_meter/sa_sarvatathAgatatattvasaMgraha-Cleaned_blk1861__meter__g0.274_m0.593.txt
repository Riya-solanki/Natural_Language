oṃ padmasaṃbhava padmahasta sādhu hūṃ । vajravegena cākramya dvitīyaṃ maṇḍalantathā ।
tatra madhye samālekhyaṃ jaṭāmadhye tathāgataṃ ।।
tasya pārśveṣu sarveṣu bhṛkuṭyādiprayogataḥ ।
padmacinhadharā lekhya yathāvadanupūrvaśaḥ ।।
