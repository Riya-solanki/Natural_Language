utkṛṣṭasarjikā turyamapānāṃ sūkṣmacūrṇakam ।
kṣiptvā sthālyāṃ hi saṃdhyāyāṃ kumbhamānaṃ jalaṃ kṣipet ।।
tyaktavyaṃ kābasammanītāryasajjikāpayaḥ ।
utkṛṣṭāśmanā vedāsteṣāṃ cūrṇaṃ tu sūkṣmakam ।।
gandhakāmalasārasya maṇaikaṃ sūkṣmacūrṇakam ।
kṣiptvaivātra hyubhe cūrṇe piṣṭvā caikātmatāṃ nayet ।।
kṣipetpañcamaṇān sthālyāṃ yasyāṃ matighaṭadvayam ।
svarjikā jalamanyasyāṃ muktvā cullyopari kṣipet ।।
eraṇḍatailavattailam uparyāyāti gandhakam ।
tatsthālyā muhurādāya kṣipecchītaṃ ca kūmpake ।।
