sāraṇāyantra
kṛtvā mūṣāṃ dīrghāṃ bandhitatribhāgapraṇālikāṃ tāṃ ca ।
tasyāgre prakaṭamūṣā sacchidrā sudṛḍhamṛttikāliptā ।।
tasminprakṣipya rasaṃ sāraṇatailānvitaṃ tapte ।
pradrāvya tulyakanakaṃ kṣipte'smin milati rasarājaḥ ।।
