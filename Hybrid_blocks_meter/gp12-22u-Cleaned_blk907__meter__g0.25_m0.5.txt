go-sūktaṃ bhāṣate gāvaḥ padaṃ sarva-suparvaṇām ।
tad brahmādau suparvatvam avan vinda gavendratām ।। [anuṣṭubh]
tavedaṃ kiyad aiśvaryaṃ yad brahmāvadhi-pālanaṃ ।
vaikuṇṭha-kuṇṭhatā-kāri yasya goloka-vaibhavam ।। [anuṣṭubh]
sarvasya pālakāt kvāpi khaṇḍa-pālanam iṣyate ।
jagat-prakāśakād bhānor nija-dhāma-prakāśavat ।। [anuṣṭubh]
nāsmākaṃ kevalam idaṃ mataṃ kintu vidher api ।
yad bhaved asya cendrasya durbuddheḥ śuddhi-bhāvanam ।। [anuṣṭubh]
tvat-kṛpāyāḥ paraṃ sthānaṃ vayam ity eṣa vāsavaḥ ।
āśiśriye yad asmāṃs tad dvāra-mātrāya kalpate ।। [anuṣṭubh]
yasmān mṛgyati tām eva mad-vidhād bhavataḥ kṛpām ।
kūpād iva rasāntaḥ-sthāṃ tarṣī ghana-rasa-sthitim ।। [anuṣṭubh]
tad eva deva paśyendraḥ paśyaṃs tava pada-dvayam ।
sūcayaty abhiṣekaṃ te netra-dhārā-sahasrataḥ ।। [anuṣṭubh]
