ātmādeḥ khalu prameyasya tatvajñānātriḥśreyasādhigamaḥ taccaiduttarasūtreṇānūdyate।
heyaṃ tasya nirvartakaṃ hānamātyantikaṃ tasyopāyo adhigantavya ityetāni khalu catvāryarthapadāni samyagbuddhvā niḥśreyasamadhigacchati।
