tadvat dharmasya lope syāt na śrautī taddhite punaḥ ।
dharmaḥ sādhāraṇaḥ ।
taddhite kalpabādau tvārthyeva ।
tena pañca ।
udāharaṇam dhanyasyānanyasāmānyasaujanyotkarṣaśālinaḥ karaṇīyaṃ vacaśvetaḥ satyaṃ tasyāmṛtaṃ yathā ।।
ākṛṣṭakaravālo asau saṃparāye paribhraman ।
pratyarthisenayā dṛṣṭaḥ kṛtāntena samaḥ prabhuḥ ।।
karavālaivācārastasya vāgamṛtopamā ।
viṣakalpaṃ mano vetsi yadi jīvasi tatsakhe ।।
