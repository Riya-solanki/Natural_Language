manoguṇenādṛṣṭena samāveśite manasi
saṃyogavyucchedo na syāt tatra kiṅkṛtaṃ
śarīrādapasarpaṇaṃ manasa iti ?।
karmāśayakṣaye
tu karmāśayāntarād
vipacyamānādapasarpaṇopapattiriti।
adṛṣṭādevāpasapraṇamiticet ?, yo adṛṣṭaḥ
śarīropasarpaṇahetuḥ sa
evāpasarpaṇaheturapīti na
ekasyajīvanaprāyaṇahetutvānupapatteḥ ।
evaṃ ca sati ekamadṛṣṭaṃ
jīvanaprāyaṇayorheturitiprāptaṃ
naitadupapadyate ।।
