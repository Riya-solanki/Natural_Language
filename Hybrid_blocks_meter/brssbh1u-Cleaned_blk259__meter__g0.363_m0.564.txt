smṛtiśca śārīraparamātmanorbhedaṃ darśayati- aīśvaraḥ sarvabhūtānāṃ hṛddeśer'juna tiṣṭati ।
bhrāmayansarvabhūtāni yantrārūḍhāni māyayā' ।
ityādyā ।
atrāha- kaḥ punarayaṃ śārīro nāma paramātmano anyaḥ, yaḥ pratiṣidhyate aanupapattestu na śārīraḥ' ityādinā ।
śrutistu- anānyo ato asti draṣṭā śrotā' ityevañjātīyakā paramātmano anyamātmānaṃ vārayati ।
tathā smṛtirapi- akṣetrajñaṃ cāpi māṃ viddhi sarvakṣetreṣu bhārata' ityevañjātīyaketi ।
atrocyate- satyametat ।
para evātmā dehendriyamanobuddhyupādhibhiḥ paricchidyamāno bālaiḥ śārīra ityupacaryate ।
yathā ghaṭakarakādyupādhivaśādaparicchinnamapi nabhaḥ paricchinnavadavabhāsate, tadvat ।
tadapekṣayā ca karmakartṛtvādibhedavyavahāro na virudhyate prāk atattvamasi' ityātmaikatvopadeśaghaṇāt ।
gṛhīte tvātmaikatve bandhamokṣādisarvavyavahāraparisamāptireva syāt ।।
