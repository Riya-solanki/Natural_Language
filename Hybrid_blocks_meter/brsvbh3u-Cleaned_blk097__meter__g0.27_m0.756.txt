sthānaviśeṣātprakāśādivat ।
buddhyādyupādhisthānaviśeṣayogādudbhūtasya jāgratsvapnayorviśeṣavijñānasyopādhyupaśame abhibhave suṣuptāvasthānamiti ।
tathā bhedavyapadeśo api trividho brahmaṇa upādhibhedāpekṣayeti ।
yathā saudhajālamārganiveśinyaḥ savitṛbhāso jālamārgopādhibhedādbhinnā
