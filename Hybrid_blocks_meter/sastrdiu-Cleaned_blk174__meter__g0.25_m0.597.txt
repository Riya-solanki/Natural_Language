praṇāḍyāstīndriyasaṃprayogajatvamiti tadvyāvṛttyarthaṃ tatsaṃprayogajatvamiti ।
viśeṣaṇaṃ vaktavyamiti cet, tanna ।
nahīha yathākathañcitsaṃprayogajanyatvaṃ lakṣaṇaṃ, kintu sākṣāttajjanyatvam ।
yadica yathākathañcidityāśrīyeta tato viśeṣaṇe satyapyativyāptimallakṣaṇaṃ syāt- ārādignimadhyakṣayato
