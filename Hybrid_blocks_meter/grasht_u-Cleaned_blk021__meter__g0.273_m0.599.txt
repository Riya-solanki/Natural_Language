jarāmaraṇaniṣedhatvena kimādhikyaṃ tadāha ya ityādi ।।
ye evaṃvidharasasiddhāste vandyā abhivādanayogyāḥ stutyāśca ।।
vadi abhivādanastutyoḥ ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭā atyaktaśarīrāḥ na tyaktaṃ śarīraṃ yaiste jīvanmuktā ityarthaḥ ।।
śarīraṃ dvividhaṃ sthūlasūkṣmabhedāt pṛthivyaptejovāyvākāśātmakaṃ sthūlaṃ kośatrayātmakaṃ sūkṣmam ।।
yathā sūtram ।
vijñānamayaṃ manomayaṃ prāṇamayam etat kośatrayaṃ militaṃ sūkṣmaśarīramutpadyate ।।
anye eva sūkṣmaśarīram ।।
atyaktaśarīrarasasiddhāśca ucyante ।
manthānabhairavo yogī siddhabuddhaśca kanthaḍī ।
koraṇṭakaḥ surānandaḥ siddhapādaśca carpaṭī ।।
kaṇerī pūjyapādaśca nityanātho nirañjanaḥ ।
kapālī bindunāthaśca kākacaṇḍīśvaro gajaḥ ।।
āllamaḥ prabhudevaśca ghoḍācolī ca ṭhiṇṭhinī ।
bhālukir nāgadevaśca khaṇḍī kāpālikas tathā ।।
ityādayo mahāsiddhā rasabhogaprasādataḥ ।
khaṇḍayitvā kāladaṇḍaṃ trilokyāṃ vicaranti te ।।
iti ।।
punaḥ kiṃviśiṣṭāḥ tanuṃ prāptāḥ śarīraṃ grahītāraḥ ।।
kiṃviśiṣṭāṃ tanuṃ haragaurīsṛṣṭijāṃ haro mahādevaḥ gaurī pārvatī tayoḥ sṛṣṭiḥ sarjanaṃ maithunasaṃyogas tajjātā putrā evetyarthaḥ ।।
punaryeṣāṃ mantragaṇāḥ kiṃkarāḥ mantrasamūhā ājñākarā ityarthaḥ ।।
