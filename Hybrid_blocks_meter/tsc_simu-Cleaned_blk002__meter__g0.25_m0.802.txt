raghunandanasutenedamiṣṭikāpuravāsinā ।
kānyakubjadvijāgryeṇa ṣimānandena tanyate ।।
sāṃkhyopajña namastubhyaṃ kapilāya mahātmane ।
pañcaviṃśatitattvānāṃ tattvajñānapravartakaḥ ।।
duḥkhatrayābhibhūtasya śāntirme syāt kutastviha ।
tathopadiśa janmādi yathā na syāt kṛpānidhe ।।
evaṃ pṛṣṭo muniḥ prāha nirviṇṇāya kṛpānidhiḥ ।
pañcaviṃśatisūtrāṇi vyākhyātāni mahātmabhiḥ ।।
babandhuḥ kavayaḥ kāvye vyācikhyuḥ kavayaḥ pare ।
sāṃkhyaśāstrasya yāthārthyaṃ tadevedaṃ vivicyate ।।
ṣimānandena mandena kukṣimbhariśatādinā ।।
