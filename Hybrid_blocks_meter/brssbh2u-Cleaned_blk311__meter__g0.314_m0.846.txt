yo ayamavidyādinirodhaḥ pratisaṃkhyānirodhāntaḥpātī paraparikalpitaḥ, sa samyagjñānādvā saparikarātsyātsvayameva vā ।
pūrvasminvikalpe nihartukavināśābhyupagamahāniprasaṅgaḥ ।
uttarasmiṃstu mārgopadeśānarthakyaprasaṅgaḥ ।
evamubhayathāpi doṣaprasaṅgādasamañjasamidaṃ darśanam ।।
