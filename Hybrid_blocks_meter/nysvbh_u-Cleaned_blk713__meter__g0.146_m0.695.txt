bāhyaprakāśānugrahanirapekṣatāyāṃ ceti
cārthaḥ, yad rūpamabhivyaktam ।
udbhūtaṃ bāhyaprakāśānugrahaṃ ca nāpekṣate tadviṣayo abhibhavaḥ
viparyaye abhibhavābhāvāt anudbhūtarūpatvāccānupalabhyamānaṃ bāhyaprakāśānugrahāccopalabhyamānaṃ
nābhibhūyate, iti evamupapannam asti cākṣuṣo raśmiriti ।।
