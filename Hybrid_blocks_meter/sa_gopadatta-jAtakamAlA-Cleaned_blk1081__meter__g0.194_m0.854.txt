saṃrāgāruṇatārakāntamukulakṣībeṇa yā cakṣuṣā
kāntaṃ snigdhavidagdhamugdhamadhureṇodīkṣamāṇā purā ।
cakre manmathavāgurāhariṇakaṃ saivādya saṃrāgiṇāṃ
saṃtrāsaṃ vihagāpanītanayanā kaṣṭaṃ karoty aṅganā ।।
