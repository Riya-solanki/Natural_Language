tamo moho mahāmohastāmisraścāndhatāmisraḥ ।।
aṣṭakau tau tamau mohau prakṛtyaṣṭakaniṣṭhitau ।
ātmajñānābhimānyeyaṃ prāptyādyaiśvaryamānitā ।।
nirvṛtte viṣaye dṛṣṭe pañcānuśravike tathā ।
moho ahamabhimāno yo mahāmoho daśātmakaḥ ।।
aṣṭaiśvarye daśaviṣaye siddhatāmisra ucyate ।
viṣādaḥ so andhatāmisro maraṇe pratipadyate ।।
avidyā pañcaparvaiṣā bhinnā dvāṣaṣṭibhedataḥ ।
