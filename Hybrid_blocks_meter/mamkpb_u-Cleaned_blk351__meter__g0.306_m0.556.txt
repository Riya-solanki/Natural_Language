eteṣāṃ sādhāraṇadharmādīnāma dṛṣṭo antaḥ niśvayo yatra sa dṛṣṭāntaḥ ।
tvayi dṛṣṭa eva tasyā nirvāti mano manobhavajvalitam ।
āloke hi himāṃsorvikasati kusumaṃ kumuddhatyāḥ ।।
eṣa sādharmyeṇa ।
vaidharmyeṇa tu tavāhave sāhasakarmaśarmaṇaḥ karaṃ kṛpāṇāntikamāninīṣataḥ ।
bhaṭāḥ pareṣāṃ viśarārutāmaguḥ, dadhatyavāte sthiratāṃ hi pāṃsavaḥ ।।
