atha bhāṇaḥ-
svasya vānyasya vā vṛttaṃ viṭena nipuṇoktinā । 
śaurya-saubhāgya-saṃstutyā vīra-śṛṅgāra-sūcakam ।।
buddhi-kalpitam ekāṅkaṃ mukha-nirvahaṇānvitam । 
varṇyate bhāratī-vṛttyā yatra taṃ bhāṇam īrate ।।
eka-pātra-prayojye' smin kuryād ākāśa-bhāṣitam । 
anyenānuktam apy anyo vacaḥ śrutveva yad vadet ।।
iti kiṃ bhaṇasīty etad bhaved ākāśa-bhāṣitam । 
lāsyāṅgāni daśaitasmin saṃyojyānyatra tāni tu ।।
geya-padaṃ sthita-pāṭhyam āsīnaṃ puṣpa-gandhikā । 
pracchedakas trimūḍhaṃ ca saindhavākhyaṃ dvimūḍhakam ।
uttamottamakaṃ cānyad ukta-pratyuktam eva ca ।।
