nanu hetavo vilakṣaṇaḥ kathamekavidhasāmarthyayuktāni svalakṣaṇāni janayanti ।
na janayeyuryadi vilakṣaṇāḥ syuste api tvekajātīyā eva ।।
nanvevaṃ tatsaṃbandhasidhyarthaparāparajātyapekṣāyāmanavasthā ।
naiṣa doṣaḥ, yathā tālabījamekajātīyaṃ pariṇāmaparaṃparayā tālajātīsaṃbaddhāṃ
