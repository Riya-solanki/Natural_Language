parā nāṃ lakṣaṇaṃ veditavyaṃ jñānecchubhirnānyathā veditavyam ।
padārthānāṃ pārthivānāṃ khagendra viśeṣāṇāṃ caramākhyo viśeṣaḥ ।।
sa evaḥ syātparamāṇurdvijendra yontyovi śeṣovayavaśca sa smṛtaḥ ।।
garuḍa uvāca ।
he kṛṣṇa he mādhava sāttvatāṃ pate padārthānāṃ caramāṃśaḥ parāṇu ।।
