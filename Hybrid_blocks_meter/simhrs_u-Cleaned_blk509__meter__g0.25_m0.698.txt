sarve' pi sattva-mūlatvād bhāvā yadyapi sāttvikāḥ । 
tathāpy amīṣāṃ sattvaika-mūlatvāt sāttvika-prathā ।।
anubhāvāś ca kathyante bhāva-saṃsūcanād amī । 
evaṃ dvairūpyam eteṣāṃ kathitaṃ bhāva-kovidaiḥ ।।
anubhāvaika-nidhinā sukhānubhava-śālinā ।
śrī-siṃha-bhūbhujā sāṅgam anubhāvā nirūpitāḥ ।।
