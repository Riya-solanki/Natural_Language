parivṛttiralaṃkāraḥ ।
udāharaṇam latānāmetāsāmuditakusumānāṃ marudayaṃ mataṃ lāsyaṃ datvā śrayati bhṛśamāmodamasamam ।
latāstvadhvanyānāmahaha dṛśamādāya sahasā dadatyādhivyādhibhramiruditamohavyatikaram ।।
atra prathame ardhe samena samasya dvitīye, uttamena nyūnasya ।
nānāvidhapraharaṇairnṛpa saṃprahāre svīkṛtya dāruṇaninādavataḥ prahārān ।
dṛpātārivīravisareṇa vasuṃdhareyaṃ nirvipralambhaparirambhavidhirvitīrṇā ।।
atra nyūnenottamasya ।।
