yat asatyaṃ jñānam'ityādinā mantravarṇena brahmoktaṃ tadevopāyabhūtena brāhmaṇena svapradhānyena gīyate-'brahma pucchaṃpratiṣṭhā'iti ।
avayavavacanatve tvasya mantre prādhānyaṃ, brāhmaṇe tvaprādhānyamityupāyopeyayormantrabrāhmaṇayorvipratipattiḥ syāditi ।
netaro anupapatteḥ ।।
