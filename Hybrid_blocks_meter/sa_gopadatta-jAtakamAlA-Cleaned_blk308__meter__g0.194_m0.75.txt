dūrād eva tam āpatantam anilapreṅkholanācañcalaiḥ
sadyo agniḥ pariṣaṣvaje priyam iva jvālābhujair āyataiḥ ।
karma kṣetraviśeṣabṛṃhitaphalaṃ karmāntarāvyāhataṃ
nikṣepaṃ vapuṣo vipākacapalaṃ nodīkṣate prāṇinām ।।
