mahāpāśupatān dṛṣṭvā samutthāya maheśvaraḥ ।
saṃpariṣvajatādhyakṣāṃste praṇemurmaheśvaram ।।
tatastadadbhutatamaṃ dṛṣṭvā sarve gaṇeśvarāḥ suciraṃ vismitākṣāśca vailakṣyamagamat param ।।
vismitākṣān gaṇān dṛṣṭvā sailadiryogināṃ varaḥ ।
prāha prahasya deveśaṃ śūlapāṇiṃ gaṇādhipam ।।
