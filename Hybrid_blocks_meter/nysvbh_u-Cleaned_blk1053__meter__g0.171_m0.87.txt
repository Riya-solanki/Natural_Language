ayaṃ khalu vādo nityaṃ pratyācaṣṭe nityasya ca pratyākhyānamanupapannam ।
kasmāt ?, yathopalabdhi vyavasthānāt ।
yasyotpattivināśadharmakatvamupalabhyate
pramāṇatastadanityam yasya nopalabhyate tad
viparītam, na ca paramasūkṣmāṇāṃ bhūtānām ākāśakāladigātmamanasāṃ
tadguṇānāṃ ca keṣāñcit sāmānyaviśeṣasamavāyānāṃ
cotpattivināśadharmakatvaṃ pramāṇata upalabhyate
tasmād nityānyetānīti ।।
