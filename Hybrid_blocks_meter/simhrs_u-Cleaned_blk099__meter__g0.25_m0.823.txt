tatha pīṭhamardaḥ-
nāyakānucaro bhaktaḥ kiñcid ūnaś ca tad-guṇaiḥ । 
pīṭhamarda iti khyātaḥ kupita-strī-prasādakaḥ ।।
kāma-tantra-kalā-vedī viṭa ity abhidhīyate । 
sandhāna-kuśalaś ceṭaḥ kalahaṃsādiko mataḥ ।
vikṛtāṅga-vaco-veṣair hāsya-kārī vidūṣakaḥ ।।
