aṛtau bhāryāmupeyāt' ityanujñā ।
āgurvāṅganāṃ nopagacchet' iti parihāraḥ ।
tathā aagnīṣomīyaṃ paśuṃ saṃjñapayet' ityanujñā ।
ana hiṃsyātsarvābhūtāni' iti parihāraḥ ।
evaṃ loke api mitramupasevitavyamityanujñā ।
śatruḥ parihartavya iti parihāraḥ ।
evaṃprakārāvanujñāparihārāvekatve apyātmano dehasaṃbandhātsyātām ।
dehaiḥ saṃbandho dehasaṃbandhaḥ ।
kaḥ punardehasaṃbandhaḥ ।
dehādirayaṃ saṃghāto ahamevetyātmani viparītapratyayotpattiḥ ।
dṛṣṭā ca sā sarvaprāṇināmahaṃ gacchāmyahamāgacchāmyahamandho ahamanandho ahaṃ mūḍho ahamamūḍha ityevamātmikā ।
nahyasyāḥ samyagdarśanādanyannivārakamasti ।
