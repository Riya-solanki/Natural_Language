etasminn abhilāṣādi maraṇāntam anekadhā ।
tat-tat-sañcāri-bhāvānām utkaṭatvād daśā bhavet ।। 
tathāpi prāktanair asyā daśāvasthāḥ samāsataḥ ।
proktās tad-anurodhena tāsāṃ lakṣaṇam ucyate ।। 
abhilāṣaś cintānusmṛti-guṇa-saṅkīrtanodvegāḥ ।
savilāpā unmāda-vyādhī jaḍatā mṛtiś ca tāḥ kramaśaḥ ।।
