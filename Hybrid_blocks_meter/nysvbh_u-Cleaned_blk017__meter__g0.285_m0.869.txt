atha dṛṣṭāntaḥ।
pratyakṣaviṣayo artho dṛṣṭāntaḥ, catra laukikaparīkṣakāṇāṃ darśanaṃ na vayāhanyate।
sa ca prameyam।
tasya pṛthagvacanaṃ ca।
tadāśrayāvanumānāgamau।
tasmin sati syātāmanumānāgamau, asati ca na syātām।
tadāśrayā nyāyapravṛttiḥ।
dṛṣṭāntavirodhena ca parapakṣapratiṣedho vacanīyo bhavati।
dṛṣṭānsamādhinā ca svapakṣaḥ sthāpanīyo bhavati।
nāstikaśca dṛṣṭāntamabhyupagacchan nāstikatvaṃ jahāti।
anabhyupagacchan kiṃsādhanaḥ paramupālabheta ?
niruktena ca dṛṣṭāntena śakyamabhidhātum,
