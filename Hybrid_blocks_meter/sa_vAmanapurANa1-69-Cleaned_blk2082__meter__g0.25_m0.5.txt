viharadhvaṃ mahīpṛṣṭhe pūjyamānā narairiha ।
vasatirbhavatīnāṃ ca udyāneṣu vaneṣu ca ।।
vanaspatiṣu vṛkṣeṣu gacchadhvaṃ praṇipatyāmbikāṃ kramāt ।।
dakṣu savāsu jagmustāḥ stūyamānāśca kinnaraiḥ ।
andhako api smṛtiṃ labdhvā apaśyannadrinandinīm ।
svabalaṃ nirjitaṃ dṛṣṭvā tataḥ pātālamādravāt ।।
