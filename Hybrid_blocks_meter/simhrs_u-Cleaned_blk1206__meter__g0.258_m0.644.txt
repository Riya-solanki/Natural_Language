atha sandhiḥ-
ekaikasyās tv avasthāyāḥ prakṛtyā caikayaikayā ।
yogaḥ sandhir iti jñeyo nāṭya-vidyā-viśāradaiḥ ।। 
patākāyās tv avasthānaṃ kvacid asti na vā kvacit ।
patākā-virahe bījaṃ binduṃ vā kalpayet sudhīḥ ।। 
mukhya-prayojana-vaśāt kathāṅgānāṃ samanvaye ।
avāntarārtha-sambandhaḥ sandhiḥ sandhāna-rūpataḥ ।।
