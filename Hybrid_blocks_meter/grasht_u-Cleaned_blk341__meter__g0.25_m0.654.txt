jāraṇa of lead
varanāgaṃ rasarājaṃ bījavaraṃ sāritaṃ tathā tritayam ।
gandhakaśilālasahitaṃ nirnāgaṃ dīpavartito bhavati ।।
baddhvā sudṛḍhe vastre poṭalikāyāṃ śikhīkṛto dīpaḥ ।
taile magnaṃ kṛtvā nirnāgaṃ jāyate kṣipram ।।
