vidhyantaramāha rasetyādi ।।
evamuktavidhānena rasavaikrāntakaṃ dvandvānvitaṃ svakīyadvandvasahitaṃ milati pṛthagbhāvaṃ tyajati ।।
kena samaṃ hemnā saha ।।
tadrasavaikrāntasattvaṃ hemnā saha nirvyūḍhaṃ kuryāt tena rasavaikrāntasattvahemayogena raso bandhamupayāti baddho bhavatīti ।।
