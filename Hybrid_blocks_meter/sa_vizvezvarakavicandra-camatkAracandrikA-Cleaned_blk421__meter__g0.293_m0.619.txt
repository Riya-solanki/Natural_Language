atra śṛṅgāratvādarthasya mṛdutvam । bandhasya mahāpraṇarūkṣavarṇaprāyatvaṃ prauḍhatvam ।
prauḍhārthamṛdusaṃdarbā sātvatī vṛttiriṣyate ।।
yathā
śrīsiṅgabhūpakaravālahalārimauler- muktāphalāni patitānyabhitaḥ sphuranti ।
kīlālamāṃsakalitāsu raṇasthalīṣu bījāṅkurāṇi yaśasāmiva nirmalāni ।।
