cittaṃ yatra prasādya kṣaṇam api manujo anuttame dakṣiṇīye
śrīsaubhāgyaprakarṣaṃ bhavagatiṣu paraṃ prāpya nirdvandvaramyam ।
paścād ālokya lokaṃ vitathavilapitaṃ pratyayagrāhamātraṃ
jñātvā prāpnoti śāntaṃ katham iva na mahān puṇyarāśir bhavet saḥ ।।
