tadbhūtasya tu nātadbhāvo jaiminer api niyamāt tadrūpābhāvebhyaḥ ।
ārohavatpratyavaroho api kadācidūrdhvaretasāṃ syāditi mandāśaṅkānivāraṇārthamidamadhikaraṇam ।
pūrvadharmeṣu yāgahomādiṣu ।
rāgato vā gṛhastho ahaṃ patnyādiparivṛtaḥ syāmiti ।
niyamaṃ vyācaṣṭe-tathāhi-atyantamātmānamiti ।
atadrūpatāmārohatulyatābhāvaṃ vyācaṣṭe-yathāca brahmacaryaṃ samāpyeti ।
abhāvaṃ śiṣṭācārābhāvaṃ vibhajate-na caivamācārāḥ śiṣṭā iti ।
atirohitārthamanyat ।।
