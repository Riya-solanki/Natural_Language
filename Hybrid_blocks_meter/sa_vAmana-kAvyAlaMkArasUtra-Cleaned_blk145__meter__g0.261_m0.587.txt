evaṃ guṇālaṅkārāṇāṃ bhedaṃ darśayitvā śabdaguṇanirūpaṇārthamāha 
ojaḥprasādaśleṣasamatāsamādhimādhuryasaukumāryodāratārthavyaktikāntayo bandhaguṇāḥ ।।bandhaḥ padaracanā, tasya guṇaḥ bandhaguṇāḥ ojaḥprabhṛtayaḥ ।।
tān krameṇa darśayitumāha 
gāḍhabandhatvamojaḥ ।।
