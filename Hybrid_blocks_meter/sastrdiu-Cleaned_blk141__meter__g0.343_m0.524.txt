nanu ṣaṣṭho api vikalpo asti tadevedamiti tatkathaṃ pañcadhā vikalpa ityucyate ।
natvayaṃ jātivikalpo vyaktipratyabhijñā hīyam ।
na saṃjñāvikalpaḥ ।
anavagatasaṃjñānāmapi bhāvāt ।
tiryañco api hyadyajātāḥ svamātaraṃ prathamamupalabhya kṣaṇāntare punarupalabhamānāḥ saiveyamiti śabdānuvedhaśūnyameva pratyabhijānanti, tasmādiyaṃ ṣaṣṭhī kalpanetipṭhacavidhatvānupapattiḥ ।।
