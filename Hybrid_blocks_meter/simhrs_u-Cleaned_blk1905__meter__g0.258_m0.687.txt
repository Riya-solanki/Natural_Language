atha prahasanam-
vastu-sandhy-aṅka-lāsyāṅga-vṛttayo yatra bhāṇavat । 
raso hāsyaḥ pradhānaṃ syād etat prahasanaṃ matam ।।
viśeṣeṇa daśāṅgāni kalpayed atra tāni tu । 
avagalitāvaskandau vyavahāro vipralambha upapattiḥ ।
bhayam anṛtaṃ vibhrāntir gadgada-vāk ca pralāpaś ca ।।
