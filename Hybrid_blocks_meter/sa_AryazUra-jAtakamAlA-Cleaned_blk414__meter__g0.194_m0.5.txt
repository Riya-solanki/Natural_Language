maitrī tasya balaṃ dhvajāgraśabalaṃ tvācāramātraṃ balaṃ
nāsau vetti rūṣaṃ na cāha paruṣaṃ samyak ca gāṃ rakṣati ।
dharmastasya nayo na nītinikṛtiḥ pūjārthamarthaḥ satām
ityāścaryamayo api durjanadhanaṃ garvaṃ ca nālambate ।।
