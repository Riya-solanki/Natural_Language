jātaḥ khalvayaṃ
kumāro asmin janmany agṛhīteṣu
harṣabhayaśokahetuṣu harṣabhayaśokān
pratipadyate liṅgānumeyān, te ca smṛtyanubandhādutpadyante
nānyathā smṛtyanubandhaśca pūrvābhyāsamantareṇa
na bhavati pūrvābhyāsaśca pūrvajanmani sati
nānyathā iti sidhyatyetat avatiṣṭhate ayamūrdhvam
śarīrabhedāditi ।।
