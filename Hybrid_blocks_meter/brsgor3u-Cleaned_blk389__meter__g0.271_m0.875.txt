saṃnyāsagārhasthyadvayamatra sūtrakṛtoktam ।
tato anyadāśramadvayaṃ nāstīti kasyacidbhramaḥ syāttaṃ nirasyati-maunavaditi ।

itarayorapīti vācye bahūktiravāntarabhedamapekṣya ।
