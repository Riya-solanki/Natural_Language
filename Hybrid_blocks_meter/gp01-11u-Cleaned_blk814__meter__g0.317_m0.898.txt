gokula-pati-kula-tilaka tvam asīha ।
kṛta-sukṛta-vraja-racita-sukha-vraja, nayanānandi-samīha ।।
ānandodbhava-janma-mahotsava-nandita-gopa-samāja ।
pūta-nikāmṛti-nava-maṅgala-kṛti-valayita-gokula-rāja ।।
dhairya-nivartana-śakaṭa-vivartanam anu bhavyena parīta ।
satṛṇāvartaka-vāyu-nivartaka-parameśenānīta ।।
madhura-prāṅgaṇa-viracita-riṅgana jalaja-nayana supuṇya ।
nānā-keliṣu nṛtya-kalāliṣu darśita-vara-naipuṇya ।।
tarṇaka-vāladhi-śabalita-tanvadhi-valayita-mañjula-śobha ।
jaratī-nivahe kautuka-kalahe prabalita-mithyā-lobha ।।
māṃ mātaram anu sukham udvitanu pratataṃ satataṃ kṛṣṇa ।
drutam urarīkuru tanu-vṛddhiṃ puru-khelāvali-kṛta-tṛṣṇa ।।
tribhuvana-darśana-vismaya-marśana-niścita-vaiṣṇava-māya ।
hari-varivasyā-sukhadatamaḥ syā vigata-jarāmara-kāya ।। ।। । [ x x ]
