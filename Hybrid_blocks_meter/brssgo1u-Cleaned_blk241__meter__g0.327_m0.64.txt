naiṣa doṣaḥ ।
bhūtākāśasyāpi vāyvādikrameṇa kāraṇatvopapatteḥ ।
vijñāyate hi- atasmādvā etasmādātmana ākāśaḥ saṃbhūtaḥ ।
ākāśādvāyuḥ ।
vāyoragniḥ' ityādi ।
jyāyastvaparāyaṇatve api bhūtāntarāpekṣayopapadyete bhūtākāśasyāpi ।
tasmādākāśaśabdena bhūtākāśasya grahaṇamityevaṃ prāpte brūmaḥ- aākāśastalliṅgāt' ākāśaśabdena brahmaṇo grahaṇaṃ yuktam ।
kutaḥ, talliṅgāt ।
parasya hi brahmaṇa idaṃ liṅgam- asarvāṇi ha vā imāni bhūtānyākāśādeva samutpadyante' iti ।
parasmāddhi brahmaṇo bhūtānāmutpattiriti vedānteṣu maryādā ।
