atha pravāsaḥ-
pūrva-saṅgatayor yūnor bhaved deśāntarādibhiḥ ।
caraṇa-vyavadhānaṃ yat sa pravāsa itīryate ।। 
taj-janyo vipralambho' pi pravāsatvena saṃmataḥ ।
harṣa-garva-mada-vrīḍā varjayitvā samīritāḥ ।। 
śṛṅgāra-yogyāḥ sarve' pi pravāsa-vyabhicāriṇaḥ ।
kāryataḥ sambhramāc chāpāt sa tridhā tatra kāryajaḥ ।। 
buddhi-pūrvatayā yūnoḥ saṃvidhāna-vyapekṣayā ।
vṛtto vartiṣyamāṇaś ca vartamāna iti tridhā ।।
