[] kaṃsas tu keśi-dhvaṃsanam api śṛṇvan vyagra-manā vigra iva gṛhān na niḥsasāra ।
so' yaṃ tava vrajādhīśa sutaḥ sūtamanorathaḥ ।
keśinaṃ cātra yaścakre yamasya prativeśinam ।।
iti śrī-śrī-gopāla-campūm anu keśi-dhvaṃsana-śaṃsanaṃ nāma dvātriṃśaṃ pūraṇam ।।
