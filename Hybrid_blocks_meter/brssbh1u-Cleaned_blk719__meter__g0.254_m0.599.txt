itaśca na pradhānasyāvyaktaśabdavācyatvaṃ jñeyatvaṃ vā ।
yasmāntrayāṇāmeva padārthānmagnijīvaparamātmanāmasmingranthe kaṭhavallīṣu varapradhānasāmarthyādvaktavyatopanyāso dṛśyate ।
tadviṣaya eva ca praśnaḥ ।
nāto anyasya praśna upanyāso vāsti ।
tatra tāvat asa tvamagniṃ svargyamadhyeṣi mṛtyo prabrūhi taṃ śraddadhānāya mahyam' ityagniviṣayaḥ praśnaḥ ।
ayeyaṃ prete' vicikitsā manuṣye astītyeke nāyamastīti caike ।
etadvidyāmanuśiṣṭastvayāhaṃ varāṇāmeṣa varastṛtīyaḥ ।।
a iti jīvaviṣayaḥ praśnaḥ ।
aanyatra dharmādantrādharmānyatrāsmatkṛtākṛtāt ।
anyatra bhūtācca bhavyācca yattatpaśyasi tadvada ।।
a iti paramātmaviṣayaḥ ।
prativacanamapi alokādimamagniṃ tamuvāca tasmai yā iṣṭakāyāvatīrvā yathā vā' ityagniviṣayam ।
ahanta ta idaṃ pravakṣyāmi guhyaṃ brahma sanātanam ।
yathā ca maraṇaṃ prāpya ātmā bhavati gautama ।
yonimanye prapadyante śarīratvāya dehinaḥ ।
sthāṇumanye anusaṃyanti yathākarma yathāśrutam' iti ।
vyavahitaṃ jīvaviṣayam ।
ana jāyate mriyate vā vipaścit' ityādibahuprapañcaṃ paramātmaviṣayam ।
naivaṃ pradhānaviṣayaḥ praśno asti ।
apṛṣṭatvāccānupanyasanīyatvaṃ tasyeti ।
