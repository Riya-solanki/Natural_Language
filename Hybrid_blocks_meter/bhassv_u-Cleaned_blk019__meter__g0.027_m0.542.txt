{yaugandharāyaṇaḥ} -
{upasṛtya} bhoḥ kiṅkṛteyam utsāraṇā 
{kāñcukīyaḥ} - bhos tapasvin 
{yaugandharāyaṇaḥ} -
{ātmagatam} tapasvinn iti guṇavān khalv ayam ālāpaḥ 
aparicayāt tu na śliṣyate me manasi 
{kāñcukīyaḥ} - bhoḥ śrūyatām 
eṣā khalu gurubhir abhihitanāmadheyasyāsmākaṃ mahārājadarśakasya bhaginī padmāvatī nāma 
saiṣā no mahārājamātaraṃ mahādevīm āśramasthām abhigamyānujñātā tatrabhavatyā rājagṛham eva yāsyati 
tad adyāsminn āśramapade vāso abhipreto asyāḥ
