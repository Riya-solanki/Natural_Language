vedāntamīmāṃsāśāstrasya vyācikhyāsitasya asmābhiḥ, idamādimaṃ sūtram ।
pūjitavicāravacano mīmāṃsāśabdaḥ ।
paramapuruṣārthahetubhūtasūkṣmatamārthanirṇayaphalatayā ca vicārasya ca pūjitatā ।
tasya mīmāṃsāyāḥ śāstram, sā hyanena śiṣyate śiṣyebhyo yathāvatpratipādyata iti ।
sūtraṃ ca bahvarthasūcanāt bhavati ।
yathāhuḥ-
