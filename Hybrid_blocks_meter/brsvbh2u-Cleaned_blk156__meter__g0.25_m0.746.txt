samavadhānaṃ kāraṇānāṃ vinyāsabhedatatprayojanābhijñaprekṣāvatpūrvakaṃ dṛṣṭamiti nācetanādbhavitumarhati ।
tadidamuktambhavedupapannaḥ saṃghāto yadi saṃghātasya kiñcinnimittamavagamyeteti ।
itaretarapratyayatve apīti ।
itaretarahetutve apītyarthaḥ ।
uktamabhisaṃdhimavidvān paricodayatinanvavidyādibhirarthādākṣīpyata iti ।
pariharatiatrocyate, yadi tāvaditi ।
kimākṣepa utpādanam, āho jñāpanam ।
tatra na tāvat kāraṇamanyathānupapadyamānaṃ kāryamutpādayati, kintu
