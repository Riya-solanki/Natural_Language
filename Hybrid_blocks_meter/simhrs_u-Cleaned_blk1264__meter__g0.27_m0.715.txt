atha vidhutam-
nāyakāder īpsitānām arthānām anavāptitaḥ ।
aratir yad bhaved tad dhi vidvadbhir vidhutaṃ matam । 
athavānunayotkarṣaṃ vidhutaṃ syān nirākṛtiḥ ।। 
yathā tatraiva bhārgava-bhaṅga-nāmani caturthe' ṅke, śatānandaḥ-
