atideśo ayam ।
etena viyadvyākhyānena mātariśvāpi viyadāśrayo vāyurvyākhyātaḥ ।
tatrāpyete yathāyogaṃ pakṣā racayitavyāḥ ।
na vāyurutpadyate chāndogānāmutpattiprakaraṇe anāmnānādityekaḥ pakṣaḥ ।
asti tu taittirīyāṇāmutpattiprakaraṇa āmnānam aākāśādvāyuḥ' iti pakṣāntaram ।
tataśca śrutyorvipratiṣedhe sati gauṇī vāyorutpattiśrutisaṃbhavādityaparo abhiprāyaḥ ।
asaṃbhavaśca asaiṣānastamitā devatā yadvāyuḥ' ityastamayapratiṣedhāt, amṛtatvādiśravaṇācca ।
pratijñānuparodhādyāvadvikāraṃ ca vibhāgābhyupagamādutpadyate vāyuriti siddhāntaḥ ।
astamayapratiṣedho aparavidyāviṣaya āpekṣikaḥ ।
agnyādīnāmiva vāyorastamayābhāvāt ।।
kṛtapratividhānaṃ cāmṛtatvādiśravaṇam ।
