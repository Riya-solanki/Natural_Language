tatraiṣāṃ samayahṛdayāni bhavanti ।
oṃ vajracakra hūṃ ।।
oṃ vajraghaṇṭa hūṃ ।।oṃ vajradaṇḍakāṣṭha hūṃ ।।oṃ vajrāyudha hūṃ ।।
vajravegena cākramya dvitīyaṃ maṇḍalaṃ tathā ।
vajrakuṇḍalipūrvāṃstu vajrakodhānniveśayet ।।
