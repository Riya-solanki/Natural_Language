atha śuddhatyāgaḥ ।
granthamabhyasya medhāvī jñānavijñānatatparaḥ ।
palālamiva dhānyārthī tyajedgranthamaśeṣataḥ ।।
padamicchasi brahmatvaṃ tadā vijñānataḥ śṛṇu ।
sarvārtheṣu ca vairāgyaṃ sarvabhūteṣu cātmatā ।।
muktimicchasi cettvantu viṣayānviṣavattyaja ।
kṣamārjavadayātoṣasatyaṃ pīyūṣavadbhaja ।।
manasā dhyāyate yogī kṛpaṇastu dhanaṃ yathā ।
manasā yena pītvā ca tena jitvā jagattrayam ।।
vihāya kāmānyaḥ sarvānpumāṃścarati nispṛhaḥ ।
nirmamo nirahaṅkāraḥ sa śāntimadhigacchati ।।
akiñcanaśca dāntaśca śāntaśca samamānasaḥ ।
sa liṅgānāśramaṃ tyaktvā caredavidhigocaraḥ ।।
dehe ca śṛṇu taṃ tāta guptaprakaṭalakṣaṇam ।
daśaguptakaraṃ mokṣadvādaśaṃ sahajasthitiḥ ।।
nirapekṣaṃ muniṃ śāntaṃ nirvairaṃ samadarśanam ।
anuvrajyāmahaṃ nityaṃ pūyayedaṅghrireṇubhiḥ ।।
kadā śambho bhaviṣyāmi karmanirmūlanakṣamaḥ ।
ekākī nispṛhaḥ śānto pāṇipātro digambaraḥ ।।
sarvadharmānparityajya māmekaṃ śaraṇaṃ vraja ।
ahaṃ tvāṃ sarvapāpebhyo mokṣayiṣyāmi mā śucaḥ ।।
nirālambapadaṃ prāpya citte tatra layaṃ gate ।
nivartante kriyāḥ sarvāḥ sa yogī nirguṇaḥ paraḥ ।।
viṣayendriyakāraṇamākarṣacittavibhramaḥ ।
yathā bahusapatnīnāṃ luṭhanti patimekataḥ ।।
sādhu sādhu śṛṇu svārthamasthiyantrācca dūrataḥ ।
asya bhāgakṛtā loke mamatvaṃ śvānavatkṛtam ।।
śṛṇu tāta dvayaṃ tyājyamāśā bhedastathaiva ca ।
āśayā dīnatāṃ kṛtvā bhedaśca bhedavardhanam ।।
rodanahāsyakā a; anena piśācasthitimāśrayet ।
asya saṅgaṃ sadā tyajya bhava cinmātra sarvadā ।।
chāyākāryaṃ yathā nāsti tathaivāyaṃ hi dehakaḥ ।
tattvaṃ gṛharasaṃ tyajya paramāṃ sthitimāśraya ।।
goṣpadaṃ pṛthivī meruḥ sthāṇurākāśamudrikā ।
tṛṇaṃ tribhuvanaṃ rāma vairāgyālaṃkṛtā kṛtiḥ ।
manuṣyāṇāṃ sahasreṣu kaścidyatati siddhaye ।।
iti vairāgyam ।।
