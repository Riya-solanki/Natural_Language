aṅgeṣūpāsaneṣu saṃśayaḥ ।
kiṃ tāni yajamānakarmāṇyāhosvidṛtvikkarmāṇīti ।
kiṃ tāvatprāptam yajamānakarmāṇīti ।
kutaḥ - phalaśruteḥ ।
phalaṃ hi śrūyate - avarṣati hāsmai varṣayati ha ya etadevaṃ vidvānvṛṣṭau pañcavidhaṃ sāmopāste' ityādi ।
tacca svāmigāmi nyāyyam ।
tasya sāṅge prayoge adhikṛtatvāt ।
adhikṛtādhikāratvāccaivañjātīyakasya ।
phalaṃ ca kartaryupāsanānāṃ śrūyate - avarṣatyasmai ya upāste' ityādi ।
