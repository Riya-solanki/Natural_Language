aṇu mana ekaṃ
ceti dharmasamuccayaḥ jñānāyaugapadyāt,
mahattve manasaḥ sarvendriyasaṃyogād yugapad
viṣayagrahaṇaṃ syāditi ।।
manasaḥ khalu bhoḥ
sendriyasya śarīre vṛttilābho nānyatra
śarīrāt, jñātuśca puruṣasya śarīrāyatanā
buddhyādayo viṣayopabhogo
jihāsitahānam īpsitāvāptiśca sarve
ca śarīrāśrayā vyavahārāḥ, tatra khalu
vipratipatteḥ saṃśayaḥ kimayaṃ
puruṣakarmanimittaḥ śarīrasargaḥ ? atha vā bhūtamātrādakarmanimittaḥ ?
iti, śrūyate khalvatra vipratipattiriti।
