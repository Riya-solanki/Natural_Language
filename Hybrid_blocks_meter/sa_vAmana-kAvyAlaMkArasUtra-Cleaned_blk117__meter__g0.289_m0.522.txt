sa mahātmā bhāgyavaśānmahāpadamupāgataḥ ।
kiṃ bhāgyavaśānmahāpadamupāgataḥ, āhosvidabhāgyavaśānmahatīmāpadamiti saṃśayakṛd vākyaṃ, prakaraṇādyabhāve satīti ।।māyādivikalpitārthamprayuktam ।।
māyādinā kalpito'rtho yasmiṃstanmāyādikalpitārthamaprayuktam । atra stokamudāharaṇam ।।
kramahīnārthamapakramam ।।
uddeśotānāmanuddeśitānāṃ ca kramaḥ sambandhaḥ । tena vihīno'rtho yasmiṃstatkramahīnārthamapakramam । yathā
