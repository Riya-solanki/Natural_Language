prāṅniṣpatterniṣpattidharmakaṃ
nāsat upādānaniyamāt ।
kasyacidutpattaye kiñcidupādeyaṃ na sarvaṃ
sarvasyety asadbhāve niyamo nopapadyate
iti।
na sat prāgutpattervidyamānasyotpattiranupapanneti।
na sadasat sadasatorvaidharmyāt asat' ityarthābhyanujñā aasat' ityarthapratiṣedhaḥ
etayorvyāghāto vaidharmyaṃ vyāghātādavyatirekānupapattiriti
।।
