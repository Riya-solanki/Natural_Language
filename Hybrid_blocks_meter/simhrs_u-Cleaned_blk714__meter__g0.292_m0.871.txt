atha nidrā-
mada-svabhāva-vyāyāma-niścintatva-śramādibhiḥ । 
mano-nimīlanaṃ nidrā ceṣṭās tatrāsya-gauravam ।।
āghūrṇamāna-netratvam aṅgānāṃ parimardanam । 
niḥśvāsocchvāsane sanna-gātratvaṃ netra-mīlanam ।
śarīrasya ca saṅkoco jāḍyaṃ cety evam ādayaḥ ।।
