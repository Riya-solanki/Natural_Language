dhṛteśca mahimno asyāsminnupalabdheḥ ।
sautro dhṛtiśabdo bhāvavacanaḥ ।
dhṛteśca parameśvara eva daharākāśaḥ ।
kutaḥ, asya dhāraṇalakṣaṇasya mahimno asminneveśvara eva śrutyantareṣūpalabdheḥ ।
nigadavyākhyānamasya bhāṣyam ।।
