tathāhi- vasante brāhmaṇamupanayīta, grīṣme rājanyaṃ, śaradivaiśyam' iti dvitīyānirdeśādupanayanasaṃskṛtāstraivarṇikāḥ kimasmābhiḥ kartavyamityapekṣante ।
tatsaṃnidhau cādhyayanamanirdiṣṭakartṛkaṃ vidhīyamānaṃ kartāramapekṣate ।
