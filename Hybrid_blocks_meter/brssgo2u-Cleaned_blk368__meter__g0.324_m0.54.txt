āśrayāśrayaḥ saṃghātakartā tacchūnyeṣvityarthaḥ ।
aāśrayāśrayiśūnyeṣu'iti pāṭhe upakāryopakārakatvaśūnyeṣvityarthaḥ ।
dvitīyaṃ śaṅkate-athāyamiti ।
saṃghātasyāvidyādīnāṃ cotpattāvanyonyāśrayaḥ syāditi dūṣayati-kathamiti ।
svābhāvikaḥ khalvayaṃ saṃghātānāṃ hetuhetumadbhāvena pravāho na saṃhantāramapekṣate, pūrvasaṃghātāśrayā avidyādaya uttarasaṃghātapravartakā iti nānyonyāśrayadoṣo apītyāśaṅkate-atha manyasa iti ।
svabhāvasya niyamāniyamayorapasiddhāntāpātaḥ syāditi parihārārthaḥ ।
pūryate galati ceti pudgalo dehaḥ ।
kiñca bhoktuḥ kṣaṇikatvapakṣe bhogāpavargavyavahāro api durghaṭa ityāha-api ceti ।
yo yadicchati sa tatkāle nāsti cedicchāvyarthā, asti cet kṣaṇikatvabhaṅga ityarthaḥ ।
prakṛtaṃ saṃghātanirāsamupasaṃharati-tasmāditi ।।
