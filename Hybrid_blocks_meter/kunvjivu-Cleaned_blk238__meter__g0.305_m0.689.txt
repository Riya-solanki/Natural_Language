atrāsminnevaṃvidhaiḥ padairlāvaṇyamatiricyate paripoṣaṃ prāpnoti ।
kīdṛśaiḥparasparamanyonyaṃ protaiḥ saṃśleṣaṃ nītaiḥ ।
anyacca kīdṛśaiḥaluptavisargāntaiḥ, aluptavisargāḥ śrūyamāṇavisarjanīyā antā yeṣāṃ tāni tathoktāni taiḥ ।
hrasvaiśca laghubhiḥ ।
saṃyogebhyaḥ pūrvaiḥ ।
atiricyate iti saṃbandhaḥ ।
tadidamatra tātparyampūrvoktalakṣaṇaṃ lāvaṇyaṃ vidyamānamanenātiriktatāṃ nīyate ।
yathā
