niyama ityatrārthābhyanujñā aniyama iti tasya pratiṣedhaḥ anujñātatiṣiddhayośca vyāghātādanarthāntaratvaṃ na bhavati
aniyamaśca niyatatvād niyamo na bhavatīti।
nātrārthasya tathābhāvaḥ pratiṣidhyate, kiṃ tarhi ? tathābhūtasyārthasya
niyamaśabdenābhidhīyamānasya niyatatvād
niyamaśabda evopapadyate, soyaṃ niyamādaniyame
pratiṣedho na bhavatīti ।।
