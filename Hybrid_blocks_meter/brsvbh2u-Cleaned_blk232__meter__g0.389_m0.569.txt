vipratiṣedhāc ca ।
guṇibhyaḥ khalvātmabhyo jñānādīn guṇān bhedenoktvā punarabhedaṃ brūteātmāna evaite bhagavanto vāsudevā iti ।
ādigrahaṇena pradyumnāniruddhayormano ahaṅkāralakṣaṇatayātmano bhedamabhidhāyātmana evaita iti tadviruddhābhedābhidhānamaparaṃ saṃgṛhītam ।
vedavipratiṣedho vyākhyātaḥ ।।
