jāraṇa of lead
kṛtvātra dīrghamūṣāṃ sudṛḍhāṃ dhmātaṃ tu bhasmagartāyām ।
kṣiptvā śilālacūrṇaṃ paścātsūtaṃ tataḥ śilācūrṇaṃ ।।
saṃsthāpya bhasmanāto dhmātaṃ syātsvāṃgaśītalaṃ yāvat ।
ākṛṣya tatra sūtaṃ jñātvā nāgaṃ subhakṣitaṃ sakalam ।।
