viraktaṃ ca nityam eva prakṛti-vikriyāto vidyān mukha-varṇāc ca
ūnam atiriktaṃ vā dadāti
pratilomaiḥ sambadhyate
vyapadiśyānyat karoti
ucitam ācchinatti
pratijñātaṃ vismarati anyathā vā yojayati
svpakṣaiḥ saṃjñayā bhāṣate
mitra-kāryam apadiśyānyatra śete
pūrva-saṃsṛṣṭāyāś ca parijanena mithaḥ kathayati
tasya sāra-dravyāṇi prāg avabodhād anyāpadeśena haste kurvīta
tāni cāsyā hastād uttama-rṇaḥ prasahya gṛhṇīyāt
vivadamānena saha dharmastheṣu vyavahared
