tadetat vyācaṣṭe-tuśabda iti ।
tadityuttarapakṣapratijñāṃ vibhajate-tadbrahmeti ।
pūrvapakṣī karkaśāśayaḥ pṛcchati-katham ।
kutaḥ prakārādityarthaḥ ।
siddhāntī svapakṣe hetuṃ prakārabhedamāha-samanvayāt ।
samyaganvayaḥ
