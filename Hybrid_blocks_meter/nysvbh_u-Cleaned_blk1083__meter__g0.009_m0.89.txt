atheme saṃkhyaikāntāḥ
sarvamekam sad aviśeṣāt, sarvaṃ dvedhā
nityānityabhedāt, sarvaṃ tredhā jñātā jñānaṃ
jñeyamiti, sarvaṃ caturdhā pramātā pramāṇaṃ
prameyaṃ pramitiriti, evaṃ yathāsaṃbhavamanye apīti।
tatra parīkṣā
