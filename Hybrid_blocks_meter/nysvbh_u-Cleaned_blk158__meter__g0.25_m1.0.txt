tantrārthasasthitiḥ tantrasaṃsthitiḥ।
tantramitaretarābhisambaddhasyārthasamūhasyopadeśaḥ śāstram।

abhyupagamasaṃsthitiranavadhāritārthaparigrahaḥ।
