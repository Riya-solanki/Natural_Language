viśeṣavidhānamāha mākṣikasattvam ityādi ।।
mākṣikasattvaṃ tāpyasāraṃ nāgaḥ sīsakaḥ taṃ vihāya nānyatkimapyasti krāmaṇaṃ na krāmaṇamiti bhāvaḥ ।।
khalviti jijñāsāyām ।।
dalasiddhe vidhau saṃskāraiḥ pūrṇatāṃ nīte sati asau vidhiḥ saphalaḥ ।।
