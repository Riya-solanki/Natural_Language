atha garvaḥ-
aiśvarya-rūpa-tāruṇya-kula-vidyā-balair api ।
iṣṭa-lābhādinānyeṣām avajñā garva īritaḥ ।। 
anubhāvā bhavanty atra gurv-ājñādy-ājñā-vyatikramaḥ ।
anuttara-praadānaṃ ca vaimukhyaṃ bhāṣaṇe' pi ca ।। 
vibhramāpahnutī vākya-pāruṣyam anavekṣaṇam ।
avekṣaṇaṃ nijāṅgānām aṅga-bhaṅgādayo' pi ca ।।
