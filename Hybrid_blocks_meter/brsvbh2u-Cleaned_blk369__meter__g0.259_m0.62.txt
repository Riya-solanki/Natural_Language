apica-
pañcavṛttir manovad vyapadiśyate ।
aviparyayo mithyājñānamatadrūpapratiṣṭham'yathā marumarīcikādiṣu salilādibuddhayaḥ ।
atadrūpapratiṣṭhatā ca saṃśaye apyasti tasyaikāpratiṣṭhānāt ।
ataḥ so api saṃgṛhītaḥ ।
aśabdajñānānupātī vastuśūnyo vikalpaḥ' ।
yadyapi mithyājñāne apyasti vastuśūnyatā tathāpi na tasya vyavahārahetutāsti ।
asya tu paṇḍitarūpavicārāsahasyāpi śabdajñānamāhātmyādvyavahārāhetubhāvo astyeva ।
yatha puruṣasya caitanyamiti ।
nahyatra ṣaṣṭhyarthaḥ saṃbandho asti, tasya bhedādhiṣṭhānatvāt ।
caitanyasya puruṣādatyantābhedāt ।
yadyapi cātrābhāvapratyayālambanā vṛttirneṣyate tathāpi vikṣepasaṃskāralakṣaṇā manovṛttirihāstyeveti sarvamavadātam ।।
