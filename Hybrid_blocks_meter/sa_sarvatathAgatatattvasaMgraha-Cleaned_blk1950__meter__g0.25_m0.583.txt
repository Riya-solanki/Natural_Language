pracaṇḍā duṣṭadamanī padmamuṣṭiḥ susādhikā ।
lāsyā ratiṃ dhanaṃ mālā sarvaṃ gītā sukhaṃ nṛtyā ।।
dhūpā lhādaṃ śubhaṃ puṣpādīpā dṛṣṭiṃ gandha sugandhatāṃ ।।
hayagrīvā samā[karṣaṇā]moghā tu vaśaṅkarī ।
padmasphoṭā mahābandhā sarvāveśā tu ghaṇṭike-ti ।।
