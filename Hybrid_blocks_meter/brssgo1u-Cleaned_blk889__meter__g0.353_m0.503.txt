tatparihartavyam ।
yatrocyate- parihṛtaṃ caitat anopāsātraividhyādāśritatvādiha tadyogāt' ityatra ।
trividhaṃ hyatropāsanamevaṃ sati prasajjyeta jīvopāsanaṃ mukhyaprāṇopāsanaṃ brahmopāsanaṃ ceti ।
na caitannyāyyam ।
upakramopasaṃhārābhyāṃ hi brahviṣayatvamasya vākyasyāvagamyate ।
tatropakramasya tāvadbrahmaviṣayatvaṃ darśitam ।
upasaṃhārasyāpi niratiśayaphalaśravaṇādbrahmaviṣayatvaṃ dṛśyate- asarvānpāpmano apahṛtya sarveṣāṃ ca bhūtānāṃ śraiṣṭhyaṃ svārājyamādhipatyaṃ paryeti ya evaṃ veda' iti ।
