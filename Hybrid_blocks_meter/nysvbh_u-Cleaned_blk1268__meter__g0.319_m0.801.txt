"tadartham" itiprakṛtam।
jñāyate aneneti jñānam ātmavidyāśāstraṃ tasya grahaṇam adhyayanadhāraṇe, abhyāsaḥ ।
satatakriyā ।
adhyayanaśravaṇacintanāni, tadvidyaiśca saha saṃvāda
iti prajñāparipākārthaṃ, paripākastu saṃśayacchedanam
avijñātārthabodhaḥ adhyavasitābhyanujñānamiti,
samāpavādaḥ saṃvādaḥ ।।
