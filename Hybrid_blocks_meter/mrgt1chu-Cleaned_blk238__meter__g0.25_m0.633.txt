athedānīṃ pāścātyapaṭalāntasūcitābhidhānasyātmano lakṣaṇamucyata iti pāṭalikaprākaraṇikau sambandhau jñeyau ।। 
sautrādayastu prāgvadunneyāḥ pratipaṭalamucyamānā atipaunaruktyamāvahanti ।। 
viśvasya jagato nimittaṃ pravartanaheturātmā tadbhogasādhanāya tanukaraṇabhuvanādīnāmutpatteḥ ।। 
tasyātmano lakṣaṇamīśvaroktyanantaram avasaraprāptaṃ kathyate ।। 
tac ca yadyapi ।
tena svabhāvasiddhena bhavitavyaṃ jagatkṛtā ।। 
ityatra īśvarasiddhyanuṣaṅgeṇa ।। 
caitanyaṃ dṛkkriyārūpaṃ tadastyātmani sarvadā ।
sarvataśca yato muktau śrūyate sarvatomukham ।। 
ityādinoktaprāyaṃ kathitakalpaṃ tathāpyuddeśaḥ sopapattikatatsvarūpasaṃkṣepa ucyate ।।
