atha virahotkaṇṭhitā-
anāgasi priyatame cirayaty utsukā tu yā । 
virahotkaṇṭhitā bhāva-vedibhiḥ sā samīritā ।।
asyās tu ceṣṭā hṛt-tāpo vepathuś cāṅga-sādanam । 
aratir bāṣpa-mokṣaś ca svāvasthā-kathanādayaḥ ।। athā mamaiva-
cirayati manaḥ-kānte kāntā nirāgasi sotsukā
madhu malayajaṃ mākandaṃ vā nirīkṣitum akṣamā ।
galita-palitaṃ no jānīte karād api kaṅkaṇaṃ
parabhṛta-rutaṃ śrutvā bāṣpaṃ vimuñcati vepate ।।
