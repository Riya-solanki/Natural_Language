samagrāṇāṃ rasānāṃ saṃghaṭanānāṃ ca ।
udāharaṇam parimlānaṃ pīnastanajaghanasaṃgādubhayata- stanormadhyasyāntaḥ parimilanamaprāpya haritam ।
idaṃ vyastanyāsaṃ śrlathabhujalatākṣepavalanaiḥ kṛśāhgyāḥ saṃtāpaṃ vadati bisinīpatraśayanam ।।
yadyapi guṇaparatantrāḥ saṃghaṭanādayastathāpi
