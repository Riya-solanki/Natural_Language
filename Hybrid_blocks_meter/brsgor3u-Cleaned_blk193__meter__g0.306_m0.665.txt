nāmyaikyāt nāmasaṃkaro yuktaḥ, tathā cākṣistho ahariti nāmavān satyabrahmatvādādityasthavaditi prāpte siddhāntasūtraṃyojayati-naveti ।
nāmnorūpāsanasthānaviśiṣṭasaṃbandhitvādityarthaḥ ।
tasyo apaniṣadaharahamiti ca vākyadvayena tacchabdaparāmṛṣṭayoḥ saṃnihitasthānaviśiṣṭayoḥ puruṣayornāmasaṃbandhapareṇopasaṃhārānumānaṃ bādhyamiti bhāvaḥ ।
viśeṣyaikyānnāmasaṃkara ityāśaṅkya sthānabhedena viśiṣṭapuruṣabhedānnāmavyavasthāmāha-nanvityādinā ।
viśiṣṭasaṃbandhe dṛṣṭāntamāha-astīti ।
pratidṛṣṭāntasya svarūpasaṃbandhitvādviśiṣṭe dhyeye prakṛte dṛṣṭāntaṃ nāstītyāha-grāmeti ।।
