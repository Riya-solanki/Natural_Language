sādharmyeṇa vaidharmyeṇa vā sāmānyaṃ viśeṣeṇa yat samarthyate viśeṣo vā sāmānyena so arthāntaranyāsaḥ ।
krameṇodāhaṇam nijadoṣāvṛtamanasāmatisundarameva bhāti viparītam ।
paśyati pittopahataḥ śaśiśubhraṃ śaṅkhamapi pītam ।।
susitavasanālaṃkārāyāṃ kadācana kaumudī- mahasi sudṛśi svairaṃ yāntyāṃ gato astamabhūdvidhuḥ ।
tadanu bhavataḥ kīrtiḥ kenāpyagīyata yena sā priyagṛhamagānmuktāśaṅkā kva nāsi śubhapradaḥ ।।
guṇānāmeva daurātmyāt dhuri dhuryo niyujyate ।
asaṃjātakiṇaskandhaḥ sukhaṃ svapiti gaurgaliḥ ।।
aho hi me bahvaparāddhamāyuṣā yadapriyaṃ vācyamidaṃ mayedṛśam ।
ta eva dhanyāḥ suhṛdaḥ parābhavaṃ jagatyadṛṣṭvaiva hi ye kṣayaṃ gatāḥ ।।
