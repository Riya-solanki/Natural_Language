iti ca brahmaṇo anupamānatvaṃ darśayati ।
aato anyadārtam' iti ca brahmaṇo anyeṣāmākāśādīnāmārtatvaṃ darśayati ।
tapasi brahmaśabdavadākāśasya janmaśrutergauṇatvamityetadākāśasaṃbhavaśrutyanumānābhyāṃ parihṛtam ।
tasmādbrahmakāryaṃ viyaditi siddham ।।
