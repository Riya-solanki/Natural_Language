prastāvanā-sthāpaneti dvidhā syād idam āmukham । 
vidūṣaka-naṭī-pāripārśvikaiḥ saha saṃlāpan ।।
stoka-vīthy-aṅga-sahitāny āmukhāṅgāni sūtra-bhṛt । 
yojayed yatra nāṭya-jñair eṣā prastāvanā smṛtā ।।
sarvāmukhāṅga-vīthy-aṅga-sametair vākya-vistaraiḥ । 
sūtradhāro yatra naṭī-vidūṣaka-naṭādibhiḥ ।।
saṃlapana prastutaṃ cārtham ākṣipet sthāpanā hi sā । 
śṛṅgāra-pracure nāṭye yogyaḥ syād āmukha-kramaḥ ।।
ratnāvalydike prāyo lakṣyatāṃ kovidair ayam । 
vīrādbhutādi prāye tu prāyaḥ prastāvanocitā ।।
anargha-rāghavādyeṣu prāyaśo vīkṣyatām iyam । 
hāsya-bībhatsa-raudrādi-prāye tu sthāpanā matā ।।
vīra-bhadra-vijṛmbhādau sā prāyeṇa samīkṣyatām । 
kathitāny āmukhāṅgāni vīthy-aṅgāni pracakṣmahe ।।
āmukhe' pi ca vīthyāṃ ca sādhāraṇye' pi saṃmataḥ । 
vīthy-aṅga-saṃprathā teṣāṃ vīthyām āvaśyakatvataḥ ।।
udghātyakāvalagita-prapañca-trigate chalam । 
vākkely-adhibale gaṇḍam avasyandita-nālike ।।
asat-pralāpa-vyāhārau mṛdavaṃ ca trayodaśa । 
tatrodghātyakam anyonyālāpa-mālā dvidhā hi tat ।
gūḍhāratha-pada-paryāya-kramāt praśnottara-kramāt ।।
