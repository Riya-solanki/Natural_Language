saṃniveśaviśeṣeṇa yatra nyastā varṇāḥ khaḍgamurajapajhādyākāramullāsayanti taccitraṃ kāvyam ।
kaṣṭaṃ kāvyametaditi diṅbhātraṃ pradarśyaṃte ।
udāharaṇam mārāriśakrarāmebhamukhairāsāraraṃhasā ।
sārārabdhastavā nityaṃ tadārtiharaṇakṣaṇā ।।
mātā natānāṃ saṃghaṭṭaḥ śriyāṃ bādhitasaṃbhramā ।
mānyātha sīmā rāmāṇāṃ śaṃ me diśyādumādimā ।।
saralā bahulārambhataralālibalāravā ।
vāralābahulāmandakaralābahulāmalā ।।
bhāsate pratibhāsāra rasābhātāhatāvibhā ।
bhāvitātmā śubhā vāde devābhā bata te sabhā ।।
rasāsāra rasā sārasāyatākṣa kṣatāyasā ।
sātāvāta tavātāsā rakṣatastvastvataṃkṣara ।।
saṃbhavino apyante prabhedāḥ śaktimātraprakāśakā na tu kāvyarūpatāṃ dadhatīti na pradarśyante ।।
