ityādāvabhinayādayaḥ ।
itthaṃ saṃyogādibhirardhāntarābhidhāyakatve nivārite apyanekārthasya śabdasya yat kvacidarthāntarapratipādanaṃ tatra nābhidhā niyamanāttasyāḥ ।
na ca lakṣaṇā mukhyārthabādhādyabhāvāt ।
api tvañjanaṃ vyañjanameva vyāpāraḥ ।
yathā bhadrātmano duradhirohatanorviśālavaṃśonnateḥ kṛtaśilīmukhasaṃgrahasya ।
yasyānupaplutagateḥ paravāraṇasya dānāmbusekasubhagaḥ satataṃ karo abhūt ।।
