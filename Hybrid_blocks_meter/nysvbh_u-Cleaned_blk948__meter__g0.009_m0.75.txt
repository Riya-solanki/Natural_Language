ayaṃ khalvadhyāpako adhīte vrajati kamaṇḍaluṃ
dhārayati panthānaṃ paśyati śṛṇotyāraṇyajān
śabdān bibheti vyālaliṅgāni bubhutsate
smarati ca gantavyaṃ sthānīyamiti
kramasyāgrahaṇād yugapadetāḥ kriyā iti
prāptaṃ manaso bahutvamiti ।।
