atha asūcyavibhāgaḥ-
asūcyaṃ tad dvidhā dṛśyaṃ śrāvyaṃ cādyaṃ tu darśayet । 
dvedhā dvitīyaṃ svagataṃ prakāśaṃ ceti bhedataḥ ।।
svagataṃ svaika-vijñeyaṃ prakāśaṃ tad dvidhā bhavet । 
sarva-prakāśaṃ niyata-prakāśaṃ ceti bhedataḥ ।।
sarva-prakāśaṃ sarveṣāṃ sthitānāṃ śravaṇocitam । 
dvitīyaṃ tu sthiteṣv apy eṣv ekasya śravaṇocitam ।।
dvidhā vibhāvyate' nyac ca janāntam apavāritam । 
tripatākā-kareṇānyān apavāryāntarā kathām ।।
anyenāmantraṇaṃ yat syāt taj janāntikam ucyate । 
rahasyaṃ kathyate' nyasya parāvṛtyāpavāritam ।।
itthaṃ śrāvyaṃ ca dṛśyaṃ ca prayujya susamāhitaiḥ ।
pātrair niṣkramaṇaṃ kāryam aṅkānte samam eva hi ।।
aṅka-cchedaś ca kartavyaḥ kālāvasthānurodhataḥ । 
dinārdha-dinayor yogyam aṅke vastu pravartayet ।।
