adṛṣtavigrahāgataṃ marīcicakravistaram ।
anugrahaikakāraṇaṃ namāmy ahaṃ gurukramam ।।
yogānandābhidhānadvijavaravadanāyātapīṭheśidevīpādāvadyotitājñapraṇayanajanitānantasotsāhaharṣaḥ ।
stotre bhāvopahāre vivaraṇam akarod ramyadevo dvijanmājanmādyāyāsabhaṅgapravaṇamatilasatsadrahasyopadeśaḥ ।।
