tato astraṃ sumahāvegaṃ jagrāha madhusūdanaḥ 
pārjanyaṃ nāma bhagavān yamāntakanibhaṃ raṇe ।। 
tato vitimire loke sa cāgniḥ praśamaṃ gataḥ । dānavā moghasaṃkalpāḥ sarve abhuvaṃs tadā bhṛśam । 
dānavāstraṃ praśāntaṃ tu parjanyāstre abhimantrite 
tato devagaṇāḥ sarve praṇedur jahṛṣus tadā ।।
