neti pūrvasūtrādanuvartate ।
śārīraśca nāntaryāmīṣyate ।
kasmāt ।
yadyapi draṣṭṛtvādayo dharmastasya saṃbhavanti tathāpi ghaṭākāśavadupādhiparicchinnatvānna kārtsyena pṛthivyādiṣvantaravasthātuṃ niyantuṃ ca śaknoti ।
apicobhaye api hi śākhinaḥ kāṇvā mādhyandinānāścāntaryāmiṇo bhedenainaṃ śārīraṃ pṛthivyādivadadhiṣṭhānatvena niyamyatvena cādhīyate- ayo vijñāne tiṣṭhan' iti kāṇvāḥ ।
aya ātmani tiṣṭhan' iti mādhyandināḥ ।
aya ātmani tiṣṭhan' ityasminstāvatpāṭhe bhavatyātmaśabdaḥ śārīrasya vācakaḥ ।
ayo vijñāne tiṣṭhan' ityasminnapi pāṭhe vijñānaśabdena śārīra ucyate ।
vijñānamayo hi śārīraḥ ।
tasmācchārīradanya īśvaro antaryāmīti siddham ।
kathaṃ punarekasmindehe dvau draṣṭāravupapadyete, yaścāyamīśvaro antaryāmī yaścāyamitaraḥ śārīraḥ ।
kā punarihānupapattiḥ ।
anānyo ato asti draṣṭā' ityādi śrutivacanaṃ virudhyeta ।
atra hi prakṛtādantaryāmiṇo anyaṃ draṣṭāraṃ, śrotāraṃ, mantāraṃ, vijñātāraṃ cātmānaṃ pratiṣedhati ।
niyantrantarapratiṣedhārthametadvacanamiticet, na, niyantrantarāprasaṅgādaviśeṣaśravaṇācca ।
