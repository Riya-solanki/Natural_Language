na ।
aśrutatvāt ।
satyamavarohādapyasaṃpūraṇamupapadyate ।
śrutistu tṛtīyasthānasaṃkīrtanenāsaṃpūraṇaṃ darśayati- aetattṛtīyaṃ sthānaṃ tenāsau loko na saṃpūryate' iti ।
tenānārohādevāsaṃpūraṇamiti yuktam ।
avarohasyeṣṭādikāriṣvapyaviśiṣṭatve sati tṛtīyasthānoktyānarthakyaprasaṅgāt ।
tuśabdastu śākāntarīyavākyaprabhavāmaśeṣagamanāśaṅkāmucchinatti ।
evaṃ satyadhikṛtāpekṣaḥ śākhāntarīye vākye sarvaśabdo avatiṣṭhate ।
ye vai kecidadhikṛtā asmāllokātprayanti candramasameva te sarve gacchantīti ।।
