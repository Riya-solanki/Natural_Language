iha mṛtasīsakena kāñcanaṃ sārayitvā tasya mṛtakāñcanasya cūrṇaṃ yat pratisāritarase kṣipyate ।। 
etat krāmaṇaṃ kathyate ।। 
iti pratisāritasūtasya krāmaṇasaṃskāraḥ ṣoḍaśaḥ ।। 
nāgakāñcanamāraṇavidhiś cāgre svayameva vakṣyati ।। 
nāgavallyāśca patreṇa tadataṃttāmitaram ।
ratimātraṃ kṣipet kuryān nāgapatrasya veḍhanīm ।।
gālayitvātha taccūrṇaṃ sarvaloheṣu nikṣipet ।
gālyamāneṣu tāyeta sahasrasya pravedhakam ।।
