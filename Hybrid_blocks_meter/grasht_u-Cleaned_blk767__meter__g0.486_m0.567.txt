pūrvoktaguṇānāha saratītyādi ।।
pūrvavidhānā sūtaḥ sarati sāritaḥ sūto mukhaṃ na dahati hastapādādi ca aṅgavibhāgaṃ naiva dahati ।।
punaḥ phaṇiyogāt nāgasaṃyogataḥ mākṣikayutahemagairikayā saha tāpyamilitasvarṇagairikayā sārdhaṃ krāmati ।।
