sūto nāgavaṅgaparimukto bhavati nāgavaṅgābhyāṃ doṣābhyāṃ virahitaḥ pārado bhavatītyarthaḥ kiṃviśiṣṭaḥ san samutthitaḥ san ।।
pātanayantre sthālīdvayasampuṭe samyagvidhānenotthitaḥ san ।।
kasmāt kāñjikaṃ sauvīraṃ pūrvavarṇitaṃ tatkvāthasaṃyogād ityarthaḥ ।।
hi niścitam ।।
amunā virecanena uktaśodhanena sūtaḥ suviśuddho bhavet viśeṣaśuddho bhaved ityutthāpanam ।।
anena vidhinā hiṅgulasthasya sūtasyāpi utthāpanaṃ bhavati ।।
svedanādikayogena svarūpāpādanaṃ punaḥ ।।
tadutthāpanamityuktaṃ mūrchāvyāpattināśanam iti ।।
ut ūrdhvaṃ sthāpanam utthāpanam ।।
atra yantraṃ tu ।
aṣṭāṅgulaparīṇāhamānāhena daśāṅgulam ।
caturaṅgulakotsedhaṃ toyādhāro aṅgulādadhaḥ ।।
adhobhāṇḍe mukhaṃ ca tasya bhāṇḍasyoparivartinaḥ ।
ṣoḍaśāṅgulavistīrṇapṛṣṭhasyāsye praveśayet ।।
pārśvayor mahiṣīkṣīracūrṇamaṇḍūraphāṇitaiḥ ।।
vilipya śoṣayet saṃdhiṃ jalādhāre jalaṃ kṣipet ।
cullyām āropayedetat pātanāyantram īritam ।।
iti ।।
