bhedābhedavikalpe tāvatkecidāhuḥ- bhinnameva sāmānyaṃ vyaktibhyaḥ ।
na ca pṛthagupalabdhiprasaṅgo vyaktisaṃbandhitvāt ।
kaḥ punaḥ saṃbandhaḥ, samavāyaḥ ।
kaḥ punarasau, ayutasiddhānāmihapratyayahetuḥ saṃbandha iti kecit ।
tattvayuktam ।
ihapratyayāsiddheḥ ।
iyaṃ gauriti hi sarvadā sarveṣāṃ pratītirnehagotvamiti ।
kāceyamayutasiddhiḥ, yutasiddhyabhāvaḥ ।
kā punaryutasiddhiḥ, pṛthaggatimatvaṃ pṛthagāśrayāśritatvaṃ vā, tadabhāvo ayutasiddhiḥ ।।
