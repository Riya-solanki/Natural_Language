māninījanavilocanapātā- nuṣṇabāṣpakaluṣānanugṛhṇan ।
mandamandamuditaḥ priyayau khaṃ bhītabhīta iva śītamayūkhaḥ ।।
kramādekadvitriprabhṛtiparipāṭīḥ prakaṭayan kalāḥ svairaṃ svairaṃ navakamalakandāṅkurarucaḥ ।
purandhrīṇāṃ preyovirahadahanoddīpitadṛśāṃ kaṭākṣebhyo bibhyannibhṛta iva candro abhyudayate ।।
