tatra saṅkṣiptiḥ -
saṅkṣipta-vastu-viṣayā yā māyāśilpa-yojitā । 
sā saṅkṣiptir iti proktā bharatena mahātmanā ।। athā anargha-rāghave-
nīto dūraṃ kanaka-hariṇa-cchadmanā rāmabhadraḥ
paścād enaṃ drutam anusaraty eṣa vatsaḥ kaniṣṭhaḥ ।
bibhyad bibhyat praviśati tataḥ parṇaśālāṃ ca bhikṣur
dhig dhik kaṣṭaṃ prathayati nnijām ākṛtiṃ rāvaṇo' yam ।।
