utkṛṣṭasarjikākṣāramaṇānāṃ sūkṣmacūrṇakam ।
saṃdhyāyāṃ tu maṇaṃ sthālyāṃ kumbhamānaṃ jalaṃ kṣipet ।।
prātastyaktvā rasaṃ tyaktvā bhūyo'pi prakṣipenmaṇam ।
evaṃ ṣaḍvāsare kuryāt ṣaṇmaṇaṃ sarjikājalam ।।
nītā ye ca jalaṃ śeṣam atyacchaṃ kurute sudhīḥ ।
gandhakāmalasāro'pi vāriṇā tena peṣayet ।।
taṃ ca vārinibhaṃ kuryātprakṣipetkuṃpake sudhīḥ ।
kumbhavastramṛdāveṣṭya dattvāsye cāmracātikām ।।
ahorātraṃ jvalatyagniṃ channā yatra ca cūhyake ।
nikṣipya kuṃpakaṃ garbhe rakṣā deyā ṣaḍaṅgulām ।।
nikṣiptaśca rahāsmoyann ekaviṃśativāsarān ।
vilagnaḥ sarjikākṣāraḥ kumpyantaḥ parito bhraman ।।
nālikerajalābho'bhūtpītatoyo hi gandhakaḥ ।
hṛtipīṭhīti vikhyātaṃ vārigandhakajaṃ tvidam ।।
