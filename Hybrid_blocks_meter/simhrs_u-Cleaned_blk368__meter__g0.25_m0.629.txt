āndhrī lāṭī ca saurāṣṭrīty ādayo miśra-rītayaḥ ।
santi tat-tad-deśa-vidvat-priya-miśraṇa-bhedataḥ ।। 
ta eva pada-saṅghātās tā evārtha-vibhūtayaḥ ।
tathāpi navyaṃ bhavati kāvyaṃ grathana-kauśalāt ।। 
tāsāṃ grantha-gaḍutvena lakṣaṇaṃ nocyate mayā ।
bhojādi-grantha-bandheṣu tad-ākāṅkṣibhir īkṣyatām ।।
