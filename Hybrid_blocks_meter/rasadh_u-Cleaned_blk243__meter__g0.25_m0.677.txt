mṛdu vartaya patrāṇi pātālasya gurutmanā ।
piṇḍyāṃ piṣṭasya te ṣoṭaṃ kṣiptvā kāryo hi golakam ।।
taṃ sarāvapuṭe kṣiptvā sandhikarpaṭamṛtsnayā ।
veṣṭayitvā puṭaṃ deyaṃ bhūmau kukkuṭasaṃjñakam ।।
nirdhūmairjvaladaṅgāraiḥ ṣoṭo abhūt pītavarṇakaḥ ।
gālanīyā catuḥṣaṣṭigadyāṇāḥ śuddharūpyajāḥ ।।
tanmadhye ṣoṭagadyāṇe kṣiptaṃ syātsvarṇamuttamam ।
gāndhikīha drutiḥ pīṭhī catuḥṣaṣṭipravedhikā ।।
