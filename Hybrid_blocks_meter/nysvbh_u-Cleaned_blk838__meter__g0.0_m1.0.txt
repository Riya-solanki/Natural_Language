saṃmūrchanalakṣaṇādavayavavyūhād dravyāntare
dadhni utpanne gṛhyamāṇe pūrvaṃ payodravyam avayavavibhāgebhyo
nivṛttamityanumīyate yathā mṛdavayavānāṃ
vyūhāntarād dravyāntare sthālyāmutpannāyāṃ
pūrvaṃ mṛtpiṇḍadravyaṃ mṛdavayavavibhāgebhyo
nivartate iti, mṛdvaccāvayavānvayaḥ payodadhnoḥ,
nāśeṣanirodhe niranvayo dravyāntarotpādo
ghaṭate iti ।।
