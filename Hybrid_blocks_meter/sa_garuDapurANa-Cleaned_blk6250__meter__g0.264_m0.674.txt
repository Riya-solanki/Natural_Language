śrīgaruḍamahāpurāṇam- 
sūta uvāca ।
prathamo aṣṭākṣaraiḥ pādo dvitīyo dvādaśākṣaraiḥ ।
tṛtīyaḥ ṣoḍaśārṇaiśca viṃśadvarṇaiścaturthakaḥ ।।
sāmānyalakṣaṇaṃ padacaturūrdhvābhiradhasya hi ।।āpīḍaḥ sarvalaḥ proktaḥ pūrvapādāntagadvayaḥ ।।
dvitīye aṣṭākṣaraiḥ pāde kalikā prathamer'kaje ।
lavalī syāttṛtīye atha pūrvavaccāṣṭa kākṣare ।
proktā cāmṛtadhāreyaṃ caturaṣṭākṣare sati ।।
