prakṛtau paramātmatvaṃ pratipadyaiva tuṣṭibhāk ।।
mādhyasthyamavalambeta ambhaḥ sā tuṣṭirucyate ।
buddhau ca paramātmatve tuṣṭiḥ sā salilā smṛtā ।।
ahaṃkāre yadā sā syādāyācchā iti śabdhitā ।
tanmātreṣu yadā sā syāt tuṣṭirdṛṣṭirnigadyate ।।
ādhyātmikāścatasrastu tuṣṭayaḥ prabhavanti hi ।
mokṣastāsvapi nāstyeva tattvajñānādyathoditā ।।
arthānāmarjane duḥkhamarjitānāṃ ca rakṣaṇe ।
tyāge duḥkhaṃ kṣaye duḥkhaṃ dhigarthaṃ duḥkhabhājanam ।। iti
doṣadarśanasantuṣṭaḥ parabrahmaiva mokṣabhāk ।
suteyaṃ pañcamī tuṣṭistattvajñānādyabhāvataḥ ।।
rakṣaṇe sutarā ṣaṣṭhī saptamī kṣayadarśanāt ।
sunetrā saṅgadoṣā cāṣṭamī kurmarīcikā ।।
arthe hiṃsādidoṣeṇa nivṛttiśca samaskarī ।
tuṣṭistu navamī proktā hyuttamāmbhasikā tava ।।
