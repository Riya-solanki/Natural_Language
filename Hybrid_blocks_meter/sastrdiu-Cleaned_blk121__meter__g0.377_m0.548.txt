maivam ।
unmīlitanetrasya vikalpo api gaurayamityaparokṣāvabhāsa eva pratīyate nacāsāvanyasaṃsargakṛtaḥ pramāṇābhāvāt ।
abhilāpasaṃsargayogyasyāpi sāmānyākārasyābhilāpaliṅgādibhiravaiśadyamindriyaiśca vaiśādyamupapadyata eva ।
nahyayaṃ parokṣāparokṣavibhāgo viṣayakṛtaḥ, yadi hi tathā syāt sarvadaiva sāmānyaṃ parokṣaṃ syāt, nacaivamasti,
