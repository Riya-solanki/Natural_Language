sāmyapratibhāsamātraṃ manohāritāyāḥ kāraṇamityalaṅkaraṇam ।
yasmādevaṃvidhe viṣaye vicchittividhānasya saṃniyata pratīyamānamalaṅkaraṇamupanibadhnanti, na punarabhidhīyamānameva ।
pratīyamānatvaṃ parivṛtterapi dṛśyate ।
yathā
