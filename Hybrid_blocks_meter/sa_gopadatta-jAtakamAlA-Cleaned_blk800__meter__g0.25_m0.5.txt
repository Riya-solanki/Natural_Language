kiṃ dharmo astam itaḥ prajāhitakaraḥ kiṃ lokapālā mṛtāḥ
tejaḥ kopaviṣātisaṅgarabhasaṃ kiṃ proṣitaṃ bhoginām ।
śūnyaṃ kiṃ nu patiprabhābhir adhunā niḥśuklamūlaṃ jagad
yena tvaṃ vyasanaṃ duruttaram idaṃ prāpto vināśāya te ।।
