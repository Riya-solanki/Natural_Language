jighratyaneneti ghrāṇaṃ gandhaṃ gṛhṇātīti।
rasayatyaneneti rasanaṃ rasaṃ gṛhṇātīti।
caṣṭe aneneti cakṣū rūpaṃ paśyatīti।
tvaksthānamindriyaṃ tvak।
tadupacāraḥ sthānāditi।
śṛṇotyaneneti śrotraṃ śabdaṃ gṛhṇātīti।
evaṃ samākhyānirvacanasāmarthyādbodhyaṃ svaviṣayagrahaṇalakṣaṇānīndriyāṇīti।
bhūtebhya iti।
nānāprakṛtīnāmeṣāṃ satāṃ viṣayaniyamaḥ, naikaprakṛtīnām।
sati ca viṣayaniyame svavaṣiyagrahaṇalakṣaṇtvaṃ bhavatīti ।।
