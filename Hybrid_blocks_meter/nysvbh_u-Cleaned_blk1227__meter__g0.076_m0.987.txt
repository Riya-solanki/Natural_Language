kāryadravyaṃ kāraṇadravyāśritaṃ tatkāraṇebhyaḥ
pṛthaṅ nopalabhyate viparyaye pṛthak grahaṇāt
yatrāśrayāśritabhāvo nāsti tatra pṛthag
grahaṇamiti, buddhyā vivecanāttu bhāvānāṃ
pṛthag grahaṇam ।
atīndriyeṣvaṇuṣu yadindriyeṇa gṛhyate tadetayā
buddhyā vivicyamānamanyaditi ।।
