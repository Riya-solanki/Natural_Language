tadevamadhyāsasvarūpaṃ phalaṃ ca vyavahāramuktvā tasya nimittamāha - itaretarāvivekena iti ।
vivekagrahaṇenetyarthaḥ ।
atha aviveka eva kasmānna bhavati, tathā ca nādhyāsa ityata āha - atyantaviviktayordharmadharmiṇoḥ ।
paramārthato dharmiṇoratādātmyaṃ vivekaḥ dharmāṇāṃ cāsaṃkīrṇatā vivekaḥ ।
