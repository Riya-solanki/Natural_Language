atha krodhaḥ-
vadhāvajñādibhiś citta-jvalanaṃ krodha īritaḥ ।
eṣa tridhā bhavet kordha-kopa-roṣa-prabhedataḥ ।। 
vadha-cchedādi-paryantaḥ krodhaḥ krūra-janāśrayaḥ ।
abhyarthanāvadhiḥ prāyaḥ kopo vīra-janāśrayaḥ ।। 
śatru-bhṛtya-suhṛt-pūjyāś catvāro viṣayās tayoḥ ।
muhur daṣṭoṣṭhatā bhugna-bhrukuṭī-danta-ghaṭṭanam ।। 
hasta-niṣpīḍanaṃ gātra-kampaḥ śastra-pratīkṣaṇam ।
sva-bhujāvekṣaṇaṃ kaṇṭha-garjādyāḥ śātrava-krudhi ।।
