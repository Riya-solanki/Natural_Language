atha āvegaḥ-
cittasya sambhramo yaḥ syād āvego' yaṃ sa cāṣṭadhā । utpāta-vāta-varṣāgni-matta-kuñjara-darśanāt ।।
priyāpriya-śruteś cāpi śatrava-vyasanād api । 
tatrautpātas tu śailādi-kampa-ketūdayādayaḥ ।।
taj-jāḥ sarvāṅga-visraṃso vaimukhyam apasarpaṇam । 
viṣāda-mukha-vaivarṇya-vismayādyās tu vikriyāḥ ।।
