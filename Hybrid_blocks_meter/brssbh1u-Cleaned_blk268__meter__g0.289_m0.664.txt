FN dharmādharmattvamupādhirityarthaḥ ।
ayameva viśeṣo vaiśeṣyaṃ ।
svārthe ṣyañ pratyayaḥ ।
viśeṣasyātiśayārtho vā ।
dharmādeḥ svāśraye phalahetutvamatiśayastasmāditi sutrārthaḥ ।
vibhavo bahavaścātmana iti vādinām ।
ardheti ।
ardhaṃmukhamātraṃ jaratyā buddhāyāḥ kāmayate nāṅāgānīti so ayamardhajaratīnyāyaḥ ।
manomayatvādiviśiṣṭasyaiveśvarasya dhyānārthaṃ hārdatve api nirdeṣatvāttasminneva śāṇḍilyavidyāvidye sarvaṃ ityādivākyaṃ samanvitamityarthaḥ ।
