na ।
tasyāḥ pāramparyajatve apyavirodāt ।
yadāpi hyākāśaṃ vāyuṃ ca sṛṣṭvā vāyubhāvāpannaṃ brahma tejo asṛjateti kalpyate, tadāpi brahmajatvaṃ tejaso na virudhyate ।
yathā tasyāḥ śrutaṃ tasyā dadhi tasyā āmikṣetyādi ।
darśayati ca brahmaṇo vikārātmanāvastānaṃ atadātmānaṃ svayamakuruta' iti ।
tathāceśvarasmaraṇaṃ bhavati- abuddirjñānamasaṃmohaḥ' ityādyanukramya abhavati bhāvā bhūtānāṃ matta eva pṛthagvidhāḥ' iti ।
yadyapi buddhyādayaḥ svakāraṇebhyaḥ pratyakṣaṃ bhavanto dṛśyante tathāpi sarvasya bhāvajātasya sākṣātpraṇāḍyā veśvaravaṃśyatvāt ।
etenākramavatsṛṣṭivādinyaḥ śrutayo vyākhyātāḥ ।
tāsāṃ sarvathopapatteḥ ।
kramavatsṛṣṭivādinīnāṃ tvanyathānupapatteḥ ।
pratijñāpi sadvaṃśyatvamātramapekṣate nāvyavahitajanyatvamityavirodhaḥ ।।
