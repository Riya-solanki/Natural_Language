Vijñapriyā
rasavata eva kāvyatvāṅgīkāre bījamāhakāvyasya prayojanamiti ।
kṛtyākṛtyeti rasāsvādamukhapiṇḍadānadarśanāttādṛśamukhapiṇḍasyaiva mukhyaprayojanatvamuktam ।
tādṛśopadeśastu yathāsambhavamevetyuktam ।
tathā ca"śūnyaṃ vāsagṛhamityādi'; "yaḥ kaumārahara'; ityādiślekeṣu tādṛśopadeśābhāve api kāvyatvamakṣuṇṇam ।
