udāharaṇāpekṣa udāharaṇatantra udāharaṇavaśaḥ।
vaśaḥ sāmarthyam।
sādhyasādharmyayukte udāharaṇe sthālyādidravyamutpattidharmakamanityaṃ dṛṣṭam, tathā ca śabda utpattidharmaka iti sādhyasya śabdasyotpattidharmakatvamūpasaṃhriyate ।
sādhyavaidharmyayuktaṃ punarudāharaṇe ātmādidravyamanutpattidharmakaṃ nityaṃ dṛṣṭam, na ca tathānutpattidharmakaḥ śabda iti, anutpattidharmakatvasyopasaṃhārapratiṣedhenotpattidharmakattvamupasaṃhriyate ।
tadidamupasaṃhāradvetamudāharaṇadvaitādbhavati।
upasaṃhriyate aneneti copasaṃhāro veditavya iti।।
