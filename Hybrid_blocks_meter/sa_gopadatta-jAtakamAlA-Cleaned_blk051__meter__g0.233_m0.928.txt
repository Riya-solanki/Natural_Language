prāsāde śaradabhraśubhraśikhare puṣpāvalīmaṇḍite
vīṇāveṇumṛdaṅgagītamadhure saṃprāpya citraṃ sukham ।
nityaṃ prajvalitasphuṭānalaśikhāsaṃtānajālākulaṃ
prāpyāvīcim anekaduḥkhavihataḥ kiṃ nāma kartā bhavān ।।
