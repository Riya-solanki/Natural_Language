।। salakṣaṇamanonirdeśaḥ ।।
mūlamana ālayavijñānamāśritya nityamātmamohātmadṛṣṭyātmamānātmasnehaiḥ saṃprayuktaṃ vijñānamekajātīyasantānavarti arhatvāryamārganirodhasamāpattikāle vyāvartate ।।
vijñānaskandhaḥ soniddeśanirdeśaḥ pariniṣṭhitaḥ ।।
