neyaṃ syān navanīta-piṇḍa-visṛtir muktās tu muktāmbudāḥ ।
neyaṃ dīrṇa-haridra-nīra-vikṛtiḥ kintu prabhā vidyutāṃ
parvaivedam atīva harṣa-mahasā varṣā-vapur nirmame ।। iti ।
