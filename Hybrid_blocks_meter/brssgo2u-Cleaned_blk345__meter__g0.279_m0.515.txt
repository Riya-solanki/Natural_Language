nāyaṃ doṣaḥ ।
yato ghṛtakāṭhinyavilayanavadapi vināśopapattimavocāma ।
yathā hi ghṛtasuvarṇādīnāmavibhajyamānāvayavānāmapyagnisaṃyogāddravabhāvāpattyā kāṭhanyavināśo bhavati, evaṃ paramāṇūnāmapi paramakāraṇabhāvāpattyā mūrtyādivināśo bhaviṣyati ।
tathā kāryarambho api nāvayavasaṃyogenaiva kevalena bhavati, kṣīrajalādīnāmantareṇāpyavayavasaṃyogāntaraṃ dadhihimādikāryārambhadarśanāt ।
tadevamasārataratarkasaṃdṛbdhatvādīśvarakāraṇaśrutiviruddhatvācchruti pravaṇaiśca śiṣṭairmanvādibhiraparigṛhītatvādatyantamevānapekṣāsminparamāṇukāraṇavāde kāryā śreyorthibhiriti vākyaśeṣaḥ ।।
