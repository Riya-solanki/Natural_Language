karmavaśānniṣpanne śarīre ceṣṭendriyārthāśraye nimittabhāvādavaśyambhāvī
buddhīnāmutpādaḥ, na ca prabalopi san bāhyo artha
ātmano buddhyutpāde samartho bhavati tasyendriyeṇa
saṃyogād buddhyutpāde sāmarthyaṃ dṛṣṭamiti
।।
