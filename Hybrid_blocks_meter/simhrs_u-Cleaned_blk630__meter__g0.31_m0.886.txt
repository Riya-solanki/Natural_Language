atha ālasyam-
svabhāva-śrama-sauhitya-garbha-nirbharatādibhiḥ । 
kṛcchrāt kriyonmukhatvaṃ yat tad ālasyam iha kriyāḥ ।।
aṅga-bhaṅgaḥ kriyā-dveṣo jṛmbhaṇākṣi-vimardane । 
śayyāsanaika-priyatā tandrī-nidrādayo' pi ca ।।
