sādṛśyavatī yathā
kṣoṇībhṛtkaṭakoparodhanapaṭuḥ vyāpārabhavyodayā- māśāpūraṇa kāraṇaśriyamiva prāpnoti kīrtiśriyam ।
śrīsiṅgakṣitipālamaṇḍanamaṇe recarlavaṃśāgraṇī- rānandānanusandadhāti viduṣāṃ dravyaiḥ svakāvyairiva ।।
