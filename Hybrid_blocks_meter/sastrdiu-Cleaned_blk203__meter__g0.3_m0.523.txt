prakāśanamaṅgīkartavyam ।
na ca nirākārasyopalabdhirastīti siddhaṃ jñānātmakatvaṃ nīlādeḥ ।
avaśyaṃ ca svapnādibodhe bandhyāsuto yāti, aṅgulyagre hastiyūthaśatamityādau cātyantāsaṃbhavādbāhyasya jñānātmakatvamevākāraṇāmabhyupagantavyam ।
tathānyatrāpyastu, tasmānna bāhyamastīti prāpte ।
