kamaṇḍalumānānīyate ।
tasmādadhidaivaṃ satyasyopaniṣaduktā na tasyaivādhyātmaṃ bhavitumarhati ।
yathā cācāryasya gacchato anugamanaṃ vihitaṃ na tiṣṭhato bhavati, tasmānnopaniṣadoḥ saṃkaraḥ kintu vyavasthitiḥ ।
tadidamuktaṃ-svarūpānapāyāditi ।।
