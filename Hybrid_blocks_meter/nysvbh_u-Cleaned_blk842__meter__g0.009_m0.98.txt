idaṃ tu cintyate
kasyeyaṃ buddhiḥ ātmendriyamano arthānāṃ guṇa
iti, prasiddhopi khalvayamarthaḥ aparīkṣāśeṣaṃ
pravartayāmīti prakriyate, soyaṃ buddhau
saṃnikarṣotpatteḥ saṃśayaḥ viśeṣasyāgrahaṇāditi।
tatrānayaṃ viśeṣaḥ
