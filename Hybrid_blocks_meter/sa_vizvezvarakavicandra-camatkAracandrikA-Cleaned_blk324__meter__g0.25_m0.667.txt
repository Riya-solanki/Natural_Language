catuṣpādānvitaṃ padyaṃ cādirmātreti tad dvidhā ।
chandovicitikośeṣu tayorjñeyastu vistaraḥ ।।
padyabandhamayaṃ kāvyaṃ kṣudrākṣudratayā dvidhā ।।
kṣudraṃ muktakakāvyaṃ tad bhāmahādyaiḥ prapañcitam ।
akṣudraṃ sargabandhādi praśastodāttavastukam ।।
