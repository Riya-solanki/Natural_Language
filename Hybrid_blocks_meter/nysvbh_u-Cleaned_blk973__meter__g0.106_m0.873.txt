karmanimitte śarīrasarge
tena śarīreṇātmano viyoga upapannaḥ।
kasmāt ?, karmakṣayopapatteḥ upapadyate khalu karmakṣayaḥ samyagdarśanāt,
prakṣīṇe mohe vītarāgaḥ
punarbhavahetukarma
kāyavāṅmanobhirna karoti ityuttarasyānupacayaḥ
pūrvopacitasya vipākapratisaṃvedanāt prakṣayaḥ,
evaṃ prasavahetorabhāvāt patite asmin
śarīre punaḥ śarīrāntarānutpatterapratisaṃdhiḥ।
akarmanimitte tu śarīrasarge bhūtakṣayānupapattestadviyogānupapattiriti
।।
