atha mṛtiḥ-
vāyor dhanañjayākhyasya viprayogo ya ātmanā । 
śarīrāvacchedavatā maraṇaṃ nāma tad bhavet ।।
etac ca dvividhaṃ proktaṃ vyādhijaṃ cābhighātajam । 
ādyaṃ tv asādhya-hṛc-chūla-viṣūcy-ādi-samudbhavam ।।
amī tatrānubhāvāḥ syur avyaktākṣara-bhāṣaṇam । 
vivarṇa-gātratā manda-śvāsādi stambha-mīlane ।
hikkā parijanāpekṣā-niśceṣṭendriyatādayaḥ ।।
