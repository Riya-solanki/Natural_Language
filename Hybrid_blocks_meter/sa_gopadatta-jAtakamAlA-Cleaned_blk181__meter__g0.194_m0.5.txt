durvṛttāyā mama tad adhunā karmaṇā preritas tvaṃ
bhuṅkṣvāsahyaṃ vyasanam athavā svasya duśceṣṭitasya ।
ete anye ca vyasananivahā mūrdhni teṣāṃ sphuranti
tyaktvā lajjāṃ sujanadayitāṃ ye pramādaṃ bhajante ।।
