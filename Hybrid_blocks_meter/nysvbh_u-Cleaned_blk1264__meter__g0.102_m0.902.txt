tasya ।
buddhinimittāśrayasya śarīrendriyasya dharmādharmābhāvādabhāvo apavarge
tatra yaduktam
"aṇvargepyevaṃ prasaṅgaḥ "
iti tadayuktam, tasmāt sarvaduḥkhavimokṣopavargaḥ।
yasmāt sarvaduḥkhabījaṃ sarvaduḥkhāyatanaṃ
cāpavarge vicchidyate tasmāt sarveṇa duḥkhena
vimuktirapavargaḥ na nirbījaṃ nirāyatanaṃ
ca duḥkhamutpadyate iti ।।
