sāraṇāyantra
kṛtvāṣṭāṃgulamūṣāṃ dhūrtakusumopamāṃ dṛḍhāṃ ślakṣṇām ।
aparā madhyagatāpi ca sacchidrā ca saptāṃgulā kāryā ।।
niruddhatāṃ ca kṛtvā sūtaṃ prakṣipya tailasaṃyuktam ।
nirdhūmaṃ karṣāgnau sthāpya mūṣāṃ susaṃdhitāṃ kṛtvā ।।
