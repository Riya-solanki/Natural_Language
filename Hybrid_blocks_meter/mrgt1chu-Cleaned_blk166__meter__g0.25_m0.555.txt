paramārthataḥ parameśvarasyāśarīratvāc chaktireva dehakāryaṃ kurvatī dehākhyayoktā ।। 
tasyāścaikasyā api kṛtyabhedād vāmādibhedabhinnatvam ।। 
yathā vahniśakter dāhyaprakāśyārthaviṣaye dāhaprakāśaprakaraṇād gauṇam anekatvaṃ vastuta ekatvāt tasyāḥ ।।
