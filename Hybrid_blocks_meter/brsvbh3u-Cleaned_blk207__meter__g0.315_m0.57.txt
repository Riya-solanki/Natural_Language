varṇakāntaramāha-
athavaitāsviti ।
yathā hi sukṛtaduṣkṛtayoramūrtayoḥ kalpanaṃ nāñjasaṃ mūrtyanuvidhāyitvātkampasya ।
tathānyadīyayoranyatra saṃcāro apyanupapanno amūrtatvādeva ।
tasmādyatra vidhūnanamātraṃ śrutaṃ tatra kampanena varaṃ svakāryārambhāccālanamātrameva lakṣyatāṃ na tu tato apagatyānyatra saṃcāraḥ kalpanāgauravaprasaṅgāt ।
tasmātsvakāryārambhāccālanaṃ vidhūnanamiti prāpte abhidhīyate-yatra tāvadupāyanaśrutistatrāvaśyaṃ tyāgo vidhūnanaṃ vaktavyam ।
kvacidapi cedvidhūnanaṃ tyāge vartate tathā satyanyatrāpi tatraiva vartitumarhati ।
evaṃ hi na varteta yadi vidhūnanamiha mukhyaṃ labhyeta ।
na caitadasti ।
tatrāpi svakāryāccālanasya lakṣyamāṇatvāt ।
naca prāmāṇikaṃ kalpanāgauravaṃ lohagandhitāmācarati ।
apicānekārthatvāddhātūnāṃ tyāge api vidhūyate mukhyameva bhaviṣyati ।
prācuryeṇa tyāge api loke prayogadarśanāt ।
vinigamanahetorabhāvāt ।
gaṇakārasya copalakṣaṇatvenāpyarthanirdeśasya tatra darśanāt ।
tasmāddhānārtha evātreti yuktam ।।
