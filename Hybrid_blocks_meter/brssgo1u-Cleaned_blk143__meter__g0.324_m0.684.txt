FN atrottamapuruṣastūbhayatra prathamapuruṣe chāndasavat ।
tadā cetanaṃ santaṃ mumukṣumacetano asītibruvacchāstraṃ viparītavādibhūtvā puṃso anarthāyetikṛtvā syādapramāṇamiti yojanā ।
atra vihatirmuktibhāktvābhāvaḥ ।
ayathā satyābhisaṃdhastaptaṃ paraśuṃ sa na dahyate atha mucyate' iti ।
ukthaṃ prāṇaḥ ।
mahāvākyotthaṃ jñānamidamucyate ।
