vicāra-sundaro naiṣa mārgaḥ syād ity udāsmahe ।
kaiśikī-vṛtti-bhedānāṃ narmādīnāṃ prakalpanam ।। 
yatra karuṇam āśritya rasābhāsatva-kāraṇam ।
rasābhāsa-prakaraṇe vakṣyate tad idaṃ sphuṭam ।। 
tat-tan-nyāya-pravīṇena nyāya-mārgānuvartinā ।
darśitaṃ siṃha-bhūpena spaṣṭaṃ vṛtti-catuṣṭayam ।।
