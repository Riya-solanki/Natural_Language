pradhānam aṅgam iti ca tad vastu dvividhaṃ punaḥ । 
pradhānaṃ netṛ-caritaṃ pradhāna-phala-bandhi ca ।।
kāvye vyāpi pradhānaṃ tad yathā rāmādi-ceṣṭitam । 
nāyakārtha-kṛd-aṅgaṃ syān nāyaketara-ceṣṭitam ।।
nityaṃ patākā prakarī cāṅgaṃ bījādayaḥ kvacit । 
bījatvād bījam ādau syāt phalatvāt kāryam antataḥ ।।
tayoḥ sandhān a-hetutvān madhye binduṃ prakalpayet । 
yathāyogaṃ patākāyāḥ prakaryāś ca niyojanam ।।
