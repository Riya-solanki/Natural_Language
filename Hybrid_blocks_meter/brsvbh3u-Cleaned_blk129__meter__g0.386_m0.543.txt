viparīvartamānasyānuvādaḥ ।
na cātra guṇādbhedaḥ, samidādipadānāṃ karmanāmadheyānāṃ guṇavacanatvābhāvāt ।
agṛhyamāṇaviśeṣatayā ca kiṃvacanavihitakiṅkarmānuvādena kasya guṇavidhitvamiti na vinigamyate ।
na cāpūrvā nāma jyotirādivadvidhānasaṃbandhaṃ prathamamavagataṃ, yataḥ pūrvabuddhivicchedena vidhīyamānaṃ karma pūrvasmātsaṃjñāto vyavacchindyāt ।
kintu prathamata eva karmasāmānādhikaraṇyenāvagatāḥ samidādayastadvaśātkarmanāmadheyatāṃ pratipadyamānā ākhyātasyānuvādatve anuvādā vidhitve vidhayo na tu svātantryeṇa kasyacidīśate ।
tasmāt svarasasiddhāprāptakarmavidhiparatvātkarmaṇyayamabhyāso bhāvanānubandhabhūtāni bhindāno bhāvanāṃ bhinatti yathā tathā śākhāntaravihitā api vidyāḥ śākhāntaravihitābhyo vidyābhyo abhyāso bhetsyatīti ।
aśakteśca ।
nahyekaḥ puruṣaḥ sarvavedāntapratyayātmikāmupāsanāmupasaṃhartuṃ śaknoti sarvavedāntādhyayanāsāmarthyādanadhītārthopasaṃhāre adhyayanavidhānavaiyathryaprasaṅgāt ।
pratiśākhaṃ bhede tūpāsanānāṃ nāyaṃ doṣaḥ ।
samāptibhedācca ।
keṣāñcit śākhināmoṅkārasārvātmyakathane samāptiḥ ।
keṣāñcidanyatra ।
tasmādapyupāsanābhedaḥ ।
anyārthadarśanādapi bhedaḥ ।
tathāhi-'naitadacīrṇavrato adhīte'iti acīrṇavratasyādhyayanābhāvadarśanādupāsanābhāvaḥ ।
kvacidacīrṇavratasyādhyayanadarśanādupāsanāvagamyate ।
tasmādupāsanābheda iti ।
atra siddhāntamāha-
sarvavedāntapratyayaṃ codanādyaviśeṣāt ।
