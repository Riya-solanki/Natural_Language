sarvaṃ nānā ।
na kaścideko bhāvo vidyate, kasmāt ?, bhāvalakṣaṇapṛthaktvāt
ubhāvasya lakṣaṇam ।
abhidhānam ।
yena lakṣyate bhāvaḥ sa samākhyāśabdaḥ tasya
pṛthagviṣayatvāt ।
sarvobhāvasamākhyāśabdaḥ samūhavācī, akumbhaḥ' iti saṃjñāśabdo gandharasarūpasparśasamūhe
badhnapārśvagrīvādisamūhe ca vartate, nidarśanamātraṃ
cedamiti ।।
