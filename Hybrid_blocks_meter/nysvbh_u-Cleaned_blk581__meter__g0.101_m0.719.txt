vyaktiḥ padārthaḥ, kasmāt ? yāśabdaprabhṛtīnāṃ
vyaktāvupacārāt।
upacāraḥ ।
prayogaḥ, ayā gaustiṣṭhati yā gaurniṣaṇṇā'
iti nūdaṃ vākyaṃ jāterabhidhāyakam abhedāt
bhedāttu dravyābhidhāyakam, agavāṃ samūhaḥ' iti bhedād dravyābhidhānaṃ na jāteḥ
abhedāt, avaidyāya gāṃ dadātīti dravyasya tyāgo na jāteḥ amūrtatvāt pratikramānukramānupapatteśca, parigrahaḥ ।
svatvenābhisaṃbandhaḥ akauṇḍinyasya gauḥ' abrāhmaṇasya
gauḥ' iti dravyābhidhona dravyabhedāt saṃbandhabheda
ityupapannam abhinnā tu jātiriti, saṃkhyā
adaśa gāvo viṃśatirgāvaḥ' iti bhinnaṃ dravyaṃ
saṃkhyāyate na jātiḥ abhedāditi, vṛddhiḥ ।
kāraṇavato dravyasyāvayavopacayaḥ aavardhata
gauḥ' iti niravayavā tu jātiriti, etenāpacayo
vyākhyātaḥ, varṇaḥ aśuklā gauḥ kapilā gauḥ'
iti dravyasya guṇayogo na sāmānyasya,
samāsaḥ agohitaṃ gosukham' iti dravyasya
sukhādiyogo na jāteriti, anubandhaḥ
sarūpaprajananasantānaḥ agaurgāṃ janayatīti
tad utpattidharmatvād dravye yuktaṃ na jātau
viparyayāditi।
dravyaṃ vyaktiriti hi nārthāntaram
।।
