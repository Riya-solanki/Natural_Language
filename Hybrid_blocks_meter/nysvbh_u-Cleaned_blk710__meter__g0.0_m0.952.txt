bāhyena prakāśenānugṛhītaṃ cakṣurviṣayagrāhakam
tadabhāve anupalabdhiḥ, sati ca prakāśānugrahe
rūpasparśopalabdhau ca satyāṃ tadāśrayasya dravyasya
cakṣuṣā grahaṇam, rūpasyānudbhūtatvāt
seyaṃ rūpānabhivyaktito rūpāśrayasya dravyasyānupalabdhirdṛṣṭā,
tatra yaduktam
"tadanupalabdherahetuḥ " iti
etadayuktam ।।
