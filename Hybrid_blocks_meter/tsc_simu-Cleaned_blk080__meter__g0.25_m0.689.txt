manasaḥ saṅkalpo viṣayaḥ ।
etānyekādaśa vaikṛtāhaṃkārājjātāni ityavādi smaiva buddhipratibimbitamarthaṃ puruṣa upalabhate ।
taduktaṃ buddhisthamarthaṃ puruṣaścetayate ।
ahaṃ dharmaṃ kariṣyāmītyadhyavasāyo buddherlakṣaṇam ।
dharmo jñānaṃ virāgaḥ aiśvaryametat sāttvikabuddheḥ ।
tadviparītamavirāgādi tāmasam ।।
