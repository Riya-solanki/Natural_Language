ādipadāttamodhvaṃsapākayorgrahaṇam ।
mūrtadravyāśrayatvaṃ hyakāśasya kāryaṃ, tacca pralaye apyasti paramāṇvāśrayatvāt ।
ato na prāgabhāva ityarthaḥ ।
prāgabhāvasattvaṃ sphuṭayati-kiṃ hīti ।
sthūlāśrayo avakāśaḥ sūkṣmāśrayacchidramaṇvāśrayaḥ suṣiramiti bhedaḥ ।
kiñcātmavadākāśo na jāyate, vibhutvāt, asparśadravyatvāccetyāha-pṛthivyādīti ।
tasmāduktatarkabalādgauṇī draṣṭavyetyanvayaḥ ।
bhedoktergauṇatve vaidikodāharaṇamāha-vede apyāraṇyāniti ।
ākāśeṣviti bhedavyapadeśo gauṇa iti saṃbandhaḥ ।।
