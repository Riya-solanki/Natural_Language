masattvāt ।
dvividhaṃ ca vastūnāṃ tattvaṃ sattvamasattvaṃ ca ।
tatra pūrvaṃ svataḥ, paraṃ tu parataḥ ।
yathāhuḥ- asvarūpapararūpābhyāṃ nityaṃ sadasadātmake ।
vastuni jñāyate kiñcidrūpaṃ kaiścitkadā ca na ।।
aiti ।
tatkiṃ
