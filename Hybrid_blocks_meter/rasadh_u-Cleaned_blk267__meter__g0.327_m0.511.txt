śuddharasasya daśa gadyāṇān vajramūṣāmadhye prakṣipya tāṃ mūṣāṃ vālukāyantre caṭāyya dinamekaṃ mṛdvagniṃ jvālayet ।।
upari bhūnāgasatvasya binduṃ binduṃ punaḥ punaḥ kṣipet ।।
tato rasasya mukhaṃ jāyate ।।
sarvadattaṃ jīryate ।।
tato nisāhāmatisūkṣmāṃ cilhāṃ prapiṣya vastreṇa niścotya raso grāhyaḥ ।।
tato yasmiñjale palaṃ māti tāvanmātraṃ kāntapātramākaṇṭhaṃ pūrayitvā kāntapātraṃ vālukāyantre caṭāyitvādho mṛduragniḥ punaḥ punarahorātraṃ jvālanīyaḥ ।।
tato'bhrakadrutisūtayor aikyeṇa vābhraṣoṭo jāyate ।।
tatastaṃ ṣoṭaṃ vajramūṣāyāṃ prakṣipya gadyāṇān madhye prakṣipya gāḍhaṃ gālayitvā samuttārayet ।।
tato'yamakṣayo nirmalastejovān gadīyāṇaḥ kṣipyate ।।
sarvottamaṃ rūpyaṃ bhavati ।।
asau vaṃge śatamadhye vedhako rasaḥ tathā yaḥ sadā prabhāte ratimātraṃ ṣoṭaṃ karoti ।।
sa ṣaḍbhirmāsairakṣayanīrogadeho jāyate iti trividhābhrakadrutikarmāṇi ।।
atha bhūnāgaḥ ।।
