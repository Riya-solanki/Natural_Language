caturvargā dharmārthakāmamokṣāḥ, tadrūpasya phalasya prāptirityarthaḥ ।
kāvyasya caturvargaphalaprāpakatvaṃ darśayatirāmādivaditi ।
pravṛttinivṛttyupadeśadvāreṇetipravṛttyupadeśena pravṛttasya dharmotpattiḥ sākṣādeva, nivṛttyupadeśena nivṛttasya dharmotpattiḥ pratibandhakaduritānutpattyā ca, dvedhāpi teṣāṃ prāpakatvam ।
pravṛttijanyadharmāttu sākṣādevārthādayaḥ ।
