-prapañcasya punaratyantāsata ityādinānirvacanīyamataṃ nirastam ।
idānīṃ śūnyamataṃ nirasyati-naceti ।
śūnyavādino hi śūnyasyāpi bhāsanamabhyupagacchanti, upapattiśca svasamānākāraparvapratyayasāmarthyena āsāditāsādhāraṇasvabhāvabhedaṃ yajjñānaṃ tadevāsatprakāśayati ।
naca tanmate asatprakāśanajñānasyāpi śūnyatvātsarvatrānāśvāsaprasaṅga iti vācyam, yato jñānamapi pūrvajñānādhīnasattvamityupagamyamānatvāt ।
