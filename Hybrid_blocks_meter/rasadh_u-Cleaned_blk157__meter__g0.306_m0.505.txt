sāralohacūrṇaṃ kāṃsyacūrṇaṃ ca samamātrayā mūṣāyāṃ kṣiptvā tata āvartya ekapiṇḍaṃ kriyate ।।
tatastasmātpiṇḍād ardhamātrayā śuddhanāgaṃ madhye kṣiptvā mūṣāyāṃ punaḥ punarāvartya jāraṇīyam ।।
tataḥ punarapyardhamātrayā nāgaṃ kṣiptvā jāryam ।।
yadi caivaṃ punaḥ punarjāraṇena kāṃsyāt ṣaḍguṇaṃ nāgaṃ jīrṇaṃ bhavati ।।
tadā vyoṣarājir jāyate ।।
iti vyoṣarājiḥ kāṃsyarājir ityarthaḥ ।।
