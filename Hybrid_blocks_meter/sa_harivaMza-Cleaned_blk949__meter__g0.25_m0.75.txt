sṛñjayasyābhavat putro vīro rājā puraṃjayaḥ 
janamejayo mahārāja puraṃjayasuto abhavat ।। 
puraṃjayān mahārāja putro abhūjjanamejayaḥ । 
janamejayasya rājarṣer mahāsālo abhavat sutaḥ 
deveṣu sa parijñātaḥ pratiṣṭhitayaśās tathā ।।
