tato haro varaṃ prādāt kedārāya vṛṣadhvajaḥ ।
puṇyavṛddhikaraṃ brahman pāpaghnaṃ mokṣasādhanam ।।
ye jalaṃ tāvake tīrthe pītvā saṃyamino narāḥ ।।
ṣaṇmāsād dhārayiṣnti nivṛttāḥ parapākataḥ ।
teṣāṃ hṛtpaṅkajeṣveva malliṅgaṃ bhavitā dhruvam ।।
