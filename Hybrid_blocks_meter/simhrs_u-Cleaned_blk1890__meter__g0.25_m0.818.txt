atha garbhāṅkaḥ-
aṅka-prasaṅgād garbhāṅka-lakṣaṇaṃ vakṣyate mayā । 
rasanāyaka-vastūnāṃ mahotkarṣāya kovidaiḥ ।।
aṅkasya madhye yo' ṅkaḥ syād asau garbhāṅka īritaḥ । 
vastu-sūcaka-nāndīko diṅ-mātra-mukha-saṅgataḥ ।।
arthopakṣepakair hīnaś cūlikā-parivarjitaiḥ । 
aneṣyad-vastu-viṣayaḥ pātraiś tri-caturair yutaḥ ।।
nātiprapañcetivṛttaḥ svādhārāṅkāṅga-śobhitaḥ । 
prastutārthānubandhī ca pātra-niṣkramaṇāvadhiḥ ।।
prathamāṅke na kartavyaḥ so' yaṃ kāvya-viśāradaiḥ । 
so' yam uttara-rāme tu rasotkarṣāya kathyatām ।।
netur utkarṣako jñeyo bāla-rāmāyaṇe tv ayam । 
amogha-rāghave so' yaṃ vastūtkarṣaika-kāraṇam ।।
