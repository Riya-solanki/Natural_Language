[] tatra śrī-vraja-rājñī-
maṇi-maya-vara-pīṭhe yātṛ-mukhyāntarāle
tanaya-nava-vadhūbhiḥ sevitārāt-pradeśā ।
suta-mukha-vidhu-kāntiṃ sā gavākṣāt pibantau
suta-sucarita-tṛṣṇak kṛṣṇa-mātā vyarājīt ।।
[] atha rājñā vrajasya madhuram ājñāpyate sma-"aye madhukaṇṭha-snigdhakaṇṭhau! vayam utkaṇṭhitāḥ smaḥ । tataḥ kiñcid uṭṭaṅkyatām ।"
