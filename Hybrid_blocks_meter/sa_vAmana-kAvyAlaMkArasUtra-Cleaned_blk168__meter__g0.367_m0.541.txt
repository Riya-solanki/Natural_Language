।।
na pṛthak, ārohāvarohayorojaḥprasādarūpatvāt ।।
na pṛthaksamādhirguṇaḥ ārohāvarohayorojaḥprasādarūpatvāt । pjorūpaścārohaḥ prasādarūpaścāvaroha iti ।।
na, asaṃpṛktatvāt ।।
yaduktam ojaḥprasādarūpatvamārohāvarohayoḥ tanna । sampṛktatvāt । sampṛktau khalvojaḥprasādau nadīveṇikāvad vahataḥ ।।
anaikāntyācca ।।
na cāyamekāntaḥ । yadojasyārohaḥ prasāde cāvarohaḥ ।।
ojaḥprasādayoḥ tīvrāvasthā, tāviti cedabhyupagamaḥ ।।
ojaḥprasādayoḥ kvacidbhāge tīvrāvasthāyāmāroho'varohaścetyevaṃ cenmanyase, abhyupagamaḥ na vipratipattiḥ ।।
viśeṣāpekṣitvāttayoḥ ।।sa viśeṣo guṇāntarātmā ।।ārohāvarohanimittaṃ samādhirākhyāyate ।।ārohāvarohagramaḥ samādhiriti gauṇyā vṛttyā vyākhyeyam ।।kramavidhānārthaṃ vā ।।
pṛthakkaraṇamiti । pāṭhadharmatvaṃ ca na sambhavatīti na pāṭhadharmāḥ sarvatrādṛṣṭeḥ ityatra vakṣyāmaḥ ।।
pṛthakpadatvaṃ mādhuryam ।।
bandhasya pṛthakpadatvaṃ yat tanmādhuryam pṛthakpadāni yasya sa pṛthakpadaḥ । tasya bhāvaḥ pṛthakpadatvam । samāsadairdhyanivṛttiparaṃ caitat ।
ajaraṭhatvaṃ saukumāryam ।।
bandhasyājaraṭhatvamapāruṣyaṃ yat yat saukumāryam । pūrvoktamudāharaṇam । viparyayastu yathā
