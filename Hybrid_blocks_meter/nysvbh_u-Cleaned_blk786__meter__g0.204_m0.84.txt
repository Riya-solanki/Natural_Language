svān gandhādīn nopalabhante ghrāṇādīni।
kena kāraṇeneticet ?, svaguṇaiḥ saha ghrāṇādīnāmindriyabhāvāt ।
ghrāṇaṃ svena gandhena samānārthakāriṇā
saha bāhyaṃ gandhaṃ gṛhṇāti tasya svagandhagrahaṇaṃ
sahakārivaikalyānna bhavati evaṃ
śeṣāṇāmapi ।।
