punaruktamiti prakṛtam, nidarśanam autpattidharmakatvādanityam'
ityaktvā arthādāpannasya yo abhidhāyakaḥ śabdastena
svaśabdena brūyāt aanutpattidharmakaṃ nityam'
iti tacca punaruktaṃ veditavyam arthasaṃpratyayārthe
śabdaprayoge pratītaḥ so artho arthāpattyeti
।।
