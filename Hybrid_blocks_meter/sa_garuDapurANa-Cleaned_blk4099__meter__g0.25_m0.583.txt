tasyāyustatra vaṃśe abhūdyayātirbharataḥ kuruḥ ।
śantanustasya vaṃśe abhūdgaṅgāyāṃ śantanoḥ sutaḥ ।।
bhīṣmaḥ sarvaguṇaiyukto brahmavaivartapāragaḥ ।।
śantanoḥ satyavatyāṃ ca dvau putrau saṃbabhūvatuḥ ।
citrāṅgadantu gandharvaḥ putraṃ citrāṅgado avadhīt ।।
