sarāgo jāyate ityarthādāpadyate, ayaṃ jāyamāno
rāgānuviddho jāyate rāgasya pūrvānubhūtaviṣayānucintanaṃ
yoniḥ pūrvānubhavaśca viṣayāṇāṃ pūrvasmin
janmani śarīramantareṇa nopapadyate soyamātmā
pūrvaśarīrānubhūtān viṣayān anusmaran
teṣu teṣu rajyate tathā cāyaṃ dvayorjanmanoḥ
pratisandhiḥ, evaṃ pūrvaśarīrasya pūrvatareṇa
pūrvataraśarīrasya pūrvatamena ityādinānādiścetanasya
śarīrayogaḥ anādiśca rāgānubandha iti
siddhaṃ nityatvamiti ।।
