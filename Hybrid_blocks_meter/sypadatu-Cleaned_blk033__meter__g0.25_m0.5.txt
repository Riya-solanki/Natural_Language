[] saṃcārayan kalabhakaṃ gajanartakaṃ vā
veśyāṅgaṇeṣu bhagadatta ivendradattaḥ
udvīkṣyate stananiviṣṭakarāmbujābhir
vyāghro mṛgībhir iva vāravilāsinībhiḥ
api caiṣā bhartur no adhirājasya syālaṃ pāraśavaṃ kauśikaṃ siṃhavarmāṇaṃ mitram apadiśantī sarvān kāminaḥ pratyākhyānena vrīḍayati kiṃ bravīṣi - kiṃ ca tasyaiṣātikāmitayāvamanyata iti yuṣmaddeśaupayikam eva kila satatam atisevanam kiṃ bravīṣi - deśaupayikam adeśaupayikam iti nāvagacchāmi vispaṣṭam abhidhīyatām iti evam anugṛhītaḥ kathaṃ na kathayiṣyāmi śrūyatām
śravaṇanikaṭajair nakhāvapātair
vanagajadamya ivāṅkitaḥ pratodaiḥ
vivṛtajaghanabhūṣaṇāṃ vivastrāṃ
vṛṣa iva vatsatarīm ihopayāti
kiṃ bravīṣi - tena hy anenaivopāyanenainam upasthāsyāmīti
yady evam indrasvāmī vijñāpyaḥ
daśanamaṇḍalacitrakakundarāṃ
dayitamālyanivāsitamekhalām
