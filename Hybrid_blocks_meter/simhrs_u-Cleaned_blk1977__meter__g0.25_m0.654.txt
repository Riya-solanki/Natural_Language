atha nāṭaka-paribhāṣā-
atha rūpaka-nirmāṇa-parijñānopayoginī । 
śrī-siṃha-dharaṇīśena paribhāṣā nirūpyate ।।
paribhāṣātra maryādā pūrvācāryopakalpitā । 
sā hi naur atigambhīraṃ vivikṣor nāṭya-sāgaram ।।
eṣā ca bhāṣā-nirdeśa-nāmabhis trividhā matā । 
tatra bhāṣā dvidhā bhāṣā vibhāṣā ceti bhedataḥ ।।
caturdaśa vibhāṣāḥ syuḥ prācyādyā vākya-vṛttayaḥ । 
āsāṃ saṃskāra-rāhityād viniyogo na kathyate ।।
uttamādiṣu tad-deśa-vyavahārāt pratīyatām । 
bhāṣā dvidhā saṃskṛtā ca prākṛtī ceti bhedataḥ ।।
kaumāra-pāṇinīyādi-saṃskṛtā saṃskṛtā matā । 
iyaṃ tu devatādīnāṃ munīnāṃ nāyakasya ca ।
liṅgināṃ ca viṭādīnm anīcānāṃ prayujyate ।।
