sajātīyair vijātīyair bhāvair ye tv atiraskṛtāḥ ।
kṣrābdhivan nayanty anyān svātmatvaṃ sthāyino hi te ।। 
bharatena ca te kathitā rati-hāsotsāha-vismaya-krodhāḥ ।
śoko' tha jugupsā bhayam ity aṣṭau lakṣma vakṣyate teṣām ।।
