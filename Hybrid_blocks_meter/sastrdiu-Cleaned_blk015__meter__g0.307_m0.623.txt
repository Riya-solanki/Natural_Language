tatrāpekṣāsaṃnidhiyogyatvairevaṃ vijñāyate- traivarṇikairevopanītairakṣaragrahaṇenādhyayanādiparamparayārthajñānaṃ kartavyamiti ।
evañca phalavadadhyayanakarkatṛbhūtamāṇavasaṃskāratvādupanayanamadhyayanīṅgaṃ, adhyayanamapi dṛṣṭārthajñānārtham, arthajñānaṃ tvanuṣṭhānaupayikatvātprasiddhaphalakameveti sarveṣāṃ phalavattvam ।
