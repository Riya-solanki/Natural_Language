atha vyādhiḥ-
doṣodreka-viyogādyair syād vyādhir atra tu ।
gātra-stambhaḥ ślathāṅgatvaṃ kūjanaṃ mukha-kūṇanam ।। 
srastāṅgatākṣi-vikṣepa-niḥśvāsādyās tu vikriyāḥ ।
saśīto dāha-yuktaḥ sa dvividhaḥ parikīrtitaḥ ।। 
hanu-sañcālanaṃ bāṣpaḥ sarvāṅgotkampa-kūjane ।
jānu-kuñcana-romāñca-mukha-śoṣādayo' pi ca ।।
