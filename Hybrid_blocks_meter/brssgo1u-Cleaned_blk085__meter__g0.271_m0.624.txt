ucyate- anavagatātmavastūpadeśaśca tathaiva bhavitumarhati ।
tadavagatyā mithyājñānasya saṃsārahetornivṛttiḥ prayojanaṃ kriyata ityavāśiṣṭamarthavattvaṃ kriyāsādhanavastūpadeśena ।
apica abrāhmaṇo na hantavyaḥ' iti caivamādyā nivṛttirupadiśyate ।
naca sā kriyā ।
nāpi kriyāsādhanam ।
akriyārthānāmupadeśo anarthakaścet abrāhmaṇo na hantavyaḥ' ityādinivṛttyupadeśānāmānarthakyaṃ prāptam ।
taccāniṣṭam ।
naca svābhāvaprāptahantyarthānurāgeṇa nañaḥ śakyamaprāptakriyārthatvaṃ kalpayituṃ, hananakriyānivṛttyaudāsīnyavyatirekeṇa ।
nañaścaiṣa svabhāvo yatsvasaṃbandhino abhāvaṃ bodhayatīti ।
abhāvabuddhiścaudāsīnyakāraṇam ।
sā ca dagdhendhanāgnivatsvayamevopāśāmyati ।
tasmātprasaktakriyānivṛttyaudāsīnyameva abrāhmaṇo na hantavyaḥ' ityādiṣu pratiṣedhārthaṃ manyāmahe, anyatra prajāpatitrātādibhyaḥ ।
tasmātpuruṣārthānupayogyupākhyānādibhūtārthavādaviṣayamānarthakyābhidhānaṃ draṣṭavyam ।
yadapyuktaṃ- kartavyavidhyanupraveśamantareṇa vastumātramucyamānamānarthakaṃ syāt asaptadvīpā vasumatī' tyādivaditi, tatparihṛtam ।
rajuriyaṃ nāyaṃ sarpa iti vastumātrakathane api prayojanasya dṛṣṭatvāt ।
