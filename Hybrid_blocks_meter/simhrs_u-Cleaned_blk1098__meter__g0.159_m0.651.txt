yathā karuṇākandale-
kulasya vyāpattyā sapadi śatadhoddīpita-tanur
muhur bāṣpaṃ śvāsān malinam api rāgaṃ prakaṭayan ।
ślathair aṅgaiḥ śūnyair asakṛd uparuddhaiś ca karaṇair
yuto dhatte glāniṃ karuṇa iva mūrto yadupatiḥ ।।
