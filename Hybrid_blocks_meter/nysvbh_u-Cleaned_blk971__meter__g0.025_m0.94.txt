yo ayamakarmanimitte śarīrasarge
sati niyama ityucyate ayam
"śarīrotpattinimittavat
saṃyogotpattinimittaṃ karma "
ityanena pratyuktaḥ।
kastāvadayaṃ niyamaḥ ? yathaikasyātmanaḥ
śarīraṃ tathā sarveṣāmiti niyamaḥ, anyasyānyathā
anyasyānyathā ity aniyamaḥ ।
bhedaḥ
uvyāvṛttiviśeṣa
iti, dṛṣṭā ca janmavyāvṛttiḥ uccābhijano
nikṛṣṭābhijana iti praśastaṃ
ninditamiti vyādhibahulam arogamiti
samagraṃ vikalamiti pīḍābahulaṃ sukhabahulamiti
puruṣātiśayalakṣaṇopapannaṃ
viparītamiti praśastalakṣaṇaṃ ninditalakṣaṇamiti
paṭvindriyaṃ mṛdvindriyamiti sūkṣmaśca bhedo aparimeyaḥ,
soyaṃ janmabhedaḥ pratyātmaniyatāt
karmabhedādupapadyate।
asati karmabhede pratyātmaniyate
niratiśayatvādātmanāṃ samānatvācca pṛthivyādīnāṃ
pṛthivyādigatasya
niyamahetorabhāvāt sarvaṃ sarvātmanāṃ
prasajyeta, na tvidamitthambhūtaṃ
janma,tasmād nākarmanimittā śarīrotpattiriti
।।
