svabhāvād, yathā-
uttānām upadhāya bāhulatikām ekām apāṅgāśrayām
anyām apy alasāṃ nidhāya vipulābhoge nitamba-sthale ।
nīvīṃ kiñcid iva ślathāṃ vidadhatī niḥśvāsam unmuñcatī
talpotpīḍana-tiryag-unnata-kucā nidrāti śātodarī ।।
