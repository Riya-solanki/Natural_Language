śrīvāmanapurāṇaṃ- pulastya uvāca । 
tataḥ samāgaccati vāsudeve mahī cakampe girayaca celuḥ ।
kṣubdhāḥ samudrā śrīvāmanapurāṇaṃ- pulastya uvāca ।
tataḥ samāgaccati vāsudeve mahī cakampe girayaśca celuḥ ।
kṣubdhāḥ samudrā divi ṛkṣamaṇḍalo babhau viparyastagatirmaharṣe ।।
