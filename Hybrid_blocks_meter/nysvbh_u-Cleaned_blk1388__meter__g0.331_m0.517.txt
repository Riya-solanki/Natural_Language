vipratipattyapratipattyorvikalpānnigrahasthānabahutvamiti saṃkṣepeṇoktaṃ tadidānīṃ virbhajanīyam।
nigrahasthānāni khalu parājayavastūny aparādhādhikaraṇāni prāyeṇa pratijñādyavayavāśrayāṇi tattvavādinam atattvavādinaṃ cābhisaṃplavante, teṣāṃ vibhāgaḥ
