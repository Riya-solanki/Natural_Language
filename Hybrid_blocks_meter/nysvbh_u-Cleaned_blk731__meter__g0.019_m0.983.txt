ādarśodakayoḥ prasādaḥ ।
rūpaviśeṣaḥ svo dharmaḥ- niyamadarśanāt,
prasādasya vā svo dharmo rūpopalambhanam,
yathādarśapratihatasya parāvṛttasya nayanaraśmeḥ
svena mukhena saṃnikarṣe sati svamukhopalambhanaṃ
pratibimbagrahaṇākhyam ādarśarūpānugrahāt
tannimittaṃ bhavati ādarśarūpopaghāte tadabhāvāt
kuḍyādiṣu ca pratibimbagrahaṇaṃ na bhavati,
evaṃ kācābhrapaṭalādibhiravighātaścakṣūraśmeḥ
kuḍyādibhiśca pratighātaḥ dravyasvabhāvaniyamāditi
।।
