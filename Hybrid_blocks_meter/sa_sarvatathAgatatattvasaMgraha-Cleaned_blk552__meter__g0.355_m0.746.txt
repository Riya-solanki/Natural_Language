atha vajrapāṇiḥ punarapīdaṃ vajraguhyaṃ nāma mahāvajramaṇḍalamabhāṣat । athātaḥ saṃpravakṣyāmi vajramaṇḍalamuttamaṃ ।
vajradhātupratīkāśaṃ vajraguhyamitismṛtaṃ ।।
mahāmaṇḍalayogena sarvamaṇḍalamālikhet ।
sarvamaṇḍalamadhyeṣu buddhamudrāḥ samālikhet ।।
