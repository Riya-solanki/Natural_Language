himāṃśuriva dīptyāsau caṇḍāṃśuriva tejasā ।
niśāhnoriva kartā ca durjanaiḥ sahanālayaḥ ।।
nirvāpaṇaviśeṣeṇa tadvadvarṇaṃ bhavedyadā ।
mṛdulaṃ citrasaṃskāraṃ tadbījamiti kathyate ।।
iti paribhāṣā ।।
bījapraśaṃsanamāha itītyādi ।।
ityuktavidhānena rakto'pi rasendro bījena vinā karmakṛnna bhavati bījenaiva karmakārī syādityarthaḥ ।।
tadbījaṃ dvividhaṃ dviprakāraṃ pītasitaṃ ekaṃ pītaṃ aparaṃ sitaṃ śvetaṃ svarṇarūpyakriyāyogyam ityarthaḥ ।।
tadbījaṃ siddhaṃ sarvalakṣaṇopetaṃ rase pārade niyojyaṃ nāsiddhamiti ।।
