vaicitryaṃ saukumāryaṃ ca yatra saṃkīrṇatāṃ gate ।
bhrājete sahajāhāryaśobhātiśayaśālinī ।।
mādhuryādiguṇagrāmo vṛttimāśritya madhyamām ।
yatra kāmapi puṣṇāti bandhacchāyātiriktatām ।।
mārgo asau madhyamo nāma nānārucimanoharaḥ ।
spardhayā yatra vartante mārgadvitayasaṃpadaḥ ।।
