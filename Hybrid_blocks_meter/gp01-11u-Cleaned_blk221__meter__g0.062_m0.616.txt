samyag vījanam antarā pramadasūhāsa-prasū-sūktayaḥ ।
rucyānāṃ pariveṣaṇaṃ muraripor dṛṣṭi-prasādāmṛtaṃ
yatraivaṃ suhṛdāṃ sabhojana-vidhiḥ sūte na kiṃ vā sukham? ।। [śārdūla]
