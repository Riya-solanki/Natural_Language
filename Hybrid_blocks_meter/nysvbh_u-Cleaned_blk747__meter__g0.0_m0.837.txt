arthaḥ prayojanaṃ tat pañcavidhamindriyāṇām
usparśanenendriyeṇa sparśagrahaṇe sati na
tenaiva rūpaṃ gṛhyate iti rūpagrahaṇaprayojanaṃ
cakṣuranumīyate, sparśarūpagrahaṇe ca tābhyāmeva
na gandho gṛhyate iti gandhagrahaṇaprayojanaṃ ghrāṇamanumīyate,
trayāṇāṃ grahaṇe na taireva raso gṛhyate
iti rasagrahaṇaprayojanaṃ rasanamanumīyate,
na caturṇāṃ grahaṇe taireva śabdaḥ śrūyate iti
śabdagrahaṇaprayojanaṃ śrotramanumīyate, evamindriyaprayojanasyānitaretarasādhanasādhyatvāt
pañcaivendriyāṇi ।।
