prāṇābhṛcceti ।
sūtre cakāraḥ pūrvasūtrasthanaño anuṣaṅgārthaḥ ।
sarvajñapadasamānādhikaraṇaṃ ātmaśabdo na jīvavācītyatacchabdastasmādityarthaḥ ।
nanu anānumānaprāṇabhṛtāvatacchabdāt'ityekameva sūtraṃ kimarthaṃ na kṛtaṃ ubhayanirāsahetorekatvādityata āha-pṛthagiti ।
yogaḥ sūtram ।
uttarasūtrasthahetūnāṃ jīvamātranirāsenānvaye api subodhārthaṃ prāṇabhṛcceti pṛthaksūtrakaraṇamityarthaḥ ।।
