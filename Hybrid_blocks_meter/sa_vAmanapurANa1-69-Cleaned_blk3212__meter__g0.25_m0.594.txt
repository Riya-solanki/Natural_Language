vimānāni ca subhrāṇi pakṣivāhyāni nārada ।
samāruhyādravan sarve yato daityabalaṃ sthitam ।।
etasmin viṣṇuḥ suraśreṣṭha adhiruhya samabhyagāt ।।
tamāgataṃ sahasrākṣastrailokyapatimavyayam ।
vavanda mūrdhnāvanataḥ saha sarvaiḥ surottamaiḥ ।।
