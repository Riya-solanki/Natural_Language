upapadyate iti, ātmana eva smaraṇaṃ na buddhisaṃtatimātrasyeti,
tuśabdo avadhāraṇe।
katham ?
jñasvābhāvyāt jña ityasya svabhāvaḥ
usvo dharmaḥ ayaṃ khalu ajñāsyati
jānāti ajñāsīt' iti trikālaviṣayeṇānekena
jñānena saṃbadhyate taccāsya trikālaviṣayaṃ
jñānaṃ pratyātmavedanīyam ajñāsyāmi
jānāmi ajñāsiṣam' iti vartate tad yasyāyaṃ
svo dharmastasya smaraṇaṃ na buddhiprabandhamātrasya
nirātmakasyeti ।।
