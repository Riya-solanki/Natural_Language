nanvevaṃ sati pratardanavākyanirṇayenaivedamapi vākyaṃ nirṇīyeta ।
na nirṇīyate ।
ayasya caitatkarma' ityasya brahmaviṣayatvena tatrānirdhāritatvāt ।
tasmādatra jīvamukhyaprāṇaśaṅkā punarutpadyamānā nirvartyate ।
prāṇaśabdo api brahmaviṣayo dṛṣṭaḥ aprāṇabandhanaṃ hi somya manaḥ' ityatra ।
jīliṅgamapyupakramopasaṃhārayorbrahmaviṣayatvādabhedābhiprāyeṇa yojayitavyam ।।
