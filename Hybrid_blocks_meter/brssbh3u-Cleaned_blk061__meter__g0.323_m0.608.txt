iṣṭādikāriṇaścandramasaṃ gacchatītyuktam ।
ye tvitare aniṣṭādikāriṇaste api kiṃ candramasaṃ gacchantyuta na gacchantīti cintyate ।
tatra tāvadāhuḥ - iṣṭādikāriṇa eva candramasaṃ gacchantītyetanna ।
kasmāt ।
yato aniṣṭādikāriṇāmapi candramaṇḍalaṃ gantavyatvena śrutam ।
ethāhyaviśeṣeṇa kauṣītakinaḥ samāmananti- aye vai ke cāsmāllokātprayanti candramasameva te sarve gacchanti' iti ।
pañcabhyāmāhutāvityāhutisaṃkhyāniyamāt ।
yasmātsarva eva candramasamāsīdeyuḥ ।
iṣṭādikāriṇāmitareṣāṃ ca samānagatitvaṃ na yuktamiti cet ।
