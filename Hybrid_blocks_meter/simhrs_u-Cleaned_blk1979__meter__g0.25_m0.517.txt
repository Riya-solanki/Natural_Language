atha nirdeśa-paribhāṣā-
sākṣād anāma-grāhyāṇāṃ janānāṃ pratisaṃjñayā । 
āhvāna-bhaṅgī nāṭyajñair nirdeśa iti gīyate ।।
sa tridhā pūjya-sadṛśa-kaniṣṭha-viṣayatvataḥ । 
pūjyās tu devo munayo liṅginas tat-samāstriyaḥ ।।
bahuśrutāś ca bhagavac-chabda-vācyā bhavanti hi । 
āryeti brāhmaṇo vācyo vṛddhas tāteti bhāṣyate ।।
upādhyāyeti cācāryo gaṇikā tv ajjukākhyayā । 
mahārājeti bhūpālo vidvān bhāva itīryate ।।
chandato nāmabhir vācyā brāhmaṇais tu narādhipāḥ । 
deveti nṛpatir vācyo bhṛtyaiḥ prakṛtibhis tathā ।।
sārvabhaumaḥ parijanair bhaṭṭa-bhaṭṭāraketi ca । 
vācyo rājeti munibhir apatya-pratyayena vā ।।
vidūṣakeṇa tu prāyaḥ sakhe rājan nitīcchayā । 
brāhmaṇaiḥ sacivo vācyo hy amātya saciveti ca ।।
śaiṣāir āryety athāyuṣman iti sārathinā rathī । 
tapasvi-sādhu-śabdābhyāṃ praśāntaḥ paribhāṣyate ।।
svāmīti yuva-rājas tu kumāro bhartṛ-dārakaḥ । 
āvutteti svasur bhartā syāleti pṛtanā-patiḥ ।।
bhaṭṭinī svāminī devī tathā bhaṭṭāriketi ca । 
paricārajanair vācyā yoṣito rāja-vallabhāḥ ।।
rājñā tu mahiṣī vācyā devīty anyāḥ priyā iti । 
sarveṇa patnī tv āryeti pitur nāmnā sutasya vā ।।
tāta-pādā iti pitā mātāmbeti sutena tu । 
jyeṣṭhās tv āryā iti bhrātrā tathā syur mātulādayaḥ ।।
