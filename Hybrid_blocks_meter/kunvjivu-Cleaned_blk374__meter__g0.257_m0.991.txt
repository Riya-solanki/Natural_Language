abhidheyāntaratamastasyātiśayapoṣakaḥ ।
ramyacchāyāntarasparśāttadalaṅkartumīśvaraḥ ।।
svayaṃ viśeṣaṇenāpi svacchāyotkarṣapeśalaḥ ।
asaṃbhāvyārthapātratvagarbhaṃ yaścābhidhīyate ।।
alaṅkāropasaṃskāramanohārinibandhanaḥ ।
paryāyastena vaicitryaṃ parā paryāyavakratā ।।
