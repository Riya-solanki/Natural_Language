dharmasya kāraṇavacanam।
yadi dharmo nimittāntaram? tasya heturvācyo yata utpadyata iti।
yogasamādhijasya kāryāvasāyavirodhātprakṣaye saṃvedanenivṛttiḥ।
yadi yogasamādhijo dharmo hetuḥ? tasya kāryāvasāyavipodhātprakṣaye saṃvedanam amatyantaṃ nivarteta।।
