atha ṣaṭ-triṃśad bhūṣaṇāni-
evam aṅgair upāṅgaiś ca suśliṣṭaṃ rūpaka-śriyaḥ । 
śarīraṃ vas tv alaṅkuryāt ṣaṭ-triṃśad bhūṣaṇaiḥ sphuṭam ।।
bhūṣaṇākṣara-saṅghātau hetuḥ prāptir udāhṛtiḥ । 
śobhā saṃśaya-dṛṣṭāntāv abhiprāyo nidarśanam ।।
siddhi-prasiddhī dākṣiṇyam arthāpattir viśeṣaṇam । 
padoccayas tulya-tarko vicāras tad-viparyayaḥ ।।
guṇātipāto' tiśayo niruktaṃ guṇa-kīrtanam । 
garhaṇānunayo bhraṃśo leśa-kṣobhau manorathaḥ ।।
anukti-siddhiḥ sārūpyaṃ mālā madhura-bhāṣaṇam । 
pṛcchopadiṣṭa-dṛṣṭāni ṣaṭ-triṃśad-bhūṣaṇāni hi ।।
