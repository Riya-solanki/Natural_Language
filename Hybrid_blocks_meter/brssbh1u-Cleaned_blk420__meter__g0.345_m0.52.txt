yathā brahmaṇaḥ pratipādakaḥ vaiśeṣiko heturukto naivamarthāntarasya vaiśeṣiko hetuḥ pratipādako astītyāha ।
nānumānikaṃ sāṃkhyasmṛtiparikalpitaṃ pradhānamiha dyubhvādyāyatanatvena pratipattavyam ।
kasmāt, atacchabdāt ।
tasyācetanasya pradhānasya pratipādakaḥ śabdastacchabdaḥ, na tacchabdo atacchabdaḥ ।
na hyatrācetanasya pradhānasya pratipādakaḥ kaścicchabdo asti, yenācetanaṃ pradhānaṃ kāraṇatvenāyatanatvena vāvagamyeta ।
tadviparītasya cetanasya pratipādakaśabdo atrāsti- ayaḥ sarvajñaḥ sarvavit' ityādiḥ ।
ata eva na vāyurapīha dyubhvādyātanatvenāśrīyate ।।
