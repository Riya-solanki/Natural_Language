atha śaṅkā-
śaṅkā cauryāparādhādyaiḥ svāniṣṭotprekṣaṇaṃ matam ।
tatra ceṣṭāmuhuḥ pārśva-darśanaṃ mukha-śoṣaṇam ।। 
avakuṇṭhana-vaivarṇya-kaṇṭha-sādādayo' pi ca ।
śaṅkā dvidyeyam ātmotthā parotthā ceti bhedataḥ ।। 
svākārya-janitā svotthā prāyo vyaṅgyeyam iṅgitaiḥ ।
iṅgitāni tu pakṣma-bhrū-tārakā-dṛṣṭi-vikriyāḥ ।।
