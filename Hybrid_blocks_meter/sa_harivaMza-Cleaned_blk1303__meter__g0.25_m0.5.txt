yat pṛcchasi mahārāja divyāṃ puṇyāṃ kathāṃ śubhām 
kimarthaṃ bhagavān viṣṇur vasudevakule abhavat ।। 
sureśo ripusūdanaḥ ।devalokaṃ samutsṛjya ।
tat te ahaṃ saṃpravakṣyāmi śṛṇu sarvam aśeṣataḥ 
vāsudevasya māhātmyaṃ caritaṃ ca mahādyuteḥ ।।
