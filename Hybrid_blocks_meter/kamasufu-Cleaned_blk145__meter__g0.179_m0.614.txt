iti gamyopāvartanam 
bhavanti cātra ślokāḥ
tāmbūlāni srajaś caiva saṃskṛtaṃ cānulepanam āgatasyāharet prītyā kalā-goṣṭhīś ca yojayet
dravyāṇi praṇaye dadyāt kuryāc ca parivartanam saṃprayogasya cākūtam nijenaiva prayojayet
prīti-dāyair upanyāsair upacāraiś ca kevalaiḥ gamyena saha saṃsṛṣṭā rañjayet taṃ tataḥ param
