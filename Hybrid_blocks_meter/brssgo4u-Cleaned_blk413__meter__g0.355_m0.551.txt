tamupāsakaṃ brahmalokagatamāha-hiraṇyagarbhaḥ mayā khalvimā āpo amṛtarūpā mīyante bhujyante tavāpyasau loko amṛtodakalakṣaṇa ityarthaḥ ।
śrutyantaramāha-sa yatheti ।
bhogasāmye sa dṛṣṭānto yathetyarthaḥ ।
teno ityuśabdo apyarthaḥ ।
salokatāmapītyanvayaḥ ।
sāyujyaṃ samānadehatvaṃ krameṇa muktirvā ।।
