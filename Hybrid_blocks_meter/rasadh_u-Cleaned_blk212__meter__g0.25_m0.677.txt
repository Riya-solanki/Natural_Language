agnisaṃyuktamūlāni mukhāulyāḥ samānayet ।
śrīkhaṇḍaṃ gharṣayet teṣāṃ sasardhena pralepayet ।।
vajramūṣāṃ taraṃgagarbhe prakṣipyo jātyahīrakaḥ ।
kṣiptvā śrīkhaṇḍaśeṣārdhaṃ mūṣāmagniṣṭake kṣipet ।।
dhmātvādyāgninibhāṃ kṛtvā vajridugdhena ḍhālayet ।
sukhenāthānayā yuktyā mriyante jātyahīrakāḥ ।।
tasmiṃsteṣāṃ kṛte cūrṇaṃ prakṣipetkumpake sudhīḥ ।
yo vajrabhasmanā karma prabhāvo agre bhaviṣyati ।।
