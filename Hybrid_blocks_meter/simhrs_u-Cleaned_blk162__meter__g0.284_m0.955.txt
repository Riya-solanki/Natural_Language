atha preṣyābhisārikā-
bāhu-vikṣepa-lulita-srasta-dhammilla-mallikā । 
calita-bhrū-vikārādi-vilāsa-lalitekṣaṇā ।।
maireyāviratāsvāda-mada-skhalita-jalpitā । 
preṣyābhiyāti dayitaṃ ceṭībhiḥ saha garvitā ।।
priyaṃ kaṅkaṇa-nikvāṇa-mañju-vyajana-vījanaiḥ । 
vibodhya nirbhartsayati nāsābhaṅga-puraḥsaram ।।
