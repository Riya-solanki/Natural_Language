tan-mataṃ nānumanute dhīmān śrī-siṃha-bhūpatiḥ । 
bhāvānubandhābhāve ca nāyikātva-parāhateḥ ।।
tasyāḥ prakaraṇādau ca nāyikātva-vidhānataḥ ।
anāyikā-varṇane tu rasābhāsa-prasaṅgataḥ ।।
tathā prakaraṇādīnām arasāśrayatāgateḥ ।
rasāśrayaṃ tu daśadhety ādi-śāstra-virodhataḥ ।।
tasmāt sādhāraṇa-strīṇāṃ guṇa-śālini nāyake । 
bhāvānubandhaḥ syād eva rudraṭasyāpi bhāṣaṇāt ।। atrāha rudraṭaḥ- 
īrṣyā kula-strīṣu na nāyakasya
niḥśaṅka-kelir na parāṅganāsu ।
veśyāsu caitad dvitayaṃ prarūḍhaṃ
sarvasvam etās tad aho smarasya ।। iti ।
