satyamuktam ।
upakramatantratvādupasaṃhārasya tādātmyasaṃpattiḥ seti manyate ।
tathā prāpte abhidhīyate - aātmagṛhītiḥ sadeva somyedamagrāsīt' ityatra chandogānāmapi bhavitumarhatītaravat ।
yathā akatama ātmā' ityatra vājaneyināmātmagṛhītastathaiva ।
kasmāt ।
uttarāttādātmyopadeśāt ।
anvayādite cetsyādavadhāraṇāt ।
yaduktamupakramānvayādupakrame cātmaśabdaśravaṇābhāvānnātmagṛhītiriti tasya kaḥ parihāra iti cetso abhidhīyate syādavadhāraṇāditi ।
bhavedupapannehātmagṛhītiḥ ।
avadhāraṇāt ।
tathāhi - ayenāśrutaṃ śrutaṃ bhavatyamataṃ matamavijñātaṃ vijñātam' ityekavijñānena sarvavijñānamavadhārya tatsaṃpipādayiṣayā asadeva-' ityāha ।
taccātmagṛhītau satyāṃ saṃpadyate ।
anyathā hi yo ayaṃ mikhya ātmā sa na vijñāta iti naiva sarvavijñānaṃ saṃpadyeta ।
tathā prāgutpatterekatvāvadhāraṇaṃ jīvasya cātmaśabdena parāmarśaḥ svāpāvasthāyāṃ ca tatsvabhāvasaṃpattikathanaṃ paricodanāpūrvakaṃ ca punaḥ punaḥ atattvamasi' ityavadhāraṇamiti ca sarvametattādātmyapratipādanāyāmevāvakalpate na tādātmyasaṃpādanāyām ।
nacājñopakramatantratvopanyāso nyāyyaḥ ।
nahyupakrama ātmatvasaṃkīrtanamanātmatvasaṃkīrtanaṃ vāsti ।
sāmānyopakramaśca na vākyaśeṣagatena viśeṣeṇa virudhyate viśeṣākāṅkṣitvātsāmānyasya ।
sacchabdārtho api ca paryālocyamāno na mukhyādātmano anyaḥ saṃbhavatyato anyasya vastujātasyārambhaṇaśabdādibhyo anṛtatvopapatteḥ ।
āmnānavaiṣamyamapi nāvaśyamarthavaiṣamyamāvahati ।
āhara pātraṃ pātramāharetyevamādiṣvarthasāmye api taddarśanāt ।
tasmādevañjātīyakeṣu vākyeṣu pratipādanaprakārabhede api pratipādyārthābheda iti siddham ।।
