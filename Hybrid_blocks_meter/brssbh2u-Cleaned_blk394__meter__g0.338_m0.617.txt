syādetat ।
yathā karaṇagrāmaṃ cakṣurādikamapratyakṣaṃ rūpādihīnaṃ ca puruṣo adhitiṣṭhatyevaṃ pradhānamapīśvaro adhiṣṭhāsyatīti ।
tathāpi nopapadyate ।
bhogādidarśanāddhi karaṇagrāmasyādhiṣṭhitatvaṃ gamyate ।
nacātra bhogādayo dṛśyante ।
