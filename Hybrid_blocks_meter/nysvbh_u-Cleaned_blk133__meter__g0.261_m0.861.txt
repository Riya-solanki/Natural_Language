aprakṣayaśca dharmasya niranumānamutpattidharmakasyānityatvāt।
yogasamādhijo dharmo na kṣīyata iti nāstyanumānam।
utpattidharmakamanityamiti viparyayasya tvanumānam।
yasya tu saṃvedanoparamo nāsti tena saṃvedanaheturnitya ityanujñeyam।।
